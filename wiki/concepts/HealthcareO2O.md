# Healthcare O2O

Healthcare O2O is the mobile-internet period's attempt to turn medical and health services into on-demand local services in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode covers drug delivery, accompaniment, nurse visits, doctor visits, elderly care, massage, testing, blood draws, and other service experiments.

The source's main lesson is that healthcare O2O is more fragile than ordinary local services. Frequency can be low, service quality is nonstandard, medical risk is high, provider credentials matter, and delivery or labor cost can overwhelm gross margin.
