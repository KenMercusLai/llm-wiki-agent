# HMO Managed Care

HMO managed care is the health-maintenance model discussed near the end of [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode explains it as a fixed per-person payment logic that gives the healthcare organization an incentive to keep users healthier, reduce unnecessary hospitalization, and manage risk earlier.

The source treats [[PingAnGoodDoctor]] as the clearest company example and [[DigitalHealthCommunity]] as a city-level public-health analogue. The key difficulty is localization: China's public insurance, public hospitals, family-doctor system, and specialist access do not map directly to the U.S. commercial-insurance HMO setting.
