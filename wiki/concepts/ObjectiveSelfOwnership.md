# Objective Self-Ownership

Objective self-ownership is [[sp-02-wo-you-ziji-de-yuzhou-ruhe-zai-hundun-zhong-zuo-ge-qingxing-de-xingdongpai-748593778]]'s practice of recognizing one's "客观自我" without turning it into either shame or excuse. [[QianJing|钱静]] names school, major, height, family background, and credential facts as conditions to own before making decisions, not as proof that the person should despise themselves or stop acting.

The source uses a card-game image: play the hand actually held rather than spending life resenting the absence of a perfect hand. This is not fatalism. It is a precondition for [[ActionAgainstAnxiety]], because action starts to work only when a person stops narrating their facts as disqualification.

## Key Claims
- Objective facts should be recognized before they are interpreted as limits, excuses, or insults.
- Self-deprecating language can train both the speaker and listeners to treat the speaker as less worthy.
- Good decisions require knowing the actual hand, but knowing the hand is different from surrendering to it.
- Owning the objective self supports ambition because it turns desire into concrete work on real constraints.
