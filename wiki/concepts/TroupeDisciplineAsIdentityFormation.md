# Troupe Discipline As Identity Formation

Troupe discipline as identity formation is the episode's frame for how artistic apprenticeship can remake a child through bodily control, humiliation, punishment, and dependence. [[64-bawangbieji-fengmo-yu-chenghuo-656094350]] develops the concept through the early scenes of [[FarewellMyConcubine|《霸王别姬》]], where Xiaodouzi enters Guan's troupe after his mother cuts off his sixth finger.

The source stresses that the troupe is not only a school. It functions like a small authoritarian system: children are beaten, trained past pain, forbidden private judgment, and made to accept that survival and recognition come through submission. [[DuanXiaolou|小石头 / 段小楼]] initially protects Xiaodouzi, but the same system later turns protection, performance, and coercion into one continuous formation.

## Key Claims
- Training can produce art while also producing trauma.
- A school-like institution can act politically when it destroys private judgment and demands total submission.
- The same discipline that makes mastery possible can also narrow the forms of selfhood available to the child.
