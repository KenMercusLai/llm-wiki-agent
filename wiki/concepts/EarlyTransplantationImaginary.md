# Early Transplantation Imaginary / 早期移植想象

Early transplantation imaginary is the source's frame for how skin grafts, tooth transplants, artificial respiration, freezing-and-revival experiments, and early organ-transfer speculation made body repair and body assembly thinkable. In [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]], this material sits beside [[JohnHunter|John Hunter]] and [[CadaverTradeAnatomyFear]].

For [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]], the concept helps explain why the creature's stitched body is not only a horror image. It also reflects a period trying to learn whether body parts could be moved, revived, joined, or functionally restored.
