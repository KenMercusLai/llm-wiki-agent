# Battery Consistency And Safety

Battery Consistency And Safety is the quality-control problem in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] where many cells must operate together inside a vehicle pack. [[ZhangCongzhi]] explains that voltage, internal resistance, capacity, materials, electrolyte, separator quality, manufacturing defects, and PACK integration can compound across thousands of cells.

The episode treats safety as a manufacturing-system result. Moisture, burrs, dust, magnetic contaminants, and process instability can create performance decay, abnormal charging, reduced range, or thermal-runaway risk, so quality control is central to industrial advantage rather than a final inspection step.
