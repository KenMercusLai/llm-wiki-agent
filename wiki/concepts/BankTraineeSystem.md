# Bank Trainee System

Bank trainee system is the historical-to-modern recruitment and training pattern discussed in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]]. The episode compares [[WarOfFaith]]'s bank practice-student setting with modern management trainee programs: candidates are screened, trained, rotated, assessed, and either placed into roles or eliminated.

The concept connects the drama's talent-entry story to the wiki's existing bank-career pages. A finance career is not only about intelligence; it also depends on institutional gates, credentials, training curriculum, assessment criteria, department demand, and role boundaries.

## Key Claims
- Training can cover accounting, abacus or calculation skills, counterfeit recognition, business processes, and trade-related finance.
- Entry gates such as education, documents, background checks, and school signals can determine who gets access to a financial institution.
- Rotations and final placement make a trainee program a labor-allocation system, not just a classroom.
- The drama's fire-exam scene is treated by the source as theatrical character testing rather than a plausible normal bank exam.
