# Creation As Consumption

Creation as consumption is the idea that making something can itself be the entertainment experience. In [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]], [[Xiaoning]] and [[Koji]] note that creative processes such as coding with AI, photography, editing, or generating interactive content can satisfy the same emotional need that play does.

## Key Claims
- AI can blur the line between tool use and play because iteration, surprise, and authorship feel rewarding.
- A generated game may be less important than a generative entrance that lets others continue remixing or shaping the experience.
- This does not remove the platform problem: creators and consumers still need return loops, distribution, status, and feedback.
