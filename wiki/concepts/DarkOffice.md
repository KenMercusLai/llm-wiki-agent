# Dark Office

Dark office is [[ZhangShaofeng]]'s analogy from "dark factory" manufacturing automation to office work in [[weishenme-gongsi-yong-buhao-ai-cong-jiaolv-dao-xingdong-de-3-ge-guanjian-dongzuo-duitan-bairong-zhineng-zhang-shaofeng-lgarngnaqran2c9p4jssurvt6ces]]. The idea is that agents such as [[OpenClaw]] may eventually complete long office workflows that previously required white-collar or gray-collar human labor.

## Key Claims
- The concept is about workflow automation and work restructuring, not only individual productivity.
- It depends on [[DigitalEmployees]] being able to take roles, follow rules, use systems, hand off context, and be measured against human output.
- The Bairong source is cautious about the transition: first simulate existing processes and authority boundaries before rewriting the whole organization.
- A dark office still needs [[AIOrganizationDesign]], incentives, compliance boundaries, and [[AgentFacingInterfaces]] before it can be operationally safe.
