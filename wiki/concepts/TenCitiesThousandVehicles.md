# Ten Cities Thousand Vehicles

Ten Cities Thousand Vehicles is the 2009 Chinese new-energy vehicle program highlighted in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. [[ZhangCongzhi]] treats it as a foundational inflection because government subsidies created early demand before private EV adoption was mature.

The program matters in the source because demand creation pulled firms, engineers, local governments, and supply-chain actors into the power-battery field. It is the policy-demand layer beneath later company success by [[CATL]], [[BYD]], and other domestic battery producers.
