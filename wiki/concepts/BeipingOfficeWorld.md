# Beiping Office World

Beiping office world is the literary setting pattern [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]] draws from [[LaoShe|老舍]]'s [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]]. The finance office is not only background; it is a small system where punctuality, pretty documents, promotion, superstition, factional influence, favors, dinner pressure, sexual brokerage, and avoidance all structure what characters can do.

The concept extends [[WorkplaceHiddenRules]] through fiction. Unlike a modern advice source that teaches hidden rules for survival, this source shows how hidden rules can become absurd and spiritually narrowing when everyone already knows the system is petty but still treats it as life itself.

## Key Claims
- A fictional office can diagnose bureaucracy by making small routines feel morally consequential.
- Promotion and punishment in the novel depend on competence, chance, superstition, and factional pressure together.
- Office satire becomes darker when workplace rules leak into marriage, housing, family rescue, and personal fantasy.
