# Solid-State Battery Commercialization

Solid-State Battery Commercialization is the source's caution against treating solid-state batteries as an imminent replacement for mature lithium-ion batteries. In [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]], [[HeQianran]] and [[ZhangCongzhi]] say real progress may arrive around 2030, but scale and replacement speed will likely lag market hype.

The episode identifies several bottlenecks: unclear definitions between all-solid-state and semi-solid-state designs, interface problems, lithium dendrites, pressure requirements, cycle-life material separation, cost, supply-chain immaturity, energy-density uncertainty, and safety evaluation.
