# Streaming Author Brand

Streaming author brand is the pattern where an author's name becomes a platform-facing genre promise, helping viewers choose a show even when individual titles blur together. In [[fault-lines-venezuelas-paltry-earthquake-response-6a44e38375e7a3e96111931b]], [[HarlanCoben]] is the case: [[Netflix]] can package his thrillers as familiar family mysteries with twists, melodrama, glossy settings, and recognizable branding.

This differs from an [[EntertainmentIPFlywheel]] built around owned characters or worlds. The repeated asset is the author's name and formula, not a single story universe. It still connects to [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] because production location, tax breaks, title branding, and platform discovery all shape whether the author name becomes a reliable streaming signal.
