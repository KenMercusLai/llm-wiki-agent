# Internet Hospital

Internet hospital is the licensed institutional form for online medical services in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode uses Wuzhen, Yinchuan, and later national policy to show how online consultation, electronic prescriptions, follow-up visits, remote care, and hospital-backed service scope became a regulated category.

The concept's key tension is scope. Early local pilots could be broad, but national rules later required internet hospitals to rely on physical medical institutions and respect first-diagnosis, doctor identity, prescription, and AI-substitution boundaries.
