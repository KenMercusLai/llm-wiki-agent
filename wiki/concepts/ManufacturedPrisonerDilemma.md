# Manufactured Prisoner Dilemma

Manufactured prisoner dilemma is the artificial competition structure that makes people under shared domination distrust or exploit one another. [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] develops it through [[NarkinaFive]], where prison work groups compete for rewards and punishments while believing discipline can preserve their chance of release.

The episode explicitly connects this mechanism to companies, team-building exercises, and organizational games that teach people to protect themselves by sacrificing others. In the source, the prison revolt begins only after the false promise of release breaks and prisoners can see that obedience will not save them.

## Key Claims
- Competition can turn people with shared interests into local rivals.
- Reward and punishment systems can make domination feel like peer failure rather than institutional design.
- Hope can stabilize the dilemma when people believe compliance will eventually restore freedom.
- The dilemma collapses when the institution's promise is exposed as false.
