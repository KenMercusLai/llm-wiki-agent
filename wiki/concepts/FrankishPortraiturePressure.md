# Frankish Portraiture Pressure

Frankish portraiture pressure is the source's frame for the Western-style visual logic pressing on the court workshop in [[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]]. In [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]], the pressure appears through the Sultan's secret book, the desire for recognizable portrait likeness, perspective, individual presence, and the possibility that the ruler's image can occupy the center of a painting in a new way.

The concept is not simple modernization triumph. The episode stresses that [[OrhanPamuk|帕慕克]] does not resolve the conflict by saying East and West should blend cleanly. Western portraiture opens technical and expressive possibilities, but it also threatens [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]]'s inherited relation to memory, anonymity, mastery, and sacred order.
