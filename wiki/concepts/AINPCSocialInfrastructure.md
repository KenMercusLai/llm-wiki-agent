# AI NPC Social Infrastructure

AI NPC social infrastructure is the use of AI characters as designed social participants rather than isolated chatbots. In [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]], [[Xiaoning]] uses [[Xingbugudi]] and its NPC Nalo to show how an AI character can model conversation, rescue cold starts, and regulate atmosphere in multiplayer scenes.

## Key Claims
- The valuable unit is not only an NPC answering player questions, but an AI character embedded in scene design, IP, social roles, and group norms.
- In multiplayer environments, the AI can act as host, facilitator, conversational example, and atmosphere regulator.
- This differs from generic companion chat because the AI's behavior is constrained by the game world, character identity, and social setting.
