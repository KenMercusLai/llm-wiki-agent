# Local Battery Industrial Clusters

Local Battery Industrial Clusters is the city-level industrial-policy pattern in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. The episode uses [[Changzhou]] and [[Yibin]] to show how local governments combine funds, land, suppliers, talent pipelines, electricity, universities, and anchor firms to create battery-production capacity.

The concept is not just "subsidy." Changzhou shows supplier density and a four-hour industrial circle, while Yibin shows how a later city can use green power, hydropower, a university city, and [[CATL]] to join an existing national chain.
