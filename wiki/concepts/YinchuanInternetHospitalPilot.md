# 银川互联网医院试点

Yinchuan Internet Hospital Pilot is the local-policy experiment in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode says Yinchuan issued an early internet-hospital license and allowed a broad set of services, drawing companies such as [[WeDoctor]], [[DXY]], [[PingAnGoodDoctor]], [[ChunyuDoctor]], and JD-related healthcare players into local internet-hospital setup.

The source treats the pilot as a short-lived wide-open moment. Once national policy required internet hospitals to rely on physical institutions, the Yinchuan model became more historically important than generally repeatable.
