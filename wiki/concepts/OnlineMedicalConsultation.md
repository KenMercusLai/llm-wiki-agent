# Online Medical Consultation

Online medical consultation is the light-diagnosis and question-answering category in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. [[ChunyuDoctor]] is the source's main example: users had many low-acuity questions that could reduce anxiety without immediately going to a hospital.

The episode's central caution is economic and clinical. Consultation is useful, but it is hard to standardize, hard to price, and often sits upstream from the higher-value parts of healthcare: tests, drugs, procedures, hospitalization, chronic management, and insurance payment.
