# Battery Recycling Loop

Battery Recycling Loop is the source's next-stage supply-chain opportunity around recovering materials from used batteries. In [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]], [[HeQianran]] argues that the [[UnitedStates]] lacks a large domestic battery-production base but has a large consumer base that can become a future recoverable battery resource.

The concept connects battery manufacturing to [[CriticalMineralsGeopolitics]]: recycling does not remove resource constraints, but it can turn past consumption into domestic material access and reduce some exposure to mined inputs.
