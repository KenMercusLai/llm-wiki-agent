# Alien Abduction Narrative

Alien abduction narrative is the episode's frame for stories of being taken by extraterrestrials, examined, impregnated, controlled, or returned with altered memory. [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] treats these stories as modern variants of older demon, witchcraft, sexual-violation, and reproduction-anxiety narratives.

The source does not reduce every witness to a liar. It emphasizes [[MemoryContaminationRisk]], hypnosis, cultural templates, and the need to protect real victims of harm without turning every recovered or dramatic story into confirmed alien evidence.

## Key Claims
- Abduction stories often inherit old narrative structures even when their surface technology is modern.
- Hypnosis and cultural expectation can shape memory reports.
- The right response is neither ridicule nor automatic belief; it is careful corroboration.
