# Silicon Carbon Governance

Silicon carbon governance is [[BairongIntelligence]]'s organization frame in [[e225-saasye-shuqianyi-shizhi-zhengfa-ai-ruhe-biange-zuzhi-jiagou]] for companies made of both carbon-based human employees and silicon-based AI employees. It makes [[DigitalEmployees]] a management architecture rather than a metaphor: AI workers have roles, names, email accounts, job numbers, KPIs, performance reviews, training, and retirement.

## Key Claims
- The concept assumes AI workers become a majority in some organizational layers, while humans retain responsibility for strategy, supervision, edge cases, signatures, and accountability.
- Each AI employee needs a human partner or manager who can train, evaluate, retrain, or remove it from service.
- Reporting lines may become less simple than humans managing AI; some humans may follow AI-managed workflows or report into systems that allocate work.
- The governance problem includes HR records, incentive design, audit trails, compliance, escalation, and permission boundaries.
- The frame extends [[AIOrganizationDesign]] from model-company talent structure into everyday enterprise operating structure.
