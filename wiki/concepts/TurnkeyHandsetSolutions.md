# Turnkey Handset Solutions

Turnkey Handset Solutions are integrated phone-design packages that let manufacturers build handsets without developing every module themselves. In [[no-208-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-shang-motuoluola-nuojiya-he-ailixin-de-zhushen-huanghun-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-23-998056376]], [[MediaTek]]'s 2004 turnkey approach is presented as a turning point in Chinese feature-phone manufacturing.

## Source Position
- Turnkey designs bundled core phone functions and reduced the need for deep R&D across baseband, multimedia, wireless, GPS, and interface modules.
- This lowered the barrier for small firms, design houses, and regional brands, accelerating [[ShanzhaiPhones]] and reshaping [[Huaqiangbei]].
- The concept also explains why first-generation brands such as [[BirdMobile]] and [[Kejian]] lost advantage: license access and simple branding were less valuable once many firms could assemble a phone.
