# Draft Wheel

The draft wheel is a draft-order proposal in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] where every team moves through a predetermined sequence of pick positions over time. The design removes the direct connection between a team's season record and its draft slot.

The source treats the wheel as the cleanest anti-tanking proposal because losing no longer improves draft position. Its weakness is recovery speed: bad teams could remain bad while waiting for their scheduled high pick, and small-market teams may fear losing one of the few mechanisms that offsets free-agent disadvantages.

## Key Claims
- The draft wheel directly attacks [[SportsTanking]] by removing record-based draft rewards.
- It creates predictability and fairness across long time horizons.
- It can be hard to sell politically because weak teams may need help sooner than the wheel provides.
- The proposal shifts the problem from anti-tanking to [[SportsCompetitiveBalance]] and small-market confidence.
