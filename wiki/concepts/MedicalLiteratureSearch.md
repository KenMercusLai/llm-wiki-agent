# Medical Literature Search

Medical literature search is the professional knowledge-access layer in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode frames [[PubMed]]'s free web access to MEDLINE as one of the first internet tools that meaningfully improved doctors' and medical students' work.

The concept matters because it explains why early internet healthcare was not primarily consumer-facing. [[LiTiantian]]'s path from literature retrieval to [[DXY]] shows that professional information needs can create durable communities before appointment booking, online consultation, or drug ecommerce become visible consumer categories.
