# AI Interactive Content Platforms

AI interactive content platforms are the episode's "AI TikTok" or AI-era creator-platform question: if ordinary users can generate interactive experiences, what platform helps them create, distribute, consume, remix, and return? In [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]], [[Xiaoning]] argues that creation tools are necessary but not sufficient.

## Key Questions
- Who is the creator, and how is their value visible if AI does much of the generation?
- Who is the consumer, and why do they return repeatedly rather than sample a novelty once?
- Does the platform distribute finished works, remixable prompts, play states, templates, characters, or social scenes?
- Is success measured by raw users, by retention, by creator status, by remix behavior, or by [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]]?
