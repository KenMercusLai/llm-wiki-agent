# Attention As Weather

Attention as weather is the source's frame for attention as an atmosphere or geology that holds bodies rather than a resource individuals simply pay. In [[bayo-akomolafe-the-untimely-cenyca-k7ry]], [[BayoAkomolafe]] uses the phrase to explain why some insights are ignored until repeated by a person with higher-status credentials.

The concept connects time to value. If attention bends toward race, expertise, whiteness as practice, settlement, and institutional status, then modernity shapes not only calendars and clocks but also what can arrive, be heard, or become real.

## Key Claims
- Attention is socially and materially patterned before individuals choose where to focus.
- Race, credentials, institutional value, and modernity can decide which insights become audible.
- Cracks in attention may be sites where [[TheUntimely]] arrives unexpectedly.
