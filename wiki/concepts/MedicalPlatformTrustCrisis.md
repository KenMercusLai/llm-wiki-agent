# Medical Platform Trust Crisis

Medical platform trust crisis is the source's frame for moments when search ranking, online ads, hospital outsourcing, health content, or sales incentives make medical information look more trustworthy than it is. In [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]], the [[WeiZexiIncident]] and Quanjian controversy are the main examples.

The concept matters because trust failure changes the whole industry. After the Wei Zexi case, medical ads, commercial appointment services, and online healthcare enthusiasm faced sharper public and regulatory scrutiny. After the Quanjian investigation, professional content brands such as [[DingxiangDoctor]] gained more public importance as counterweights to questionable medical claims.
