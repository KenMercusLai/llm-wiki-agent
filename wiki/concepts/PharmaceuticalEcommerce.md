# Pharmaceutical Ecommerce

Pharmaceutical ecommerce is the medicine-sales and online-pharmacy branch of [[InternetHealthcare]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode traces it from early online drugstore attempts through regulatory shutdown, OTC permission, license changes, prescription-drug policy shifts, and later growth around [[AlibabaHealth]], [[JDHealth]], [[DingdangKuaiyao]], [[Meituan]], and [[Pinduoduo]].

The source treats pharmaceutical ecommerce as more commercially durable than online diagnosis because products, inventory, delivery, pricing, and repeat prescriptions are more standardizable. The ceiling remains real: prescription boundaries, medical-insurance payment, public-hospital control, and trust all constrain what can move online.
