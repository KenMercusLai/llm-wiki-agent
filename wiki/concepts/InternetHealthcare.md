# Internet Healthcare

Internet healthcare is the umbrella industry frame in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. It covers hospital IT, medical literature retrieval, doctor communities, appointment booking, online consultation, drug ecommerce, internet hospitals, medical content, AI-assisted workflows, and long-term health management.

The episode's core position is that healthcare cannot be "internetized" like a standard consumer service. Platform matching works poorly when doctor supply is scarce, diagnosis is risky, service quality is hard to standardize, public hospitals control the core resource, medical insurance shapes payment, and regulation must protect patient trust.
