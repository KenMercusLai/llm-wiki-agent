# Customer Success Engineer

Customer Success Engineer, abbreviated CSE, is the mature-product adoption role contrasted with [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] in [[ep128-cong-palantir-dao-openai-fde-hui-chengwei-ai-shidai-zui-zhongyao-de-xin-gangwei-ltozkutz-gvff4xu-feyzflhvz2u]]. In the episode's framing, a CSE helps customers actually use an existing SaaS product, adapt to the vendor's practices, configure workflows, train users, and keep receiving value after purchase.

The distinction matters because FDE work sits earlier in the product cycle. CSEs usually operate when the product is already standardized enough to teach, configure, and renew; FDEs enter when customer demand is still ambiguous, the business problem must be defined, the solution may require new engineering, and useful learning must flow back into product or model development.

## Key Claims
- CSE work is tied to mature product usage, retention, training, configuration, and ongoing customer value.
- [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] work is tied to zero-to-one implementation, product discovery, custom engineering, and reusable-product extraction.
- In enterprise AI, relabeling CSE or project-manager jobs as FDE does not create real FDE capability unless the role also gains engineering depth, customer-site authority, and product-feedback responsibility.
- The CSE/FDE distinction helps separate post-sale adoption from [[BusinessLedAITransformation]], where the customer may not yet know what AI should do.
