# Dynastic Legitimacy Backfire / 王朝合法性回旋镖

Dynastic legitimacy backfire / 王朝合法性回旋镖 is the central mechanism in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]]. The episode argues that [[WesternHanDynasty|Western Han]] rulers strengthened their authority through [[MandateOfHeavenLegitimacy]], but by doing so also made it thinkable that Heaven could abandon the Liu family and validate [[WangMang|王莽]].

The backfire is not only conceptual. Once succession trouble, disasters, court anxiety, and [[AuspiciousOmenPolitics|祥瑞]] accumulated, sacred legitimacy became a weapon that could be turned against the dynasty that had depended on it.

## Key Claims
- A legitimacy story can become dangerous when its logic can be used by opponents.
- Sacred signs reduce political dispute only while interpretation stays controlled.
- The source treats Wang Mang's usurpation as a result of Han political theology returning against its creators.
- The mechanism connects founding myths, prophecy, elite self-interest, and public crisis.
