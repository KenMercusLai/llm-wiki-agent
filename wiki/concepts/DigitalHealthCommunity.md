# 数字健共体

Digital health community is WeDoctor's city-level health-management idea in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode uses Tianjin's chronic-disease management example to show a route from internet healthcare toward prevention, triage, grassroots return, and lower per-person medical spending.

The concept is close to [[HMOManagedCare]] but adapted to a public-health and local-government context. Rather than selling a single online consultation, the system tries to manage diabetes and other chronic conditions through monitoring, screening, education, medication, and referral paths.
