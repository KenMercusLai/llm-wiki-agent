# Building Public

Building public is the practice of sharing product ideas, progress, prototypes, failures, and launch attempts while the product is still being made. In [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]], [[ChenJingchu]] argues that it is one of the best AI-era product levers because AI has lowered build cost and social platforms can supply early exposure, feedback, and distribution.

The source also gives [[Xiaohongshu]]'s platform version through [[Shanbin]]: public posting helps builders find needs, users, collaborators, and peers before a product is complete. The implied rule is to publish earlier instead of waiting for perfection, then use public response to decide whether to continue, revise, or change direction.

## Key Claims
- Public posting can expose early [[CustomerPull]] before a formal launch.
- When [[VibeCoding]] makes prototypes cheaper, distribution and feedback loops become more important.
- The practice supports [[FastProductValidation]] when attention, comments, demos, and purchase questions reveal whether a project resonates.
- Social platforms can become app-store-like [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]] channels for independent AI products.
- Building public also creates personal identity and peer connection, not only acquisition.
