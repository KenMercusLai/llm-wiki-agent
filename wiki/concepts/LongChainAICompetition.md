# Long-Chain AI Competition

Long-chain AI competition is [[YinQi]]'s frame for the foundation-model race in [[131-yin-qi-churen-jieyue-xingchen-dongshizhang-de-fangtan]]. The point is that model competition is not a single benchmark contest: it spans talent density, research, compute, capital, application pull, data collection, business-model closure, terminal strategy, and organization design.

## Key Claims
- The model itself remains central, but model strength alone does not make a company durable.
- Foundation-model companies must avoid commercial paths that cannot support the scale of required R&D investment.
- [[AIPlusTerminals]] is one proposed way to give a foundation-model company stronger product pull and differentiated data.
- Physical-world routes lengthen the chain further because vehicle, cabin, robot, and interaction data take years to collect and productize.
- [[AIOrganizationDesign]] is part of the chain because very high talent density still has to become coordinated work.
