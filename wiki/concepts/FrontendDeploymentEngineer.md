# Frontend Deployment Engineer

Frontend Deployment Engineer, abbreviated FDE in the source, is described as a role for bringing AI into enterprise workflows. The job involves mapping processes, culture, context, databases, and existing work systems, then helping digital workers fit into the organization.

## Role In The Episode
- The host says [[OpenAI]] and [[Anthropic]] have both discussed this kind of role.
- The role is framed as a likely bottleneck because enterprise AI adoption may be slower and more context-heavy than headline excitement suggests.
- It connects agent capability to real organizational change.

## Connections
- [[ContextEngineering]] — enterprise context is the deployment substrate.
- [[AgenticWorkflow]] — what FDE work tries to install inside companies.
