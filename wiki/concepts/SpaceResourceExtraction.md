# Space Resource Extraction

Space resource extraction is the commercial-use side of the space-law distinction in [[tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says no one can claim territory on the [[Moon]], but extracted resources may be commercially exploited, much as fish caught on the high seas can be owned even though the sea itself is not owned.

The source presents extraction as one driver of a renewed lunar race. If resource locations are partly known, getting there first may create strategic or economic advantages, even before the full technical and commercial pathway is settled.
