# 华生力 / Watson Power

华生力 is the fictional ability that makes people near 小何 suddenly reason with detective-level clarity. In [[158-quanyuan-mingzhentan-tuili-shupian-lai-yibao-929377122]], [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] treats it as the core engine of [[QuanYuanXianYiRen|《全员嫌疑人》]] and [[QuanYuanMingZhenTan|《全员名侦探》]]: 小何 does not take the usual genius-detective role, but his presence turns suspects, witnesses, victims' relatives, staff, or bystanders into temporary "名侦探."

The device reverses the detective-assistant hierarchy. Instead of the assistant documenting brilliance, the assistant-like figure becomes the condition that lets brilliance appear in others.

## Key Claims
- 华生力 lets the series change solvers without changing its central anchor.
- The device creates comic escalation because many ordinary people can suddenly produce polished deductions.
- It supports [[PuzzleSnackMystery|推理薯片]] by making each case an ensemble reasoning game.
- It also turns 小何's kidnapping frame into a meta-mystery: someone may want either to block the ability or use it.
- The device is not realistic psychology; its function is to make the mystery format flexible and funny.
