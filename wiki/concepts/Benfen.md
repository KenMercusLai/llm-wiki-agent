# Benfen / 本分

Benfen is the episode's frame for doing what should be done after stripping away pressure, temptation, ego, and external scoring. In [[e44-li-xiaobo-duihua-mengyan-zheci-jiu-zheyang-ba-lpcrvfgnseaed-eambk9ofnvuq2]], [[LiXiaobo]] connects it to [[DuanYongping]] and to daily choices in company strategy, product design, and ordinary life. [[MengYan]] links it with [[AsItIsPractice]] and the question of whether a person can act naturally when the right thing appears.

The concept is not passive obedience. It asks whether a decision is fitting to the thing itself: a fund platform should not turn investment into attention extraction; an investor should not let markets dominate family life; a founder should not confuse moral language with actual user responsibility.

## Key Claims
- Benfen is both a question of what to do and how to do it.
- In organizations, benfen has to be transmitted from the top through repeated concrete decisions, not only slogans.
- Benfen can support [[ValuesAsOperationalAsset]] when a value changes product rules, fees, hiring, and communication.
- It overlaps with [[PurposeDrivenBusiness]], but it is more situational and practice-oriented than brand purpose.
- Benfen becomes difficult when [[FinancialGravity]], status, revenue, or ego make the "fitting" action less profitable or less visible.
