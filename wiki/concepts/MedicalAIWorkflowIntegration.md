# Medical AI Workflow Integration

Medical AI workflow integration is the source's practical frame for AI in healthcare. In [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]], many companies and hospitals explore medical large models after 2023, but the episode cautions that value is more likely to come from integration into existing workflows than from a standalone AI doctor chatbot.

The source points to imaging assistance, medical-record reading, follow-up, pharmacy review, triage, and health-management support as more plausible use cases. It also repeats the regulatory boundary that AI cannot replace real doctors for diagnosis and treatment responsibility.

[[tech-20251222-1222-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251222-1222-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds a physician-workflow example through [[HassanBenchikran]]. He describes using AI through ambient scribe tools and research-model digests when patients mention unfamiliar diseases, while the episode separately shows [[DoctorGuidedAIInterpretation]] as a workflow for reviewing patient AI outputs inside the appointment.
