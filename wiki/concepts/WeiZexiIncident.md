# 魏则西事件

The Wei Zexi incident is the source's central trust-breaking case for Chinese internet healthcare. In [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]], Wei found a treatment path through [[Baidu]] search, received treatment connected to a hospital department and promoted claims, and died in 2016 after his condition worsened.

The episode uses the incident to connect search advertising, public-hospital department outsourcing, private medical capital, and misleading treatment promotion. Its industry effect was broader than one company: medical advertising contracted, platform trust fell, and online healthcare moved from mobile-internet enthusiasm into a more cautious regulatory period.
