# Muscle As Longevity Infrastructure

Muscle as longevity infrastructure is [[zhongnian-san-zhanghu-xianjinliu-jirou-shuimian-lnyomru5v2yzo1-otuyw2mdj-vae]]'s reframing of muscle away from appearance and toward reserve capacity. The source, drawing on [[Outlive|《超越百岁》]], treats skeletal muscle as a body account that supports posture, movement, glucose handling, illness recovery, fall prevention, and old-age independence.

The concept extends [[EmbodiedAging]] backward into prevention. Instead of waiting until stairs, suitcases, knees, and household tasks become difficult, the episode asks what reserve can be built during midlife. It also connects to [[MindBodyUnion]] by rejecting the casual cultural split where intellectual work treats the body as optional maintenance.

## Key Claims
- Muscle quantity and strength are not the same as visible leanness or aesthetic lines.
- Muscle loss can be hard to notice in the mirror, while strength and functional capacity may decline faster than expected.
- Strength training is account-like because earlier deposits can protect later independence when illness, bed rest, or age draws reserves down.
- The source emphasizes progressive overload, protein intake, and sleep-supported repair, while avoiding an extreme supplement or body-hacking stance.
- Practical adherence matters: walking, cycling, a nearby gym, and a small back-training routine may be more valuable than an ideal plan that never repeats.
