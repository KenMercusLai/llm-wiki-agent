# Forward Deployed Product Manager

Forward Deployed Product Manager, or FDPM, is the product-side counterpart to [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]]. In [[Jove]]'s [[Cresta]] account, the FDPM is closer to a "Forward Deployed CEO": the role understands customer needs, agent behavior, quality expectations, and trust, while FDEs carry more of the technical implementation, testing, and product-feedback loop.

## Key Claims
- FDPMs help decide which use cases should be attempted, what good agent behavior means, and whether the customer has enough data, access, and operational readiness.
- The role carries customer communication and product judgment, especially when nontechnical stakeholders need confidence that an AI agent will behave safely and usefully.
- The episode presents FDPM/FDE pairing as a specialization pattern: enterprise AI deployment needs both engineering depth and product/customer trust ownership.
- A typical project can involve one FDPM with two or three FDEs, and one FDPM may cover multiple deployments as the team scales.
