# Modern Prometheus Reading / 现代普罗米修斯读法

Modern Prometheus reading is the source's way of using [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]]'s subtitle. In [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]], [[Prometheus|Prometheus / 普罗米修斯]] is not only a mythic decoration; he lets the episode compare divine fire, human creation, and Romantic ambition with experimental science.

The "modern" part matters. Shelley keeps the mythic charge of making life, but she routes it through natural philosophy, chemistry, electricity, anatomy, and exploration. That turns an old myth into [[ScienceFictionContemporaryScienceIntegration]] and [[CreatorResponsibilityEthics]].

## Key Claims
- The subtitle helps connect mythic creation with modern scientific apparatus.
- Promethean ambition becomes dangerous when making outpaces responsibility.
- The reading frame preserves awe and warning together instead of treating the novel as simply anti-science.
