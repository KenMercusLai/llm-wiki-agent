# Sodium-Ion Battery Storage

Sodium-Ion Battery Storage is the future route [[HeQianran]] says she is more optimistic about in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. The episode says sodium-ion batteries can reduce reliance on lithium, cobalt, nickel, and sometimes copper, making them strategically interesting for storage use cases where energy density is less important than cost, life, and stability.

The source presents grid-scale static storage as the clearer first market. It also suggests that AI data centers and large GPU clusters may create load shocks and voltage fluctuation problems where cheap, stable battery buffering becomes valuable.
