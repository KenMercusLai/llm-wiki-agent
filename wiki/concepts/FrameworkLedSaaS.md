# Framework-Led SaaS

Framework-led SaaS is software built around a named operating methodology, expert community, or professional framework. In [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]], [[Ninety]] is built around the [[EOSWorldwide]] ecosystem, where the language of the method, the coaches who teach it, and the companies self-implementing it all shape product strategy.

## Key Claims
- A known framework gives the product a clear vocabulary, use case, and customer education path.
- The expert community can supply early trust and distribution, especially when coaches or implementers bring software into client work.
- Licensing can improve legitimacy and terminology fit, but it can also slow marketing, teaching, and product changes.
- A framework-led product must eventually decide whether it is only a tool for the framework or a broader platform.
