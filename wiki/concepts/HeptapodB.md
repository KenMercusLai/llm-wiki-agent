# Heptapod B

Heptapod B / 七文B is the written language of the [[Heptapods|heptapods / 七肢桶]] in [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]]. In [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]], [[LouiseBanks|Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯]] discovers that it is not a transcription of the spoken language but a separate system whose meaning appears as a completed graphic whole.

The episode treats the writing system as the hinge between language and time. Because a Heptapod B utterance has no privileged beginning or end, it becomes a model for [[NonlinearTimePerception]] and for the possibility that language can reorganize cognition beyond ordinary sequence.

## Key Claims
- A writing system can have a different logic from speech rather than merely recording it.
- Nonlinear writing makes the whole utterance relevant before any individual component is placed.
- The story connects graphic form, bodily orientation, physics, and time perception into one speculative system.
- Learning the language is not just decoding; it transforms the learner's relation to memory, future, and choice.
