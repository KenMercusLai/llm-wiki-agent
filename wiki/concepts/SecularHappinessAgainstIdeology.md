# Secular Happiness Against Ideology

Secular happiness against ideology is the closing value frame [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]] draws from [[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]]. After the novel stages murder, religious pressure, artistic rivalry, and the conflict between [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]] and [[FrankishPortraiturePressure]], the hosts emphasize Shekure, Black, and the children pursuing ordinary safety, marriage, family continuity, and worldly happiness.

The concept does not dismiss art or belief. It marks the source's refusal to let grand civilizational arguments consume the people living inside them. Shekure's legal tactics, household decisions, grief management, and demand that Black protect her children all keep the novel grounded in survival and everyday life.

[[135-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-san-dan-823746670]] adds a direct anti-doctrine version. After moving through [[HGWells|H. G. Wells / H. G. 威尔斯]], [[JeanPaulSartre|Jean-Paul Sartre / 萨特]], and [[AynRand|Ayn Rand / 安·兰德]], the source warns that any "ism" can become a false master key; real life, real feelings, and real loved ones should remain stronger than abstract performance.
