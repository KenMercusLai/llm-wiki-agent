# Voice Interaction

Voice interaction is presented as a still-open opportunity for AI-native products. The host argues that typing is not the most comfortable human interface, especially for Chinese input, and that voice can carry richer information than text.

## Source Notes
- The episode connects voice to early WeChat usage patterns and voice messages.
- It also mentions interactive audio experiences, including a game controlled by spoken pitch.
- Voice is framed as both an interface and a content format.
