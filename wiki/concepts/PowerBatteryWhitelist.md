# Power Battery Whitelist

Power Battery Whitelist is the 2015 policy mechanism discussed in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. [[HeQianran]] says the whitelist excluded Japanese and Korean battery suppliers from the subsidized Chinese EV market and accelerated domestic firms such as [[CATL]] and [[BYD]].

The source uses the whitelist to show that China's battery rise was not pure market selection. Policy protection gave local firms enough demand, time, and investment confidence to build [[BatteryManufacturingKnowHow]] and a domestic [[PowerBatteryIndustryChain]].
