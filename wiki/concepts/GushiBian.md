# 古史辨 / Doubting Antiquity

古史辨 / Doubting Antiquity is the historical-scholarship frame used by [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] to explain the background of [[LiShui|《理水》]]. The episode presents [[GuJiegang|顾颉刚]] and the ancient-history-doubt current as questioning inherited accounts of Yao, Shun, [[YuTheGreat|大禹]], and early Chinese antiquity.

The source treats this method ambivalently. Doubting ancient history can be an important break from unexamined tradition, but [[LuXun|鲁迅]]'s satire targets the moment when philological cleverness and evidential performance detach from living suffering. The "禹是一条虫" scene is therefore not only a joke about scholarship; it is a warning about interpretation losing contact with the flood below.
