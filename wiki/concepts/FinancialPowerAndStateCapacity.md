# Financial Power And State Capacity

Financial power and state capacity is the EP23 frame that banks, accounting systems, currencies, taxation, bonds, and resource trade are instruments for organizing an economy, not just neutral financial products. In [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]], [[GuZhun]]'s accounting and tax work, [[CentralBankOfChina]]'s financing role, [[TreasuryBondSpeculation]], and [[BorderRegionCurrencyCredit]] all show finance as a way to build, contest, or exploit political order.

The concept connects state capacity to verification and trust. A government or movement needs ledgers, taxes, credible money, usable goods, financing channels, and enforcement; the same instruments can also become extraction tools when elite actors control information and institutions.
