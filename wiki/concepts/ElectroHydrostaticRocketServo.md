# Electro-Hydrostatic Rocket Servo

Electro-hydrostatic rocket servo actuation is the engine-control approach highlighted in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]]. The episode says [[LongMarch10B]] uses an electro-hydrostatic servo that combines electric actuation with hydraulic force transmission for engine gimbal control.

The source frames the servo as the rocket's "muscle" in terminal descent. Traditional hydraulic servos can be reliable but heavy and leakage-prone, while purely electric systems can reduce weight but raise reliability and jam-risk questions. The electro-hydrostatic route is presented as a compromise for precise, high-load, high-reliability attitude control during final recovery.
