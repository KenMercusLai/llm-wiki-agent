# Enterprise Prearranged Agents

Enterprise Prearranged Agents is [[YangMeng]]'s distinction in [[144-dui-yang-meng-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-xiaofei-dianzi-si-yu-sheng-di-san-lei-gongsi-duan-ce-moxing-chanpin-fangfa-youxi-moshi-lnjleqjgjo1txupouvygmdv7oo8b]] between personal AI use and enterprise AI use. Personal AI can often compose tools and steps ad hoc; enterprise AI needs successful methods, processes, data access, permissions, and workflows pre-arranged into stable agents.

For [[AnkerInnovations]], this means an AI middle platform rather than scattered accounts. The company connects multiple models and entry points, then tries to turn working methods into reusable agents that can call company data and procedures reliably.

## Key Claims
- Enterprise AI is less about individual prompt cleverness and more about encoding proven organizational methods.
- Agents need stable access to the data, workflow state, process rules, and tools that already run the company.
- Hardware companies face extra digitization work because much operational data exists in physical supply chains, product testing, manufacturing, and offline routines.
- The transformation path is: people embrace AI, capabilities become agents, agents connect to each other, then organization form changes around them.
- Prearranged agents extend [[AIOrganizationDesign]] because they make process ownership, permissions, and cross-team coordination explicit.
