# Mother Goddess Evidence Leap

Mother goddess evidence leap is the episode's warning against a common chain of overclaiming: female figurines become goddess worship, goddess worship becomes mother-goddess religion, and mother-goddess religion becomes proof that women held social or political power. [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] says every step in that chain needs separate evidence.

The concept protects [[MatriarchyQuestion]] from romantic archaeology. The episode notes that female images could be deities, ritual objects, puberty-related objects, toys, erotic objects, or something else. Even real goddess worship, saintly mother imagery, or sea-goddess devotion does not automatically improve actual women's legal status, bodily autonomy, property control, or political voice.

## Key Claims
- Female representation is not the same as female rule.
- Religious reverence for mothers or goddesses can coexist with restricted women.
- Archaeological interpretation needs intermediate evidence before reaching social-structure claims.
- The concept extends the wiki's broader evidence guardrail against attractive but under-supported pattern reading.
