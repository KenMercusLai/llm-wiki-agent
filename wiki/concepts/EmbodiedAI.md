# Embodied AI

Embodied AI refers to physical AI, robotics, and systems that act in real-world environments. The episode treats the area as highly valued and bubbly, but also important enough that the host does not want to dismiss every participant as speculative.

## Commercialization Mentioned
- Robot performances and rentals.
- Data collection.
- Some industrial scenarios.
- Sales to research institutions.
