# Gold Plan

The Gold Plan is a draft-order mechanism in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] where eliminated teams improve draft position by winning after they are out of playoff contention. The episode says the plan was developed by [[AdamGold]] and adopted by the [[ProfessionalWomensHockeyLeague|PWHL]].

The plan's appeal is that it reverses the late-season incentive. Instead of asking bad teams to lose for a better pick, it gives fans and players a reason to keep competing after playoff hopes are gone.

## Key Claims
- The Gold Plan keeps games meaningful after elimination by attaching draft value to wins.
- It preserves a weak-team path to better picks without making current losses valuable after elimination.
- Very bad teams may still struggle to earn the top pick if they are too weak to win after elimination.
- Teams might try to game the system by losing early enough to be eliminated sooner.
