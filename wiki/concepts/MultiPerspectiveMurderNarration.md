# Multi-Perspective Murder Narration

Multi-perspective murder narration is the way [[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]] lets the murder mystery unfold through many first-person voices, including lovers, suspects, objects, images, colors, and the murderer. In [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]], [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] treats this structure as central to the book's meaning: who speaks, who sees, and who controls the frame matter as much as who killed whom.

The form mirrors the art debate. Western portraiture pulls toward centered individual likeness, while [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]] holds a more distributed relation to scene, memory, and meaning. The novel's many voices make the reader compare perspectives rather than settle into one authoritative eye.
