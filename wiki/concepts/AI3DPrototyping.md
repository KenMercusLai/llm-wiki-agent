# AI 3D Prototyping

AI 3D prototyping is the possible production shift in [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]] where AI lowers the barrier to building 3D interactive worlds. [[Xiaoning]] argues that 3D is one of the biggest historical thresholds separating small teams from large game productions.

## Key Claims
- If AI handles more of the 3D pipeline, small teams may be able to test many "first episode" prototypes before committing to full production.
- The unsolved technical issues include topology, rigging, animation, consistency, asset integration, and playable-system quality.
- AI 3D matters most when it changes experimentation density, not merely when it produces impressive standalone assets.
