# High Sensitivity As Difference / 高敏感作为差异

High sensitivity as difference is the episode 187 reframing of "豌豆公主" from insult into a possible description of real perceptual variation. In [[187-wandou-gongzhu-gaomin-he-hundun-shi-ni-zhenming-999958166]], the hosts argue that a person who notices heat, texture, sound, pressure, or small discomfort more intensely is not automatically spoiled, manipulative, or morally weak.

The concept is deliberately not a diagnosis. It is a social and interpretive frame: when someone reports an experience others do not share, the first question should not be whether they deserve ridicule. The episode connects this to [[SelfFeelingTrust|相信自己的感受]], ordinary comfort choices, and resistance to [[HardshipCultureAsEducation|吃苦文化作为教育]], while still distinguishing honest sensitivity from exaggeration.

## Key Claims
- Sensitivity can be a real difference in perception rather than a failure of character.
- People should be able to name discomfort without immediately being accused of luxury, affectation, or entitlement.
- Comfort goods and environmental adjustments can be legitimate responses to varied bodies, not only consumerist decadence.
- Honesty matters: the concept defends truthful expression of experience, not status performance around fragility.
