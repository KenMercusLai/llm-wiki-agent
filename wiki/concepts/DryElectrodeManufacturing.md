# Dry Electrode Manufacturing

Dry Electrode Manufacturing is a possible battery-production route discussed in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. [[HeQianran]] describes it as a route that could matter because incumbent Chinese firms have deep know-how in wet coating, while dry electrode processes might let other ecosystems compete on a different manufacturing basis.

The episode frames dry electrodes as a possible "curve change" rather than a guaranteed breakthrough. Its strategic importance comes from changing where the tacit process bottleneck sits.
