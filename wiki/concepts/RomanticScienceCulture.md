# Romantic Science Culture / 浪漫主义科学文化

Romantic science culture is the source's frame for a period when poetry, political radicalism, natural philosophy, chemistry, electricity, medicine, and speculation about life overlapped. In [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]], [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]] grows up in a household and social world where writers and scientific figures could belong to the same conversation.

The concept is especially visible through [[PercyShelley|Percy Shelley / 珀西·雪莱]], [[ErasmusDarwin|Erasmus Darwin]], [[WilliamGodwin|William Godwin / 威廉·戈德温]], and the [[VillaDiodati|Villa Diodati / 迪奥达蒂别墅]] circle. Their world lets [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] combine mythic creation, experiment, bodily fear, and radical responsibility without belonging to a single modern disciplinary shelf.
