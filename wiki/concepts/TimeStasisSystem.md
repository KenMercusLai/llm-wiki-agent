# Time-Stasis System

Time-stasis system is [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]]'s reading of the mad tea party in [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》]]. The scene is funny because time has stopped at six o'clock: there is always tea time, but no later time in which cups can be washed, seats reset, or mess resolved.

The concept is useful beyond the specific scene because it turns a joke into a system problem. When time stops, procedure continues without recovery capacity; participants respond by rotating around the table instead of fixing the accumulated disorder. That makes the tea party a local example of [[NonsenseLogic]] and [[DreamLogicNarrative]].

## Key Claims
- A stopped clock can create system behavior, not only atmosphere.
- Repetition becomes absurd when cleanup, closure, and consequence are removed.
- The tea party's social pressure comes from being trapped in a permanent phase of a routine.
- Time-stasis helps explain why Wonderland's nonsense feels structured rather than random.
