# Power Battery Industry Chain

Power Battery Industry Chain is the full-stack battery production system described in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]]. [[HeQianran]] says China's advantage comes from covering upstream resources, midstream materials, cell manufacturing, PACK, and BMS integration rather than only from one chemistry or one lead company.

The concept matters because power batteries are system goods. Materials, equipment, cell consistency, pack design, vehicle integration, recycling, and storage uses all interact, so a country or city with dense suppliers can improve faster than a single firm importing equipment into a thin ecosystem.

## Key Claims
- Full-chain coverage turns manufacturing feedback into shared supplier learning.
- Battery advantage depends on process, materials, equipment, and local coordination as much as on chemistry.
- The industry-chain frame explains why [[CATL]], [[BYD]], and [[ATL]] can be discussed alongside [[Changzhou]] and [[Yibin]] rather than only as isolated companies.
