# Diffusion Transformers

Diffusion transformers, or DiT, are discussed in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] as a [[FAIR]]-period architecture result connected to [[XieSaining]]'s broader [[RepresentationLearning]] line. Xie says DiT began from thinking about diffusion-model representations rather than simply deciding to make a diffusion model.

## Source View
The source treats DiT as meaningful but not as a field-defining revolution on the scale of LeNet, AlexNet, [[ImageNet]], ResNet, Transformer, GPT-3, BERT, CLIP, ViT, or GAN. It then connects later REPA and RAE work to the question of whether strong representations can guide or become the encoder foundation for generative models.
