# Cyber Data Theft and Leak Operations

Cyber data theft and leak operations are attacks where intrusion and stolen data are paired with deliberate disclosure or attention campaigns. In [[tech-20260305-0305-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260305-0305-mp-tech-pod-128]], [[RafePilling]] describes this as part of the evolution of [[IranLinkedCyberOperations]] beyond DDoS and website defacement.

The concept differs from simple website disruption because the harm can continue after the initial compromise. The episode points to attacks on the Albanian government beginning in 2022 as an example where data theft was paired with a continuing campaign meant to undermine confidence, spread uncertainty, or damage institutional credibility.

## Key Claims
- Theft and leaks can turn a technical intrusion into a reputational and political operation.
- The impact can be amplified by information campaigns that direct public attention to the stolen material.
- Sensitive data changes the risk profile: medical, psychiatric, financial, or personal records can harm people even if core services recover.
- Data-destruction tools, wipers, and ransomware-like software can combine confidentiality loss with availability loss.
