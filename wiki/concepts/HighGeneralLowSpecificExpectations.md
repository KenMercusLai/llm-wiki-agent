# High General, Low Specific Expectations

High general, low specific expectations is the episode's expectation model for escaping perfectionist planning. In [[129-ruhe-chengwei-zhenzheng-de-buwanmei-zhuyizhe-hunshen-shi-jiner-799220151]], [[QinZong|秦总]] distinguishes broad life expectation from concrete task expectation: a person can keep faith that happiness, love, achievement, and growth are possible while setting today's action threshold very low.

The source's bad pattern is the reverse: low general expectation and high specific expectation. In that state, a person feels pessimistic about life overall but demands that each dish, essay, workout, episode, or interaction meet a punishing standard. The episode treats that pattern as exhausting because every small task becomes a referendum on the self.

## Key Claims
- Broad optimism gives action emotional room; low concrete thresholds make action executable.
- Over-high concrete standards can destroy motivation even when the larger wish is healthy.
- Tiny completed actions can gradually raise general expectation because the person accumulates evidence of agency.
- The concept is compatible with [[PositiveExpectationTraining]] when optimism remains tied to behavior rather than magical certainty.
