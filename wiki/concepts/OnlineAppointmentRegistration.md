# Online Appointment Registration

Online appointment registration is the healthcare platform route represented by [[WeDoctor]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. It solved a concrete user problem: reducing the uncertainty, queuing, and information friction of hospital registration and expert appointments.

The source also shows why this category is institution-dependent. Appointment platforms need hospitals to release supply, public-hospital resources remain scarce, and policy can sharply limit commercial cooperation around registration and add-on appointments.
