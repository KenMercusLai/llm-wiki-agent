# Domestic AI Relations

Domestic AI relations is the episode's way of reading artificial intelligence through household intimacy, labor, and etiquette rather than through battlefield or workplace replacement. In [[130-tuokaerqiuke-guaidan-gushiji-xunzhao-he-gaobie-803543569]], the concept comes from 〈拜访〉 in [[BizarreStories|《怪诞故事集》]], where the household's "爱公" figures cook, clean, garden, care for a child, handle money, receive guests, tire, shut down, and restart.

The source treats this as strong robot fiction because the technological premise is folded into ordinary domestic order. AI is not simply a threat outside the door; it is the condition that lets the narrator write, paint, raise a child, avoid strangers, and sustain a home. The result extends [[RobotHumanBoundary]] toward dependency, consumption, sexuality, gendered labor, and family self-deception.

## Key Claims
- Domestic AI stories can reveal care and power more sharply than stories focused only on machine rebellion.
- Shutdown, restart, task satisfaction, eye-contact rules, and paid unlocking make intimacy look like both relationship and device management.
- The household's stability depends on invisible labor, so the AI premise exposes ordinary arguments about whose work counts.
- The story is a form of [[ScienceFictionRealism]] because its invented household makes recognizable family arrangements easier to see.
