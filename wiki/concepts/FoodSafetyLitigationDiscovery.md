# Food Safety Litigation Discovery

Food safety litigation discovery is the information-gathering role of lawsuits in [[the-sneaky-way-companies-get-new-chemicals-into-our-food]]. [[BillMarler]] says subpoena power, documents, and testimony let him investigate the [[DailyHarvest]] tara-flour case in ways that public records alone did not.

The concept matters because [[SecretGRAS]] can keep ingredient decisions outside ordinary FDA notification. When that happens, litigation may become the path that reveals the supply chain from [[DailyHarvest]] to [[Smirks]] and [[MolinosPeru|Molinos]].

The source also shows a limit: [[ConsumerArbitrationBarrier|arbitration clauses]] can keep many claims out of court, so discovery may depend on plaintiffs who did not sign the relevant terms.
