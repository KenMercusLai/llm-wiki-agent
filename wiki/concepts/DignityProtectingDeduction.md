# 体面保护式推理 / Dignity-Protecting Deduction

体面保护式推理 is the episode 168 pattern where the detective act is completed by care, not exposure. In [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]], [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]] uses clues about a bicycle, tofu pudding, and cilantro to infer [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]]'s hidden family pressure, but he does not turn the inference into a public reveal.

The concept differs from mystery's usual reveal scene. The point is not to assemble everyone and announce the truth; the point is to protect a friend's self-respect while still acting on what the truth requires. 阿里 lets [[MengTongxueFangxuehou|孟同学]] accept a partial theft theory, borrows a bicycle, and brings a hot bowl of tofu pudding to 严良.

## Key Claims
- In some mysteries, the ethical endpoint is discretion rather than revelation.
- The solver may need to manage other people's interpretations so that a vulnerable person is not forced into confession.
- Compassion is not a substitute for deduction; it changes what the deduction should do once made.
- The concept is especially strong in youth fiction because adolescent help is often clumsy, concrete, and indirect.
- This pattern turns [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]] into a friendship story without dissolving its reasoning structure.
