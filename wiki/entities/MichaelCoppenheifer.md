# Michael Coppenheifer

Michael Coppenheifer appears in [[tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128]] describing how students use AI and how colleges should respond. He says students ask [[ChatGPT]] and similar tools to inspect [[Reddit]] and [[Instagram]] for campus vibe, then use AI to compare acceptances, scholarships, and pros and cons.

Coppenheifer also frames the institutional side of [[HigherEducationAIDiscoverability]]. He says colleges should pay attention to how AI systems describe them, test their AI presence like "secret shoppers," and keep public digital information current so AI tools do not surface outdated or incomplete material.
