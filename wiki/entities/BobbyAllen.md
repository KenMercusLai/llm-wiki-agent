# Bobby Allen

Bobby Allen appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as the [[NPR]] reporter investigating the [[Kalshi]] prediction-market boom for [[PlanetMoney]]. His reporting arc moves from trader forums and hands-on app use into [[PredictionMarketLegalBoundary]], [[PredictionMarketEthics]], and [[EventContractManipulationRisk]].

The episode uses Bobby as both observer and participant. He enters Kalshi Discord chats expecting stories of addiction or financial harm, learns trader vocabulary around "degens," "sharps," and "alpha," then makes small bets with [[MaryChilds]] to test how the app changes attention and emotion.
