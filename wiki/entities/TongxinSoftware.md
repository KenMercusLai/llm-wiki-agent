# Tongxin Software

Tongxin Software is the company at the center of [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode uses a reported annual-meeting dress-code conflict to examine how [[Deepin]], [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]], and [[ChengmaiTechnology]] assets were combined into a company serving [[XinchuangOperatingSystems]] and government-enterprise customers through [[TongxinUOS]].

## Source Position
- Tongxin is presented as the commercial and institutional-market successor to an earlier community Linux lineage.
- Its customer base is described as government, state-owned, and enterprise-heavy, which makes formal image management more understandable than in a purely developer-led company.
- The episode argues that threatening dismissal over clothing still crosses a management boundary, especially if the employee was a core technical contributor.
- The company is used as a case of [[TechnicalCultureSalesCultureTension]] and [[OpenSourceCommunityCommercialization]].

## Connections
- [[TongxinUOS]] — commercial operating-system product.
- [[Deepin]] and [[HiweedLinux]] — community lineage behind the company.
- [[LiuWenhuan]], [[WangJiping]], [[LinWei]], [[WangYong]], and [[ZhangLei]] — people tied to the company's formation or later governance.
- [[KylinOS]] — domestic operating-system competitor discussed in the source.
