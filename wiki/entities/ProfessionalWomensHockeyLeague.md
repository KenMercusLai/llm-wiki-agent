# Professional Women's Hockey League

The Professional Women's Hockey League appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as the league that adopted the [[GoldPlan]] for draft ordering. The episode uses the PWHL as a live alternative to the [[NationalBasketballAssociation|NBA]] lottery because it rewards eliminated teams for winning rather than losing.

The PWHL case is useful because it shows new leagues can test mechanisms that older, higher-revenue leagues may resist. It also keeps the tradeoff visible: the Gold Plan can improve late-season effort, but it may not guarantee that the weakest team receives the best young talent.
