# Michel de Montaigne / 蒙田

Michel de Montaigne / 蒙田 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] through a pun around essays and probation. The episode says [[FriedrichNietzsche|尼采]] criticizes Montaigne's reports as too short while Montaigne has somehow remained on a "trial period" for decades.

The gag turns literary form into workplace status. It is a small example of [[PhilosophyWorkplaceSatire]] because a writer associated with exploratory self-writing becomes an employee evaluated by report length and employment procedure.
