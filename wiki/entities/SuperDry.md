# Super Dry

Super Dry is the [[AsahiGroup]] beer product used in [[dang-heike-gongpo-le-riben-de-guomin-pijiu-chule-jugong-daoqian-tamen-hai-neng-zuo-shenme-feat-top-of-japan-keji-luandun]] as the consumer-facing signal of a cyber incident. The hosts describe Japanese supermarket shelves where competitors remained available while Asahi supply was visibly constrained.

The product matters because it turns [[RansomwareBusinessContinuity]] from an abstract IT problem into retail reality. If a flagship item cannot be ordered, allocated, shipped, or replenished normally, consumers experience the cyberattack as an ordinary shortage rather than as a network event.
