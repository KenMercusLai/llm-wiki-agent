# Caterpillar

Caterpillar is the industrial company at the center of [[tech-20260216-0216-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260216-0216-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode frames the company as a less obvious winner from AI infrastructure because data centers need power quickly, and some developers are using Caterpillar natural gas generators as [[DataCenterOnsitePower]] instead of waiting for grid interconnection.

The source emphasizes a role shift for Caterpillar's generator business. Equipment that was traditionally used for backup power, such as emergency generation for hospitals, is increasingly being used as primary power for data centers. That turns Caterpillar into part of the wiki's [[AIEnergyBottleneck]], [[AIComputeContinuity]], and [[MaaSInfrastructure]] branch rather than only a heavy-equipment manufacturer.

## Key Claims
- Caterpillar manufactures engines and generators that can convert natural gas into electricity for large facilities.
- AI data-center growth creates demand for power-generation equipment outside the usual backup-power market.
- The source says Caterpillar has maxed out production capacity and has a growing backlog for power generation equipment.
- Strong AI data-center demand may lengthen waits for traditional generator customers such as hospitals.
