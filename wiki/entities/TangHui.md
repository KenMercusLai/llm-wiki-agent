# 唐辉 / Tang Hui

Tang Hui appears in [[vol-262-qu-xibanya-mai-zuqiu-julebu-yichang-huangdan-de-shangye-maoxian-992614191]] as [[LiXiang]]'s partner in the [[JumillaCF]] acquisition and as the other half of the "西甲欢乐多" football-media pairing. In the source, Tang is part of the founding investor group that tried to turn a Spanish lower-tier club into a development platform for Chinese players.

## Key Claims
- Tang's role makes the project a media-and-fan passion case as much as a pure financial investment.
- The Jumilla experience is used to show how difficult it is for outside investors to convert football knowledge, enthusiasm, and contacts into enforceable club control.

## Connections
- [[LiXiang]] - partner and narrator of the source.
- [[JumillaCF]] - club acquired by the investor group.
- [[ChinesePlayerOverseasArbitrage]] and [[FootballClubControlRisk]] - source concepts tied to the partnership.
