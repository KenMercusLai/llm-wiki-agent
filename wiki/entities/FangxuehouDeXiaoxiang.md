# 《放学后的小巷》

《放学后的小巷》 is [[ZhongShengli|钟声离]]'s Chinese [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]] collection as discussed in [[168-fangxuehou-de-xiaoxiang-zhi-qingchun-zhi-nanian-de-youyi-richang-tuili-qiangshi-huigui-953669535]]. The episode presents it as a short-story sequence connected by a shared protagonist and a school-side alley that students call "堕落巷."

The discussed first story, "不加香菜", follows [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]], [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]], and [[MengTongxueFangxuehou|孟同学]] through a tiny puzzle about tofu pudding, a borrowed bicycle, and a mismatched gear shifter. Its force comes from [[DignityProtectingDeduction|体面保护式推理]]: the solution matters because 阿里 understands 严良's poverty and self-respect without exposing him.

## Key Claims
- The book is presented as Chinese daily-life mystery rather than murder-centered or trick-centered detective fiction.
- Its "堕落巷" setting turns a school perimeter into [[SchoolAreaMemoryEcology|放学后记忆生态]].
- The first story's clues are ordinary objects: food preference, bicycle parts, prize ownership, and timing.
- The episode values the book not only for inference but for its youth friendship, poverty, embarrassment, and period texture.
- The source notes that the author has acknowledged one later ending as somewhat over-forceful, so the episode praises the book while leaving room for craft limits.
