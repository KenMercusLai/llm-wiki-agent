# Fantagraphics Books

Fantagraphics Books is the comics and graphic-novel publisher in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]]. [[ChristianStClair]] says two Fantagraphics books were printed in [[India]] and expected in the United States in early April before the [[StraitOfHormuz]] crisis made the shipment uncertain.

In the wiki, Fantagraphics is a small cargo-owner case inside global shipping risk. The source shows how even a niche publishing shipment can be exposed to [[MaritimeChokepointTolling]], [[ChokepointShippingConfidence]], carrier uncertainty, and possible [[GeneralAverage]] costs.
