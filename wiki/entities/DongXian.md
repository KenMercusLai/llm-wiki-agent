# Dong Xian / 董贤

Dong Xian / 董贤 is [[HanAidi|Emperor Ai of Han]]'s favorite and the figure who mishandles the succession crisis in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]]. The episode says Emperor Ai entrusted him with the imperial seal before death, but Dong Xian blocked information without taking effective political action.

His role is mainly diagnostic. He had palace and military proximity but lacked the judgment needed to convert possession into rule, allowing [[WangZhengjun|王政君]] and [[WangMang|王莽]] to recover control. That failure sharpens the source's distinction between symbols, access, and actual political capacity.

## Key Claims
- Dong Xian's possession of the seal did not become durable authority.
- His indecision created a path for Wang Mang's reentry.
- The episode uses him to show that sacred or formal objects matter only when backed by organization and judgment.
