# Maarva Andor

Maarva Andor is [[CassianAndor]]'s mother figure in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode says she understands the evil of [[GalacticEmpire]] earlier and more clearly than Cassian does, while Cassian initially remains more focused on survival, family care, and finding his sister.

Her funeral message on [[Ferrix]] becomes the trigger for community anger to become public resistance. The source emphasizes that she does not demand that others sacrifice for her; she names her own wish to have fought harder, making her important to [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]] and [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
