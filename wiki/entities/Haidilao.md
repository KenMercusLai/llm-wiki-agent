# Haidilao / 海底捞

Haidilao appears in [[e232-canyin-chuhai-you-xin-zhaor-ma-cong-dingtaifeng-yu-gongcha-naxia-meiguo-chudai-xiaoguan-liaoqi-3bb14dd4-0b13-4173-9d5b-0e63bf98ae1d]] as a Chinese hot-pot chain trying to make the restaurant experience legible in the [[UnitedStates]]. The episode frames Haidilao's overseas value around service, social dining, hot-pot explanation, and cultural performance rather than only the domestic reputation for extreme service.

In the source, Haidilao is useful because hot pot has to teach a dining procedure. The category works when the meal becomes a social experience with enough guidance and atmosphere; it weakens if consumers read it only as unfamiliar equipment and sauces.
