# Nigel Farage

Nigel Farage is the [[ReformUK]] figure at the center of [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]]. The source says he triggered a [[Clacton]] by-election while under scrutiny over gifts and donations, including money linked to [[ChristopherHarborne]] and [[GeorgeCottrell]], and that he denies wrongdoing by arguing the gifts were personal rather than political.

The episode's political reading is that the contest becomes a case of [[PoliticalFarceAccountability]]. [[CountBinface]] makes the election funny and visible, but the source argues the farce comes from Farage creating a by-election with no major-party opponents while an investigation into his finances remains unresolved.
