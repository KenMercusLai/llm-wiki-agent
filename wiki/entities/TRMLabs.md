# TRM Labs

TRM Labs is the crypto-compliance and blockchain-intelligence company whose report anchors [[tech-20260212-0212-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260212-0212-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the episode, [[AriRedbord]] uses the report to separate absolute growth in illicit crypto activity from the faster growth of lawful adoption, then focuses on the places where enforcement pressure is most useful.

The page's wiki role is to connect crypto market structure to investigative and compliance work. TRM Labs appears as a source for claims about illicit transaction volume, sanctions-related crypto activity, scam losses, and the use of AI in scams.
