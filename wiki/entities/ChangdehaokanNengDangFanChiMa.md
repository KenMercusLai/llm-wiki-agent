# 《长得好看能当饭吃吗》

《长得好看能当饭吃吗》 is the book used by [[155-meimao-neng-dang-fan-chi-ma-xiang-zhuanqian-gai-zuo-dian-sha-jieju-shi-ying-bimian-shenme-xingwei-jingjixue-siwei-you-shenme-yong-920167210]] as a light entry point into economics for ordinary life. The episode draws from it to connect [[EverydayBehavioralEconomics]], pricing, consumption traps, [[MarginalIncomeScaling]], and [[BeautyEconomics]] rather than treating economics as only formulas or macro policy.

The book's source role is connective. It lets [[QinZong|秦总]] move from [[LossAversion]] and [[MentalAccounting]] into everyday purchases, then from marginal returns into income strategy, and finally into [[AttractivenessLaborMarketPremium|appearance-related labor-market premiums]].
