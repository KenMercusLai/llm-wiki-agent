# Yuan Shanla / 袁善辣

Yuan Shanla is the Wuhan official who appears in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] around both [[EastStarAirlines]]' approval and its later restructuring crisis. The episode says Yuan helped push the airline application and later coordinated parts of the [[AirChina]]/[[ChinaNationalAviationHolding]] negotiation when East Star was in financial trouble.

The source treats Yuan carefully because much of his role is contested. [[LanShili]]'s family publicly accused him in 2011, Yuan denied the accusations, and a provincial discipline inspection investigation at the time reportedly did not confirm Lan's specific claims. Yuan later fell in a separate anti-corruption process, but the episode stresses that this later outcome does not automatically prove the earlier accusations.

## Source Position
- Yuan is a key node in the source's [[LocalGovernmentEnterpriseRescue]] story.
