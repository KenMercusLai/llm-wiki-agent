# U.S. Department of Labor

The U.S. Department of Labor appears in [[tech-20260327-0327-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260327-0327-mp-tech-pod-128]] through a free AI literacy course meant to make workers "AI ready." The course is presented as a text-message program that can be completed in about a week, with short lessons on generative AI, prompting, and large language models.

The source uses the department to ground [[AIWorkerLiteracy]] as a public-policy response to worker anxiety. The course can make AI less mysterious, but [[MariaCurie|Maria Curi]] stresses that it cannot by itself prevent layoffs, job displacement, or companies' speculative use of AI productivity claims.
