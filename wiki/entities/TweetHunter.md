# Tweet Hunter

Tweet Hunter was a Twitter-focused SaaS product created by [[ThibautLouisLucas]] and his co-founder after a series of rapid product experiments. In [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], Lucas says he first built it for himself because Twitter was bringing users to his other products and he wanted a better way to study and write effective tweets.

## Key Claims
- Tweet Hunter reached about $1,000 MRR in roughly three weeks, making it one of Lucas's clearest early [[FastProductValidation]] wins.
- The product grew from about $3,000 to $20,000 in monthly revenue within a few weeks after a distribution partnership with [[JKMolina]].
- Lucas kept [[Tapio]] separate rather than turning Tweet Hunter into a generic social-media tool, reinforcing product focus by network and customer workflow.
- Tweet Hunter was later sold with [[Tapio]] to [[Lempire]].
