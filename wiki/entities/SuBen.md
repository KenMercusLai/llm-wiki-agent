# 苏本

苏本 is the editor remembered by [[XiaoMan]] in [[da-chang-xiao-min-women-bixu-kezhi-dui-xitong-yu-shangan-de-qidai-lps-6oqp9ljyfw6wpfkutcks6dgf]]. The episode says SuBen brought XiaoMan into the publishing world, encouraged the idea that edge positions can become a writing perspective, and had promised to help publish the [[DaChangXiaomin]] project before becoming ill and dying in autumn 2024.

In the source, SuBen is not only a publishing actor. Her death becomes one of the emotional reasons XiaoMan felt the book had to be completed, and her editorial belief becomes part of the episode's larger argument that [[NonfictionPublicness]] can give marginal or ordinary workplace experience public meaning.
