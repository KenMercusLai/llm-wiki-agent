# 《黑客帝国》 / The Matrix

[[TheMatrix|《黑客帝国》 / The Matrix]] appears in [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]] as a later science-fiction work that explicitly echoes [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》]] through rabbit-hole imagery and the movement from ordinary reality into a hidden rule-world.

The episode uses the comparison to widen Alice's afterlife from children's literature into cyberpunk and technologically mediated reality. In the wiki, The Matrix therefore connects [[OtherworldPortalNarrative]] to [[DreamLogicNarrative]] and [[RuleShiftingMathematics]]: another world is entered through a threshold, but its strangeness is backed by rules rather than pure hallucination.

## Key Claims
- The Matrix is used as a modern Alice afterlife rather than only as a standalone film example.
- Its rabbit-hole language shows how Alice became a reusable metaphor for reality transition.
- The comparison supports the episode's claim that strange worlds often hide precise rule systems.
