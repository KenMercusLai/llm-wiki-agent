# 殷姐

殷姐 is one of the three hosts in [[ritan-chuantai-jiemei-liaotianhui-chongqi-rensheng-yeshi-wo-zui-yaojiao-616116425]]. She is introduced as living in "Mexico's Dali," running a restaurant and guesthouse after an earlier accounting and auditing path.

Her role in the source is to push against the restart premise. She says she does not really want to restart life because past right and wrong choices have made her current self. When forced to choose, she would revisit university-graduation recruiting to understand why she did not receive more offers, not necessarily to pick a different life. She also brings the episode's strongest language of self-consistency, learning capacity, hospitality, and Buddhist-inflected non-attachment.
