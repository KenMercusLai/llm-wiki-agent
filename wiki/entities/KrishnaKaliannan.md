# Krishna Kaliannan

Krishna Kaliannan is the founder of [[CatalinaCrunch]], featured in [[catalina-crunch-krishna-kaliannan-from-homemade-keto-cocoa-puffs-to-breakfast-aisle-breakthrough-5aa86015-e7c2-448e-a84e-d1b34d2fcac2]]. The episode frames his founder path around [[DietaryConstraintProductInsight]]: type 1 diabetes and epilepsy made low-sugar, low-carb food personally important, while nostalgia for cereal turned the constraint into a product.

## Key Points
- Krishna was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes around age 17 and later diagnosed with epilepsy, both of which pushed him toward lower-carbohydrate eating and the ketogenic diet.
- After working at a hedge fund, he tried many software and internet products, including [[EscapeMyBubble]], before focusing on food.
- He formulated early cereal versions himself using protein powder, cocoa, monk fruit or stevia, baking powder, almond flour, coconut flour, and later other fibers.
- He moved through apartment baking, overnight commercial-kitchen work, co-manufacturing, and a self-run Indiana finishing and packaging setup.
- His first meaningful market signal came when a friend paid unprompted for homemade cereal.
- As Catalina Crunch scaled, he brought in [[DougBehrens]] as CEO and shifted more attention toward nutrition strategy.
