# 《康熙来了》 / Kangxi Coming

《康熙来了》 appears in [[129-ruhe-chengwei-zhenzheng-de-buwanmei-zhuyizhe-hunshen-shi-jiner-799220151]] as the variety-show setting through which [[QinZong|秦总]] discusses [[XiaoS|小S]]. The source treats the show as an affective and cultural memory: watching Xiao S helped the host imagine a woman who could be publicly funny, imperfect, relaxed, and still self-possessed.

In this wiki branch, the show matters because it gives [[PerfectionismAsAvoidance]] a gendered media counterexample. It is not presented as a full media-history topic; it functions as one source's example of public looseness against image control.
