# 孙悟空

孙悟空 is one of the main character-lineage cases in [[43-xiyouji-duo-ni-shi-shenme-yaojing-618154182]]. The episode resists explaining him through a single origin. Instead, it places him inside [[MythicSourceLayering]]: [[Hanuman|哈努曼]], [[Wuzhiqi|无支祁]], monkey-demon tales, Shi Pantuo, Hu/monkey wordplay, Daoist alchemical language, and earlier drama traditions all become possible contributors.

The source also treats changes to 孙悟空's family and body as literary choices. Earlier drama material gives him a larger kin network, while the novel's stone-monkey version makes him more independent and easier to center inside the pilgrimage narrative. The episode's "金公" discussion is a warning against flat literalism: a term can belong to Daoist vocabulary rather than ordinary zoology.
