# Afghanistan

Afghanistan enters the wiki through [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] discusses [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]]'s [[YiGeAfuhanNvrenDeLaixin|《一个阿富汗女人的来信》]]. The episode focuses on Afghan women's lives under war, regime change, patriarchal control, religious and social pressure, and family-level survival.

The source does not provide a comprehensive political history of Afghanistan. It uses the country as a lived setting where [[ProtectionAsControl]], [[WarGenderedCivilianHarm]], and [[AfghanWomenFirstPersonWriting]] become concrete through ordinary actions such as teaching, leaving home, riding in a bus, listening to music, wearing a burqa, going to court, and seeking refuge.
