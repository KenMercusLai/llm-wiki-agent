# Conservative Party (UK)

The Conservative Party (UK) appears in [[coming-in-andy-britains-prime-minister-in-waiting-6a3512974a2a3be0f419384b]] through the episode's by-election signal segment. The source says the Conservatives win Aberdeen South from the SNP, using that result as evidence that [[ReformUK]] may no longer dominate the right side of British politics uncontested.

In this source, the Conservative role is not the main Labour succession story but part of the pressure map around [[UnitedKingdom]] politics. [[AndyBurnham]]'s Makerfield win strengthens [[LabourPartyUK]], while Conservative recovery in another seat suggests Reform can be squeezed by a traditional right-wing party as well as by [[RestoreBritain]] on the harder right.

[[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] adds that the Conservatives are described as undergoing a modest revival under [[KemiBadenoch]], even though the major parties are absent from [[NigelFarage]]'s [[Clacton]] by-election. The source therefore separates national Conservative recovery from the artificial structure of the Farage-[[CountBinface]] contest.
