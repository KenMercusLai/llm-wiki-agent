# Rebecca Jackson

Rebecca Jackson appears in [[the-250-year-experiment-americas-birthday-6a477d9aecd123914320b4ad]] as the middle-position panelist in [[TheIntelligence]]'s America-at-250 discussion. She emphasizes representation problems: redistricting and weakened voting-rights protections can reduce electoral competition and leave many voters effectively unrepresented even when elections still occur.

Jackson also contributes the episode's [[HistoricalMemoryContest]] thread through reporting from Montgomery, Alabama. The source contrasts memorials and museums about slavery and racial terror with official efforts to present a cleaner national story, making historical memory part of the democratic-resilience question rather than a separate culture-war detail.
