# Emma Fiquade

Emma Fiquade is the [[IsraelAcabla]] founder who calls into [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. She describes the Toronto-made women's fashion label as drawing on her engineering background while trying to add personality to a minimal aesthetic.

Her question is why strong wholesale traction through Hudson's Bay and Nordstrom has not created enough DTC lift. [[GuyRaz]] frames the issue as wholesale acting more like distribution than marketing, while [[KennethCole]] presses her to clarify the brand's emotional meaning and consider whether retail can be a brand-building environment rather than only a sales channel.
