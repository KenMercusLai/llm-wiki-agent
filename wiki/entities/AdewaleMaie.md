# Adewale Maie

Adewale Maie appears in [[why-the-us-has-no-guaranteed-paid-vacation]] as the labor researcher whose first trip to Europe turned the vacation-policy question into a lived contrast. In [[Spain]], he noticed working-age adults eating, playing, and spending weekday hours outside, then learned they had ordinary jobs.

His role in the source is observational and comparative. The episode uses Maie's report on vacation laws across 21 rich countries to frame the [[UnitedStates]] as an outlier in [[PaidVacationAsLaborRight]] rather than simply a country with a different leisure mood.
