# Joseph Turow

Joseph Turow appears in [[tech-20260120-0120-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260120-0120-mp-tech-pod-128]] as an expert voice on retail personalization and [[SurveillancePricing]]. He argues that personalization is not only an internet-era phenomenon, since older merchants also kept informal customer knowledge, but digital commerce changes the scale, precision, and visibility of the practice.

Turow's contribution is to keep the legal and ethical claims separate. In the episode, he says surveillance pricing is legal and economically recognizable as price differentiation, while still problematic because many consumers do not know it is happening.
