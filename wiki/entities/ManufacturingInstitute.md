# Manufacturing Institute

The Manufacturing Institute appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through [[CarolynLee]]. In the source, it represents the workforce-development side of manufacturing policy: skills, training routes, and perceptions of modern factory work all shape whether openings get filled.

The organization connects strategic manufacturing debates to worker preparation. Domestic production ambitions in [[SupplyChainSovereignty]] and [[TechManufacturingReshoring]] become weaker if the [[UnitedStates]] lacks the training institutions needed to staff and maintain factories.
