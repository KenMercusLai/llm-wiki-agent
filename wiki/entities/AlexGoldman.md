# Alex Goldman

Alex Goldman appears in [[theres-no-business-like-dough-business]] as the host of [[Hyperfixed]]. In the episode, Hyperfixed is the route by which [[JedKronfeld]]'s question about three [[WetzelsPretzels|Wetzel's Pretzels]] locations reaches [[PlanetMoney]].

Goldman's role is source-pipeline context. The economic substance comes from Planet Money's reporting with [[JohnFisher]] and [[RickyAlam]], but the listener-question framing matters because it turns a small urban retail oddity into a case about [[ImpulseRetailClustering]].
