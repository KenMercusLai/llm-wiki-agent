# 《书楼吊堂》

《书楼吊堂》 is the [[KyogokuNatsuhiko|京极夏彦]] work invoked near the end of [[sp-06-chuantai-he-liwuya-chao-ge-highliao-zuo-dushu-bozhu-de-shoumu-xinde-819825708]]. The source uses its metaphor that books are like graves to turn the episode's otherwise practical talk about reading content into [[BookAsGraveMemoryWork]].

The episode's use of the book is narrow but conceptually important. It does not summarize the novel; it borrows the image to argue that books are not only information containers. They are places where today's reader can return to past writing, past people, and past moments of connection.
