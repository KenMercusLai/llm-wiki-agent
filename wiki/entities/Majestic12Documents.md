# Majestic 12 Documents / MJ-12文件

The Majestic 12 documents enter the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as a claimed Truman-era UFO and alien-body file that the episode treats as forged. The case shows how document-like form can create authority even when the provenance and details do not survive scrutiny.

The episode uses MJ-12 with [[DonationOfConstantine]] to show that forgery is not only a modern fringe-media problem. False documents can become powerful because they satisfy institutional, religious, political, or conspiratorial needs.
