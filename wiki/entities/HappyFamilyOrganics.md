# Happy Family Organics

Happy Family Organics is the baby-food company founded by [[ShaziVisram]] and referenced in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]] as the foundation for her Advice Line perspective. The episode uses Happy Family partly as a contrast with [[HealthyBaby]], Shazi's later company, and partly through a clip about early in-store demos that did not work as expected.

The Happy Family reference reinforces a recurring CPG lesson in the wiki: even a mission-led baby or family product has to survive customer education, trial, packaging, retail context, and repeated buying behavior before the mission becomes business value.
