# Jeffrey Hollender

Jeffrey Hollender is the [[SeventhGeneration]] co-founder and former CEO who appears with [[GuyRaz]] on [[advice-line-with-jeffrey-hollender-of-seventh-generation-f8ce2410-1176-47d9-be44-03f9fa945714]]. The episode presents him as a purpose-driven business advisor drawing lessons from Seventh Generation, his founder relationship with [[AlanNewman]], and his book [[BuiltForABetterWorld]].

## Key Points
- Jeffrey argues that responsible business needs a business case, not only moral language.
- He sees [[GreenHushing]] as a sign that social and environmental work may be continuing while public messaging becomes more cautious.
- His advice to early founders repeatedly ties mission to customer acquisition, product function, trial, and repeat purchase.
- He warns that fast growth can hurt employees and the organization when founders chase scale for its own sake.
