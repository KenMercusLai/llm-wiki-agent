# Wuhan Deepin Technology

Wuhan Deepin Technology is the formal company stage of the [[Deepin]] project in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says it was founded in 2011 with [[LiuWenhuan]], [[ZhangLei]], and [[WangYong]] as co-founders.

## Source Position
- The company marks the shift from community operating-system work to formal financing, certification, government procurement access, and institutional legitimacy.
- The episode says [[LiuWenhuan]] continued funding the company through a difficult early period.
- Later investment from security and internet-company actors is presented as part of Deepin's move toward more regularized operation.

## Connections
- [[Deepin]] — project and distribution around which the company formed.
- [[HiweedLinux]] and [[LengGanghua]] — predecessor project and early creator.
- [[TongxinSoftware]] — later company formed from Deepin-related and [[ChengmaiTechnology]] assets.
- [[OpenSourceCommunityCommercialization]] — transition pattern shown by the company stage.
