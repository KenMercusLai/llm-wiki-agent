# Tsathoggua / 萨托古雅

Tsathoggua / 萨托古雅 is a Smith mythos being discussed in [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]]. The [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode uses him in two comic-horror registers: in [[SevenGeases|《七咒赋》]], he refuses a human offering because he is already full; in the Eibon story thread, he is a supernatural ally whose metal plate lets [[Eibon|伊波恩]] escape toward Saturn.

The figure helps the episode show how [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]] differs from a narrower monster-horror expectation. Tsathoggua is frightening because he belongs to an older, nonhuman order, but he is also funny because his responses can be practical, lazy, appetite-based, or socially inconvenient.

## Key Claims
- Tsathoggua makes [[AntiAnthropocentricSatire]] concrete: humans can be too insignificant even to serve as useful sacrifice.
- His presence links Smith's Hyperborean material to the wider [[CthulhuMythos|Cthulhu Mythos / 克苏鲁神话]].
- The episode uses him to demonstrate [[HorrorHumorWeirdFiction]] rather than pure dread.
- He also supports the episode's claim that Smith's mythos includes friendship, refuge, and mistaken prophecy as well as destruction.
