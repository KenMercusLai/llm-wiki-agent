# St. Louis Cardinals

The St. Louis Cardinals appear in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]]'s team before the 1969 trade to the [[PhiladelphiaPhillies|Philadelphia Phillies]]. Under the [[BaseballReserveClause|reserve clause]], the Cardinals effectively controlled Flood's MLB labor market even after his existing contract period, making the team the source's concrete example of [[LaborMonopsony|labor monopsony]].
