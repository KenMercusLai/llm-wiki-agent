# 蘑菇街 / Mogujie

Mogujie is the women's fashion discovery and social-commerce case in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode says Mogujie once sent substantial traffic to [[Taobao]], then had to shift toward its own ecommerce platform after Alibaba blocked guide links, later relying heavily on livestream GMV and platform operation services.
