# 《消逝世界漫游指南》 / Last Chance to See

《消逝世界漫游指南》 / Last Chance to See is the [[DouglasAdams|Douglas Adams]] and [[MarkCarwardine|Mark Carwardine]] endangered-species travel book discussed in [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. The episode says it grew out of a [[BBC]] project from the 1980s and follows visits to species at the edge of disappearance rather than presenting conservation as abstract instruction.

The book's importance in the episode is tonal as much as factual. Its journeys through [[Madagascar]], [[KomodoIsland]], the former Zaire, [[YangtzeRiver|the Yangtze]], and [[NewZealand]] make extinction funny, embarrassing, bureaucratic, beautiful, and morally hard at the same time. That makes it a central case for [[HumorousConservationWriting]] and [[LastChanceEcologicalWitnessing]].
