# Yu Yi

Yu Yi is an AI-first practitioner and [[WhatsNextKejiZaozhidao]] S10E18 guest in [[1-ren-gongsi-kang-5-ge-ren-de-huo-hai-yao-guan-50-ge-agents-s10e18-e3a21dde-0bba-4ec2-bf12-5043500ae5c6]]. The source describes him as someone who has spent about 5,000 hours treating himself as an AI-first experiment, with most daily work routed through AI while he preserves final judgment over high-quality outcomes.

Yu Yi's distinctive contribution is the partner-like agent frame. He wants agents to accumulate experience, push back on his views, and work toward the user or customer rather than merely obeying his immediate instruction. At the same time, he keeps explicit red lines around deletion, protocol changes, spending, and socially damaging actions, making his practice a concrete example of [[HumanAgentCollaboration]] plus [[AgentPermissionBoundaries]].
