# Britt Young

Britt Young is the writer and [[UCBerkeley]] lecturer interviewed in [[3d-printing-was-supposed-to-disrupt-prosthetic-costs-it-hasnt]]. The source says she has used a prosthetic arm throughout her life, which makes her discussion of [[ThreeDPrintedProsthetics]] both reported and personally grounded.

Young's core contribution is to separate prosthetic access from a narrow manufacturing-cost story. She argues that low-cost printed devices can be uncomfortable or fragile, professional additive manufacturing remains expensive, and access often turns on [[ProstheticInsuranceCoverage]], [[AssistiveDeviceClassification]], and skilled fitting.
