# 《是，首相》 / Yes, Prime Minister

《是，首相》 / Yes, Prime Minister is the sequel to [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]] discussed in [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]]. The episode names it as part of the same satire system, extending the conflict from a departmental minister's office into the prime-ministerial level.

In the source, the sequel matters less as a separate plot summary than as evidence that the same administrative logic survives promotion. [[JamesHacker|Hacker]] can move upward, but [[HumphreyAppleby|Humphrey]], [[ArnoldRobinson|Arnold]], and the permanent official layer still define what information reaches elected power and how risky action is contained.
