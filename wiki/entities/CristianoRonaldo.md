# Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo appears in [[youve-come-a-long-way-bibi-israels-crucial-election-6a59f9ae461a6a41901ae410]] as one of the era-defining footballers for whom the tournament may be remembered as a last [[FIFAWorldCup]]. The source pairs him with [[LionelMessi]] as part of the generational memory of the expanded tournament.

The episode does not use Ronaldo for tactical analysis. His function is historical framing: the World Cup may be remembered not only for the winner or [[SportsPoliticalInterference]], but also for the closing chapter of the Messi-Ronaldo tournament era.
