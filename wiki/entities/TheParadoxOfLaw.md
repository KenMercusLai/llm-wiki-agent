# 《法律的悖论》 / The Paradox of Law

[[TheParadoxOfLaw|《法律的悖论》]] is [[LuoXiang|罗翔]]'s book as discussed by [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]]. The episode treats the book as an accessible route into criminal-law philosophy rather than as a technical code manual.

The source frames the book's "paradox" as a discipline for legal and public judgment. Cases about battered women, criminal records, punishment purposes, [[VeilOfIgnorance]], and self-harm force readers to hold legal order, moral sympathy, freedom, proportionality, and state-power limits together without collapsing them into one slogan.
