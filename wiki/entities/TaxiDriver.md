# Taxi Driver

*Taxi Driver* appears in [[snap-judgement-japan-pms-electoral-landslide-6989c0fc61be18927058bb7d]] through a 50th-anniversary essay. The episode treats the film less as a definitive portrait of New York than as a portrait of [[TravisBickle]]'s unstable perception of the city, with the 1970s urban setting functioning as a mirror for his alienation and violent fantasy.

The source's main contribution is cultural interpretation. It argues that [[TaxiDriver]] has often been misread: Travis's climactic violence and famous mirror line have been turned into a masculine iconography that misses the film's horror. That makes the film a case for [[AntiheroMisreading]] and [[AlienatedMaleViolence]].
