# Orville Nelson

Orville Nelson is the barber in [[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]] whose early hair-straightener formula became the starting point for [[UltraWave]]. The episode says his mixture included ingredients such as egg, potato, and sodium hydroxide, but it separated and did not work reliably enough for scalable retail.

The source uses Nelson to show that [[JohnsonProducts]] emerged from practical barbershop knowledge as well as formal business execution. [[GeorgeEJohnson]]'s contribution was to observe Nelson's product problem, experiment with emulsification and texture, and turn a local practice into a stable commercial product.
