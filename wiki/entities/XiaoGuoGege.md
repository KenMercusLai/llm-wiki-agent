# 小果哥哥 / XiaoGuoGege

XiaoGuoGege is the [[Luanfanshu]] episode 267 guest whose [[AIShortDrama]] case centers on [[AnhuiXiaoMujiang]]. The source says he had earlier experience building a large [[WeChat]] public account, which the host uses to frame his advantage as content intuition and platform sense rather than only model literacy.

In the episode, XiaoGuoGege describes starting from near-zero AI short-drama production knowledge, using [[Doubao]] to adapt a [[FanqieNovel]] story, relying on video-generation tools such as [[Seedance]], and editing imperfect outputs into a finished drama. His breakout claim is that [[AnhuiXiaoMujiang]] cost under 3,000 RMB, reached 350 million plays on one platform, and had paid him roughly 500,000 RMB by the time of recording.

## Key Points
- His workflow treats AI as scriptwriter, prompt assistant, video generator, and production accelerator, but he still emphasizes editing ability and manual judgment.
- The episode presents him as a practical creator rather than a platform insider; he did not initially know that distributor and paid-traffic amplification were driving the breakout.
- His follow-up attempts show the fragility of the route: later works reportedly failed [[Douyin]] review or were removed, even when [[Hongguo]] accepted some of them.
- His choice to test different genres after the breakout contrasts with a stricter [[ShortDramaEconomics]] tactic of repeatedly copying the proven topic.
