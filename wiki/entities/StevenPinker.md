# Steven Pinker

Steven Pinker appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] as a modern contrast to [[JohannJakobBachofen]]. The episode summarizes Pinker through [[TheBetterAngelsOfOurNature|《人性中的善良天使》]] and the claim that greater female voice or "feminization" of social power can help reduce violence.

The source uses Pinker methodologically as much as substantively. Bachofen explains mother-right through myth, religion, and spiritual evolution; Pinker is presented as relying more on psychology, biology, and statistical violence trends. This contrast helps define [[FemaleCivilizingPower]] without treating it as a simple matriarchal proof.
