# 孟同学（《放学后的小巷》）

孟同学 is one of the student friends in the first [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] story discussed in [[168-fangxuehou-de-xiaoxiang-zhi-qingchun-zhi-nanian-de-youyi-richang-tuili-qiangshi-huigui-953669535]]. He contributes the ten yuan for tofu pudding and egg pancakes, and his modified bicycle becomes one of the story's key clue objects.

The mismatch between his seven-speed shifter and six-speed wheel set lets [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]] guide him toward a partial theft explanation. That guidance matters because it protects [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]] from later direct questioning, turning 孟同学 into both clue carrier and social risk that 阿里 has to manage gently.

## Key Claims
- 孟同学's bicycle part carries the mechanical clue that opens the mystery.
- His willingness to accept a plausible explanation helps 阿里 avoid exposing 严良.
- His role shows how [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]] can depend on ordinary group interaction, not only solitary genius.
