# Hookie

Hookie is one of the three hosts in [[ritan-chuantai-jiemei-liaotianhui-chongqi-rensheng-yeshi-wo-zui-yaojiao-616116425]]. In the episode, she describes having studied life sciences before moving into technology, internet investing, and content consulting, making her a concrete case for later-life professional redirection.

Her strongest contribution to the source is a blend of embarrassment, loss, and confidence-building. She uses the [[BrushUpLife|《重启人生》]] premise to discuss a school-uniform embarrassment, the decision not to continue life-science lab work, an old wish to work around professional sports, the loss of her dog Lily, and the confidence she built after entering a strong girls' school and learning to find strengths beyond grades.
