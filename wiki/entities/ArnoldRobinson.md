# Arnold Robinson / 阿诺德

Arnold Robinson is the senior civil-service figure in [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]] and [[YesPrimeMinister|《是，首相》 / Yes, Prime Minister]] discussed in [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]]. The episode describes him as the older and more powerful operator behind [[HumphreyAppleby|Humphrey Appleby]], connected to the prime-ministerial layer of the system.

Arnold matters in the source because he makes the bureaucracy look like a whole professional order rather than one antagonist. In the open-government plot, his conversation with Humphrey frames transparency as dangerous and helps convert a reform slogan into a manageable non-event.
