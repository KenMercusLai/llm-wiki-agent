# Stanley Milgram / 斯坦利·米尔格拉姆

Stanley Milgram / 斯坦利·米尔格拉姆 appears in [[123-renxing-shiyan-dangxin-ni-ziji-779479569]] as a classic social-psychology experimenter associated with obedience and shock-experiment debates. The episode uses him mainly as background: his work, [[SolomonAsch|Asch]]'s conformity work, and [[PhilipZimbardo|Zimbardo]]'s prison experiment are familiar enough that the hosts choose less familiar studies for the main discussion.
