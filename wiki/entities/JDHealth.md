# 京东健康

JD Health is the healthcare arm of JD's medicine and health business in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode traces JD's online medicine path from the 2014 JD Medicine City through independent health-business operation, financing, and later emphasis on fast delivery, chronic prescriptions, medical-insurance payment, and home-care services.

The source treats JD Health as a commercially durable [[PharmaceuticalEcommerce]] case because medicine retail, prescription refills, logistics, and chronic-disease supplies are easier to standardize than diagnosis. Its later medical-model and workflow efforts are linked to [[MedicalAIWorkflowIntegration]], but the episode remains cautious about standalone AI doctor products.
