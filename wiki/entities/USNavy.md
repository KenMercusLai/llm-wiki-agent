# U.S. Navy

The U.S. Navy appears in [[tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the customer for [[GeckoRobotics]]' $71 million contract. The episode says the Navy wants 80% of ships and aircraft ready on short notice by 2027, while cited reporting says fewer than half of ships completed repairs on time last year.

The wiki relevance is readiness and industrial capacity. The Navy case connects ship inspection, repair delays, robotics, AI modeling, and shipbuilding capacity to [[DefenseRoboticsMaintenance]], [[DefenseTechStartupProcurement]], and the broader [[UnitedStates]]-[[China]] industrial competition frame.
