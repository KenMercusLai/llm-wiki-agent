# Frankenstein Castle / 弗兰肯斯坦城堡

Frankenstein Castle appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as a Rhine-region place Mary and Percy Shelley passed during their 1814 travels. The episode connects the castle to local stories about [[JohannConradDippel|Johann Conrad Dippel]], alchemy, body experiment legends, and the later question of how the name Frankenstein entered literary imagination.

The source keeps the castle as one plausible ingredient rather than a total explanation. In the wiki, it therefore belongs beside [[RomanticScienceCulture]], [[AlchemyChemistryContinuity]], and [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] as part of the novel's historical atmosphere.
