# 《周秦行纪》

《周秦行纪》 is the disputed erotic tale discussed in [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]]. The episode summarizes it as a story of [[NiuSengru|牛僧儒]] entering a grand house, meeting historical consorts, and sleeping with Wang Zhaojun, then immediately stresses that many scholars doubt the attribution.

Its wiki role is interpretive rather than bibliographic. The source uses the text as a cautionary case for [[LateTangPoliticalPoetics]] and [[InterpretationAndOverinterpretation]]: style, attribution, factional motive, and political attack all matter before a story can be treated as evidence about an author's character.
