# Clearspace

Clearspace is the screen-time reduction app discussed in [[tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the episode, [[MariaHollenhorst]] describes a user paying for Clearspace to interrupt habitual app use with pauses, breathing prompts, movement checks, and other friction before opening selected apps.

The app matters to the wiki because it turns [[ScreenTimeFriction]] into a consumer product. Rather than removing a phone or banning an app, Clearspace changes the moment before use: a person has to notice the impulse, wait, breathe, or move before continuing.
