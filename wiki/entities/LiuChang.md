# 刘畅 / Liu Chang

Liu Chang is a [[TalkSanlian|Talk三联]] speaker in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]], where he explains [[Homer]], [[TheIliad|《伊利亚特》]], and [[TheOdyssey|《奥德赛》]] through oral performance, fixed epithets, honor conflict, and the return home.

His account extends the wiki's Homeric branch by linking [[OralFormulaicEpic]] to performance practice and by making [[AncientGreekHonorCommunity]] central to why [[Achilles|阿基里斯]]' quarrel with [[Agamemnon|阿加门农]] and [[Odysseus|奥德修斯]]' homecoming are more than adventure plots.
