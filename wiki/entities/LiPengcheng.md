# Li Pengcheng

Li Pengcheng is a Beijing-based builder focused on deaf speech rehabilitation and one of the contestants interviewed in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. The podcast used a sign-language interpreter during his segment.

His team built [[KenanVoiceChanger]], an AI voice-repair project meant to make unclear speech easier for others to understand. Pengcheng says doctors once suggested he might need ten years or even a lifetime to learn speech, but he did not want life to be consumed by rehabilitation; the product is meant to help communication during the learning process.
