# Xiao'e

Xiao'e is a Beijing Normal University Future Design School graduate student and founder of "Jili Guagua" and "Jili Gugu" in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. Her hackathon project won a hardware-track award and explored Doraemon-like AI gadgets for helping people regain real-world focus.

The source describes a "memory toaster" that locks a phone like bread in a toaster and uses printed physical feedback, plus a camera that records and analyzes focus using multimodal AI. Her account also emphasizes the event's team-forming intensity and peer-help culture.
