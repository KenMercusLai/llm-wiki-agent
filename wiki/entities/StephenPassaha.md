# Stephen Passaha

Stephen Passaha appears in [[indicators-of-2025-and-what-to-watch-in-2026]] as the source-identified participant choosing electricity rates as a 2026 affordability indicator. The episode uses his selection to shift household-cost attention from groceries and rent toward power bills, where the source says electricity prices rose faster than general inflation.
