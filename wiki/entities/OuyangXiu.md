# 欧阳修 / Ouyang Xiu

Ouyang Xiu appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as the senior literary figure who recognizes [[SuShi|苏轼]] and [[SuZhe|苏辙]] during their examination rise. The episode uses his appreciation of Su Shi's writing to show how literary talent could become an entry point into [[NorthernSong|北宋]] official life.

The source also notes that Ouyang Xiu earlier appreciated [[WangAnshi|王安石]], which makes him a bridge rather than a simple faction marker. He helps place Su Shi and Wang Anshi inside the same literary-official world before their governing styles and political judgments diverge through the [[WangAnshiReforms|王安石变法]] and [[SongFactionalPolitics|宋代党争]].
