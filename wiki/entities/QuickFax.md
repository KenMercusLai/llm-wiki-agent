# QuickFax

QuickFax is [[RonHash]]'s later online faxing product discussed in [[50-cents-a-pool-the-pricing-model-behind-a-saas-exit]]. The source says the idea came from Ron needing to send a single fax and finding that existing services pushed subscriptions or account creation.

QuickFax is presented as a small, concrete workflow product: users can send a fax without an account for $1.50. In the episode, Ron links the product to SEO, Google ads, simple UX, and positive reviews from users who expected faxing to be painful.
