# Heated Rivalry

Heated Rivalry is the Canadian television series discussed in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]]. The episode describes it as a romance series about two professional male hockey players that became a surprise hit, with sold-out replica jerseys and international distribution making fandom visible as business evidence.

The series anchors several wiki themes at once. [[UnderservedRomanceAudience]] explains why queer joy, female desire, and romance readership were commercially important rather than niche; [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]] explains how the show was funded; [[ProducerOwnedIPUpside]] explains why ownership made merchandise and long-term value matter; and [[AttentiveStreamingStorytelling]] explains why the creators resisted designing for distracted viewing.
