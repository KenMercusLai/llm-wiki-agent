# Scott Brennan

Scott Brennan is the [[NYUCenterOnTechnologyPolicy]] researcher interviewed in [[the-little-known-regulatory-bodies-that-can-make-or-break-ai-data-centers]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, he explains why state [[PublicUtilityCommissions]] matter to AI policy as data centers place larger demands on electricity grids.

His role in the source is to translate utility regulation into AI-infrastructure terms. Brennan emphasizes that commissions can influence planning, rates, long contracts, infrastructure approval, and upfront payments, making them potential protectors against [[DataCenterCostShifting]].
