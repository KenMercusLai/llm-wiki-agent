# Spin Master

Spin Master is the toy and children's entertainment company co-founded by [[RonnenHarary]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The episode names [[PAWPatrol]], Air Hogs, Bakugan, and Rubik's Cube products as examples of the company's brand and product portfolio.

Ronnen frames Spin Master's current challenge as balancing acquisition with internal innovation. The company used earnings from PAW Patrol to acquire a larger evergreen toy business, but he says Spin Master had become too rigid and needed to lean back into ideation, AI, and internal product invention.
