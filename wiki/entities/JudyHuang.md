# Judy Huang

Judy Huang appears in [[chef-vs-robot]] as co-owner of [[TingWong]]. She presents [[PhuongHoangChiang]]'s credentials, explains why [[WokHei]] matters to diners, and says skilled wok work is hard to staff because the labor is intense and technique-dependent.

Huang's view makes the source more than a cost comparison. She understands why restaurants might use robots, but worries that automation can flatten the flavor and tradition that come from human wok training.
