# 中华凤头燕鸥 / Chinese Crested Tern

The Chinese crested tern is the endangered bird species discussed in [[ganguan-fangda-shijie-he-renning-liao-guanniao-ziran-yu-ziyou-e583dac2-bad8-4208-8d35-0c3de8594779]]. [[RenNing]] describes a Zhejiang island breeding project where researchers attract large crested terns with sound playback and decoys because Chinese crested terns tend to mix with them.

The species functions in the wiki as a concrete [[ConservationIntervention]] case. It shows that human action can sometimes support threatened species, but only after careful observation of habitat, migration path, colony behavior, disturbance, and monitoring evidence.
