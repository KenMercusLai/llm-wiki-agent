# Kleiner Perkins

Kleiner Perkins appears in [[socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8-socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8]] as the venture firm where [[MamoonHamid]] led [[Rippling]]'s Series A. The episode places the investment inside [[ParkerConrad]]'s post-[[Zenefits]] rebuilding period, when investor interest in Rippling was strong but still filtered through Conrad's public reputation.

[[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] adds Kleiner Perkins through [[Google]]'s 1999 financing. [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]] wanted [[JohnDoerr]] and Kleiner partly because of the firm's [[AOL]] relationship, while they wanted [[SequoiaCapital]] for its [[Yahoo]] relationship. [[RonConway]] frames the round as a difficult but strategic pairing of firms that did not usually invest together.
