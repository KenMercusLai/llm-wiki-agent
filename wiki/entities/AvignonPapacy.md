# 阿维尼翁教廷 / Avignon Papacy

阿维尼翁教廷 / Avignon Papacy is the medieval papal-political background explained in [[54-meigui-de-mingzi-xia-zhen-yu-jia-zheng-yu-xie-quanshi-yu-guodu-quanshi-638214699]]. The episode uses it to situate [[TheNameOfTheRose|《玫瑰的名字》 / The Name of the Rose]] beyond the murder plot: the monastery meeting is shaped by conflict among the papacy, the emperor, Franciscan poverty debates, French royal influence, and later church division.

This background matters because it makes the abbey deaths politically exploitable. [[BernardGui|贝尔纳 / Bernard Gui]] can turn Salvatore, Remigio, and the unnamed village girl into leverage because the delegation needs a story about heresy and disorder, not only a truthful solution to the murders.
