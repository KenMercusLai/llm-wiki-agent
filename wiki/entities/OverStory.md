# OverStory

OverStory is the [[ConvectiveCapital]] portfolio company [[BillClerico]] highlights in [[socialradarss2-billclerico-final]]. The episode describes it as using satellite imagery to help utilities monitor vegetation growth around power lines.

Its role in the source is practical rather than speculative. Utility power-line networks are too large for purely manual inspection to catch every high-risk vegetation area, and wildfire liability makes better prioritization economically meaningful.
