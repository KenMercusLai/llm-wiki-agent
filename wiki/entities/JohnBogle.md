# John Bogle

John Bogle is referenced in [[e44-li-xiaobo-duihua-mengyan-zheci-jiu-zheyang-ba-lpcrvfgnseaed-eambk9ofnvuq2]] through [[Vanguard]] and the question of whether a founder knows where "enough" is. [[MengYan]] treats Bogle less as a generic index-fund hero and more as an example of founder character, governance, and restraint in a financial industry where extracting more from customers is often technically possible.

The source connects Bogle to [[KnowingEnough]], [[PassiveInvesting]], and customer-aligned financial institutions. His role in this ingest is to anchor the episode's claim that investor-friendly products require both economic structure and a founder culture able to resist higher take rate.
