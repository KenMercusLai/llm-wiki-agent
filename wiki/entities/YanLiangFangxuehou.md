# 严良（《放学后的小巷》）

严良 is the high-performing student and friend in the first [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] story discussed by [[168-fangxuehou-de-xiaoxiang-zhi-qingchun-zhi-nanian-de-youyi-richang-tuili-qiangshi-huigui-953669535]]. The episode describes him as handsome, athletic, academically strong, and initially almost impossibly good, but the mystery gradually reveals vulnerability beneath that surface.

His prize mountain bike, his habit of not riding it, his request to borrow [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]]'s bike, his avoidance of eating with friends, and his no-cilantro tofu pudding become the clues through which 阿里 infers family financial pressure. 严良 is therefore the protected center of [[DignityProtectingDeduction|体面保护式推理]]: the solution matters because it preserves his self-respect.

## Key Claims
- 严良's apparent excellence hides economic pressure and family responsibility.
- His self-respect makes direct pity or public exposure damaging.
- The no-cilantro clue transforms a food preference into evidence of care for his family.
