# Artemis Accords

The Artemis Accords appear in [[tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128]] as U.S.-led, non-legally binding principles and guidelines for space exploration behavior. [[SadiaPekkanen]] says about 61 countries have agreed to the basic rules, while [[China]] and [[Russia]] have not signed.

In the episode, the Accords matter because lunar activity is moving from visits to sustained operations. Rules about safety zones, technology placement, bases, research stations, transparency, and resource use become part of [[LunarResourceGovernance]] rather than a purely symbolic diplomatic statement.
