# 钱静

钱静 is the guest in [[sp-02-wo-you-ziji-de-yuzhou-ruhe-zai-hundun-zhong-zuo-ge-qingxing-de-xingdongpai-748593778]]. The episode identifies her as a Beijing Normal University teacher, workplace-psychology researcher, and online writer/speaker who shares advice about work, study, and life decisions across Bilibili, Xiaoyuzhou, WeChat, and other platforms.

In the source, her role is not to provide pure emotional comfort. She answers listener problems through a practical psychology frame: examine fit before repeating civil-service or graduate exams, analyze workplace power instead of stopping at "PUA" labels, use rent and physical separation to start adulthood, and resist anxiety through concrete action.

Her central contribution is [[ZhongyongSelf]]. She argues that Chinese relational life needs a selfhood model different from simple Western boundary language: a person still has to recognize, own, and practice a self while moving through family, work, hierarchy, and cooperation.
