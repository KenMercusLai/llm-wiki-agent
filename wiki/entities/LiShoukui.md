# 李守奎

李守奎 is named in [[57-shuojie-hanzi-shiniaopi-weisha-shi-shi-zitou-642970319]] as the author of [[ShuojieHanzi150Jiang|《说解汉字150讲》]], one of the episode's main introductory references. The source uses his book to move popular interest in Chinese characters away from clever modern拆字 and toward documented form history.

His role in the wiki is therefore tied to [[ChineseCharacterEvidenceDiscipline]]. The episode treats his work as a reader-friendly route into why oracle-bone script, bronze inscriptions, seal script, clerical script, and later forms need to be compared before explaining a character.
