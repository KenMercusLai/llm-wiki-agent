# Barry Prizant

Barry Prizant is the autism expert and author behind [[UniquelyHuman|《这世界唯一的你》 / Uniquely Human]] as presented in [[46-zhe-shijie-weiyi-de-ni-qing-xiangxin-na-duo-manman-zhanfang-de-hua-623717720]]. The episode treats his core contribution as a change in stance: autistic behavior should be interpreted as coping, communication, or self-protection before it is classified as noncompliance.

His cases in the episode emphasize asking why, reducing threat, creating predictable and positive experiences, and letting parents' close observation matter. That positions him as the source's bridge between [[AutismAsHumanDifference]], [[BehaviorAsCommunication]], [[AutismSafeEnvironmentDesign]], and [[AutismFamilySupport]].
