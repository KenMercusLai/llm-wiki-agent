# Oliver

Oliver is the [[InvisibleTechnologies]] enterprise-business VP interviewed in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]]. Drawing on prior private-equity consulting experience at [[McKinsey]], he argues that enterprise AI adoption requires workflow redesign rather than tool overlay, and that private-equity firms want AI for signaling, portfolio value creation, and exposure to a high-growth deployment market.
