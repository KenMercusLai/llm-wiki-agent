# Daishen

Daishen is [[EddyLu]]'s co-founder in [[tsr-s3-eddylu-v2-tsr-s3-eddylu-v2]]. The source uses his first name throughout and presents him as Lu's long-running partner across early failed businesses, cream puff stores, [[GrubWithUs]], [[GrubTonight]], and [[GOAT]].

Daishen is central to the GOAT origin because his sneaker enthusiasm and bad eBay purchase exposed the problem. After he bought a fake retro Jordan 5 Grape, the founders saw that expensive sneaker resale needed platform-level [[AuthenticationLedMarketplaceTrust]] rather than leaving each buyer to judge scattered listings alone.

## Key Claims
- Daishen and [[EddyLu]] began working together in 2007 and quit their jobs on the same day.
- He helped operate the cream puff stores after the 2008 financial crisis removed the founders' ability to pay managers.
- His childhood interest in Air Jordans and later fake-sneaker purchase helped create the [[GOAT]] opportunity.
- He designed the first sneaker authenticator test, making authentication a hiring and operations problem as well as a marketplace promise.
- His mother was especially proud when he and Eddy became debt-free after the cream puff-store aftermath.
