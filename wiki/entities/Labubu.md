# Labubu

Labubu appears in [[139-paopao-mate-he-pinduoduo-zhide-touzi-me-feng-tou-quan-1-139-1]] as the blockbuster character/IP behind a large share of [[PopMart]] revenue. The episode says Labubu's family has reached roughly 40% of Pop Mart revenue, which makes the IP both evidence of brand power and a concentration-risk signal.

The source uses Labubu to show why [[ConsumerBrandMoat]] is not enough by itself. A breakout IP can create pricing power, demand heat, and cultural visibility, but investors still need to ask whether the hit can be extended, whether the next IP can be created, and whether growth expectations already price in too much success.
