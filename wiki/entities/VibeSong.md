# Vibe Song

Vibe Song is [[KunNi]]'s popularity-award-winning project from the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. It is an online competition platform where anyone can host or join a [[VibeCoding]] contest by setting a topic, number of participants, duration, and prize.

The product makes hackathon work more public and streamable: audiences can watch the coding process, enter the venue, comment, test products, suggest changes, and vote. It connects [[AIHackathons]] with [[AIInteractiveContentPlatforms]] because the act of building becomes a participatory media format, not only a private development process.
