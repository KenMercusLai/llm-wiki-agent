# DraftKings

DraftKings appears in [[tech-20260324-0324-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260324-0324-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the sportsbook where the bettors tied to the [[JontayPorter]] case became the biggest money winners for [[NationalBasketballAssociation|NBA]] bets on the day of the first suspicious game. The source uses DraftKings as part of the licensed sports-betting system that generates trackable wager data.

The episode does not treat DraftKings as the central actor; it treats the sportsbook data environment as the relevant infrastructure. That makes DraftKings part of the [[SportsbookIntegrityMonitoring]] comparison used to evaluate [[PredictionMarketIntegrityOversight]].
