# Center for a New American Security

The Center for a New American Security is the defense and national-security think tank affiliation for [[StaceyPettijohn]] in [[tech-20260319-0319-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260319-0319-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In this wiki, it enters through a [[MarketplaceTech]] episode about how low-cost drones change military strategy, defense economics, and supply-chain control.

The episode uses Pettijohn's CNAS expertise to connect [[Shahed136]]-style drones, [[LucasDrone]], [[CommercialOffTheShelfWeaponization]], [[DroneDefenseEconomics]], and [[CounterDroneLayeredDefense]]. CNAS is therefore part of the wiki's defense-analysis branch rather than a general profile subject.
