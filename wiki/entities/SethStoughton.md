# Seth Stoughton

Seth Stoughton appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as a law professor and former police officer who argues that controversial federal-agent conduct should not be understood only as a training failure. The source uses his view to build [[LawEnforcementCultureAccountability]] around supervisor orders, peer expectations, and organizational signals.

Stoughton also frames accountability as weak if agents perceive legal risk or public discipline as performative. In the episode, he expects lawsuits around immigration-enforcement conduct but does not think costly settlements alone will necessarily change the administration's tactics.
