# 《开膛手杰克结案报告》

《开膛手杰克结案报告》 is the book discussed in [[50-kaitangshou-jieke-jiean-baogao-nvfayi-de-zhengyi-zhizuo-630666604]]. The episode presents it as [[PatriciaCornwell]]'s attempt to close the [[JackTheRipper]] case by identifying [[WalterSickert]] as the murderer.

The book's value in the episode is double-edged. It supplies a dense archive of suspect-focused clues: paintings, correspondence, paper, language habits, possible body trauma, psychology, DNA tests, and archival gaps. But the hosts keep returning to the same evidentiary problem: a compelling pattern is not the same as decisive proof, especially when letters may be hoaxes and physical samples are old, contaminated, or missing.
