# Talk三联

Talk三联 is the podcast/show context for [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]], where [[GaoYiding|高一丁]] and [[ZhangCongzhi|张从志]] explain chips, semiconductor manufacturing, and AI compute for a broad audience. In that source, the show acts as a public-technology explainer: it moves from parking-lot chips and consumer devices to GPUs, fabs, [[ElectronicDesignAutomation|EDA]], lithography, and domestic AI-chip strategy.

[[ep268-mao-dong-x-tang-tang-da-huang-ni-daodi-za-le-shangcuan-xiatiao-de-huangjin-he-naxie-juebu-xiache-de-ren-lgujs-q24fzqcuutibty8k0bowbv]] adds the finance-and-household behavior branch. With [[WuShubing|吴淑冰]], [[MaoDong|毛冬]], [[TangTao|唐涛/唐唐]], [[MoChen|莫晨]], and [[AntFortune|蚂蚁财富]], the show translates gold from a price chart into [[HouseholdGoldSavings]], [[OnlineGoldAccumulation]], allocation discipline, gifts, and long-term practice.

[[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]] adds the summer-reading and classics branch. With [[GaoYiding|高一丁]], [[XiaoChuzhou|萧楚舟]], [[SunRuoxi|孙若曦]], and [[LiuChang|刘畅]], the show moves from [[TheIliad|《伊利亚特》]], [[TheOdyssey|《奥德赛》]], [[TheEpicOfGilgamesh|《吉尔伽美什史诗》]], and [[PoeticEdda|《诗体埃达》]] toward [[EpicModernAfterlife]], [[OralEpicRepetition]], [[EpicHeroHumanLimits]], and [[NorseMythology]].

The episode positions industrial reporting as a translation layer. Instead of treating chips only as investment symbols or geopolitical slogans, it asks what has to happen across [[SemiconductorSupplyChain|design, manufacturing, packaging, testing]], software ecosystems, and user-facing token prices before [[ComputeFreedom|算力自由]] becomes practical.
