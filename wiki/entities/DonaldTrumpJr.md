# Donald Trump Jr.

Donald Trump Jr. appears in [[how-much-money-president-trump-and-his-family-have-made]] as one of [[DonaldTrump]]'s sons involved in the family business and investment activity during Trump's second term. The source quotes him defending the family's resumed business posture and discusses his estimated gain from a partnership at [[1789Capital]].

[[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] adds him as an adviser to [[Kalshi]] after the platform's election-market legal fight.
