# Sean Parker

Sean Parker appears in [[tsr-ronconway-part4-v2-tsr-ronconway-part4-v2]] as [[SeanFanning]]'s [[Napster]] counterpart: more outgoing, more involved in external action, and central to the company's financing and survival drama. [[RonConway]] says Parker and Fanning had to solve the John Fanning board problem over a single weekend so [[HummerWinblad]]'s financing could close.

After Napster, Parker started [[Plaxo]], later introduced Conway to [[MarkZuckerberg]] and Facebook, pursued cancer-immunotherapy philanthropy, and remained emotionally tied to Fanning. Conway's wedding and Airtime stories use Parker as evidence that founder relationships can remain active startup infrastructure long after the original company shuts down.
