# Mico World

Mico World, referred to as 米可世界 in [[ep101-duihua-simon-ai-chuangyezhe-de-diyi-xiang-jibengong-shi-ba-zhang-suan-mingbai-lhrrhfslnd1z9cuu2vkuxbb5pvjx]], is an overseas social and pan-entertainment company focused on markets including the Middle East and Southeast Asia. The source says the company has more than a thousand employees and built earlier game-social products before forming [[MicoAILab]].

The episode presents Mico World as an operator with concrete experience in localized social acquisition, user segmentation, and payment design. Its Middle East case is used to show that social products may need to separate atmosphere creation, high-value-user acquisition, local cultural boundaries, and in-product monetization rather than treating "social demand" as one uniform signal.

## Key Points
- Mico World built a voice/game social product that Simon describes as one of the hotter Middle East game-social products around 2019.
- The company used product mechanisms and paid acquisition more than heavy manual operations in that early case.
- It segmented countries by social atmosphere and payment potential, rather than only by total user volume.
- Its AI direction keeps continuity with games, social, and pan-entertainment instead of leaving those company strengths behind.
