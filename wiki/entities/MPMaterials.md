# MP Materials

MP Materials is the company that owns the [[MountainPassRareEarthMine]] in the later part of [[battlefield-rare-earths-how-the-us-lost-to-china]]. The episode says Mountain Pass was sold to MP Materials after [[Molycorp]]'s failed comeback, and that a Chinese state company bought a stake in MP Materials.

The company's wiki role is to show how entangled rare-earth supply chains can remain even after a U.S. mine restarts. The source says MP Materials sent rare-earth ore to [[China]] for refining until 2025, meaning domestic mining still depended on foreign processing.

In 2025, the [[USDepartmentOfDefense|Department of Defense]] took a 15 percent stake in MP Materials, and the company was looking to refine everything in the [[UnitedStates]]. That makes MP Materials a central case for [[StateBackedRareEarthRebuilding]] and for the difference between owning a mine and having a complete rare-earth supply chain.
