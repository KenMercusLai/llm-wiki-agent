# 卓越网 / Amazon China

Joyo is the book and media ecommerce company in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode says it was incubated by the Kingsoft/Lei Jun circle, used hit-item logic in books and audiovisual products, and was acquired by [[Amazon]], whose China ecommerce business later struggled with localization and eventually retreated from domestic ecommerce.
