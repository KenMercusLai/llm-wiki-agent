# PaloAltoDelivery

PaloAltoDelivery was the rough predecessor to [[DoorDash]] in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]]. [[TonyXu]] says the team launched it on January 12, 2013 with a landing page, eight PDF menus, and a Google Voice number that rang the founders' phones.

The project is the episode's clearest [[JankyMVP]] case. It did not support online transactions, driver assignment software, or a full marketplace experience. Customers called, founders placed the takeout order, picked up food in a car, delivered it, and collected payment with a Square reader. That was enough to test whether customers cared if restaurants that had never offered delivery could suddenly deliver.
