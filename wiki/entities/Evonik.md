# Evonik

Evonik is a chemical and materials company mentioned in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as one of the European suppliers behind modern running-shoe midsole materials. The source treats Evonik as part of the upstream base for [[SupercriticalFoamMidsole]] competition.

## Key Claims
- Material suppliers can influence the pace and ceiling of shoe-brand innovation.
- Because suppliers are not always exclusive to one brand, long-term technical barriers can be harder for consumer brands to preserve.
- The running-shoe market is therefore shaped by both consumer marketing and industrial materials capability.
