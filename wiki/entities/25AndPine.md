# 25 & Pine

25 & Pine is the family furniture company discussed in [[advice-line-with-jeffrey-hollender-of-seventh-generation-f8ce2410-1176-47d9-be44-03f9fa945714]]. [[ChristinaMolinaro]] explains that the company makes shared furniture for parents and children, including pieces that can function as child seats, adult stools, benches, or tables.

## Key Points
- The company started with hand-built furniture in an apartment garage, moved to a basement, and then moved into commercial space after a TikTok video went viral.
- The source says sales over roughly five years reached about $600,000.
- Its core challenge is reaching parents who feel the problem before they know the product exists.
- The advice centers on continuing to adapt social media, adding email storytelling, showing the problem with humor, testing paid amplification, and seeding products with smaller creators.
