# Legion

Legion appears in [[how-to-make-a-book-into-a-bestseller]] as the [[WilliamPeterBlatty]] sequel whose absence from the [[NewYorkTimesBestsellerList|New York Times bestseller list]] led to litigation. The source uses the case to show that bestseller ranking can affect rights value and future promotion even when the list is not a transparent raw-sales chart.

The book's wiki role is narrow: it anchors the legal boundary around [[BestsellerListOpacity]] and the Times's protected editorial judgment.
