# National Popular Vote Interstate Compact

The National Popular Vote Interstate Compact appears in [[sp-05-liuqing-linyao-qiangsha-yibao-jutou-an-beihou-de-sikao-xia-fenlie-de-meiguo-hui-you-xianzheng-weiji-ma-790241978]] as a workaround for the Electoral College. [[LinYao|林垚]] describes the idea as a state-level agreement under which participating states would allocate their electoral votes to the national popular-vote winner once enough states joined.

The source uses the compact to distinguish partial reform from full constitutional repair. It may bypass one electoral problem without a formal amendment, but it does not solve [[PresidentialSystem|presidential-system]] risk, Senate malapportionment, or the broader [[USConstitutionalReformConstraint]].
