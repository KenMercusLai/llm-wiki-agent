# David Autor

David Autor is the [[MIT]] economist interviewed by [[GregRosalsky]] in [[why-economists-got-free-trade-with-china-so-wrong]]. The supplied markdown renders the name as "David Otter" in some summary lines, but the episode listing and the research context identify the economist as Autor.

His contribution to this source is the labor-market correction to simple free-trade optimism. Autor explains that the [[ChinaShock]] made [[FreeTradeDistributionalCost]] visible because import competition hit specific local labor markets, not an abstract national worker, and because affected workers often did not move or retrain as quickly as theory implied.
