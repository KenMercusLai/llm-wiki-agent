# D.A. Davidson

D.A. Davidson is the financial-services firm affiliation for [[GilLuria]] in [[tech-20260312-0312-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260312-0312-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The [[MarketplaceTech]] episode identifies Luria as head of technology research there while using his analysis to test whether [[Bitcoin]] functions as a safe haven during geopolitical turmoil.

In the wiki, D.A. Davidson anchors the episode's market-analysis perspective rather than a product or policy role. Its contribution is the analyst framing that Bitcoin may fall short as immediate [[DigitalGold]] while still retaining crisis relevance through liquidity, capped supply, and [[CrossBorderCryptoCapitalFlight]].
