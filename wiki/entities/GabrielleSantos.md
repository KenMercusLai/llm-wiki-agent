# Gabrielle Santos

Gabrielle Santos is the material scientist in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] who shows the hosts [[Tutiplast]]'s research lab in [[Manaus]]. The episode says she has a Ph.D. and is the only person with that qualification working in the factory.

Her team is trying to turn nutshells, including Brazil nut shells, into biodegradable plastics. The prototype is not finished, but the episode uses her work as a concrete example of [[LocalizedInnovationAdvantage]] and [[BiodegradableAmazonPlastics]]: innovation that might be grounded in Brazil's own materials, workforce, and environmental context.
