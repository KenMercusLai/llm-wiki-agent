# Ken Ono

Ken Ono is the mathematician discussed in [[137-dui-hong-letong-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-ai-for-math-ba-shuxue-biancheng-lean-shuxue-tianshu-zhong-de-zhengming-zhijue-bei-chuangzao-yu-bei-faxian-de-lha-faiwxtget0qmbcosts3cb5vb]] as a senior hire for [[Axiom]]. The source presents him as a theory builder who connects mathematical fields, finds good problems, and brings human research taste into the company's [[AIForMath]] project.

## Source Position
- Hong says Ono considered joining larger AI labs before choosing Axiom.
- The episode uses Ono to explain why human mathematicians still matter when [[AxiomProver]] gets stronger: they can propose good questions, connect domains, and build theory rather than merely fill proof steps.
- His role helps position Axiom as a math-centered deep-tech company rather than only an AGI or model-scaling effort.
