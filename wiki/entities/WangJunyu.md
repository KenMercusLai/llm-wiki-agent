# 王俊玉

王俊玉 is the entrepreneur and product-builder voice in [[vol-165-zuoke-shengdongjixi-longxia-he-vibe-coding-zhengruhe-gaibian-womende-siwei-laizi-xiaobai-chuangyezhe-he-gongchengshi-butong-shijiao-de-taolun-1-6642-1]]. The episode identifies him as a serial entrepreneur and co-founder of 万斗甲, and uses his perspective to connect [[OpenClaw]], [[VibeCoding]], product teams, and organizational process redesign.

## Source Position
- He names proactivity, long memory, and [[AISkills]] as the most important lessons from OpenClaw-like agents.
- He argues that skills make an agent more like a trainable new employee because the agent can remember how to do work, not only who the user is.
- He treats startup and company owners as especially able to use "小龙虾" because they already imagine the company as a machine with automatable processes.
- His [[DigitalEmployees]] frame is managerial: using AI well means setting goals, defining process, managing progress, and knowing what should escalate to people.
- He expects smaller teams with higher individual leverage, while warning that people should focus on their own strengths rather than only defensively listing what AI cannot do.
