# Peter Mandelson

Peter Mandelson is the senior Labour figure whose relationship with [[JeffreyEpstein]] becomes the scandal trigger in [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]]. The episode says [[KeirStarmer]] brought Mandelson back into a senior ambassadorial role despite knowing the friendship had continued.

The source frames Mandelson less as the sole cause of Starmer's weakness than as the event that made existing doubts harder to ignore. For a prime minister selling competence and clean government, the episode treats the appointment as a damaging judgment error.
