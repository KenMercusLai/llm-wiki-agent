# Odin / 奥丁

Odin is a central god in [[NorseMythology]] as presented through [[PoeticEdda|《诗体埃达》]] and [[ProseEdda|《散文埃达》]] in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The source emphasizes him less as a simple warrior and more as a knowledge-seeker preparing for [[Ragnarok|诸神的黄昏]].

The episode contrasts Edda material with the [[MarvelCinematicUniverse|漫威宇宙]] version. In the source's account, Odin's relation to [[Loki|洛基]] and [[Thor|托尔]] is substantially reworked by modern superhero storytelling, making Odin a useful case for [[EpicModernAfterlife]].
