# Stanley Tang

Stanley Tang appears in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]] as one of the [[DoorDash]] co-founders. [[TonyXu]] places Tang with [[AndyFang]] on the undergraduate computer-science side of the founding group, while Xu and [[EvanMoore]] were connected through business school.

In the episode, Tang is part of the team that moved from small-business problem discovery into [[PaloAltoDelivery]], the rough first test that became DoorDash. His role is mainly source-contextual: the episode emphasizes the team's collective [[CustomerDiscoveryByDoingWork]], [[JankyMVP]] testing, and [[ThreeSidedMarketplaceValidation]] rather than separating each co-founder's contribution.
