# Los Angeles Dodgers

The Los Angeles Dodgers appear in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as the source's contemporary example in the [[SportsCompetitiveBalance|competitive-balance]] debate after [[BaseballFreeAgency|baseball free agency]]. The episode says the Dodgers had MLB's highest payroll and won the World Series two years in a row, making them a case for owners who argue that richer teams can buy championships.

The source uses the Dodgers to contrast [[MajorLeagueBaseball|MLB]] payroll freedom with the salary-cap systems in the [[NationalFootballLeague|NFL]] and [[NationalBasketballAssociation|NBA]].
