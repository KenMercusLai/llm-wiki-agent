# Daniel Cohn

Daniel Cohn is the artist whose question, "does the ocean have memory?", catalyzes the [[OceanMemoryProject]] in [[melody-jue-ocean-memory-wxagwid1oeg]]. The source presents the question as deceptively simple: it opens scientific, artistic, historical, sensory, ecological, and philosophical ways of asking what memory can be in seawater.

Cohn's role is important because the question does not resolve into one answer. In [[MelodyJue]]'s telling, it becomes a collaborative prompt linking [[OceanMemory]], [[JodyDeming]]'s microbial sea-ice work, sensory art, and underwater archives.
