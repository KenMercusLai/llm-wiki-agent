# Gordon Moore

Gordon Moore appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] through [[MooreLaw|Moore's Law]], the semiconductor scaling observation that became an industry rhythm for packing more components onto integrated circuits over time. The episode uses Moore's Law to explain why chips became cheaper and more powerful across decades.

In the source, Moore matters less as a biography and more as the origin point for a coordination frame: the industry invested around expectations of regular density improvement, while the post-Moore period forces companies toward process innovation, architecture, [[AdvancedPackaging|advanced packaging]], and system-level efficiency.
