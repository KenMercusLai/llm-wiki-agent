# Marine Le Pen

Marine Le Pen is the [[NationalRally]] leader whose renewed 2027 presidential bid anchors [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]]. The episode says a Paris appeal court upheld her conviction over European Parliament funds but shortened the practical effect of the office ban, allowing her to run while she continues to claim innocence.

The source treats Le Pen as both candidate and family-political project. She inherited a party associated with [[JeanMarieLePen]] and worked to make it more electorally acceptable, making her candidacy a concrete test of [[FarRightNormalization]] in [[France]].

Her return also changes the internal party sequence. [[JordanBardella]] had been preparing as the possible substitute candidate, but Le Pen's eligibility puts him back in second place while leaving a tension between her working-class economic appeal and his more business-friendly pitch.
