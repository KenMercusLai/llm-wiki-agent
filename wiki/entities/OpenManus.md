# OpenManus

OpenManus is mentioned in [[weishenme-manus-bixu-chuhai-liaoliao-guochan-da-moxing-de-wenkesheng-kunjing-keji-luandun]] as one of the products or projects moving into similar AI-agent territory after [[Manus]] became visible. The episode does not analyze OpenManus deeply; it uses the reference to show that task planning, local browser operation, and multi-tool execution were becoming more widely available.

In the source's argument, OpenManus contributes to [[ModelProviderToolCompetition]] and agent commoditization pressure: if similar workflows can be reproduced by open-source projects, model companies, and domestic products, an early agent startup may need to sell, specialize, or deepen its workflow reliability before the market catches up.
