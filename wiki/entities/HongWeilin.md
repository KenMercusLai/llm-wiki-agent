# 洪蔚琳

洪蔚琳 is the journalist whose [[ZhengmianLianjie|正面连接]] article introduced the story of [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]] to a wider Chinese-language readership in [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]]. [[AnQi|安琪]] says the article circulated widely in her social feed and became the path through which she contacted the author.

In the source, 洪蔚琳 functions as a reporting bridge between witness and publication. Her article did not remain only journalism; it became the discovery route for a book project and later appears as part of the publication's support network.
