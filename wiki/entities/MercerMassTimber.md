# Mercer Mass Timber

Mercer Mass Timber is the mass-timber actor referred to as Mercer in [[tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode links Mercer to use of [[CrossLaminatedTimber]] in the Teddy Roosevelt Presidential Library project and has [[NateFoster]] explain the speed advantage of off-site manufactured panels.

The page is named narrowly to avoid confusion with [[RobertMercer]]. In this source, Mercer matters as a construction case: panels can be fabricated elsewhere, shipped flat, lifted by crane, and placed quickly, making mass timber relevant to both [[FireResilientConstruction]] and post-disaster [[HousingAffordabilitySupplyMechanics]].
