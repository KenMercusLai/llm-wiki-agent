# 《小市民》系列

《小市民》系列 is [[YonezawaHonobu|米泽穗信]]'s daily-life mystery series as discussed in [[131-richang-zhimi-tuili-shi-wo-wei-neng-shuo-chukou-de-zhenxin-805617229]]. The episode introduces 小鸠 and 小佐内 as high-school students who once got into trouble because of their strong reasoning ability and now agree to live like ordinary "small citizens."

The discussed case centers on dessert. 小佐内 asks 小鸠 to buy mango puddings and grapefruit Charlotte cakes; only three Charlotte cakes remain, and 小鸠 eats one while waiting at her home. He then tries to erase the evidence with wrappers, plates, utensils, drinks, and tissue details, but 小佐内 notices the remaining clues and reconstructs the missing cake.

## Key Claims
- The series is used as the episode's lightest daily-life mystery example.
- The "crime" is scaled down to stealing a cake, but the evidence logic remains recognizably deductive.
- The pleasure overlaps with [[PuzzleSnackMystery|推理薯片]] because the case foregrounds clue-cleaning, wrong confidence, and final recognition.
- 小佐内's final response makes the puzzle social: if 小鸠 left no clue at all, the game would be less interesting.
