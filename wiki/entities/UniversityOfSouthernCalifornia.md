# University of Southern California

University of Southern California appears in [[tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the institution running the [[UnderstandingAmericaStudy]]. The Marketplace Tech episode uses that panel as the experimental setting for testing whether smartphones change survey performance on knowledge questions.

Within this source, USC matters as research infrastructure rather than as a broad institutional profile. The panel makes it possible to compare smartphone and non-smartphone survey modes under assignment rather than treating all device differences as respondent self-selection.
