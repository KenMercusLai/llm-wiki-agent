# Consumer Reports

Consumer Reports is the survey source cited in [[tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128]] for current physical video viewing behavior. The episode says the survey found that nearly half of people in the U.S. watch Blu-rays and DVDs, and that 15% still watch VHS tapes.

In the wiki, this makes Consumer Reports a measurement input for [[PhysicalVideoMediaRevival]]. The figures help the episode argue that physical video formats have not disappeared into pure nostalgia, even after streaming became the dominant consumer-video interface.
