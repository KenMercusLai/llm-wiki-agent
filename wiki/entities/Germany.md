# Germany

Germany appears in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]] through its permanent brigade deployment to [[Lithuania]]. [[AntonLaGuardia]] presents the deployment as historically significant because German troops are being stationed abroad on a permanent basis with infrastructure for families, schools, tanks, and armoured vehicles.

The episode uses Germany as the clearest concrete sign of [[EuropeanDefenseAutonomy]] becoming more serious. It also frames the deployment as a reversal of Cold War roles: Germany is now providing forward reassurance for Lithuania in the way allies once provided reassurance for Germany.

[[111-huayi-modishou-kuailai-he-lishixuejia-yiqi-tuili-tonghua-zhenxiang-751190766]] adds a medieval folklore branch through [[Hamelin|哈默尔恩]] and [[PiedPiperOfHamelin|花衣魔笛手]]. In this source, Germany is not a modern security actor but the setting for a local legend whose meaning depends on city law, church power, mills, migration, class hierarchy, famine, plague, and [[LegendAsSocialHistory]].
