# NeuroShake

NeuroShake appears in [[why-is-there-a-supplement-craze-if-they-dont-even-work]] as the food product used in the [[Prevagen]] case after [[FoodAndDrugAdministration|FDA]] safety concerns about the jellyfish-protein ingredient. [[MelanieBenish]] explains that introducing the ingredient through food and self-certifying it as generally recognized as safe could then support its use in a supplement.

The page exists because NeuroShake makes [[GRASSelfCertification]] concrete. It is not presented as an important consumer product on its own; its wiki role is to show how food classification, safety review, and supplement marketing can be chained together.
