# Death with Dignity

Death with Dignity is the campaign group cited in [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]] for expected U.S. state consideration of assisted-dying bills. The episode says the group is especially hopeful about Virginia, Maryland, and Massachusetts.

The source uses the group to frame gradual policy expansion rather than rapid national change, with Oregon's long-running data used by advocates to answer fears about eligibility creep and coercion.
