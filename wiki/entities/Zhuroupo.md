# Zhuroupo / 猪肉婆

Zhuroupo is the Shunde restaurant case in [[137-cong-shunde-zhuroupo-dao-hanguo-shengshuidong-naxie-ai-wufa-qudai-de-tiyan-xiaofei-feng-tou-quan-1-137-1]]. The episode presents it as a strongly recommended but queue-sensitive destination restaurant whose brand comes from a female founder image, Shunde food reputation, and the difficulty of standardizing the experience.

For the wiki, Zhuroupo extends [[RestaurantExperienceDesign]] and [[ExperientialRetail]] into destination dining. Its value is not only menu taste; it also includes travel intent, local food culture, the story carried by [[XunweiShunde]], and the social proof of a restaurant people are willing to visit repeatedly or queue for.

## Source Position
- The hosts say Zhuroupo has several stores, including a Shanghai store, but its core reputation remains tied to [[Shunde]].
- The episode estimates the company may have meaningful profits, while marking that estimate as an oral judgment rather than verified reporting.
- Whether it is "worth it" depends partly on each customer's tolerance for queue time, showing that the experience includes friction as well as taste.
