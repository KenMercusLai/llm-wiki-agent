# 蒲松龄 / Pu Songling

蒲松龄 / Pu Songling enters this wiki branch as the author of [[LiaozhaiZhiyi|《聊斋志异》]], the literary source [[JonathanSpence|史景迁]] uses in [[TheDeathOfWomanWang|《王氏之死》]]. In [[148-wangshi-zhisi-duanjing-tuiyuan-shang-de-lishi-zhuangjing-892588934]], [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] treats him as a writer whose ghost stories preserve local fears, moral fantasies, hunger, sexual danger, female competence, and bureaucratic absurdity around the world of [[Tancheng|郯城]].

The episode's methodological point is careful: Pu's fiction is not a replacement for legal proof. It is useful because [[FictionAsHistoricalEvidence]] can reveal social imagination that official documents flatten. Stories such as 《促织》, 小二, 细柳, and 云翠仙 let the episode compare fantasy agency with the narrower life space available to [[WangShiTancheng|王氏]].
