# He Tao

He Tao is the core person behind [[Deerflow]] in [[duihua-minimax-yan-junjie-m3-10x-jihua-10t-moxing-he-zhineng-de-zhongju-lqtilt8flvmv99v0gshhyfyraibe]]. He uses the project to discuss open-source AI workflow building, codebase governance, long-term engineering responsibility, and the spread of AI into family and personal-care contexts.

## Key Views
- Vibe coding should not be mistaken for engineering; durable software requires long-term maintainability.
- One-off SWE benchmarks do not fully measure whether agents can sustain and evolve a real codebase.
- Clean Code, Google best practices, Amazon best practices, and similar engineering standards can be turned into [[AISkills]] or agent context.
- Developers remain responsible for code committed under their accounts, even if an agent generated it.
- AI can help ordinary people and families by organizing private context such as medical records, medications, and questions for doctors.
