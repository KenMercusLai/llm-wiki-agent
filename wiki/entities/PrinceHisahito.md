# Prince Hisahito

Prince Hisahito appears in [[missing-peace-will-israel-imperil-iran-deal-6a3bae9fe1238e54b0e886c3]] as the male heir the episode describes as the only realistic future successor. His role in the source is structural: a monarchy with only one plausible younger male heir faces a demographic bottleneck.

The episode contrasts Hisahito's eligibility with [[PrincessAiko]]'s popularity and exclusion. That contrast makes [[JapaneseImperialSuccession]] a problem of both continuity and legitimacy.
