# Jim Ratcliffe

Jim Ratcliffe appears in [[e243-te-lang-pu-huanxing-hongpai-zhiwai-meiguo-ziben-ruhe-yingkong-quanqiu-zutan]] as the [[INEOS]] owner who bought a minority stake in [[ManchesterUnited]] from the [[GlazerFamily]]. The episode says Ratcliffe's British identity and supporter status make him easier for some fans to trust than the Glazers, especially around sporting culture and long-term club building.

The source also treats Ratcliffe as a financial-risk variable. [[INEOS]] is described as a large industrial group with strong debt and financial structuring capability, so the episode does not assume that Ratcliffe's arrival automatically removes [[FootballClubFinancialEngineering]] risk.

## Key Claims
- Ratcliffe is presented as both a potential sporting reset and a more sophisticated financial actor.
- His stake does not fully end Glazer-family exposure to Manchester United's future valuation.
- The episode separates fan hope from balance-sheet risk.
