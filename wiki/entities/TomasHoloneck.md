# Tomas Holoneck

Tomas Holoneck is the [[UniversityOfCambridge]] researcher interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] in [[tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128]] about [[AIGriefBots]] and post-mortem avatars. The source presents him as focused on how personal data can be transformed into simulations of deceased people and why that raises [[PostMortemAIConsent]] questions.

Holoneck's main contribution to the wiki is the governance frame. He does not argue that all digital memorial experiments should stop; he distinguishes potentially valuable family memory and survivor testimony from company-led systems that could exploit grief, violate privacy, or represent a person without consent.
