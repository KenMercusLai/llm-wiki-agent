# Salim Ramji

Salim Ramji appears in [[vanguard-1]] as [[Vanguard]]'s first outside CEO, named in May 2024 after previously leading [[BlackRock]]'s iShares business. The episode treats his appointment as a strategic signal because Vanguard needs stronger technology, customer service, advice, fixed income, retirement, and private-market capabilities while preserving the fee discipline created by [[FundholderMutualOwnership]].

Ramji therefore extends the wiki's [[FounderSuccession]] and [[StrategyFollowsStructure]] branches. The question is not simply whether a new CEO can modernize operations, but whether the company can enter higher-fee or access-based markets, including a [[Blackstone]] alliance, while remaining recognizably Vanguard.
