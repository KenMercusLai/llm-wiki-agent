# Jeff Denning

Jeff Denning is the economist in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] whose Texas study provides the episode's strongest evidence for the [[BennettHypothesis]]. The source says Denning and colleagues studied the 2006 creation of [[GradPLUSLoans|Grad PLUS loans]] and found that graduate-school prices rose after students could borrow more.

The episode presents his estimate as causal in that setting: about 64 cents of higher price for every additional loan dollar. That makes Denning central to the policy case for [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]], while still leaving open whether the same effect appears across fields, states, and institutions.
