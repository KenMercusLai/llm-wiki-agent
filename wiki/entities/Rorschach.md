# 罗夏 / Rorschach

罗夏 / Rorschach is discussed in [[01-maitian-shouwangzhe-20shiji-de-meiguo-jiabaoyu-523554422]] as a darker and more extreme comparison point for [[HoldenCaulfield|Holden Caulfield / 霍尔顿]]. The hosts connect both figures to a refusal of dirt, compromise, hypocrisy, and moral ugliness.

The comparison is also a guardrail. The episode says Holden remains "怂" and fundamentally unlikely to threaten others in the way an extreme purity figure might. Rorschach therefore helps define what Holden is not: he is a wounded adolescent with a protective fantasy, not a violent avenger. This ties the source to [[AntiheroMisreading]] because the attractive image of uncompromising purity can be detached from the story's warning.
