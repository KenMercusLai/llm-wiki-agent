# Automatic / Automatic Auctions

Automatic / Automatic Auctions is the post-[[BoSo]] company direction described in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]]. The source says [[HarjTaggar]], [[KulvirTaggar]], and later [[PatrickCollison]] shifted from a UK student marketplace toward software that helped small businesses and eBay sellers sell online more easily.

The company is a [[FounderProductFit]] caution in the episode. Harj says the team built tools for heavy online sellers even though they were not themselves heavy sellers, which limited how deeply they understood the user's real workflow. After Demo Day, the company raised money from investors including [[PaulBuchheit]], then later received acquisition offers from [[LiveCurrentMedia]] and [[Meta|Facebook]].
