# Alan Turing / 图灵

Alan Turing appears in [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]] through the episode's discussion of morphogenesis. The source says Turing published equations shortly before his arrest that help explain how similar cells can differentiate and arrange patterns such as hair follicles through growth factors, responsive cells, and concentration gradients.

In this wiki branch, Turing is not mainly a computing figure. He is the bridge between mathematical form and biological pattern formation, extending [[EvolutionaryTraitInterpretation]] and [[HairAsBiosocialSignal]] beyond surface function into developmental mechanism.
