# Anna Kerr

Anna Kerr explains the legal-AI segment in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]]. She describes courts encountering more AI-assisted self-representation and professional filings, turning generative AI from a productivity tool into a courtroom reliability problem.

Her segment frames [[VibeLawyering]] as AI-generated legal confidence without enough legal grounding. It also distinguishes risky generic chatbot use from [[HumanInTheLoopLegalAI]], where purpose-built tools such as [[GarfieldAI]] may help people access court while humans keep responsibility for legal judgment and advocacy.
