# Wei Qing / 韦青

Wei Qing is the guest in [[e42-mengyan-duihua-weiqing-chenmode-zhujue-lip25o2kub9dknpjhfgc4lz-76w0]], where he speaks from an engineering, [[Microsoft]], reading, and systems-thinking background. The episode presents him as someone who treats engineering as a lifelong identity rather than a job title, and who repeatedly turns AI and management questions back toward people, common sense, language, incentives, and body-mind state.

His central contribution is [[SilentProtagonist]]: the visible person, product, or result is often less important than the quiet accumulation that made it possible. He applies that frame to [[Microsoft]]'s transformation, organizational rewards, machine learning, AI risk, information civilization, personal agents, attention systems, and the risk that humans become machine-like before machines become human-like.
