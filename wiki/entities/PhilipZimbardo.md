# Philip Zimbardo / 菲利普·津巴多

Philip Zimbardo / 菲利普·津巴多 appears in [[123-renxing-shiyan-dangxin-ni-ziji-779479569]] through two routes: the familiar Stanford prison experiment mentioned in the setup, and the later masked/anonymous shock study used to explain [[Deindividuation]]. The episode notes his connection with [[StanleyMilgram]] and says he later stopped some experimental lines and turned toward textbook work.

The source uses Zimbardo less as a hero than as a case for why experiment, situation, identity, and ethics have to be kept together.
