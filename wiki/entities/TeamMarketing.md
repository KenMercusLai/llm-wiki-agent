# Team Marketing

Team Marketing appears in [[e243-te-lang-pu-huanxing-hongpai-zhiwai-meiguo-ziben-ruhe-yingkong-quanqiu-zutan]] as the long-running commercial partner associated with UEFA club competitions before [[RelevantSports]] won newer commercial-development work. The episode uses the change as evidence that European football's rights layer is being re-bid and globalized.

The page's role in the wiki is comparative. Team Marketing represents the established European competition-commercialization structure, while CAA and Relevant Sports represent the episode's Americanization and rights-intermediation claim.

## Key Claims
- Team Marketing is treated as the incumbent UEFA club-competition commercial partner in the source.
- The shift away from it is used to show how important the commercial-rights layer has become.
- The episode frames rights intermediation as a control point alongside club ownership and bank financing.
