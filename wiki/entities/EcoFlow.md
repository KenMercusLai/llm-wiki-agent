# EcoFlow / 正浩

EcoFlow, discussed as 正浩, appears in [[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]] as another company associated with [[DJI]] alumni. The episode uses it to show how DJI-trained teams can move into adjacent or new hardware markets such as outdoor power and portable energy storage.

EcoFlow also gives the source a category-expansion contrast. DJI itself is trying to enter outdoor power and other newer categories, but alumni-led companies may already occupy some of those spaces, which makes [[HardwareTalentSpillover]] a competitive issue as well as a retention issue.
