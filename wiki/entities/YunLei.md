# 运雷

运雷 appears in [[e158-zichan-peizhi-yu-youxiao-qianyan-qu-zhao-genghaode-geng-buyiyangde-geng-tiejin-shidaide-luzri-gwmqhb02k9xmf6mcdsaqkc]] as the [[Mianji]] guest explaining how his move from domestic equity investing into international business and [[NanfangQuanqiu]] management changed his asset-allocation framework. He presents [[AssetAllocation]] as a combination of client-result design, expected-return estimation, [[AssetCorrelation]] research, and disciplined movement of the [[EfficientFrontier]].

His distinctive stance is that a fund manager cannot only be a security picker. In multi-asset FOF work, the manager also has to be a product manager who decides what risk-return path the client actually needs, what strategic base is understandable, and where tactical views deserve limited risk budget.
