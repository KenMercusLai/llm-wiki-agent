# Legend Biotech / 南京传奇

Legend Biotech appears in [[e235-20-nian-nei-car-t-zhiyu-aizheng-yu-liucheng-boshi-liaoliao-aizheng-zhiliao-de-diceng-zhexue-90f96f60-25be-45ac-b832-56776a23d534]] as the Chinese CAR-T commercialization case behind a multiple-myeloma product developed with [[JohnsonAndJohnson]]. The episode uses the company to show that [[CARTCellTherapy|CAR-T]] can become a major pharmaceutical business once strong clinical response moves a product earlier in treatment lines.

The source says the Legend-Johnson & Johnson product reached about $1.9 billion in 2025 sales and moved from fourth-line toward second-line treatment because of efficacy. In the episode, this supports Liu's claim that blood-cancer CAR-T is constrained less by whether it works than by [[ExVivoCARTManufacturing|manufacturing cost and scale]].

## Key Claims
- Legend Biotech is used as a proof point that CAR-T can pass the "blockbuster drug" commercial threshold.
- The product-line movement from late-line to earlier-line therapy is presented as the business consequence of strong efficacy.
- The company is a Chinese biotech example inside a field Liu says developed in China and the United States on a near-synchronous timeline.
