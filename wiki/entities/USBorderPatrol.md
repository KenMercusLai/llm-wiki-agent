# U.S. Border Patrol

U.S. Border Patrol appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as one of the agencies whose recruits are trained through [[FederalLawEnforcementTrainingCenters]]. The episode names it alongside [[USImmigrationAndCustomsEnforcement|ICE]] and [[USCustomsAndBorderProtection|CBP]] while discussing immigration enforcement growth, training capacity, and public scrutiny.

The source does not give a separate institutional history of Border Patrol. Its role is to mark the broader enforcement ecosystem around [[USDepartmentOfHomelandSecurity]] and to show that training and culture questions are not limited to ICE alone.
