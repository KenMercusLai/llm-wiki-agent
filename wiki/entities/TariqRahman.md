# Tariq Rahman

Tariq Rahman is the [[BangladeshNationalistParty]] front figure in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]]. [[MarkJohnson]] reports travelling with him on a bulletproof campaign bus from Dhaka to a rally in Mymensingh after Rahman returned from 17 years of self-exile in London.

The source treats Rahman as both electorally powerful and politically vulnerable. He denies wrongdoing, and newly independent courts have quashed convictions made against him during [[SheikhHasina]]'s rule, but the episode says an old American diplomatic cable alleging corruption still shadows his return.
