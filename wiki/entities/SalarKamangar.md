# Salar Kamangar

Salar Kamangar appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as the young early [[Google]] operator [[RonConway]] was told to work with after the investment process. Conway describes him as head of operations and later credits him with figuring out the [[AdWords]] strategy that turned Google's usage into revenue.

The source makes Kamangar important because Google had strong search usage before it had a clear revenue model. His role connects [[DistributionBeforeMonetization]] to the eventual advertising system that made search economically powerful.
