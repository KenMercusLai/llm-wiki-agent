# 汽水音乐 / Qishui Music

Qishui Music appears in [[quanmian-yazhi-buliu-kongdang-zijie-tiaodong-ruhe-zuo-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-7-ji-lqszvmur6jv8b9xt8rjgmwfdqtec]] as a ByteDance music product whose growth is tied to [[Douyin]] account, interest, and music-signal infrastructure. [[XuHongliang]] contrasts his Qishui onboarding with traditional music apps: one-tap Douyin login and years of short-video behavior can immediately inform music recommendation.

For the wiki, Qishui Music extends [[PlatformIPStrategy]] and [[RecommendationDistributionAdvantage]] into music. The product is not only a separate app; it is a case where a short-video platform's BGM, viral-song, and user-interest signals become a growth and personalization asset.
