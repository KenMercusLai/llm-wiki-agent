# Momenta

Momenta is the autonomous-driving company discussed in [[132-dui-xinghaitu-chuangshiren-gaojiyang-de-3-xiaoshi-fangtan]] as [[GaoJiyang]]'s post-[[Waymo]] operating school. The source presents Momenta as a result-oriented, customer-pressure-heavy organization whose mass-production delivery culture helped Gao move from research and engineering into product and company-building.

## Key Points
- Gao says he chose Momenta because [[CaoXudong]] was technically strong, decisive, AI-oriented, and long-term in his goals.
- The company is described as pursuing a mass-production flywheel from around 2018: ship assisted-driving products, collect data, generate revenue, and eventually move toward Robotaxi.
- Gao says he worked across perception, localization, parking, infrastructure, planning/control, and NOA mass production, becoming a "catfish" who entered unfamiliar modules and forced progress.
- The deepest lesson he says he took from Momenta is customer-centered work: understanding the customer's real problem and proposing a better solution, not simply obeying requests.
