# William Bass

William Bass is the forensic anthropologist named in [[121-jijian-fayi-jianshi-shouce-yiqie-fanzui-jieyou-henji-775064343]] as the founder of the University of Tennessee body farm. The episode uses him to show that knowledge about corpse decomposition does not come only from accumulated lore; it also comes from direct, controlled, and ethically uncomfortable observation.

Bass anchors [[BodyFarmForensicResearch]] in the wiki. Bodies exposed in open air, shade, water, burial, trunks, and other conditions make [[ForensicDeathTimeInference]] more empirical, while also showing why death-time estimates remain dependent on environment rather than one universal timetable.
