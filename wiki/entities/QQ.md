# QQ

QQ is Tencent's messaging product used in [[cong-qq-huiyuan-dao-doubao-baoyue-zhongguoren-weishenme-zong-juede-ruanjian-gai-mianfei-keji-luandun]] as the historical comparison for [[Doubao]] paid membership. The hosts recall QQ membership as an early Chinese internet charging model that preserved free access while adding paid privileges.

## Key Points
- QQ membership is presented as a precedent for charging without removing a core free service.
- The episode notes that SMS and early mobile payment channels helped QQ membership scale.
- The comparison also shows a difference: QQ had strong social-network lock-in, while AI assistants such as [[Doubao]] may have lower switching costs.
