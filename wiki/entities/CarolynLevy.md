# Carolyn Levy

Carolyn Levy is one of the [[TheSocialRadars]] hosts in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]], [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]], [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]], and [[socialradarspod-paulb-final]], interviewing [[TrevorBlackwell]], [[TracyYoung]], [[TonyXu]], and [[PaulBuchheit]] with [[JessicaLivingston]]. In the Blackwell source she helps frame Blackwell's role in early [[YCombinator]] culture; in the Young source she helps draw out the second-time founder, remote-work, values, and family-life dimensions of [[TigerEye]]; in the Xu source she is part of the interview format around [[DoorDash]]'s customer-discovery and marketplace-validation path; and in the Buchheit source she helps surface Gmail, FriendFeed, and YC judgment lessons.

Her role in the wiki is mainly source-contextual: she is part of the interview format that lets startup participants clarify details from [[Viaweb]], [[Anybots]], [[PlanGrid]], [[DoorDash]], [[Gmail]], [[FriendFeed]], YC origin stories, and later company-building lessons.
