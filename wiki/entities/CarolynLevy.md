# Carolyn Levy

Carolyn Levy is one of the [[TheSocialRadars]] hosts in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]] and [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]], interviewing [[TrevorBlackwell]] and [[TracyYoung]] with [[JessicaLivingston]]. In the Blackwell source she helps frame Blackwell's role in early [[YCombinator]] culture; in the Young source she helps draw out the second-time founder, remote-work, values, and family-life dimensions of [[TigerEye]].

Her role in the wiki is mainly source-contextual: she is part of the interview format that lets startup participants clarify details from [[Viaweb]], [[Anybots]], [[PlanGrid]], YC origin stories, and later company-building lessons.
