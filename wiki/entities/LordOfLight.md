# 《光明王》 / Lord of Light

《光明王》 / Lord of Light is the [[RogerZelazny|Roger Zelazny / 罗杰·泽拉兹尼]] work briefly introduced in [[jiaqi-moyu-geng-jiankang-574391976]] and recommended again in [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]]. The earlier source describes it as a far-future story on another planet that uses language resembling Hindu and Buddhist religious conflict to tell a rebellion story.

The later source still does not close-read the novel, but it strengthens the page's role. [[Beimin|北明]] recommends it as a brilliant, ornate work about resistance to tyranny, while [[Doudou|逗逗]] says it can appeal to wuxia readers because of its intense battle scenes and martial-arts-like devices. In the wiki, the book now anchors Zelazny's [[MythicScienceFictionStyle]] beside the shorter stories in [[ForABreathITarry|《趁生命气息逗留》]].
