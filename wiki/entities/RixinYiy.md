# 日新月异 / Rixin Yiy

Rixin Yiy is [[HouChao]]'s live-action short-drama company in [[269-zhenren-duanju-de-xiayizhan-yu-ai-gongsheng-gongyehua-he-hao-gushi-lmwhgqd0rge4mr0fwp5qpr2scwxt]]. The episode presents it as a [[ShortDramaIndustrialization]] case: at peak it could run more than 200 projects at once, with a city-level capacity ceiling shaped by project coordination and scarce key roles rather than only by money.

The company's operating model depends on strong IP supply, script classification and tagging, repeated high-frequency scenes, costume/prop reuse, middle-office controls, and an ROI-driven decision system. Hou Chao says its single-drama cost can sit around the low hundreds of thousands of RMB because that cost base depends on batch production and an employee system.

Rixin Yiy's use of AI is not limited to generated video. The source says the company uses AI editing, AI assistant tools, script/database linkage, scene-number and character consistency checks, electronic continuity records, actor notices, attendance records, and other workflow controls to make live-action production more auditable.
