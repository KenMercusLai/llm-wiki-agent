# Russo-Chinese Bank

Russo-Chinese Bank is the foreign-bank example in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]] tied to indemnities, colonial finance, foreign-exchange activity, speculation, and later collapse or cleanup. The source uses it as a less stable contrast to banks whose public trust and China business endured longer.

Its wiki role is to keep [[ShanghaiForeignBanks]] from becoming a simple prestige story: foreign banks could supply trade finance and credibility, but they also carried geopolitical, speculative, and institutional risks.
