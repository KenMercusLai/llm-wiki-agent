# Google Calendar

Google Calendar is the scheduling product used as a mundane AI example in [[tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128]]. [[ChristopherMims]] says calendar management is one of his least favorite tasks, making it a useful target for [[GooglePersonalIntelligence]] and [[Gemini]] voice instructions.

The source positions Google Calendar as a good test of [[MundaneAIUseCases]]. The assistant does not need to create a dramatic artifact; it creates value by reducing small repeated planning friction inside a tool the user already has to use.
