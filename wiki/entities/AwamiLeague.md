# Awami League

The Awami League is the party of [[SheikhHasina]] in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]]. The episode presents the party's long period in power as a source of institutional weakening, reserve depletion, and public anger before the uprising that opened [[Bangladesh]]'s new election cycle.

The party is temporarily banned from participating in the election, but [[MarkJohnson]] argues that the next government will probably need to rehabilitate it in some form because many Bangladeshis would still vote for it. In the source, that makes the Awami League question central to whether the [[DemocraticTransitionElection]] becomes a durable settlement or a one-sided purge.
