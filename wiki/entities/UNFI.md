# UNFI

UNFI is the distributor discussed in [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]] as a gatekeeper for [[JustinsNutButter]]'s early [[WholeFoodsMarket]] path. The episode presents UNFI as part of a classic CPG chicken-and-egg problem: distributors want enough retail demand, while retailers prefer products already available through distribution.
