# Jesse and Ben's

Jesse and Ben's is the frozen french fry brand discussed in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. [[JesseKoenig]] describes the product as made with non-GMO potatoes, grass-fed beef tallow or avocado oil, salt, and seasonings, then sold frozen for home air-fryer or oven use.

The Advice Line question is whether to market first to people already avoiding seed oils and ultra-processed foods or to broader fry buyers. [[JeniBritton]] and [[GuyRaz]] push the brand toward taste, quality, and hot sampling as the lead, with health and ingredient differences as supporting proof. The episode's update says the company grew from about 400 stores to more than 5,000 stores across chains including Whole Foods, Sprouts, Target, and Costco, and raised $10 million.
