# Alexei Chirikov / 阿列克谢·齐里科夫

Alexei Chirikov appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] as [[VitusBering]]'s colleague and later deputy in the [[GreatNorthernExpedition]]. He participates in the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]] and commands the Saint Paul during the second voyage toward [[Alaska]].

The source uses Chirikov to show the expedition's divided-command problem. He is capable and ambitious, but the requirement that major decisions be agreed with Bering, the Senate's later willingness to let him countermand orders, and the Saint Paul landing-party disappearance all make [[ExpeditionCommandFragmentation]] a central risk rather than a footnote.
