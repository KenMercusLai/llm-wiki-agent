# Mindhunter

The source uses it to show the emotional side of cancellation debates: a loyal audience can feel abandoned even when a platform says the budget and viewership do not justify continuation.

Its wiki role is to connect fandom frustration with [[StreamingAudienceRetention]]. The episode's framing suggests that cancellations should be read through audience size, budget, loyalty, and catalog strategy rather than through fan attachment alone.
