# 豆瓣 / Douban

豆瓣 / Douban appears in [[140-haikeyi-de-jin-nvshi-suoyi-ren-weishenme-yao-nuli-a-854173655]] as the public book-rating and review surface that affects [[Jinzi|金子]] after publishing [[HaikeyiDeJinNvshi|《还可以的金女士》]]. The source treats Douban less as a technology object than as a scoring environment: even when a creator knows that reviews are partial, one low score or hostile comment can dominate attention.

This makes Douban part of the wiki's [[CreatorEvaluationPressure]] branch. In this source, platform scoring revives [[RedPenLogic]] because book reception begins to feel like being graded again, not only like hearing from readers.
