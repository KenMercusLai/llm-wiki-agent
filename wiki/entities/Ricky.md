# Ricky

Ricky is the guest in [[vol-106-dibai-zhende-biandi-shi-huangjin-lpxchutxf7yomjdfykwzvvnmbaq8]], where he speaks with [[DavidWeng|大卫翁]] after a two-week visit to [[Dubai]] and [[AbuDhabi|Abu Dhabi]]. His perspective is finance-oriented: the episode follows what he heard from bankers, developers, brokers, lawyers, and businesspeople about capital flow, residency, real estate, crypto, and Chinese outbound companies.

Ricky's role in the wiki is as a short-field-visit observer, not a definitive authority on the UAE. He repeatedly qualifies his claims, especially around [[DubaiRealEstateMarket]], [[DubaiGoldenVisaResidency]], and investment opportunities, by saying listeners should discount the observations and seek correction from people who know the Middle East better.
