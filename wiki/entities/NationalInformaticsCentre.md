# National Informatics Centre

The National Informatics Centre appears in [[strait-and-narrowing-the-iran-deal-crumbles-6a57608bf821a8210984481f]] as the government technology organization behind many Indian public websites. [[LeoMirani]] describes it as reasonably competent, but constrained by officials, political demands, and institutional processes that can override good user-experience judgment.

In the wiki, the NIC is less a blame object than an example of how [[GovernmentWebsiteUsability]] depends on governance. If a technical team cannot push back on bad requirements or redesign paper procedures, even competent implementation can produce cluttered and confusing [[PublicServiceDigitalization]].
