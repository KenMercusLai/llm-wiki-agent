# Han River / 汉江

The Han River appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as part of the water geography that makes [[Wuhan]] and [[Hankou]] a flood-prone urban site. The episode links the city's history to the meeting of the [[YangtzeRiver|长江]] and Han River, wetland settlement, and later defensive infrastructure.

The source also uses the Han River to show how flood control can become political. Earlier military use, dike work, and maintenance failures appear as part of the region's long [[FloodControlRiskTransfer]] problem rather than as purely technical water management.
