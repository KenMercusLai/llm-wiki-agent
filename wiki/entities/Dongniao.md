# 懂鸟 / Dongniao

Dongniao is the Chinese bird-recognition tool mentioned by [[RenNing]] in [[ganguan-fangda-shijie-he-renning-liao-guanniao-ziran-yu-ziyou-e583dac2-bad8-4208-8d35-0c3de8594779]]. The episode uses it to explain how AI can help identify birds while still failing on rare species when training examples are limited.

Its main wiki value is as a concrete [[AIRecognitionBias]] case. A bird classifier may learn photographer habits, camera types, image backgrounds, or common-species priors rather than the species traits a human birder would use, so human review remains part of [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]].
