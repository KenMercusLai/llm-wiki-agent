# Quark

Quark is an Alibaba product surface discussed in [[ep117-doubao-yuehuo-guoyi-ali-zaizao-qianwen-shibushi-wanle-lmp0pzdig2ijow5k3cnnnvvqq6sa]] through the question of why [[Alibaba]] would push [[Qwen]] as a separate AI assistant instead of relying on an existing browser/search/netdisk-style product. The hosts argue that Quark has useful traffic and history, but also inherited product baggage that may make it harder to become the central AI-native assistant brand.

## Key Points
- Quark can serve as an entry and distribution surface for Qwen.
- The source describes newer Quark surfaces as exposing the Qwen brand more directly.
- Quark illustrates [[AIProductFragmentation]] inside a large company: several products may all want to become the AI entry point.
- The hosts see a separate [[Qwen]] assistant as potentially cleaner for [[AIAssistantServiceEntry]] than trying to convert every Quark user habit.
