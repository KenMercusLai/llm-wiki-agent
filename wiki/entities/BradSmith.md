# Brad Smith

Brad Smith is discussed in [[tech-20260116-0116-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260116-0116-mp-tech-pod-128]] as [[Microsoft]]'s president and public voice for a data-center responsibility pledge. The episode quotes his view that communities have questions about electricity prices, water supply, and who will fill new jobs as AI data centers expand.

Smith's role in the wiki is source-scoped: he represents Microsoft's attempt to answer [[DataCenterCostShifting]] and [[DataCenterBacklash]] before they harden into permitting, utility-rate, or election constraints. The episode treats the pledge as more detailed than a generic promise while still leaving open whether other hyperscalers will follow.
