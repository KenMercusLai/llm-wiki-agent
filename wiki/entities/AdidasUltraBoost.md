# Adidas Ultra Boost

Adidas Ultra Boost is the running/lifestyle shoe discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as one of the few products Gaide considers truly era-defining. The episode says it brought TPU foam into mainstream consumer attention, but its weight kept it from dominating road-racing competition.

## Key Claims
- Ultra Boost is treated as a commercial and lifestyle breakthrough rather than the same kind of marathon-racing breakthrough as [[NikeVaporfly]].
- Its case shows that [[RunningShoeTechnology]] can create value through comfort, daily wear, and fashion adoption even when the product is not the fastest racing shoe.
- The source uses Ultra Boost to distinguish brand-marketing "era" language from actual market and consumer adoption.
