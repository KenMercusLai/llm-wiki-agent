# Grundfos / 格兰富

Grundfos appears in [[shangye-xiaoyang-43-ai-shidai-shui-zai-gei-fuwuqi-jiangwen-992085076]] as the company case for AI-era data-center cooling. The episode uses Grundfos to move the infrastructure discussion from generic data centers into pumps, variable-frequency control, water treatment, integrated cooling stations, and monitoring systems that keep high-density AI racks operating.

Its role in the wiki is to make [[DataCenterThermalManagement]] concrete. The source presents pumps and water systems as active control infrastructure rather than commodity facility equipment: as AI workloads rise and fall, the cooling system must change flow, pressure, and speed without wasting energy or risking heat buildup.

## Key Claims
- Grundfos is framed as a supplier of pumps, pump-control systems, integrated cooling stations, and intelligent monitoring for data-center cooling.
- The episode says cooling-system optimization can materially reduce energy use, with Grundfos cited for a possible reduction of up to 70% in some cases.
- Grundfos's [[HenanSmartSupercomputingCenter]] example is used to show the value of prefabricated, pre-tested cooling modules for fast deployment.
- The company case connects water technology to [[AIComputeContinuity]] because unreliable cooling can degrade performance, trigger shutdowns, or shorten equipment life.
