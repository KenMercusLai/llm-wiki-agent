# Nationalist Government / 国民政府

The Nationalist Government appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as both a modernization project and a constrained disaster-response actor. The episode links it to anti-superstition campaigns in [[Wuhan]] and [[Hankou]], civil-war pressures, relief organization, public-health work, labor-for-relief rules, military management of refugees, and the U.S. wheat-loan arrangement.

The source does not treat the government as simply absent. It could mobilize officials, doctors, international experts, and finance through figures such as [[SongZiwen]]. But it also shows the limits of [[DisasterResponseStateCapacity]] when fiscal pressure, war, legitimacy concerns, expert ideology, violence, and survivor distrust shaped relief.

[[41-shijianqiao-fuchouan-chaoyue-habeimasi-de-qing-yu-fa-614550038]] adds a law, media, and public-opinion angle. In the [[ShiJianqiaoRevengeCase]], the Nationalist Government appears through censorship pressure, party-state influence on legal institutions, special pardon power, and later wartime patriotic mobilization. The episode treats the pardon of [[ShiJianqiao]] as [[PardonAsStatePower]]: the state absorbed public sympathy while also showing that executive discretion could override the practical force of court judgments.
