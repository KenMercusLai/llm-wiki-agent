# Guantanamo Bay

Guantanamo Bay appears in [[how-to-get-what-greenland-has-with-permission]] as [[DanielImmerwahr]]'s clearest example of [[MilitaryBaseSovereignty]]. The episode says the base is in [[Cuba]], that Cuba has asked the [[UnitedStates]] to leave, and that the U.S. has not left.

The example matters because it shows how strategic control can persist without formal annexation. In the source's Greenland argument, Guantanamo Bay helps explain why military access, jurisdiction, flags, and host-country consent or non-consent can become politically sensitive even when a state does not claim to own the surrounding country.
