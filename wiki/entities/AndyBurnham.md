# Andy Burnham

Andy Burnham is one of the possible [[LabourPartyUK]] successors discussed in [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]] and then the likely successor in [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]]. The earlier episode treats him as a recognizable alternative to [[KeirStarmer]] but says he lacks a current route into the premiership. The later episode says a by-election victory gives him that route, though it remains unclear whether Labour will hold a contest or arrange a coronation.

The newer source also limits Burnham's room for maneuver. He may unite Labour because he looks like a winner, but the episode says Labour's 2024 manifesto leaves him narrow policy space.
