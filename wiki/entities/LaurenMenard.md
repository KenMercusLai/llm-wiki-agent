# Lauren Menard

Lauren Menard is the founder of [[Gob]] and a caller in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. Her Advice Line question is how to scale two different verticals without losing focus: live-event venues, where Gob has a national partnership and product-in-context exposure, and direct-to-consumer sleep customers, where early reviews are strong.

The episode says Gob had raised a $1 million pre-seed round and was raising a seed round on a SAFE note. Lauren's larger ambition is to build a new category of overlooked single-use personal care products made with biomaterials.
