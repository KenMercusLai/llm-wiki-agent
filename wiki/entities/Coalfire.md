# Coalfire

Coalfire is the cybersecurity company represented by [[CharlesHenderson]] in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. Henderson frames AI-assisted scam websites as an economic change rather than a brand-new fraud category.

For the wiki, Coalfire anchors the cost-and-scale interpretation of [[AIAssistedWebsiteScams]]. The episode uses Henderson's comments to distinguish the old existence of fake sites from the newer ability to produce many official-looking sites with less technical labor.
