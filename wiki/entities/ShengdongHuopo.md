# 声动活泼

声动活泼 is the media organization behind the [[ShengdongJixi]] context in [[vol-165-zuoke-shengdongjixi-longxia-he-vibe-coding-zhengruhe-gaibian-womende-siwei-laizi-xiaobai-chuangyezhe-he-gongchengshi-butong-shijiao-de-taolun-1-6642-1]]. Its internal AI Hackathon is used as the episode's concrete proof that [[VibeCoding]] and AI tools can let non-technical employees turn repeated media-workflow pain into usable software prototypes.

## Source Position
- Employees built practical tools around audio work, title brainstorming, image generation or processing, and other day-to-day production needs.
- [[XuTao]] later built a news-crawling and AI topic-recommendation prototype, then noticed the boundary between useful demo and stable company system.
- The organization becomes a media-company case for [[BusinessLedAITransformation]]: workflow owners can discover and prototype automation, but production adoption still needs engineers, permission rules, and maintenance.
