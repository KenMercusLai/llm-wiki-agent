# Weilai Buyuan

Weilai Buyuan is the home-robotics company founded by [[ZhangYi]] and discussed in [[jushen-zhineng-de-taotian-da-paomo-zhong-ta-yijing-ba-jiqiren-songjin-300-ge-jiating-duihua-zhang-yi-weilaibuyuan-chuangshiren-ceo-lic8b7dkxts3qjrs6af1rgbf4xrf]]. The source describes the company as building the [[F2HomeRobot]], a household robot focused on child care, light chores, and real-home service behavior rather than industrial robotics or pure companionship.

The company is presented as a roughly 100-person, engineering-heavy team that first kept development quiet, then used rental deployments in about 300 Shanghai homes to gather feedback. Its strategy combines [[ConsumerRoboticsFullStack]] self-development, consumer-grade cost reduction, and a [[HouseholdRobotDataFlywheel]] built from real family use.

## Key Points
- It uses rental and trial deployment before broad sales, partly to test stability, maintenance burden, use duration, renewal, and referral.
- It positions the robot's value as service quality, especially child care and education-like companionship, rather than only housework replacement.
- It self-develops important parts where industrial-grade components are too costly or mismatched for family use.
- It treats world-model and VLA progress as important but unfinished, making real household feedback and engineering integration part of the moat.
