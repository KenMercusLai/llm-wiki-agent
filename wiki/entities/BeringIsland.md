# Bering Island / 白令岛

Bering Island is the island where the Saint Peter survivors landed in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] after the [[GreatNorthernExpedition]]'s American voyage went wrong. The episode says the crew first mistook it for [[Kamchatka]], while [[VitusBering]] and [[GeorgWilhelmSteller]] doubted that interpretation.

The island becomes the episode's survival laboratory. Sick sailors, weakened officers, blue foxes, food scarcity, makeshift shelter, [[ScurvyNutritionInsight]], [[SeaOtter]] hunting, [[StellersSeaCow]], and the decision to dismantle the Saint Peter into a smaller vessel all show how imperial hierarchy bends under practical need. The island also becomes a hinge between discovery and ecological harm because animals unfamiliar with humans were quickly drawn into survival and trade.
