# 乌台诗案 / Wutai Poetry Case

The Wutai Poetry Case is the major political-legal crisis in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode explains "乌台" as the censorial institution and presents the case as a moment when [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s poems, memorial language, and circulated manuscripts were gathered into accusations of slandering policy and ruler.

The source's emphasis is that the case was not only about literary cleverness. Poems expressing sympathy for people jailed or beaten over new-law debt could be read as attacks on the [[WangAnshiReforms|王安石变法]] and [[SongShenzong|宋神宗]]. That makes the case a core example of [[PoetryAsPoliticalEvidence]] inside [[SongFactionalPolitics]].

Su Shi was jailed for 103 days, feared execution, and worried that friends and family would be dragged down with him. The episode names a wide rescue network, then treats the sentence to [[Huangzhou|黄州]] as both punishment and the beginning of the mature Dongpo phase.
