# Reality Labs

Reality Labs is [[Meta]]'s virtual-reality and augmented-reality division in [[tech-20260219-0219-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260219-0219-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says Meta recently laid off people in the division and uses that as evidence that AI has overtaken VR as the company's more urgent strategic priority.

[[MikeIsaac]] does not say Meta has abandoned VR. Instead, he frames the likely long-term ambition as convergence among AI, apps, smart glasses, and virtual reality, while noting that consumer adoption of VR remains uncertain and less natural than glasses-based interaction.
