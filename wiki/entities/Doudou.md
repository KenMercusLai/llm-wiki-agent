# 逗逗

逗逗 is the editor guest in [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] discusses [[RogerZelazny|Roger Zelazny / 罗杰·泽拉兹尼]] and [[ForABreathITarry|《趁生命气息逗留》]]. In the source, she explains that [[ForABreathITarry|《趁生命气息逗留》]] and [[TheDoorsOfHisFaceTheLampsOfHisMouth|《脸上的门，口中的灯》]] were acquired together, alongside rights including [[LordOfLight|《光明王》]] and 《安珀志》.

Her role is partly publishing context and partly interpretation. She helps frame the collection as an entry point for readers who like humanistic, carefully built short fiction, and she repeatedly links Zelazny's robot stories to the question of "how a person becomes a robot" and "how a robot becomes a person."
