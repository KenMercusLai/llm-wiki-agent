# 李德裕

李德裕 appears in [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]] as the "Li" pole of the [[NiuLiPartyStruggle|牛李党争]]. The episode contrasts him with [[NiuSengru|牛僧儒]] and [[LiZongmin|李宗闵]] while explaining the late-Tang political background behind [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]].

The source uses Li Deyu most concretely in the Tibet/Tubo policy contrast: he is associated with the more assertive line of accepting a rebel general and considering military action, while 牛僧儒 argues from alliance obligations and the cost of renewed conflict. The page remains source-scoped rather than a full political biography.

[[90-youyang-zazu-xuedizi-he-wuzetian-shouzhishang-de-heimao-712539933]] adds a lighter [[YouyangZazu|《酉阳杂俎》]] connection: the episode names Li Deyu among [[DuanChengshi|段成式]]'s literary-political circle and says he appears repeatedly in the book's material field. This extends his page only as a source-context link, not as a new biographical claim.
