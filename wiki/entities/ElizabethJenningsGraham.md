# Elizabeth Jennings Graham

Elizabeth Jennings Graham appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] as the episode's public-transit civil-rights case. The source says that in 1854 New York she resisted racial exclusion on public transportation, was forcibly removed, sued, received compensation, and helped push fairer access and seating rights for Black passengers.

In the episode, Jennings Graham is not treated only as a historical anecdote. She becomes an example of [[PrincipledDisobedience|principled disobedience]] whose action turns personal humiliation and institutional unfairness into public change.
