# Glenn Hall

Glenn Hall appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as a former [[CharltonCounty]] administrator discussing why the [[FolkstonGeorgia|Folkston]] detention facility matters locally. The source says he describes the county's need for a hospital, emergency medical care, and public dollars.

Hall is also part of the episode's moral tension. He acknowledges the humanity of detained people behind barbed wire and agrees that the county should not rely on the detention center in the long term, making him a source witness for both [[DetentionAsLocalEconomicDevelopment]] and [[CarceralEconomicDependency]].
