# Rebel Alliance

The Rebel Alliance is the anti-imperial force connected to [[RogueOne|《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One]] and [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode stresses that at this stage the rebellion is not yet a clean, unified moral machine: [[LuthenRael]], [[MonMothma]], [[SawGerrera]], and other groups differ in methods, risk tolerance, and moral boundaries.

The source uses that unevenness to avoid simple hero worship. The rebellion may be necessary, but it can still involve secrecy, betrayal, funding risk, factional suspicion, and decisions that raise [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
