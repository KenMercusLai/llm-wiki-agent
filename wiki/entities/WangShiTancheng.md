# 王氏 / Woman Wang

王氏 / Woman Wang is the unnamed woman at the center of [[JonathanSpence|史景迁]]'s [[TheDeathOfWomanWang|《王氏之死》]]. [[148-wangshi-zhisi-duanjing-tuiyuan-shang-de-lishi-zhuangjing-892588934]] stresses that even her personal name is lost; she enters the record through a murder case in [[Tancheng|郯城]], where she was married into the poor Ren household, fled with another man, was abandoned, returned through a Taoist temple, and was killed by her husband.

The episode treats Wang as a negative case for [[FemaleSelfPossession]]. It marks some biographical details as inference rather than fact: she may have grown up with few family protections and little mobility, but the archive mainly shows what others did to her. Her significance in the wiki is methodological as much as biographical: she shows why [[MicrohistoryFromHostileArchives]] has to reconstruct constrained lives from law, poverty, rumor, household power, and the fantasies preserved in [[LiaozhaiZhiyi|《聊斋志异》]].
