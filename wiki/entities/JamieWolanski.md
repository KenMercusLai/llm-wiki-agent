# Jamie Wolanski

Jamie Wolanski is the game consultant in [[board-games-3-whats-in-a-name]] who translates [[SellMeASasquatch]] from an internal favorite into a retail test. Earlier in the project she praised the mechanic while warning that the name and theme would be decisive.

Her main contribution is the [[RetailShelfAppeal]] frame. She explains that shoppers may only notice a board game from a few feet away and for a few seconds, and that big-box game purchases often involve gift buying or an immediate game-night need. Her positive reaction to the Sasquatch, Bigfoot, and Yeti direction gives the [[PlanetMoney]] team confidence that the theme can work with the likely shopper.
