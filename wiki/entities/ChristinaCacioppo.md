# Christina Cacioppo

Christina Cacioppo is the [[Vanta]] founder and CEO interviewed in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]] by [[JessicaLivingston]] and [[CarolynLevy]] on [[TheSocialRadars]]. The episode traces her from Ohio and [[StanfordUniversity]] through [[UnionSquareVentures]], self-directed programming study, [[Dropbox]], and then the founding of Vanta.

The source uses Christina as a founder-path counterexample. She did not begin as a childhood programmer or immediate startup founder; she first learned from observing founders at USV, then deliberately learned to code because she felt blocked from starting a software company without the ability to build.

Her Vanta insight came from a concrete enterprise-software bottleneck. At [[Dropbox]], the [[DropboxPaper]] team hit a launch constraint because customer contracts required security and compliance commitments. Later, customer conversations led Christina back to [[SOC2Audit]] pain, and the first Vanta-like product became a spreadsheet gap assessment before it became software.
