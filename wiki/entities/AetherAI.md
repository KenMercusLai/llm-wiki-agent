# Aether AI

Aether AI is the company founded by [[HuangBiwei]] and discussed in [[na-tiao-luxian-caineng-tongwang-shijie-moxing-de-zhongju-duihua-huang-biwei-aether-ai-chuangshiren-lgg-env6jrpgvyiwtxw6bocdzdmr]]. The source presents the company as building [[CausalWorldModels]], with a first landing area in [[EmbodiedAI]] as a generalized robot brain.

## Strategy
Aether AI's route is to combine causal research, large-scale data, simulation, video-model learning, and robotics deployment rather than relying on imitation learning alone. Huang says the company uses simulated data, egocentric data, video data, and teleoperation data, with teleoperation serving as the last-mile mapping from physical regularities to robot embodiment.

## Operating Notes
- First-version training is described as needing roughly seven to eight thousand hours of data and several hundred GPUs.
- The team reportedly had about four hundred cards at the time of the episode.
- Financing is described as roughly 20 million USD, mainly for compute, data, and hiring.
- Hiring needs include causal algorithm talent, video-model training talent, and robotics full-stack talent.
