# Havana

Havana is the Cuban city setting for [[dark-times-for-cubas-economic-experiment]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode uses [[YaserGonzalezCabrera]]'s [[CityCleta]] bike-tour business in Havana to make [[Cuba]]'s macroeconomic crisis concrete: phone calls are unreliable, blackouts interrupt daily life, fuel shortages reduce movement, and foreign tourists have largely disappeared from the business.

The episode also places Havana inside the brief tourism boom created by Cuba's partial opening. [[BarackObama]]'s 2016 visit, foreign visitors, global brand events, films, and concerts made the city look like a destination that private entrepreneurs could build around, but the source later shows that same tourism dependence becoming a vulnerability when sanctions, oil stress, and the pandemic hit.
