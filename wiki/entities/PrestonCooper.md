# Preston Cooper

Preston Cooper appears in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] as an [[AmericanEnterpriseInstitute]] researcher defending the logic of graduate [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]]. The source says he estimates the new caps may affect about 30% of graduate borrowers because most borrowers already stay within the new limits.

Cooper's argument is market-pressure focused. If students can choose cheaper schools for similar degree paths, then expensive programs, including some at [[NYU]] and [[UniversityOfSouthernCalifornia|USC]], may face pressure to lower prices or justify their return on investment.
