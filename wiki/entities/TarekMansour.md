# Tarek Mansour

Tarek Mansour appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as the CEO and co-founder of [[Kalshi]]. The episode presents him as the main defender of Kalshi's [[PredictionMarketLegalBoundary|derivatives-not-gambling]] argument and of the broader [[PredictionMarketPublicGoodClaim]] that market prices can make people better informed about the future.

Tarek says the idea emerged around 2016 after he heard [[GoldmanSachs]] customers wanted ways to trade on events such as Brexit and [[DonaldTrump]]'s election. He argues Kalshi differs from a casino because it charges a percentage of trades rather than profiting directly from bettor losses, and because speculation is not the same as gambling.

The episode also uses Tarek to show the platform's regulatory strategy. Kalshi received [[CommodityFuturesTradingCommission|CFTC]] marketplace approval in November 2020, fought regulators over election markets in 2023, and treated the legal win as a catalyst for broader sports, awards, and pop-culture markets.
