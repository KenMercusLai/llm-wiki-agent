# Coyote

Coyote is [[TimFerriss]]'s tabletop analog card game discussed in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. The episode says the game took about two years to develop and was meant to be easy to learn, quick to play, and a response to screen time, loneliness, and digital malaise.

In the wiki, Coyote matters less as a game-design case than as a [[FounderIdentityDiversification]] case. Tim uses it to show how an established founder or creator can build something outside the expected menu of media, investing, or startup advice in order to learn, meet people, and keep business identity from narrowing too far.
