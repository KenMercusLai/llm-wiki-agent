# Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire is the oppressive state analyzed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] through [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]. The episode argues that the series makes the Empire feel like a real political entity: it has lazy and zealous functionaries, police incentives, intelligence services, prisons, labor systems, public spectacle, and methods for reshaping local life.

The Empire's importance in this source is concrete operation. [[Aldhani]] shows cultural displacement through [[FalseChoiceGovernance]], [[SyrilKarn]] shows [[InstitutionalOvercompliance]], [[NarkinaFive]] shows [[CarceralLaborGovernance]], and [[Ferrix]] shows how repression can generate [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]].
