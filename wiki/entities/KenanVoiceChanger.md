# Kenan Voice Changer

Kenan Voice Changer is [[LiPengcheng]]'s hackathon project from [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. The product uses AI to repair unclear speech so that people with speech difficulties can communicate more easily while they are still in rehabilitation.

The team did not win a prize because hardware failed and the project struggled on-site, but the episode says many participants remembered it. Other contestants helped the team with 3D printing and modeling, making the product a concrete [[AssistiveAI]] case and a peer-help example inside [[AIHackathons]].
