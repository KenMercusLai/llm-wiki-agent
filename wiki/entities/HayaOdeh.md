# Haya Odeh

Haya Odeh is a [[Replit]] co-founder whose role in [[tsr-s3-replit-v2-tsr-s3-replit-v2]] centers on design, user research, and the decision to treat Replit as a real company rather than a side project. The episode traces her path from graphic design in Jordan into web design, New York internationalization work, UX/UI training, and eventually the user interviews that showed how much people loved the still-simple Replit product.

The source makes Haya central to Replit's [[AccessibleProgrammingPlatform]] thesis. She pushed to talk to users, lived with the product for about a year, and framed simplicity as hard design work for technical users, non-technical users, and everyone between. Her story also anchors [[SpouseCoFounderDynamics]]: she and [[AmjadMasad]] had to manage marriage, children, investor bias, decision transparency, and the shared emotional load of building the company.
