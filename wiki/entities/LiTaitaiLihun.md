# 李太太（《离婚》） / Mrs. Li

李太太 is [[LaoLiLihun|老李]]'s wife in [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]], discussed in [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]]. The episode says Lao Li wants to leave the arranged marriage because the two share little language or romance, but it also refuses to make her only a comic obstacle.

After being brought from the countryside to Beiping, she absorbs small-city gossip, suspicion, and domestic assertiveness quickly, which intensifies Lao Li's sense of suffocation. Yet her care during Lao Li's illness complicates the marriage plot: the source makes divorce desire legible without turning the wife into a flat burden or the fantasy of exit into a pure moral solution.
