# Decimus Junius Brutus / 德奇姆斯

Decimus Junius Brutus / 德奇姆斯 is the trusted Caesar ally whose betrayal becomes operationally decisive in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]]. The episode describes him as a [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]] confidant with military merit, a gladiator force, and enough personal access to persuade Caesar to attend the Senate meeting he considered skipping.

The source uses Decimus to make the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination]] concrete. He is not only one more name in the conspirator list; he supplies the practical bridge between republican resentment and the physical arrival of Caesar at Pompey's Curia.
