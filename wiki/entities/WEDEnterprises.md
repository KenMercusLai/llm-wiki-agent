# WED Enterprises

WED Enterprises is the personal company [[WaltDisney]] formed to develop Disneyland in [[the-walt-disney-company-1]]. Walt recruited Disney artists and animators into WED to design the park, while Walt Disney Productions hesitated to fund the full project internally.

WED matters because it made the ownership and governance structure around [[Disneyland]] more complicated. The source notes that WED retained valuable rights such as trains, monorail, and name royalties before later buyouts, making it a concrete [[StartupGovernance]] and [[FinancialGravity]] case inside the Disney story.
