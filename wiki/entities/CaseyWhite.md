# Casey White

Casey White is the [[JajuPierogi]] founder/caller in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. Her Advice Line question is how to keep growing a grocery and food-service CPG company without assuming that large outside equity is the only path.

The advice she receives frames capital as a control decision. [[JeniBritton]] warns that outside investors bring opinions and reduced founder control, while [[GuyRaz]] and Britton suggest less dilutive financing and stronger advisor support as ways to prepare for later growth.
