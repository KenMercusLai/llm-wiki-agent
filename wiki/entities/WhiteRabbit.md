# White Rabbit / 白兔

The White Rabbit is the time-pressure figure who pulls [[AliceWonderlandCharacter|Alice / 爱丽丝]] into [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》 / Alice's Adventures in Wonderland]] in [[179-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-shang-975483805]]. The episode emphasizes his waistcoat, watch, glasses, and repeated worry about being late as the first sign that Wonderland's time and order do not follow ordinary rules.

The source also follows the Rabbit's later return with gloves and fan, and his mistake of treating Alice as the servant Mary Ann. This turns him from a mysterious lure into part of Wonderland's social misrecognition and procedural comedy.

## Key Claims
- The Rabbit is the portal figure for [[DreamLogicNarrative]]: he makes the impossible pursuit feel urgent enough to begin.
- His watch and lateness frame time as unstable, not only as a character quirk.
- His gloves and fan trigger another size-change episode, linking him to [[SizeChangeIdentity]].
