# Belgium

Belgium enters the wiki through [[119-anlesi-xianchang-zhengyin-shengming-ruci-zhengui-770771087]] as the assisted-death comparison case where the episode says euthanasia can extend to psychiatric patients and minors under complex processes. The source uses Belgium to make the hard edge of [[AssistedDyingSafeguards]] visible: terminal physical illness is not the only possible eligibility boundary.

The Belgian material is presented through opposing views as well as suffering cases. A father whose daughter died by suicide sees prohibition as inadequate, while a bioethics opponent argues that psychiatric patients need support, care, and treatment rather than assisted death.
