# Delicious

Delicious appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as a social bookmarking site whose Popular page influenced [[Reddit]]. [[SteveHuffman]] says Delicious Popular surfaced links from user behavior, but the material could be tedious because people bookmarked pages they wanted to return to later rather than links they wanted to discuss.

The source uses Delicious as half of Reddit's product formula: a dynamic, user-powered list from Delicious Popular combined with the comment-community energy of [[Slashdot]].
