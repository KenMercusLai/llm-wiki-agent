# Christina Tosi

Christina Tosi is the [[MilkBar]] founder and [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-christina-tosi-of-milk-bar-fd7f2406-fda8-4c1d-936c-d20210215f96]]. The episode presents her after a [[FounderRoleTransition]] away from the CEO seat and back toward creative and culinary work, where her product taste and baking experimentation can remain central to the brand.

As an advisor, she pushes founders to use customer behavior and creative constraints as operating tools. For [[TheBeauCollective]], she emphasizes pre-selling memberships, matching the Phoenix market to the Park City proof base, and negotiating landlord support. For [[CottonClara]], she looks for language broader than gifting, crafting, or wellness and suggests a maker community frame. For [[VashonIslandCoffeeDust]], she treats packaging, counter visibility, bundling, and daily ritual as ways to convert gift recipients into repeat buyers.
