# 5G Robotics

5G Robotics is the dexterous-hand company discussed in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]], distinct from the telecom standard. The episode says its second-generation direct-drive hand has 20 degrees of freedom and drew strong attention at ICRA because of dexterity, back-bending behavior, heat handling, and mechanical performance.

[[ChenZhePeter]] compares 5G's possible role to [[UnitreeRobotics]]: if it can provide stable, low-cost, reliable research hardware at scale, it may shape the data and model standards for [[DexterousManipulation]].
