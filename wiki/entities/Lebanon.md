# Lebanon

Lebanon appears in [[missing-peace-will-israel-imperil-iran-deal-6a3bae9fe1238e54b0e886c3]] as the location where a ceasefire clause could weaken the broader America-[[Iran]] deal. The episode says [[Israel]] doubts that the Lebanese government can disarm [[Hezbollah]] or stop it from rebuilding military capacity in the south.

The source's Lebanon segment is about enforcement, not just territory. If a formal ceasefire does not change what Israel believes Hezbollah can do near the border, Lebanon remains a trigger point for renewed war even while outside powers pursue a larger diplomatic bargain.
