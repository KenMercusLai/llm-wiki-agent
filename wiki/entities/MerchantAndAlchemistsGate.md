# The Merchant and the Alchemist's Gate

[[MerchantAndAlchemistsGate|《商人和炼金术之门》 / The Merchant and the Alchemist's Gate]] is the [[TedChiang|Ted Chiang / 江峰南]] time-gate story discussed in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode emphasizes its nested Arabian-Nights-like narration and its refusal to treat time travel as a way to rewrite history.

Characters can meet future or past versions of themselves, receive advice, misread temptation, and return with grief or relief. What they cannot do is erase the past. The story therefore adds a narrative form of [[PredestinedChoice]]: agency lies in understanding, repentance, and acceptance inside a fixed temporal structure.
