# Yobi

Yobi is the probiotic-based skin, scalp, and hair-care company founded by [[RuchiGupta]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. The company grew out of Gupta's effort to solve her daughter's severe eczema and cradle cap with help from dermatologists and microbiologists.

The episode frames Yobi as a trust-heavy consumer health brand. It sells through its website, Amazon, and professional channels such as spas, salons, physician offices, and mini spas. Guy and [[SusanGriffinBlack]] advise keeping DTC while building professional discovery locally, because Ruchi's medical authority and founder story can make [[MissionDrivenCustomerEducation]] and [[CustomerPull]] stronger.
