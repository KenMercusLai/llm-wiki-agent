# How I Built This

How I Built This is the podcast/show context for [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]], [[advice-line-with-jeffrey-hollender-of-seventh-generation-f8ce2410-1176-47d9-be44-03f9fa945714]], [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]], and [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. In the Justin's Nut Butter episode, [[GuyRaz]] interviews [[JustinGold]] about the founding, growth, sale, and later ownership return of [[JustinsNutButter]]. In the Jeffrey Hollender Advice Line episode, Guy and [[JeffreyHollender]] advise founders from [[25AndPine]], [[RedTruckOrchards]], and [[Petaluma]] on mission-driven consumer products, customer education, trial, and growth pace. In the e.l.f. episode, Guy interviews [[JoeyShamah]] about building [[ELFCosmetics]] through low-price beauty, media attention, retail validation, and private-equity transition. In the Susan Griffin-Black Advice Line episode, Guy and [[SusanGriffinBlack]] advise [[Yobi]], [[CultureWineCompany]], and [[CaneDogCoffee]] on relationship-led, locally focused channel growth.
