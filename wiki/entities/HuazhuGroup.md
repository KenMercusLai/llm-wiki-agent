# Huazhu Group

Huazhu Group is the hotel-chain group connected to [[JiQi]], [[HantingHotel]], and other brands in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The source presents Huazhu as a large standardized lodging supplier that can push users toward its own app or mini-program rather than rely entirely on [[Ctrip]].

The episode is ambivalent about Huazhu. It treats chain standardization as valuable because it lowers lodging uncertainty, while also citing user complaints about inconsistent quality and brand dilution. In platform terms, Huazhu matters because a large hotel group has more leverage over [[HotelPMSInventoryControl]] and direct booking than a single hotel or homestay.

## Connections
- [[JiQi]], [[HomeInns]], and [[HantingHotel]] — source path into Huazhu.
- [[Ctrip]] — OTA platform whose power Huazhu can partly resist through direct booking.
- [[HotelPMSInventoryControl]], [[HotelPlatformPricingPower]], [[TravelSuperAppConvenience]], and [[HomestayDifferentiation]] — concepts tied to hotel bargaining power.
