# Department of Education

The Department of Education appears in [[vol-113-cong-jiqian-ye-zhiku-wenjian-zhong-goule-telangpu-2-0-zhizheng-jihua-beihou-de-ren-jigou-sixiang-he-mailuo-lihnknc-3om5evexocb6a6zdwueu]] as one of the clearest institutional targets shared by [[Project2025]] and the [[RepublicanParty]] 2024 platform. The episode says conservative dissatisfaction with education focuses on federal power, progressive cultural influence, teachers' unions, higher education, and the desire to return authority to states and families.

In this source, the department is not just an education-policy agency. It is a symbol of [[AdministrativeStateDismantling]] because abolishing it would transfer programs, funding, and authority away from a federal institution that conservatives portray as captured by education associations and progressive ideology.
