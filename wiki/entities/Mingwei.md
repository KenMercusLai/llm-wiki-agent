# Mingwei

Mingwei is one of the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] contestants interviewed in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. His team explored making anime-style large heads for future embodied AI or agents, including image, 3D asset, surface-texture, and ordering flows.

The source's reusable point is that faces can act as emotional interfaces for AI bodies. Mingwei says the project became vivid when someone mounted a head on moving hardware and the crowd reacted strongly, suggesting that small physical motion plus a face can produce attention and perceived personality.
