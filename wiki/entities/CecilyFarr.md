# Cecily Farr / 西西莉·法尔

Cecily Farr / 西西莉·法尔 is a [[MarksAndCo|Marks & Co.]] staff member in the correspondence discussed by [[134-chaling-shizi-jie-84-hao-zaici-yuanliang-shijie-de-liyou-821075226]]. The episode describes her privately writing to [[HeleneHanff|Helene Hanff]] and saying the shop staff enjoy reading Helene's letters and imagining what she is like.

Cecily's role is small but structurally important. Her private letter shows that the correspondence has already escaped a two-person commercial exchange. The shop is reading, discussing, and emotionally receiving Helene as a person, which helps turn the source into a case of [[EpistolaryFriendship]] and bookshop community.
