# 梁杰

梁杰 is the economics teacher and guest in [[61-zicong-yongyou-jingjixue-de-siwei-fangshi-rensheng-dou-bian-jiandan-le-650559353]], a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode on [[TheEconomicWayOfThinking|《经济学的思维方式》 / The Economic Way of Thinking]]. The source identifies him as teaching economics at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics and as the author of an introductory economics book for beginners.

His role in the episode is pedagogical. He explains why [[PaulHeyne|Paul Heyne / 保罗·海恩]]'s book is useful for ordinary readers, why an economics textbook's many editions can signal teaching durability, and why economics should be understood as a way to reason about social order and personal choice rather than as a formula-heavy subject only for specialists.
