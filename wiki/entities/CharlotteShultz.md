# Charlotte Shultz

[[CharlotteShultz|Charlotte Shultz]] appears in [[tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3-tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3]] as [[GeorgeShultz|George Shultz]]'s wife and [[TylerShultz|Tyler Shultz]]'s step-grandmother during the Theranos legal-pressure episode. Tyler says Charlotte privately told him that Henry Kissinger thought the Theranos box did not actually exist, which showed that skepticism was present even inside George's elite network.

Charlotte's practical role is larger than a passing family detail. When Tyler was being pressed to sign an affidavit, she gave him a phone number and told him to call George's estate attorney outside. That call moved Tyler from family-and-company pressure into independent legal advice, becoming one of the episode's key breaks in [[WhistleblowerLegalIntimidation]].
