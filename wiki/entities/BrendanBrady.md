# Brendan Brady

Brendan Brady is one of the creators interviewed in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] about [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]]. In the episode, he explains the show's financing stack: [[Crave]] supplied a license fee, government-supported subsidies and tax credits supplied another layer, [[SphereAbacus]] provided a distribution advance, and the producers filled part of the gap by reinvesting fees.

Brady's role turns the source into a concrete [[ProducerOwnedIPUpside]] case. He contrasts retained rights and long-term upside with the U.S. pattern where creators may earn more upfront while giving up ownership. He also frames the audience as an [[UnderservedRomanceAudience]], arguing that media often underestimates female desire and romance readership.
