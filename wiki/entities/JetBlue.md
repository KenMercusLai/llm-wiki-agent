# JetBlue

JetBlue appears in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] as the airline whose attempted merger with [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] was blocked by the Biden administration's [[USDepartmentOfJustice|U.S. Department of Justice]] and upheld in federal court, according to the episode. The source uses the blocked deal to show that Spirit's fate was already tied to antitrust policy before the later bailout discussion.

JetBlue's role is therefore merger context rather than a full company profile. The episode frames the blocked acquisition as one possible route that did not resolve Spirit's [[BudgetAirlineCostSqueeze]], leaving later options such as another buyer or government rescue.
