# August Derleth / 阿古斯特·德雷斯

August Derleth appears in [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]] as one of the important early figures associated with [[CthulhuMythos|Cthulhu Mythos / 克苏鲁神话]]. The episode's shorthand is that [[HPLovecraft|H. P. Lovecraft / 洛夫克拉夫特]] is the founder, [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]] is the literary peak, and Derleth is the systematizer.

The page is source-scoped: this wiki records Derleth here because the episode uses him to clarify that Cthulhu literature was not born as a single closed canon. Its development depended on writers, editors, magazines, correspondence, later classification, and reader memory.

## Key Claims
- Derleth's role in the episode is primarily taxonomic and system-building.
- His mention helps separate [[OpenSharedMythos]] from modern franchise continuity.
- He provides a contrast with Smith: systematization is different from Smith's ornate poetic weirdness.
- The episode uses the Lovecraft-Smith-Derleth triad to make a quick map for Chinese listeners entering the field.
