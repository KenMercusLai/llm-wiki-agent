# Texas Public Policy Foundation

The Texas Public Policy Foundation appears in [[its-my-tree-why-cant-i-cut-it-down]] through [[ChanceWeldon]], its litigation director. In the episode, the foundation enters the [[CantonMichigan]] tree dispute as a property-rights legal advocate.

The source's interest in the foundation is not a broad institutional profile. It matters because its litigation strategy connects local [[TreeProtectionOrdinances]] to [[RegulatoryTakings]] doctrine and to the narrower requirement that permit conditions be proportional to the harms they address.
