# Aaron Cheung

Aaron Cheung is [[AdoraCheung]]'s brother and co-founder in [[PathJoy]] and [[Homejoy]], as discussed in [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]]. The episode credits him with strong customer-acquisition, ad-funnel, and customer-acquisition-cost work during Homejoy's early growth.

His role matters to the source because Homejoy began from a practical cleaning problem he experienced and then became a broader marketplace experiment. The episode also notes an investor-dynamics issue: even when Adora was CEO and could answer the questions, some investors addressed Aaron instead, leading her to attend some meetings without him so investors would focus on her leadership.
