# KLH

KLH is the European company named in [[tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128]] as building with [[CrossLaminatedTimber]] materials in a Colby College dormitory project. The source uses KLH as evidence that mass-timber construction is not only a one-home experiment after the L.A. wildfires.

For the wiki, KLH is a source-scoped institutional example in the [[FireResilientConstruction]] branch. Its role is to make [[CrossLaminatedTimber]] more legible as an off-site fabricated building system that can scale into larger projects, while still leaving cost, permitting, and local-market adoption as open questions.
