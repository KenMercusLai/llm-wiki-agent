# Hakan Fidan

Hakan Fidan is one of the possible Erdogan successors discussed in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]]. The source describes him as a former head of Turkey's intelligence agency, a former development-agency leader, and the current foreign minister at the time of the episode.

The episode says a December survey put Fidan ahead of other successor names among Turks who wanted a new [[JusticeAndDevelopmentParty]] leader. His public appeal is contrasted with [[BilalErdogan]]'s family proximity and dynastic-liability problem.
