# Saw Gerrera

Saw Gerrera appears in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] as one of the rebel faction figures in [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]. The episode uses him to show that the anti-[[GalacticEmpire]] side is fragmented by ideology, trust, tactics, and willingness to cooperate.

His conversation with [[LuthenRael]] is important because it forces the phrase "for the greater good" into a specific moral situation: not self-sacrifice, but accepting the death of another rebel group. That makes him a node in the source's [[SacrificingOthersEthics]] cluster.
