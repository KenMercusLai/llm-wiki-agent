# Larry Baker

Larry Baker appears in [[riding-with-the-repo-man-update]] as the Ohio repo man whose work gives the episode its ground-level view of [[AutoRepossession]]. In the 2019 reporting he searches for delinquent cars at night, balancing lender instructions against the risk of confrontation and his own judgment about when taking a vehicle is too harsh.

The 2026 update says Baker had retired from the business, but his later account still anchors the changing repossession environment. He describes more desperate borrowers, more attempts to block or hide cars, and a family member being shot while trying to repossess a car. That makes him the source's practical witness for how [[CarAffordabilityStress]], delinquency, and recovery technology show up as physical risk.
