# Wes Harrell

Wes Harrell appears in [[the-supreme-court-struck-down-a-bunch-of-trumps-tariffs-now-what]] as the broker describing the market for potential tariff-refund claims. He says hundreds of millions of dollars in deals had already been done before the Supreme Court ruling, making him the source's entry point into [[TariffRefundClaimsMarket]].

The episode uses Harrell to show how legal uncertainty can become a tradable financial asset. Importers could sell potential claims for cash upfront, while buyers accepted legal complexity, delay, and collection risk in exchange for a discounted claim.
