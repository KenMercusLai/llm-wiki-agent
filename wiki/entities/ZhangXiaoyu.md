# Zhang Xiaoyu / 张潇雨

Zhang Xiaoyu is one of the speakers in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]]. The episode presents him as the personal and narrative bridge: he starts from friendship, podcasting, emotional state, missed opportunities, and his earlier search for a market-crash strategy, then helps translate [[XuZhe]]'s technical account of [[BlackSwan]], [[Antifragility]], and options into a life question.

His strongest contribution is the move from markets to [[LifeAntifragility]]. He treats writing, podcasting, public exposure, and meeting people as small-downside ways to invite positive black swans, while also naming health, money, relationships, and bad partners as areas where unacceptable downside should be closed first.
