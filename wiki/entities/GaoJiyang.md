# Gao Jiyang

Gao Jiyang is the founder of [[Xinghaitu]] and the central guest in [[132-dui-xinghaitu-chuangshiren-gaojiyang-de-3-xiaoshi-fangtan]]. The source presents his path as a sequence from physics competition and Tsinghua training, through early deep-learning exposure at [[SenseTime]], engineering work at [[Waymo]], mass-production autonomous-driving work at [[Momenta]], and finally [[EmbodiedAI]] entrepreneurship.

## Key Views
- AI should be applied where it is a bottom-level variable of the industry, which is why autonomous driving became attractive before robotics.
- High-performing technical work still depends on induction, decomposition, measurement, schedule discipline, and practical execution.
- [[Waymo]] gave him strong engineering training, while [[Momenta]] gave him customer-facing, result-oriented operator training.
- [[Xinghaitu]] has to build both the robot body and the intelligence layer because the whole machine is the product and the data-collection carrier.
- The long-term moat in robotics comes from [[PhysicalWorldDataFlywheel]], [[RealRobotDataStrategy]], and the ability to turn machines into useful production workers.
- Algorithm innovation matters, but it has to be judged inside [[EmbodiedAIValueChain]] and by customer value rather than by isolated publication or demo speed.
