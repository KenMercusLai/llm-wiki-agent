# Wayfinder Ventures

Wayfinder Ventures is [[YuriSagalov]]'s investment firm in [[tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final-tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final]]. The episode says Sagalov moved into investing after [[AeroFS]], a [[YCombinator]] visiting-partner period, and years of informal angel investing with small LP pools.

Wayfinder's source role is to connect founder experience with early B2B investing. Sagalov describes the fund as writing friendly checks, focusing on B2B startups, and helping founders with [[FounderLedSales]] from zero to roughly $1 million in revenue. His advice that founders should ask investors for references, including from difficult outcomes, makes Wayfinder part of the wiki's [[InvestorReferenceChecking]] and founder-investor-quality branch.
