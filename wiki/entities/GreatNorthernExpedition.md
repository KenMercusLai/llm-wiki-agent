# Great Northern Expedition / 大北方探险

The Great Northern Expedition is the enlarged second Bering expedition in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. What [[VitusBering]] hoped would be a better-equipped voyage toward America became a multi-thousand-person [[RussianEmpire|Russian imperial]] program including ships for America and Japan, scientific teams, mapping, settlement building, schools, shipyards, mines, roads, and administrative prestige.

The episode treats this expansion as a source of failure risk. [[ExpeditionCommandFragmentation]] put scientists, officers, committees, and bureaucrats into overlapping authority structures, while [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]] kept food, labor, ships, and transport under strain. The later wreck and survival on [[BeringIsland]] make the expedition's ambitions and limits visible in one place.
