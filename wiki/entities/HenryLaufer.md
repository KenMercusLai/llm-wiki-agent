# Henry Laufer

Henry Laufer appears in [[vol-103-wenyi-fuxing-keji-ximengsi-de-fengshen-zhilu-shi-lianghua-zhiwang-gengshi-dongcha-renxing-de-dashi-lulzvnaxdb4klqag-p2yyqlmiikl]] as the [[RenaissanceTechnologies]] figure associated with moving the [[MedallionFund]] toward a single integrated model. The source treats that move as a major step because one system could coordinate many markets, signals, correlations, and constraints instead of leaving separate models to compete independently.

In the episode's telling, Laufer's contribution turns diversification from a portfolio slogan into a modeling architecture. A shared data and model layer made it easier to compare signals, control overlap, and find small effects across asset classes.
