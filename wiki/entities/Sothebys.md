# Sotheby's

Sotheby's appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as the auction house whose bidder data supports the episode's claim that younger buyers are entering Old Masters sales. The source says the share of bidders under 40 in Old Masters sales this year is nearly triple the share from five years earlier.

In the wiki, Sotheby's is mainly an evidence node for [[OldMastersMarketRevival]]. Its importance is not only price, but audience composition: younger bidder participation suggests the category is being reframed rather than merely recycled among established collectors.
