# Han Boxiao

Han Boxiao is the vivo X-series product leader interviewed in [[ai-shidai-de-chaoji-rukou-haishi-shouji-ma-s10e17-523a0d42-4c16-4dd6-a2ab-9277fec1a731]]. He explains [[Vivo]]'s view that phones remain the central AI entry point and that product scenarios should pull technology requirements rather than follow raw hardware specifications.

His contribution to the source is the terminal-product perspective. He argues that foldables must justify their extra cost through larger-screen productivity, that vivo and [[MediaTek]] now cooperate earlier in chip definition, and that terminal-side AI must be implemented across hardware, model, system, and application layers.
