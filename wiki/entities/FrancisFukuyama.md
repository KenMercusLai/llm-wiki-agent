# Francis Fukuyama / 福山

Francis Fukuyama / 福山 is named in [[jiaqi-moyu-geng-jiankang-574391976]] as a possible future political-theory author for [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]]. The hosts say they might avoid 《历史的终结与最后的人》 because it is unavailable, but may discuss the newer [[IdentityPoliticsBook|《身份政治》]] because it speaks to current social conflict and the need to understand concrete people.

The source is only a programming preview, not a treatment of Fukuyama's thought. This page should therefore keep claims tied to the episode unless a later source reads the books directly.
