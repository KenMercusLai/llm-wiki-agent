# Cicero / 西塞罗

Cicero / 西塞罗 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as an orator, statesman, and republican witness to the late Roman crisis. The episode notes his role in suppressing the Catiline conspiracy and his later sorrow over the disappearance of republican liberty, even as his personal relation with [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]] could become friendlier.

The source uses Cicero to show the limits of elite republican politics. He was not brought into the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination conspiracy]], partly because conspirators saw him as insufficiently bold, but after Caesar's death he briefly joined the search for compromise before the conflict moved beyond him.

Cicero's eventual killing by Antony's side underscores the episode's claim that the assassination did not restore the [[RomanRepublic|Roman Republic]]. The republic's language survived, but armed successors decided the outcome.
