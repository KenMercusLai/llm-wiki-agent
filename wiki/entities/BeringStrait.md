# Bering Strait / 白令海峡

Bering Strait appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] as the geographic proof target of the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]]. The episode says [[VitusBering]] reached the open waters later named for him and turned back after discussion, a decision criticized in hindsight but framed by the source as a survival-oriented judgment under limited information.

The strait matters in the episode because it condenses several questions: whether Asia and America were connected, whether the [[RussianEmpire]] could find a northern/eastern route toward trade, and how cautious navigation under uncertainty differs from retrospective hero scoring.
