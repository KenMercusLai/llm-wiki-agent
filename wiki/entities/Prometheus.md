# Prometheus / 普罗米修斯

Prometheus appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] through the subtitle of [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]], "The Modern Prometheus." The episode presents Prometheus as a Romantic-era creation and fire figure whose mythic energy could be rewritten through experiment, electricity, and scientific ambition.

In the wiki, Prometheus anchors [[ModernPrometheusReading]]: a myth about giving fire or creating humanity becomes a literary structure for asking what a maker owes to what is made, especially when the maker's power outpaces care.
