# 12gigs

12gigs is the first real paying [[Amplitude]] customer in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]]. The source describes it as a mobile slots casino gaming company whose buyer asked [[SpenserSkates]] how much Amplitude cost after seeing the product.

The pricing moment is the page's main relevance. Spenser nearly priced the product at $50 per month, remembered advice to charge more, asked for $1,000 per month, and learned from the buyer's reaction that the product was still cheap for a customer with a serious analytics pain. The episode makes 12gigs a compact [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]] and [[CustomerEvidenceStrategy]] signal.
