# Google Inactive Account Manager

Google Inactive Account Manager appears in [[tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128]] as [[TomasHoloneck]]'s example of a design intervention for digital remains. The episode describes it as a [[Google]] account feature that can delete an account after a set period of inactivity.

Its role in the wiki is conceptual rather than product-review oriented. The feature shows that [[DigitalMemorialization]] and [[DigitalPreservation]] are not always about saving everything: deletion, expiration, and account-inactivity planning can also be forms of respect for privacy and future burden.
