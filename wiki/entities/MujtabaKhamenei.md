# Mujtaba Khamenei

Mujtaba Khamenei appears in [[the-mourning-show-the-politics-of-khameneis-funeral-6a4b75084955fc4dc12ffcc7]] as [[AliKhamenei]]'s son, described by the episode as the supposed successor and current supreme leader. His absence from public view after his father's killing is the segment's central succession signal.

The episode says Islamic convention would normally have him leading the funeral prayers, so his absence is politically costly even if officials explain it as a security precaution. In the wiki's broader politics branch, he extends [[AutocraticSuccession]] from Turkey's party-managed succession problem into a theocratic and dynastic uncertainty case.
