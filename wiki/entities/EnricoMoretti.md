# Enrico Moretti

Enrico Moretti appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through the episode's local multiplier discussion. The source cites his estimate that each manufacturing job creates 1.6 additional local jobs over 10 years, while an average tech job creates five additional jobs.

His role is to discipline the regional-revitalization claim. Manufacturing can matter for [[TradableSectorLocalGrowth]] and local services, but [[ManufacturingRegionalMultiplier]] is not automatically larger than the multiplier attached to other high-value tradable sectors.
