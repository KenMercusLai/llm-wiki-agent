# Clark (Creo)

Clark is presented in [[e238-liaoliao-harness-shidai-ai-first-de-zuzhi-jiagou-cong-xinren-ren-dao-xinren-ai-51260de8-60ef-4b76-b3e5-2e559c4a0923]] as a [[Creo]] co-founder responsible for go-to-market. He supplies the market-side version of the episode's [[AIFirstOrganization]] thesis: once engineering output accelerates, the bottleneck can move to finding market demand, deciding which features are ready to sell, and judging whether agent-generated go-to-market work is good.

Clark also discusses broader data access for agents inside a company. He treats faster data querying and cross-team context as valuable, while acknowledging that broad read/write authority requires better [[AgentPermissionBoundaries]], [[AgentIdentityAndAuthentication]], and governance.
