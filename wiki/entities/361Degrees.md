# 361 Degrees

361 Degrees is a Chinese sportswear brand discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as one of the domestic companies experimenting with ultra-light racing-shoe concepts. The source places it within the broader Chinese running-shoe effort to catch foreign brands in materials, foaming, weight reduction, and performance credibility.

## Key Claims
- 361 Degrees is part of the source's domestic super-shoe comparison alongside [[LiNing]] and [[QiaodanSports]].
- The episode suggests that Chinese brands are no longer absent from top-end racing-shoe experimentation.
- The next step is not only lighter shoes, but better-tested, stable products that elite and ordinary runners can trust.
