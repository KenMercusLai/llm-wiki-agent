# 《今昔续百鬼·云》 / Konjaku Zoku Hyakki: Kumo

《今昔续百鬼·云》 is the [[KyogokuNatsuhiko|京极夏彦]] book used by [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]] as an accessible entrance into the [[HyakkiYagyoSeries|百鬼夜行系列]]. The episode chooses it because its medium-length stories are shorter, gentler, and less spoiler-sensitive than the main long novels.

The source presents the book as an early timeline point in the Kyogokudo universe: [[ChuzenjiAkihiko|中禅寺秋彦]] has not yet fully become the central detective figure, while [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]] and [[NumagamiRenji|沼上蓮次]] carry the fieldwork and comic misadventure structure.

## Key Claims
- The book gives new readers a lower-risk way to sample Kyogoku's mixture of folklore research, mystery plotting, humor, and human aftercare.
- Its first story, [[AnganKozo|《暗眼小僧》]], demonstrates the source's core pattern: a supposed yokai incident becomes a human, social, and interpretive problem.
- The episode says the later stories include 泥田坊, a hand-with-eye yokai, and 寄生佛, with 寄生佛 presented as closer to horror mystery and linked to [[ChuzenjiAkihiko|中禅寺秋彦]]'s appearance.
