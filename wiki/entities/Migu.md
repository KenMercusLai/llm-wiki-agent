# 咪咕 / Migu

Migu appears in [[vol-264-ba-shijiebei-zuowei-fangfa-999416048]] as one of the recurring Chinese platform buyers for World Cup streaming rights. The episode names Migu as a live-rights buyer in 2018, a buyer again in 2022, and one of the two named 2026 buyers alongside [[Xiaohongshu]].

In the source, Migu's role helps show that sports rights are not only media inventory. They can also be used by telecom-linked or platform businesses to gain attention, test streaming capacity, and participate in national-scale live-event consumption.
