# O-Design / Odyssey

O-Design, also referred to through Odyssey in [[ai4s-xuyao-kuangren-yu-yexinjia-duihua-yinglingdian-odin-ruguo-shen-cunzai-wo-zenneng-rongren-ziji-bushi-shen-gonglu-boke-lhceyip6dqomrwk38uvqjwoomxyz]], is [[HaotianOdin]]'s early all-modal molecular-design exploration before [[YinglingdianAI]] matured around a platform company. The source describes it as an open-source project where one model could attempt design across small molecules, RNA, DNA, and proteins.

Its wiki importance is not that the source proves production-grade capability. The episode says the project had simple downstream validation and promising results, and uses a student handling multiple targets to show how AI may compress some engineering labor in scientific work. In that narrower sense, O-Design is an early demonstration node for [[AllModalMolecularWorldModel]], [[AIDrugDiscoveryPlatform]], and [[ScientificDiscoveryAutomation]] rather than a fully validated drug platform.
