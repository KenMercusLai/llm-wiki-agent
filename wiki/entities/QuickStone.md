# QuickStone

The episode frames QuickStone's AI interest around [[ProactiveAgents]], agent infrastructure, and hardware that gives agents richer context. Yihao also describes AI-native fund work where projects, people, and relationships are structured with help from [[ClaudeCode]] and similar agent tools.
