# 水仙

水仙 is a former [[YiLaoYongYi]] producer and college-student voice in [[ep36-di-yi-pi-you-biye-jiaolv-de-00-hou-kaishi-xuehui-yong-shixi-yinzhen-zhike-lj5s97tbtj2gly7oppujgsv3cgk8]]. Her account centers on repeated internships, resume pressure, uncertainty about future direction, and the anxiety relief that comes from staying busy.

## Key Claims
- She describes internships as partly necessary because graduation competition makes it hard to leave a resume empty.
- Her "饮鸩止渴" framing captures how work can calm [[GraduationAnxiety]] without resolving the underlying choice problem.
- She values [[BigCompanyHalo]] because a recognizable company name can help the next application even when the career goal is not yet clear.
- Her questions about messages, mentors, gifts, mistakes, peers, and bosses expose the [[WorkplaceHiddenRules]] that schools often do not teach directly.
