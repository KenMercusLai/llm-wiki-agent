# Heinrich Blücher / 海因里希·布鲁歇尔

Heinrich Blücher / 海因里希·布鲁歇尔 appears in [[103-ta-shi-cichuan-yewu-de-guang-ta-shi-hanna-alunte-737939789]] as [[HannahArendt|Hannah Arendt]]'s second husband and exile partner. The episode presents him as a self-educated German worker-class socialist who had participated in anti-Nazi action before being forced into Paris exile.

Blücher's relationship with Arendt is contrasted with [[MartinHeidegger|Heidegger]]'s secrecy and power imbalance. The source describes the Arendt-Blücher bond as more reliable because it was built on trust, independent space, shared danger, and practical mutual rescue during [[StatelessExile]] from [[France]] to the [[UnitedStates]].
