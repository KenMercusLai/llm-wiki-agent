# ICE

ICE is the guest in [[139-paopao-mate-he-pinduoduo-zhide-touzi-me-feng-tou-quan-1-139-1]]. The episode introduces him as an investor and educator with experience across U.S. study and work, consulting, financial data, asset management, domestic enterprise management, and entrepreneurship.

In 2023, ICE founded [[ShengshiInvestmentAcademy]] to teach fundamental investing by combining investment experience with company-operating experience. In this episode, he uses [[PopMart]] and [[Pinduoduo]] to show why investors need to unpack slogans, define their time horizon, identify the main contradiction, and match the game to their own temperament.
