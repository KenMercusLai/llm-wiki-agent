# Haotian Odin / 浩天

Haotian Odin, referred to as Odin in [[ai4s-xuyao-kuangren-yu-yexinjia-duihua-yinglingdian-odin-ruguo-shen-cunzai-wo-zenneng-rongren-ziji-bushi-shen-gonglu-boke-lhceyip6dqomrwk38uvqjwoomxyz]], is the founder of [[YinglingdianAI|英灵殿]]. The episode describes him as 26 years old, trained at Zhejiang University and the University of Washington, and formerly connected to [[BakerLab]] before returning to China to build [[ODesign|O-Design / Odyssey]] and then the company.

The source presents him as both an [[AIForScience]] technical founder and a strongly self-stylized founder. Technically, he argues for an [[AllModalMolecularWorldModel]] across small molecules, proteins, RNA, and DNA, with [[AIDrugDiscoveryPlatform]] work as the near-term commercial form and [[ScientificDiscoveryAutomation]] as the long-term aspiration. Personally, he frames entrepreneurship as dissatisfaction turned into responsibility: leave prestigious paths when they become a "jade lock," keep doing the right thing, and resist financing narratives that pull the company away from its real scientific question.

This page uses `HaotianOdin` to distinguish the founder from the existing [[Odin|Odin / 奥丁]] mythology page. The source itself deliberately plays with the name: Odin explains that his English name connects both to the sound of his Chinese name 浩天 and to the Norse god, while [[YinglingdianAI|英灵殿]] borrows the [[Valhalla|Valhalla / 瓦尔哈拉]] image of warriors gathering for a final battle.
