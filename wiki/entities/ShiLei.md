# 时雷 / Shi Lei

时雷 is referenced in [[vol-105-ruhe-panduan-yige-touzi-zuhe-shifou-shihe-ziji-lmaowq8820pa0jyjw6z93b6hstpe]] as the person behind [[ShifenXiyin|十分吸引]] and as a source for the phrase "复杂适应性系统" in the episode's portfolio discussion. The host says Shi Lei has tried to localize [[BridgewaterAssociates|Bridgewater]]-style all-weather thinking for Chinese-market asset allocation.

The episode treats Shi Lei's framework as intellectually useful but difficult for many ordinary investors. Its own contribution is to translate that adaptation idea into the simpler rules of diversification, liquidity, limited turnover, and [[PortfolioSuitability]].
