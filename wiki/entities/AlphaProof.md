# AlphaProof

AlphaProof is the [[GoogleDeepMind]] AI-for-math system referenced in [[137-dui-hong-letong-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-ai-for-math-ba-shuxue-biancheng-lean-shuxue-tianshu-zhong-de-zhengming-zhijue-bei-chuangzao-yu-bei-faxian-de-lha-faiwxtget0qmbcosts3cb5vb]].
