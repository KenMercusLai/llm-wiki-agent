# Zach Edwards

Zach Edwards is the [[SilentPush|Silent Push]] speaker quoted in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. He says smaller brands and e-commerce operations are being targeted by impostor websites, and that scammers can use bought ads or social targeting rather than relying only on unsolicited messages.

His role in the wiki is to connect [[FakeRetailWebsiteImpersonation]] to brand operations. The episode uses his comments to show that a luxury or niche brand may first learn about a fake site when customers report lost money, making [[BrandImpersonationMonitoring]] a practical business problem.
