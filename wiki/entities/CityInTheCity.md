# 城中之城

城中之城 is the Chinese financial drama discussed in [[ep26-xiang-zuo-ren-shang-zhi-ren-que-kun-zai-cheng-zhong-zhi-cheng-lgbvd08kgko5onekgvnu4aovfz6t]]. The episode uses the show as a narrative hook for testing which bank workplace scenes feel real and which are compressed or distorted for drama.

[[YiLaoYongYi]] does not treat the drama as a documentary. The hosts use characters such as Zhao Hui, Tao Wuji, Su Jianren, and Miao Che to discuss [[BankOrganizationalHierarchy]], [[BankInternalAudit]], [[BankDueDiligence]], [[WorkplaceRelationshipBoundaries]], boss resources, internal transfers, and career self-protection inside finance work.
