# Ronnen Harary

Ronnen Harary is the [[SpinMaster]] co-founder and [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The episode presents him as an operator who still spends part of his time on Spin Master board work and special projects, including a worldwide AI studio, while also promoting his book *No Experience Necessary*.

As an advisor, Ronnen repeatedly pushes founders to separate the brand's durable promise from the literal form it currently takes. With [[YearlyCo]], he points out that the name does not say gold, leaving room for non-gold milestone products. With [[IslandBeeCompany]], he challenges [[FelixCollin]] to decide whether fear of fast growth is hiding a larger consumer-products ambition. With [[WanderingSoulBeer]], he treats workspace and schedule boundaries as an operating system for founder creativity rather than only a personal wellness issue.
