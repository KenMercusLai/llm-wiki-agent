# Jove

Jove is the [[Cresta]] FDE leader interviewed in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]]. He defines [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] work as staying close enough to customers to make AI applications run in production, then feeding deployment lessons back into product. His account emphasizes strong engineering ability, AI-agent testing experience, customer-facing judgment, and enough seniority to work directly with CTOs, IT directors, and business leaders.
