# Tony Gilroy

Tony Gilroy is the creator figure highlighted in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] for his work on [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] and [[RogueOne|《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One]]. The episode praises him less for franchise management than for noticing political and institutional details that many genre works flatten.

In the source, Gilroy's importance is that he can turn bureaucracy, local policing, intelligence work, prisons, and moral compromise into entertaining drama. That places him behind the episode's concepts of [[FalseChoiceGovernance]], [[InstitutionalOvercompliance]], [[CarceralLaborGovernance]], and [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
