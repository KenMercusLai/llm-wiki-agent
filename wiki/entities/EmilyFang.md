# Emily Fang

Emily Fang is a source voice in [[battlefield-rare-earths-how-the-us-lost-to-china]] explaining the China side of the rare-earth story. The episode uses her comments to place [[Molycorp]]'s earlier openness to Chinese visitors in historical context, when China was not yet perceived as the economic competitor it later became.

Fang also frames the current policy turn. The source says [[China]] limited exports of some rare earths after the April 2025 tariff confrontation, and Fang describes the [[UnitedStates]] response - loans, grants, equity stakes, and price-floor coordination - as resembling the state-driven planning China used to dominate the industry.
