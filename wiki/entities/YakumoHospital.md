# 八云医院

八云医院 appears in [[137-sangeng-banye-juran-yao-chi-xiangjiao-shide-zailai-yigen-835431951]] as the institution where [[ShikanoYasuaki|鹿野靖明]] spent part of childhood and adolescence. The episode uses it as the institutional counterpoint to later [[DisabilityIndependentLiving]]: it keeps children alive and medically watched, but also exposes them to death, euphemism, observation, and loss of everyday autonomy.

The hospital matters in the source because 鹿野's later conflicts with doctors and insistence on returning home are not only stubbornness. They are tied to a life history in which medical institutions could decide, conceal, and manage too much of a disabled person's world.

## Key Claims
- 八云医院 is presented as both a place of care and a place where subjectivity can be narrowed by institutional routine.
- The death of peers and the euphemistic handling of death help explain 鹿野's distrust of medical authority.
- The later respirator and discharge conflicts are more legible when read against this early institutional background.
