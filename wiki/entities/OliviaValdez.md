# Olivia Valdez

Olivia Valdez appears in [[tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128]] as [[CarlyUrban]]'s co-author and an experimental psychologist. The episode credits her with pushing the idea that people taking surveys on phones may not be paying close attention, turning a puzzling financial-literacy trend into a device-effect research question.

Her role matters because the source's answer is not only about personal finance. It turns [[FinancialLiteracyMeasurement]] into a broader [[SurveyModeEffects]] problem where attention, fatigue, effort, and device context can change what a knowledge survey appears to measure.
