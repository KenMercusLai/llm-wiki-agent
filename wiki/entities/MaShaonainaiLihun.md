# 马少奶奶（《离婚》） / Ma Shaonainai

马少奶奶 is the young neighbor in [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]] who becomes [[LaoLiLihun|老李]]'s imagined poetry and moon in [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]]. She lives with her mother-in-law after Ma Shaoye leaves under the sign of revolution and free love.

The episode treats her less as a developed romance partner than as a test of Lao Li's fantasy. When Ma Shaoye returns in a vulgar and self-serving form and Ma Shaonainai reconciles with him, Lao Li's imagined alternative life collapses. This makes her central to [[RomanticProjectionTrap]] and to the source's broader claim that escape fantasies can be as unreliable as the ordinary world they oppose.
