# Traction Tools

Traction Tools is the competing EOS-related software product discussed in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. [[MarkAbbott]] says another implementer and a software client pursued a similar idea after he had begun discussing his software vision in the EOS community.

## Key Points
- Entered a license with [[EOSWorldwide]] while [[Ninety]] initially remained "EOS compatible."
- Served as a competitive reference point for Ninety's product design, license decisions, and positioning.
- Abbott says Ninety sought to differentiate through UI/UX, service quality, and a broader long-term platform vision.
