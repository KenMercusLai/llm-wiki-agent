# Beeper

Beeper is the universal-chat app [[EricMigicovsky]] discusses briefly in [[tsr-s4-ericm-v2-tsr-s4-ericm-v2]]. The source presents it as Eric's post-[[Pebble]] company, a [[YCombinator]] Summer 2021 startup, and a side project that began during COVID because he wanted one place to communicate across many chat networks.

The episode does not provide a full Beeper operating history; the hosts explicitly say they want a future part two for that story. Its value in this ingest is as a second example of [[BuildForYourselfFounderFit]]: Eric again begins from a tool he personally wants, this time around fragmented social and work messaging rather than wrist notifications.
