# 吴清源

吴清源 appears in [[172-haoyun-shi-shenme-weisha-shuo-zong-bilei-hui-bai-haoyun-961254860]] as the Go example for seeing from a different level. The episode says his play looked strange or unreasonable in its own time, and later became easier to understand when compared with [[AlphaGo]]-style analysis.

The page is source-scoped: Wu is not being summarized as a full historical Go figure here. He is used as an analogy for how [[LuckAsInformationBandwidth]] can depend on frame shifts, not simply more effort inside the old frame.
