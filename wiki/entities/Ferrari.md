# Ferrari

Ferrari is the F1 team and luxury car brand used in [[formula-1-1]] as an early legitimacy and brand-power case for [[FormulaOne]]. The source emphasizes that Ferrari is the only team to have participated in every F1 season and that its racing heritage reinforced a celebrity-linked luxury road-car business.

Ferrari also functions as a contrast with the British constructor cluster. While much of F1 developed around UK-based engineering talent, Ferrari gave the sport continuity, glamour, and a distinctive national/luxury identity.

## Source Position
- Ferrari's continuous participation made it central to F1's legitimacy.
- The brand converted racing credibility into luxury aspiration and road-car demand.
- Ferrari's importance gave it special weight inside [[LeagueStakeholderAlignment]] and team-distribution politics.
