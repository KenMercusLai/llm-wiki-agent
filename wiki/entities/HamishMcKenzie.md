# Hamish McKenzie

Hamish McKenzie is presented in [[tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov-tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov]] as the writer and later [[Substack]] co-founder who pushed [[ChrisBest]] from media criticism toward company formation. Best says he sent Hamish an essay draft about broken internet media incentives, and Hamish responded that the critique was not new unless they could answer what should be done about it.

The source makes Hamish important because he embodied the first customer problem. Best says Hamish was the kind of writer who understood the need for a better publishing and payment route, which helped turn the abstract manifesto into a practical paid newsletter product.
