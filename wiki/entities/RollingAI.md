# Rolling AI

Rolling AI is the enterprise AI consulting and implementation company discussed in [[openai-he-anthropic-gongtong-kanhao-de-fde-ai-shidai-de-xin-gangwei-chuxian-jiu-fengong-songdong-duitan-rolling-ai-ljlatrjimrlnbe-luqmat0c74xo6]]. Partners [[Agan]] and [[LiuKai]] describe the company as working less like a software vendor and more like a business builder that deploys agents and [[DigitalEmployees]] into customer workflows.

## Position In The Episode
- The company reports serving nearly one hundred enterprise customers since the GPT-3.5 API period, with current work focused on annual large-client relationships.
- Its method emphasizes [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] work: business integration, knowledge governance, system connection, rapid prototyping, and agent orchestration.
- Case examples include a dairy and nutrition platform, chain-store revenue forecasting, and a rental-platform property-manager workflow.
- The guests position Rolling AI as [[ServiceAsSoftware]], where software or agents carry an ongoing service and business-outcome promise.
