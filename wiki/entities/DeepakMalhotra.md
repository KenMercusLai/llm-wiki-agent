# Deepak Malhotra / 马尔霍特拉

Deepak Malhotra is named in [[106-bie-zai-haipa-chongtu-kuai-lai-xuexi-tanpan-shu-742539645]] as one of the authors associated with [[HarvardClassicNegotiation|《哈佛经典谈判术》]]. The episode uses the book to argue that negotiation skill is a learnable management and decision practice rather than a personality gift.

Within this source, Malhotra's relevance is the method frame: negotiators should identify [[BATNA]], calculate [[ReservationValue]], estimate the other side's alternatives, and use [[InterestBasedNegotiation]] to discover why stated demands matter.
