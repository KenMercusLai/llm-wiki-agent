# Recep Tayyip Erdogan

Recep Tayyip Erdogan is Turkey's dominant political figure in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]]. The episode says he has ruled since 2003, first as prime minister and then as president, and now faces a succession question because age, term limits, possible constitutional routes, and a possible snap election all affect whether he can stand again.

The source presents Erdogan's position as stronger than that of any visible successor. It says his camp may prefer another Erdogan run because [[JusticeAndDevelopmentParty]] figures such as [[HakanFidan]] and [[BilalErdogan]] carry different legitimacy, loyalty, and public-appeal problems.
