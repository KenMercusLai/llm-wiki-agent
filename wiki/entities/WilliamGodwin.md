# William Godwin / 威廉·戈德温

William Godwin appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]]'s father and as a radical thinker whose household gave Shelley unusual access to books, visitors, and political arguments. The source treats the Godwin home as an intellectual environment rather than only a family drama.

Godwin's remarriage and complex blended family also matter in the episode. They make Shelley's education rich but emotionally pressured, linking the later [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] imagination to both radical public thought and private household insecurity.
