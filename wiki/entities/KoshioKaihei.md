# 小塩海平 / 小盐海平

小塩海平 is the Japanese plant scientist and author behind [[KafunshoToJinrui|《花粉症与人类》 / 花粉症と人類]] as used in [[97-huafen-zheng-yu-renlei-cong-niandeteren-aqiu-dao-kongqi-jinghuaqi-728113059]]. The episode introduces him as a pollen-allergy sufferer and long-term researcher of hay fever, using his book to connect plant science, immunology, environmental history, and modern consumer infrastructure.

Within the wiki, his role is less as a biographical subject and more as a bridge figure: he makes [[PollenAsHistoricalObject|pollen]] readable across botany, [[PalynologyAsEvidence|palynology]], [[HayFeverEnvironmentalHistory|disease history]], and [[AllergySourceControl|source-control policy]].
