# Bob Bozeman

Bob Bozeman appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as a general partner in Angel Investors One whom [[RonConway]] brought to [[Google]]'s 165 University Avenue office. Conway asked Bozeman to test whether [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]]'s search engine was meaningfully different from existing search products.

Bozeman's thumbs-up during the meeting became Conway's technical-validation signal. The episode uses him as an example of how early investors sometimes combined network access with quick expert checks before committing to [[OutlierDrivenAngelInvesting]] decisions.
