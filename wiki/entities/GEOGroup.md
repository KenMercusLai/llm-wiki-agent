# GEO Group

GEO Group appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the private operator of the large ICE detention facility in [[FolkstonGeorgia|Folkston, Georgia]]. The source says GEO Group began operating the former state prison as an immigration detention center in 2017.

The source ties GEO Group to [[PrivateImmigrationDetention]] through scale and contracts: the Folkston facility expanded from 1,100 beds to capacity for up to 3,000 people, while GEO Group had a $96 million federal contract and the county/city received about $1 million.
