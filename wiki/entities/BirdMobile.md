# Bird Mobile

Bird Mobile, or 波导, is a first-generation domestic Chinese handset brand in [[no-208-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-shang-motuoluola-nuojiya-he-ailixin-de-zhushen-huanghun-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-23-998056376]]. The episode traces Bird from pagers in Ningbo/Fenghua to its 1999 mobile-phone production license, 2000 listing, Li Wen endorsement, and the slogan remembered as "手机中的战斗机."

## Source Position
- Bird represents the licensed, channel-driven first wave of domestic phones before [[MediaTek]] turnkey designs and [[ShanzhaiPhones]] made entry much cheaper.
- Its success shows how local brand marketing, distribution, and regulatory access mattered when handset manufacturing was still gated by licenses and foreign/OEM designs.
- The episode also links Bird indirectly to later global emerging-market handset strategy because overseas executive Zhu Zhaojiang later built Transsion in Africa with a team rooted in Bird.
