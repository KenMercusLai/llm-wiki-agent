# Cindy McCaffrey

In the source, McCaffrey belongs to Google's transition from technical breakthrough to company. [[PageRankSearchRelevance]] and [[SearchQualityOperatingCadence]] mattered, but Google also needed marketing, business development, and distribution support as it built its own brand.
