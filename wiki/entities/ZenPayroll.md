# ZenPayroll

ZenPayroll is the original name of [[Gusto]] in [[tsr-s4-gusto-v3-tsr-s4-gusto-v3]]. [[EddieKim]] bought the domain shortly before Demo Day, and [[PaulGraham]] approved the name at the time. The company publicly launched under this name in December 2012 after narrowing its first product to a carefully scoped payroll workflow.

The source frames the later 2015 rebrand as a strategy signal. Payroll remained the trust-critical core, but the founders wanted the company name to fit a broader [[EntrepreneurshipInfrastructure]] path that included benefits and other small-business workflows.
