# Orland Bishop

Orland Bishop appears in [[bayo-akomolafe-the-untimely-cenyca-k7ry]] through [[BayoAkomolafe]]'s story about his son's daily Christmas greeting. In the source, Bishop asks why Bayo is imposing neurotypical time on the child, reframing the family scene as a philosophical challenge rather than a behavioral problem.

His role in this wiki is narrow but important: he helps turn [[AutisticTime]] from a private parenting anecdote into a critique of [[ModernTimeDiscipline]]. The intervention supports the talk's broader claim that what looks like disorder, lateness, awkwardness, or mismatch may contain a different relation to time.
