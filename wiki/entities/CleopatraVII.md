# Cleopatra VII / 克娄巴特拉七世

Cleopatra VII / 克娄巴特拉七世 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as both a familiar popular symbol around [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]] and a politically charged presence in Rome. The episode follows Caesar's Egyptian intervention after [[Pompey]]'s murder, his support for Cleopatra, and the later Roman unease around her arrival with Caesarion.

The source suggests Cleopatra sharpened the Roman legitimacy problem. Her royal and possibly divine kingship context, public works ambition, and statue in Caesar's new forum made Roman elites more anxious that Caesar's personal rule was moving beyond republican form.

Cleopatra also matters in the post-Caesar sequence through her later alliance with [[MarkAntony]] and defeat by [[OctavianAugustus|Octavian / Augustus]], which closes the episode's path from republican crisis into the [[RomanEmpire|Roman Empire]].
