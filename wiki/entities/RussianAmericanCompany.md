# Russian-American Company / 俄美公司

The Russian-American Company appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] as the later institutional consolidation of Russian commercial activity around [[Alaska]] and the North Pacific. The episode says that after disorder among private Russian companies, Paul I dissolved and merged them into the Russian-American Company.

The company is important here because it turns route discovery into resource governance and colonial extraction. [[SeaOtter]] skins, marine-animal depletion, conflict among companies, and the later judgment that Alaska had lost much of its fur value all become part of [[ExplorationToExtractionPipeline]] and [[RussianNorthPacificExpansion]].
