# Square

Square appears in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]] as the commerce and payments company that acquired [[Weebly]] in 2018. [[DavidRusenko]] says the acquisition made intuitive sense because both companies served small-business commerce from different directions, with Weebly starting from website creation and Square starting from payments and merchant tools.

The source also says the [[Weebly]] codebase became the underpinnings of [[SquareOnline]]. Rusenko emphasizes cultural fit after the deal, saying that Square and Weebly had similar values and that almost all of the Weebly team joined after the acquisition.
