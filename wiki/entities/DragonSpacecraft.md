# Dragon Spacecraft

Dragon Spacecraft is the SpaceX vehicle whose interior work gives [[145-koushu-spacex-kaifashi-he-qiangaoguan-honglide-liao-masike-yongrenguan-zuida-ipo-taikong-yu-ai-renlei-wenming-kuozhang-qianzou]] a concrete product-design layer. [[LouisHong]] says he first worked in Dragon-related design, development, and manufacturing, with special attention to the human-facing interior.

The source calls the interior the "highest-tech IKEA" because the pieces astronauts touch must be light, strong, expensive, manufacturable, and coordinated with spacecraft systems. That makes Dragon a useful bridge between [[FirstPrinciplesManufacturing]] and user-facing hardware design: the interior is not just decoration, but a tight engineering surface under weight, acceleration, safety, and reliability constraints.
