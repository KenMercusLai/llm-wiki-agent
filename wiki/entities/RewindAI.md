# Rewind AI

Rewind AI is [[DanSiroker]]'s pre-[[Limitless]] personal-memory product in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]]. Dan describes it as a native Mac app that locally captured screen content and audio, then used OCR and speech recognition to make a user's personal history searchable and usable.

The source frames Rewind as the bridge between [[MindEmulationFoundation]] and Limitless. The foundation raised a verification problem around digital mind emulation; Rewind turned the more practical version into [[PersonalAIMemory]] by preserving what a person had seen, said, or heard.

Rewind is also the fundraising case in the episode. Dan publicly posted a seven-minute Series A pitch, received many investment offers, used valuation submissions to understand market price, and eventually chose NEA. The source therefore connects Rewind to [[PublicFundraisingPitch]] as well as to memory-product design.

## Key Claims
- Rewind used local capture, OCR, and speech recognition to preserve personal computer and audio context.
- The product showed Dan that users may accept new memory behavior when privacy and convenience are credible enough.
- Rewind was later rebranded into Limitless as the product moved toward cloud-based AI and in-person context capture.
- The Series A process used public distribution and investor-question feedback as a fundraising tactic.
