# Figure AI

Figure AI appears in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] through its livestream of three humanoid robots sorting packages for more than 100 hours. The episode treats the demo as a concrete [[RobotLogisticsSorting]] case: the robots stood near a conveyor, turned packages label-side up, and made humanoid value easier for industrial customers to understand.

The source records Figure's claim that the work used Helix 02 autonomously, while also noting public debate about whether some behavior may have involved teleoperation. For the wiki, Figure is therefore a strong commercial-demo signal but not yet independent proof of fully autonomous long-duration logistics operation.
