# Sadia Pekkanen

Sadia Pekkanen appears in [[tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the expert explaining how renewed lunar exploration connects space law, resources, technology placement, and diplomacy. Her core distinction is that international space law blocks territorial ownership on the [[Moon]], while [[SpaceResourceExtraction]] can still make extracted resources commercially usable.

Pekkanen frames the new lunar race as less a simple flag-planting competition than a contest over presence, rules, and sustained operations. Her comments connect [[Artemis2]] and the [[ArtemisAccords]] to [[LunarResourceGovernance]], safety zones, [[China]] and [[Russia]] as non-signatories, and the need for transparent rules in a hostile operating environment.
