# David Kirtley

David Kirtley is the founder and CEO of [[Helion]] in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]]. The source presents him as a founder who moved from teenage concern about energy and clean water into plasma physics, space propulsion, and then back into [[CommercialFusionPower]] as a company-building problem.

Kirtley's account connects technical taste to operating focus. He argues that traditional fusion programs often made commercial electricity hard to justify because prototypes could require tens or hundreds of billions of dollars. Helion's route instead depends on faster prototypes, [[FusionEnergyRecovery]], in-house manufacturing, modern power electronics, and [[DeepTechProductFocus]] around one product: low-cost, clean baseload generators.

The episode also makes Kirtley a [[FounderMode]] case. He describes regretting periods when [[Helion]] drifted toward bureaucracy, then returning to hiring, front-line problem solving, bottleneck removal, and giving teams resources before moving to the next constraint.
