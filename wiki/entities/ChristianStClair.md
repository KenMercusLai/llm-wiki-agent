# Christian St. Clair

Christian St. Clair is the [[FantagraphicsBooks]] production manager in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]]. The episode follows him as he tracks two books printed in [[India]] that he first believes are stranded on the bright pink container ship [[OneMajesty]] after an attack near the [[StraitOfHormuz]].

His role is to make [[ChokepointShippingConfidence]] concrete at the level of an ordinary publishing schedule. A geopolitical standoff becomes a delayed book launch, uncertain container information, possible insurance exposure through [[GeneralAverage]], and dependence on negotiations far outside the publisher's control.

## Connections
- [[FantagraphicsBooks]] - publisher whose shipment he manages.
