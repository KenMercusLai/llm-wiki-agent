# 《辐射》 / Fallout

《辐射》 / Fallout is the post-apocalyptic game series referenced in [[03-laiboweici-de-zange-yao-you-guang-napa-feitu-zhishang-528163687]]. The hosts use it early to make wasteland conventions concrete, including bottle-cap currency and the imagined social order after nuclear collapse.

The episode later treats Fallout as part of the afterlife of [[CanticleForLeibowitz|《莱博维茨的赞歌》 / A Canticle for Leibowitz]]. The link is not only setting. Both place technical ruin, salvaged objects, communities after collapse, and religious or pseudo-religious imagery inside [[PostApocalypticCivilizationPreservation]] and [[ApocalypticLiterarySymbolism]].
