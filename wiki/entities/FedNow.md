# FedNow

FedNow appears in [[socialradarsseason2-dimitri-final]] as the new U.S. payment rail [[DimitriDadiomov]] discusses when describing the future of [[ModernTreasury]]. The source frames it as faster, real-time, 24/7 payment infrastructure rather than as a finished product experience by itself.

The episode's point is that new rails increase coordination work. Companies and banks still need software, workflow design, exception handling, and reconciliation to use faster payments safely, making FedNow part of the [[MoneyMovementInfrastructure]] opportunity.

## Connections
- [[ModernTreasury]] and [[DimitriDadiomov]] - company and founder discussing the rail.
- [[MoneyMovementInfrastructure]] - concept that FedNow extends.
- [[FinancialOperationsResilience]] - faster payments can change operational requirements and failure modes.
