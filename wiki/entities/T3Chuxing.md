# T3 Chuxing

T3 Chuxing appears in [[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] as one of the Chinese mobility partners [[CaoXudong]] names when describing [[Momenta]]'s Robotaxi strategy. Cao says Momenta would rather cooperate as an ASG supplier than build a self-operated Robotaxi platform in China.

The source places T3 Chuxing beside [[Didi]] and [[AutoNavi]] to make a commercialization point: Robotaxi capability still has to meet platform distribution, dispatch, fleet, safety, and service operations.
