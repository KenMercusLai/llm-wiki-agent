# Rupert Murdoch

[[RupertMurdoch|Rupert Murdoch]] appears in [[tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3-tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3]] as both the owner behind the [[WallStreetJournal|Wall Street Journal]] and an investor in [[Theranos]]. [[TylerShultz|Tyler Shultz]] says the Journal was under pressure while reporting the story, but the source says Murdoch did not stop the reporting.

The episode uses Murdoch mainly as a media-independence stress case. An owner-investor conflict could have created pressure against a newsroom investigating a company in which the owner had financial exposure. In the wiki, this extends [[MediaOwnershipIndependenceRisk]] by adding a case where the risk was present but the public-interest reporting continued.
