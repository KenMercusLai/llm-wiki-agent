# Zoe Chase

Zoe Chase is one of the [[PlanetMoney]] reporters whose 2014 [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] flight reporting is replayed in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]]. With [[JacobGoldstein|Jacob Goldstein]], she flew Spirit from New York's LaGuardia Airport to Fort Lauderdale and documented the cheap fare, add-on fees, dense seating, and visible passenger irritation.

The episode uses Chase's earlier reporting as evidence that Spirit's customer experience was deliberately stripped down rather than accidentally bad. Her segment helps connect the lived cabin experience to [[AirlineUnbundling]], [[UltraLowCostCarrierModel]], and [[StatedRevealedPreferenceGap]].
