# Baiji / 白鱀豚

The baiji is the Yangtze river dolphin discussed in the China section of [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. In the episode's account of [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》]], [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] contact [[ZhouKaiya|周开亚]] at Nanjing University, then go to the Tongling protection station to attempt underwater recording, but they do not find a baiji.

The segment is especially sharp because local publicity is abundant while the animal remains absent. Baiji beer, hotels, shoes, cola, scales, receptions, and meetings become the episode's strongest [[ConservationPublicityGap]] case: symbolic promotion can grow around a species even when the conditions for its survival are collapsing.
