# Molinos (Peru)

Molinos is the Peruvian company identified in [[the-sneaky-way-companies-get-new-chemicals-into-our-food]] as the producer behind the [[TaraFlour]] supply chain. The episode says Molinos had long made tara gum and later began manufacturing tara flour as a high-protein ingredient from the tara tree.

The page is source-scoped because the episode does not treat Molinos as a broad corporate profile. Its wiki role is to mark the upstream point in a [[FoodAdditiveRegulation]] case where a new ingredient moved through [[Smirks]] into [[DailyHarvest]] before serious safety questions were publicly resolved.
