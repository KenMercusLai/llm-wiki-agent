# Initialized Capital

Initialized Capital appears in [[socialradarspod-garrytan-final]] as the investment firm [[GarryTan]] began as a side project while he was a [[YCombinator]] partner. In the episode, [[JessicaLivingston]] says she introduced Tan to early limited partners, including Alex Bangash.

The source frames Initialized as a continuation of [[FounderInvestorLearning]]. Tan had been a founder, designer, YC participant, YC designer in residence, and YC partner; investing let him keep helping founders beyond the earliest seed moment. His later reflection is that startups never stop being hard, so founders need high-integrity investors, strong networks, and benevolent support after Series A and Series B as well.
