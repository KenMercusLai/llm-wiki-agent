# Huang Biwei

Huang Biwei is the [[AetherAI]] founder interviewed in [[na-tiao-luxian-caineng-tongwang-shijie-moxing-de-zhongju-duihua-huang-biwei-aether-ai-chuangshiren-lgg-env6jrpgvyiwtxw6bocdzdmr]]. The source presents her as a long-time [[CausalAI]] researcher with experience at institutions including the Max Planck Institute, CMU, and UCSD, and as an entrepreneur trying to turn causal research into a robot-brain product.

## Thesis
Huang argues that [[WorldModels]] only become practically useful for physical tasks when they learn latent causal variables, causal structure, and action-conditioned transition dynamics. Her strategic choice is to focus on [[EmbodiedAI]] because language and code tasks are already relatively mature under the LLM paradigm, while robotics still needs a deeper model of physical regularities.
