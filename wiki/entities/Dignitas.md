# Dignitas

Dignitas appears in [[119-anlesi-xianchang-zhengyin-shengming-ruci-zhengui-770771087]] as the Swiss assisted-suicide organization that helped the father of the later [[LifeCircle]] founder die after cancer. The source uses it as part of the personal and institutional path by which assisted suicide becomes concrete before the reporting turns to Life Circle's own cases.

In this wiki branch, Dignitas is not expanded into a full organizational history. It is tracked as a Swiss institutional reference point inside [[AssistedDyingLaws]], [[AssistedDyingSafeguards]], and [[CrossCulturalAssistedDeathEthics]].
