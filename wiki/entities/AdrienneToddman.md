# Adrienne Toddman

Adrienne Toddman appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] as the [[NationalRentalHomeCouncil]] voice warning that restrictions on corporate ownership could chill new rental-home construction. The episode uses her to present the industry-side argument that rental homes can let families live in neighborhoods they could not afford to buy into.

Her role is to connect the corporate-landlord debate to [[BuildToRentHousing]] and [[NeighborhoodOpportunityAccess]]. The source keeps that claim as one side of a tradeoff, because the same section also discusses price pressure and neighborhood crime findings.
