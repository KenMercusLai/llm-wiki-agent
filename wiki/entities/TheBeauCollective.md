# The Beau Collective

The Beau Collective is the Park City fitness and retail business discussed in [[advice-line-with-christina-tosi-of-milk-bar-fd7f2406-fda8-4c1d-936c-d20210215f96]]. The caller, Whitney, describes it as a community hub with morning fitness classes and a curated retail space selling apparel, gifts, home goods, and brands, with revenue roughly split between fitness and retail.

The business had operated profitably in Park City for about 10 years and was planning a Phoenix expansion. [[ChristinaTosi]] and [[GuyRaz]] advise testing demand before overextending: pre-sell memberships, make sure Phoenix has the same lifestyle and affluence fit as Park City, negotiate tenant improvements from the landlord, and consider community investment before institutional capital.
