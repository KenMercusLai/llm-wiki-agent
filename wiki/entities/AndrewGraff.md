# Andrew Graff

Andrew Graff is the Laguna Beach founder/caller behind [[Plantamica]] in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. He has invested about $30,000 of his own capital, worked with a chemist, lawyer, manufacturer, and soil testing lab, and asks whether to court investors or first prove traction.

The advice he receives is that the product is interesting but still needs live market evidence. [[GuyRaz]] pushes him toward launch, retail pilots, reorder data, customer posts, and unit economics before raising money; [[ShaziVisram]] adds that clean-fragrance positioning and third-party validation could help make the product easier to trust.
