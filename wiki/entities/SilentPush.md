# Silent Push

Silent Push is the threat-intelligence company represented by [[ZachEdwards]] in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. Silent Push enters the wiki through [[BrandImpersonationMonitoring]]. If fake sites can be created quickly and promoted through ads, brands need ways to discover impostor domains, warn customers, and protect trust before the issue becomes visible through customer losses.
