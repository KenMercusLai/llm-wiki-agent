# United States Pharmacopeia

United States Pharmacopeia appears in [[why-is-there-a-supplement-craze-if-they-dont-even-work]] through [[MarionNestle]]'s discussion of supplement testing marks. The episode says a USP mark can indicate third-party testing for listed ingredients, accurate amounts, and non-harmful levels.

The source uses USP as a practical consumer signal, not as evidence that a supplement works for healthy people. Its role is inside [[ThirdPartySupplementTesting]] and [[SupplementLabelAccuracy]], where the first question is whether the product matches the package.
