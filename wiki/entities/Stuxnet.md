# Stuxnet

Stuxnet appears in [[can-computer-hackers-get-inside-your-mind]] as the precedent for hidden [[CyberSabotage]] against [[Iran]]'s nuclear program. The episode describes it as malware that manipulated centrifuges while making systems report that conditions looked normal, making it a central comparison for [[Fast16|Fast 16]].

The comparison matters because Fast 16 is presented as "Stuxnet-like" in strategic logic even though its suspected mechanism is different. Where Stuxnet is described through physical sabotage, Fast 16 appears to operate through [[CalculationIntegrityAttack]] against high-precision physics modeling and possible [[NuclearWeaponsModeling]].
