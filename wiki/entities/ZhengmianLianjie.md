# 正面连接

正面连接 is the media/reporting context for the article that led [[AnQi|安琪]] to [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]] in [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]]. The episode says the article, written by [[HongWeilin|洪蔚琳]], circulated widely and carried the same title as the later book, [[YiGeAfuhanNvrenDeLaixin|《一个阿富汗女人的来信》]].

The source makes 正面连接 part of a discovery chain: journalism made the author's situation visible, an editor acted on that visibility, and a book project then created a longer-lasting reading and royalty channel.
