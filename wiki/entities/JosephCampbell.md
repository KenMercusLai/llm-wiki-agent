# Joseph Campbell / 约瑟夫·坎贝尔

Joseph Campbell / 约瑟夫·坎贝尔 appears in [[122-pinuocao-chengzhang-de-cantong-jiqi-suo-chuangzuo-de-777464172]] through the episode's use of the [[HeroJourneyNarrative]] framework. The source invokes Campbell to show why [[AdventuresOfPinocchio|《木偶奇遇记》]] can be read as a growth myth rather than only a children's cautionary tale.

The episode maps Pinocchio's movement through ordinary world, call, refusal, mentor, tests, symbolic death, whale/shark-belly darkness, rescue, return, and transformation. Campbell is therefore a framework node in this wiki rather than a full biography page.
