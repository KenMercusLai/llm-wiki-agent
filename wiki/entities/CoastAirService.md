# Coast Air Service

Coast Air Service represents the source's "北京海岸航空服务公司" acquisition thread in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says [[Ctrip]] bought the company in 2002 to gain airline resources, ticketing-agent qualifications, and offline paper-ticket delivery know-how.

The company is used to show that early online travel was still partly offline fulfillment. Before electronic tickets and mature mobile booking, [[OnlineTravelAgency]] platforms needed physical delivery, invoice, ticketing, and qualification infrastructure.
