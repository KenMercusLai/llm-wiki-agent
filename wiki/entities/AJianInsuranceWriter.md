# 阿健

阿健 is the former insurance claims worker and author behind [[ShiYiZhiCiZouBaoxian|《事已至此，走保险》]], as presented in [[159-yao-jingming-yao-shanliang-yao-jiejue-wenti-931818201]]. The episode says he initially resisted the insurance industry after graduation, then entered a claims department and learned that the work was less about selling policies than about field investigation, professional judgment, and difficult settlement decisions.

His wiki role is to ground [[InsuranceClaimsHandling]] in practice. Through cases involving delivery riders, injured workers, cancer claimants, borrower accident insurance, flooded factories, and children's third-party liability, he shows how adjusters decide what facts matter, when a clause is firm, when [[DiscretionaryInsurancePayment]] is defensible, and when complaint pressure or fraud tactics distort a case.

## Key Claims
- 阿健's claims stories make insurance legible as labor: reviewing records, visiting accident sites, tracing policy continuity, interviewing witnesses, checking surveillance, and writing defensible reports.
- His stance is pragmatic rather than sentimental. A claims worker can be humane, but kindness must still fit policy responsibility, evidence, and anti-fraud boundaries.
- The episode uses 阿健's career to show why ordinary policyholders need [[InsuranceClaimsInformationAsymmetry]] reduced before a crisis, not after they are already exhausted.
