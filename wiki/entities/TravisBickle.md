# Travis Bickle

Travis Bickle is the central character in the [[TaxiDriver]] segment of [[snap-judgement-japan-pms-electoral-landslide-6989c0fc61be18927058bb7d]]. The episode describes him as a damaged, alienated Vietnam veteran who wants connection but cannot communicate, and whose taxi becomes a symbol of isolation rather than belonging.

The source reads Travis as a warning case for [[AlienatedMaleViolence]]. His disgust, attraction to decadence, racism, misogyny, political self-righteousness, and attachment to guns create a path where violence becomes his bid for recognition. The segment argues that treating him as a simple masculine hero is [[AntiheroMisreading]].
