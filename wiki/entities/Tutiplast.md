# Tutiplast

Tutiplast is the [[Manaus]] company visited in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] as the episode turns from subsidy-dependent assembly to possible local innovation. The company makes injection-molded plastic parts and has a research lab where [[GabrielleSantos]] works on biodegradable plastics from plant waste.

The source treats Tutiplast as an example rather than a proven national answer. Its importance is that it points to [[LocalizedInnovationAdvantage]]: a Brazilian firm might build useful materials from Amazon-adjacent inputs such as nutshells instead of only assembling imported high-tech components under the [[ZonaFrancaDeManaus|free-zone]] model.
