# Loki / 洛基

Loki is a Norse mythic figure discussed in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]] through the contrast between [[PoeticEdda|Edda]] material and the [[MarvelCinematicUniverse|漫威宇宙]]. The source notes that Marvel's family relations simplify or change older Norse relationships: Loki is closer to [[Odin|Odin]]'s sworn-brother generation than to [[Thor|Thor]]'s brother in the episode's account.

Loki therefore becomes a compact example of [[EpicModernAfterlife]]: a mythic figure can remain recognizable by name and function while modern media rewrites kinship, motivation, and audience sympathy.
