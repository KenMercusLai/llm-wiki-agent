# Digg

Digg appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as the larger, earlier competitor to [[Reddit]]. [[SteveHuffman]] says Digg had launched first, was bigger, and benefited from Kevin Rose's fame among the target audience.

Huffman also treats Digg as a learning surface. Being smaller let Reddit watch larger competitors take heat and make mistakes first, a pattern he says later extended to Twitter and Facebook as well.

[[tsr-s5-jared-v3final-tsr-s5-jared-v3final]] adds Digg as part of [[Scribd]]'s launch-distribution burst. [[JaredFriedman]] says Scribd quickly reached [[HackerNews]], TechCrunch, [[Slashdot]], Digg, [[Reddit]], and mainstream media, helping the company move from nearly broke to multiple Series A term sheets.
