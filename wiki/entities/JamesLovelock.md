# James Lovelock

James Lovelock is referenced in [[indy-johar-civilizational-optioneering-tyeyt7r-zfu]] through *Novacene*. [[IndyJohar]] uses that frame to describe Earth as a planetary system that stored solar energy in hydrocarbons, released it into human civilization, and then became able to perceive itself through human and machine capacities.

The source uses Lovelock conceptually rather than biographically. His role is to anchor [[PlanetarySelfAwareness]], which then supports Johar's claim that the Long Now should preserve [[CivilizationalOptionality]] across human, machine, and ecological systems.
