# Leap Forward

Leap Forward is [[DavidRusenko]]'s climate-focused fund in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]]. By the episode's June 2024 date, Rusenko says he had started the fund the previous year after several years of climate-related nonprofit, political, and policy work.

The fund's thesis is [[EconomicClimateTechAdoption]]: climate technologies such as solar, batteries, EVs, and heat pumps can now win because they are becoming economically attractive, not only because customers care about emissions. Rusenko says his founder experience helps climate entrepreneurs with product, engineering, capital sequencing, and the zero-to-scale company-building work that can sit between technology proof and a durable business.

The episode names [[JessicaAlter]] as Rusenko's main partner at the fund and gives [[BlueDot]] and [[ElectricAir]] as portfolio examples. It also highlights the [[ClimateStartupCommercializationGap]], especially for science-heavy companies that must move from bench scale or prototype into first commercial scale before more capital becomes available.
