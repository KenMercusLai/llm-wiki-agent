# Without a Doubt

Without a Doubt is [[SurbhiSarna]]'s book discussed in [[tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3-tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3]]. The episode uses the title to clarify Sarna's founder psychology: she was not claiming total certainty about every startup decision, only that patients needed the product and that she wanted to work on it.

The book belongs to the same source branch as [[NVisionMedical|nVision Medical]], [[WomensHealthDiagnosticGap]], and [[SoloFounderFundraisingBias]]. It makes Sarna's story legible as both a women's-health memoir and a founder account of building through rejection, FDA work, clinical evidence, acquisition choices, and later [[YCombinator]] partner work.
