# Synopsys / 新思科技

Synopsys appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] as one of the major global [[ElectronicDesignAutomation|EDA]] vendors. The episode presents EDA as a software toolchain rather than a single program, and says incumbent vendors such as Synopsys benefit from decades of iteration and customer feedback.

In the source, Synopsys matters because chip design capability is inseparable from the software used to describe, simulate, verify, and prepare hardware for fabrication. That makes EDA dependence part of [[DomesticAIChipCatchUp|domestic AI-chip catch-up]], not only a procurement detail.
