# J.P. Morgan

J.P. Morgan appears in [[ep76-chuanyue-1940-wo-yu-gupiao-dazuoshou-lifumoer-de-zuihou-duihua-lr0fb5yfiwoxaamdwjjgh4rjeprg]] as the financier organizing market stabilization during the 1907 panic. The episode says Morgan asked [[JesseLivermore]] to stop pressing his short positions because further selling could deepen systemic stress.

## Source Position
- Morgan is not the focus of the episode, but he marks the point where a private trading win becomes a market-stability issue.
- His role reinforces the episode's distinction between being right on direction and understanding system-level consequences.
