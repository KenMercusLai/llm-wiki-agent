# Win

Win is the guest in [[bie-zai-guonei-juan-le-qu-meiguo-kankan-zhiyao-chanpin-hao-jiu-you-ren-fufei-de-shichang-keji-luandun]], where his CES and U.S. trip supplies the episode's first-person observations. He discusses [[Tesla]] FSD and Robotaxi, [[Waymo]], Bay Area infrastructure, CES networking, Chinese AI model adoption in the U.S., and why Chinese founders should evaluate overseas markets through payment behavior rather than habit.

His strongest contribution is [[PaymentLedMarketSelection]]. He argues that small teams should not assume China is the first or best market; they should ask where users pay for good products, then handle marketing, company setup, taxes, and payment infrastructure around that market. In the episode, this argument connects [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]], weak domestic [[SoftwarePaymentCulture]], and [[AIAgentOverseasCommercialization]] into one startup-market judgment.
