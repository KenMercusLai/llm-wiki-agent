# Bangladesh Nationalist Party

The Bangladesh Nationalist Party, or BNP, is presented in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]] as the favourite to win [[Bangladesh]]'s competitive election after [[SheikhHasina]] and the [[AwamiLeague]] are removed from power. The source describes it as one of two long-established forces dominating the post-uprising field, alongside [[JamaatEIslamiBangladesh]].

The BNP's current campaign is tied to [[TariqRahman]], who returned from long self-exile in London. That gives the party visibility and momentum, but the episode also makes his reputation a vulnerability because earlier corruption allegations remain politically salient even after courts quashed convictions made in absentia.
