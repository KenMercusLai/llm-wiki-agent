# Lee Woods

Lee Woods appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the [[AmericanGiantsMuseum]] guide or interpreter who explains why giant roadside figures worked as advertising. He says the stranger or quirkier the figure was, the better it attracted drivers.

His comments give the Route 66 segment its commercial-history angle. [[Route66]] nostalgia is not only scenery or migration myth; it also includes business owners using signs, statues, and oddness to make local stops visible.
