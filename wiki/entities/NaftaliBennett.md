# Naftali Bennett

Naftali Bennett appears in [[youve-come-a-long-way-bibi-israels-crucial-election-6a59f9ae461a6a41901ae410]] as a former Israeli prime minister and one of the opposition figures challenging [[BenjaminNetanyahu]] in [[IsraeliElection2026]]. The episode says Bennett and [[YairLapid]] have joined parties under the Together banner.

The source uses Bennett mainly to show the opposition's coordination problem. Polling can give anti-Netanyahu parties enough seats, but the episode warns that ideological distance, Arab-party participation, and coalition discipline may matter as much as the topline majority.
