# 张锐

Zhang Rui is the founder of [[ChunyuDoctor]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode says he left NetEase in 2011 to build what became Chunyu Doctor, centered on self-diagnosis and light online consultation.

In the source, Zhang's company carries the mobile-internet optimism around [[OnlineMedicalConsultation]], but also the difficulty of turning low-acuity reassurance into a high-growth platform business. His 2016 death is presented as part of the episode's account of the sector's harsh period after the early enthusiasm.
