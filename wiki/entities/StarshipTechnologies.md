# Starship Technologies

Starship Technologies is the sidewalk-delivery-robot company discussed in [[fault-lines-venezuelas-paltry-earthquake-response-6a44e38375e7a3e96111931b]]. The episode describes its small six-wheeled robots operating in places such as Milton Keynes and presents them as a practical autonomy case because they move slowly, carry low-risk cargo, and can use existing city infrastructure.

The source distinguishes Starship Technologies from more speculative autonomy narratives. Its significance is economic as much as technical: the company says it has recently beaten human delivery drivers on unit economics, while competitors that rely heavily on remote operators may have weaker scaling logic.
