# 杨璐 / Yang Lu

Yang Lu appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as a journalist who has reported on China's power-battery industry. She emphasizes the 2023 milestone of China's 20 millionth new-energy vehicle and the shift from "market for technology" toward domestic innovation.

Her reporting supplies the episode's factory and city texture: [[BYD]]'s early semi-manual battery production, the [[Changzhou]] four-hour supply-chain circle, and [[Yibin]]'s attempt to turn green power, universities, and [[CATL]] into a new industrial base.
