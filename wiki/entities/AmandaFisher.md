# Amanda Fisher

Amanda Fisher appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as a [[BetterMarkets]] policy voice and former SEC chief of staff critiquing [[Kalshi]]'s strategy. The episode uses her comments to frame prediction-market growth as [[PredictionMarketLegalBoundary|legal arbitrage]] rather than a simple technology breakthrough.

Her argument is that event-market companies can exploit the gap between derivatives regulation and gambling law while regulators catch up. She also stresses the oversight-capacity problem: the [[CommodityFuturesTradingCommission|CFTC]] is much smaller than the SEC and relies heavily on exchange self-policing, which becomes strained when markets look like sports betting, political wagering, or insider-information trading.
