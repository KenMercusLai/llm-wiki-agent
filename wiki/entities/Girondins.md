# Girondins / 吉伦特派

The Girondins / 吉伦特派 are the moderate republican faction in [[125-mala-zhisi-beibi-de-ansha-huo-zhengyi-de-chujue-783972516]]. The episode links them to the early republican phase of the [[FrenchRevolution|French Revolution]], to Bordeaux/Gironde geography, and to a more restrained constitutional republicanism than the later [[JacobinClub|Jacobin]] terror.

For the source, the Girondins matter because [[CharlotteCorday|Charlotte Corday]] acts from sympathy with them. She is not framed as a royalist reactionary, but as someone who supports the republic while opposing [[JeanPaulMarat|Marat]]'s calls for killing and the revolutionary logic that would consume Girondin leaders.

The faction also helps the episode discuss interest, price control, and the taboo against admitting partial interests inside revolutionary language. The conflict with the Jacobins shows how a revolution can turn policy disagreement into proof of treason.
