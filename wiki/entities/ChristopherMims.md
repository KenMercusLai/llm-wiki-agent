# Christopher Mims

Christopher Mims is the technology writer and author interviewed in [[tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128]] about [[HowToAI|How to AI]]. In the episode, he presents AI as a practical assistant rather than a replacement for human expertise.

Mims' contribution to the wiki is a consumer-level operating frame. He argues that people should learn AI by applying it to their own odd, mundane, and disliked tasks, while remembering that [[AIHallucination]], lack of agency, lack of taste, and weak judgment make [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]] essential.
