# Nasdaq Composite

The Nasdaq Composite appears in [[ep57-mei-gu-dong-dang-dong-sheng-xi-jiang-zhe-hui-shi-zou-shi-liu-lmeihcoqezy-5jxqddeottydswz5]] as the tech-heavy U.S. index whose technical break and mega-cap dependence anchor much of the episode's caution. [[Daxiong]] notes that the Nasdaq had broken below its 200-day moving average and that short-term moving averages were turning weaker, making aggressive bottom-fishing unattractive.

[[so-are-we-in-an-ai-bubble-here-are-clues-to-look-for]] adds the dot-com comparison. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode says the Nasdaq's post-2000 collapse shows how a technology bubble can damage investors and help push the economy into recession, while still leaving some later-useful infrastructure behind.
