# Gadi Barlevy

Gadi Barlevy appears in [[so-are-we-in-an-ai-bubble-here-are-clues-to-look-for]] as the Chicago Fed economist explaining the macro-policy side of bubbles. The episode notes that he is speaking for himself rather than for the Federal Reserve.

Barlevy's main contribution is [[LeanVersusCleanBubblePolicy]]: policymakers can either lean against a suspected bubble while it grows or wait to clean up after it bursts. He also explains why leverage matters. If a bubble is financed through heavy borrowing, falling prices can turn into defaults, bank losses, tighter credit, and a deeper recession.
