# 王冕

王冕 is presented in [[58-rulin-waishi-jiashe-kaogong-chengle-weiyi-chulu-645207859]] as both a real historical figure and an idealized opening figure in [[RulinWaishi|《儒林外史》]]. The episode highlights his poverty, filial care, simplicity, reluctance to pursue the examination route, and refusal to flatter officials.

In the source's reading, Wang Mian establishes an alternative value system before the main satirical world fully unfolds. His refusal of official summons, supported by Qin Laohan's practical help, frames dignity as something that can exist outside [[ImperialExaminationAsOnlyExit]]. That opening ideal later echoes through [[DuShaoqing|杜少卿]], Shen Qiongzhi, and the city eccentrics at the novel's close.
