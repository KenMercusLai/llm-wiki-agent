# Tower of Babylon

[[TowerOfBabylon|《巴比伦之塔》 / Tower of Babylon]] is a [[TedChiang|Ted Chiang / 江峰南]] story discussed in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode frames it as a retelling of the biblical Babel story in which divine intervention is less central than the story's physical model of the cosmos.

The key twist is spatial rather than merely religious: builders reach and pierce the vault of heaven, only to return to the ground. The episode reads this as a finite-but-unbounded cosmological thought experiment, extending the wiki's [[ScienceReligionCivilizationTension]] branch through a story where religious cosmology becomes exact speculative geometry.
