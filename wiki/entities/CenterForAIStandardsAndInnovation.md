# Center for AI Standards and Innovation

The Center for AI Standards and Innovation is identified in [[tech-20260710-tech-pod-128-tech-20260710-tech-pod-128]] as the government body conducting frontier-model testing around releases such as [[OpenAI]]'s [[GPT56|GPT-5.6]]. [[MariaCurie|Maria Curi]] says the center has been central across administrations while going through political turmoil, including fights over whether to use the language of AI "safety."

The episode uses the center to show that [[FrontierModelReleaseGovernance]] is becoming institutional rather than only informal company self-review. It also says the center works on standards in the United States and with parallel bodies in allied countries, while some thresholds and test details may remain classified.
