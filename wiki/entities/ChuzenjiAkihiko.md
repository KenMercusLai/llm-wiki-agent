# 中禅寺秋彦 / Chuzenji Akihiko

中禅寺秋彦 is the central Kyogokudo figure in [[KyogokuNatsuhiko|京极夏彦]]'s larger [[HyakkiYagyoSeries|百鬼夜行系列]] universe, as framed by [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]]. The episode does not make him the protagonist of [[KonjakuZokuHyakkiKumo|《今昔续百鬼·云》]], but it treats the book as important because it includes his first meeting with [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]].

The source describes the wider pattern of Kyogoku plots as moving through many apparent explanations before Chuzenji eventually appears with an explanation that can sound superstitious or strange but leads back toward a rational reconstruction.

## Key Claims
- Chuzenji marks the larger detective-explanatory center of the Kyogokudo universe, even when [[KonjakuZokuHyakkiKumo|《今昔续百鬼·云》]] foregrounds Tatara instead.
- The episode uses him to explain why Kyogoku's mysteries are not only clue games: the final explanation reorganizes belief, social history, and what the living should do next.
