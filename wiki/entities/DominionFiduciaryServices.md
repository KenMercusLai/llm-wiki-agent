# Dominion Fiduciary Services

Dominion Fiduciary Services appears in [[the-leaked-tapes-that-show-how-the-rich-avoid-taxes]] through a pitch deck obtained by [[LaurenLoricchio]]. The deck promoted the [[MaltaTaxLoophole]] using a hypothetical venture capitalist with a large capital gain and millions of dollars in potential tax liability.

The firm matters in the source as the marketing layer of [[TaxTreatyArbitrage]]. The episode's point is not only that treaty language existed, but that advisers and fiduciary service providers could package it as a practical strategy for wealthy clients holding appreciated assets.

## Source Position
- The pitch-deck example makes the tax savings concrete through a hypothetical gain.
- The source treats the deck as evidence of promotion, not as a full legal adjudication of the firm's conduct.
- Dominion's role connects high-end tax planning to [[TaxAvoidanceEvasionBoundary|contested legality]] and later [[InternalRevenueService]] response.
