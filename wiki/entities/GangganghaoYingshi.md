# 刚刚好影视 / Gangganghao Yingshi

Gangganghao Yingshi is [[LiJiajia]]'s short-drama company in [[269-zhenren-duanju-de-xiayizhan-yu-ai-gongsheng-gongyehua-he-hao-gushi-lmwhgqd0rge4mr0fwp5qpr2scwxt]]. The source contrasts it with [[RixinYiy]]: Gangganghao is described as doing one project at a time with higher per-project cost, more emphasis on script and actor fit, and a stronger willingness to take creative risk when the team believes the story is good.

The company is tied to a story method rather than a pure production-capacity thesis. Li Jiajia says its confidence in free short drama came from works such as [[SuTaitaiGaodiaoLihunle]] and from the belief that full-story immersion and character motivation can still win under [[Hongguo]]-style completion-rate pressure.

The source positions Gangganghao as a live-action answer to the AI shock: it does not deny AI's cost and iteration advantages, but argues that [[LiveActionShortDrama]] can still matter if the work creates emotional residue and character nuance that users remember.
