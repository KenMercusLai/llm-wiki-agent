# March Hare / 三月兔

The March Hare is the host figure in the mad tea party discussed in [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]]. The episode treats the character as part of the same idiom-driven and period-language field as the [[MadHatter|Mad Hatter / 疯帽匠]], using him to show how [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》]] often turns sayings into bodies and scenes.

At the tea party, the March Hare helps sustain a conversation where politeness, answers, seats, and objects do not stabilize. That makes him less an independent psychological portrait than a node in [[TimeStasisSystem]] and [[NonsenseLogic]].

## Key Claims
- The character belongs to Wonderland's idiom-made-visible design.
- His tea-party role helps turn ordinary hospitality into procedural confusion.
- He supports the episode's claim that Wonderland nonsense is rule-governed but not aligned with ordinary social sense.
