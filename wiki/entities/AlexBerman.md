# Alex Berman

Alex Berman is the LinkedIn influencer partner associated with [[Tapio]] in [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]]. [[ThibautLouisLucas]] describes finding Berman to replicate the distribution playbook that [[JKMolina]] had used with [[TweetHunter]].

## Key Claims
- Berman's role shows how Lucas treated influencer fit as part of product strategy, not merely launch promotion.
- The Tapio partnership extended [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]] from Twitter to LinkedIn.
- The partnership sits inside the broader pattern of combining focused SaaS products with audience-specific distribution.
