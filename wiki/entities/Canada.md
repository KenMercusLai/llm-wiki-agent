# Canada

Canada is the national context for [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]]. The episode uses [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]] to show how Canadian television can be financed through a blend of public support, tax credits, license fees, distribution advances, producer reinvestment, and retained IP.

The source's Canada branch is about production structure, not national culture alone. [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]] and [[ConstraintDrivenMediaProduction]] let a lower-budget series become internationally legible, while [[Crave]], [[BellMedia]], and [[SphereAbacus]] show the institutional path from domestic commissioning to wider distribution.
