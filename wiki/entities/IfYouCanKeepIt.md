# If You Can Keep It

If You Can Keep It is the [[1A]] series promoted at the end of [[congress-has-voted-to-eliminate-government-funding-for-public-media]]. The promo describes it as a series about major political stories and why they matter for democracy.

In the wiki, the series belongs to the same public-media branch as [[NPR]]'s appeal, but its emphasis is political explanation rather than funding. It connects [[1A]], [[JenWhite]], [[WAMU]], and [[PublicServiceJournalism]].
