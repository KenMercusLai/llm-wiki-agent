# Susan Wojcicki

Susan Wojcicki appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] in three connected roles. First, [[Google]] moved from [[StanfordUniversity]] into her Menlo Park garage before [[RonConway]] met [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]]. Second, Conway says she later helped preserve the early wall of search-count metrics. Third, the closing remembers her work building and running [[YouTube]].

The source treats Wojcicki as part of Google's institutional memory. Her garage marks the research-project-to-company transition, the preserved metrics wall connects to [[SearchQualityOperatingCadence]], and her YouTube leadership connects Google to online video and [[LegalRiskAcquirerFit]].

[[tsr-ronconway-part4-v2-tsr-ronconway-part4-v2]] adds Conway's memory of Wojcicki's lung-cancer death and her family's work on earlier precursor detection. This does not change her Google/YouTube role, but it extends the Conway branch where founder and company relationships also become health, philanthropy, and family-support networks.
