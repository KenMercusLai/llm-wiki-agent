# Sonalight

Sonalight was the voice-control app [[SpenserSkates]] and [[CurtisLiu]] took through [[YCombinator]] Winter 2012 before pivoting to [[Amplitude]]. In [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]], the app let Android phones listen in the background so users could send and receive texts by voice.

The source uses Sonalight as a [[TechnicalDemoRetentionGap]] case. The Demo Day pocket-phone demonstration generated excitement, but real users often tried the app once or twice and did not return. The founders found that a successful first voice-recognition match correlated with better retention, but they could not directly improve the underlying recognition because they depended on an unauthorized [[Google]] API.
