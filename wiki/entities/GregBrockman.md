# Greg Brockman

Greg Brockman appears in [[tsr-s4-samaltman-v4-tsr-s4-samaltman-v4]] as part of the November 2023 [[OpenAIBoardCrisis]]. [[JessicaLivingston]] frames the crisis as the week when [[SamAltman]] and Brockman were ousted by [[OpenAI]]'s board.

The episode does not give Brockman's separate account. His role in this ingest is as an OpenAI leadership figure whose removal made the board conflict a company-wide governance crisis rather than only a CEO transition.
