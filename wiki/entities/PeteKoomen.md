# Pete Koomen

Pete Koomen is [[DanSiroker]]'s co-founder in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]]. Dan says he convinced Pete to leave [[Google]] after the 2008 [[BarackObama]] campaign, and the two worked through [[CarrotSticks]] and [[Spreadly]] before building [[Optimizely]].

Pete's role in the source is mainly team-contextual. The episode uses the Dan/Pete path to show how [[YCombinator]] helped founders focus after weak early ideas and how an idea grounded in the founders' own pain can create stronger [[FounderProductFit]].
