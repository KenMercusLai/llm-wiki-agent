# National Security Agency

The National Security Agency appears in [[can-computer-hackers-get-inside-your-mind]] through a leaked malware-related list where [[JuanAndresGuerreroSaade|JAGS]] first notices [[Fast16|Fast 16]]. The episode emphasizes the strange instruction attached to Fast 16, which told operators to move on rather than escalate or investigate.

The source does not prove that the NSA created Fast 16. It treats the leaked-list entry as a clue about sensitivity and operational context, while leaving authorship, target, and purpose officially unconfirmed.
