# H-E-B

H-E-B is the Texas supermarket chain that tested [[ELFCosmetics]] on four-sided spinner racks in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. [[JoeyShamah]] says the test sold out quickly and gave e.l.f. a new pitch: the brand created incremental, impulse-driven category sales rather than merely stealing from higher-priced cosmetics.

The H-E-B test anchors the wiki's [[RetailIncrementality]] concept because it converted shopper behavior into retailer-facing proof.
