# John Watson / 华生

John Watson / 华生 is the Holmes narrator figure used in [[144-bei-zuzhou-de-munaiyi-fuermosi-hui-he-laoshe-lianshou-tanan-ma-878168738]] as one half of [[TheCursedMummy|《被诅咒的木乃伊》]]'s manuscript game. In the source's account, Watson's version of events restores [[SherlockHolmes|福尔摩斯]] to the controlled, brilliant detective image after [[NatsumeSoseki|夏目漱石]] has described the same encounter as chaotic, comic, and nearly deranged.

The episode therefore treats Watson as more than an assistant who records deductions. His narrative voice is a stabilizing convention inside [[HolmesPasticheTradition|Holmes pastiche]]: when Watson tells the story, readers know how to read Holmes's behavior, deductions, and eccentricity. When Soseki tells it, the same behavior becomes strange enough to create a second puzzle about perception and reliability.
