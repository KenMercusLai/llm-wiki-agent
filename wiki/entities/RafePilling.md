# Rafe Pilling

Rafe Pilling appears in [[tech-20260305-0305-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260305-0305-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[Sophos]] expert interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] about [[Iran]]-linked cyber threats to American banks. He explains the 2011-2013 bank attacks as distributed denial-of-service incidents that used compromised computers to overwhelm banking websites with traffic.

Pilling also broadens the episode beyond bank outages. He describes [[IranLinkedCyberOperations]] as having evolved toward phishing, vulnerability scanning, [[CyberDataTheftAndLeakOperations]], and [[IndustrialControlSystemCyberRisk]], while arguing that banks are probably better prepared for DDoS campaigns than health care organizations and other sensitive-data holders.
