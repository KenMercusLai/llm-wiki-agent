# 见手青 / Jianshouqing

[[Jianshouqing|见手青]] is the risky blue-staining mushroom group foregrounded in [[150-chi-jun-he-acuo-liao-yunnan-de-jizong-songrong-jianshouqing-ca-koushui-897563165]]. The episode treats it as a test case for [[WildMushroomFoodSafety]] because internet fascination often turns poisoning into a joke about hallucination, while 阿错 and 李飘 stress severe illness and death risk.

In the source, 见手青 is not dismissed as inedible; it is treated as a local delicacy whose preparation and reheating boundaries are serious enough that 阿错 refuses casual proxy-buying for outside friends. The wiki should therefore treat this page as a risk and culture page, not as cooking advice.

## Key Claims
- The episode rejects "见小人" as an adequate description of poisoning risk.
- 见手青's popularity can make danger worse when novelty, price, or online humor outruns local caution.
- The source recommends that outsiders avoid casual handling and rely on trusted local restaurants if they choose to eat this kind of mushroom.
- This page should not be used as a field-identification or preparation guide.
