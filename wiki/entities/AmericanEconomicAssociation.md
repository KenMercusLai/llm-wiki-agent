# American Economic Association

The American Economic Association appears in [[would-you-trust-an-economist-with-your-economy]] as the setting for a panel on economics and politics. [[BenCastleman]] describes the session with [[JasonFurman]], other government-experienced economists, and [[OrenCass]] as a scene where professional economics had to answer public and political criticism.

The association matters here because the episode treats the panel as institutional self-examination. The profession is not only debating models; it is being asked why it missed important harms, how much authority it should have in policy, and what it owes the public after visible errors.
