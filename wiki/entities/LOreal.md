# L'Oreal

L'Oreal appears in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] as the strategic buyer that offered roughly $225 million for [[ELFCosmetics]] before the deal collapsed. [[JoeyShamah]] says L'Oreal cited discomfort with e.l.f.'s low-cost supply chain, while he believed [[UrbanDecay]] was the real focus at the time.

The failed process matters because it left e.l.f. available for the later [[TPG]] deal at a higher valuation.
