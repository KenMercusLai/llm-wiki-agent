# 高固

高固 is the Qi warrior whom [[109-xianliao-zuozhuan-zhi-chunqiu-yundonghui-747508773]] nominates for throwing events. The episode recounts the story in which he enters the Jin line on foot, throws or uses a large stone against enemy troops, kills and intimidates opponents, and returns safely.

His main wiki role is idiomatic as well as athletic. The source uses him to explain "余勇可贾": after returning from the enemy line, he tells fellow soldiers that anyone who wants courage can buy his surplus courage. The episode reads the scene as physical force, battlefield performance, and verbal bravado at once.
