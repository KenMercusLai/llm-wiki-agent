# Edward Threate

Edward Threate appears in [[peace-fire-further-us-iran-strikes-6a4f7acffe878dc8e27e6fbf]] as the family representative explaining the [[ThreateFillingStation]] on [[Route66]] in [[LutherOklahoma]]. He says the station was a safe haven for Black travellers during Jim Crow, when they could not assume they would be served at ordinary gas stations, restaurants, or lodging stops.

His account turns the site into a family and community memory project. The episode says his family owned 160 acres there and still owns the land, and that the station's restoration matters because descendants and the surrounding community can still recognize it as a refuge.
