# BuzzFeed

BuzzFeed appears in [[tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128]] through its going-concern warning and AI pivot. The episode says BuzzFeed warned there was substantial doubt about its ability to continue operating, after a steep public-market decline from its 2021 listing period.

The source frames BuzzFeed as a [[MediaAIRescueStrategy]] case. In 2023, the company announced a hard turn into AI, including personalized quizzes, then later shut down its Pulitzer Prize-winning news division. Its newer AI games, quizzes, and interactive products, including [[QuizParty]], are treated skeptically by [[AnitaRamaswamy]], who references criticism that the products may look like [[AISlop]] rather than a durable media future.
