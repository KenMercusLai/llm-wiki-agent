# Gob

Gob is [[LaurenMenard]]'s company discussed in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. The episode describes Gob as reinventing single-use personal care products that disappear after use, starting with a mycelium-based single-use foam earplug intended as an alternative to petroleum-based foam earplugs.

The Advice Line question is whether Gob should scale live-event venue distribution and direct-to-consumer sleep customers at the same time. [[TimFerriss]] advises Lauren to use the venue channel to build reserves and learning before spending heavily on DTC acquisition, while [[GuyRaz]] and Tim suggest adjacent partnerships such as festivals, airlines, venue social proof, and sleep-related brands.
