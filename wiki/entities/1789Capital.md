# 1789 Capital

1789 Capital appears in [[how-much-money-president-trump-and-his-family-have-made]] through [[DonaldTrumpJr]]'s partnership. [[DavidKirkpatrick]] estimates Don Jr.'s gain from the role at about $19.6 million and argues the opportunity was unlikely to be explained only by conventional work experience.

[[e233-guigu-youyi-de-quanli-wang-ruhe-xingcheng-liaoliao-bide-tier-de-sixiang-qimeng-pintu-2a529abf-3c14-4ae7-a0a5-fe1340a66c7d]] adds a different role for 1789 Capital inside [[RightWingMediaInfrastructure]]. The [[SiliconValley101]] episode names it as a capital node connected to [[RockbridgeNetwork]]-adjacent politics, [[XAI|xAI]], [[SpaceX]], and youth/influencer-oriented conservative culture.

## Source Position
- The source uses 1789 Capital as a smaller family-finance example beside [[JaredKushner]]'s Saudi-backed private-equity case.
- The firm is not analyzed as a full investment strategy; it is used to illustrate how political family proximity can become an asset.
- The episode treats the gain as part of family enrichment rather than a direct payment to the president.
