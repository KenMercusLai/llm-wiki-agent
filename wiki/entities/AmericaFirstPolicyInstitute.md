# America First Policy Institute

The America First Policy Institute, or AFPI, is one of the three main policy sources compared in [[vol-113-cong-jiqian-ye-zhiku-wenjian-zhong-goule-telangpu-2-0-zhizheng-jihua-beihou-de-ren-jigou-sixiang-he-mailuo-lihnknc-3om5evexocb6a6zdwueu]]. The episode describes it as a post-2020 Trump-aligned think tank whose agenda often expresses similar priorities to [[Project2025]] in more moderate or policy-administrative language.

AFPI's role in the source is to show likely implementation paths. It foregrounds inflation, wasteful federal spending, energy supply, legal immigration reform, supply-chain dependence, and fair reciprocal trade while softening some of Project 2025's more maximal culture-war language.

## Key Claims
- AFPI places inflation at the top of its agenda, connecting it to energy, spending, regulation, and border policy.
- It frames immigration through legal-system integrity, skills, and individual merit, making it central to [[MeritBasedImmigrationFilter]].
- It promotes [[SupplyChainSovereignty]] by arguing that the [[UnitedStates]] should rely more on domestic workers and allies.
- Its trade language supports [[TradeReciprocityProtectionism]] by calling for more equal tariff treatment.
