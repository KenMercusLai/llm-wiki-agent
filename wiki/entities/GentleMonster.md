# Gentle Monster

Gentle Monster is the Korean eyewear brand used in [[137-cong-shunde-zhuroupo-dao-hanguo-shengshuidong-naxie-ai-wufa-qudai-de-tiyan-xiaofei-feng-tou-quan-1-137-1]] to explain [[KoreanCultureLedConsumerMarketing]]. The hosts emphasize that its stores function as art-installation destinations and social-media scenes, not only as places to buy glasses.

The episode treats Gentle Monster as an [[ExperientialRetail]] and [[ConsumerBrandMoat]] case. Eyewear can have high retail markup, but Gentle Monster adds trend value by making the store visit itself part of the product, including multi-floor Seoul spaces and tourist-facing destination stores such as the Shenzhen MixC World location mentioned by the hosts.

## Source Position
- Store design is framed as a core brand asset rather than decoration.
- The brand's retail experience helps turn eyewear into fashion, tourism, and photo-sharing behavior.
- The source uses Gentle Monster to support the broader claim that Korea is especially strong at marketing, cultural packaging, and place-based consumer experience.
