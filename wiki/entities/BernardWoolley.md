# Bernard Woolley / 伯纳德

Bernard Woolley is [[JamesHacker|James Hacker]]'s private secretary in [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]], discussed in [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]]. The episode presents him as structurally trapped: he serves the minister day to day, but his promotion path, professional identity, and long-term incentives remain inside the civil-service system.

His famous answer that civil servants support the winning side condenses the source's institutional point. Bernard is not simply cowardly or neutral; he is the person whose position makes institutional loyalty, personal survival, and procedural correctness collide.

In the [[BaillieCollege|Baillie College]] story, Bernard's later recollection also undercuts Hacker's diary, showing how mock-archival narration becomes part of [[InstitutionalMechanismSatire]].
