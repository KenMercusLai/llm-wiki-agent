# Vanta

Vanta is the trust-management and compliance-automation company founded by [[ChristinaCacioppo]], covered in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]]. In the episode, Christina describes Vanta as helping companies build security programs and then get credit for that work with customers through audits, questionnaires, and trust-facing evidence.

The company emerged from [[SOC2Audit]] pain Christina had seen at [[Dropbox]] when [[DropboxPaper]] could not launch broadly without satisfying customer security and compliance commitments. Its earliest product was not software but a [[ManualComplianceMVP]]: a spreadsheet gap assessment first used with [[Segment]] and then adapted for [[Front]].

Vanta's early go-to-market also makes it a [[YCombinator]] case. The company joined YC in Winter 2018 because its likely buyers were startup founders, and Christina wanted a forcing function for learning sales. Vanta targeted two $10,000 contracts per week, reached roughly $180,000 by the end of YC, and used [[AnnualUpfrontSaaSCashFlow]] to fund conservative early hiring.
