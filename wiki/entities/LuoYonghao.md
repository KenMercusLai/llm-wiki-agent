# Luo Yonghao

Luo Yonghao is the founder of [[Smartisan]] in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode uses Luo as the clearest case where founder charisma, taste, and public presentation could produce enormous attention, but could not by themselves solve phone manufacturing, supply-chain, and quality-control problems.

His role in the source is therefore double. Luo helped make smartphone launches, interface detail, and design language culturally important, while Smartisan's difficulties show that [[SmartphoneBrandSupplyChainExecution]] mattered more than a compelling founder narrative alone.
