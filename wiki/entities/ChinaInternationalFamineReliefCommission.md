# China International Famine Relief Commission / 华洋义赈会

The China International Famine Relief Commission, or 华洋义赈会, appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as the major civil charity organization involved in the 1931 flood response. The episode places it alongside state officials, religious charities, overseas Chinese networks, public-health experts, and international advisers.

The source uses the commission to show both capacity and misalignment. Organized charity could raise funds and distribute relief, but elite assumptions about poverty, labor discipline, food, vaccination, and proper behavior could produce [[DisasterReliefMismatch]] when they did not match survivor needs.
