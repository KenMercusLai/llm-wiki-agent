# 大卫翁 / David Weng

大卫翁 is the [[QizhulouYanBinke|起朱楼宴宾客]] host and speaker in [[vol-104-putongren-ganggu-wanquan-shengcun-zhinan-chuantai-sandian-xiaban-lmzivgi6d3guv7wajyjw9cv9zz90]]. In this episode, he brings an institutional and personal-investing perspective on Hong Kong equities, including earlier fund work on Greater China allocation, the [[ChinaSouthernAirlines]] AH discount trade, a steel-pipe-stock loss, and renewable-power rerating examples through [[DatangRenewablePower]] and [[LongyuanPower]].

In [[vol-106-dibai-zhende-biandi-shi-huangjin-lpxchutxf7yomjdfykwzvvnmbaq8]], he hosts [[Ricky]]'s field report on [[Dubai]], [[AbuDhabi|Abu Dhabi]], capital flow, real estate, crypto, residency, and Chinese outbound-business opportunities. His role is mainly to test whether Dubai's opportunity story should be read as ordinary investing, migration, geopolitics, or city-development signal.
