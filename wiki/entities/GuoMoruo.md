# 郭沫若

郭沫若 appears near the end of [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] as a source-scoped example of the opportunistic reversal that the episode sees in [[QiSi|《起死》]]. The hosts connect his earlier attacks on [[LuXun|鲁迅]] and later praise after Lu Xun's death to the pattern of using whatever position becomes useful.

The episode also suggests that some [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]] myth rewritings may be in dialogue with Guo's earlier myth-themed writing and with Creation Society attacks on Lu Xun. The point is not to reduce every story to Guo, but to show how Republican literary politics can sit behind ancient-story satire.

## Connections
- [[LuXun|鲁迅]] - writer whom the episode says Guo attacked and later publicly praised.
- [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]] - myth-rewriting collection possibly in dialogue with Guo's own mythic writing.
- [[QiSi|《起死》]] - source story used to frame opportunistic reversals.
- [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]] - broader concept for the "whatever works" logic.
