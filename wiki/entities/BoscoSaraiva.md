# Bosco Saraiva

Bosco Saraiva appears in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] as a [[Manaus]] local who grew up around the factory economy and worked in local industry. He represents the resident-side view of the [[ZonaFrancaDeManaus|Zona Franca de Manaus]]: the tax incentives created thousands of opportunities and changed the city's economic base.

His account is also the episode's clearest statement of [[IndustrialSubsidyDependence]]. When asked what would happen without the incentives, he says the industrial hub would become economically unviable, making Manaus both a job-creation success and a fragile industrial-policy case.
