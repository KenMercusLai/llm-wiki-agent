# Philadelphia Phillies

The Philadelphia Phillies appear in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as the team to which the [[StLouisCardinals|St. Louis Cardinals]] traded [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]] in 1969. The source says Flood did not want to go to Philadelphia because of the team's performance and because Black players had been treated badly by Phillies fans.

The Phillies matter here less as a team-history subject than as the destination that exposed how little choice the [[BaseballReserveClause|reserve clause]] left a player once [[MajorLeagueBaseball|MLB]] clubs controlled assignment rights.
