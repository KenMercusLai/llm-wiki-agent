# Jess Jang

Jess Jang appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] participant giving a Valentine to [[JesseDoherty]]'s [[WashingtonPost|Washington Post]] article on competitive [[MicrosoftExcel|Microsoft Excel]].

Her segment frames spreadsheets as a high-skill expressive system rather than merely office software. By admiring Excel competitors and the article's sports-writing tone, she helps the source turn [[CompetitiveSpreadsheeting]] into a small economics-of-skill and spectacle case.
