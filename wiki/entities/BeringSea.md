# Bering Sea / 白令海

Bering Sea is one of the places whose name leads the episode [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] back to [[VitusBering]]. In the source, it is part of the North Pacific geography through which the [[RussianEmpire]] sought routes, prestige, knowledge, and later commercial access.

The page is source-scoped. It records the sea as a named trace of the expeditions and as the wider setting that links [[BeringStrait]], [[BeringIsland]], [[Kamchatka]], [[Alaska]], [[SeaOtter]], and [[RussianNorthPacificExpansion]].
