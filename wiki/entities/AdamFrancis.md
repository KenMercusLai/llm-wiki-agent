# Adam Francis

Adam Francis is the CEO [[TomRinks]] brought into [[SunBum]] as the company moved beyond founder-led brand creation in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. Tom says Francis was necessary because the next stage required stronger margins, scalability, production, and financial management.

The source makes Francis part of the [[FounderRoleTransition]] pattern: Tom could remain valuable as a brand builder while a more operational CEO helped Sun Bum scale toward the later [[SCJohnson]] acquisition.
