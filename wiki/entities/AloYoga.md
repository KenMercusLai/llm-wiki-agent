# Alo Yoga

Alo Yoga is the premium athletic-apparel brand discussed in [[138-angpao-zhongguo-zhong-zhiying-chaoji-xingxing-bu-banka-feng-tou-quan-1-138-1]] as another high-status circle brand near [[Lululemon]]. The episode treats Alo as especially strong at marketing, scarcity, and aspirational identity, while questioning whether the product experience matches the brand atmosphere.

## Key Claims
- Alo is framed as a beneficiary of identity demand after [[Lululemon]] became more mainstream.
- The source says the brand is strong at creating a "super girl" social signal and scarcity feeling.
- The host's store visit in Korea leads to a skeptical product-quality assessment, making Alo a case where brand mood may outrun product proof.
