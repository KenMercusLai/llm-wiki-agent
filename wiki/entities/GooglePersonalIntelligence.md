# Google Personal Intelligence

Google Personal Intelligence is the account-integrated assistant capability named in [[tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128]]. [[ChristopherMims]] says it lets [[Gemini]] access Google accounts and add [[GoogleCalendar]] appointments from spoken instructions.

The wiki importance is not the product name alone, but the pattern. Google Personal Intelligence turns [[AIAssistantAugmentation]] into [[AIAssistantServiceEntry]] by letting an assistant act inside a user's existing account and productivity tools. That creates usefulness through convenience, while also implying [[AgentPermissionBoundaries]] because the assistant can touch real personal data and scheduled commitments.
