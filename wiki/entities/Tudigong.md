# 土地

土地 is the local land-god office discussed in [[136-chunri-mingmei-liaoliao-guishen-827911486]]. The episode presents land gods as low-ranking but close to everyday life: more like respected local elders, neighborhood aunties and uncles, or grass-roots administrators than distant cosmic rulers.

In the source, land gods make [[LocalDeityGovernance]] concrete. A death may be reported at a land temple, almost like canceling a household registration in the world of the living. The episode also notes that land-god worship often includes paired or female forms such as 土地婆 or 土地奶奶, making it one of the more intimate and gender-varied zones of folk deity practice.
