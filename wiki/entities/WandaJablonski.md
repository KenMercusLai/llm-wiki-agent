# Wanda Jablonski

Wanda Jablonski appears in [[the-secret-meeting-that-launched-opec]] as the oil journalist whose reporting and relationships helped producer countries understand the [[SevenSistersOilMajors|Seven Sisters]] system. The episode describes her publication, Petroleum Intelligence Weekly, as a highly influential industry source.

Her role is not simply biographical. In the episode, Jablonski shows how information can change bargaining power: once officials from oil-producing countries could compare notes about company behavior, they had a stronger basis for forming [[OPEC]] and pursuing [[OilProducerSupplyCoordination]].
