# Red Truck Orchards

Red Truck Orchards is the Michigan cherry vinegar company discussed in [[advice-line-with-jeffrey-hollender-of-seventh-generation-f8ce2410-1176-47d9-be44-03f9fa945714]]. [[PhilHolstead]] describes it as a vinegar made from whole cherries through a double-fermentation process, sold online and through about 50 Michigan retailers.

## Key Points
- The company wants to support distressed farms in Northwest Michigan while building a new vinegar base around cherries.
- A bottle costs $17.99, and the company is not yet profitable in the episode.
- Its main challenge is communicating usage and benefits quickly enough to create trial.
- Guy and Jeffrey recommend simple daily-use messaging, sampling, regional storytelling, social education, and tracking repeat purchase.
