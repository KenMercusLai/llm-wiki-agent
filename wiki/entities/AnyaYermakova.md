# Anya Yermakova

Anya Yermakova is a collaborator with [[MelodyJue]] and [[JacobCram]] on *Invisible Kelp Forest from Smell to Sound*, discussed in [[melody-jue-ocean-memory-wxagwid1oeg]]. The project uses fiction and sound installation to translate underwater olfaction in a kelp forest.

Yermakova's role belongs to the source's art-science approach to [[Chemosensation]]. Translating smell into sound is treated less as a literal data display than as a way to make human listeners encounter [[OceanMemory]] and [[MultispeciesArchives]] through unfamiliar sensory terms.

## Connections
- [[MelodyJue]] and [[JacobCram]] — collaborators on the kelp-forest olfaction project.
- [[Chemosensation]] — sensory focus of the project.
- [[OceanMemory]] and [[MultispeciesArchives]] — broader memory frames the project supports.
