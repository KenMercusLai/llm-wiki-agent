# 《盛世的崩塌》

《盛世的崩塌》 is the Tang-history book previewed in [[jiaqi-moyu-geng-jiankang-574391976]]. The hosts describe it as a strong, lively, and somewhat counter-routine account of the An Lushan Rebellion era, linking politics, literature, and ordinary life rather than treating the crisis only as court history.

The episode names [[GuoJinglong|郭静龙]] as the author and says the book opens with Nanzhao and Tang military conflict around the Xuanzong period, including attention to Tang army grave sites near Xiaguan. The wiki does not yet have a full reading of the book; this page stores the episode's preview as a future branch signal.
