# Kejian

Kejian, or 科健, is presented in [[no-208-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-shang-motuoluola-nuojiya-he-ailixin-de-zhushen-huanghun-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-23-998056376]] as an early influential domestic Chinese handset brand. The episode says Kejian was backed by the Chinese Academy of Sciences system and launched the KCH2000, described in the source as the first mainland Chinese self-owned-brand Chinese GSM phone.

## Source Position
- Kejian complicates the simple claim that [[BirdMobile]] was the first important domestic handset brand; the source says Kejian had earlier technological and brand significance.
- Its peak around 2002 and Everton shirt sponsorship show the first wave's ambition, while its later decline illustrates the fragility of brands built on incomplete technology, repair, and after-sales systems.
- Kejian belongs to the same first-wave pattern as TCL, Amoi, Panda, and other brands that were later hit by cheaper [[TurnkeyHandsetSolutions]] and [[ShanzhaiPhones]].
