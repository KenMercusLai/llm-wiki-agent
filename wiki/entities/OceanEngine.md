# Ocean Engine

Ocean Engine appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as ByteDance's unified advertising system. The episode says ByteDance integrated Toutiao, Douyin, Xigua, Huoshan, and other traffic into Ocean Engine in 2019, creating a more unified buying and optimization surface.

The source uses Ocean Engine to explain why ByteDance's domestic advertising revenue could pass and then greatly exceed Tencent's. The key claim is structural: unified traffic, recommendation data, conversion measurement, and advertiser workflow can compound faster than scattered product-level monetization.

[[quanmian-yazhi-buliu-kongdang-zijie-tiaodong-ruhe-zuo-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-7-ji-lqszvmur6jv8b9xt8rjgmwfdqtec]] adds the internal-demand side: [[XuHongliang]] says ByteDance EUG became a major buyer of internal traffic after 2020, with "亲兄弟明算账" accounting and strict internal rebates. The source links Ocean Engine to self-attribution, internal traffic allocation, and distinctions among new, reactivated, reinstalled, silent, and device-changed users.
