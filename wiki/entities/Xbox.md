# Xbox

Xbox appears in [[jiu-shidai-diantai-28-shiti-youxi-de-shidai-zhongjie-zhiji-buru-chongxin-dingyi-yongyou-998430652]] as a comparison platform when the episode asks whether [[Sony]]'s reported physical-disc retreat will become an excuse or reference point for other console ecosystems.

The episode does not present Xbox as a counterweight that will definitely preserve physical games. Instead, it suggests that console platform holders share incentives around [[DigitalGameDistribution]], storefront control, and reduced dependence on retail channels.
