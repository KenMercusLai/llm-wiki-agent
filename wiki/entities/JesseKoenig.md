# Jesse Koenig

Jesse Koenig is the [[JesseAndBens]] founder/caller in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. He brings the Advice Line question of how to position a better frozen fry: either toward people already concerned about seed oils and ultra-processed foods or toward a broader audience that mainly needs to taste the difference.

The advice he receives is to let product quality lead, use hot sampling and demos to create immediate sensory proof, and listen for customer wording that can travel through word of mouth and retail buyer conversations.
