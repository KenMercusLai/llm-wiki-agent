# SBM Intelligence

SBM Intelligence appears in [[latin-lessons-the-donroe-doctrine-boost-6a4233e26b6690f46aae4e3d]] as the organization behind the [[JollofIndex]]. The episode uses that index to make [[Nigeria]]'s food-price shock legible through ingredients for a familiar meal rather than through abstract inflation numbers.

The source's use of SBM Intelligence matters because it turns household food baskets into a recurring economic signal. It also allows comparison with [[Ghana]], where the same jollof frame shows a different inflation and currency outcome.

## Connections
- [[JollofIndex]] - food-basket measure associated with SBM Intelligence in the source.
- [[Nigeria]] and [[Ghana]] - country cases compared through the index.
- [[FoodInflation]] - broader concept measured through meal affordability.
