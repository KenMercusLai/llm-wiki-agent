# Tocqueville Road Trip

Tocqueville Road Trip is the [[EconomistPodcasts]] series introduced in [[trailer-tocqueville-road-trip-6a315642780d0f7579af216c]]. [[JohnPrideaux]] presents it as a modern retracing of [[AlexisDeTocqueville]]'s 1831 journey through the [[UnitedStates]], using [[DemocracyInAmerica]] as the historical guide.

The trailer frames the series around a present-day test of [[AmericaAsIdea]]. By speaking with Americans across social positions, the show will ask whether the democratic promise Tocqueville saw is still alive, damaged, or outdated as an explanatory model.
