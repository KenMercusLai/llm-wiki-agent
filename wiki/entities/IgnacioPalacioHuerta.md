# Ignacio Palacio Huerta

Ignacio Palacio Huerta is the economist whose penalty database drives [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]]. The episode says he began collecting thousands of professional penalties in the 1990s and used that data to test whether high-stakes players behaved close to [[GameTheory]] predictions.

He becomes the practical bridge from theory to competition. Before the 2008 Champions League final, [[ChelseaFC]] asked him for a report on [[ManchesterUnited]] penalty habits. His advice helped identify [[CristianoRonaldo]]'s paused-run-up pattern and [[EdwinVanDerSar]]'s diving tendency, but the shootout also showed that data can create new predictability when a team follows it too uniformly.
