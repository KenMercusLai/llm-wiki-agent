# 鸡枞 / Jizong

[[Jizong|鸡枞]] is the mushroom that [[ACuo|阿错]] places at the center of her personal hierarchy in [[150-chi-jun-he-acuo-liao-yunnan-de-jizong-songrong-jianshouqing-ca-koushui-897563165]]. The episode presents it as fresh, sweet, tender, and connected to termite nests, making it a useful case for [[YunnanWildMushroomCulture]] and [[ForagingEthics]].

The source stresses that preserved forms such as oil chicken termite mushroom or frozen chicken termite mushroom are compromises for storage. Fresh chicken termite mushroom is treated as a seasonal event whose value depends on local timing, known mushroom nests, and not damaging the termite ecology that lets it return.

## Key Claims
- 鸡枞's appeal in this source is freshness, tenderness, sweetness, and aftertaste, not only a vague "chicken flavor."
- Its termite-nest ecology makes it a food example of ecological dependence.
- Local sayings about encouraging, thanking, or not exposing chicken termite mushroom function as informal protection of a recurring resource.
