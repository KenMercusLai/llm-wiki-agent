# AlphaGeometry

AlphaGeometry is the geometry-solving AI system referenced in [[137-dui-hong-letong-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-ai-for-math-ba-shuxue-biancheng-lean-shuxue-tianshu-zhong-de-zhengming-zhijue-bei-chuangzao-yu-bei-faxian-de-lha-faiwxtget0qmbcosts3cb5vb]]. [[HongLetong]] uses it to connect her childhood habit of turning geometry into algebraic or symbolic manipulation with the broader idea that AI systems may solve mathematics in non-human styles.
