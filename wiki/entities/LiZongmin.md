# 李宗闵

李宗闵 appears in [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]] as one of the figures grouped with [[NiuSengru|牛僧儒]] in the [[NiuLiPartyStruggle|牛李党争]]. The episode uses him mainly to make the factional field legible before moving into [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]].

The page is source-scoped. Its value is as a connector between 牛僧儒's political setting, [[LiDeyu|李德裕]]'s rival pole, and the broader [[LateTangPoliticalPoetics]] concern with reputation, faction, and literary afterlife.
