# Jacobin Club / 雅各宾俱乐部

The Jacobin Club / 雅各宾俱乐部 appears in [[125-mala-zhisi-beibi-de-ansha-huo-zhengyi-de-chujue-783972516]] as the revolutionary club that eventually becomes associated with radical republicanism and terror. The episode notes that it began as a broader "friends of the constitution" milieu before factional splitting and purges hardened it into a more exclusive revolutionary force.

[[JeanPaulMarat|Marat]], [[MaximilienRobespierre|Robespierre]], and [[GeorgesDanton|Danton]] form the episode's simplified Jacobin leadership cluster. Through them, the source connects [[RevolutionaryVirtuePolitics|virtue politics]], denunciation, public sincerity, price controls, anti-interest rhetoric, and mass fear into [[RevolutionaryTerrorPolitics]].

The Jacobin case also extends [[PostVictoryOppressionRisk]]. The episode's danger is not only that rebels may overthrow an old authority, but that they may then treat their own coercion as justice and the next dissent as treason.
