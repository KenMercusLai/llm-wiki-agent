# Arkema

Arkema is a chemical and materials company mentioned in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as an upstream supplier in the running-shoe foam ecosystem. The source places Arkema with [[BASF]] and [[Evonik]] as part of the material base that lets brands build lighter, softer, and more resilient midsoles.

## Key Claims
- Running-shoe innovation depends on supplier materials that brands can foam, tune, and integrate into product geometry.
- Supplier access can reduce durable brand-only technology barriers because multiple brands may reach similar base materials.
- The source uses Arkema to make the race-shoe story a materials ecosystem rather than only a Nike-versus-Adidas story.
