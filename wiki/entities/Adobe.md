# Adobe

Adobe appears in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as the professional creative-tool comparison case for [[Meitu]]. The episode says Adobe embeds AI into tools such as [[AdobePhotoshop]], emphasizes copyright-safe model training, and still faces investor concern because AI inference cost has not yet translated into enough incremental AI revenue.

The source uses Adobe to show that owning a strong application franchise does not automatically solve the AI transition. A professional tool can have brand, workflow, file-format habit, and user trust, but if new AI functions increase cost faster than monetization, the [[AIApplicationLayerMoat]] still needs pricing power, workflow lock-in, and clear output value.
