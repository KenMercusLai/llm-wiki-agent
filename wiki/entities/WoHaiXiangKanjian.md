# 《我还想看见》

《我还想看见》 is [[Ajiao|阿娇 / 柱子哥]]'s book discussed in [[184-zhenshi-yu-wo-you-wanjun-zhi-zhong-huanying-xianv-ajiao-990545010]]. The episode presents it as a second-stage writing project after an earlier, more tool-like cancer book: it aims to show the person beyond treatment, including travel, work, identity, death views, and changing emotional states across years.

The book matters to the wiki because it turns dated personal writing into [[PublicIllnessWriting]]. Ajiao insists on keeping writing dates because her world view, death view, and emotional position changed across treatment years; the date becomes evidence of process rather than a decorative note.

## Key Claims
- The book is not only a cancer-survival text; it is a wider self-record of a person living under recurring illness.
- It supports [[ProfessionalDignityBeyondPatientIdentity]] because Ajiao wants readers to evaluate her writing without sympathy inflation.
- It makes [[HospiceCare]] and death quality part of public conversation rather than private family avoidance.
- Its structure supports [[WholeProcessCancerDocumentation]] by preserving changing views instead of smoothing them into a single inspiring message.
