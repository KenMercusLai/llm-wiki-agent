# Qiaodan Sports

Qiaodan Sports is a Chinese sportswear brand discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as one of the domestic brands experimenting with very light racing shoes. The source uses Qiaodan Sports as part of the evidence that Chinese brands are active participants in the super-shoe race rather than only low-end imitators.

## Key Claims
- Qiaodan Sports is grouped with [[LiNing]] and [[361Degrees]] as a Chinese brand that explored sub-100-gram concept racing shoes.
- The source's broader claim is that domestic brands can move quickly, but still need more stable product teams and more original technical leadership.
- Overseas growth is presented as an opportunity if Chinese brands can pair supply-chain strength with credible performance products.
