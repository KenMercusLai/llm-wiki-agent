# Cassian Andor

Cassian Andor is the central character connecting [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] and [[RogueOne|《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One]] in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode describes him at the start as a marginal young man, small-time hustler, and survivor from a broken industrial environment rather than a natural revolutionary hero.

The source tracks his movement through the [[Aldhani]] raid, [[NarkinaFive]] imprisonment, and [[Ferrix]] uprising. His significance is not that he commands the people around him, but that he gradually stops treating [[GalacticEmpire]] as a background condition and begins choosing risk within the [[RebelAlliance]] orbit.
