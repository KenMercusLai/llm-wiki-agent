# Sony Pictures

Sony Pictures appears in [[dang-heike-gongpo-le-riben-de-guomin-pijiu-chule-jugong-daoqian-tamen-hai-neng-zuo-shenme-feat-top-of-japan-keji-luandun]] as a contrast case to [[AsahiGroup]]. The hosts discuss the 2014 Sony Pictures hack to show that some data loss cannot be solved by restoration alone: unreleased films, employee information, and private communications can lose value or cause harm once exposed.

The case widens the episode's security lesson. [[OfflineBackupRecoveryDrills]] can restore encrypted systems, but leaked information may still create business, legal, and reputational damage. This distinguishes operational recovery from confidentiality recovery.
