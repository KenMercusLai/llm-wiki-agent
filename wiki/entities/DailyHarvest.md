# Daily Harvest

Daily Harvest is the food company at the center of [[the-sneaky-way-companies-get-new-chemicals-into-our-food]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode focuses on its lentil and leek crumble, which contained [[TaraFlour]] and was linked in the source to severe illness, liver problems, and gallbladder removals.

The page matters because Daily Harvest turns [[FoodAdditiveRegulation]] from an abstract legal system into a consumer-product failure. The source says customers who accepted the company's terms of service faced [[ConsumerArbitrationBarrier|arbitration limits]], while non-signers helped litigation move forward and expose the [[Smirks]] and [[MolinosPeru|Molinos]] supply chain through [[FoodSafetyLitigationDiscovery]].

The episode also says Daily Harvest was later purchased by [[Chobani]]. That sale is presented as part of the limited-consequence frame rather than as evidence that Chobani caused the earlier product problem.
