# Mujian

Mujian is [[Roi]]'s AI simulation interactive content platform in [[ta-xiang-zao-yi-ge-ai-shidai-de-chaoji-youlechang-duitan-roi-mujian-chuangshiren-ceo-ltfh-on-4qqz-ardqury4g0jejec]]. The source says the company had completed an angel round, had roughly ten full-time people, and was still early enough that revenue and profit had not yet been established.

The product thesis is that users want to experience lives and roles they cannot access offline: large-scale dating scenarios, historical travel, sponsor-mother reward fantasies, stock-trading jokes, coffee-shop scenarios, or other repeatable virtual life fragments. Roi wants creators to use tools, courses, prompts, code, media, and AI models to build these experiences inside a platform rather than selling scattered files or links elsewhere.

Mujian's operator problem is to combine creation tools, content distribution, account identity, consumption, creator monetization, token cost, and platform-level taste. Roi compares the long-term ambition to an AI-era [[Xiaohongshu]] for virtual lives, but argues that the platform needs an AI-native core, model stability, low latency, feedback design, and support for [[AISuperCreators]] rather than only a feed of small interactions.
