# Maria Hollenhorst

Maria Hollenhorst is the reporter for the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode [[tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128]]. Her reporting uses a Clearspace user, her own test of the app, [[TanyaSujohn]]'s design critique, and [[BenGoldhersh]]'s physical anti-scrolling experiments to frame phone reduction as a market and design problem.

Her role in the wiki is to connect everyday screen-time anxiety to [[DigitalDetoxEconomy]] and [[ScreenTimeFriction]]. The segment does not treat phone overuse only as weak discipline; it asks why consumers buy tools to resist devices and platforms designed to be hard to leave.
