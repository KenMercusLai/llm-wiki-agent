# Xinghaitu

Xinghaitu is the [[EmbodiedAI]] robotics startup founded by [[GaoJiyang]] and discussed in [[132-dui-xinghaitu-chuangshiren-gaojiyang-de-3-xiaoshi-fangtan]]. The source describes the company as building whole machines, foundation models, and post-training tools rather than selling only a robot "brain" or an algorithm module.

The company's strategic claim is that a robotics startup needs a [[PhysicalWorldDataFlywheel]]: the robot body enters real scenes, collects useful operation data, improves models, and becomes a more valuable production worker. That is why [[Xinghaitu]] moved from an early last-mile-delivery idea toward [[WheelBasedDualArmRobots]], manipulation intelligence, and developer-to-production markets.

## Key Points
- Early backers for the last-mile delivery direction included IDG, Baidu Ventures, and GSR Ventures, but Gao later judged the original plan as immature.
- [[ZhaoHang]] and Tianwei were named as early founding participants, while [[YangZeyi]] later helped the team fill the whole-machine and hardware-system gap.
- The source says the company had more than 150 customers and had open-sourced datasets and base models by the time of the interview.
- The technical architecture combines a vision-language task decomposition layer with [[VisionLanguageActionModels]] for physical action.
- [[XuHuazhe]]'s planned departure is framed as a move toward 2C family applications, not as a rejection of algorithm innovation inside [[Xinghaitu]].
- Gao describes the company culture as customer-value oriented, high-intensity, and wolf-like, while still anchored by a long-term productivity vision.
