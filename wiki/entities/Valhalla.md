# Valhalla / 瓦尔哈拉

Valhalla is the heroic afterlife hall in [[NorseMythology]] discussed by [[XiaoChuzhou|萧楚舟]] in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The episode links it to [[Odin|Odin]]'s preparation for [[Ragnarok|Ragnarok]]: fallen warriors are gathered not simply as reward, but as part of the coming final battle.

In the source, Valhalla also helps explain why games and fantasy reuse Norse myth so readily. It turns death, glory, training, and doom into a vivid system that can be adapted into playable or cinematic hero worlds.
