# Stephanie Waldrop

Stephanie Waldrop appears in [[riding-with-the-repo-man-update]] as the borrower-side case in the [[PlanetMoney]] account of [[SubprimeAutoLending]]. She bought a used Ford Fusion with a high-rate subprime loan after a dealer said her credit would not support the Ford Flex she wanted.

Her case makes [[ConsumerLoanRisk]] concrete. A $466 monthly payment at 23% interest was possible while she earned about $4,000 a month as a restaurant manager, but became unaffordable after she left that job for a lower-paid role that was better for her mental health. The repossession hurt her [[PersonalCreditRecord]], added a $700 repossession fee if she wanted the car back, and made finding work harder because the car itself was part of her mobility and income infrastructure.
