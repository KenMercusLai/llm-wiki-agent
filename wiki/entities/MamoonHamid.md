# Mamoon Hamid

Mamoon Hamid appears in [[socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8-socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8]] as the [[KleinerPerkins]] investor who led [[Rippling]]'s Series A. [[ParkerConrad]] says Rippling received many term sheets despite the [[Zenefits]] controversy, and names Hamid as the investor who led the round.

In the source, Hamid functions as a validation point in Conrad's [[FounderReputationRecovery]] arc: some investors remained wary because of Conrad, while others underwrote Rippling and Conrad as the same reason to both worry and invest.
