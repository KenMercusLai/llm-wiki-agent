# Kissmetrics

Kissmetrics appears in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]] as one of the analytics products [[SpenserSkates]] and [[CurtisLiu]] considered insufficient for their [[Sonalight]] retention questions. In the source, it functions as part of the market map around the eventual [[Amplitude]] pivot.

The episode's claim is not that Kissmetrics lacked value in general. It is that the founders wanted a sharper [[ProductAnalytics]] workflow for understanding product behavior, cohorts, and retention, which became the opportunity they pursued.
