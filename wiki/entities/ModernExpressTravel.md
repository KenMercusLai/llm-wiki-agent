# Modern Express Travel

Modern Express Travel represents the source's "现代运通" acquisition thread in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says it was a telephone-plus-internet hotel-booking competitor with meaningful monthly booking volume before [[Ctrip]] acquired it around 2000.

The source uses the acquisition to explain how Ctrip scaled by buying operating capacity, not just traffic. [[WangShengli]] and team capacity are treated as part of the move from early experiment to stronger hotel-booking platform.

- [[Ctrip]] — acquirer and platform that absorbed the booking volume.
- [[WangShengli]] — founder/operator connected to the company in the source.
