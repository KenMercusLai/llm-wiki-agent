# 《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》 / Eichmann in Jerusalem

[[103-ta-shi-cichuan-yewu-de-guang-ta-shi-hanna-alunte-737939789]] treats 《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》 / Eichmann in Jerusalem as the work that made [[HannahArendt|Hannah Arendt]] both famous and fiercely attacked. The episode says Arendt went to Jerusalem to observe [[AdolfEichmann|Adolf Eichmann]] after his capture in [[Argentina]] and trial in [[Israel]], expecting a chance to look directly at a perpetrator of [[TheHolocaust|the Holocaust]].

The controversy in the source has two linked parts. First, Arendt described Eichmann as a bureaucratic, ordinary-seeming man rather than a monstrous personality, giving rise to [[BanalityOfEvil]]. Second, she criticized some [[JewishCouncils|Jewish Councils]] for cooperating with Nazi procedures, a claim the episode frames through [[ResponsibilityVsGuilt]] rather than victim-blaming.
