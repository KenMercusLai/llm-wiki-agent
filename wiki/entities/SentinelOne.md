# SentinelOne

SentinelOne appears in [[can-computer-hackers-get-inside-your-mind]] as the cybersecurity company where [[JuanAndresGuerreroSaade|JAGS]] works. The episode uses JAGS's role there to ground the [[Fast16|Fast 16]] investigation in professional malware research rather than in a purely speculative story.

In this source, SentinelOne is not the target or alleged creator of the malware. It is the institutional context for [[AIAssistedMalwareReverseEngineering]], old-malware reconstruction, and the public announcement that Fast 16 appeared to be a major [[CyberSabotage]] weapon.
