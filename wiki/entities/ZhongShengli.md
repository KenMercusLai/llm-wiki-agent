# 钟声礼

钟声礼 is the author of [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] as discussed in [[168-fangxuehou-de-xiaoxiang-zhi-qingchun-zhi-nanian-de-youyi-richang-tuili-qiangshi-huigui-953669535]]. The [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode treats the book as a recent Chinese [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]] work that has reached beyond a narrow mystery-reading circle.

The source emphasizes craft balance rather than total praise. The hosts value the book's ability to make a tofu pudding order, a bicycle, and a school alley carry friendship and dignity, while noting that 钟声礼 has reportedly said one later reversal may be too forceful.

[[131-richang-zhimi-tuili-shi-wo-wei-neng-shuo-chukou-de-zhenxin-805617229]] mentions 钟声礼 and [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] as material the host considered for the broader [[DailyLifeMystery|日常之谜]] episode before saving it for later. This supports treating episode 168 as the later full expansion of the recommendation.

## Key Claims
- 钟声礼 is used by the episode as a case of contemporary Chinese mystery breaking past small-circle readership.
- The author's relevant contribution is not only puzzle design, but daily-life emotional inference.
- The episode keeps praise source-scoped by acknowledging imperfections in later structure.
- Episode 131 situates the author beside Japanese daily-life mystery examples while preserving the later episode's Chinese-publishing context.
