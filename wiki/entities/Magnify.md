# Magnify

Magnify is the finance-search project discussed by [[TangHaocheng]] in [[ep69-ai-shidai-lailin-touzi-buzai-shi-danji-moshi-lppxmdl3wlrrtswxwpzekz-qzygn]]. The episode describes it as using natural-language technology to help ordinary retail investors and U.S. registered investment advisers search ETF and mutual-fund products by conditions.

In the episode's arc, Magnify is a precursor to broader [[AIInvestmentResearch]] and [[FinancialAIAgents]]: it focuses on finding suitable fund products, while the newer AI assistant ambition extends into information gathering, reasoning, watchlists, alerts, and decision support.
