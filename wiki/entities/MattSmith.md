# Matt Smith

Matt Smith is the founder behind [[WanderingSoulBeer]] and a caller in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. He has worked in the beer industry for about 15 years and brews through several New England breweries while selling through stores and restaurants in several states.

Matt's question is how to keep a personal, people-centered brand identity without letting the grief and work of the business consume him. The episode uses his case to show that founder separation is operational: a shared office, working hours, regular walks, or music time can protect the founder and also create more space for ideas.
