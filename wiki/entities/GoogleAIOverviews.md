# Google AI Overviews

Google AI Overviews are the AI-generated search summaries discussed in [[tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] Bytes episode, [[AnitaRamaswamy]] says [[Google]] is making source links more prominent inside the summaries, partly because publishers, users, and regulators are pressing for clearer attribution and trust signals.

The product is important to the wiki because it sits at the junction of [[OpenWebTrafficDecline]], [[SearchAdvertisingDecline]], [[GenerativeEngineOptimization]], and [[AIAnswerSourceAttribution]]. AI Overviews can answer users directly, but that same convenience may reduce outbound clicks to publishers such as [[DailyMail]], while making source visibility and [[PlatformAntitrust]] scrutiny more important.
