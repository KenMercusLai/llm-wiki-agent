# MakeMyTrip

MakeMyTrip appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as part of [[Ctrip]]'s global online-travel investment and expansion story. The source uses it to show that Ctrip's market ambitions extended beyond China's domestic OTA consolidation.

In the episode, MakeMyTrip sits beside [[Skyscanner]] and the Trip.com brand as evidence that online-travel platforms can seek scale across countries once booking infrastructure, traffic acquisition, supplier relationships, and app habits become reusable.
