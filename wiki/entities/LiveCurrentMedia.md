# Live Current Media

Live Current Media appears in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]] as the acquirer of [[AutomaticAuctions]]. [[HarjTaggar]] says the team chose Live Current's roughly $5 million cash-and-stock offer over an all-stock offer from [[Meta|Facebook]], partly because Facebook stock was widely seen as overvalued at the time.

The entity's main wiki role is in the episode's decision-under-uncertainty branch. The acquisition gave Harj a first startup outcome, but the later reflection shows how hard it was in the moment to value private company stock, strategic fit, and personal opportunity against a concrete offer.
