# Magna Carta / 大宪章

Magna Carta / 大宪章 appears in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]] as a historical symbol of distrust toward unchecked royal power. The episode uses its "king under law" meaning to explain why modern criminal law is not only about catching criminals, but also about limiting the authority that punishes.

In the source, Magna Carta belongs to [[CriminalLawAsPowerLimitation]]: legal procedure matters because the state can become more dangerous than the criminal when punishment is not bounded by law.
