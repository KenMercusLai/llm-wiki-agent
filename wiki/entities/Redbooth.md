# Redbooth

Redbooth is the project-management company that merged with [[AeroFS]] around 2017 in [[tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final-tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final]]. [[YuriSagalov]] says he became CEO of the combined company and that Redbooth continued as a profitable project-management business while AeroFS itself was wound down.

The source uses Redbooth less as a product case than as a founder-responsibility case. Sagalov says the merger was not forced by investors and that one reason to pursue it was protecting employees whose green-card processes could have been damaged by an abrupt shutdown.
