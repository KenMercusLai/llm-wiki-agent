# Raul Castro

Raul Castro appears in [[dark-times-for-cubas-economic-experiment]] as the Cuban leader who expanded private-sector space after [[FidelCastro]] became ill. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode says Raul acknowledged that [[Cuba]]'s economy was in poor shape, allowed small businesses to hire outside their families, and broadened permitted occupations to nearly 200 options.

The source frames Raul's reforms as [[ConstrainedMarketReform]]. They widened opportunity for businesses such as [[CityCleta]], but [[RicardoTorres]] says the state still wanted private activity to remain a complement to the state sector rather than become more important than it.
