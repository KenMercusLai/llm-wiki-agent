# Shi Congbin / 施从斌

Shi Congbin appears in [[41-shijianqiao-fuchouan-chaoyue-habeimasi-de-qing-yu-fa-614550038]] as [[ShiJianqiao]]'s father and the central figure in her revenge claim. The episode says he was captured by [[SunChuanfang]], then executed and subjected to head-display, which Shi Jianqiao later presented as an intolerable humiliation rather than an ordinary wartime death.

His role in the source is mostly posthumous. Shi Jianqiao's leaflets, statements, and courtroom arguments repeatedly returned to his death to make the killing of Sun legible as filial duty, restoration of dignity, and moral response.

## Key Claims
- Shi Congbin's death supplies the emotional and moral ground of the case.
- The source distinguishes battlefield death from humiliating treatment after capture; this distinction made the revenge argument stronger.
- His memory was mediated through Shi Jianqiao's public storytelling, not only through military history.
