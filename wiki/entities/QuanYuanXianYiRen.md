# 《全员嫌疑人》

《全员嫌疑人》 is the main mystery collection discussed in [[158-quanyuan-mingzhentan-tuili-shupian-lai-yibao-929377122]], a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode that frames the book as [[PuzzleSnackMystery|推理薯片]]. The episode says the book contains seven short stories, all using [[ClosedCircleMystery|暴风雪山庄]]-style confinement, plus a larger wrapper puzzle that asks readers to identify the real culprit from among the story protagonists.

The book's recurring character is 小何, a police officer whose [[WatsonPower|华生力]] makes nearby people suddenly reason like detectives. That device lets each case produce a new temporary "名侦探" while keeping 小何 in the assistant-like position.

## Key Claims
- The book is presented as puzzle-first mystery rather than realist crime fiction.
- Its pleasure comes from bounded suspect pools, clue reversals, failed theories, and final answers.
- The seven-case wrapper structure turns the collection itself into an additional mystery.
- 小何's [[WatsonPower|华生力]] makes the book an ensemble-detective premise rather than a single-genius detective series.
