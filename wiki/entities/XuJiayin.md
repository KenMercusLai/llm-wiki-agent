# Xu Jiayin

Xu Jiayin enters the wiki through [[building-things-and-breaking-things-in-china-summer-school-world-tour]] as the founder of [[Evergrande]] and the person the episode uses to symbolize the scale of China's property-developer wealth and excess. The source places him after [[DesmondShum]]'s smaller airport-logistics story to show how the real-estate boom expanded from lucrative local deals into a national debt cycle.

In the episode, Xu matters because Evergrande's missed payments, enormous liabilities, and fraud allegations make [[ChinaRealEstateDebtCycle]] legible as a systemic problem rather than only a colorful boom-era anecdote.
