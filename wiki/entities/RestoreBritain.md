# Restore Britain

Restore Britain appears in [[coming-in-andy-britains-prime-minister-in-waiting-6a3512974a2a3be0f419384b]] as a harder-right party putting pressure on [[ReformUK]]. The episode treats it as part of the by-election signal that Reform may be losing its uncontested position in British politics.

The page records Restore Britain only through this source's pressure-map role. [[LabourPartyUK]] can pressure Reform electorally when represented by [[AndyBurnham]], the [[ConservativePartyUK]] can pressure Reform from the traditional right, and Restore Britain can make Reform look less singular to voters seeking a more extreme alternative.
