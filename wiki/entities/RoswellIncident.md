# Roswell Incident / 罗斯维尔事件

The Roswell Incident enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as a Cold War secrecy case that became durable [[UFOConspiracyCulture]]. The episode presents the likely origin as a high-altitude reconnaissance-balloon crash rather than proof of alien bodies.

Its importance is not that the story was simply silly. The episode uses Roswell to show how official secrecy, military technology, document gaps, and public imagination can reinforce each other until an ordinary explanation feels less emotionally satisfying than a hidden alien one.

[[95-dushi-chuanshuo-lieqi-gushi-he-women-neixin-shenchu-de-jiaolv-723831611]] adds Roswell as an [[UrbanLegend|urban legend]] case. The episode emphasizes information opacity and the way mundane components, including logo-marked parts, could be reinterpreted as alien writing once a mystery frame had taken hold.
