# Keir Starmer

Keir Starmer is the British prime minister at the center of [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]]. The episode frames him as having survived an immediate [[LabourLeadershipCrisis]] while remaining politically vulnerable because his government looks directionless, unpopular, and slow to deliver.

The source treats the [[PeterMandelson]]-[[JeffreyEpstein]] affair as a judgment problem for Starmer rather than the root cause of his weakness. His larger problem is [[PoliticalDeliveryGap]]: the episode says voters and Labour MPs see limited visible progress on small boats, NHS waiting times, and the government's broader mission.
