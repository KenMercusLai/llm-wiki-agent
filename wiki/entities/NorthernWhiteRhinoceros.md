# Northern White Rhinoceros / 北部白犀牛

The northern white rhinoceros is the strongest extinction case in [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. In the episode's retelling of [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》]], [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] see the subspecies when the global population is described as only 22 animals.

The hosts then contrast that late-1980s encounter with the current state of only two surviving females, making the northern white rhinoceros a central example of [[FunctionalExtinction]] and [[LastChanceEcologicalWitnessing]]. The episode treats the animal not as a mascot for despair, but as proof that some "last chance" observations really can become last glimpses of a living population.
