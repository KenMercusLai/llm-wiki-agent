# Stacy Schiff / 斯泰西·西夫

Stacy Schiff / 斯泰西·西夫 is the author of [[TheWitchesSalem1692|《猎巫：塞勒姆1692》]] in [[91-liewu-sailemu-1692-cong-yizheng-kaishi-714732018]]. The episode uses her account of the [[SalemWitchTrials|塞勒姆猎巫]] to move from dramatic historical detail into a broader explanation of [[WitchHuntMechanism|猎巫机制]].

In the wiki, Schiff currently matters less as a biographical figure than as the source author behind a historical case. Her book gives the episode a narrative path from girls' symptoms and village rumor to [[SpectralEvidence]], executions, later apology, and modern public-judgment analogies.
