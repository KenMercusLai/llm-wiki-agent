# Lai Xinlu

Lai Xinlu is the [[ShareAI]] founder interviewed in [[tan-mi-claude-code-gao-dong-agent-harness-dui-tan-lai-xin-lu-lkluk3i7c4gzw4jvxee7odsfgis3]] about [[AgentHarness]] design. He argues that harness means the model-external system around an agent: execution tools, context and state, memory, compression, handoff, and governance across multiple agents.

His technical position is model-centered. He accepts that harness design strongly affects what agents can do, but he says the model remains the first source of agent intelligence, followed by context and then tools. He favors "more context, less control" and CLI/Unix-style agent interfaces over brittle prompt-node or flow-graph orchestration.
