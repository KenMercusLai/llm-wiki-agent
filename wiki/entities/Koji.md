# Koji

In the [[Xiaoban]] source, Koji frames the robot as the first companion robot he has genuinely wanted to buy. In the [[Youju]] crossover, he draws out [[Xiaoning]]'s map of [[AIInteractiveEntertainment]], AI NPCs, creator platforms, and the missing consensus product for AI games.
