# Koji

Koji is a [[ShizilukouCrossing]] host appearing in [[wo-yudao-le-di-yige-zhenzheng-xiang-mai-de-peiban-jiqiren-duihua-shibo-yueban-dongli-chuangshiren-gonglu-boke-lrydelizm0-hbk68u5cqe3ti-epb]], [[duihua-minimax-yan-junjie-m3-10x-jihua-10t-moxing-he-zhineng-de-zhongju-lqtilt8flvmv99v0gshhyfyraibe]], [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]], [[dang-women-zai-taolun-harness-de-shihou-women-zai-taolun-shenme-shendu-duitan-minimax-hermes-agent-lvhm1cfno7mqmfv3g0aajmw4zdpd]], and [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. In the [[Xiaoban]] source, Koji frames the robot as the first companion robot he has genuinely wanted to buy. In the [[MiniMax]] source, he moderates a Developer Meetup roundtable across model building, AI coding, open-source agents, financial AI, and interpretability. In the [[Youju]] crossover, he draws out [[Xiaoning]]'s map of [[AIInteractiveEntertainment]], AI NPCs, creator platforms, and the missing consensus product for AI games. In the Hermes Agent source, he frames the discussion around what people mean by [[AgentHarness]] after the domestic [[OpenCloud]] and [[OpenClaw]] wave. In the Xiaohongshu hackathon source, he frames [[AIHackathons]] as process and relationship networks, calling the event an "AI-era Paris" for ambitious creators.

In the [[Moxt]] source, Koji interviews [[ZhangHaoran]] about [[AINativeWorkspace]], organization-level context, and AI coworkers.
