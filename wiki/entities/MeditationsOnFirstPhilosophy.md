# Meditations on First Philosophy / 《第一哲学的沉思》

[[MeditationsOnFirstPhilosophy|《第一哲学的沉思》]] is one of the harder [[ReneDescartes|Descartes]] texts named in [[174-du-dikaer-shi-jian-dashi-966856702]]. The episode does not give it the same entry-level role as [[DiscourseOnMethod|《谈谈方法》]], but it places it in the reading path for people who want the more demanding version of [[MethodicDoubt]], certainty, God, and mind-body argument.

## Source Position
- The source treats the book as part of the deeper Descartes corpus rather than the best first read for general listeners.
- Its themes sit behind the episode's discussion of doubt, the thinking self, and the relation between thought and material extension.
- The text helps connect [[CogitoErgoSum]], [[CartesianDualism]], and [[SubstanceAndExtension]] into a larger metaphysical system.
