# 张一贞

张一贞 is the [[Mianji]] guest in [[e145-shangzhongle-4000-dian-zhishang-de-xinli-anmo-lrk6eqfqkjnihzf5pimbek4kqm9e]], where he discusses A-share exposure after a large rally, valuation indicators, trend-following discipline, stock-bond allocation, and the psychological cost of drawdowns. His practical stance is not a single market call; it is a shift from all-in bull-market excitement toward [[MultiStrategyAllocation]], [[AShareValuationIndicators]], and drawdown-aware [[InvestmentRiskManagement]].

## Key Points
- Uses multiple valuation and behavior indicators rather than treating any single metric as a timing oracle.
- Separates value exposure from momentum exposure so subjective judgment and rule-based [[TrendFollowing]] do not interfere with each other.
- Treats [[DrawdownPsychology]] as an investment constraint because long losses can damage action capacity even when the final return is acceptable.
- Frames ordinary-investor survival around staying present, keeping ammunition, and reducing emotional dependence on a single asset market.
