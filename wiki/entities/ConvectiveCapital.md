# Convective Capital

Convective Capital is [[BillClerico]]'s fire-tech-focused investment firm in [[socialradarss2-billclerico-final]]. The episode links the firm's thesis to Clerico's post-[[WePay]] ranch life in Mendocino County, nearby wildfire exposure, and volunteer firefighting experience.

The fund treats wildfire resilience as an overlooked startup market rather than only a public-sector cost. Clerico argues that suppression, forest management, detection, home hardening, air filtration, utilities, and insurance all create possible company opportunities, but that startups need help finding first customers inside conservative agencies, utilities, and insurers.

Convective Capital's wiki role is to connect regulated fintech pattern recognition to [[FireTechClimateResilience]]. Clerico compares fire tech to fintech in 2008: unglamorous, regulated, institution-heavy, and therefore easy for many investors to dismiss before the market infrastructure exists.
