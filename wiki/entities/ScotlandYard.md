# Scotland Yard

Scotland Yard is the policing institution discussed in [[50-kaitangshou-jieke-jiean-baogao-nvfayi-de-zhengyi-zhizuo-630666604]] through the [[JackTheRipper]] investigation. The episode presents early investigative practice as strikingly underdeveloped by later standards.

The case exposed weaknesses in evidence preservation, crime-scene photography, medical coordination, filing, press handling, and search procedure. For the wiki, Scotland Yard anchors [[ModernCriminalInvestigationFormation]]: institutional failure did not solve the case, but the failure itself helped make later forensic and investigative discipline more visible as a need.
