# Alex Rosenberg

Alex Rosenberg appears in [[venezuelas-recent-economic-history-update]] as the importer whose business makes Venezuela's currency controls concrete. The source says he needed government approval to convert bolivars into dollars before importing goods, and later waited for approval to import nonwoven fabric used in surgical clothing.

His case shows how [[CurrencyControlTrap]] became an [[ImportApprovalBottleneck]]. When official dollars stopped arriving, importers could not pay suppliers, imports froze, and shortages spread from clothing into medicine, electronics, food, and medical supplies.
