# Token Grant

Token Grant is a project from [[ZhengkeFund]] and [[ShizilukouCrossing]] described in [[agent-yuannian-di-500-tian-shenme-zai-xiaoshi-shenme-zai-dansheng-weishenme-women-bugai-zai-touzi-gui-siwei-de-ruanjian-lhwdxfpke3bmamjk4e6knk-5sn-b]]. It provides 50,000 RMB in sponsorship for people building from zero to one in the AI era, without taking equity.

## Strategic Meaning
- The project treats token budget as an early-stage constraint for AI builders.
- It fits the episode's view that early founders may be less blocked by headcount or small amounts of capital than by access to model usage and experimentation capacity.
- It is connected to agent-native projects such as [[CodePilot]] and [[YouyouAgent]].
