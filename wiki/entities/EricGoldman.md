# Eric Goldman

Eric Goldman appears in [[tech-20260205-0205-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260205-0205-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the expert explaining a Los Angeles trial over alleged social-media addiction and youth mental-health harms. His contribution is to shift the frame from general platform criticism to legal proof: plaintiffs have to show [[PlatformLegalCausation]], not merely that young people were harmed or that platform design influenced behavior.

Goldman also broadens the stakes beyond the named defendants. He argues that social-media litigation could alter internet users' experience by changing product design, pricing, platform availability, and communication tools. He expects [[Section230]] to return on appeal and warns that legal theories developed against social media could become [[InternetLiabilitySpillover]] for video games, generative AI model makers, and other internet services.
