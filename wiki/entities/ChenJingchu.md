# Chen Jingchu

Chen Jingchu is a serial entrepreneur and independent builder interviewed in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. The source says he began building projects in high school, is associated with the Insta app, has attended close to 20 hackathons, and recently became absorbed in [[VibeCoding]].

His hackathon project was a self-discipline device that recognized behaviors such as smoking or drinking through a head-mounted camera and triggered an electrode on the neck. More important for the wiki, Chen argues that [[BuildingPublic]] is one of the strongest AI-era product levers because build cost is low and social platforms can provide early exposure, feedback, and [[FastProductValidation]].
