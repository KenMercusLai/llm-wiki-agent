# Wanhua Chemical

Wanhua Chemical is a Chinese chemical-materials company mentioned in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as part of the domestic catch-up layer in running-shoe foams. The source places Wanhua alongside other Chinese research and manufacturing actors trying to close the materials gap with European suppliers.

## Key Claims
- Chinese running-shoe competitiveness depends partly on domestic materials and foaming capability, not only brand design.
- Upstream suppliers can make Chinese brands less dependent on foreign material leaders over time.
- The source treats material catch-up as a key condition for domestic brands to compete abroad through performance and price.
