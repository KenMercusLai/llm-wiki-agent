# U.S. Department of Justice

The U.S. Department of Justice appears in [[vol-265-kuayue-50-nian-de-meiguo-banben-zhizi-1001004591]] as the antitrust review body for the [[WarnerBrosDiscovery]] transaction discussed by the episode. The source says approval risk is a central constraint on media consolidation once the deal size reaches the scale of national studios, streaming libraries, and news assets.

The page's role is to connect antitrust review to [[PoliticalRegulatoryLeverage]]. The episode's claim is not that every approval is corrupt, but that political access can become economically valuable when a family or firm needs regulators to clear transactions that ordinary capital alone cannot complete.
