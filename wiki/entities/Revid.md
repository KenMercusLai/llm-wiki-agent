# Revid

Revid is an AI video creation and editing product associated with [[TeaMaker]]. In [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], [[ThibautLouisLucas]] says he acquired a small tool called Typeframe that became Revid, and that Revid became his most successful product at more than $600,000 per month.

## Key Claims
- Revid shows Tea Maker's move from founder-built products toward acquiring or partnering around products that can benefit from Lucas's distribution systems.
- The product fits the broader AI-era claim that building is easier but growth depends on [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]].
- As an AI video tool, Revid also connects to existing wiki themes around [[VideoModels]] and the commercialization of AI-enabled content production.
