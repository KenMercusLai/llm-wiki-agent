# 《开市大吉》

《开市大吉》 is the [[LaoShe|老舍]] short-story selection discussed in [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]], and also the title story inside that selection. The episode says the collection is not arranged chronologically; it is curated around conflicts that still feel present, moving from lighter comedy toward more painful insight into social life.

As the title story, 《开市大吉》 centers on several people opening a "大众医院" that wraps profit-seeking in public-service language. The hosts read it as a satire of medical fraud, brand staging, status signaling, and the customer's own vanity, not just of dishonest providers.
