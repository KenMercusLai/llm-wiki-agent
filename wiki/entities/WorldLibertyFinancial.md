# World Liberty Financial

World Liberty Financial is the Trump-family crypto company discussed in [[ep77-si-shi-wan-nian-xin-fu-ye-zhuan-le-san-shi-si-yi-te-lang-pu-jiao-ni-ruhe-gao-qian-lnrx8vkdjedxbmmcwxlnwow-pcab]]. The episode presents it as a key branch of [[PoliticalInfluenceMonetization]] because token sales, stablecoin narratives, and investor access convert the Trump brand into crypto-linked family wealth.

## Source Position
- The hosts say [[DonaldTrump]] changed from criticizing crypto to embracing it during the 2024 campaign period.
- The source describes family ownership allocations, token sales, a USD1 stablecoin branch, and equity/warrant transactions as part of the wealth calculation.
- [[BarronTrump]] is used as the most vivid beneficiary case because the episode attributes a large young-age paper and cash-value bundle to him.
- The episode links the project to [[CryptocurrencyMarketStructure]], [[Stablecoins]], and [[PaperWealthVsCashValue]] rather than treating it as a conventional operating company.
