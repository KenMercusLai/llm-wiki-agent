# Chiang Kai-shek / 蒋介石

Chiang Kai-shek appears in [[41-shijianqiao-fuchouan-chaoyue-habeimasi-de-qing-yu-fa-614550038]] through the [[NationalistGovernment]]'s handling of [[ShiJianqiao]]. The episode says [[FengYuxiang]] recorded that he persuaded Chiang to grant a special pardon after the courts had imposed and upheld a prison sentence.

Chiang also appears later in the episode's wartime section, where the Hechuan aircraft-donation campaign connected Shi Jianqiao's patriotic public identity to Nationalist wartime mobilization. The source says Chiang sent an air-force representative to the donation ceremony and arranged a flight performance.

## Key Claims
- Chiang's role in the source is institutional: he represents executive discretion over the legal outcome and state recognition of wartime patriotic mobilization.
- The pardon shows how the [[NationalistGovernment]] could turn public sympathy into sovereign state action.
- The aircraft-donation scene shows Shi Jianqiao's revenge identity being redirected into anti-Japanese national mobilization.
