# Lempire

Lempire is the company that acquired [[TweetHunter]] and [[Tapio]] from [[ThibautLouisLucas]] and his team. In [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], Lucas describes the deal as $2 million upfront with a possible $10 million total based on six milestones; five milestones were achieved, so the final outcome was about $8 million.

## Key Claims
- The acquisition created a meaningful exit but also later regret because the products grew sharply during the earnout.
- Lucas says annual revenue was about $1.5 million at acquisition and about $8 million by the end of the earnout, making the effective sale price feel low in hindsight.
- The deal ended the profit-and-exit-share arrangements with distribution partners such as [[JKMolina]].
