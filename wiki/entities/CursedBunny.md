# 《诅咒兔》 / Cursed Bunny

《诅咒兔》 / Cursed Bunny is the [[BoraChung|郑宝拉]] short-story collection discussed in [[117-zuzhoutu-guaitan-wenxue-ye-yao-shangzhuo-chifan-766574341]]. The episode uses it to argue that [[GuaitanLiterature|怪谈文学]] deserves serious literary attention because its grotesque premises can handle gendered fear, exploitation, revenge, capitalism, family violence, and loneliness without becoming abstract thesis fiction.

The episode focuses on four stories. 《头》 makes domestic and bodily dread literal through a toilet creature that calls a woman "mother." The title story makes revenge beautiful and cursed through a rabbit lamp made for a friend's ruined family business. 《伤疤》 turns monster-killing into a question about whether justice can destroy the whole dependent order around the monster. 《不受家》 uses a fox that bleeds gold and a family that repeats the violence on children to show extraction becoming inheritance.

The collection's wiki role is therefore not only "horror story collection." It is a bridge between [[WeirdFiction]], [[FolkloreTraumaEncoding]], [[GenderedDomesticHorror]], and [[PostRevengeEmptiness]].
