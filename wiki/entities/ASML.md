# ASML / 阿斯麦

ASML appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] as the Dutch equipment company treated by the source as the sole current maker of the highest-end EUV lithography systems. The episode uses ASML to explain why [[PhotolithographyBottleneck|lithography]] became a shorthand for advanced semiconductor manufacturing constraints.

The source is careful not to make ASML the entire story. It stresses that high-end chip production also depends on materials, inspection tools, process equipment, cleanrooms, yield, and manufacturing experience, so access to one machine does not by itself create an advanced [[SemiconductorSupplyChain|semiconductor supply chain]].
