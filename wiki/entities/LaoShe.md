# 老舍 / Lao She

老舍 is the Chinese writer whose short fiction anchors [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]], a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode on [[KaiShiDaJi|《开市大吉》]] and related stories. The episode presents him not only as a humorous writer but as an observer of durable social mechanisms: vanity, etiquette failure, family power, fake public-interest language, workplace favors, and self-deception.

The source's interpretation is that Lao She's comedy often turns into sympathy and unease. His characters can be foolish, harmful, or ridiculous, yet the episode stresses that they remain shaped by ordinary fear, interest, ignorance, and social pressure rather than existing as simple villains.

[[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]] adds a full-novel case through [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]]. The episode argues that Lao She's humor is inseparable from vernacular rhythm, crosstalk-like timing, and painful restraint: [[LaoLiLihun|老李]], [[ZhangDageLihun|张大哥]], [[XiaoZhaoLihun|小赵]], and [[DingErLihun|丁二]] are funny because they make a whole Beiping office and marriage world feel socially recognizable and spiritually cramped.
