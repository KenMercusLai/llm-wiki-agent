# 郭静龙

郭静龙 is the author associated in [[jiaqi-moyu-geng-jiankang-574391976]] with [[ShengshiDeBengta|《盛世的崩塌》]], a book the hosts praise and may invite him to discuss. The episode says he lives in Dali, making the possible guest appearance part of the show's Dali-period reading network rather than a completed interview.

The source gives only a preview, not a full author profile. In this wiki, his page should therefore be treated as a source-scoped pointer to a future Tang-history and An Lushan Rebellion branch.
