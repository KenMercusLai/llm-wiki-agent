# Barcode Hero

Barcode Hero is [[BlakeScholl]]'s first startup in [[tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio-tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio]]. Scholl describes it as a mobile-commerce, gamification, and barcode-scanning company that combined fashionable pieces of his resume and the market rather than a problem he deeply wanted to exist.

The company matters to the wiki less as a standalone product and more as founder-formation evidence. Scholl says the stressful experience made him fear he had damaged his career, but the sale gave him enough breathing room to rethink how he chose startups. He also says he adjusted the sale so investors made money, and the episode later treats that as one reason earlier investors were willing to back [[BoomSupersonic]].
