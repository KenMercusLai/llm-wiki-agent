# 段成式 / Duan Chengshi

段成式 / Duan Chengshi is the author of [[YouyangZazu|《酉阳杂俎》]] as presented in [[90-youyang-zazu-xuedizi-he-wuzetian-shouzhishang-de-heimao-712539933]]. The episode describes him as high-born, with powerful family background and friendships among late-Tang literary-political figures, but more interested in collecting strange knowledge than in building a major power career.

The source uses him as a different kind of Tang literary figure from [[NiuSengru|牛僧儒]] in the [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]] branch. He is not mainly read through factional authorship or repayment ethics; he becomes the collector whose curiosity preserves bits of religion, rumor, natural observation, story motifs, and casual violence that later readers can test, compare, and reinterpret.
