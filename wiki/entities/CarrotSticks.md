# Carrot Sticks

Carrot Sticks is the online math-game startup [[DanSiroker]] and [[PeteKoomen]] tried before [[Optimizely]], discussed in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]]. Dan treats it as a weak idea-selection case: the founders were not parents, teachers, or children, and the product was built in Flash just as the iPad made that technical choice worse.

The source uses Carrot Sticks to sharpen [[FounderProductFit]]. The problem was not only that the idea failed; it was that the founders lacked lived access to the customer, buyer, and usage context they were trying to serve.
