# Ruan Yisan / 阮仪三

Ruan Yisan is the urban-planning and heritage-conservation figure discussed in [[no-209-jinshang-wangshi-zou-xikou-dao-qiaojia-dayuan-ranhou-mei-le-999367322]]. The episode describes him finding severe damage and demolition pressure in [[PingyaoAncientCity]] around 1980, then seeking time from provincial authorities to redo the plan for free.

The source uses Ruan's intervention to show that heritage cities are not preserved only by cultural value. They need planning decisions, administrative pauses, expert argument, and a development path that does not destroy the old fabric before tourism value is recognized.

## Key Claims
- Ruan's role is presented as timely intervention before Pingyao's old-city structure was irreversibly weakened.
- The episode frames planning as a condition of later [[HeritageTourismCommercialization]], not as an afterthought.
- His work is paired with [[ChenCongzhou]]'s principle for separating old-city preservation from new-city development.
