# John Harvey

John Harvey is the comedian, scriptwriter, and author identified in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] as the performer behind [[CountBinface]]. The source says he has repeatedly used the character in high-profile British elections, normally winning only a small vote share but sometimes gaining enough public attention to become a symbolic protest option.

In the Clacton segment, Harvey's role is to make satire operational inside an actual ballot. His [[CountBinface]] campaign works because British electoral openness lets absurd candidacies expose the performative and self-serving parts of mainstream political behavior.
