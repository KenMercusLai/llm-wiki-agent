# Pakistan

Pakistan enters the wiki through the [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]] thread. In [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]], it is the displacement setting where sending advance royalties to Hadiya became difficult because of account, identity, and banking constraints. In [[184-zhenshi-yu-wo-you-wanjun-zhi-zhong-huanying-xianv-ajiao-990545010]], it becomes the setting for the [[HadiyaRescueEvent|哈迪亚营救事件]], where detention and deportation risk required urgent coordination.

The country page is source-scoped. The wiki is not making a general claim about Pakistan; it records how these episodes use Pakistan as the practical context where refugee status, documents, local bureaucracy, security agencies, banking, and public exposure can all matter at once.
