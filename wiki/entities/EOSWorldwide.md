# EOS Worldwide

EOS Worldwide is the organization behind the Entrepreneurial Operating System methodology discussed in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. [[MarkAbbott]] describes EOS as a training and development ecosystem with implementers, coaches, trademarked terms, and a large community of companies using the framework.

## Key Points
- [[GinoWickman]] founded EOS and wrote *Traction*, which Abbott says resembled the book he had planned to write.
- Abbott joined the EOS implementer community before launching [[Ninety]], building relationships and learning the methodology.
- Ninety initially positioned itself as "EOS compatible" before entering a license with EOS Worldwide near the end of 2018.
- The license improved terminology fit for coaches but constrained how Ninety could teach, market, and change the software.
