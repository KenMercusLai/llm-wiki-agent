# 《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》 / Nian Nu Jiao: Reminiscence at Red Cliff

《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》 appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as one of [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s Huangzhou-period masterpieces. The source groups it with [[RedCliffFu|《赤壁赋》]] and 《后赤壁赋》 to show how the [[Huangzhou|黄州]] exile becomes a creative high point.

The episode uses the work as part of a larger argument about scale. Su Shi's mature writing is not just cheerful recovery; it turns political loss and personal danger into historical imagination, making [[EarnedOptimism]] a disciplined stance rather than personality sunshine.
