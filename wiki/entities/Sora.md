# Sora

Sora is discussed in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as [[OpenAI]]'s AI video product and as the episode's opening case for questioning the "models eat applications" narrative. The source treats Sora's reported shutdown as a product, model-quality, inference-cost, and platform-positioning problem rather than as a simple verdict on AI video.

In the episode's interpretation, Sora's attempt to be more than a tool made the mismatch sharper: an AI video community/platform needs strong enough generation quality, content supply, user habit, and economics at the same time. The source therefore uses Sora to strengthen the case for starting from [[VerticalWorkflowAI]] and usable tool workflows before trying to become a full platform.
