# Louis Hong / 洪力德

Louis Hong is the guest in [[145-koushu-spacex-kaifashi-he-qiangaoguan-honglide-liao-masike-yongrenguan-zuida-ipo-taikong-yu-ai-renlei-wenming-kuozhang-qianzou]]. The episode presents him as a former [[SpaceX]] employee who joined in 2012, left in 2019, and at departure managed seven departments and thousands of rocket parts.

His value to the wiki is the insider-manufacturing lens: he connects [[DragonSpacecraft]] interiors, [[Falcon9]] production, reusable-launch economics, accident review, production scaling, and [[ElonMusk]]'s leadership style into one operating system. After SpaceX, he moved into batteries and hard-tech investing, which makes his comments on [[SpaceEconomyInfrastructure]], [[SpaceBasedAIInfrastructure]], and [[SpaceXMafia]] part operating memory and part investor thesis.
