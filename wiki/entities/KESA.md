# KESA

KESA is the screenwriter and AI-content creator interviewed in [[e234-weilai-shipai-dianying-hai-cunzai-ma-yu-daoyan-luchuan-liaoliao-ai-gei-yingshiren-de-kongju-yu-ziyou-b2be7093-3366-4ee2-8a7a-625f06206ae5]]. The source identifies him as the founder of [[StoryStorm]] and says he moved from traditional screenwriting into AI content after experiencing long, uncertain IP development cycles.

In the episode, KESA's case anchors the creator-side promise of [[AIVideoProductionWorkflow]]. His AI short 《加班夜》 was made over a weekend with [[Midjourney]] images and iterative script changes, showing how AI can turn a small idea into a lightweight prototype without the cost of a live-action shoot.

KESA does not make the source a pure replacement story. His move highlights why AI feels freeing to creators who are blocked by platform schedules, production coordination, actor availability, and long feedback loops, while still leaving story and judgment as human responsibilities.
