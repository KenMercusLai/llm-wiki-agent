# 洪涛

洪涛 enters the wiki through [[51-yanshi-fanrenlei-tonghua-gushi-geliefu-youji-ke-shen-le-qu-le-632315320]], where the host says reading his 《格列佛游记与古今政治》 changed her view of [[JonathanSwift|Swift]] and [[GulliversTravels|《格列佛游记》]]. The episode uses Hong Tao to move beyond the claim that Swift was simply anti-human or that the later voyages only praise antiquity against modernity.

His role in the source is interpretive rather than biographical. He helps frame the novel as an inquiry into political possibility: what happens when modern politics assumes fixed human nature, technical power outruns legitimacy, or pure rational order stops caring about human life?
