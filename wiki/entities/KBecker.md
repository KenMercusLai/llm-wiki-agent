# K Becker

K Becker is [[KimberlyBecker]]'s clothing brand for women over 40 discussed in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. The episode says the company is based in Woolwich, Maine, uses fabrics mostly from Japan and Italy, including deadstock fabric, launched in October 2023, doubled sales year over year, and expected about $75,000 in sales by the end of the year.

The Advice Line question is whether K Becker can move away from carrying inventory and toward making garments after customer orders. [[TimFerriss]] recommends testing limited drops with a fixed number of orders and a four- to six-week wait before changing the whole model, while [[GuyRaz]] suggests positioning the model as made to order rather than pre-order.
