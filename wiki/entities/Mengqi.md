# 梦琪 / Mengqi

Mengqi is the founder of [[InvokoAI]] interviewed in [[yige-ai-chuangshiren-de-xurongxin-zhuang-he-yumei-zhidian-duitan-invoko-ai-chuangshiren-mengqi-lsi79o-z19zplvmqdbpzzneogpk3f]]. The episode presents her as an AI software founder who moved from vertical Agent and 2B growth-agent ideas toward the consumer browser/desktop product [[Clico]] after concluding that much of the earlier story was driven by [[FounderEgo]], investor language, and overconfident logical deduction.

Her strongest wiki role is as a reflective founder-operator case. She argues that AI application founders cannot rely on labels such as RL, Agent, digital employee, or global-first launch; they need [[CustomerPull]], stable product experience, user research, payment evidence, and enough maintenance discipline to make software useful after the first demo.
