# 光轮智能

光轮智能 is the robotics simulation and data company founded by [[XieChen]] in 2023, discussed in [[134-shuju-de-zongshu-he-xiechen-liao-xinshidai-de-shiyou-lishi-bantu-shuju-jinzita-dingjia-yu-recipe]]. The episode frames the company as a [[DataEngineLearningLoop]] provider for [[EmbodiedAI]] rather than a simple annotation vendor.

## Source Position
- The company builds physically realistic simulation environments and interactive assets for robot training and evaluation.
- Its work includes asset measurement, physics parameter capture, simulation teleoperation, algorithmic data collection, model-assisted labeling, and human-in-the-loop quality control.
- [[XieChen]] says the company wants to be understood as a Data Engine, not a Data Factory, because feedback, evaluation, and environment design matter as much as raw files.
- Its strategic claim is that [[RoboticsSimulationEvaluation]] can make large-scale robot testing and data generation possible when real robot fleets are too small.
- The company sits between model-brain teams, robot-body teams, and real scenario owners, making [[DataRecipeCoCreation]] part of its product and customer relationship.
