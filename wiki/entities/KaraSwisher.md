# Kara Swisher

Kara Swisher appears in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] as the interviewer whose conversation with [[JacobTierney]] and [[BrendanBrady]] supplies the episode's main material. [[PlanetMoney]] uses her interview to turn the cultural success of [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]] into a business discussion about financing, IP ownership, distribution, streaming attention, and AI.

Her role in this source is connective rather than biographical. She links media-industry questions about [[StreamingConsolidation]] and [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] to creative questions about [[UnderservedRomanceAudience]], [[AttentiveStreamingStorytelling]], and [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]].
