# James Graham / 詹姆斯·格雷厄姆

James Graham appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] through the eighteenth-century "Temple of Health" and the commercial performance of electrical medicine. The episode uses him to show that electricity was not only a laboratory topic but also a therapeutic, erotic, class-marked, and theatrical promise.

In the wiki, Graham belongs to [[ElectricTherapySpeculation]] and [[ScientificPublicSpectacle]]. He shows the hopeful and promotional side of electricity before modern medical evidence standards, while [[GiovanniAldini|Giovanni Aldini]] shows the more disturbing corpse-animation side.
