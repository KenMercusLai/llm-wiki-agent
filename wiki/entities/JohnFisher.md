# John Fisher

John Fisher appears in [[theres-no-business-like-dough-business]] as the [[WetzelsPretzels|Wetzel's Pretzels]] corporate development voice. The episode identifies him as then head of development, responsible for helping franchisees find and build locations.

Fisher's main contribution is the company's "pretzels to the people" logic. He explains why Wetzel's can place several stores in the same mall, arena, or transit complex: pretzels are usually an impulse product, so nearby locations may trigger different purchase occasions rather than merely splitting a fixed pool of planned buyers.

He also marks the franchising boundary. The episode says Wetzel's does not allow unrelated franchisees to operate under the same roof, which makes clustered stores a coordinated [[FranchiseLedConsumerChainExpansion]] and [[ImpulseRetailClustering]] case rather than simple internal rivalry.
