# Charlie Sporck

Charlie Sporck appears in [[tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final-tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final]] as the CEO of [[NationalSemiconductor]] during [[RonConway]]'s early career. Conway describes him as an icon whose frugality, intensity, and work expectations shaped National's culture.

The episode makes Sporck important less as a biographical subject than as a management signal from early Silicon Valley semiconductor culture. Conway's stories about long hours, competitive sales, and even a Washington tariff fight show how National turned operational urgency into employee expectations. That context feeds the source's broader [[SemiconductorTalentGenealogy]] claim: later Silicon Valley companies inherited executives and operating norms from the semiconductor generation.
