# 《酉阳杂俎》 / Youyang Zazu

《酉阳杂俎》 / Youyang Zazu is the Tang miscellany at the center of [[90-youyang-zazu-xuedizi-he-wuzetian-shouzhishang-de-heimao-712539933]]. The episode presents [[DuanChengshi|段成式]]'s book as much more than a collection of ghost stories: it contains court rumor, botanical and animal notes, festival and funeral customs, temple and mural records, Buddhist hells, Daoist practices, strange tales, poems, and fragments of cultural memory.

The source's main claim is that the book's disorder is the point. Stories such as flying walnuts, skeleton women, eye-heaps, [[YeXian|《叶限》]], goose-cage scholar, heavenly throne seizure, underworld paperwork, child-slave killing, and romantic natural lore all belong to a mixed archive. That makes the book a key source for [[TangMiscellanyArchiveValue]], while also extending [[TangChuanqi]], [[ChineseFolkReligionLayering]], [[StoryMotifTransmission]], and [[ClassicReadingComplexity]].
