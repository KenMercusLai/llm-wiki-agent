# Square Online

Square Online appears in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]] as the product continuation of [[Weebly]] inside [[Square]]. [[DavidRusenko]] says Weebly's codebase became the underpinnings of Square Online after the 2018 acquisition, connecting drag-and-drop website creation to small-business commerce.

The page is intentionally narrow until more sources discuss Square's broader product strategy. In this source, Square Online matters because it shows how a capital-efficient website-builder startup became part of a larger commerce platform rather than ending only as a financial exit.
