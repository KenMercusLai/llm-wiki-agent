# 达菲辛 / Dafeixin

达菲辛 / Dafeixin is the professional hair-growth brand sponsoring [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]]. The episode's sponsor segment uses the brand to introduce [[Minoxidil|minoxidil]] and basic distinctions among postpartum shedding, stress-related shedding, chemotherapy-related shedding, androgenetic hair loss, alopecia areata, and other hair-loss patterns.
