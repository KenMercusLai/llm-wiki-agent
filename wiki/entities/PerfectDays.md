# 《完美的日子》 / Perfect Days

《完美的日子》 / Perfect Days is discussed in [[vol-102-aoguo-jiuye-bingheqi-de-riben-nianqingren-qu-nali-xunzhao-xingfugan-chuantai-qingdao-kuaima-lmpluij7rcfq49cxcyb65x3f7zfn]] as a cultural image of stripped-down, highly ordered daily life. [[Dalaoshi|大老师]] reads the toilet-cleaner protagonist's routine, tree shadows, film camera, books, and careful work as a metaphor for finding peace after a more expansive or prosperous era has receded.

The source does not present the film as a simple poverty romance. Its key point is self-consistent choice: ordinary work and small perceptions can become [[ArtisanalAttention]] when the person accepts the life rhythm and does the practice with care.
