# 《写诗真好玩》

《写诗真好玩》 is named in [[182-zhua-yi-ba-feng-xi-xi-lian-gun-dao-niba-li-qu-xie-shi-he-shucai-liao-tongshi-980330792]] as [[ShuCai|树才]]'s second children's poetry-writing book. The episode treats the title as a statement of method: "写诗" is almost secondary, while "真好玩" is what makes poetry available to children.

In the source, the book belongs to [[PoetryEducationAsPlay]] rather than to achievement training. Its implied value is to invite children into language, sound, feeling, and imagination without making poetry another task that adults can score, publish, or display.
