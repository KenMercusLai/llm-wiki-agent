# Lu Qi

Lu Qi appears in [[dang-yinchaoji-baidu-kaishi-shixue-shi-tianzai-haishi-renhuo-keji-luandun]] as the former [[Baidu]] executive whose tenure the hosts treat as a possible missed transformation window. The source says his arrival once gave observers hope that Baidu would regain strategic intensity and that his willingness to touch medical advertising would have challenged a major revenue base.

In the episode's interpretation, Lu Qi's departure marks the loss of an internal force that might have pushed Baidu away from pure search-ad dependence. That makes him a supporting example for [[CashCowStrategicInertia]] and [[LargeCompanyOrganizationalInertia]] rather than the sole explanation for Baidu's decline.
