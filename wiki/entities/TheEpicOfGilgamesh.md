# The Epic of Gilgamesh / 吉尔伽美什史诗

The Epic of Gilgamesh is the Mesopotamian epic discussed by [[SunRuoxi|孙若曦]] in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The source presents it not as a single authorial book, but as a long-formed, edited, fragmentary tablet tradition whose missing pieces remain part of the reading experience.

The episode's central reading is that [[Gilgamesh|吉尔伽美什]] becomes humanly intelligible when [[Enkidu|恩启都]] dies. Grief makes him fear his own death, seek immortality, fail the sleep test, lose the rejuvenation plant to a snake, and return to Uruk with the knowledge that kingly power cannot erase human limits.
