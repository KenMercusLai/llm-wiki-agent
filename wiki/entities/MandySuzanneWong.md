# Mandy Suzanne Wong

Mandy Suzanne Wong is the creator of *Duology, Awabi or Abalone*, discussed by [[MelodyJue]] in [[melody-jue-ocean-memory-wxagwid1oeg]]. Jue reads the work as a bridge among Japanese ama divers, abalone, endangered cultural memory, healthy ocean conditions, and nonhuman sensory life.

The source uses Wong's abalone story to make [[Chemosensation]] concrete. The planktonic abalone recognizes safe smells, algae, and rocks, while [[OceanAcidification]] figures both ecological danger and breakdowns in family and cultural transmission.
