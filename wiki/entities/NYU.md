# NYU

NYU is the university context in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] where [[XieSaining]] moved after [[FAIR]]. The source says he was attracted by New York, NYU's AI layout, the Center for Data Science environment, and [[YannLeCun]]'s presence.

## Role In The Source
NYU is presented as a more open, cross-disciplinary, almost company-like academic environment. It also becomes the place where Xie had to think about academic resource constraints, student compute access, and how to keep doing ambitious [[RepresentationLearning]] and [[WorldModels]] work outside the most resource-rich industrial labs.

[[the-little-known-regulatory-bodies-that-can-make-or-break-ai-data-centers]] adds a technology-policy branch through the [[NYUCenterOnTechnologyPolicy]]. In that source, [[ScottBrennan]] uses AI data-center growth to show why [[PublicUtilityCommissions]], utility rates, and [[DataCenterCostShifting]] should be part of AI policy.

[[e236-99-de-zuoye-doushi-ai-xie-de-dangdai-mingxiao-sheng-yanli-daxue-hai-shengxia-shenme-b066c769-00d2-4341-bf61-a82bca4181e4]] adds an undergraduate and student-AI-use branch. [[KelentoHouTaiyu|Kelento 侯泰宇]] chose NYU partly for environment, social access, and [[YannLeCun]]'s AI presence, while [[JackRaoJiewu|Jack 饶街五]] used AI heavily during NYU data-science coursework before graduate study at [[ColumbiaUniversity]]. The source makes NYU part of the wiki's [[AIDefaultLearningEnvironment]], [[AIAsTutor]], and [[DegreeAsTrustCredential]] discussion.

[[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] adds a graduate-affordability branch. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode cites analysis saying [[NYU]] is among the institutions with the most borrowers potentially affected by the Trump administration's [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]], making the university a concrete case in the [[BennettHypothesis]] and [[LoanCapAccessRisk]] debate.
