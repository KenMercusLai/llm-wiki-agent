# NYU

NYU is the university context in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] where [[XieSaining]] moved after [[FAIR]]. The source says he was attracted by New York, NYU's AI layout, the Center for Data Science environment, and [[YannLeCun]]'s presence.

## Role In The Source
NYU is presented as a more open, cross-disciplinary, almost company-like academic environment. It also becomes the place where Xie had to think about academic resource constraints, student compute access, and how to keep doing ambitious [[RepresentationLearning]] and [[WorldModels]] work outside the most resource-rich industrial labs.
