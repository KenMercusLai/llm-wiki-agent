# Vera Hill

Vera Hill appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] as a 77-year-old resident of [[EuclidHall]] on Manhattan's Upper West Side. She had worked at Mount Sinai Health System, later found New York rent unaffordable, entered a shelter, and eventually moved into the SRO-style room.

Her story grounds the episode's human case for [[SingleRoomOccupancyHousing]] when paired with services. Vera's room lacks a private kitchen and uses shared bathroom facilities, but the source emphasizes that help is available and that she still values helping others, making her case more specifically about [[SupportiveHousingManagement]] than about bare-bones cheap rooms alone.
