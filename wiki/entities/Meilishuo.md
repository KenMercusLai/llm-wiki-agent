# 美丽说 / Meilishuo

Meilishuo is the women's fashion guide-community peer to [[Mogujie]] in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode presents the pair as "succeeding because of Taobao, failing because of Taobao": guide traffic worked while outbound links were allowed, then became fragile after [[Alibaba]] changed platform access.
