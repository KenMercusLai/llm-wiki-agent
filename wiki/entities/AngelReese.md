# Angel Reese

Angel Reese appears in [[diary-of-a-wnba-negotiator]] as another signal of [[WomensSportsBusinessGrowth]]. The episode points to streaming attention around one of her preseason games as evidence that women's basketball demand was no longer confined to a small legacy audience.

The source uses Reese as a market-attention example rather than as a labor negotiator. In the episode's argument, athlete-driven fan interest helps explain why WNBA players pushed for [[SportsLaborRevenueSharing]] instead of only fixed salary increases.
