The ingested episodes cover [[ZhangYueguang]]'s product transition from [[Miaoya]] to [[Docky]], [[YinQi]]'s appointment as chair of [[StepFun]], [[GaoJiyang]]'s [[Xinghaitu]] robotics path, [[XieSaining]]'s [[AMILabs]] world-model startup story, [[XieChen]]'s [[GuanglunIntelligence]] data/simulation view of robotics, [[LouisHong]]'s [[SpaceX]] manufacturing and space-infrastructure account, [[YaoShunyu]]'s frontier-model training perspective from [[Anthropic]] and [[GoogleDeepMind]], [[Tristan]]'s [[NaturalSelection]] product thesis around [[EVE]], [[Elys]], [[CyberAvatars]], and [[AISocialNetworks]], [[HongLetong]]'s [[Axiom]] thesis around [[AIForMath]], [[LeanTheoremProver]], [[AutoFormalization]], and [[FormalVerification]], [[LuoFuli]]'s [[Xiaomi]] model-team thesis around [[OpenClaw]], [[OpenCloud]], [[AgentPostTraining]], [[AgentRL]], [[MemoVR]], and flatter [[AIOrganizationDesign]], and [[SuYu]]'s agent-history thesis around [[LanguageAgent]], [[OpenClawMoment]], [[ContinualLearning]], [[WorldModels]], and [[UniversalDigitalAgent]].

Episode 141 adds [[Freda]]'s investor lens through [[TokenMaxxing]], [[AIEconomicDiffusion]], and [[HumanConnectionUnderAI]]. It connects the show's agent and model-company thread to market metrics, software revaluation, hyperscaler capex, public/private AI valuation, organization redesign, and the emotional experience of trying to keep up with accelerating tools.

Episode 143 adds [[HeXiaopeng]] and [[XPeng]] through [[PhysicalAI]], [[StitchedAIArchitecture]], [[XPengIron]], [[XPengGX]], and [[HumanoidRobotCommercialization]]. It connects the show's embodied-AI thread to autonomous-driving route changes, robot full-stack manufacturing, physical-world data costs, vehicle terminals, and CEO-led organization change.

Episode 144 adds [[YangMeng]] and [[AnkerInnovations]] through [[ConsumerElectronicsLifecycle]], [[ThirdTypeCompany]], [[CreatorCulture]], [[InMemoryComputingForEdgeAI]], [[OnDeviceModelHierarchy]], [[TrueSmartHome]], [[HouseholdSecurityRobots]], and [[EnterprisePrearrangedAgents]]. It connects the show's embodied-AI and organization-design thread to a consumer-electronics operator's path from Amazon-channel products toward edge-side models, smart-home autonomy, security robots, and AI-native internal workflows.

Episode 142 adds [[DaiYusen]] through [[AgentHarness]], [[ModelHarnessCoEvolution]], [[AgentMarketplace]], [[AIInvestmentMetrics]], and [[AIEconomicDiffusion]]. It connects the show's agent and model-company thread to an investor's view of why [[ClaudeCode]], [[Codex]], [[Manus]], and [[OpenCloud]] matter as product/runtime layers, why terminal AI returns remain unresolved, and why the next major AI company may not resemble [[ByteDance]].
