# Hiweed Linux

Hiweed Linux is the early community Linux distribution that [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]] presents as the predecessor to [[Deepin]]. The episode says [[LengGanghua]] built it in his spare time from a Debian base and added Chinese localization work.

## Source Position
- Hiweed represents the hobbyist and community origin of the later domestic operating-system lineage.
- The source treats its naming history as informal and partly anecdotal, including the later reason to move away from the Hiweed name.
- Its importance is not commercial scale but continuity: the project creates the technical and community substrate for [[Deepin]].

## Connections
- [[LengGanghua]] — creator in the episode's account.
- [[Deepin]] — later renamed and expanded project.
- [[ChineseDomesticOperatingSystems]] — historical branch where a community distribution later entered a policy and enterprise market.
- [[OpenSourceCommunityCommercialization]] — concept illustrated by the transition from Hiweed to Deepin and Tongxin.
