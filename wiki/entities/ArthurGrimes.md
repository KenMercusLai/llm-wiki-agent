# Arthur Grimes

Arthur Grimes appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the New Zealand economist who helped design [[InflationTargeting]]. The episode says he worked as chief economist at the [[ReserveBankOfNewZealand]], studied other countries' inflation-control methods around 1985 and 1986, and proposed targeting inflation directly.

The source presents Grimes' 0% to 2% by 1992 target as both technical policy and communication strategy. The target made low inflation legible enough for [[DonBrash]] to try to make expectations coordinate around it.
