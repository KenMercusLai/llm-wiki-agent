# UPI

UPI appears in [[strait-and-narrowing-the-iran-deal-crumbles-6a57608bf821a8210984481f]] as one of [[India]]'s successful government-linked technology systems. The episode groups it with [[Aadhaar]] as evidence that India can build major digital infrastructure even while many routine government websites remain hard to use.

The page is source-scoped: this episode does not give a full payments history, but uses UPI as a contrast case for [[GovernmentWebsiteUsability]]. The relevant lesson is organizational rather than purely technical: successful public infrastructure needs authority, competence, and service ownership rather than just outsourcing or web-page implementation.
