# 《病人家属，请来一下》

《病人家属，请来一下》 is mentioned in [[70-yisheng-ni-zai-xiang-shenme-shao-kan-baidu-yiji-chiyao-shi-bie-chi-xiyou-a-668238394]] as a companion work to [[YishengNiZaiXiangShenme|《医生，你在想什么》]] by [[WangXingDoctor|王兴医生]]. The episode does not summarize this book in depth, so the wiki keeps the page source-scoped: it matters here as evidence that Wang's medical popularization spans the doctor, patient, and family sides of care.

Within the episode's branch, the title points toward the role of families in medical decisions, consent, records, risk communication, and emotionally charged treatment choices. That connects it to [[DoctorPatientCommunication]] and [[MedicalRiskManagement]] without treating the episode as a full source on family caregiving.
