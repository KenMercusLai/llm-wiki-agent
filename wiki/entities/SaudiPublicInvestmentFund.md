# Saudi Public Investment Fund

Saudi Public Investment Fund is the sovereign wealth fund discussed in [[ep77-si-shi-wan-nian-xin-fu-ye-zhuan-le-san-shi-si-yi-te-lang-pu-jiao-ni-ruhe-gao-qian-lnrx8vkdjedxbmmcwxlnwow-pcab]] through its alleged investment into [[JaredKushner]]'s private-equity firm. The source says the investment was about $2 billion and frames it as economically important because a 2% management fee can create a large recurring revenue stream.

## Source Position
- The episode claims an internal review questioned the investment's commercial basis, but that Saudi leadership approved it.
- The hosts connect the investment to Trump-era Saudi relations, including early presidential travel choices and arms-sale politics.
- The fund is not analyzed as a portfolio institution; it is used as a case for foreign-capital routes into [[PoliticalInfluenceMonetization]].
