# Albert Einstein / 阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦

Albert Einstein appears in [[106-bie-zai-haipa-chongtu-kuai-lai-xuexi-tanpan-shu-742539645]] through the episode's salary example involving the [[InstituteForAdvancedStudy|Institute for Advanced Study / 普林斯顿高等研究院]]. The source uses the story to show that negotiation success is not always maximum extraction from the counterparty.

In the episode's interpretation, a generous offer can build satisfaction, loyalty, and trust when the relationship matters. That makes Einstein's case part of [[ConcessionSignaling]], [[NegotiationAsBargaining]], and relationship-aware calculation rather than a simple salary anecdote.
