# Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther King Jr. appears in [[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]] through a 1966 visit to [[JohnsonProducts]]' research facility and headquarters. The episode says [[GeorgeEJohnson]] recalled King's organization needing payroll help and [[IndependenceBank]] loaning it more than $100,000.

The source uses King to connect consumer-business success to civil-rights infrastructure. Johnson Products is not framed only as a company selling [[UltraWave]] or [[AfroSheen]], but as a business with enough community capital and institutional reach to support movement work.
