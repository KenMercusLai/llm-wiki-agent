# Maryland Transportation Authority

The Maryland Transportation Authority is the transportation agency represented by chief engineer [[JimHarkness]] in [[tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The source uses the agency's [[FrancisScottKeyBridge]] rebuild to show how public infrastructure replacement can include [[StructuralHealthMonitoring]] and operational data systems, not only new concrete and steel.

In the episode, the authority's role is to rebuild a bridge that serves commuters and [[PortOfBaltimore]] freight traffic after the [[DaliContainerShip]] strike and collapse. The agency expects the new bridge to handle larger and heavier loads while giving engineers real-time or near-real-time condition data.
