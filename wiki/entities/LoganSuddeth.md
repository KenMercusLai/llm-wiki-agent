# Logan Suddeth

Logan Suddeth appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as a 25-year-old [[Kalshi]] trader who says he left a private-credit job because after-hours trading was earning more than his salary. The episode uses him to make [[PredictionMarketTraderAlpha]] concrete.

His example supports the source's distinction between prediction markets as public forecasting tools and prediction markets as competitive trading arenas. Logan presents his edge as research-driven, including a profitable Time Person of the Year bet, which shows how domain-specific information work can become a private payoff without necessarily proving broader public value.
