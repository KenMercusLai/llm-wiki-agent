# 王航

Wang Hang is the founder of [[Haodf]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode describes his route through public-health work, pharmaceuticals, 3721, Yahoo China, and Qihoo 360 before founding Haodf in 2006.

The source uses Wang's company as a case of founder restraint in [[InternetHealthcare]]: Haodf tried to structure doctor information and patient reviews while avoiding several direct monetization routes that could compromise medical trust.
