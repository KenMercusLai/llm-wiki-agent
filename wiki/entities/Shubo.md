# Shubo

Shubo is the cofounder of [[Axiom]] mentioned in [[137-dui-hong-letong-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-ai-for-math-ba-shuxue-biancheng-lean-shuxue-tianshu-zhong-de-zhengming-zhijue-bei-chuangzao-yu-bei-faxian-de-lha-faiwxtget0qmbcosts3cb5vb]]. The episode identifies him through his long-running conversations with [[HongLetong]] at a Palo Alto cafe, where they discussed mathematics, AI, history, science, and eventually the possibility of building an [[AIForMath]] company.

## Source Position
- The source treats Shubo's joining as important because Hong saw the problem as too large and lonely for one founder.
- His connection to Hong starts as intellectual friendship rather than a conventional founder search.
- The episode uses the relationship to frame Axiom's origin as a mix of technical conviction, mathematical curiosity, and repeated high-density discussion.
