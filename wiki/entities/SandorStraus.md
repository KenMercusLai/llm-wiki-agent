# Sandor Straus

Sandor Straus appears in [[vol-103-wenyi-fuxing-keji-ximengsi-de-fengshen-zhilu-shi-lianghua-zhiwang-gengshi-dongcha-renxing-de-dashi-lulzvnaxdb4klqag-p2yyqlmiikl]] as the data-builder behind a major part of [[RenaissanceTechnologies]]'s early advantage. The source describes him collecting, cleaning, and digitizing market records, including historical and intraday material, before data infrastructure became an obvious hedge-fund status symbol.

The episode uses Straus to turn data work from a support function into a core moat. In the Renaissance story, models mattered because the firm had unusual, cleaned, long-horizon data on which to search for weak signals and test them.
