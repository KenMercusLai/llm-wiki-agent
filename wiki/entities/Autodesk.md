# Autodesk

Autodesk is the software company that acquired [[PlanGrid]] in 2018 for $875 million, according to [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. The episode uses the acquisition less as a financial endpoint than as the context for what [[TracyYoung]] and [[RalphGoody]] later wanted to study and change.

After leaving Autodesk in March 2020, Young and Goody expected to take a long family cruise, but COVID canceled that plan. The unexpected pause gave them time to examine PlanGrid's operating history, which became part of the foundation for [[TigerEye]] and [[SecondTimeFounderOperatingJudgment]].
