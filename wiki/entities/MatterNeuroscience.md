# Matter Neuroscience

Matter Neuroscience is the company co-founded by [[BenGoldhersh]] and mentioned in [[tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says the company is developing a six-pound phone case priced at $209, making phone use physically burdensome as a way to reduce casual scrolling.

In the wiki, Matter Neuroscience is an example of [[ScreenTimeFriction]] moving from software prompts into physical product design. Its proposed case sits inside the [[DigitalDetoxEconomy]], where consumers pay for devices that make a phone less convenient to use.
