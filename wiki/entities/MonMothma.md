# Mon Mothma

Mon Mothma is discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] as the political-line figure in [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]. The episode presents her as someone moving from conscience inside formal politics toward the future leadership of the [[RebelAlliance]], but not by a clean heroic route.

Her source function is to make rebellion costly outside combat. Funding resistance puts her finances, family, marriage customs, and public identity under pressure, while her contrast with [[LuthenRael]] clarifies different moral boundaries inside anti-[[GalacticEmpire]] action.
