# Dollar General

Dollar General appears in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] as one of the original dollar-store chains [[JoeyShamah]] expected to buy [[ELFCosmetics]]. Like [[FamilyDollar]], it rejected the early pitch, exposing the gap between the intended channel and the product's actual first demand path.

The rejection made e.l.f. a useful example of [[ValidatedLearning]]: a founder's channel hypothesis can fail even when the underlying consumer product later proves strong.
