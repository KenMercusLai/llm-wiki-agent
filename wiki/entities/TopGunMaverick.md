# Top Gun: Maverick

Top Gun: Maverick appears in [[vol-265-kuayue-50-nian-de-meiguo-banben-zhizi-1001004591]] as the film that gave [[Skydance]] and [[DavidEllison]] broader Hollywood credibility. The episode says the 2022 release became a major box-office success during a period when theaters were still under pressure from the pandemic and streaming.

The source uses the film less as a review object than as a business proof point. It shows how a company backed by family capital and attached to known IP could move from co-financing and production support into more ambitious media-control deals involving [[Paramount]] and [[WarnerBrosDiscovery]].
