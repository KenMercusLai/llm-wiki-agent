# Jean-Marie Le Pen

Jean-Marie Le Pen appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the family and party-origin figure behind [[MarineLePen]]'s political story. The episode says he co-founded the National Front in the 1970s, creating the party lineage that later became [[NationalRally]].

The source uses him mainly to explain the burden of [[FarRightNormalization]]. Marine Le Pen's campaign is not just another candidacy in [[France]]; it tests whether a party with a xenophobic and anti-Semitic inherited reputation can be brought close enough to mainstream respectability to win a [[TwoRoundPresidentialElection]].
