# Jackie Robinson

Jackie Robinson appears in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as the retired baseball player who testified for [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]] when active players were afraid to do so. The source says Robinson argued that the [[BaseballReserveClause|reserve clause]] was one-sided in favor of owners and should be changed so players had more control over their destiny.

The episode uses Robinson's testimony to show how race, public legitimacy, and labor control were intertwined. Press coverage was warmer when Robinson made the argument than when Flood did, which makes Robinson part of the source's [[PublicOpinionLitigationStrategy|public-opinion strategy]] branch.
