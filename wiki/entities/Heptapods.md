# Heptapods

Heptapods / 七肢桶 are the alien species in [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]] and [[ArrivalFilm|《降临》 / Arrival]]. In [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]], the episode emphasizes their seven-limbed body, lack of front/back orientation, and matching non-linear writing system.

Their spoken language and written language do not share the same logic. [[LouiseBanks|Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯]] first names the spoken system language A, then discovers [[HeptapodB]] as a written system whose symbols form a completed whole rather than a sequential chain.
