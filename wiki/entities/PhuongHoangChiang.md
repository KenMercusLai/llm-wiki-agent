# Phuong Hoang Chiang

Phuong Hoang Chiang is the human Cantonese-style wok chef in [[chef-vs-robot]], working at [[TingWong]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode says he has 13 years of chef experience, earns $35 an hour, can produce roughly 20 to 30 dishes per hour, and has won a chef competition in Guangdong province.

In the source, Phuong represents the craft side of the automation test. His cooking demonstrates [[WokHei]], high-heat timing, sauce control, and sensory judgment that [[RobbyWokbot]] can imitate only partly. He wins two of the three taste-test dishes.
