# Jim Harkness

Jim Harkness is the [[MarylandTransportationAuthority]] chief engineer interviewed in [[tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128]] about rebuilding the [[FrancisScottKeyBridge]] after its collapse.

In the source, Harkness explains the replacement bridge through civil-engineering operations rather than spectacle: a higher, longer cable-stayed bridge with 600-foot towers, mechanical access, elevators, [[StructuralHealthMonitoring]], and data that can be compared with computational models. His key point is that [[SensorDrivenInfrastructureMaintenance]] can make bridge behavior under real traffic and [[BridgeLoadCapacity|load]] conditions more visible to engineers.
