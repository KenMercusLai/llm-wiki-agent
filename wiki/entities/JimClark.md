# Jim Clark

Jim Clark appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] as the founder figure who made the early browser opportunity investable. [[RonConway]] says Clark recognized that the internet needed a friendly access layer, found [[MarkAndreessen]] and other University of Illinois engineers working on browser technology, and brought them to Silicon Valley to create [[Netscape]].

Conway also says he wanted to invest in Netscape, but [[MikeHomer]] told him Clark and [[JohnDoerr]] had already completed the round. In the source, Clark therefore sits at the center of [[BrowserAsInternetUnlock]] and [[NetscapePlatformEcosystem]].
