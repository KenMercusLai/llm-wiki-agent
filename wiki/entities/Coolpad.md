# Coolpad

Coolpad is one of the "中华酷联" companies in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode describes Coolpad's technical roots in dual-SIM, dual-standby, and multi-standard phones, which made it attractive to operators during the 3G contract-phone period.

Its decline in the source comes from the same dependency that powered its rise. When [[OperatorSubsidizedHandsets]] retreated and retail brands mattered more, Coolpad's carrier-channel advantage became harder to convert into consumer pull; later moves around Dazen, ivvi, 360, and LeTV added strategic conflict rather than durable renewal.
