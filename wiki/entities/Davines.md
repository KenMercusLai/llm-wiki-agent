# Davines

Davines is the hair-care brand impersonated in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, [[MeganMcCartyCorino]] searches for a Davines curl moisturizing mousse and encounters a sponsored [[Google]] result claiming to be the official North America shop, but the site is fake.

The brand matters to the wiki as a concrete [[FakeRetailWebsiteImpersonation]] case. The scam worked because the product category already carries counterfeit concerns, the mobile site looked nearly identical to the legitimate store, and the URL difference was subtle enough that the consumer noticed only at the final purchase step.
