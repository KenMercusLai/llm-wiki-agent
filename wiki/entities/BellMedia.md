# Bell Media

Bell Media appears in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] through [[Crave]] and the [[SphereAbacus]] distribution advance connected to [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]]. The episode uses Bell Media's relationship with HBO catalog distribution in Canada to explain how the show could later reach [[HBO]] internationally without HBO being the original creative controller.

The source makes Bell Media part of a [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] branch rather than only a broadcaster label. Commissioning, catalog relationships, financing advances, and territory sales can all shape whether a lower-budget Canadian show finds larger markets.
