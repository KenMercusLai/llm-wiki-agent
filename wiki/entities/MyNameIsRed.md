# 《我的名字叫红》 / My Name Is Red

[[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]] is the [[OrhanPamuk|奥尔罕·帕慕克]] novel discussed across [[153-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-shang-917670296]] and [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]]. The upper episode builds the world: [[Istanbul]], the 1590/1950 mirror, [[PersianMiniaturePainting]], [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]], [[SufiImageLegitimacy]], [[DivinePerspectiveAesthetics]], [[FrankishPortraiturePressure]], Elegant Effendi's murder, Black's return, Enishte's secret book, and Shekure's first coded contact. The lower episode follows the second half: Shekure, Black, Esther, Hasan, Enishte, Master Osman, Butterfly, Stork, and Olive move through letters, marriage bargaining, workshop suspicion, two murders, a horse-drawing clue, and the final exposure of Olive.

The episodes treat the book as a murder mystery built out of art theory. Its clue system depends on [[MiniatureStyleSignatureTension]]: a painter may avoid signing work, but habits of hand and memory can still appear first as anxiety about style and later as the cracked horse's nose. Its form depends on [[MultiPerspectiveMurderNarration]] and [[StoryWithinStoryNarration]], where corpses, dogs, murderers, children, images, and inherited romances all speak. Its emotional endpoint depends on [[SecularHappinessAgainstIdeology]]: after the grand argument over art, faith, and cultural identity, Shekure still wants safety, children, marriage, beauty, and ordinary happiness.
