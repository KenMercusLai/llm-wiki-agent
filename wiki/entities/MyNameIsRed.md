# 《我的名字叫红》 / My Name Is Red

[[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]] is the [[OrhanPamuk|奥尔罕·帕慕克]] novel discussed in [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]]. The source focuses on the novel's second half: Shekure, Black, Esther, Hasan, Enishte, Master Osman, Butterfly, Stork, and Olive move through letters, marriage bargaining, workshop suspicion, two murders, a horse-drawing clue, and the final exposure of Olive.

The episode treats the book as a murder mystery built out of art theory. Its clue system depends on [[MiniatureStyleSignatureTension]]: a painter may avoid signing work, but habits of hand and memory can still appear in a horse's nose. Its emotional endpoint depends on [[SecularHappinessAgainstIdeology]]: after the grand argument over [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]] and [[FrankishPortraiturePressure]], Shekure still wants safety, children, marriage, beauty, and ordinary happiness.
