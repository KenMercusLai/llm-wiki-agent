# Joseph Schumpeter

Joseph Schumpeter appears in [[e162-kangbo-zhouqi-zhong-de-ai-xin-jishu-zong-zai-xiaotiao-qi-baofa-bad-times-make-good-people-limyzch9la0bbwe8y9geofgqargl]] as part of the economic-cycle vocabulary behind long-wave thinking. The episode groups Schumpeter's long-wave framing alongside Juglar and Kitchin cycles, then contrasts that economic-cycle tradition with [[CarlotaPerez]]'s more technology-installation-oriented framework.

The episode's use of Schumpeter is conceptual rather than biographical. His role is to anchor the idea that innovation, economic growth, and institutional change can interact over long periods, which is why AI can be discussed through [[KondratievCycle]] rather than only through near-term product metrics.
