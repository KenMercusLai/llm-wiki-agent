# American Enterprise Institute

The American Enterprise Institute appears in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] through [[PrestonCooper]], who discusses the Trump administration's graduate [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]]. In this source, AEI functions as the policy-research affiliation for an argument that loan caps can discipline high-priced graduate programs.

The institution's role is narrow here: it is not profiled as a think tank generally, but as the context for Cooper's claim that most graduate borrowers remain under the new caps and that the affected group is concentrated in expensive programs.
