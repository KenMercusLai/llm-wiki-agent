# Segment

Segment appears in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]] as one of [[Vanta]]'s first customer-discovery cases. [[ChristinaCacioppo]] says the first Vanta-like deliverable was a spreadsheet gap assessment for Segment, mapping what the company needed for [[SOC2Audit]], what it already had, and what gaps remained.

The source uses Segment to show how a manual customer artifact can precede software. The spreadsheet was specific enough to help one company, then adaptable enough that Christina could reuse it for [[Front]], supporting Vanta's thesis that startup security and compliance needs had repeatable best-practice structure.

[[tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly-tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly]] adds Segment as market context for [[Hightouch]]. [[KashishGupta]] says two Hightouch co-founders had been early engineers at Segment, and that Segment's model helped the founders understand a later enterprise gap: large customers wanted to bring their own database rather than move customer data into another vendor-controlled store.
