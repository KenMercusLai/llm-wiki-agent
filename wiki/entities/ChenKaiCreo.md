# 陈凯 / Chen Kai (Creo)

陈凯 / Chen Kai is presented in [[e238-liaoliao-harness-shidai-ai-first-de-zuzhi-jiagou-cong-xinren-ren-dao-xinren-ai-51260de8-60ef-4b76-b3e5-2e559c4a0923]] as [[Creo]]'s CEO. His main contribution to the episode is the organization thesis: [[AIFirstOrganization|AI-first]] work is not adding tools to old processes, but redesigning the company so AI can drive daily production and humans focus on direction, values, architecture, and final review.

Chen Kai frames the shift as moving from trusting people by default to learning how to trust AI through guardrails, planning checks, execution review, and shared mechanisms. He also argues that product-manager work changes when market and engineering alignment can be partly absorbed by agents and when product judgment is distributed across engineers and managers.
