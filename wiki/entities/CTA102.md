# CTA-102

CTA-102 enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as an astronomy case where a periodic radio signal was once treated as a possible extraterrestrial-civilization clue before being understood as a natural object. The episode pairs it with the early "little green men" nickname around pulsar discovery.

Its role is to defend scientific restraint without making science boring. [[ScientificSelfCorrection]] can turn a premature alien explanation into a natural phenomenon that is more durable, stranger, and richer than the first fantasy.
