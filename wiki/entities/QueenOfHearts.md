# Queen of Hearts / 红心王后

The Queen of Hearts is the card-queen authority figure in [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]]. The episode follows her through the rose-painting scene, the croquet ground, and the trial, reading her constant beheading commands as a comic exposure of power that issues verdicts before reality can support them.

For the wiki, the Queen anchors [[ArbitraryAuthorityProcedure]]. Her croquet game uses living animals and card soldiers as unstable equipment, and her courtroom logic asks for sentence before evidence. [[AliceWonderlandCharacter|Alice / 爱丽丝]] resists by continuing to ask what the rules and evidence actually are.

## Key Claims
- The Queen's threats create fear, but the episode notes that executions often do not actually happen.
- Her games and trials make authority look absurd because rules, evidence, and tools shift under the participants.
- Alice's resistance to the Queen is a key lower-half case of [[FemaleSelfPossession]].
