# 英利绿色能源 / Yingli Green Energy

Yingli Green Energy appears in [[vol-264-ba-shijiebei-zuowei-fangfa-999416048]] as the first Chinese sponsor in World Cup history. The episode says Yingli sponsored the 2010 tournament for 80 million dollars, making it the opening case in the source's Chinese-sponsor chronology.

The company functions mainly as a historical marker in the episode. Later cases such as [[WandaGroup]], [[Hisense]], [[Mengniu]], and [[Lenovo]] show how Chinese World Cup sponsorship expanded from first-time visibility into a broader globalization, trust, and technology-display strategy.
