# 唐涛 / 唐唐 / Tang Tao

Tang Tao, also referred to as 唐唐 in the episode title, appears in [[ep268-mao-dong-x-tang-tang-da-huang-ni-daodi-za-le-shangcuan-xiatiao-de-huangjin-he-naxie-juebu-xiache-de-ren-lgujs-q24fzqcuutibty8k0bowbv]] as a long-term fund buyer and [[AntFortune|蚂蚁财富]] community creator. He explains gold less as a one-time price call than as a position whose role depends on size, cost basis, holding period, and user purpose.

His key contribution is behavioral segmentation. He describes three kinds of "课金玩家": rational recurring buyers, people saving gold for parents or children, and younger buyers who treat small gold purchases as a monthly reward. That makes him central to the source's links among [[AssetAllocation]], [[InvestmentRiskManagement]], [[HouseholdGoldSavings]], and [[OnlineGoldAccumulation]].
