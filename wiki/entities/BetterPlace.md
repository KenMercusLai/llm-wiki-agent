# Better Place

Better Place appears in [[socialradarsseason2-dimitri-final]] as one of [[DimitriDadiomov]]'s startup experiences before [[ModernTreasury]]. The source says the company grew from roughly 10 people to about 600 while he was there, giving him a close view of the people side of scaling.

The episode does not make Better Place a central product case. Its wiki role is background for Dadiomov's later founder judgment: before building [[MoneyMovementInfrastructure]], he had seen how organizations change as they grow and how operational complexity can become a company-level problem.
