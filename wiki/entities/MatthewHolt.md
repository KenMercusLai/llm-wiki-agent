# Matthew Holt

Matthew Holt appears in [[tech-20260324-0324-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260324-0324-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a former head of a company that examined betting data for fraud. In the episode, he describes the betting pattern around [[JontayPorter]] as suspicious and helps explain how licensed sportsbooks can detect manipulation.

His contribution is operational: every wager in a licensed sportsbook can be geolocated and tracked, then compared against suspicious patterns and reported through integrity-monitor systems. The wiki uses him to connect the Porter example to [[SportsbookIntegrityMonitoring]] rather than treating it as only a sports scandal.
