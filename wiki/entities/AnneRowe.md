# Anne Rowe

Anne Rowe appears in [[youve-come-a-long-way-bibi-israels-crucial-election-6a59f9ae461a6a41901ae410]] as the reporter narrating the final segment on [[A23A]]. Her account turns the iceberg's calving, grounding, drift, rotation, breakup, ecological effects, and climate meaning into a source for [[IcebergClimateSymbolism]].

The episode uses Rowe's segment as an obituary-style environmental story. A23A is not presented only as an object measurement; it becomes a way to talk about polar life cycles, Southern Ocean ecology, and anxiety about warming seas.
