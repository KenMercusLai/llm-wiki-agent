# BoSo

BoSo is the UK student marketplace [[HarjTaggar]] and [[KulvirTaggar]] applied to [[YCombinator]] with in Winter 2007 in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]]. The name stood for "buy or sell online," but the source says it sounded like "Bozo" to Americans, creating jokes and even an awkward immigration moment at SFO.

[[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] adds [[TomBlomfield]]'s participant view of the early Oxford student-marketplace environment. Tom says he joined a larger team through Oxford Entrepreneurs before it narrowed, giving BoSo an additional role as his first step away from the safer law, banking, consulting, and private-equity path around him.

The source uses BoSo as the starting point for [[StartupLegitimacyTransfer]]. YC did not merely fund the idea; the interview made Harj and Kulvir feel treated like serious adults, gave Harj's family outside validation, and helped push the team beyond a narrow student-marketplace concept toward the more ambitious [[AutomaticAuctions]] direction.
