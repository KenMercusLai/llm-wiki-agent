# Daniel Kan

Daniel Kan appears in [[tsr-s3-kylevogt-v3final-tsr-s3-kylevogt-v3final]] as the co-founder [[KyleVogt]] brought into [[Cruise]] a few months after starting the company as a solo founder. Vogt says he initially tried to find a co-founder before beginning Cruise, then hired early engineers and later added Daniel because he saw practical startup grit and operating traits he associated with [[JustinKan]].

The source uses Daniel Kan less as a biographical subject than as a co-founder fit signal. Cruise had a hard technical problem, long timelines, hardware cost, and investor skepticism; Daniel's value was connected to making that work more company-like after Vogt had narrowed the first autonomous-driving wedge.
