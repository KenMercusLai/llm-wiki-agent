# StepFun

StepFun is the foundation-model company discussed in [[131-yin-qi-churen-jieyue-xingchen-dongshizhang-de-fangtan]]. [[YinQi]] says he participated from the company's early planning phase, that it was founded in April 2023, and that he became its chair while also chairing [[QianliTechnology]].

## Source Position
- StepFun is positioned as a company trying to become one of China's strongest foundation-model companies while also becoming commercially closed-loop.
- The name is tied to the mathematical step function, representing non-linear change from quantity to quality.
- Yin says StepFun should continue foundation-model R&D and try to remain in the world-class group over the next three years.
- Its model directions are described as base models, all-modal models, and [[VisionLanguageActionModels]].
- Its commercial pull is [[AIPlusTerminals]], especially the first vehicle scenario shared with [[QianliTechnology]].
- The company is presented as needing [[AIOrganizationDesign]]: high talent density, strong coordination, mission, and ego control.
