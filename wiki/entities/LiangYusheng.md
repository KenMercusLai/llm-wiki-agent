# 梁羽生 / Liang Yusheng

梁羽生 / Liang Yusheng appears in [[112-xianliao-jinyong-diyidan-mingjiao-jingran-jintian-hai-cunzai-754593095]] as one of the learned representatives of new-school wuxia beside [[JinYong|金庸]]. The episode does not give a full Liang Yusheng reading; it uses him as a comparison point for the kind of cultural and literary preparation that could make martial-arts fiction feel rooted rather than merely invented.

The source's contrast matters because it defines [[WuxiaIntertextualCraft]] as more than naming flair. In the hosts' view, Jin Yong and Liang Yusheng drew from classical learning, poetry, historical materials, and older genre conventions, while later wuxia could feel weaker when it inherited surface form without the same stock of cultural material.
