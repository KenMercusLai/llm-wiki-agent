# Ralph Goody

Ralph Goody is a [[PlanGrid]] co-founder and [[TigerEye]] co-founder discussed in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. He is also [[TracyYoung]]'s husband, making the episode's spouse co-founding discussion concrete rather than theoretical.

The source uses Young and Goody's working relationship to show why co-founder role clarity matters. Young says building with a significant other is hard and not something she would broadly recommend, but their shared history, trust, and clear responsibility boundaries help [[TigerEye]] make decisions without turning every overlap into conflict.
