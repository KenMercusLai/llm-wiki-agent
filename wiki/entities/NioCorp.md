# NioCorp

NioCorp is the company where [[MarkSmith]] works in the later section of [[battlefield-rare-earths-how-the-us-lost-to-china]]. The episode says NioCorp is trying to develop a rare-earth mine in Nebraska and has received millions of dollars in government loans.

The company matters as a live example of [[StateBackedRareEarthRebuilding]]. Smith says NioCorp has been meeting with defense, commerce, interior, and [[WhiteHouse]] officials, and he says he would view the U.S. government as a strong equity partner if it invested directly.

NioCorp extends the episode beyond the [[MountainPassRareEarthMine]] story. It shows that rebuilding rare-earth capacity is not only about one reopened legacy mine; it is also a financing and permitting problem for newer projects that need government help before private markets can trust the economics.
