# PlayStation

PlayStation is the [[Sony]] console platform used in [[jiu-shidai-diantai-28-shiti-youxi-de-shidai-zhongjie-zhiji-buru-chongxin-dingyi-yongyou-998430652]] to discuss the move from physical discs toward [[DigitalGameDistribution]].

The source argues that PlayStation's physical-to-digital transition is especially sensitive because console players have long used discs for resale, collection, lending, and offline confidence. Even when modern discs require patches or online activation, the physical object still gives players a stronger sense of control than a purely digital license.
