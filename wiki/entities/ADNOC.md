# ADNOC

ADNOC appears in [[roaring-trades-oil-majors-secret-success-story-6a4636f160cad2674e6d9674]] as a national oil company trying to expand into energy trading. The episode lists it with [[ExxonMobil]] as a new competitor recruiting traders and trying to narrow the advantage held by European majors.

The source's point is that national resource ownership does not automatically create [[EnergyTradingScaleAdvantage]]. Trading requires information, logistics, risk systems, and market relationships built through repeated global transactions, not only barrels in the ground.

## Connections
- [[ExxonMobil]] - another catch-up competitor in the source.
- [[BP]], [[Shell]], and [[TotalEnergies]] - European trading incumbents.
- [[EnergyTradingScaleAdvantage]] - concept that explains why catch-up may take years.
