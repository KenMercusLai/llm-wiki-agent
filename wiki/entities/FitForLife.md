# Fit For Life

Fit For Life is the post-e.l.f. company [[JoeyShamah]] describes in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. The company licenses brands such as Reebok, Gaiam, and Fila for fitness products including mats, weights, jump ropes, and weighted vests.

The episode presents Fit For Life as evidence that Joey did not settle into retirement after leaving [[ELFCosmetics]]; he moved into operating, licensing, and consumer-product expansion.
