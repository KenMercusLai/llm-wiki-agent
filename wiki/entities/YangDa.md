# 杨大

杨大 is the resurrected skull figure in [[QiSi|《起死》]] as discussed by [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]]. Once revived by [[Zhuangzi|庄子]], he does not become grateful or spiritually converted. He asks where his clothes, baggage, and umbrella went, then demands compensation.

The episode uses 杨大 to bring abstract philosophy down to survival. His refusal to accept [[QiWuLun|《齐物论》]] language turns the story away from mystical enlightenment and toward responsibility, poverty, police power, and [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]].

## Connections
- [[QiSi|《起死》]] - story where Yang Da appears.
- [[Zhuangzi|庄子]] - figure whose resurrection intervention creates Yang Da's practical claim.

- [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]] - concept for the story's critique of abstract language used to escape concrete responsibility.
