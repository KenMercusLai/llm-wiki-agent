# Stanford Startup Garage

Stanford Startup Garage is the Stanford class context for [[DoorDash]] in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]]. [[TonyXu]] says the founding group worked together in the class while exploring ways to help small businesses with marketing, sales, and operational problems.

In this source, Startup Garage matters less as classroom branding than as a forcing function for field discovery. The team had to leave campus, speak with merchants, and eventually practice [[CustomerDiscoveryByDoingWork]], which led to the delivery problem and the launch of [[PaloAltoDelivery]].
