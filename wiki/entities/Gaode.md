# Gaode

Gaode appears in [[ep117-doubao-yuehuo-guoyi-ali-zaizao-qianwen-shibushi-wanle-lmp0pzdig2ijow5k3cnnnvvqq6sa]] as part of [[Alibaba]]'s service ecosystem behind [[Qwen]]. The hosts use map and local-life capabilities to illustrate why a mobile AI assistant could become useful beyond search: it can help compare places, route users, and connect recommendations to real movement and local service decisions.

## Key Points
- Gaode is one of the "hands and feet" that could let [[Qwen]] act on user intent in local, travel, and mobility scenarios.
- The source's assistant thesis depends on integrating map context with shopping, travel, ticketing, and other Alibaba surfaces.
- Local recommendation through an assistant can improve decision efficiency, but it also raises ranking, advertising, and platform-transparency questions.
