# 蒲莲生 / Pu Liansheng

蒲莲生 / Pu Liansheng is the physician in the Chinese 1986 case described by [[119-anlesi-xianchang-zhengyin-shengming-ruci-zhengui-770771087]]. According to the episode, [[WangMingchen]] asked Pu to help Wang's mother die during late-stage liver cirrhosis, and Pu agreed by administering medication.

The court story, as the episode summarizes it, sits at the boundary between mercy, homicide, and absence of law: first instance reasoning treated the act as intentional deprivation of life but with minor circumstances and limited social harm, so it did not constitute a crime.
