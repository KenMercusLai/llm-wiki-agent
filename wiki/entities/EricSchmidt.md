# Eric Schmidt

Eric Schmidt appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as the CEO [[Google]] eventually hired after its financing terms included an expectation that a CEO would be brought in. [[RonConway]] mentions Schmidt while explaining how Google moved from founder-led early company to a larger operating organization.

The source does not treat Schmidt as the center of the episode. His role is a governance and scaling marker around [[LarryPage]], [[SergeyBrin]], [[KleinerPerkins]], and [[SequoiaCapital]].
