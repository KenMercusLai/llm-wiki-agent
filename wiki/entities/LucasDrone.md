# Lucas Drone

Lucas Drone is the U.S. low-cost uncrewed system discussed by [[StaceyPettijohn]] in [[tech-20260319-0319-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260319-0319-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says the United States announced its combat debut and describes it as essentially reverse-engineered from the Iranian [[Shahed136]], though with shorter range and a smaller payload.

In the wiki, Lucas Drone matters as evidence that [[LowCostDroneWarfare]] is not only an adversary problem. The United States is also adopting cheaper, good-enough systems when cost, availability, range, and attrition economics matter more than exquisite performance.
