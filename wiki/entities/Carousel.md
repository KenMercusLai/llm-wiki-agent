# Carousel

Carousel appears in [[tsr-s4-drewhouston-v5-tsr-s4-drewhouston-v5]] as a [[Dropbox]] photo-sharing product that [[DrewHouston]] later killed during the company's strategic refocus. The episode frames Carousel as part of Dropbox's attempt to diversify beyond basic storage before [[GooglePhotos]] made the incumbent free-storage playbook impossible to ignore.

The useful wiki role is strategic rather than product-historical. Carousel helps explain [[StrategicFocusUnderIncumbentPressure]]: a startup facing bundled, free, platform-backed competition may need to stop funding adjacent products even when they fit the broad original surface.
