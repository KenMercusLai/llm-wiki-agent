# Mary Childs

Mary Childs appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] host who joins [[BobbyAllen]] in testing [[Kalshi]] by betting on whether [[DonaldTrump]] would say specific words in a speech about [[Iran]].

Her role is to make the user-experience critique concrete. The episode says Mary lost a five-dollar bet on the word "tariff," and the experiment helped shift the discussion from abstract [[PredictionMarketPublicGoodClaim|public-good claims]] to the gambling-like feeling of rapid event markets, loss-chasing, and attention narrowed to settlement triggers.
