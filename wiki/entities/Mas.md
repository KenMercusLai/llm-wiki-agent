# Mas

Mas is [[Ninety]]'s AI companion bot mentioned in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. [[MarkAbbott]] says the name is short for Maslow and describes it as a way to answer questions about what is working or not working across an organization.

## Key Points
- Represents Ninety's move from workflow software toward AI embedded in organizational operating data.
- Depends on the platform's accumulated context about vision, values, roles, accountabilities, rocks, metrics, and feedback.
- Connects Ninety's AI strategy to [[AINativeSaaSThreat]] and [[SaaSTrustMoat]] because proprietary workflow context may be harder to recreate than a generic app surface.
