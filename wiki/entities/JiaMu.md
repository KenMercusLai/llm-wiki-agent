# 贾母 / Jia Mu

[[167-ming-ruo-zhao-shuang-weishenme-hongloumeng-bu-xuyao-daoming-949917324]] makes 贾母 central to a [[RedChamberLegalReading]] of [[HongLouMeng|《红楼梦》]]. The episode argues that she may be protecting [[LinDaiyu|林黛玉]] after Lin Ruhai's death, managing Daiyu's money separately, and possibly arranging a future Bao-Dai marriage rather than simply allowing the [[JiaFamilyHousehold|贾府]] to consume Lin family assets.

This reading also explains why the episode rejects the later "掉包计" as implausible. Under elite marriage procedure and Jia Mu's established care for Daiyu, a sudden bride swap is not only emotionally out of character but legally and ritually hard to sustain.
