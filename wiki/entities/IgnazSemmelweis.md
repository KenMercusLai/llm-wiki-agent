# Ignaz Semmelweis

Ignaz Semmelweis appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] as the episode's cautionary medical case. The source says his handwashing evidence was correct and important, but his combative framing triggered defensive resistance from doctors, which delayed broader acceptance.

The episode uses Semmelweis to separate truth from persuasion. A dissenter may have the stronger evidence and still fail if the presentation attacks the receiving group's identity, moral self-image, or professional status too directly. This makes him central to [[InsiderDissentStrategy|insider dissent strategy]] and [[ConstructiveDissent|constructive dissent]].
