# Maiquer Group / 麦趣尔

Maiquer Group appears in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] through the Li brothers' cooperation with [[LanShili]] around the attempted acquisition of [[ThaiOrientAirlines]]. The episode says the Li side put substantial money into the project, while Lan did not deliver the promised cash contribution and later moved shared equity into companies he controlled.

The resulting criminal accusation became one of Lan's major post-East-Star cases. A court later acquitted him of contract fraud, finding insufficient evidence of the required criminal intent to illegally possess funds, but the episode stresses that the acquittal did not make the cooperation dispute or equity movement disappear as a civil controversy.
