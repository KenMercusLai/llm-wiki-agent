# Seasteading Institute

Seasteading Institute appears in [[e233-guigu-youyi-de-quanli-wang-ruhe-xingcheng-liaoliao-bide-tier-de-sixiang-qimeng-pintu-2a529abf-3c14-4ae7-a0a5-fe1340a66c7d]] as the clearest example of [[PeterThiel]]'s earlier politics of exit. The episode describes the idea as building cities in international waters outside ordinary state jurisdiction.

In the source, seasteading is important because it contrasts with Thiel's later involvement in U.S. electoral politics. [[PoliticalExitToEntry]] names that shift from technological or geographic escape toward using [[DonaldTrump]] and [[JDVance]]-era politics as a power route.
