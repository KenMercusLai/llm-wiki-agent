# Stephanie Byrne

Stephanie Byrne appears in [[the-laws-of-the-office-revisited]] as the episode's concrete [[PeterPrinciple]] case. She loved a social-media-specialist role at a large university because it involved creativity, independence, and behind-the-scenes work.

After promotion into a web-content role, the job shifted toward meetings, directing other people, and leading trainings. The episode treats her choice to step back into work closer to her strengths as a way to beat the Peter Principle without assuming every upward move is a better fit.
