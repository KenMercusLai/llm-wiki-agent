# Andreas Vesalius / 安德烈亚斯·维萨里

Andreas Vesalius appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the anatomy-history figure who challenged inherited Galenic authority through direct dissection and anatomical publication. The episode uses him to move from Victor Frankenstein's assembled body into the longer history of anatomical knowledge.

Vesalius matters because the novel's horror depends on a real epistemic shift: bodies become knowable by being opened and compared. That makes him part of [[CadaverTradeAnatomyFear]] and [[ObservationBeforeInference]] rather than only a background medical name.
