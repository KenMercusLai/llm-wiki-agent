# 《话剧观众须知二十则》

《话剧观众须知二十则》 is one of the [[LaoShe|老舍]] pieces discussed in [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]]. The episode uses it to connect old theater manners to present-day disputes over talking, coughing, eating, applauding, phone use, children, and audience interaction in cinemas, theaters, and concert settings.

The source treats the piece as more than a manners list. It becomes a concrete case for [[CivicAudienceEtiquette]]: a shared cultural setting only works when people notice the tacit contract of that setting and leave room for others to experience it.
