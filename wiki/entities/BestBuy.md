# Best Buy

Best Buy appears in [[ep86-mianzi-dizi-rizi-caibao-zhi-jiang-zhe-san-jian-shi-lukmnlkkuxvrvv12frosd9teg7bj]] as a retail inventory-risk example. The episode uses the rise of online purchasing and showroom behavior to explain why a store can look busy while inventory, sales conversion, and margins are under pressure.

## Source Position
- Best Buy is used to show that inventory is only valuable if it can be sold at acceptable prices.
- The episode connects electronics retail to [[InventoryWriteDownRisk]] because goods can become obsolete or require discounting.
- The case reinforces the broader point that balance-sheet assets need business-context interpretation, not only accounting labels.
