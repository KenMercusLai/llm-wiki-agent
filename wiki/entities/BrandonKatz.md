# Brandon Katz

Brandon Katz is the [[GreenlightAnalytics]] director of insights and content strategy interviewed in [[tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the episode, he explains [[Netflix]]'s second-season viewer losses as a [[StreamingAudienceRetention]] problem rather than a simple quality or cancellation problem.

Katz's central distinction is that first seasons can attract curiosity while second seasons test loyalty. He argues that the [[BingeReleaseModel]], long production gaps, content abundance, and stronger competition can all make it harder for viewers to finish, remember, and return to a show.
