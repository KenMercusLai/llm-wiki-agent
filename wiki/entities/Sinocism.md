# Sinocism

Sinocism is the China-focused newsletter by [[BillBishop]] that became [[Substack]]'s first real user case in [[tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov-tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov]]. In [[ChrisBest]]'s account, Bishop already had an audience and wanted to charge for the newsletter, but needed publishing, email, and payment infrastructure connected.

The first-day revenue result, about $100,000 in the source's account, gave Substack a concrete early proof point. It also came with a caveat: Best says Sinocism remained the biggest customer for years, so the launch proved demand could exist but did not make customer acquisition easy.
