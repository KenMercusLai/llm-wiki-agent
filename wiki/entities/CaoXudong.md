# Cao Xudong

Cao Xudong is the founder and leader of [[Momenta]]. In [[132-dui-xinghaitu-chuangshiren-gaojiyang-de-3-xiaoshi-fangtan]], [[GaoJiyang]] describes him as technically strong, decisive, AI-oriented, and long-term in his commitment to autonomous driving.

[[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] adds Cao's own account of his method. He traces his path from aerospace-related study and statistical physics into statistical learning, then through [[MicrosoftResearchAsia]], [[SunJian]]'s experiment culture, [[SenseTime]] product delivery, and finally Momenta's [[AutonomousDrivingDataFlywheel]]. The interview presents him as a founder who combines long-horizon AI ambition with a practical insistence on customer value, mainline reuse, and [[LowCostShortCycleValidation]].

## Key Points
- Cao says Momenta's mission is "Better AI, Better Life" and frames the company as a [[PhysicalAI]] company whose autonomous-driving work can extend toward robots.
- He argues that advanced driving has moved from a fun toy toward a usable product and that supplier concentration will reward companies with data, R&D scale, customer programs, and delivery systems.
- He treats [[WorldModels]] as the bridge between cars and robotics: pretraining physical common sense, mid-training domain common sense, and post-training behavior alignment can be reused across related physical tasks.
- He says organization change was necessary when Momenta moved from a loose research-institute style into a customer-centered startup, especially after overpromising and early mass-production delivery pain.
- His decision method emphasizes years of early thinking for major choices, first-hand information, discussion with strong operators, and short-cycle evidence rather than last-minute founder impulse.
