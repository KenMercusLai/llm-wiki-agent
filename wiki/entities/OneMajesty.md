# One Majesty

One Majesty is the bright pink container ship at the center of the opening story in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]]. [[ChristianStClair]] initially believed [[FantagraphicsBooks]] books were on the vessel after it was attacked in the [[StraitOfHormuz]], but he later learned the books were still in [[India]] and had never been loaded.

The ship still matters as a logistics case. The episode uses One Majesty to explain how container tracking, carrier silence, port schedules, war risk, and [[GeneralAverage]] can turn cargo status into both an information problem and a financial-risk problem.
