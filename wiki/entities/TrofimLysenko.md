# Trofim Lysenko / 李森科

Trofim Lysenko enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as the episode's warning case for pseudoscience backed by state power. [[Beimin|北明]] uses Lysenko to show that scientific communities can be damaged when political authority appoints one doctrine as truth and suppresses ordinary genetic research.

The case qualifies the episode's defense of science. [[ScientificSkepticism]] is not worship of scientists or institutions; it depends on public evidence, criticism, and [[ScientificSelfCorrection]] staying stronger than authority.
