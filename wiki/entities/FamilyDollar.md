# Family Dollar

Family Dollar appears in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] as one of the original dollar-store targets for [[ELFCosmetics]]. The pitch failed because the buyer preferred quantity-oriented dollar-store cosmetics rather than Joey and Scott's branded value line.

The rejection matters because it forced [[JoeyShamah]] to validate the brand through PR, e-commerce, and later non-dollar-store retail rather than through the originally imagined channel.
