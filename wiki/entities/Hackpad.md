# Hackpad

Hackpad is the collaborative-document product team [[ChristinaCacioppo]] joined at [[Dropbox]] in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]]. Christina worked with the Hackpad founders and describes the team as separate from the main Dropbox organization while it was becoming [[DropboxPaper]].

In the source, Hackpad matters because it made enterprise launch constraints concrete. [[DropboxPaper]] was widely used inside Dropbox, but external launch ran into security and compliance commitments in customer contracts, helping Christina later recognize [[SOC2Audit]] pain as a startup opportunity.
