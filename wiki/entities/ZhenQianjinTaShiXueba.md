# 甄千金他是学霸 / Zhen Qianjin Ta Shi Xueba

Zhen Qianjin Ta Shi Xueba is a live-action short-drama example used by [[LiJiajia]] in [[269-zhenren-duanju-de-xiayizhan-yu-ai-gongsheng-gongyehua-he-hao-gushi-lmwhgqd0rge4mr0fwp5qpr2scwxt]]. She uses it to explain [[CharacterRelationshipStoryLogic]]: because the heroine's family relationship is changed so she does not return to the city starved for love, her motivation becomes studying and changing her life rather than revenge.

The work is useful in the wiki as a concrete case against purely trope-level reading. The source says changing the character relationship changes motivation, which changes events, which changes the story; simply labeling the work "anti-routine" or "not revenge" misses the actual production logic.
