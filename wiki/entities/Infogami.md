# Infogami

Infogami appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as the [[YCombinator]] company associated with [[AaronSwartz]] that merged with [[Reddit]]. [[SteveHuffman]] says the intended combined vision was broader than Reddit alone, involving lists and webs of structured things.

The merger became strained because Reddit had users and therefore received more attention. In Huffman's account, Infogami is the organizational context behind his close but later broken relationship with Swartz.
