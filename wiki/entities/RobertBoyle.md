# Robert Boyle / 罗伯特·波义耳

Robert Boyle appears in [[68-fengkuang-shiyan-shi-ai-zhe-gaisi-de-qiuzhiyu-664384549]] as part of the source's account of experimental science's contested status. The episode uses Boyle's vacuum experiments and dispute with [[ThomasHobbes|Thomas Hobbes]] to show that public experiment, apparatus, and witnessed demonstration were not always accepted as decisive knowledge.

In this source, Boyle stands for the move toward [[ExperimentalScienceEthics]] and method not because the episode centers him morally, but because his experimental practice helps frame why experiments need shared evidence, replication, and public standards rather than private assertion.
