# 蹦迪班长

蹦迪班长 is introduced in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] as a public-account writer and guest discussing [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] with [[Beimin|北明]] and [[ZhouMing|周明]]. The source records a high evaluation of the series, with the main reservation that the first three episodes are slow for viewers who are not already patient with [[StarWars|Star Wars]].

The episode uses his comments to connect the series with the 2022 mood around freedom, public life, Iran, Ukraine, and ordinary courage. In the wiki, that makes him part of the source's bridge from science-fiction television to [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]].
