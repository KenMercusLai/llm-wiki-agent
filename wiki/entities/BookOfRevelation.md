# 《启示录》 / Book of Revelation

《启示录》 / Book of Revelation is the biblical text the hosts explain in [[03-laiboweici-de-zange-yao-you-guang-napa-feitu-zhishang-528163687]] before turning to [[CanticleForLeibowitz|《莱博维茨的赞歌》 / A Canticle for Leibowitz]]. In the episode, it supplies the language of apocalypse, judgment, fire from heaven, beasts, final destruction, and renewal.

The page matters here as a literary-symbolic source rather than as theology alone. Nuclear explosions can look like apocalyptic fire; "bright star" language can evoke a fallen light; water and new creation can turn wasteland survival into more than logistics. That makes the text central to [[ApocalypticLiterarySymbolism]] and a counterpoint to purely secular [[ApocalypticThinking]].

[[53-meigui-de-mingzi-shang-zhen-yu-jia-zheng-yu-xie-quanshi-yu-guodu-quanshi-636490184]] adds a medieval-mystery use through [[TheNameOfTheRose|《玫瑰的名字》 / The Name of the Rose]]. In that source, Revelation language appears in library inscriptions, the elderly monk's trumpet framing, and the apparent patterning of deaths by hail, blood, and prophecy-like signs. The episode treats this symbolism as powerful but not automatically trustworthy, tying the page to [[InterpretationAndOverinterpretation]].

[[54-meigui-de-mingzi-xia-zhen-yu-jia-zheng-yu-xie-quanshi-yu-guodu-quanshi-638214699]] completes that caution. The Revelation sequence is not the true murder blueprint; it is a pattern assembled from coincidence, fear, and [[JorgeOfBurgos|Jorge]]'s later nudging. In this branch, Revelation functions as a test case for [[ConspiracyTheoryPatternSeeking]].
