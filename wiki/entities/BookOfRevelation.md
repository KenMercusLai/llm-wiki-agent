# 《启示录》 / Book of Revelation

《启示录》 / Book of Revelation is the biblical text the hosts explain in [[03-laiboweici-de-zange-yao-you-guang-napa-feitu-zhishang-528163687]] before turning to [[CanticleForLeibowitz|《莱博维茨的赞歌》 / A Canticle for Leibowitz]]. In the episode, it supplies the language of apocalypse, judgment, fire from heaven, beasts, final destruction, and renewal.

The page matters here as a literary-symbolic source rather than as theology alone. Nuclear explosions can look like apocalyptic fire; "bright star" language can evoke a fallen light; water and new creation can turn wasteland survival into more than logistics. That makes the text central to [[ApocalypticLiterarySymbolism]] and a counterpoint to purely secular [[ApocalypticThinking]].
