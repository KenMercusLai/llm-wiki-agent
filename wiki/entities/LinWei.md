# Lin Wei

Lin Wei is the later [[TongxinSoftware]] chair discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode portrays him as a relatively opaque investor or professional-manager figure whose style is associated with execution, sales discipline, cost control, and stronger organizational hierarchy.

## Source Position
- Lin is described as taking over from [[WangJiping]] around 2024.
- The episode says Tongxin's losses narrowed substantially, while attributing part of that improvement to layoffs.
- He becomes the management-style lens for the annual-meeting dress-code conflict.
- The source treats his style as part of [[TechnicalCultureSalesCultureTension]] rather than a standalone personality story.
