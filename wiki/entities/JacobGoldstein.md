# Jacob Goldstein

Jacob Goldstein is one of the [[PlanetMoney]] reporters whose 2014 [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] flight segment is replayed in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]]. With [[ZoeChase|Zoe Chase]], he documented how a very cheap ticket could coexist with fees for seat choice, bags, water, and other parts of the trip that many passengers expected to be included.

Goldstein's role in this source is to make [[AirlineUnbundling]] visible as a passenger experience rather than an abstract pricing model. The replayed segment helps show why Spirit could be disliked and commercially successful at the same time.
