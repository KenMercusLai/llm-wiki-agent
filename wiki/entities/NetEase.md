# NetEase

NetEase appears in [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]] as one of the large Chinese game companies changing its posture toward AI. [[Xiaoning]] says NetEase had not seemed as active in AI application earlier, but public signals in 2026 showed more active AI adoption.

Yanxuan represents selected-goods and ODM ecommerce, while Kaola represents the cross-border import window that NetEase later sold to [[Alibaba]].
