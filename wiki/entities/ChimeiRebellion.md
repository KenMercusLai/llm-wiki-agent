# Red Eyebrows / 赤眉

The Red Eyebrows / 赤眉 are one of the uprisings named in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] as the [[XinDynasty|Xin dynasty]] unraveled. The episode places them in a wider crisis of displacement, inflation, natural disaster, frontier stress, and popular violence under [[WangMang|王莽]].

The source does not make 赤眉 the episode's main subject. Their wiki role is to mark the social and military pressure that turned Wang Mang's ideological state project into collapse, alongside [[LulinRebellion|绿林]] and the [[KunyangBattle|Battle of Kunyang]].

## Key Claims
- The Red Eyebrows represent the popular-revolt side of Xin collapse.
- The uprising is connected in the episode to state policy failure rather than treated as isolated disorder.
- Their presence helps show that legitimacy claims failed at the social level.
