# Mark Brown

Mark Brown appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as a former instructor at the main [[FederalLawEnforcementTrainingCenters]] campus near Brunswick, Georgia. The source says he taught there for five years and uses his account to make [[FederalLawEnforcementTraining]] concrete: recruits practice in classrooms, ranges, dormitories, and a simulated city rather than only learning policy in the abstract.

Brown's most important contribution is his crowd-control example. He says trainees were taught to respect protest rights, avoid arguing with crowds, and focus on safely removing a handcuffed person, making him the episode's practical witness for the gap between formal training and visible field conduct.
