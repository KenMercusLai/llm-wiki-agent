# Project Aristotle

Project Aristotle appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] as [[Google]]'s team-effectiveness research project. The source says Google started the project in 2012 and found psychological safety to be important for strong teams.

The episode then qualifies that result through later research: psychological safety alone is not enough if a team cannot tolerate disagreement, slowdowns, conflict, and minority views. In this source, Project Aristotle therefore becomes part of [[ConstructiveDissent|constructive dissent]] rather than only a slogan about pleasant workplace culture.
