# Michele Folletti

Michele Folletti is the mayor of [[Lugano]] in [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]], where he defends the city's [[LuganoPlanB|Plan B]] Bitcoin push against concerns that the city could attract criminal money. His argument is comparative: he says mafia groups are more interested in fiat money laundering and that cash is the most anonymous form of money.

For the wiki, Folletti's role is to connect [[MunicipalBitcoinAdoption]] with [[VirtualAssetAMLRisk]] and [[CryptoPublicLegitimacy]]. The source does not establish his claim as an independent compliance finding; it records it as the mayor's defense of [[Bitcoin]] adoption in a city trying to build a crypto cluster.
