# Island Bee Company

Island Bee Company is the Martha's Vineyard family apiary discussed by [[FelixCollin]] in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The business was started by Felix's parents in 1999 and sells raw honey, candles, gift products, and [[Hive5]], a moisturizer built from hive ingredients.

The episode presents Island Bee Company as a [[FamilyBusinessScaling]] and [[ChannelFocusExperiments]] case. Guy suggests lower-risk B2B paths such as corporate gifting, weddings, hotels, and local partnerships because production is still handled by the family. [[RonnenHarary]] argues that if Felix wants a larger consumer-products company, he needs to test trade shows, distributors, retail expansion, and more consistent social storytelling rather than treating fast growth itself as the enemy.
