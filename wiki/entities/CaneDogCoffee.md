# Cane Dog Coffee

Cane Dog Coffee is the Barbados-based specialty coffee brand co-founded by [[DominicWindhamGiddens]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. The business sells widely across Barbados through hotels, restaurants, cafes, offices, supermarkets, gift shops, and local shops, with about $2 million in annual sales.

The episode frames Cane Dog's next problem as international expansion from an island operating base. [[GuyRaz]] argues that the brand should communicate Barbados and the Caribbean more immediately, then test focused routes such as regional U.S. grocers and hotel introductions instead of attempting broad national expansion. The case extends [[LocalMarketProof]] from domestic local launch into cross-border market selection.
