# 《花衣魔笛手：传说背后的欧洲中世纪》

《花衣魔笛手：传说背后的欧洲中世纪》 is the [[AbeKinya|阿布谨也]] book centered in [[111-huayi-modishou-kuailai-he-lishixuejia-yiqi-tuili-tonghua-zhenxiang-751190766]]. The episode describes it as a history book that reads [[PiedPiperOfHamelin|花衣魔笛手]] through evidence, variants, and medieval social conditions rather than as a stable children's story.

The book's importance in the source is methodological. It moves from stained glass, a missal note, manuscript evidence, city inscriptions, and local geography toward [[Hamelin|哈默尔恩]]'s mills, city law, church power, war, guilds, migration routes, religious frenzy, famine, plague, and excluded groups. That makes it a case for [[LegendAsSocialHistory]] and [[EvidenceBoundFolkloreInquiry]].
