# TPG

TPG is the private equity firm that later bought a majority stake in [[ELFCosmetics]] at roughly a $265 million valuation in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. Unlike the failed strategic-buyer process with [[LOreal]], TPG wanted [[JoeyShamah]] and his family to roll equity and stay involved.

The episode frames TPG as the ownership transition that brought in [[TarangAmin]] and helped move e.l.f. toward a public-company stage.
