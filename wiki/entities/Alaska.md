# Alaska / 阿拉斯加

Alaska appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] as the American coast approached by the [[GreatNorthernExpedition]]. [[VitusBering]]'s Saint Peter and [[AlexeiChirikov]]'s Saint Paul both reach or approach the region, but the source emphasizes uncertainty, limited landing time, failed contact, and fear of water, food, reefs, and local people.

The episode also treats Alaska as the later commercial and colonial afterlife of the expedition routes. [[SeaOtter]] demand, company conflict, [[RussianAmericanCompany]] consolidation, marine-animal depletion, and eventual sale to the [[UnitedStates]] make Alaska a key node in [[RussianNorthPacificExpansion]] and the [[ExplorationToExtractionPipeline]].
