# Immanuel Kant / 康德

Immanuel Kant / 康德 appears in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]] as a retributivist reference in the discussion of criminal punishment. The episode presents retribution not as crude revenge, but as a way to treat the offender as a responsible moral agent.

Kant's role is therefore tied to dignity and proportionality. The source contrasts that with utilitarian deterrence and with harsh punishment that treats the punished person merely as a tool.
