# 我们为什么要睡觉 / Why We Sleep

《我们为什么要睡觉》 is the sleep book cited in the original episode file and summarized in [[zhongnian-san-zhanghu-xianjinliu-jirou-shuimian-lnyomru5v2yzo1-otuyw2mdj-vae]]. The episode uses it mainly for the bedroom-temperature point: cooler sleep environments can support the body-temperature drop associated with falling asleep.

The book reference sits inside [[SleepAsDailyHealthAccount]], not a standalone medical protocol. The episode's practical emphasis is environmental: regular wake time, morning natural light, darkness, reduced late screens, earlier caffeine, alcohol caution, and temperature management.
