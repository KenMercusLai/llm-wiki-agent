# Tongcheng Travel

Tongcheng Travel is an OTA competitor and later consolidation-linked platform in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode connects its founder Wu Zhixiang to an Alibaba-style "China supplier" ground-sales background and says the company later received investment from [[Tencent]] and [[Ctrip]] before merging with [[Elong]].

Tongcheng matters in the source because it represents a distribution path different from [[Qunar]]'s search-comparison model and Ctrip's early hotel-call-center model. Its later platform position also shows how [[OTAPlatformConcentration]] can happen through partnerships and investment, not only direct acquisition.
