# Marvel Cinematic Universe / 轢ュ쮤若뉐츢

The Marvel Cinematic Universe is the modern superhero-franchise reference in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The episode uses it less as a film-history subject than as a familiar entry point for how [[NorseMythology]] reaches mass culture through [[Thor|Thor]], [[Loki|Loki]], [[Odin|Odin]], and apocalyptic battle imagery.

The source's caution is that Marvel recognition can hide how much the old material has changed. Kinship, heroic tone, cosmology, and [[Ragnarok|Ragnarok]] are modernized into a superhero world, making the MCU a practical example of [[EpicModernAfterlife]] and of the risk named by [[HomericAdaptationModernization]] in the Homeric branch.
