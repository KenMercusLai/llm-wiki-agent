# Fan Min

Fan Min is one of the [[Ctrip]] founders in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The source presents him as the founding member with hotel-management and travel-agency experience, giving Ctrip travel-industry knowledge beyond technology and finance.

The episode later contrasts Fan's operating and preserving strengths with the company's slower response to mobile competition from [[Qunar]] and [[Elong]]. This makes him part of the source's broader claim that [[OnlineTravelAgency]] platforms need both stable operations and constant adaptation to user-interface and supplier-system shifts.
