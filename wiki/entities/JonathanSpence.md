# 史景迁 / Jonathan Spence

史景迁 / Jonathan Spence enters the wiki through [[101-caoyin-yu-kangxi-hongloumeng-xing-zhong-you-shi-735044876]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] discusses his [[CaoYinAndKangxi|《曹寅与康熙》]]. The episode presents the book as a historically grounded alternative to Red Chamber riddle culture: Spence's case reconstructs [[CaoYin|曹寅]]'s service to [[KangxiEmperor|康熙]] through family status, offices, financial accounts, cultural networks, and political information.

For the source, Spence is useful because his subject is both institutional and literary. By tracing the actual [[CaoFamilyQing|清代曹家]], he gives [[HongLouMeng|《红楼梦》]] readers a way to think about imperial favor, debt, southern-tour reception, and confiscation without claiming that the novel is only a coded family record.
