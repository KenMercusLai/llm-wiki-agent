# 苏堤 / Su Causeway

The Su Causeway appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as the durable public-works trace of [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s later [[Hangzhou|杭州]] service. The episode says he dredged West Lake and used the silt to build the causeway, linking landscape memory to practical water management.

The source uses Su Causeway to resist a purely lifestyle image of Su Dongpo. The same figure associated with poems, food, and scenery also organizes infrastructure, disaster preparation, and public health, making the causeway a concrete node for [[LocalGovernanceAsCare]] and [[ScholarOfficialPublicDuty]].
