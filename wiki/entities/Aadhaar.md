# Aadhaar

Aadhaar appears in [[strait-and-narrowing-the-iran-deal-crumbles-6a57608bf821a8210984481f]] as [[India]]'s national biometric identity system and as a counterexample to the country's weak government-website pattern. [[LeoMirani]] says Aadhaar worked because technical competence was paired with authority, including high-level political rank for the person leading it.

The source uses Aadhaar to qualify the idea that Indian government technology is simply incapable. The stronger inference is institutional: [[PublicServiceDigitalization]] can work when expertise, mandate, and decision rights align, while ordinary websites can fail when those conditions are missing.
