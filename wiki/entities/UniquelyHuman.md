# 这世界唯一的你 / Uniquely Human

《这世界唯一的你》 / *Uniquely Human* is the book discussed in [[46-zhe-shijie-weiyi-de-ni-qing-xiangxin-na-duo-manman-zhanfang-de-hua-623717720]]. The episode presents it as [[BarryPrizant|Barry Prizant]]'s argument that autistic behaviors should not be treated only as symptoms to suppress, but as meaningful responses to a confusing, overwhelming, or unsafe-feeling world.

The book anchors the source's shift from correction to interpretation. Its cases support [[AutismAsHumanDifference]], [[BehaviorAsCommunication]], [[AutismSafeEnvironmentDesign]], and [[AutismFamilySupport]] by showing that trust, explanation, sensory accommodation, and caregiver observation can create more durable progress than forced exposure or compliance drills.
