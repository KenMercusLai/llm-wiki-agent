# Bob Swan

Bob Swan appears in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]] as the [[EBay]] CFO whom [[TonyXu]] worked for before Stanford. The episode uses this pre-founder period to explain Xu's exposure to senior business operators before he entered [[StanfordUniversity]] and later helped start [[DoorDash]].

The source does not analyze Swan's own career in depth. His wiki role here is contextual: he is part of the management environment around Xu before [[JohnDonahoe]] encouraged Xu to consider Stanford business school.
