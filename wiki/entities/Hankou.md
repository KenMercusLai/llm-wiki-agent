# Hankou / 汉口

Hankou is the central urban case in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]]. The episode describes it as a modernizing treaty-port and commercial city inside the larger [[Wuhan]] setting, with factories, warehouses, railways, electricity, rental housing, foreign concessions, and dense waterfront communities.

The flood made that modernity dangerous. The source highlights chemical and fuel leaks, fires on water, electric-shock deaths, collapsing houses, rising rents, crowded shelters, and market breakdown as examples of [[ModernUrbanDisasterRisk]].

Hankou also carries the episode's religious and political layer. Dragon King temples, anti-superstition campaigns, rituals, and public anger over failed defenses make the city a case for [[FolkReligionDisasterPolitics]] and [[DisasterSensoryHistory]].
