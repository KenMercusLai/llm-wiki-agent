# 《全员名侦探》

《全员名侦探》 is presented in [[158-quanyuan-mingzhentan-tuili-shupian-lai-yibao-929377122]] as the sequel or companion to [[QuanYuanXianYiRen|《全员嫌疑人》]]. The [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode folds one or two of its stories into a broader discussion of 小何, [[WatsonPower|华生力]], and comic [[PuzzleSnackMystery|推理薯片]].

The title captures the premise: many people can become "名侦探" when 小何's presence sharpens their reasoning. The source uses this to explain why the stories can keep changing solvers without abandoning a shared series engine.

## Key Claims
- The book continues the ensemble-detective mechanism established around 小何.
- Its stories are treated as part of the same light, game-like mystery mode as [[QuanYuanXianYiRen|《全员嫌疑人》]].
- The episode emphasizes the comic effect of many ordinary or eccentric people suddenly becoming capable of deduction.
- The sequel supports the episode's broader claim that puzzle pleasure can be episodic, snack-like, and still formally satisfying.
