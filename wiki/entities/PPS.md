# PPS

PPS is one of the early Chinese P2P video platforms discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. Alongside [[PPLive]], it represents the era when technical streaming quality, live-video delivery, and peer-assisted distribution could create a visible product advantage.

The episode places PPS in the broader early-platform shakeout. As [[OnlineVideoCopyrightRegime]] and rights costs intensified, independent P2P platforms faced the same survival problem as other early video companies.
