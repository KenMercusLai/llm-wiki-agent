# Adrienne Weiss

Adrienne Weiss is the brand builder who worked with [[MaxineClark]] on the early [[BuildABear]] concept in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]]. The episode credits her with helping shape the brand, logo, and store design around a child's point of view.

Her role matters because the source treats the Build-A-Bear store as a designed sequence, not just a toy inventory plan. Adrienne's work connects [[ExperientialRetail]], [[CustomerCoCreation]], and [[RetailConceptProtection]] by making the process legible, memorable, and protectable as a retail format.
