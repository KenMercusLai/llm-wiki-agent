# Shira Aviono

Shira Aviono explains the AI-and-recruiting segment in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]]. She contrasts her own low-tech application to [[TheIntelligence]] with a job market where generative AI tools let candidates, bots, and paid services submit applications at scale.

Her segment turns hiring into an [[AIHiringArmsRace]]. Recruiters may use AI screening, stricter application rules, psychometric tests, visual puzzles, or outbound sourcing, while fake profiles and remote-work infiltration risk turn the problem into [[CandidateIdentityFraud]] as well as administrative overload.
