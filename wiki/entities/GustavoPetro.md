# Gustavo Petro

Gustavo Petro appears in [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]] as the Colombian leader whose "Total Peace" strategy is punished by voters. The episode says crime, gang power, cocaine production, and armed-group territorial control worsened, making security the dominant election issue.

The source uses Petro mostly as the foil for [[AbelardoDeLaEspriella]] and for a broader [[SecurityBacklashPolitics]] pattern. The critique is not only ideological: when citizens see insecurity growing, a softer negotiation-first posture can lose legitimacy even if hardline replacements carry their own rule-of-law risks.
