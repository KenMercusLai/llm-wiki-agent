# Kuaishou Lite

Kuaishou Lite is the [[Kuaishou]] product case in [[faquan-liebian-jisuban-ruhe-yong-hongbao-sheji-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-8-ji-lijpz1zgy9h2k4qd0c45ixwenu-0]] that turned Lite-app incentives into a mass short-video growth strategy. The episode says Kuaishou Lite initially tolerated more low-quality or reward-seeking users, used easy early cashout perception to create market attention, and helped Kuaishou push toward a larger DAU target.

The source does not treat Kuaishou Lite as only "spending money". It argues that the model worked because short video had enough content consumption, ad monetization, and algorithmic retention behind the rewards. That makes Kuaishou Lite a central example for [[LiteAppGrowth]]: the reward screen can pull users through the first sessions, but product and monetization decide whether the subsidy converts into durable usage.
