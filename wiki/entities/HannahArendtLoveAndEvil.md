# 《汉娜·阿伦特：爱与恶》 / Hannah Arendt: Love and Evil

[[103-ta-shi-cichuan-yewu-de-guang-ta-shi-hanna-alunte-737939789]] presents 《汉娜·阿伦特：爱与恶》 as the main book route into [[HannahArendt|Hannah Arendt / 汉娜·阿伦特]]. The episode says the book is useful because it does not isolate Arendt's ideas from love, friendship, fear, exile, and controversy.

In the source's reading, the biography lets listeners move from Arendt's childhood, philosophical education, and relationship with [[MartinHeidegger|Martin Heidegger]] to her mentorship by [[KarlJaspers|Karl Jaspers]], her life with [[HeinrichBlucher|Heinrich Blücher]], and the political experiences behind [[EichmannInJerusalem|《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》]]. It becomes a bridge between life story and concepts such as [[BanalityOfEvil]], [[StatelessExile]], and [[PoliticalTruthTelling]].
