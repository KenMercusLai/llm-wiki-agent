# 儋州 / Danzhou

Danzhou is the farthest exile stage in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode says [[SuShi|苏轼]] was sent there in old age under restrictions such as not being allowed to sign official documents, marking a severe marginalization from [[NorthernSong|北宋]] political life.

The source still treats Danzhou as part of Su Shi's maturing arc rather than merely decline. Alongside the episode's references to southern food and schools, Danzhou extends [[EarnedOptimism]] and [[ScholarOfficialPublicDuty]] into a place where formal authority is sharply limited but teaching, presence, and moral steadiness still matter.
