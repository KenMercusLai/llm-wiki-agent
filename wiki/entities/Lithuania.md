# Lithuania

Lithuania appears in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]] as the Baltic state where [[AntonLaGuardia]] visits the Pabrade training ground near Vilnius. The segment uses Lithuania to show both confidence and nervousness: [[Germany]] is expanding its permanent military commitment, while nearby American forces had recently left.

In the source, Lithuania turns [[NATOAllianceCredibility]] into a visible force-posture question. If [[Russia]] tests the alliance with limited or ambiguous pressure, the Baltic front is where [[EuropeanDefenseAutonomy]], German reassurance, and American uncertainty all become practical rather than rhetorical.
