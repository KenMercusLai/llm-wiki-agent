# Ultra Wave

Ultra Wave is the early hair-straightening product in [[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]] that led into [[JohnsonProducts]]. [[OrvilleNelson]] had a straightener that did not work reliably; [[GeorgeEJohnson]] observed the barbershop problem, experimented for months, and created a thicker, more stable version.

Ultra Wave matters because it shows the technical and market base from which Johnson Products grew. The product served an era when straightened hair was tied to workplace respectability and assimilation pressure, while the later [[AfroSheen]] story shows how the company adapted when natural hair became politically and culturally salient.
