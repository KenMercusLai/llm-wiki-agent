# Gil Luria

Gil Luria appears in [[tech-20260312-0312-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260312-0312-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[DADavidson|D.A. Davidson]] head of technology research interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] about [[Bitcoin]] during geopolitical crisis. He argues that Bitcoin's volatility and association with risky or illicit activity make it less trusted as an immediate safe haven than gold, even though its fixed supply, global liquidity, and independence from government money can still matter during inflation, currency depreciation, or capital flight.

His comments connect Bitcoin's crisis role to two different narratives: [[DigitalGold]], where scarcity and capped issuance support an inflation-hedge argument, and [[CrossBorderCryptoCapitalFlight]], where Bitcoin's portability may be more important than price stability for people trying to move wealth out of unstable or restricted markets.
