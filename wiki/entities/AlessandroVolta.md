# Alessandro Volta / 亚历山德罗·伏打

Alessandro Volta appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the rival to [[LuigiGalvani|Luigi Galvani]] in the [[AnimalElectricityDebate|animal electricity debate]]. The source says Volta rejected the claim that the frog's body itself was the source of electricity and argued instead for electricity produced by contact between metals.

Volta's invention of the voltaic pile gives the episode a material bridge from dispute to apparatus. In the wiki, he helps keep [[GalvanicReanimationImaginary]] evidence-bounded: the body movements were real observations, but their interpretation as a life force remained contested.
