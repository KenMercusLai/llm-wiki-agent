# 黄州 / Huangzhou

Huangzhou is the exile location that gives [[SuShi|苏轼]] his "Dongpo" identity in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. After the [[WutaiPoetryCase|乌台诗案]], Su Shi arrives with low pay, monitoring, and little official scope, then farms the eastern slope and becomes "东坡居士".

The episode treats Huangzhou as the point where loss of status becomes literary and spiritual maturity. [[DongpoRou|东坡肉]] shows appetite and life maintenance under poverty, while [[RedCliffFu|《赤壁赋》]] and [[NianNuJiaoChibiHuaiGu|《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》]] show a deeper transformation of historical imagination, mortality, and scale.
