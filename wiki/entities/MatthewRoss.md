# Matthew Ross

Matthew Ross appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the economist whose research helps the episode move from classroom training to [[FieldTrainingOfficerEffects]]. The source says Ross used [[DallasPoliceDepartment]] data and found that recruits paired with more aggressive field training officers were more likely to use force for at least three years.

Ross is important because he makes training culture measurable. In the episode, his work supports the claim that who trains recruits on the job can have a durable effect on behavior, which complicates a narrow debate over formal academy hours.
