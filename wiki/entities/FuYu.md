# 傅宇 / Fu Yu

傅宇 is the [[QizhulouYanBinke|起朱楼宴宾客]]-side speaker in [[vol-102-aoguo-jiuye-bingheqi-de-riben-nianqingren-qu-nali-xunzhao-xingfugan-chuantai-qingdao-kuaima-lmpluij7rcfq49cxcyb65x3f7zfn]]. In this episode he brings a China-side perspective on youth surveys, mortgage prepayment, [[HouseholdBalanceSheetRepair]], educational pressure, and the loss of confidence in individual futures.

His recurring contribution is to connect personal emotions to structural conditions. He treats small happiness, social trust, and strategic pauses as responses to changed economic expectations rather than as isolated mindset issues.
