# Corey Staley

Corey Staley is the [[Indeed]] senior economist interviewed in [[tech-20251217-1217-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251217-1217-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, he uses Indeed job-posting data to explain why U.S. tech hiring has stabilized after a sharp post-2022 decline but remains well below its February 2020 baseline.

Staley's role in the wiki is to ground [[TechHiringStabilization]] in a concrete posting index rather than stock-market or AI-hype signals. He says the [[TechJobPostingIndex]] is at 67.2, while AI and machine-learning roles are doing better than the broader technology category. He also adds a caution around causality: [[SoftwareDeveloperHiringPullback]] may reflect AI exposure, but it also reflects pandemic-era overhiring and a [[LowFireLaborMarket]].
