# YouGov

YouGov appears in [[would-you-trust-an-economist-with-your-economy]] through a 2017 British public-trust survey cited by the episode. The source says respondents trusted nurses far more than economists, and that trust in weather forecasters was about twice as high as trust in economists.

The episode uses the YouGov result as evidence that economists' trust problem is not only a loud political-media story. It is measurable public-opinion context for [[EconomistTrustCrisis]], and it helps explain why simply repeating aggregate numbers may not persuade skeptical audiences.
