# 番茄小说 / Fanqie Novel

Fanqie Novel appears in [[quanmian-yazhi-buliu-kongdang-zijie-tiaodong-ruhe-zuo-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-7-ji-lqszvmur6jv8b9xt8rjgmwfdqtec]] as a ByteDance content product that grew out of the Toutiao novel-channel context and later became part of ByteDance's free-content expansion. The episode uses it to show where [[ByteDanceGrowthSystem]] transfers well: free or ad-supported content with large supply, recommendation distribution, paid acquisition, and measurable retention.

The source also connects Fanqie to [[Hongguo]] through creative and growth lineage. [[XuHongliang]] says the novel-advertising and material teams around Fanqie helped form part of the later short-drama growth context.

[[XiaoGuoGege]] says [[AnhuiXiaoMujiang]] began with a Fanqie Novel story licensed through a copyright center, then entered a Fanqie/Hongguo/Douyin distribution and revenue-share chain. The same episode says that adaptation channel later closed, making the case difficult to copy directly.
