# 《花粉症与人类》 / 花粉症と人類

《花粉症与人类》 is the book frame for [[97-huafen-zheng-yu-renlei-cong-niandeteren-aqiu-dao-kongqi-jinghuaqi-728113059]]. The episode presents it as a compact natural-history and medical-history account by [[KoshioKaihei|小塩海平 / 小盐海平]], moving from the biological meaning of pollen to the social and environmental history of hay fever.

The book's value in the wiki is its method: a small bodily problem becomes a route through [[PollenAsHistoricalObject]], [[PalynologyAsEvidence]], [[HayFeverEnvironmentalHistory]], [[AllergyAsImmuneResponse]], [[RagweedEcologicalFeedback]], and [[AirPurifier|air purification]].
