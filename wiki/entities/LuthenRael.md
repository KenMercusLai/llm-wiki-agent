# Luthen Rael

Luthen Rael is the hidden rebel organizer discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. In [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]], the source describes him as operating from an antique-shop cover while coordinating risky action against [[GalacticEmpire]] and maintaining contact with figures such as [[MonMothma]] and [[SawGerrera]].

The episode treats Luthen as morally serious because he knows the cost of what he does. His willingness to let another rebel group be sacrificed to preserve an intelligence source becomes the source's central case for [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
