# Flora

Flora is the fiber company discussed by [[JeniBritton]] in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. The episode describes Flora as pulling fiber from produce trimmings such as apple cores, watermelon rinds, melon rinds, and mango skins, then turning those ingredients into bars with about 13 or 14 grams of fiber.

The source connects Flora to microbiome health and to Britton's broader food-company thesis: even health-oriented products need to make customers feel a practical benefit, not only understand a nutritional claim.
