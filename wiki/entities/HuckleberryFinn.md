# Adventures of Huckleberry Finn / 《哈克贝利·费恩历险记》

Adventures of Huckleberry Finn / 《哈克贝利·费恩历险记》 appears in [[135-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-san-dan-823746670]] as [[MarkTwain|Mark Twain / 马克·吐温]]'s most controversy-loaded work in the episode. The hosts say the book was attacked as vulgar and racially problematic, was banned in 1885, and then became more commercially visible through the controversy.

The source does not settle the book's racial-language debate. Its wiki role is to show how [[ClassicReadingComplexity]] works when a canonical text is both historically important and ethically uncomfortable. The episode treats banning, sales, public outrage, and [[LouisaMayAlcott|Louisa May Alcott / 奥尔科特]]'s criticism as part of the work's reception history rather than as a replacement for reading.
