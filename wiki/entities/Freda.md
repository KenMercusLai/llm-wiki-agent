# Freda / Friday

Freda, also referred to as Friday in the source, is the Bay Area investor interviewed in [[141-freda-de-touzi-zhaji-di-2-ji-tokenmaxxing-ba-dianji-sai-jin-zhengqiji-jielisai-bian-lanqiusai-gudu-ren-de-lianjie-lmeczs2jtkze79rkpvm-rc5yw22m]]. The episode presents her as a partner at [[AltimeterCapital]] and uses her public/private-market vantage point to connect frontier model companies, software valuation, cloud and chip capex, AI startup categories, and personal anxiety in Silicon Valley.

Her main contribution is a discipline for looking past surface AI excitement. She argues that token growth, model-company revenue, software disruption, hyperscaler capex, and AI startup opportunity must be interpreted through task outcomes, usage economics, organization redesign, and market risk rather than through a single model ranking or revenue headline.
