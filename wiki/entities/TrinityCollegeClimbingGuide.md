# 《三一学院攀爬指南》 / Trinity College Climbing Guide

《三一学院攀爬指南》 is the earlier Cambridge climbing guide named in [[89-jianqiao-yepanzhe-rang-women-zhuangyan-de-shangfang-jiewa-710382629]]. The episode associates it with [[GeoffreyWinthropYoung]] and uses it to show that [[TheNightClimbersOfCambridge|《剑桥夜攀者》]] did not appear from nowhere; it emerged from an existing [[UniversityOfCambridge|Cambridge]] tradition of illicit climbing knowledge.

The guide matters in the source because it turns [[CampusNightClimbing]] into a transmissible genre: routes, warnings, jokes, and etiquette can circulate even when the activity cannot become an official student society. That underground-document form also explains why later writing can be literary and self-mythologizing without becoming safe or sanctioned.
