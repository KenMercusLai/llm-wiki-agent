# Taco Bell

Taco Bell is the fast-food company involved in the Chihuahua advertising dispute described by [[TomRinks]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source says Tom and [[JoeShields]] worked with Taco Bell for about 18 months on a Chihuahua-based campaign before Taco Bell released its own Chihuahua ads.

In the episode's account, the later lawsuit was about implied contract, recognition, and fair treatment as much as money. Tom says the campaign also damaged [[PsychoChihuahua]] sales because consumers now wanted the Taco Bell Chihuahua instead.
