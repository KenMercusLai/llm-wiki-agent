# JCET / 长电科技

JCET, also referred to as 长电科技, appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] as the packaging factory [[ZhangCongzhi|张从志]] visited while reporting on the semiconductor industry. The source uses the visit to make packaging and testing concrete: even when precision is lower than wafer fabrication, humidity, dust, equipment access, and process control still matter.

The episode also places JCET inside China's relatively stronger semiconductor segment. Domestic packaging firms are presented as having narrowed the gap faster than advanced lithography or leading-edge wafer manufacturing, partly because earlier overseas acquisitions and industry funding helped transfer and absorb [[AdvancedPackaging|advanced packaging]] know-how.
