# Miami

Miami appears in [[tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the new headquarters destination announced by [[Palantir]]. [[AnitaRamaswamy]] frames the move as partly about [[Florida]]'s business environment and partly about what Miami represents politically and culturally compared with Silicon Valley.

The city page is useful as a place anchor for [[SouthFloridaTechMigration]], where headquarters announcements, office expansion, tax conditions, regulation, and proximity to political networks can matter even before a company moves many employees.
