# Andy Bechtolsheim

Andy Bechtolsheim appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as one of [[Google]]'s earliest angel investors. [[RonConway]] repeats the story that Bechtolsheim wrote a $100,000 check before Google had a bank account, causing the check to sit unused for a time.

The episode uses Bechtolsheim as part of the early credibility stack around [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]]. Alongside [[DavidCheriton]], [[RamShriram]], and [[JeffBezos]], his check helped move Google from Stanford project to venture-backed company.
