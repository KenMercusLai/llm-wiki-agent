# Mia Liponi

Mia Liponi appears in [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the person running the [[LuganoPlanB|Plan B]] hub in [[Lugano]]. Her role is to make the city's Bitcoin experiment concrete: she says she tried living on [[Bitcoin]] alone for 11 days and found it possible, but not frictionless.

Liponi's account grounds [[CryptoPaymentPracticalityGap]]. The episode says groceries, deliveries, many medical places, parking fines, taxes, and other municipal services can be paid for with Bitcoin, while public transportation, fuel, bills, dentists, and some medical services remain difficult. That makes her testimony more useful as an adoption boundary than as a broad claim that Bitcoin has become ordinary money everywhere.
