# Yin Qi

Yin Qi is the AI entrepreneur interviewed in [[131-yin-qi-churen-jieyue-xingchen-dongshizhang-de-fangtan]]. The source presents him as chair of [[QianliTechnology]] and incoming chair of [[StepFun]], after a previous AI 1.0 period at [[Megvii]]. His role in the episode is both operator and retrospective narrator: he explains why he moved from earlier computer-vision commercialization into foundation models, cars, and terminals.

## Key Views
- [[ChatGPT]] moved him from seeing the change as language-specific to seeing it as a deeper technology shift.
- AGI needs to enter the physical world; language and digital tasks are not enough for the full intelligence problem.
- [[StepFun]] should keep building frontier foundation models, but needs [[AIPlusTerminals]] to create commercial pull and a durable data/product loop.
- Cars are the first terminal path through [[QianliTechnology]], with cabins, intelligent driving, and Robotaxi treated as places where digital and physical data can begin to merge.
- His [[Megvii]] retrospective emphasizes strategic focus, product-market fit, and avoiding "hammer looking for nail" behavior.
- [[AIOrganizationDesign]] matters because high-IQ talent can still fail without mission, collaboration, and disciplined execution.
