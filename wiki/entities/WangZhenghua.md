# Wang Zhenghua / 王正华

Wang Zhenghua is the [[SpringAirlines]] founder used as the main contrast to [[LanShili]] in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]]. The episode frames Wang's airline route as steadier, slower, thriftier, and more operationally disciplined than East Star's fast expansion.

Wang's role in the source is comparative rather than biographical. By placing [[SpringAirlines]] beside [[EastStarAirlines]], the episode argues that the first private-airline window did not mechanically doom all entrants; founder style, cost discipline, route strategy, and financing behavior shaped who survived.
