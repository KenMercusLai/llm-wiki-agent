# Elie Wiesel

Elie Wiesel is one of the writers used in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]] to illustrate [[GeorgesBeauchard]]'s literary judgment. The source describes Wiesel as a Romanian Jewish journalist whose Auschwitz memoir was rejected by many publishers before Beauchard sold the rights to *Night*.

The episode emphasizes the gap between early publisher rejection and later moral and literary importance: *Night* began with a small slow-selling print run, while Wiesel later won the Nobel Peace Prize.
