# 小满

小满 is the nonfiction writer and [[Mianji]] guest in [[da-chang-xiao-min-women-bixu-kezhi-dui-xitong-yu-shangan-de-qidai-lps-6oqp9ljyfw6wpfkutcks6dgf]]. The episode centers on her book [[DaChangXiaomin]], her movement from a Qinling village to Shenzhen and a large internet company, her layoff, and her later attempt to live from writing while raising a child with family support.

Her case is useful for the wiki because it does not treat leaving a large company as simple liberation. She says writing gives her more ownership, name-level attachment, and lightness than corporate work, but she still faces rent, childcare, royalties, publicity pressure, and future income uncertainty. That makes her a concrete case for [[CareerShoreMyth]], [[SelfDirectedWork]], [[LayoffBuffer]], [[NonfictionPublicness]], and [[FamilyLaborBoundaries]].
