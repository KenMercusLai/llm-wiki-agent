# Desmond Shum

Desmond Shum enters the wiki through [[building-things-and-breaking-things-in-china-summer-school-world-tour]] as the property developer whose 2003 Beijing airport logistics project grounds the episode's real-estate boom case. The source presents him and [[WhitneyDuan]] as using political access, local-government dealmaking, consultants, wining and dining, and expensive official-facing amenities to make an infrastructure-adjacent land project profitable.

The episode uses Shum's story to make [[ChinaRealEstateDebtCycle]] more concrete before turning to [[Evergrande]]. His case shows the earlier phase of the boom, when land, monopoly expectations, official relationships, and rapid growth could convert weak operating knowledge into very large private gains.
