# Windsurf

Windsurf is an AI coding tool discussed in [[ep108-vibe-coding-da-dizhen-cursor-dingjia-zhengyi-windsurf-shougou-fengbo-moxing-changshang-qin-erzi-men-you-jiang-ruhe-jinchang-lqn-icq1xqgk7xxxxzrpunj4fan]]. The hosts use it as a market-structure case: OpenAI was rumored to be negotiating an acquisition, [[GoogleDeepMind]] later hired or absorbed key people, and [[Cognition]] then acquired the remaining Windsurf assets or business.

The source treats Windsurf less as a product deep dive and more as a warning about [[ModelProviderToolCompetition]]. AI coding startups can accumulate users and workflow share, but their strategic position may remain fragile when model providers own the core model capability, pricing leverage, talent market, and official tool roadmap.
