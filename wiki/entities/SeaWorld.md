# SeaWorld

SeaWorld appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as a reference point for the rise and decline of whale and dolphin entertainment. The episode connects shows such as Shamu-era performances to later public backlash after incidents, documentaries, political pressure, and animal-rights campaigns.

The source says SeaWorld is phasing out performances after falling visitor numbers and pressure from campaigners and politicians. In the wiki, SeaWorld functions as the commercial-captivity contrast to [[CaptiveWhaleSanctuary]] and the [[LittleWhiteAndLittleGrey]] experiment.
