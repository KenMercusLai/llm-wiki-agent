# Syril Karn

Syril Karn is the zealous low-level order-seeker discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode contrasts him with [[CassianAndor]]: Cassian begins as a self-protective marginal figure, while Syril is a striving functionary who wants to uphold imperial order even when his superior prefers bureaucratic containment.

In the source, Syril's importance is not incompetence but over-commitment. His effort to perform duty makes the [[GalacticEmpire]] more visible and escalatory, turning him into the central character case for [[InstitutionalOvercompliance]].
