# Freit Barefoot

Freit Barefoot is the family-run barefoot shoe brand discussed in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. The episode describes it as a UK-based brand selling hiking boots, sandals, trail running shoes, kids' shoes, and gym shoes, with about $2 million in revenue across the UK, U.S., and EU.

The Advice Line question is how to prioritize U.S. growth levers such as PR, B2B expansion, digital influencers, and paid ads. [[ShaziVisram]] and [[GuyRaz]] advise Freit to explain the category more clearly, use science and children's foot-development arguments carefully, make PR and customer proof visible, and treat repeat buyers and user-generated content as a growth machine rather than separate anecdotes.
