# Quora

Quora appears in [[tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3-tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3]] as the company where [[AlexandrWang]] worked during his gap year before MIT. Wang says he built a prototype that Quora leadership liked, but [[AdamDAngelo]] encouraged him to attend at least some college rather than stay immediately.

In this source, Quora matters less as a product case than as an early Silicon Valley apprenticeship. It gave Wang startup exposure before [[YCombinator]] and before he converted his AI-data interest into [[ScaleAI]].
