# Invisible Technologies

Invisible Technologies is the workflow-customization company discussed by [[Oliver]] in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]]. The source presents Invisible as a company that makes AI "invisible" by redesigning workflows around the work to be done: it decomposes processes, decides which steps should be deterministic, which should use AI, and which require human review, then builds AI sales assistants, due-diligence support, fund-operations automation, and agent-coordination systems for enterprise and asset-management clients.
