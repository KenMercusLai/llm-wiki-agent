# Sophie Pedder

Sophie Pedder is the [[TheIntelligence]] participant in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] who explains [[MarineLePen]]'s renewed presidential campaign and the state of the 2027 [[France]] race. The episode uses her analysis to connect the appeal ruling, polling, candidate field, and [[NationalRally]]'s internal succession tension.

Her contribution is interpretive rather than biographical in the wiki: she frames Le Pen's candidacy as the test of a long [[FarRightNormalization]] project and notes that first-round strength does not settle the second-round question in a [[TwoRoundPresidentialElection]].
