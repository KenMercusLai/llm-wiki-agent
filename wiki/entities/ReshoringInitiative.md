# Reshoring Initiative

The Reshoring Initiative appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through [[HarryMoser]]. In the episode, it stands for the argument that some goods production should be brought or kept domestically because pandemics, wars, supply shocks, and defense needs can make foreign dependence costly.

The page connects the organization to the wiki's broader manufacturing-policy branch while keeping the episode's later cautions visible. Domestic factories may support [[SupplyChainSovereignty]], but the episode separately asks whether they create attractive jobs, skill ladders, and local growth.
