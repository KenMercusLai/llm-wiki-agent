# Colombia

Colombia is the country case in [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]], where [[TheIntelligence]] presents [[AbelardoDeLaEspriella]]'s narrow presidential runoff victory as a reaction against worsening security under [[GustavoPetro]].

The source frames Colombia through [[SecurityBacklashPolitics]]. Voters punish Petro's "Total Peace" strategy because crime, gang control, cocaine production, and armed-group territorial power are described as worsening. The episode says de la Espriella can promise a tougher line, but legally protected spending, weak congressional support, and rule-of-law risks limit what a hardline turn can safely accomplish.
