# 春雨医生

Chunyu Doctor is the light-consultation company founded by [[ZhangRui]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode says its early product combined self-diagnosis and online questions, initially focusing on categories such as gynecology and pediatrics where many user concerns did not necessarily require an immediate hospital visit.

The source uses Chunyu Doctor to show both the real value and the commercial difficulty of [[OnlineMedicalConsultation]]. Free consultations and doctor subsidies could acquire users, but paid willingness was limited because the higher-value medical system still sat in testing, prescriptions, procedures, hospitalization, and offline services.

## Key Points
- Chunyu Doctor made "light consultation" a recognizable mobile-healthcare category.
- Its early membership and response-time promises show an attempt to productize low-acuity doctor access.
- The company's offline clinic plan illustrates the temptation to capture downstream medical value when online consultation alone is too thin.
- [[HealthcareO2O]] and nonstandard medical service quality made aggressive offline expansion difficult.

## Connections
- [[ZhangRui]] — founder.

- [[InternetHealthcare]] — broader industry branch where light consultation had demand but weak standalone economics.
