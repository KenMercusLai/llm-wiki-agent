# Charlton County

Charlton County appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the county context for the large ICE detention facility in [[FolkstonGeorgia|Folkston, Georgia]]. The source says the county and city receive about $1 million connected to the facility while [[GEOGroup]] holds a $96 million federal contract.

The county's role is to make [[DetentionAsLocalEconomicDevelopment]] concrete. Through [[GlennHall]]'s comments, the episode presents detention revenue as a response to public-service needs, while also acknowledging that a local economy built around detention is unstable and morally uncomfortable.
