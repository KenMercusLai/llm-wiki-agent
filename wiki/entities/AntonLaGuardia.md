# Anton LaGuardia

Anton LaGuardia is the [[TheIntelligence]] diplomatic editor explaining the NATO summit segment in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]]. He treats the summit as a managed public ritual whose narrow agenda hides larger anxieties about [[DonaldTrump]], [[Russia]], American commitment, and whether European allies can defend themselves without the United States.

His reporting from [[Lithuania]] makes the abstract alliance question concrete. [[Germany]]'s brigade deployment shows new European seriousness, while the departure of American forces from nearby positions makes [[EuropeanDefenseAutonomy]] and [[NATOAllianceCredibility]] feel more urgent and less settled.
