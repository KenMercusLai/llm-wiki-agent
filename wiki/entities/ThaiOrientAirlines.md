# Thai Orient Airlines / 泰国东方航空

Thai Orient Airlines is the overseas airline acquisition target in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] that became tied to [[LanShili]]'s contract-fraud case after the East Star era. The episode says Lan first used a smaller Thai aviation shell and then sought a larger partner-funded acquisition around Thai Orient.

The company matters less as an operating airline than as a legal and financing node. The cooperation with [[MaiquerGroup]] ended in accusations that Lan failed to contribute promised capital and moved shared equity into controlled companies. Lan was later acquitted criminally, but the episode uses the case to show how aviation ambition, equity control, and disputed partnership execution kept recurring after [[EastStarAirlines]].
