# Walter Benjamin / 本雅明

Walter Benjamin / 本雅明 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] through the office copier scene. He asks [[Plato|柏拉图]] to copy a contract, turning the idea of mechanical reproduction into literal photocopying.

The joke becomes part of [[PhilosophyWorkplaceSatire]] because a major theoretical problem is translated into a fragile office machine. When the copier jams, [[FriedrichNietzsche|尼采]] and [[JacquesDerrida|德里达]] enter the same equipment gag through hammering, repair, and deconstruction.

[[103-ta-shi-cichuan-yewu-de-guang-ta-shi-hanna-alunte-737939789]] adds Benjamin's real exile-world context through [[HannahArendt|Hannah Arendt]] and [[HeinrichBlucher|Heinrich Blücher]]. The episode places him in Paris as war approaches, describes his despair and suicide at the Spanish border, and says Arendt and Blücher carried his manuscript onward. That turns Benjamin from a workplace-satire cameo into part of the wiki's [[StatelessExile]] and [[PoliticalTruthTelling]] branch.
