# Front

Front appears in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]] as an early [[Vanta]] customer-discovery case. [[ChristinaCacioppo]] says she adapted the first [[SOC2Audit]] gap-assessment spreadsheet from [[Segment]] for Front by replacing Segment-specific references and testing whether the artifact generalized.

In the source, Front helps prove that Vanta's compliance opportunity was not only one company's pain. Reusing the spreadsheet supported Christina's belief that small startups shared enough security and compliance best practices for a product to help them get far before highly custom enterprise governance work.
