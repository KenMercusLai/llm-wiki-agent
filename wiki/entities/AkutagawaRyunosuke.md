# 芥川龙之介 / Ryunosuke Akutagawa

芥川龙之介 / Ryunosuke Akutagawa appears in [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]] through his adaptation of [[DuZiChun|《杜子春》]]. The episode uses him as a comparison point rather than a full author study.

The source says Akutagawa changes the ending so the test turns on seeing suffering parents rather than seeing one's child killed. In the episode's reading, the altered ending makes [[TranscendenceAgainstHumanFeeling]] more explicit: if becoming immortal means accepting the destruction of basic human bonds, then failing the test can be ethically preferable.
