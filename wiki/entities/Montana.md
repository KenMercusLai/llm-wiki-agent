# Montana

Montana appears in [[tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the state [[JeremyScott]] cites for passing a law that closes the [[DataBrokerLoophole]]. The episode says the Montana bill requires a warrant where government agencies might otherwise purchase data directly from brokers.

The Montana example turns [[FourthAmendmentDigitalPrivacy]] from a constitutional principle into a state-level implementation path: close the purchase route, require judicial process, and prevent data buying from becoming a workaround for warrant rules.
