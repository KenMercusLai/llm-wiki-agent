# Okhotsk / 鄂霍次克

Okhotsk is the Pacific-side port and shipbuilding bottleneck in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. The episode presents [[VitusBering]]'s first expedition as needing to reach Okhotsk before it could become a maritime project at all, because ships, repairs, and crossing to [[Kamchatka]] depended on what could be built or fixed there.

In the second-expedition arc, the emptiness of promised houses, warehouses, roads, wharves, and vessels at Okhotsk becomes evidence of imperial overreach. It shows why [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]] was not background administration but one of the main determinants of success.
