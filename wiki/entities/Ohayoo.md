# Ohayoo

Ohayoo appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as ByteDance's light-game publishing case. The hosts say Ohayoo once had multiple products in the App Store free-chart top ten, showing that ByteDance's recommendation, traffic-buying, data, and distribution abilities could work well in light-game publishing.

The strategic limit is that this did not prove ByteDance could make heavy games. Ohayoo is therefore the positive half of the source's game argument: ByteDance could distribute and iterate light entertainment, while [[ChaoxiGuangnian]] exposed the harder creative-production layer.
