# Laura Nolan

Laura Nolan appears in [[inside-a-book-auction]] as one of the literary agents helping [[PlanetMoney]] turn a book possibility into a sellable publishing project. Alongside [[JaneVonMehren|Jane von Mehren]], she explains the agent role: shaping the idea, preparing publishers, arranging meetings, running the auction, and taking a commission from the resulting deal.

In the source, Nolan's work is part of [[LiteraryAgentMarketMaking]]. She and Jane do not simply wait for publishers to discover the project; they seed interest through lunches, drinks, meetings, publisher selection, and auction design.

## Key Claims
- Nolan treated Planet Money as a proven audience platform, not only as a source of book ideas.
- She helped broaden the concept from narrow pamphlets into a field guide to the global economy.
- She helped run the two-round email auction that produced 16 first-round bids.
