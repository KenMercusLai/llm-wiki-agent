# Responna

Responna is the company co-founded by [[FarzadRashidi]] and discussed in [[he-demoted-his-saas-to-sell-a-service-and-4xd-revenue-in-12-months]]. It began as an internal [[Visme]] outreach tool for finding websites, backlinks, collaborations, and mentions, then became a standalone SaaS product in 2019.

In the episode, Responna is now framed less as a self-serve outreach tool and more as a done-for-you [[AIVisibilityService]]. The company helps brands appear in AI-generated answers by getting them mentioned in third-party publisher pages that systems such as [[ChatGPT]], Claude, Google AI Overviews, and [[Gemini]] may cite.

## Key Claims
- The SaaS tool reached a few hundred thousand dollars in ARR but plateaued because customers lacked execution capacity.
- A customer conversation exposed a pricing mismatch: the buyer negotiated software fees but was willing to pay much more for delivered outcomes.
- Responna's service began manually, then became more software-backed through client ordering, publisher relationships, payment, and delivery systems.
- The company reports building roughly 2,500 brand mentions in one month and using repeat volume to negotiate better publisher access.
- Its defensibility is not only product UI; it includes [[PublisherRelationshipMoat]], workflow software, AI-assisted operations, and demand aggregation.
