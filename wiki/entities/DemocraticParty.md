# Democratic Party

The Democratic Party appears in [[sp-05-liuqing-linyao-qiangsha-yibao-jutou-an-beihou-de-sikao-xia-fenlie-de-meiguo-hui-you-xianzheng-weiji-ma-790241978]] as both the more plausible institutional channel for U.S. health-care reform and a party with communication and media-infrastructure problems. [[LinYao|林垚]] says the [[UnitedHealthcare]] killing might have had more policy effect if Democrats controlled the presidency and Congress, but even then Senate rules would make large reform difficult.

The source also criticizes Democratic campaign rhetoric around the economy and inflation. In Lin's account, voters who experience price pressure may hear Democratic messaging as denial or evasion unless the party first acknowledges the problem and then explains what it has done and what it will do next.
