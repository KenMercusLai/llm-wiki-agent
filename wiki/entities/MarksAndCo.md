# Marks & Co. / 马克斯与科恩书店

Marks & Co. / 马克斯与科恩书店 is the London old-book shop at 84 Charing Cross Road in [[EightyFourCharingCrossRoad|《查令十字街84号》 / 84 Charing Cross Road]]. In [[134-chaling-shizi-jie-84-hao-zaici-yuanliang-shijie-de-liyou-821075226]], the shop begins as a vendor found through an advertisement, then becomes a human community around [[HeleneHanff|海莲·汉芙]]'s letters.

The episode emphasizes the shop's [[OldBookMateriality]] work. It searches for old editions, handles scarcity, knows book condition, and routes obscure requests through a bookseller's patient attention rather than a simple catalog lookup. That makes the shop an early case of [[BookstoreSerendipity]]: a reader meets not just inventory, but taste, memory, and care.

The shop also becomes a memory site. [[FrankDoel|Frank Doel]] dies before Helene visits, and by the time she later reaches London the shop has closed; the source notes that the address eventually becomes a commemorated place rather than the living room of correspondence it once was.
