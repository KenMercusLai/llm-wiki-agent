# Mahesh Balakrishnan

Mahesh Balakrishnan appears in [[the-latest-tv-innovations-have-their-critics]] as VP of Consumer Technology at [[Dolby]]. He argues that motion systems can be made more intelligent, including by using metadata and automation to apply smoothing selectively.

His contribution to the wiki is the solution-side frame: instead of choosing between raw [[TVMotionStutter]] and global [[MotionSmoothing]], he describes [[SelectiveMotionSmoothing]] as a way to preserve creator intent while reducing motion problems in specific shots or scenes.
