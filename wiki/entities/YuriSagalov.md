# Yuri Sagalov

Yuri Sagalov is the interview subject in [[tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final-tsr-s3-yurisagalov-v4final]], where [[JessicaLivingston]] and [[CarolynLevy]] trace his path from graduate-student founder to [[YCombinator]] partner, angel investor, and founder of [[WayfinderVentures]]. His first startup, [[AeroFS]], entered YC in Summer 2010 as an enterprise file-sync company built around a technically ambitious peer-to-peer system.

The episode presents Sagalov as a technical founder who later turned the AeroFS experience into investor judgment. He says AeroFS had real enterprise demand but also a [[TechnicalAmbitionCustomerMismatch]]: customers wanted a practical [[EnterpriseFileSync]] product, while the team was pulled toward a difficult distributed-systems challenge. His later investing work emphasizes [[FounderLedSales]], [[InvestorReferenceChecking]], founder determination, and the habit of talking to users.
