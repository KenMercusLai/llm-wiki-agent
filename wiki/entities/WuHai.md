# Wu Hai

Wu Hai is an early hotel-booking operator in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. Through [[Shangzhixing]], the source says he brought membership-card distribution, call-center discipline, hotel-pricing systems, and booking operations into [[Ctrip]].

The episode uses Wu's later equity dispute with Ctrip as a practical founder lesson: early contribution, promised shares, and acquisition compensation need clear written agreements. The source also treats his later hotel and entertainment ventures as evidence that he remained an operating entrepreneur after leaving Ctrip.
