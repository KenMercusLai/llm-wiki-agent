# Rene Carmille / 勒内·卡米耶

Rene Carmille / 勒内·卡米耶 is the French military and statistical official presented in [[133-ibm-yu-nacui-weishenme-putongren-bu-ying-zanmei-jizeizhuyi-815684110]] as the episode's positive countercase to [[JacobusLentz|Jacobus Lentz]]. The source says Carmille appeared to cooperate with Nazi statistical demands in [[France]] while secretly corrupting data, misrouting punched cards, and preserving information for resistance purposes.

The episode treats Carmille as an early technical resister: he did not reject the machinery from outside, but used internal understanding of [[PunchedCardAdministration|punched-card administration]] to slow or damage [[DataEnabledPersecution|data-enabled persecution]]. His arrest, torture, silence, deportation to Dachau, and death make him the source's clearest figure for [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance|ordinary people resistance]] under administrative violence.
