# Trump Organization

Trump Organization appears in [[how-much-money-president-trump-and-his-family-have-made]] as the family business structure through which [[DonaldTrump]]'s sons manage many assets while the source says Trump still benefits economically. The episode uses the organization to connect [[MarALago]], foreign hospitality projects, licensing, and family management to [[PoliticalInfluenceMonetization]].

## Source Position
- The source says Trump transferred management to his sons but retained economic upside.
- It treats the organization as a conduit for hospitality and licensing gains that may be amplified by presidential status.
- The episode's accounting separates ordinary preexisting business from gains attributed to the presidency.

## Connections
- [[DonaldTrump]] and [[DonaldTrumpJr]] — family and management context.
- [[MarALago]] and [[PoliticalBrandLicensing]] — property and licensing branch.
- [[OfficeLinkedProfitAccounting]] — method for deciding which gains count.
- [[PresidentialConflictOfInterest]] — governance concern around active office and private upside.
