# Kathy Hochul

Kathy Hochul is the New York governor discussed in [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]]. The episode says she signed New York's assisted-dying law and linked the decision to her mother's death from ALS.

[[tech-20260717-0717-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260717-0717-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds Hochul as the governor behind a one-year pause on hyperscale data-center construction in New York. The [[MarketplaceTech]] Bytes episode uses the executive order to connect data-center power, water, land use, utility bills, noise, and tax breaks to [[HyperscaleDataCenterMoratorium]] and [[DataCenterBacklash]].

The source presents Hochul as insisting on especially strong [[AssistedDyingSafeguards]], including mental-fitness review and filmed, independently witnessed requests.
