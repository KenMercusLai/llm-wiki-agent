# Theodore Roosevelt / 西奥多·罗斯福

Theodore Roosevelt appears in [[106-bie-zai-haipa-chongtu-kuai-lai-xuexi-tanpan-shu-742539645]] through a 1912 campaign-photo negotiation case. The episode says Roosevelt's campaign had printed millions of photo-bearing leaflets before securing permission, creating a potential copyright-fee problem.

The case is used to show [[InterestBasedNegotiation]] and [[BATNA]] thinking. The campaign-side negotiator reframed the situation around what the photographer's alternative and incentive might be, turning a possible payment demand into a publicity opportunity for the photographer.
