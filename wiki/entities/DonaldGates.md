# Donald Gates

Donald Gates is the wrongful-conviction case used in [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]] to show the danger of overclaiming [[ForensicHairEvidence|forensic hair evidence]]. The episode says hair comparison and informant testimony helped produce a 28-year imprisonment, before later DNA testing showed that the relevant hair and semen did not belong to him.

His role in the wiki is evidentiary. Gates extends [[ContestedForensicAttribution]] from old, damaged archives into modern criminal procedure: even when hair is real physical evidence, visual comparison can become misleading when treated as conclusive identification.
