# Henry McMaster

Henry McMaster appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as the South Carolina governor expected by the episode to nominate a stand-in senator after [[LindseyGraham]]'s death. His role in the source is procedural rather than biographical: the appointment decision links Graham's death to Senate arithmetic, Republican campaign management, and continuity of representation.

The page matters because the source treats the vacancy as more than a state-level succession problem. It removes a veteran Republican dealmaker while leaving [[RepublicanParty]] control of South Carolina likely, so McMaster becomes the state actor through whom national politics and state appointment rules meet.
