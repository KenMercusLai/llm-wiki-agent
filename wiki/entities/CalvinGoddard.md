# Calvin Goddard

Calvin Goddard is the ballistics expert discussed in [[121-jijian-fayi-jianshi-shouce-yiqie-fanzui-jieyou-henji-775064343]] through the 1929 St. Valentine's Day Massacre. The episode says Goddard used shell casings and comparison microscopy to distinguish the murder weapons from police-issued firearms, then later connected the weapons to guns found with Al Capone's associates.

For the wiki, Goddard matters as a concrete figure in [[ModernCriminalInvestigationFormation]]. He represents the stage where firearm traces, microscopy, laboratory comparison, and expert testimony become part of the institutional answer to suspicion and rumor.
