# Thinking Machines Lab

Thinking Machines Lab is the model lab named in [[ai-jibao-26q2-cong-coding-dao-rsi-qiangzhe-yu-qiang-de-weilai-1-171-1]] through its release of [[InteractionModel]]. The source says the model is a 276B-parameter MoE with 12B active parameters, trained from scratch for real-time multimodal interaction.

In the episode, Thinking Machines Lab matters because it pushes [[VoiceInteraction]] beyond turn-taking chat. The company is used as the contrast to "walkie-talkie" voice assistants: the source argues that AI interaction may need to listen, speak, see action, and be interrupted like a phone call rather than a text chat with audio attached.
