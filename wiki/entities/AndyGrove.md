# Andy Grove

Andy Grove appears in [[tsr-ycoffsite-brianchesky-audioonly-final-tsr-ycoffsite-brianchesky-audioonly-final]] as the crisis-management reference [[BrianChesky]] uses while describing [[Airbnb]]'s pandemic refounding. Chesky cites Grove's idea that great companies are defined by crisis, using it to explain why the COVID collapse became a forcing function for rebuilding Airbnb's operating model.

In this wiki, Grove is currently a supporting management node rather than a full Intel history page. His relevance is the connection between crisis, operating clarity, and founder/CEO behavior under existential pressure.
