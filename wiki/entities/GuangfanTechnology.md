# Guangfan Technology / 光帆科技

Guangfan Technology is the AI agent wearable hardware company discussed in [[wwdc-26-bu-shang-le-ai-dan-li-zhenzheng-de-ai-zhushou-hai-cha-shenme-s10e15-9ab1512e-a4a8-4ea6-81b5-0ac7ec677d2d]] through founder and CEO [[DongHongguang]]. The episode describes the company as building toward a personal AI assistant form factor that combines wearable devices, sensors, cloud services, agents, skills, and service integrations.

The source emphasizes Guangfan's choice of earbuds and watches because users already accept those devices as all-day wearables. Its product argument is that a [[WearableAIAssistant]] can capture physical-world context, respond through voice, and connect to cloud services without forcing the user to pull out a phone for every small action.

## Source Position
- Guangfan's software approach is described as AI-native rather than app-list-first: services are reached through agents, skills, MCP-like interfaces, and cloud integrations.
- The episode says Guangfan has connected some cloud-oriented services and demonstrated object recognition leading to product search and confirmed purchase.
- Its hardware path avoids less-accepted pin, pendant, or short-term glasses designs, while still aiming for sensor-rich, always-available interaction.
- The source treats Guangfan's opportunity as partly a Chinese hardware-supply-chain advantage, but also warns that user education, product price, token cost, permissions, privacy, and ecosystem openness remain hard.
