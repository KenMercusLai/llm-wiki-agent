# Natural Language Incorporated

Natural Language Incorporated appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] as [[RonConway]]'s first angel investment. Conway describes it as a Berkeley company trying to make computers understand natural language decades before the current AI wave made that problem commercially obvious.

The source treats the company as too early for its market. Conway says it eventually needed a home and struck a deal with [[Microsoft]], making the company a small but useful bridge between early AI ambition, [[StartupTimingWindows]], and Conway's later [[FounderInvestorLearning]].
