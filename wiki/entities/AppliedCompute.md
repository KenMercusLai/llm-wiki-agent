# Applied Compute

Applied Compute is the post-training company named in [[ai-jibao-26q2-cong-coding-dao-rsi-qiangzhe-yu-qiang-de-weilai-1-171-1]] through its cooperation with [[Harvey]]. The source says Applied Compute helped create a legal-domain model from the GLM family, turning [[OpenSourceAIModels]] into an enterprise-specific model route.

In the wiki, Applied Compute functions as a bridge between open base models and [[EnterpriseOwnedModels]]. Its importance is that post-training, evaluation, and deployment support may let enterprises capture domain-specific advantage without waiting for frontier labs to expose the exact capability or policy boundary they need.
