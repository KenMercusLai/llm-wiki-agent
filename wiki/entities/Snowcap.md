# Snowcap

Snowcap appears in [[tsr-ronconway-part4-v2-tsr-ronconway-part4-v2]] as [[SeanFanning]]'s post-[[Napster]] attempt to fix the digital-music licensing problem that Napster could not resolve. [[RonConway]] says Fanning wanted to leave Napster on the day of [[Bertelsmann]]'s financing to work on a system for distinguishing licensed and unlicensed music, and Conway promised that [[SVAngel]] would back the idea later if Fanning stayed.

The company did not fulfill Fanning's vision, but it gives the source an important second act. Napster revealed consumer demand and legal conflict; Snowcap attempted [[DigitalMusicLicensing]] through registry and compensation infrastructure after the industry relationship had been damaged by [[CopyrightPlatformConflict]].
