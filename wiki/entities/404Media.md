# 404 Media

404 Media is the technology publication named in [[tech-20260108-0108-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260108-0108-mp-tech-pod-128]] as part of the reporting path around exposed [[FlockSafety]] camera feeds. The episode says [[FlockSafety]] told 404 Media that the exposure was a limited misconfiguration affecting a very small number of devices and that the issue had been remedied.

Its role in the wiki is evidentiary: the episode relies on 404 Media for the company's response, while [[BenJordan]] supplies the technical account of finding exposed devices through [[Shodan]]. The page keeps the remediation and scope language source-scoped because the episode does not independently audit the fix.
