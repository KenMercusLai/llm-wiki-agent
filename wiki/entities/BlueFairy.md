# Blue Fairy / 蓝仙女

The Blue Fairy / 蓝仙女 is the fairy and mother figure in [[AdventuresOfPinocchio|《木偶奇遇记》]], discussed in [[122-pinuocao-chengzhang-de-cantong-jiqi-suo-chuangzuo-de-777464172]]. The episode follows her from blue-haired girl to rescuer, apparent dead beloved sister, adult woman, mother, blue goat-like guardian sign, and final dream visitor.

In the source's reading, the Blue Fairy is not only a magical mechanism for reward and punishment. Her changes make Pinocchio experience attachment, loss, guilt, patience, and care. Her apparent death especially turns the story from external correction into an internal wound: Pinocchio suffers because forgetting or failing the mother figure has become morally real to him.

## Key Claims
- The Blue Fairy functions as mentor, mother, tester, and symbolic guide inside [[HeroJourneyNarrative]].
- Her shifting form contributes to the source's [[DreamLogicNarrative]] reading.
- Pinocchio's final gift to the impoverished fairy helps confirm that his goodness has become care rather than only compliance.
