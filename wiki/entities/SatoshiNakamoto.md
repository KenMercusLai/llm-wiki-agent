# Satoshi Nakamoto

Satoshi Nakamoto enters [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]] through a public statue in [[Lugano]], not through a biography of Bitcoin's creator. The episode describes the statue as a metal-slat work designed to appear transparent from the front, then says unknown people removed it, broke it into pieces, and threw it into the lake.

The statue makes Satoshi a symbol inside [[CryptoPublicLegitimacy]]. In Lugano's [[LuganoPlanB|Plan B]] setting, the figure stands for [[Bitcoin]] culture and the city's attempt at [[MunicipalBitcoinAdoption]], while the vandalism shows that public symbolism can provoke resistance even where payments and company formation are expanding.
