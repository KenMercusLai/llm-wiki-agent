# Johann Conrad Dippel / 约翰·康拉德·迪佩尔

Johann Conrad Dippel appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the alchemist associated with [[FrankensteinCastle|Frankenstein Castle / 弗兰肯斯坦城堡]]. The episode presents him through legends of alchemy, philosopher's-stone pursuit, soul transfer, and body experimentation.

Dippel's value in the source is atmospheric and transitional. He makes the older occult and alchemical layer visible, but the episode's reading of [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] depends on [[ScienceFictionContemporaryScienceIntegration]] rather than on claiming that one castle legend simply caused the novel.
