# BBK

BBK is the consumer-electronics company founded by [[DuanYongping]] in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode presents BBK less as a single phone brand than as the organizational source of later businesses: education electronics, audiovisual products, and communications were split into separate operating lines that eventually produced [[OPPO]] and [[Vivo]].

BBK matters in the source because its decentralization and channel instincts help explain why OPPO and vivo could build large offline networks without collapsing into the same chaotic channel problems that hurt many phone challengers.
