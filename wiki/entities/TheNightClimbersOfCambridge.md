# 《剑桥夜攀者》 / The Night Climbers of Cambridge

《剑桥夜攀者》 is the 1930s book discussed in [[89-jianqiao-yepanzhe-rang-women-zhuangyan-de-shangfang-jiewa-710382629]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] treats it as a record of [[UniversityOfCambridge|Cambridge]] roof climbing, student lore, architecture, and underground etiquette. The source presents the book as both a dangerous adventure document and a cultural artifact, so its practical climbing details are not treated as instructions.

The book's pseudonymous author Whipplesnaith is identified in the episode as [[NoelHSymington]]. It also inherits a local guide tradition represented by [[TrinityCollegeClimbingGuide|《三一学院攀爬指南》]] and [[GeoffreyWinthropYoung]], while making the larger university roofscape into a field for [[CampusNightClimbing]], [[CampusPrankEthics]], and [[RoofscapeAttention]].
