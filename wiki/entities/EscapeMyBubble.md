# Escape My Bubble

Escape My Bubble was a Chrome extension built by [[KrishnaKaliannan]] after the 2016 election of [[DonaldTrump]], discussed as part of his pre-[[CatalinaCrunch]] founder path in [[catalina-crunch-krishna-kaliannan-from-homemade-keto-cocoa-puffs-to-breakfast-aisle-breakthrough-5aa86015-e7c2-448e-a84e-d1b34d2fcac2]]. The extension inserted articles into Facebook feeds to challenge users' existing beliefs.

## Key Points
- The project reportedly gained media attention and close to 100,000 signups.
- Krishna did not know how to turn the attention into a durable business model.
- In the episode, Escape My Bubble functions as a contrast case: visible traction or press can still fail to become [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]].
