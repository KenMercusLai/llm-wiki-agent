# Alice Evans

Alice Evans appears in [[the-laws-of-the-office-revisited]] as the King's College London scholar explaining a documented but unnamed social-change dynamic. Her claim in the source is that people may want to change, but often wait until they see others changing first.

Evans gives the episode its truth boundary. She says norm campaigns work only if they show real behavior; fake posters claiming everyone is washing dishes could undermine trust. That makes her segment a bridge between [[TruthfulSocialProof]], [[NamingPowerSocialChange]], and practical [[RecognitionIncentives]].
