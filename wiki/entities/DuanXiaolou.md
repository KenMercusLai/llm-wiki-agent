# 段小楼 / Duan Xiaolou

段小楼 is the adult stage identity of Xiaoshitou in [[FarewellMyConcubine|《霸王别姬》]], as read by [[64-bawangbieji-fengmo-yu-chenghuo-656094350]]. The episode begins his arc as protective: in the troupe he shelters Xiaodouzi and absorbs punishment for helping him. Later, as the "霸王" partner of [[ChengDieyi|程蝶衣]], he becomes famous through their joint performance.

The source's sharper reading is that Duan Xiaolou is a compromised or false overlord. He saves [[Juxian|菊仙]] at Huamanlou and marries her, but under political pressure he humiliates Cheng Dieyi, denies love for Juxian, and survives by bending to reality. His final cry of "小豆子" comes too late to undo the betrayals that the episode places under [[CoercedDenunciation]].
