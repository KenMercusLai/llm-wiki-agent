# Party Guitar

Party Guitar is [[YeBowen]]'s overall-award-winning hardware product from the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. It is a portable guitar-like device for creating musical atmosphere: users choose chord groups, switch chords with a joystick, and touch strings to generate sound.

The product's important design move is to start from chords rather than isolated notes, making beginners feel competent quickly. The source reports that many people asked whether they could buy it after the demo, turning the project into a small [[CustomerPull]] and [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]] signal even before a real manufacturing plan exists.
