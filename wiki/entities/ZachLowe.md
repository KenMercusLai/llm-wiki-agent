# Zach Lowe

Zach Lowe appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as the episode's main NBA expert. He explains [[SportsTanking|tanking]] as a response to draft incentives and walks the hosts through the NBA's reverse-order draft, lottery changes, and reform proposals.

Lowe's most important contribution is the basketball-specific point that one superstar can transform a team. That makes [[SportsDraftIncentiveDesign]] especially hard in the [[NationalBasketballAssociation|NBA]] because the payoff from a top pick can be large enough to keep intentional losing attractive even after lottery odds are flattened.
