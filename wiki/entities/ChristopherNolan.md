# Christopher Nolan

Christopher Nolan is the director discussed in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] through his film adaptation of [[TheOdyssey]]. The source uses the film's backlash over casting, costuming, language, and tone to examine how famous ancient texts become modern arguments about authenticity, identity, visual spectacle, and source fidelity.

The episode is skeptical of Nolan's modernization. It says the film turns [[Odysseus]] from a flawed ancient hero into a simpler modern good person, making it a case of [[HomericAdaptationModernization]] and extending the wiki's broader [[AdaptationOriginalTextConfusion]] branch.
