# Panasonic

Panasonic appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] through [[Tesla]]'s early battery route. The episode says Tesla began with Panasonic 18650 cylindrical cells, then evolved toward 21700 and 4680 formats while trying changes such as tabless cells and structural battery ideas.

The source uses Panasonic mainly as a route-dependence example. Tesla's battery format history differs from Chinese square-shell and blade-battery directions, showing that cell form factor reflects supplier availability, vehicle architecture, production process, and later service or recycling choices.
