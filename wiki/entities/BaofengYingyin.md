# 暴风影音 / Baofeng Yingyin

Baofeng Yingyin is the all-format video player discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]] as part of China's pre-platform video history. It helped users play downloaded films and discs across many formats before web-video platforms became the default viewing surface.

The episode uses Baofeng's later market rise and collapse as a reminder that local software distribution and format compatibility were a different business from long-video platform economics.
