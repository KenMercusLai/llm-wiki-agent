# Waylon Wong

Waylon Wong appears in [[indicators-of-2025-and-what-to-watch-in-2026]] as the host framing the [[PlanetMoney]] and [[TheIndicator]] crossover and later as the participant choosing the federal funds rate as a 2026 indicator. His part of the source connects ordinary rate-cycle watching to a governance question about whether the [[FederalReserve]] can keep decisions grounded in data under presidential pressure.

The source, dated 2025-12-31, says [[JeromePowell]]'s chair term was due to end in May 2026 and that [[DonaldTrump]] wanted lower rates. That makes Wong's indicator selection a bridge between [[FederalFundsRateAsPolicySignal]], [[CentralBankIndependence]], and [[MonetaryPolicyLag]].
