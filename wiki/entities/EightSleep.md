# Eight Sleep / 8Sleep

Eight Sleep, referred to as 8Sleep in [[zhongnian-san-zhanghu-xianjinliu-jirou-shuimian-lnyomru5v2yzo1-otuyw2mdj-vae]], is the sponsor-linked smart-bed product used to make [[SleepAsDailyHealthAccount]] more concrete. The host describes a water-cooled and water-heated mattress cover that changes bed temperature across the night, monitors sleep, and wakes the user through temperature or light vibration.

The page should keep those claims source-scoped. In the episode, 8Sleep is not presented as a universal medical solution; it is a product example inside a broader [[EnvironmentOverWillpower]] argument that sleep inputs such as temperature, darkness, screen exposure, caffeine, alcohol, and wake time are easier to manage than sleep itself.
