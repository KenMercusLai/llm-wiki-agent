# The Social Radars

The Social Radars is the podcast/show context for [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]], [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]], and [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]]. In these episodes, [[JessicaLivingston]] and [[CarolynLevy]] interview founders about how companies and institutions were actually built, including [[TrevorBlackwell]] on [[Viaweb]], [[Anybots]], and early [[YCombinator]], [[TracyYoung]] on [[PlanGrid]], [[Autodesk]], and [[TigerEye]], and [[TonyXu]] on [[DoorDash]].

The show matters to the wiki as a startup-history source. Rather than only summarizing company outcomes, the episodes preserve operational memories: who wrote scripts, who printed applications, how offices were wired, what robot demos visitors saw, how construction drawings moved through job sites, how founders grieved while building, how second-time founders redesign company operating systems, and how a food-delivery marketplace began with PDF menus and founders doing deliveries.
