# Battlecode

Battlecode appears in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]] as the [[MIT]] programming competition that helped [[SpenserSkates]] discover startup culture. The source describes it as a short, intense programming contest mixing video games, AI programming, iteration, prestige, and recruiting value.

The episode treats Battlecode as startup training before the company exists. Participants had to understand an open-ended game, build quickly, improve from feedback, and compete without a standard textbook path. For Spenser, the network around Battlecode also connected him to people such as [[DrewHouston]] and [[ArashFerdowsi]], making it part of the route into [[YCombinator]]-style startup thinking.
