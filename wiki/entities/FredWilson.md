# Fred Wilson

Fred Wilson is the [[UnionSquareVentures]] partner whose blog post led [[ChristinaCacioppo]] to apply to USV in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]]. Christina says she sent links to her online presence, including Flickr, Twitter, and a Berlin design blog or Tumblr, and later saw the job as genuinely internet-sourced.

Wilson's most important role in the episode is advice. Christina wanted to start a software company but could not yet build software herself; Wilson told her she was not a "fake it till you make it" person. She interpreted that as a reason to learn the missing skill directly, which led to her self-directed coding period before [[Dropbox]] and [[Vanta]].
