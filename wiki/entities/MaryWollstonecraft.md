# Mary Wollstonecraft / 玛丽·沃斯通克拉夫特

Mary Wollstonecraft appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]]'s mother and as an eighteenth-century feminist and radical political writer. The episode uses her life to show that Shelley's childhood inheritance included arguments about revolution, education, gender, reason, and women's intellectual standing.

Her death from puerperal fever shortly after Shelley's birth becomes part of the episode's bodily and emotional background. It connects the novel's birth, loss, and creation themes to a household where female authorship, maternal absence, and radical thought were all close to the child who later wrote [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]].
