# National Women's Soccer League

The National Women's Soccer League appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as the U.S. sports league that abolished its draft in 2024. The episode uses the NWSL to show a more radical alternative to lottery reform: incoming players negotiate directly with interested teams while the league still maintains a salary cap.

The NWSL case reframes anti-tanking reform as a labor-market design problem. It removes the direct reward for losing, but it also raises questions about whether wealthy or highly committed owners can compound advantage over time.
