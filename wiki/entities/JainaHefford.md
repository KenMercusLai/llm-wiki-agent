# Jaina Hefford

Jaina Hefford appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as the [[ProfessionalWomensHockeyLeague|PWHL]] figure explaining why the league was willing to try the [[GoldPlan]]. The source presents her as a Hockey Hall of Famer and Olympic medalist speaking from a league built with a willingness to experiment.

Her role is to make the Gold Plan concrete rather than theoretical. She explains the idea that once a team is eliminated, wins begin helping draft position, turning late-season effort into a reward.
