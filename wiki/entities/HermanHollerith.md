# Herman Hollerith / 赫尔曼·霍尔瑞斯

Herman Hollerith / 赫尔曼·霍尔瑞斯 appears in [[133-ibm-yu-nacui-weishenme-putongren-bu-ying-zanmei-jizeizhuyi-815684110]] as the inventor whose punched-card tabulation system made large-scale population statistics dramatically faster before electronic computers. The episode uses his census work to explain why [[PunchedCardAdministration|punched-card administration]] was attractive to states that needed to sort people, property, labor, and transport at scale.

The source does not portray Hollerith's invention as intrinsically evil. Its point is historical afterlife: a useful statistical technology became morally dangerous when [[IBM]], [[Dehomag|德霍梅格]], and [[NaziGermany|Nazi Germany]] connected classification capacity to racial law, deportation logistics, forced labor, and camp administration.
