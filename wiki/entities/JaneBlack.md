# Jane Black

Jane Black appears in [[why-is-there-a-supplement-craze-if-they-dont-even-work]] as a Washington, D.C. food-politics reporter working with [[SarahGonzalez]] on the [[PlanetMoney]] supplement investigation. She frames "big wellness" as a growing commercial and political force, then follows how manufacturing, claim language, regulatory history, and consumer desire fit together.

Her contribution is the source's synthesis layer: supplements are not only products with questionable efficacy, but a business and policy system where [[RecurringSupplementCommerce]], [[SupplementStructureFunctionClaims]], and weak premarket safety requirements reinforce each other.
