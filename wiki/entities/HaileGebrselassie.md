# Haile Gebrselassie

Haile Gebrselassie is the elite distance runner referenced in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] through both barefoot-running discussion and the longer lineage of marathon performance. The source cites an interview in which he does not recommend modern runners run barefoot on today's hard road surfaces.

## Key Claims
- Gebrselassie's barefoot-running history is used to qualify the barefoot-shoe trend: natural running can be context-dependent and does not transfer cleanly to modern concrete or asphalt.
- The episode includes him among earlier runners who helped build the performance lineage that made sub-2 marathon goals credible.
- His example connects athlete biography to shoe-design debates about cushioning, foot strength, and road surfaces.
