# Plantamica

Plantamica is the scented soil additive brand discussed in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. [[AndrewGraff]] describes the product as pellets that release scent when watered while also providing nutrients to plants, using a biopolymer made from chitosan and sodium alginate.

The Advice Line question is whether Andrew should seek investors or first prove traction. [[GuyRaz]] advises launching, testing in a local store, sampling on-site, and collecting three to six months of reorder, customer, posting, and unit-economics data. [[ShaziVisram]] suggests clean fragrance and third-party validation as possible positioning paths.
