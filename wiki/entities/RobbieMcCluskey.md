# Robbie McCluskey

Robbie McCluskey is the [[Vidiots]] store director quoted in [[tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128]]. McCluskey gives the episode its operating details: Vidiots began in 1985, closed in 2017, reopened in 2023 as a nonprofit, and uses theater screenings to help subsidize the rental shop.

McCluskey's role in the source is to ground [[PhysicalVideoMediaRevival]] in store-level evidence rather than general nostalgia. The reported rental growth and young-customer traffic make Vidiots a case for [[VideoStoreCulture]] as a living retail and community format.
