# GOAT Clean

GOAT Clean is the used-sneaker refurbishment service discussed in [[tsr-s3-eddylu-v2-tsr-s3-eddylu-v2]]. [[EddyLu]] describes it as a way for [[GOAT]] to clean, deodorize, and touch up used sneakers so buyers can access expensive styles at lower prices.

The service extends GOAT's broader marketplace logic. Authentication answers whether a sneaker is real; GOAT Clean also makes used condition more legible and acceptable. That connects the product to [[AuthenticationLedMarketplaceTrust]], [[ServiceMarketplaceQualityControl]], and [[MarketplaceFrictionReduction]] because the platform reduces buyer uncertainty around both authenticity and condition.

## Key Claims
- Most GOAT customers buy sneakers to wear, not only to display.
- GOAT Clean helps make high-priced styles available at lower used-sneaker prices.
- Refurbishment adds operational work behind the marketplace surface, reinforcing GOAT's retail-like buying experience.
