# August Bier / 奥古斯特·比尔

August Bier appears in [[68-fengkuang-shiyan-shi-ai-zhe-gaisi-de-qiuzhiyu-664384549]] through the source's spinal-anesthesia case. The episode describes Bier and his assistant testing spinal injection with cocaine after an initial failed attempt on Bier, then verifying anesthesia by burning, pinching, striking, and pulling hair from the assistant's lower body.

The case contributes two threads. First, it is a [[SelfExperimentation]] example where bodily risk and immediate sensory testing helped establish a medical technique. Second, it raises recognition and credit questions because the assistant's experimental role later became a dispute. That links Bier's story to [[ExperimentalScienceEthics]] beyond consent alone.
