# David Victor

David Victor appears in [[tech-20260216-0216-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260216-0216-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a public policy professor at [[UCSD]] commenting on AI data-center energy choices. The source uses his "all-out arms race" framing to explain why speed can dominate energy selection when developers want data centers online quickly.

In this episode, Victor's point strengthens the wiki's [[AIEnergyBottleneck]] branch: if grid interconnection, renewables, or geothermal options cannot arrive fast enough, data-center developers may choose [[DataCenterOnsitePower]] using natural gas generators despite the longer-term energy and climate questions that choice raises.
