# BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is mentioned in [[142-chanpin-tiyan-xue-riben-quanqiu-yingxiao-xue-hanguo-feng-tou-quan-1-142-1]] as part of the Korea-side global marketing and [[FanEconomy]] comparison.
