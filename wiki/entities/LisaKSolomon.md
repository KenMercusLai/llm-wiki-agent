# Lisa K. Solomon

Lisa K. Solomon is the [[LongNow]] event host and Q&A interlocutor in [[stefan-sagmeister-finally-something-good-yqqxftsvema]]. She introduces the talk around hope and informed optimism, then asks [[StefanSagmeister]] about critics versus enthusiasts, journalism, participation, sabbaticals, constraints, beauty, labels, and AI's effect on design.

Her role in the source is to pull Sagmeister's argument out of the artwork and into practice. The questions connect [[InformedOptimism]] to [[PositiveJournalism]], [[ParticipatoryExhibitions]], [[DesignUnderConstraints]], and future design judgment.
