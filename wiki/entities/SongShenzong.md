# 宋神宗 / Emperor Shenzong of Song

Song Shenzong appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as the [[NorthernSong|北宋]] emperor under whom [[WangAnshi|王安石]]'s reform program gains power and [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s criticism becomes politically dangerous. The episode presents him as still appreciating Su Shi's talent even as the reform conflict intensifies.

The source uses Shenzong to make the [[WutaiPoetryCase|乌台诗案]] more than a literary misunderstanding. Su Shi's poems and memorial language could be read as attacks on imperial policy or the emperor himself, so [[PoetryAsPoliticalEvidence]] became a way for censorial actors to transform literary speech into a loyalty problem.
