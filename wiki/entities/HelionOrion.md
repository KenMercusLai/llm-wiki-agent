# Helion Orion

Helion Orion is the planned [[Helion]] power plant discussed in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]]. In [[DavidKirtley]]'s source account, Orion is the project connected to [[Microsoft]]'s power-purchase agreement and a target of putting electricity on the grid by 2028.

The source says Orion is planned for 50 MW of electricity delivered to the grid through a utility structure involving [[ChelanPUD]] and [[ConstellationEnergy]]. Kirtley also says the project received an environmental determination of non-significance, letting work begin quickly after the lease was signed.

Orion matters as [[HardTechCustomerIntentProof]] with delivery accountability. It is not just a fusion demo in the episode's framing; it is the first customer-backed test of whether [[CommercialFusionPower]], permitting, grid connection, manufacturing, and operating performance can converge.
