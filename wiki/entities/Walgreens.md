# Walgreens

[[Walgreens]] appears in [[tsr-s4-tylershultzpart1-final-tsr-s4-tylershultzpart1-final]] as the public launch setting for [[Theranos]] testing in Palo Alto. [[TylerShultz|Tyler Shultz]] says his first full-time day at Theranos coincided with the Walgreens launch, creating a celebratory company narrative at the same moment the validation team was seeing severe technical and regulatory problems.

The Walgreens branch matters because it turns internal lab weakness into patient-facing risk. In Tyler's account, the company launched even though no tests had been validated on the Theranos platform and patient samples were not actually being run on the advertised Theranos device. That makes Walgreens a distribution and public-trust surface for [[MedicalTestingValidationIntegrity]], not simply a retail partner named in passing.
