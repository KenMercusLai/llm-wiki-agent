# Gecko Robotics

Gecko Robotics is the Pittsburgh robotics startup in [[tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260320-0320-mp-tech-pod-128]] that won a $71 million [[USNavy|U.S. Navy]] contract. The source says Gecko uses drones and wall-climbing robots to inspect ships for defects, then uses AI to model current and future structural problems.

In the wiki, Gecko is the concrete case for [[DefenseRoboticsMaintenance]] and [[DefenseTechStartupProcurement]]. It shows how robotics startups can enter military maintenance and readiness work traditionally associated with larger defense contractors, while also raising questions about reliability, security, human error, and whether younger companies can meet military standards.
