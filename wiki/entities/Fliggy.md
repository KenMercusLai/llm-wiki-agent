# Fliggy

Fliggy appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as one of the later potential competitors to [[Ctrip]]. The source treats it as part of the broader Chinese platform environment rather than giving it a standalone operating history.
