# Fliggy

Fliggy appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as one of the later potential competitors to [[Ctrip]]. The source treats it as part of the broader Chinese platform environment rather than giving it a standalone operating history.

Its significance is that travel booking is not isolated from large-platform ecosystems. [[Alibaba]]-linked traffic, payments, merchant tools, and consumer accounts can form a competing path to [[TravelSuperAppConvenience]].

[[ep117-doubao-yuehuo-guoyi-ali-zaizao-qianwen-shibushi-wanle-lmp0pzdig2ijow5k3cnnnvvqq6sa]] adds Fliggy as one of the service surfaces that could give [[Qwen]] real "hands and feet." In that source, Fliggy matters because travel planning is exactly the kind of task where an assistant can move from answer generation into booking, itinerary, payment, and support.
