# Baruch Spinoza / 斯宾诺莎

Baruch Spinoza / 斯宾诺莎 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the figure who tells [[Plato|柏拉图]] that the absent boss-God is not what really runs [[ThinkReasonLtd|思考理性有限公司]]. The operating power is "NATURE," rendered in the episode as the capital-side trust fund.

That joke creates [[CorporateTheologyCapitalControl]]: theological language is displaced into corporate governance, where metaphysical authority and financial control overlap without being identical.

[[123-renxing-shiyan-dangxin-ni-ziji-779479569]] adds a psychology reference through the [[GilbertSpinozaHypothesis]]. In this source, Spinoza anchors the idea that people initially accept propositions and only later reject them through effortful cognition, which [[DanielGilbert]] turns into a modern social-psychology frame.
