# Neihan Duanzi

Neihan Duanzi appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as the [[ByteDance]] content product whose April 2018 shutdown set the regulatory backdrop for the [[ToutengWar]]. The hosts argue that ByteDance's fear was not abstract: with Neihan Duanzi gone and other short-video products under rectification, [[Douyin]] looked vulnerable.

The source uses Neihan Duanzi to explain why ByteDance chose public escalation. In the hosts' reading, fighting [[Tencent]] in the open was partly a way to prevent regulatory or platform pressure from staying invisible.
