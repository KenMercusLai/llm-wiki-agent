# Susan Griffin-Black

Susan Griffin-Black is the [[EOProducts]] founder and [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. In the episode, she revisits EO's COVID-era hand sanitizer surge, later sales drop, inventory overhang, vendor obligations, and first layoffs before advising founders from [[Yobi]], [[CultureWineCompany]], and [[CaneDogCoffee]].

Her advice emphasizes [[RelationshipLedGrowth]] and [[LocalMarketProof]]: founders should work deeply in nearby markets, use authentic stories to create trust, and treat relationships with vendors, channel partners, professionals, and local buyers as operating assets.
