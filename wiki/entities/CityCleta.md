# City Cleta

City Cleta is [[YaserGonzalezCabrera]]'s [[Havana]] bike-tour company in [[dark-times-for-cubas-economic-experiment]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode uses the business as a small private-enterprise case created by [[Cuba]]'s [[ConstrainedMarketReform]] and the Obama-era tourism opening.

The company becomes a stress indicator because its demand collapses when the external conditions around Cuba change. The source says City Cleta once handled about 400 tourists a month, but later saw roughly 25 customers in a year and no paying customers in the year covered by the episode. That makes the company a concrete instance of [[TourismDependentSmallEconomy]] and [[CubanDualEconomicStrategy]] failure.
