# First Kamchatka Expedition / 第一次勘察加探险

The First Kamchatka Expedition is the initial Bering-led eastern exploration project described in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. Ordered after [[PeterTheGreat]]'s death, it sent [[VitusBering]], [[MartinSpangberg]], [[AlexeiChirikov]], and their teams across Siberia toward [[Okhotsk]] and [[Kamchatka]] to build ships, map routes, and test whether Asia and America were separated.

The source emphasizes that the expedition's central difficulty was [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]]. Before reaching open water, the team had to move anchors, sails, iron, tools, weapons, and people through rivers, portages, frozen roads, shortages, local official resistance, Indigenous burden, and animal loss.
