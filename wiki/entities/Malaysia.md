# Malaysia

Malaysia appears in [[peace-fire-further-us-iran-strikes-6a4f7acffe878dc8e27e6fbf]] as a central care-capacity example in the Asian elder-care segment. The episode says Malaysia has 36 million people but only 18 licensed nursing homes, and that authorities cited more than 2,000 cases of elderly patients abandoned in hospitals between 2018 and 2022.

The source says Malaysia is expediting a senior-citizens bill, placing it within the broader [[FilialPietyLaws]] trend. Its stronger contribution is to [[ElderCareStateCapacity]]: the legal debate is driven by a gap between ageing need and available care infrastructure.
