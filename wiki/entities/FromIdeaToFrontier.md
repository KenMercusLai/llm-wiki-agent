# From Idea to Frontier

From Idea to Frontier is the Amazon Web Services creative renaissance global accelerator program named in [[1-ren-gongsi-kang-5-ge-ren-de-huo-hai-yao-guan-50-ge-agents-s10e18-e3a21dde-0bba-4ec2-bf12-5043500ae5c6]]. The episode describes it as aimed at Chinese-language [[OnePersonCompany]] founders, with certification, cloud-service credits, acceleration support, and a re:Invent opportunity for selected participants.

The wiki records those program details as source claims rather than independent verification. Conceptually, the program matters because it shows infrastructure companies treating solo AI founders as a market segment, while the episode itself cautions that credits and acceleration do not replace customer validation.
