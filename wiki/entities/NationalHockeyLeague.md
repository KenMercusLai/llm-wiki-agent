# National Hockey League / NHL

The National Hockey League appears in [[106-bie-zai-haipa-chongtu-kuai-lai-xuexi-tanpan-shu-742539645]] through the 2004 labor-dispute case. The episode uses the dispute between the league and the [[NHLPlayersAssociation|NHL Players' Association / NHLPA]] to show how salary-cap conflict, loss framing, and zero-sum assumptions can destroy value.

In the source's account, the cancelled season and later worse settlement for the player side illustrate [[ZeroSumBias]], [[EscalationOfCommitment]], and [[FramingEffect]]. The case also extends the wiki's [[SportsCollectiveBargaining]] branch by adding a negative comparison to the WNBA bargaining source.
