# 范进

范进 is the famous examination-success figure in [[RulinWaishi|《儒林外史》]], discussed in [[58-rulin-waishi-jiashe-kaogong-chengle-weiyi-chulu-645207859]] as the entry point many readers know from school. The episode treats his madness after passing not as an isolated comic scene, but as a concentrated result of [[ImperialExaminationAsOnlyExit]]: social contempt, family pressure, poverty, age, and sudden recognition collide at once.

The source pairs Fan Jin with [[ZhouJin|周进]]. Zhou Jin recognizes his own humiliation in Fan Jin and, after repeated rereading, convinces himself that Fan Jin's mediocre-looking essay is "天地间之至文." The episode uses this to keep Fan Jin's talent ambiguous while making the status machine very clear. Fan Jin's later inability to understand a Su Shi joke further lets the novel expose him without authorial lecturing.
