# 思考理性有限公司 / Think Reason Ltd.

思考理性有限公司 is the fictional company at the center of [[PlatoGoesToWork|《柏拉图上班记：哲学家的职场百态》]] and [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]]. The episode describes it as a company whose founder-boss is associated with "God," whose avatar is a triangle-framed all-seeing eye, and whose departments are staffed by philosophers.

The company is a satire machine. Its org chart turns philosophy into office life: [[FriedrichNietzsche|尼采]] fires people in HR, [[MichelFoucault|福柯]] watches screens, [[BlaisePascal|帕斯卡]] gambles at work, [[BaruchSpinoza|斯宾诺莎]] points from God to capital, and [[Voltaire|伏尔泰]] and [[JeanJacquesRousseau|卢梭]] fight as salespeople.
