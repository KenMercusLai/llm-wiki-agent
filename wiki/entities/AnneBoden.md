# Anne Boden

Anne Boden appears in [[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] as the founder figure behind the early [[StarlingBank]] effort that [[TomBlomfield]] joined after [[Grouper]]. The episode uses her mainly through Tom's recollection of informal agreements, volatile negotiations, firings, and his eventual resignation.

Because the account is one participant's perspective, the page treats Anne as part of the Starling-to-[[Monzo]] governance story rather than as a settled character judgment. The key wiki value is the [[FounderAgreementDocumentation]] lesson: equity, cash contributions, authority, and founder roles need written agreements before a team is operating under emotional and financial pressure.
