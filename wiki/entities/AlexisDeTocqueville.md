# Alexis de Tocqueville

Alexis de Tocqueville appears in [[trailer-tocqueville-road-trip-6a31522c9b6fd8dcaba7b494]] and [[trailer-tocqueville-road-trip-6a315642780d0f7579af216c]] as the French observer whose 1831 journey through the [[UnitedStates]] became [[DemocracyInAmerica]]. The trailer presents him as a guide to American society whose observations may still help explain the country.

His role in the wiki is less biographical than diagnostic. Tocqueville becomes the historical benchmark for [[AmericaAsIdea]]: the claim that the United States operates not only as a country, but as a democratic promise with global symbolic force.
