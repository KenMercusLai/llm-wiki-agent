# Dangerous Visions

Dangerous Visions is the [[ScienceFictionNewWave|New Wave science fiction]] anthology recommended near the end of [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]]. The episode uses it as a wider field marker rather than a close-read text, placing [[RogerZelazny|Roger Zelazny]]'s work in a 1960s and later movement that made science fiction more experimental, socially and psychologically alert, and less confined to hard-technology problem solving.
