# Tristan

Tristan is the founder of [[NaturalSelection]] interviewed in [[135-he-ziran-xuanze-chuangshiren-tristan-liao-elys-saibo-fenshen-linghun-context-de-huoqu-yu-liudong-he-ai-shejiao-wangluo-ltwegwvo7grn-v-rft0txlmqmcty]]. The source presents his product path as a long-running exploration of emotional value and loneliness: audio content, stranger social products, romance games, [[EVE]], and [[Elys]] all become ways to help people feel accompanied or make better connections.

His distinctive claim is that the AI product shift is not only stronger models or new interfaces, but [[ProactiveAgents]] acting with enough [[ContextEngineering]] to face the world on the user's behalf. He sees [[SubjectivityAsAIAsset]] as the scarce layer: people and creators with clear values, taste, style, past work, and self-understanding can give agents better direction as intelligence becomes more widely available.

[[zhe-keneng-caishi-ai-peiban-zhenzheng-gai-you-de-yangzi-duitan-shuaping-chanpin-eve-chuangshiren-tristan-lgvcb1tuur-1rf2qk8jv9chmwew]] sharpens the EVE side of his thesis. [[Tristan]] defines AI companionship as "super alignment" with the user's life, then works backward into [[AICompanionActiveMemory]], emotional post-training, real-world temporal awareness, independent personality, and game progression as the product mechanisms needed to make a companion feel stable rather than merely chatty.

## Key Points
- He describes [[EVE]] as the product where prior game, content, character, and AI ideas converged into a companion-like "person."
- He describes [[Elys]] as a larger network opportunity: a "field" where [[CyberAvatars]] pre-interact and discover valuable real-person connections.
- He frames [[ContextFlywheel]] as the product loop that can overcome both personal context cold start and network cold start.
- He argues that [[ChatGPT]] and [[Doubao]] are serious competitors, but also that social-network rules, graphs, protocols, and trust cannot be supplied by a model alone.
- He treats [[CharacterAI]]-style session duration as insufficient evidence of companionship, because content consumption and relationship continuity are different product outcomes.
- He sees romance-game capabilities, 3D pipelines, character methods, and human companion-chat data as part of the AI product stack, not only decorative content.
