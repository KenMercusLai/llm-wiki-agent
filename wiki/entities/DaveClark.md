# Dave Clark

Dave Clark appears in [[tsr-s3-ryanpetersen-v6-tsr-s3-ryanpetersen-v6]] as the former [[Amazon]] consumer-business executive whom [[RyanPetersen]] hired to become [[Flexport]] CEO. Petersen says he wanted stronger operational excellence, repeatability, metrics, and process improvement as Flexport scaled beyond its founder-led phase.

The episode uses the transition as a [[FounderRoleTransition]] and [[FounderSuccession]] case, but also as a boundary case. Petersen later joined [[FoundersFund]] and then returned to Flexport when the board asked him to, turning the Clark handoff into part of the source's broader [[FounderOperationalReset]] and [[FounderReturnCrisis]] arc.
