# Jed Kronfeld

Jed Kronfeld is the listener whose question starts [[theres-no-business-like-dough-business]]. He notices that [[AtlanticAvenueBarclaysCenter]] has three nearby [[WetzelsPretzels|Wetzel's Pretzels]] locations and asks why they are so close together, whether they compete, and whether they make money.

Jed's role is to provide the ordinary observation that the episode turns into an economics case. His question lets [[PlanetMoney]] examine [[ImpulseRetailClustering]], [[RetailIncrementality]], and the franchisee-level operating logic behind what first looks like redundant storefront placement.
