# David McRaney / 大卫·麦克雷尼

David McRaney is the author of [[ZhenglunYuShuofu|《争论与说服》 / How Minds Change]], the book used by [[132-zhenglun-yu-shuofu-women-weishenme-chaojia-zenme-cai-suan-ying-807583318]] to discuss argument, persuasion, belief change, and public disagreement. The episode presents the book's framework through cognitive science, social psychology, communication studies, and concrete examples rather than as a simple rhetoric guide.

In this wiki branch, McRaney matters as a popularizer of why facts alone often fail to move people. The episode's use of his work connects constructed perception, cognitive dissonance, tribal belief, group polarization, deep canvassing, and social naming into one explanation of how minds can eventually change.
