# Paul Gross

Paul Gross appears in [[tsr-ycoffsite-pg-audioonly-final-tsr-ycoffsite-pg-audioonly-final]] as a young [[YCombinator]] founder at the Founder Mode Retreat whose company works on carbon capture from locomotives. [[JessicaLivingston]] tells a story about security mistaking him for her and [[PaulGraham]]'s teenage son because he looked so young.

The episode uses Gross as a credibility case. Graham says young founders in serious industries can trigger skepticism from older investors or industry people, but Gross's tactic was to know the subject deeply enough that conversations quickly made his expertise visible.

[[tsr-ycoffsite-paulgross-v1-audioonly-tsr-ycoffsite-paulgross-v1-audioonly]] makes Gross the central interview subject and identifies his company as [[RemoraCarbon]]. Gross says Remora builds retrofit systems for semi trucks and locomotives that capture CO2 from exhaust and purify it into beverage-grade CO2. The episode traces his path from software engineering and data science into [[MobileCarbonCapture]], co-founder recruiting, the virtual [[YCombinator]] Winter 2021 batch, and hardware prototyping in Detroit.

The same source adds Gross's concrete [[FounderMode]] method: choose the company's top three risks each quarter, delegate everything else, and go deep on the few areas where founder learning and authority can unblock the company. His examples include soot and ash filtration in locomotive exhaust, CO2 liquefaction, selling CO2, government affairs, and the next manufacturing challenge.
