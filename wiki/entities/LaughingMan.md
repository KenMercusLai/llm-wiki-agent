# 笑脸男 / Laughing Man

笑脸男 / Laughing Man is discussed in [[01-maitian-shouwangzhe-20shiji-de-meiguo-jiabaoyu-523554422]] through [[GhostInTheShellStandaloneComplex|《攻壳机动队 STAND ALONE COMPLEX》]]. The episode reads him as a more public, technologically mediated, and relatively brighter version of the [[HoldenCaulfield|Holden Caulfield / 霍尔顿]] pattern.

The hosts' interpretation is that the Laughing Man is a young idealist who sees adult-world falseness, wants to protect others, and is damaged by institutional corruption. That makes him part of the source's [[LiterarySubcultureSymbolization]] branch: [[TheCatcherInTheRye|《麦田里的守望者》]] becomes a reusable symbolic language for later media rather than staying only a mid-century American school novel.
