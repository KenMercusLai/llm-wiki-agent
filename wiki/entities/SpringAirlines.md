# Spring Airlines / 春秋航空

Spring Airlines appears in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] as the surviving contrast to [[EastStarAirlines]] in China's first wave of private airlines. The episode places it in the same [[ChinesePrivateAirlineOpening]] generation, but uses founder [[WangZhenghua]] to represent a slower, thriftier, operationally grounded route.

The contrast matters because it prevents the East Star story from becoming only a story of bad timing or state pressure. Spring's survival and later public-company path suggest that private-airline opportunity existed, but [[PrivateAirlineFailureModes]] punished operators that combined high leverage, fragile cash flow, and weaker operational discipline.
