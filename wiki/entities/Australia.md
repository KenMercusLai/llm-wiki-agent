# Australia

Australia is the first country case in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]], where [[PlanetMoney]] uses it to explain [[WaterMarketDesign]]. The episode presents Australian water markets as among the world's most advanced systems for buying and selling water rights, especially in drought-prone agricultural regions.

The episode also uses Australia as a democratic-institution contrast. [[JustinWolfers]] points to Saturday elections and community voting rituals as examples of civic design that can make participation easier and more cohesive.
