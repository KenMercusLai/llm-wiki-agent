# Cocoa Beach

Cocoa Beach is the Florida beach town used as an early place signal for [[SunBum]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. [[TomRinks]] says the company used a Cocoa Beach address because it sounded like the right home for a sunscreen brand, even while early calls were routed to Grand Rapids.

The source later notes that Sun Bum created a real Cocoa Beach office, turning the borrowed place signal into an operating presence. The case illustrates [[PlaceBasedBrandPerception]]: customers, buyers, and callers read geography as part of the product's authenticity.
