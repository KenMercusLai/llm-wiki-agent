# Sabastian Sawe

Sabastian Sawe is the Kenyan marathon runner discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as the source's reported winner of the 2026 London Marathon in 1:59:30. The episode uses his result and his [[AdidasAdiosProEvo3]] shoes to frame the "human or shoe" debate.

## Key Claims
- The source treats Sawe's race as proof that sub-2 marathon performance has to be understood through athlete ability, training, shoes, and support ecosystem together.
- Sawe's training is described as extending [[EliudKipchoge]]-style seven-day cycles into ten-day cycles while adding running-specific strength work.
- His result gives [[Adidas]] a professional proof point, but the episode rejects assigning the achievement to the shoe alone.
