# RecruiterBox

RecruiterBox is the bootstrapped recruiting SaaS company [[GirishRedikar]] built before [[Sprinto]]. In [[finding-product-market-fit-after-3-years-of-failed-ideas]], he uses RecruiterBox to explain years of failed ideas, the first clear demand signal, and the compliance pain that later pointed toward Sprinto.

## Key Claims
- Early ideas included a job search engine and resume-to-job-description matching using machine learning, but these did not become successful businesses.
- RecruiterBox eventually became a hiring CRM that helped companies manage recruiting activity in one place.
- Customers paid through a poor PayPal/manual-credit process before Stripe was available to the founders; their willingness to tolerate that friction became a strong [[CustomerPull]] signal.
- The business reached more than 2,500 customers, single-digit millions of ARR, and more than 100 added customers per month before it was sold.
- Moving upmarket exposed the team to SOC 2 reports, ISO certificates, security questionnaires, consultants, and months of compliance work, creating the experiential basis for [[Sprinto]].
