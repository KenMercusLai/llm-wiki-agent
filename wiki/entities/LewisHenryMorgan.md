# Lewis Henry Morgan

Lewis Henry Morgan appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] as part of the 19th-century anthropology and social-evolution context around [[JohannJakobBachofen]]'s [[MotherRight|《母权论》 / Mother Right]]. The episode groups Bachofen and Morgan as figures who challenged the idea that patriarchal family order was humanity's original and only form.

The source uses Morgan mainly as intellectual context rather than as a detailed subject. His relevance is that mother-line descent and kinship organization became thinkable as historical institutions, helping later debates move beyond [[HenryMaine]]'s patriarchal-eternity frame.
