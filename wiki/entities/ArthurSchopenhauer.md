# Arthur Schopenhauer / 叔本华

Arthur Schopenhauer enters the wiki through [[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] says he repeatedly used the [[MunchausenBaron|敏锡豪森男爵]] self-rescue image when discussing the limits of reason and experience.

The episode's use of Schopenhauer is not mainly biographical. He functions as evidence that a comic tall-tale motif from [[AdventuresOfBaronMunchausen|《吹牛大王历险记》]] traveled into philosophy as [[MunchausenSelfBootstrapping]]: an image for reasoning that tries to pull itself out of its own ground.
