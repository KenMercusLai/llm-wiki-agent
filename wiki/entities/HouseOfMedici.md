# House of Medici / 美第奇家族

The House of Medici / 美第奇家族 appears in [[72-junzhulun-du-ta-shi-yichang-weixian-de-maoxian-673440837]] as both Renaissance patronage background and hard political force. The episode notes the family's support for artists, Machiavelli's childhood exposure to violent Medici-related politics, and the family's 1512 return to Florence with papal backing.

The Medici restoration matters because it ended the [[FlorentineRepublic|Florentine Republic]] that [[NiccoloMachiavelli|Machiavelli]] had served. His imprisonment, torture, exile, and writing of [[ThePrince|《君主论》]] are framed against that reversal.
