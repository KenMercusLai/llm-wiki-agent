# C. S. Lewis / C.S. 刘易斯

C. S. Lewis appears in [[60-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-648693204]] through his long friendship with [[JRRTolkien|J.R.R. Tolkien / 托尔金]]. The episode presents the relationship as affectionate and intellectually serious, but not uniformly supportive: the two admired each other's character while criticizing each other's literary work.

For the wiki, Lewis mainly helps the Tolkien branch show that friendship and artistic judgment can coexist with sharp disagreement. Tolkien could praise Lewis as honest, brave, intelligent, scholarly, poetic, and philosophical while also disliking *Narnia* and other works; Lewis in turn could push back on Tolkien's fantasy habits.
