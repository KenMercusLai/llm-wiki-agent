# Nassim Taleb

Nassim Taleb appears in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]] as the conceptual source behind the episode's discussion of [[BlackSwan]], [[FatTailRisk]], [[Antifragility]], [[BarbellStrategy]], and asymmetric risk. The speakers treat his work as a linked system rather than a set of slogans: randomness, induction limits, extreme events, and option-like payoffs all point toward structures that can survive or benefit from uncertainty.

The source also uses Taleb as a warning against shallow copying. Understanding the words "black swan" or "antifragile" is not the same thing as building a portfolio, career, or life structure that actually has bounded downside and meaningful upside.
