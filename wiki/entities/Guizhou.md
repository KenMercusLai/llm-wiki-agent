# Guizhou

Guizhou enters the wiki through [[building-things-and-breaking-things-in-china-summer-school-world-tour]] as [[DanWang]]'s vivid example of Chinese build capacity and possible [[InfrastructureMalinvestment]]. The source says Wang cycled through the province in 2021 and saw extremely tall bridges, while the episode notes that Guizhou has many of the world's tallest bridges and numerous airports despite being one of China's poorer provinces.

The point is not that every bridge is useless. Guizhou is used as a concrete case for the [[EngineeringState]] question: when a state can build spectacular infrastructure quickly, what feedback ensures that roads, bridges, airports, health care, schooling, transfers, sanitation, and daily public services are prioritized correctly?
