# Xin dynasty / 新朝

The Xin dynasty / 新朝 is [[WangMang|王莽]]'s short-lived regime in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]]. The episode treats it as the result of [[WesternHanDynasty|Western Han]] legitimacy turning against the Liu house through [[DynasticLegitimacyBackfire]], [[ChenweiPolitics]], and [[AuspiciousOmenPolitics]].

Its collapse is used as a political lesson. [[WangMangReforms|王莽改制]], frontier symbolic aggression, currency stress, forced mobilization, famine, and uprisings by [[ChimeiRebellion|赤眉]] and [[LulinRebellion|绿林]] made the regime's sacred claims increasingly detached from administrative reality.

## Key Claims
- The regime was born through sacred signs, court procedure, and Confucian legitimacy language.
- Its reform program was expansive but poorly grounded in implementation capacity.
- Its downfall links ideological confidence to fiscal disorder, frontier instability, and revolt.
