# 李嘉佳 / Li Jiajia

Li Jiajia is the [[GangganghaoYingshi]] representative in [[269-zhenren-duanju-de-xiayizhan-yu-ai-gongsheng-gongyehua-he-hao-gushi-lmwhgqd0rge4mr0fwp5qpr2scwxt]]. She speaks for the quality-led side of [[LiveActionShortDrama]], where character relationships, motivation, actor fit, and single-project upside matter more than maximizing project count.

Her central method is [[CharacterRelationshipStoryLogic]]: relationships determine motivation, motivation determines events, and events determine story. She uses [[ZhenQianjinTaShiXueba]] to explain why changing a protagonist's family relationship changes the whole story engine, rather than merely adding or removing "revenge" or "sweet romance" tropes.

Li also draws a boundary around AI. She agrees AI can iterate quickly and help with fantasy or large visual scenes, but argues that subtle relationship states, actor micro-expression, and collaborative "神来之笔" from directors, performers, editing, costumes, and art direction still give live action a defensible space.
