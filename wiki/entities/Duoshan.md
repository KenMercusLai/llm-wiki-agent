# Duoshan

Duoshan appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as ByteDance's social-app attempt during the period when multiple products were publicly framed as challengers to [[WeChat]]. The hosts compare it with Bullet Messenger and Matong MT in the "three against WeChat" moment.

The episode treats Duoshan as an example of ByteDance overextending from traffic and video into social before the new product had proven a relationship-level job. It also links Duoshan to later lawsuits around WeChat/QQ avatar and nickname data, making it part of the longer platform-boundary dispute.
