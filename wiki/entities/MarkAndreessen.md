# Mark Andreessen

[[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] adds Andreessen through [[Netscape]]. [[RonConway]] says [[JimClark]] found Andreessen and other University of Illinois engineers working on a browser and brought them to Silicon Valley, turning Netscape into the [[BrowserAsInternetUnlock]] case for the early internet. Conway also credits Andreessen, [[MikeHomer]], and Netscape engineers with helping normalize open-source technologies that produced an [[OpenSourceStartupCostCollapse]] for internet startups.

Mark Andreessen appears in [[socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8-socialradarsseason2-parkerconrad-v8]] as part of [[ParkerConrad]]'s account of the [[Zenefits]] aftermath. Conrad says [[SamAltman]] spoke with Andreessen about attacks against Conrad and warned that they were damaging [[AndreessenHorowitz]]'s relationship with [[YCombinator]].

In the source, Andreessen is important because the alleged intervention changed the conflict environment. Conrad says that after Andreessen spoke with [[DavidSacks]], the orchestrated negative stories stopped, even though bad press did not disappear entirely.
