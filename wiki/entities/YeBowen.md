# Ye Bowen

Ye Bowen is a student and independent developer who won the overall award at the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] with [[PartyGuitar]] in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. He had little formal music background, but his team designed a device that lets beginners produce a musical atmosphere by working from chord groups instead of individual notes.

The episode uses Ye's project to show how a polished 48-hour demo can create customer curiosity, stage confidence, and brand ambition. Ye argues that surface copying is possible, but the harder-to-copy part is the product's underlying soul: the design taste and musical interaction model.
