# Ferdinand de Saussure / 索绪尔

Ferdinand de Saussure appears in [[57-shuojie-hanzi-shiniaopi-weisha-shi-shi-zitou-642970319]] as the theorist cited for distinguishing language and writing as different systems. The episode uses this distinction to argue that Chinese characters should not be collapsed into pronunciation alone or into a naive picture-writing account.

His role in the source is to support [[HanziTransdialectalFunction]]. Because writing and speech are not identical, a stable written form can carry communication across different spoken varieties while still having its own historical, visual, and cultural logic.
