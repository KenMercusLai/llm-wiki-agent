# Brooks

Brooks is a running-footwear brand discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as a comfort-oriented slow-running shoe brand and historical running-shoe participant. The source says the racing-shoe wave pressures brands like Brooks to build competitive products even if their strongest identity is everyday fit and running comfort.

## Key Claims
- Brooks is grouped with [[NewBalance]], [[ASICS]], and [[Saucony]] as a slow-running shoe brand whose appeal depends on fit, last shape, and comfort.
- The episode contrasts this comfort heritage with the visibility that elite carbon-plate racing shoes receive.
- Smaller brand scale can limit top-athlete sponsorship and R&D intensity compared with [[Nike]] and [[Adidas]].
