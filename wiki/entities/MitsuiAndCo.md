# Mitsui & Co. / 三井物产

Mitsui & Co. appears in [[vol-108-riben-wuda-zonghe-shangshe-zhongfan-wutai-zhongyang-lhamp3hn14iihcicpa8po5dvh6gq]] as a core [[JapaneseSogoShosha|Japanese sogo shosha]] with deep zaibatsu roots. The episode treats Mitsui as an example of how trading companies combine metals, energy, machinery, finance, transport, and industrial relationships rather than operating as a single-sector resource play.

The source uses Mitsui's steel-related investments to explain [[TradingCompanyInvestmentModel|business investment]] and vertical integration. Mitsui can appear upstream in mining, midstream in processing, downstream in demand, and around the chain through transport and financing, creating a "Mitsui everywhere" coordination pattern.

Mitsui is also used in the episode's information-network section. The host mentions a Mitsui global communications system to show why sogo shosha are often associated with intelligence gathering, while warning against over-mystifying these commercial information functions as spycraft.
