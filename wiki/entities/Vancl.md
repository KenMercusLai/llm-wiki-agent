# 凡客 / Vancl

Vancl is the apparel ecommerce and self-owned brand case in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode says Vancl built from stronger warehousing and brand control than [[PPG]], made a major cultural splash through its advertising, then over-expanded categories and SKUs until inventory and organization pressure damaged the business.
