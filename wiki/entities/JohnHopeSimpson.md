# John Hope Simpson

John Hope Simpson appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as a British expert involved in the 1931 flood relief system. The episode cites him on refugee movement, including the view that many people in [[Wuhan]] were fleeing war as much as floodwater.

The source also uses Simpson's letters and estimates to show how international experts helped quantify relief while still operating inside elite assumptions and incomplete field knowledge. That makes him part of both [[DisasterResponseStateCapacity]] and [[DisasterReliefMismatch]].
