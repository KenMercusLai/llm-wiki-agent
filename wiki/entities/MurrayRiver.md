# Murray River

The Murray River appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the irrigation source connected to [[CarlyMarriott]]'s family farm in [[NewSouthWales]]. The episode uses that setting to make [[WaterRightsTrading]] concrete: drought, dams, allocation rules, and crop choices all shape the value of water.

Within the source, the river stands for the physical constraint behind the market. [[WaterMarketDesign]] can reallocate legal claims to water, but the underlying scarcity is still driven by rainfall, storage, agricultural demand, and [[ClimateAdaptation]] pressure.
