# Baillie College / 贝利学院

Baillie College / 贝利学院 is the Oxford college plot node in [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]]'s discussion of [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]]. The episode identifies it as [[HumphreyAppleby|Humphrey Appleby]]'s college and uses it to show how education prestige, government subsidy, foreign-student fees, and administrative self-interest become one satirical mechanism.

The plot turns on [[JamesHacker|Hacker]]'s plan to charge foreign students full tuition and reduce public subsidy. Baillie College fears damage to its student intake and status, while Hacker discovers that an honorary doctorate can make the college's interests feel like public policy.

This makes the college central to [[HonoursPatronageExchange]]: budget savings, policy exemptions, honorary degrees, and civil-service honours can be traded while everyone retains formal language about principle.
