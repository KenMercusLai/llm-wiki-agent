# Ruth Benedict / 本尼狄克特

Ruth Benedict appears in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]] as one of the [[ColumbiaUniversity]] anthropologists around [[HenriettaSchmerler]]. The episode says Benedict encouraged Schmerler to build an independent fieldwork plan after an earlier summer project fell through, placing her in the professional path that led to the [[WhiteMountainApache|White Mountain Apache]] reservation.

The source's criticism concerns Benedict's reaction after the murder. In the episode's account, Benedict and [[MargaretMead]] told [[FranzBoas]] that Schmerler had not followed advice, and Benedict later declined to testify while prioritizing ongoing Apache research access. That makes her a complicated figure in [[DisciplinarySelfProtectionAfterHarm]], not simply a mentor or antagonist.
