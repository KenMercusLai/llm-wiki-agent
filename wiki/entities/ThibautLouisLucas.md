# Thibaut-Louis Lucas

Thibaut-Louis Lucas is the founder of [[TeaMaker]] and the central guest in [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]]. The episode presents him as a founder who moved from failed venture-backed startups and debt into rapid product experimentation, [[TweetHunter]], [[Tapio]], an acquisition by [[Lempire]], and then a bootstrapped SaaS holding-company model.

## Key Claims
- Lucas describes his early startup failures as a lesson in [[FounderEgo]], fundraising before validation, and the danger of mistaking capital for product-market fit.
- His product-building method relies on [[FastProductValidation]], revenue, recurring usage, and [[CustomerPull]] rather than launch attention or vanity metrics.
- After the [[Lempire]] earnout, he shifted toward [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]], helping multiple products grow through shared SEO, ads, influencer, and audience systems.

## Connections
- [[TeaMaker]] - company Lucas founded.
- [[TweetHunter]], [[Tapio]], [[Revid]], and [[Outrank]] - products tied to his company-building path.
- [[JKMolina]] and [[AlexBerman]] - distribution partners for earlier social-network products.
- [[SaaSHoldingCompany]], [[CustomerPull]], and [[FastProductValidation]] - operating concepts associated with his approach.
