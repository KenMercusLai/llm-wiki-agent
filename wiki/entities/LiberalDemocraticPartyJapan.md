# Liberal Democratic Party (Japan)

The Liberal Democratic Party (Japan) is the dominant Japanese party in [[snap-judgement-japan-pms-electoral-landslide-6989c0fc61be18927058bb7d]]. The episode says the LDP has dominated Japanese politics since 1955 with only brief interruptions, but it entered the snap election after scandals, short-lived prime ministers, weak national-election results, and coalition disruption.

The source presents the election as a reversal of that fragility. Under [[TakaichiSanae]], the LDP is said to have gained more than 100 lower-house seats and won 316 seats on its own, while the governing coalition controlled more than 350 of 465 seats. That turns the party from a weakened incumbent into the institutional platform for [[ElectoralMandate]].
