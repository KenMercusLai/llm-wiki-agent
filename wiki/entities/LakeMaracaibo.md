# Lake Maracaibo

Lake Maracaibo appears in [[chevron-venezuela-and-the-paradox-of-plenty]] as the site where Venezuelan oil was discovered more than a century ago. The episode says a 1922 gusher there sent oil spraying for nine days and drew foreign companies, machinery, geologists, refineries, and corporate infrastructure into [[Venezuela]].

In the wiki, the lake is the origin point for Venezuela's [[Petrostate]] branch. The discovery helped replace an agrarian coffee-export economy with oil exports, making [[DutchDisease]], [[OilRevenueDependence]], and later [[OilConcessionBargaining]] easier to understand as consequences of a specific historical shock rather than abstract categories.
