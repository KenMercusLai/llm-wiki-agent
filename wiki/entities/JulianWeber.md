# Julian Weber

Julian Weber appears in [[socialradarsseason1-pg-v4-updated-finallll-socialradarsseason1-pg-v4-updated-finallll]] as the early [[Viaweb]] investor whose $10,000 check, legal work, board role, and 10% ownership helped keep the company alive. [[PaulGraham]] says Viaweb would have died many times without him.

Weber matters to the wiki because his small-check-plus-help model became part of Graham's memory of what founder-friendly seed investing could look like. The episode frames him as a practical precursor to [[YCombinator]] and [[FounderInvestorLearning]]: the deal could be good for both founders and investor because the founders needed money, help, and legitimacy before institutional venture capital made sense.
