# May Department Stores

May Department Stores appears in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]] as the retail company where [[MaxineClark]] developed executive, merchandising, acquisition, and operations experience before founding [[BuildABear]]. The episode describes May as a large St. Louis-based retail environment that gave Maxine exposure to store data, buying logic, corporate strategy, and mall-era retail systems.

The entity matters because Build-A-Bear did not emerge from a novice outsider's toy idea alone. May supplied the operating context behind [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]], [[MallBasedRetailExpansion]], and [[RetailConceptProtection]] by teaching Maxine how retailers, landlords, vendors, lawyers, and customers interact.
