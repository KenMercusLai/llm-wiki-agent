# BP

BP appears in [[roaring-trades-oil-majors-secret-success-story-6a4636f160cad2674e6d9674]] as one of the European oil majors whose trading desk is treated as a hidden profit engine. The episode groups BP with [[Shell]] and [[TotalEnergies]], saying the companies trade much larger oil and gas volumes than they produce themselves and can earn large profits when volatility widens spreads.

The wiki significance is not BP's production profile alone, but the way its physical-market footprint feeds [[EnergyTradingScaleAdvantage]]. Refineries, terminals, storage, shipping access, and customer relationships can become information infrastructure for buying and reselling energy flows.
