# Longbridge

Longbridge is named in [[ep89-haiwai-quanshang-da-dizhen-kuajing-touzi-xinshidai-li8ya-r5cpz3sifdjby73vh9-rxs]] as part of the cross-border brokerage cleanup affecting platforms that served or marketed to mainland Chinese investors. The episode groups it with larger brokerage names to show that the issue is not only one company, but a platform category built around overseas securities access.

## Source Position
- Longbridge appears as part of the same cross-border brokerage set as [[FutuSecurities]] and [[TigerBrokers]].
- The episode's concern is less the app itself than the combination of mainland solicitation, investor classification, and lawful fund origin.
- It reinforces the broader claim that smaller or alternative brokers are not automatically safer if the user's identity and money route are still inconsistent.
