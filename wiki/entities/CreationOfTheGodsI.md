# 《封神》 / Creation of the Gods

《封神》 is the opening comparison in [[64-bawangbieji-fengmo-yu-chenghuo-656094350]]. The host says the film's marketing emphasized surface hooks such as King Zhou and Daji, but the actual film seemed stronger than the previews suggested, partly because [[LuWeiScreenwriter|芦苇]]'s involvement made the host curious.

The episode focuses on two interpretive points. First, it reads the film as rejecting the simple "Daji made King Zhou evil" explanation and placing responsibility back on power and King Zhou himself. Second, it treats Ji Chang's meat-pie and forced-recognition sequence as a tyranny scene about breaking father-son attachment and manufacturing public confession, which then becomes a bridge to [[CoercedDenunciation]] in [[FarewellMyConcubine|《霸王别姬》]].
