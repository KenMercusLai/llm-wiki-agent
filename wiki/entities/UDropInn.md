# U Drop Inn

The U Drop Inn is the restored gas station and diner in [[ShamrockTexas]] featured in [[putins-options-an-oligarch-speaks-out-6a50c5ebbafe2fa6a7f38210]]. The episode describes it as an art-deco Route 66 landmark and says it served as the model for Ramone's House of Body Art in Pixar's *Cars*.

In the wiki, the U Drop Inn adds a built-environment case to [[Route66NostalgiaTourism]]. It shows how a former roadside service business can become a heritage signal after the transport economy that sustained it has moved to the interstate system.
