# Aaron Sojourner

Aaron Sojourner appears in [[would-you-trust-an-economist-with-your-economy]] as the labor economist explaining why [[BureauOfLaborStatistics]] staff are trusted by economists and policymakers. He describes BLS statisticians as politically neutral civil servants focused on reliability, accuracy, and technical data production rather than partisan spin.

The episode also uses Sojourner to show how institutional trust can be damaged even among insiders. He calls the firing of the agency head a major escalation and says it softens his faith in the numbers, because public statistics depend on both technical process and the belief that political actors will not punish unwelcome results.
