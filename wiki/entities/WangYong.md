# Wang Yong

Wang Yong is a Deepin-era co-founder and later hardware-product founder discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode names him as one of the [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] founders and says he left before the [[TongxinSoftware]] formation.

## Source Position
- Wang is part of the technical and company-building stage around [[Deepin]].
- The episode says he did not want to be tightly bound to the later Tongxin direction and thought the company might fail at that time.
- His later home-server and edge-computing product is interpreted as a soft-hardware integration path after years of operating-system work.
- The source uses his later path to suggest that open-source desktop operating systems can face heavy adaptation costs and hard monetization.
