# James Damore

James Damore appears in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]] as a controversial candidate case for [[Triplebyte]]. [[HarjTaggar]] says Damore signed up, performed well on Triplebyte's test, and created an internal dispute over whether the platform should allow him to maintain a profile despite public controversy around his views.

The source's Damore section matters because it exposes a governance limit in [[ObjectiveHiringAssessment]]. Even if a test measures technical skill, a hiring platform still has to decide what kinds of non-technical controversy, workplace impact, customer reaction, and employee disagreement are relevant to candidate access. The episode gives Harj's retrospective account; it should not be treated as the full record of all participants' views.
