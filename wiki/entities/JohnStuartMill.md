# John Stuart Mill / 穆勒

John Stuart Mill / 穆勒 appears in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]] as the liberal reference point for asking when law may restrict self-regarding conduct. The episode invokes his freedom principle to test drug use, self-harm, helmets, seatbelts, and the question of voluntary slavery.

The source stresses that even Mill's liberalism contains a boundary: freedom cannot be used to erase one's future freedom entirely. That makes him central to [[LiberalismPaternalismBoundary]] rather than a simple anti-regulation slogan.
