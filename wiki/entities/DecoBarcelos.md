# Deco (Barcelos piabero)

Deco is the local fisherman followed by [[PlanetMoney]] in [[the-little-pet-fish-that-saved-a-town-in-the-amazon]]. The page keeps the name source-scoped because the episode summary identifies him by first name only; his role in the wiki is as the concrete piabero voice in [[Barcelos]].

Deco shows how the [[PiaberoEconomy]] worked at household scale. The episode says an individual fish brings him only a few cents, but on good days he could collect thousands of fish, and the trade helped him raise five children and send them to school. His later work as a sport-fishing guide shows the shift from [[CardinalTetra]] collection toward [[PeacockBass]] tourism.

## Key Claims
- Deco catches ornamental fish in the flooded forest near the [[RioNegro]].
- He says orders have fallen sharply and worries the work may disappear.
- The source uses his family history to link ornamental fish to an earlier Amazon rubber economy.
- His tourism work makes him a bridge between the older fish trade and [[HeritageTourismPivot]].
