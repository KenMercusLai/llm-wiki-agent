# Steven Spielberg / 斯皮尔伯格

Steven Spielberg enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]], where [[QinZong|秦总]] and [[Beimin|北明]] use a Spielberg UFO film as a friendly entry point into [[UFOConspiracyCulture]], [[CarlSagan|Carl Sagan]], and [[TheDemonHauntedWorld|《魔鬼出没的世界》]].

The episode does not treat the film as an argument about aliens. Its role is cultural: Spielberg-style wonder can make UFO and miracle motifs emotionally available, while [[ScientificSkepticism]] asks the audience to separate story pleasure from evidential belief.
