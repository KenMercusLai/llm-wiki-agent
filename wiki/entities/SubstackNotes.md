# Substack Notes

Substack Notes is the short-form feed and discovery product discussed by [[ChrisBest]] in [[tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov-tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov]]. Best presents it as part of [[Substack]]'s move from a paid newsletter tool toward an owned media network where readers can find independent voices inside the product.

The source makes Notes a [[StrategicMustWorkProductBet]]. Best says Notes did not work well for roughly two years and was used by only a small group of highly engaged users, but Substack kept working on it because discovery was essential to the company's long-term thesis. In his view, a professional manager might have killed the feed because the short-term evidence looked weak.

Notes also created the public conflict with [[ElonMusk]] and [[Twitter]] in Best's account. Best says he warned Musk before launch, Musk told him not to launch it, Substack launched anyway, and Twitter later restricted Substack-related visibility and links.
