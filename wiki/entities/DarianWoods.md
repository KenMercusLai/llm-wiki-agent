# Darian Woods

Darian Woods appears in [[indicators-of-2025-and-what-to-watch-in-2026]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] and [[TheIndicator]] participant pitching the [[CAPERatioValuationSignal|CAPE ratio]] as a 2025 indicator. The source presents his pitch with comic staging, but the economic point is serious: elevated stock valuations can concentrate anxiety around AI, data centers, and whether asset prices have run ahead of earnings.

Woods also qualifies his own argument. The episode says he admits the link between the CAPE ratio and low-income struggles is partly stretched, which makes his role useful for keeping [[AIEquityValuationRisk]] and [[KShapedConsumerSpending]] connected without pretending one market indicator explains all household stress.
