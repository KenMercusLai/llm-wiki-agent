# Nate Blecharczyk

Nate Blecharczyk is an [[Airbnb]] co-founder in [[socialradarspod-brianchesky-final]]. [[BrianChesky]] describes him as the technical co-founder who joined Chesky and [[JoeGebbia]] after the first hosted air-bed experiment and helped build a site where people could book homes like hotels.

In the source, Blecharczyk gives the team the engineering capacity needed to turn a design-led hosting insight into a marketplace product. He also appears in the YC decision arc: the team had to persuade him to join [[YCombinator]] and move across the country if accepted, making the accelerator decision a whole-team commitment rather than only a fundraising event.

[[socialradarspod-brianchesky2-final]] adds that Blecharczyk agreed to move from Boston for YC when Airbnb was nearly out of options. The second episode also credits him with building early distribution tooling, including a way for hosts to repost Airbnb listings to Craigslist, making him part of the product and distribution iteration behind the marketplace.
