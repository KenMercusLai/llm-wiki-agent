# What's Next｜科技早知道

[[google-de-ai-celve-bu-du-moxing-du-shenme-google-cloud-next-xianchang-s10e09-073d7ee7-7bac-4958-b45a-083cc2f866e6]] adds the show's Google Cloud Next branch. The episode connects [[Google]], [[GoogleCloud]], [[TPU]], [[Gemini]], and enterprise agents to the show's existing AI-work and AI-terminal discussions, with the main emphasis on [[FullStackAIPlatform]], [[EnterpriseAgentGovernance]], and [[CapabilityOverhang]].

[[wwdc-26-bu-shang-le-ai-dan-li-zhenzheng-de-ai-zhushou-hai-cha-shenme-s10e15-9ab1512e-a4a8-4ea6-81b5-0ac7ec677d2d]] adds the show's Apple-and-wearable assistant branch through [[DongHongguang]] of [[GuangfanTechnology]].
