# Jasmine Sun

Jasmine Sun appears in [[tech-20260119-0119-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260119-0119-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the independent technology journalist whose [[NewYorkTimes|New York Times]] reporting grounds [[MarketplaceTech]]'s episode on Silicon Valley peptide use. Her interview connects cheaper [[GLP1Agonists]], [[Ozempic]] demand, research-chemical labeling, Chinese peptide suppliers, and founder-style risk tolerance into a single [[TechCultureBiohacking]] story.

Her contribution is mainly explanatory. She distinguishes legal permissibility from medical assurance: a substance may be outside the DEA-controlled-drug list while still lacking [[FoodAndDrugAdministration|FDA]] approval, known contents, clean manufacturing standards, human clinical evidence, or safe dosing context.
