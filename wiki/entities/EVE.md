# EVE

EVE is [[NaturalSelection]]'s AI game/companion product discussed in [[135-he-ziran-xuanze-chuangshiren-tristan-liao-elys-saibo-fenshen-linghun-context-de-huoqu-yu-liudong-he-ai-shejiao-wangluo-ltwegwvo7grn-v-rft0txlmqmcty]]. [[Tristan]] presents it as the continuation of a Project Her idea: an AI companion that has memory, persona, interaction, and emotional presence rather than a fixed script.

The source positions EVE as the product where Tristan's earlier experience with romance games, character design, content loops, and AI ability came together. It is part of the wiki's [[AIFriendProducts]] branch, but the episode also uses it as a contrast case: EVE creates virtual companionship, while [[Elys]] uses AI as an intermediate layer for real-person connection.

## Key Points
- The name is linked to Eve and the idea of creating silicon life.
- GPT-4 is described as the moment when scripted personas could begin to feel like living, interactive "souls."
- EVE and [[Elys]] share some memory and recommendation infrastructure, but differ in product shape: companion person versus social field.
- EVE's product thesis remains connected to [[ProactiveAgents]] and [[ContextEngineering]], because the AI should understand, remember, and initiate rather than only answer.
