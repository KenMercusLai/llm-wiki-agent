# Axio

Axio is the [[Alibaba]] international-business AI product discussed by [[ZhangKuo]] in [[e231-cong-b2b-dao-a2a-agent-xin-jijian-ruhe-rang-yiren-qiye-zuo-quanqiu-shengyi-0f4a2ab9-d3a0-41ad-8db1-6c03c851bd70]]. The source presents Axio as a vertical sourcing agent rather than a generic chatbot: it moves from product idea and research into design packs, supplier matching, communication, transaction, logistics, after-sales, and eventually daily operation.

The episode says Axio reached 10 million monthly active users by March 2026 and claims that agentic sourcing can shorten sourcing communication time to around one-fifth. Axio Work is the extension of the same system into a desktop workbench for small businesses, with storefront setup, product publishing, [[Shopify]] operations, inventory, customer service, replenishment, and partner subagents for HR, payroll, finance, and tax.

## Source Position
- Axio is the episode's concrete case for [[AgenticB2BSourcing]] and [[B2BToA2A]].
- It depends on [[Alibaba]]'s long B2B marketplace history, daily communication signals, product listings, search/index tools, transaction data, and supplier capability information.
- The product uses [[Qwen]] and other specialized models for multimodal, coding, and agent reasoning tasks.
- Zhang frames Axio's moat as a data and feedback loop: ideas, design choices, margin decisions, transactions, repeat purchases, and failures can all train or evaluate the workflow.
- Axio Work turns the sourcing agent into an [[AIAsBusinessOperator]] candidate for nontechnical small businesses.
