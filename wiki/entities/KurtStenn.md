# Kurt Stenn / 库尔特·斯坦

Kurt Stenn is the hair researcher and author of [[HairBook|《头发》 / Hair]] discussed in [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]]. The episode introduces him as an authority on hair research, formerly associated with Yale medical-school dermatology and pathology and with skin-biology work at Johnson & Johnson.

His role in the source is less biographical than methodological. By using Stenn's book, the episode treats hair as a topic that can move from follicles, skin, growth cycles, and [[HairLossFollicleCycle]] into [[HairAsPoliticalIdentity]], [[GenderedHairRegulation]], barber history, and [[ForensicHairEvidence]] without treating those as separate trivia piles.
