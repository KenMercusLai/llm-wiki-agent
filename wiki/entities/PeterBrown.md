# Peter Brown

Peter Brown appears in [[vol-103-wenyi-fuxing-keji-ximengsi-de-fengshen-zhilu-shi-lianghua-zhiwang-gengshi-dongcha-renxing-de-dashi-lulzvnaxdb4klqag-p2yyqlmiikl]] as one of the IBM speech-recognition researchers recruited into [[RenaissanceTechnologies]] for the stock-market expansion. The episode presents Brown and [[RobertMercer]] as examples of [[JimSimons]]'s preference for top technical minds from outside finance, on the theory that scientific pattern-recognition skills could transfer into markets.

Brown's source role is to connect language modeling habits with stock modeling. Both domains involve noisy sequences, hidden states, probabilities, and constant updating from data, which made speech-recognition experience relevant to [[QuantitativeInvesting]].
