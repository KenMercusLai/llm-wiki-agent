# Port Chibatao

Port Chibatao is the [[Manaus]] port used in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] to show how Amazon manufacturing depends on long supply chains. The episode describes containers reaching Manaus after traveling up the Amazon River, often carrying electronic components that factories assemble into finished goods.

The port is not just scenery in the source. It is the place where [[SubsidizedAssemblyIndustrialization]] becomes measurable: [[JohnnyFidelisSantos]] says much of the cargo comes from Asia, which helps explain why Manaus can look like a high-tech manufacturing hub while still depending on imported high-value parts.
