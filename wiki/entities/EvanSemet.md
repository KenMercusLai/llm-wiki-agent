# Evan Semet

Evan Semet appears in [[do-prediction-market-bettors-make-anything-better]] as a [[Kalshi]] trader who says he was making close to $100,000 a month on the platform while also expressing doubts about the social value of many markets.

His role is important because he is not an outside critic. The episode uses him to show that successful traders can benefit from [[PredictionMarketTraderAlpha]] while still seeing a darker side: if profits come from other users throwing money away, the [[PredictionMarketPublicGoodClaim]] becomes harder to defend as a general description of the product.

## Connections
- [[Kalshi]] - platform where he trades.
- [[PredictionMarketTraderAlpha]] - profit-side concept grounded by his case.
- [[PredictionMarketPublicGoodClaim]], [[PredictionMarketEthics]], and [[PredictionMarketLegalBoundary]] - concepts his doubts complicate.
