# Robinhood

Robinhood is used in [[e44-li-xiaobo-duihua-mengyan-zheci-jiu-zheyang-ba-lpcrvfgnseaed-eambk9ofnvuq2]] as a contrast case for [[FinancialPlatformIncentives]]. [[MengYan]] discusses Robinhood alongside low-cost wealth-management companies to show that user-friendly rhetoric and low explicit commissions do not settle the incentive question if the platform benefits from trading volume, options usage, or behavior that makes users act more often.

The episode does not treat Robinhood mainly as a product-design case. It treats it as a warning that take rate, business model, and user behavior can point a financial company toward very different outcomes even when the user interface feels simple.
