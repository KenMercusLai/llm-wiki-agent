# Adam Silver

Adam Silver appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as the [[NationalBasketballAssociation|NBA]] commissioner publicly framing tanking as an incentives problem. The source uses his comments to move the discussion away from blaming single teams and toward redesigning the rules that reward losing.

Silver's role in the episode is institutional. He represents the league's need to protect fan trust, late-season game quality, and [[SportsEntertainmentFlywheel|sports entertainment]] value while preserving enough [[SportsCompetitiveBalance]] for weak teams to improve.
