# ANUA

ANUA is one of the Korean beauty brands named in [[137-cong-shunde-zhuroupo-dao-hanguo-shengshuidong-naxie-ai-wufa-qudai-de-tiyan-xiaofei-feng-tou-quan-1-137-1]]. The hosts present it as a typical TikTok-era Korean beauty brand, useful for understanding [[KBeautyGlobalTrust]] and Korea's ability to make beauty products feel globally credible.

For the wiki, ANUA is less a standalone company analysis than a signal of the Korean beauty system: social video, skincare routines, influencer distribution, and Korean aesthetic trust can help a brand travel beyond its home market.

## Source Position
- ANUA is used as an example of K-Beauty's rapid global growth.
- The episode contrasts Korean beauty's global permission with earlier Chinese beauty brands that were more domestically anchored.
- The brand supports the source's claim that cosmetics fit Korea's strengths in emotion, beauty authority, celebrity attention, and marketing.
