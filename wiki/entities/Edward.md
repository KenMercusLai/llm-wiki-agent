# Edward

Edward is an artist-turned-designer and one of the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] contestants interviewed in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. He describes himself as "partnering with AI" rather than independently developing because he treats AI as part of the working team.

His team built [[Atoom]], a software-track second-prize product. The source uses Edward's account to argue that after [[VibeCoding]] accelerates implementation, idea selection, design taste, and aesthetic judgment become more important constraints.
