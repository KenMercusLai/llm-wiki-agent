# William Lawrence / 威廉·劳伦斯

William Lawrence appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as a doctor connected to [[PercyShelley|Percy Shelley / 珀西·雪莱]] and to the period's [[VitalismLifePrincipleDebate|vitalism debate]]. The source presents Lawrence as arguing that life arises from the organization and complexity of the body rather than from a separate life fluid.

His role helps the episode place [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] inside live scientific dispute. Victor's animation problem is not framed as a modern laboratory protocol but as a literary response to a period still arguing over whether life was material organization, special principle, electricity, or divine mystery.
