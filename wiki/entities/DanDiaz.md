# Dan Diaz / 丹·迪阿斯

Dan Diaz is discussed in [[119-anlesi-xianchang-zhengyin-shengming-ruci-zhengui-770771087]] as [[BrittanyMaynard]]'s husband and supporter. The episode emphasizes his care for Brittany, his rejection of "suicide" as the correct frame for her death, and his later work pushing California toward a dignity-death law.

For the wiki, Dan's role makes [[FamilyEthicsAtEndOfLife]] more complicated than either abandonment or heroic support. He is presented as a family member who loves the patient and still supports her chosen legal route, contrasting with [[RamonSampedro]]'s family conflict.
