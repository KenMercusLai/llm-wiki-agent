# Cadence Design Systems / 楷登

Cadence Design Systems appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] as one of the global EDA companies that shape chip design practice. The source groups Cadence with [[Synopsys]] and [[SiemensEDA|Siemens EDA]] to show that semiconductor dependence can sit in design software before a design ever reaches a fab.

The episode's interpretation is that EDA tools accumulate advantage through many years of engineering edge cases, customer feedback, process compatibility, and verification knowledge. That makes replacement difficult even when domestic chip designers have stronger talent and demand.
