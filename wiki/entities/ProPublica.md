# ProPublica

ProPublica appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the reporting outlet cited for details about the [[AlexPreti]] case. The episode says ProPublica reported that the agents who shot and killed Preti had been employed since 2014 and 2018.

In this source, ProPublica extends the wiki's accountability-journalism branch from procurement records and surveillance vendors into direct reporting on federal enforcement conduct. The page should remain tied to what the episode cites unless later sources add more detail.
