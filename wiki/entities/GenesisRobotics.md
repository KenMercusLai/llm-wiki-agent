# Genesis Robotics

Genesis Robotics appears in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] as a company that began around robot simulation and later moved toward dexterous manipulation and full-stack robot systems. The episode uses its cube-rotation, food-handling, cooking, and piano demos to show how [[DexterousManipulation]] is becoming a visible model and data frontier.

The source says Genesis publicly claimed about 200,000 hours of collected data, but [[ChenZhePeter]] still treats the field as early, similar to the Aloha behavior-cloning stage two years earlier.
