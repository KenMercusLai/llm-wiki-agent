# Arash Ferdowsi

Arash Ferdowsi is the co-founder of [[Dropbox]], introduced in [[tsr-s4-drewhouston-v5-tsr-s4-drewhouston-v5]] through [[KyleVogt]]'s MIT connection to [[DrewHouston]]. Houston expected to need a long persuasion process, but the episode says Ferdowsi dropped out within days to join Dropbox before the [[YCombinator]] interview.

Ferdowsi's role in the source is mainly co-founder validation. His quick commitment helped satisfy [[PaulGraham]]'s co-founder concern, and the stolen-laptop story immediately after YC acceptance became an accidental demonstration that Dropbox already protected the founders' own files.
