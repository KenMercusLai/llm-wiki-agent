# 程蝶衣 / Cheng Dieyi

程蝶衣 is the adult stage identity of Xiaodouzi in [[FarewellMyConcubine|《霸王别姬》]], as read by [[64-bawangbieji-fengmo-yu-chenghuo-656094350]]. The episode presents him as a character made through violent apprenticeship: his mother cuts off his sixth finger, the troupe disciplines his body, Zhang Gonggong abuses him, and [[DuanXiaolou|段小楼]] forces the line "我本是女娇娥" into his mouth with a hot pipe.

The source treats Dieyi's "虞姬" identity as both artistic greatness and wound. His love for Duan Xiaolou, hostility toward [[Juxian|菊仙]], loyalty under Japanese occupation, courtroom refusal to lie, resistance to crude new-opera directives, and final suicide all express [[RoleLifeCollapse]], [[GenderPerformanceAndTrauma]], and [[ArtDignityUnderPoliticalPressure]].
