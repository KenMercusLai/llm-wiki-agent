# Vannette Ho

Vannette Ho is the [[Financo]] investment banker who cold-called [[JoeyShamah]] in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] about running a sale process for [[ELFCosmetics]]. Joey initially refused a retainer, and the episode says she later agreed to waive it.

Her role matters because the eventual [[TSGConsumerPartners]] minority deal gave Joey financial security and outside guidance while preserving family control.
