# Buena Vista Distribution

Buena Vista Distribution is the Disney distribution arm created in 1953 and discussed in [[the-walt-disney-company-1]]. The episode presents it as a move away from reliance on outside distributors once [[TheWaltDisneyCompany]] had enough working capital and operating stability to control more of its own film distribution.

Buena Vista connects the Oswald lesson to later strategy. Losing [[OswaldTheLuckyRabbit]] showed the danger of customer/distributor dependence; decades later, direct distribution became another node in [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] and the [[EntertainmentIPFlywheel]].
