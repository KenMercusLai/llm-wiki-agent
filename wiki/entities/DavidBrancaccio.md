# David Brancaccio

David Brancaccio appears in [[tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[MarketplaceTech]] reporter whose own home was destroyed in the L.A. wildfires and who is rebuilding with future fires in mind. His role makes the episode more than an abstract construction explainer: the source uses his house to compare [[CrossLaminatedTimber]], mineral wool insulation, stucco, and other [[FireResistantBuildingEnvelope]] choices.

Brancaccio's account also carries the design and climate tradeoff. He frames wood-based mass timber as faster to assemble and potentially lower carbon if the house lasts long enough, while still acknowledging concrete-heavy alternatives, [[ThreeDPrintedHousing]], and the emotional problem of preserving neighborhood character through [[BiophilicDesign]] and references to the home that was lost.
