# Jane von Mehren

Jane von Mehren appears in [[inside-a-book-auction]] as one of the literary agents who developed and sold the [[PlanetMoney]] book project. With [[LauraNolan]], she helped shape the concept, build the proposal, generate publisher attention, and design the auction process.

The source uses Jane's work to show that agents are market makers as well as representatives. She and Laura used publisher relationships, early buzz, meeting orchestration, and competitive bidding format to make the book visible as a serious acquisition target.

## Key Claims
- Jane helped identify Planet Money's prior audience behavior as evidence that a book could sell.
- She worked with [[AlexGoldmark]], [[AlexMaiassi]], and [[LauraNolan]] over about a year on proposal material.
- She helped compare first-round bids across advances, rights packages, and publisher fit before narrowing the field.
