# Stargate AI Infrastructure

Stargate AI Infrastructure is the large U.S. AI infrastructure plan discussed in [[vol-265-kuayue-50-nian-de-meiguo-banben-zhizi-1001004591]]. The episode says [[DonaldTrump]] launched the plan at the [[WhiteHouse]] soon after taking office, with [[Oracle]] positioned as a core architecture participant.

The source treats Stargate as one of the first visible payoffs from [[LarryEllison]]'s political access. It does not say AI infrastructure demand is purely political; it explicitly notes that the AI cycle itself is lifting the sector. Its narrower claim is that policy access and procurement position can matter when scarce compute, power, and data-center capacity become strategic assets.
