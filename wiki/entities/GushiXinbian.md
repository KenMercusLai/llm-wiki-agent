# 《故事新编》

《故事新编》 is the Lu Xun collection discussed in [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]]. The episode presents it as a late, uneven, and deliberately slippery body of myth rewriting rather than as a solemn school-text monument. Its ancient stories keep being invaded by modern scholars, officials, political speech, and social habits.

The source focuses on [[LiShui|《理水》]] and [[QiSi|《起死》]] rather than the more familiar 《铸剑》 or 《非攻》. That choice lets the episode connect [[LuXun|鲁迅]]'s humor to [[GushiBian|古史辨]], [[DisasterAestheticization]], [[Zhuangzi|庄子]], Daoist afterlives, and [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]].

- [[QiSi|《起死》]] - skull-resurrection story centered on [[Zhuangzi|庄子]], [[YangDa|杨大]], and practical survival.
