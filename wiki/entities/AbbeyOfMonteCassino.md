# 卡西诺修道院 / Abbey of Monte Cassino

卡西诺修道院 / Abbey of Monte Cassino is the historic monastery discussed in [[03-laiboweici-de-zange-yao-you-guang-napa-feitu-zhishang-528163687]] as part of [[WalterMMillerJr|Walter M. Miller Jr.]]'s wartime background. The episode says Miller participated in the Allied bombing campaign around the monastery and treats the destruction as psychologically central to the later novel.

In the source, Monte Cassino becomes a bridge between history and fiction. A sacred archive-like building is destroyed by modern war, and [[CanticleForLeibowitz|《莱博维茨的赞歌》]] later imagines a post-nuclear monastery trying to preserve knowledge through another civilizational ruin. That makes the abbey important to [[ApocalypticLiterarySymbolism]] and [[PostApocalypticCivilizationPreservation]].
