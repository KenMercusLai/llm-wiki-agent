# Kate Durian

Kate Durian appears in [[the-secret-meeting-that-launched-opec]] as an OPEC watcher explaining the [[UnitedArabEmirates|United Arab Emirates]] exit from [[OPEC]]. The episode uses her analysis to connect quota disputes, decarbonization pressure, and regional security conflict.

Durian's main conceptual contribution in the source is the [[GreenParadox]] framing. If oil demand may fall over the long run, a producer with spare capacity has an incentive to pump more now rather than leave barrels underground. The source says UAE quota frustration with [[SaudiArabia]] and disruption around the [[StraitOfHormuz]] made that incentive politically harder to keep inside OPEC discipline.
