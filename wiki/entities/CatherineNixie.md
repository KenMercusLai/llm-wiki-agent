# Catherine Nixie

Catherine Nixie is the critic/interpreter in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]]'s segment on [[ChristopherNolan]]'s [[TheOdyssey]]. The source presents her as arguing that the film's scenery, psychology, and moral framing reveal the modern West as much as ancient Greece.

Her role in the wiki is to anchor the source's [[HomericAdaptationModernization]] claim. Nixie's critique is not only that the film changes plot details; it is that the adaptation makes [[Odysseus]] more comfortable for present-day audiences by muting the ancient text's moral and cultural distance.
