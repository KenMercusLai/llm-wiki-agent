# Tongxin UOS

Tongxin UOS is the commercial operating-system product discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says UOS stands for Union OS, or "unified operating system," and contrasts it with [[Deepin]] as the community edition that continues alongside the government-enterprise product.

## Source Position
- UOS is framed as the product form that lets [[TongxinSoftware]] serve [[XinchuangOperatingSystems]] customers.
- The episode emphasizes hardware and architecture adaptation, including domestic hardware environments outside ordinary x86 consumer PCs.
- Its demand is described as procurement- and deployment-driven rather than consumer-pull driven.
- The product helps explain why Tongxin's organization may lean toward sales, delivery, certification, and client-facing discipline.
