# United Airlines

United Airlines appears in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]] as one of [[HeirloomCarbonTechnologies|Heirloom Carbon Technologies]]' named customers, alongside [[Microsoft]], [[Meta]], and [[Shopify]]. The source uses the customer list to show that corporate demand for [[CarbonRemoval]] can help finance [[DirectAirCapture]] even before the technology reaches low-cost scale.

United's role in this source is narrow and source-scoped. The episode does not analyze the airline's broader climate strategy; it treats the company as evidence that hard-to-abate buyers may be willing to pay for removal while the [[CarbonRemovalCostCurve]] remains expensive.

[[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] adds United as one of the legacy carriers whose basic-economy offerings and loyalty advantages helped pressure [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]]. In this source, United is part of the incumbent-airline set connected to [[BasicEconomyCopycatStrategy]] and [[AirlineLoyaltyProgramMoat]].
