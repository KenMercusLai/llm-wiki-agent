# 龟龟

龟龟 is the [[YingdiHaike]] guest in [[ep122-yongyou-yiliang-fangche-shi-zhong-shenme-yang-de-tiyan-lhposkcf-otqhppghn1yjvpjgsrd]]. He bought a secondhand [[BTypeRVMotorhome]] for roughly 300,000 RMB, then used it for trips to Jingdezhen and from Hangzhou to Shenyang.

His case matters because he treats RV ownership as a practical operating system rather than as scenery. He weighs rent versus buy, secondhand depreciation, pet travel, winter diesel, frozen water systems, parking limits, gray/black water disposal, and 5G networking before deciding who the lifestyle fits.

## Key Claims

- Buying secondhand can make sense when rental cost, holiday use, pet logistics, and vehicle customization needs are high.
- A B-type RV can fit two people plus pets while preserving better driving feel and city passability than larger RV formats.
- RV travel is not necessarily cheaper or more comfortable than hotels after depreciation, setup work, and maintenance are included.
- The best first test is to rent an RV briefly and evaluate both driving and sleeping conditions.
