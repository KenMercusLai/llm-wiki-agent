# Northvolt

Northvolt appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as the European counterexample in the episode's battery-industry comparison. The source says Northvolt had a strong team background, very large financing, and large order commitments, yet still failed to build a durable manufacturing system.

The episode uses Northvolt to argue that imported equipment and capital do not replace [[BatteryManufacturingKnowHow]]. In this framing, process design, supplier depth, production debugging, and long-term factory iteration are the missing layers.
