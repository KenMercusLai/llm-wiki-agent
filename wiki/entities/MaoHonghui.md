# Mao Honghui / 毛红汇

Mao Honghui is the piaohao figure discussed alongside [[LeiLutai]] in [[no-209-jinshang-wangshi-zou-xikou-dao-qiaojia-dayuan-ranhou-mei-le-999367322]]. The episode says Mao played an important role in the preparation and early development of [[Rishengchang]] before conflicts over personnel and expansion pushed him toward a rival merchant house.

The source uses Mao to show that [[ShanxiPiaohao]] diffusion was not only planned imitation. Internal disagreement, talent mobility, and neighboring merchant competition could spread a business model across firms, even when the original conflict was personally and organizationally costly.

## Key Claims
- Mao's role complicates a simple Lei Lutai origin story for Rishengchang.
- His move to another merchant house helped create rival piaohao capacity.
- The episode frames competition as a byproduct of governance conflict as well as market demand.
