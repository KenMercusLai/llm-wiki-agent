# Emily Bordner

Emily Bordner is the founder of [[EBCo]] and a caller in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. Her Advice Line question is whether to keep focusing on brick-and-mortar retail, which still produces most of the company's revenue, or put more deliberate effort into wholesale, which has recently grown much faster.

The episode frames Emily's decision as both a channel-economics question and a founder-life question. She enjoys building physical store experiences where customers can try products away from screens, while wholesale may scale with less personal energy than opening more stores.
