# 《0.5 的男人》 / 0.5 no Otoko

《0.5 的男人》 / 0.5 no Otoko is the Japanese drama [[FuYu|傅宇]] discusses in [[vol-102-aoguo-jiuye-bingheqi-de-riben-nianqingren-qu-nali-xunzhao-xingfugan-chuantai-qingdao-kuaima-lmpluij7rcfq49cxcyb65x3f7zfn]]. The protagonist is described as similar to a hidden youth or NEET: staying at home, sleeping during the day, going out at night, and spending much of life around games.

The episode uses the drama to discuss de-stigmatization. The point is not to romanticize withdrawal, but to question whether cutting withdrawn people away from "normal" society helps anyone understand the pressure, injury, or low-energy self-protection behind the retreat.
