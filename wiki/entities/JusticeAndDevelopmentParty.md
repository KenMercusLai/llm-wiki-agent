# Justice and Development Party

The Justice and Development Party is [[RecepTayyipErdogan]]'s party context in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]]. The source says party leaders publicly avoid discussing a future without Erdogan, while behind closed doors possible successors compete for position and favor.

The party is presented as vulnerable to succession because so much authority has been personalized around Erdogan. [[HakanFidan]] is portrayed as a stronger public-polling successor, while [[BilalErdogan]] represents the family-succession possibility that some voters resist as dynastic.
