# Jessica Livingston

Jessica Livingston is one of the [[TheSocialRadars]] hosts interviewing [[TrevorBlackwell]] in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]] and [[TracyYoung]] in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. In the Blackwell episode she is also a participant in the history being recalled: she helped run early [[YCombinator]], pushed for a Silicon Valley program with [[PaulGraham]], and remembers the Mountain View dinners, office constraints, and robot tours around [[Anybots]].

The sources use Livingston's memory to turn YC and founder stories into operating detail. Her recollections of interviews, printed applications, wet paint, bathroom constraints, and visitors such as Demi Moore show [[StartupInfrastructureImprovisation]] at the physical-institution level, while her PlanGrid reflections emphasize [[VerticalSaaSDomainExpertise]], paid customer pain, and the value of explicit [[TigerEye]] commitments.
