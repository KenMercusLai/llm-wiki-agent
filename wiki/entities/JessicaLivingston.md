# Jessica Livingston

Jessica Livingston is one of the [[TheSocialRadars]] hosts interviewing [[TrevorBlackwell]] in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]]. In the episode she is also a participant in the history being recalled: she helped run early [[YCombinator]], pushed for a Silicon Valley program with [[PaulGraham]], and remembers the Mountain View dinners, office constraints, and robot tours around [[Anybots]].

The source uses Livingston's memory to turn YC origin stories into operating detail. Her recollections of interviews, printed applications, wet paint, bathroom constraints, and visitors such as Demi Moore show [[StartupInfrastructureImprovisation]] at the physical-institution level, not only inside software teams.
