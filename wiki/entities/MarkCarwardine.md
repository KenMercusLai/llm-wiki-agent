# Mark Carwardine / 马克·卡沃丁

Mark Carwardine is the zoologist and conservation broadcaster paired with [[DouglasAdams]] in [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》 / Last Chance to See]]. In [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]], he provides the scientific, logistical, and conservation backbone of the journeys: routes, species contacts, scientists, permits, and field interpretation.

The episode also uses Carwardine's later work as a continuity marker. After Adams's death, Carwardine continued conservation broadcasting and returned to many sites in a later [[BBC]] project, making him part of the source's claim that [[ConservationIntervention]] needs long-term institutions and people who keep showing up.
