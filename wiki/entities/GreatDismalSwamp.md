# Great Dismal Swamp

The Great Dismal Swamp appears in [[bayo-akomolafe-the-untimely-cenyca-k7ry]] as a historical example of fugitive life beyond plantation clock time. [[BayoAkomolafe]] uses it to describe forms of collaboration with place, circumstance, body, and time that are not fully inscribable in ledgers, calendars, appointments, or productivity systems.

Its role in the wiki is to ground [[FugitiveTemporality]] in a place rather than treating it only as metaphor. The swamp shows how escape from [[ColonialTemporalDiscipline]] can involve inhabiting difficult terrain and forming life worlds that the plantation order cannot fully schedule or count.

- [[BayoAkomolafe]] - speaker using the place as historical-philosophical evidence.
