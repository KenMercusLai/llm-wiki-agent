# Ruchi Gupta

Ruchi Gupta is the physician, researcher, and founder of [[Yobi]] in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. In the episode, she describes building a probiotic skin-care line after her daughter's eczema and cradle cap led her to work with dermatologists and microbiologists on a formula.

Her case shows why founder credibility can be a practical distribution asset. [[GuyRaz]] advises putting her doctor/researcher/parent story closer to the product and reaching dermatologists, pediatricians, med spas, salons, and other local professionals who can transfer trust to customers.
