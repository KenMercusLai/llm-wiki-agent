# Erin Griffith

Erin Griffith is a [[NewYorkTimes|New York Times]] reporter interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] in [[tech-20260304-0304-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260304-0304-mp-tech-pod-128]] about [[AnswerEngineOptimization]]. In the episode, she explains why brands are adapting from classic search optimization toward making themselves legible to AI chatbots that answer product, service, and institution questions directly.

Her main distinction is between human-facing marketing and chatbot-facing information. Humans often respond to novelty, surprise, stories, and hooks, while chatbots appear to reward dense, specific, factual public material that can answer user questions. Griffith also frames the trust risk: AI-generated marketing fluff, negative [[Reddit]] material, and future paid placement by companies such as [[OpenAI]] could influence answers unless chatbot providers preserve answer integrity.
