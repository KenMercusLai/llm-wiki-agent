# 张月光

张月光 is the product manager and founder interviewed in [[130-zhang-yueguang-chuangye-liangnian-shouci-fangtan-miaoya-bushi-ai-native-chanpin-liucheng-dao-shangxiawen-sheji-one-way-door-he-yinv-youxi]]. His career path runs through Alipay, [[ByteDance]], [[Alibaba]], a first startup, [[Yuanyin]], [[Miaoya]], and then a new company focused on [[AIFriendProducts]], [[AIOtomeGames]], and [[Docky]].

The source presents Zhang as a product founder trying to move from internet product methods into [[AINativeProductDesign]]. His distinctive claim is that AI-native work is not only "a product that could not exist without AI"; it requires open-ended input/output, [[ContextEngineering]], and a different collaboration pattern between product, design, engineering, and model exploration.
