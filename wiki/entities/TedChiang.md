# Ted Chiang

Ted Chiang / 江峰南 is the science-fiction author at the center of [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode presents him as a Chinese-American writer born in New York, educated in computer science, and known for unusually high award density despite a small body of work.

The source's reading of Chiang stresses method rather than biography alone. Stories such as [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]], [[DivisionByZero|《除以零》]], [[TowerOfBabylon|《巴比伦之塔》]], [[MerchantAndAlchemistsGate|《商人和炼金术之门》]], and [[UnderstandTedChiang|《领悟》]] use science, mathematics, language, religion, and consciousness as pressure tests for human rational confidence.
