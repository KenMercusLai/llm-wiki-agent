# Sun Jian

Sun Jian is the [[MicrosoftResearchAsia]] researcher whom [[CaoXudong]] credits in [[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] as a major influence. Cao says Sun's group pushed him to value experiments, anomalies, and hard empirical work rather than only elegant reasoning.

The source makes Sun Jian important for the wiki because Cao later turns that lesson into [[Momenta]]'s operating method: build, test, observe unexpected results, and let short feedback cycles discipline both technical judgment and company strategy.
