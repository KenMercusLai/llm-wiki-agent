# Simon

Simon is the [[YingdiHaike]] guest in [[ep101-duihua-simon-ai-chuangyezhe-de-diyi-xiang-jibengong-shi-ba-zhang-suan-mingbai-lhrrhfslnd1z9cuu2vkuxbb5pvjx]] and is introduced as the head of [[MicoAILab]]. He previously worked on [[MicoWorld]]'s Middle East game-social products, then shifted into AI applications for games, social, and pan-entertainment.

His background combines computer science, psychology, and cross-cultural psychology. In the episode, he treats psychology as a research discipline for product work: surveys, interviews, controlled comparisons, translation checks, and data analysis help teams understand users across language and cultural boundaries.

## Key Points
- Simon argues that AI founders need to calculate unit economics before chasing technology heat.
- He sees pure [[CharacterAI]]-style companion chat as difficult when memory depth makes prompt cost rise with relationship history.
- He prefers AI game/social directions where market size, payment habits, user segments, and paid feature surfaces are easier to estimate.
- He values AI application hires who understand games, content, and business context, not only model technique.
