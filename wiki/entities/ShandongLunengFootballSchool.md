# 山东鲁能足校 / Shandong Luneng Football School

Shandong Luneng Football School appears in [[vol-262-qu-xibanya-mai-zuqiu-julebu-yichang-huangdan-de-shangye-maoxian-992614191]] as one of the later player-supply channels for [[LiXiang]] and [[TangHui]]'s Spanish club project. The source says the Jumilla platform eventually included domestic young players sent through a cooperation with the school.

## Key Claims
- The school represents the domestic institutional route for finding Chinese players, alongside overseas Chinese players and individual domestic prospects looking for another path.
- Its role shows that [[ChinesePlayerOverseasArbitrage]] needed a repeatable supply of players, not just a club shell in Europe.

## Connections
- [[JumillaCF]] - European platform where some players could be placed.
- [[China]] and [[YouthFootballDevelopmentSystem]] - broader player-development context.
