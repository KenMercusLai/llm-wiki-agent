# Garfield AI

Garfield AI appears in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]] as the legal-AI segment's positive example. [[AnnaKerr]] says it describes itself as an AI-powered law firm approved by regulators, and that it helped a user recover money in a London court while a human lawyer argued the case.

The entity matters less as a company profile than as an example of [[HumanInTheLoopLegalAI]]. The source contrasts Garfield AI with [[VibeLawyering]] and [[LegalAIHallucination]]: legal AI may improve access to justice when built for the domain and kept under professional responsibility, but generic chatbot confidence can damage litigants and courts.
