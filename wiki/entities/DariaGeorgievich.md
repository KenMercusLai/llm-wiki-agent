# Daria Georgievich

Daria Georgievich is the psychiatrist interviewed in [[using-ai-chatbots-for-mental-health-support-poses-serious-risks-for-teens-report-finds]], where she discusses testing consumer chatbots for teen mental-health support risk. The episode identifies her as a psychiatrist at [[ColumbiaUniversity]].

Her contribution to the wiki is the concrete testing distinction behind [[ChatbotSafetyGuardrailDecay]]: a chatbot can respond appropriately to an explicit single-turn self-harm prompt while still failing to track mania, secrecy, impulsivity, or eating-disorder warning signs across a longer exchange. She also frames [[SycophanticAICompanionRisk]] as developmentally important because real human relationships do not provide constant validation.
