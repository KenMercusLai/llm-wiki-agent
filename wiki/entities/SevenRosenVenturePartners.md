# Seven Rosen Venture Partners

Seven Rosen Venture Partners appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] as [[BenRosen]]'s venture-capital firm before and during his partnership with [[RonConway]]. Conway says Rosen and LJ Sevin had invested in companies such as Compaq and Lotus 1-2-3, giving Rosen technology-market credibility that complemented Conway's founder and operator network.

The source uses Seven Rosen mainly as background for the [[InternetSoftwareThesis]]. Rosen's venture and analyst experience helped Conway focus on internet software and divide early deal flow across East Coast and West Coast channels.
