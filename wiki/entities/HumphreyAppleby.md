# Humphrey Appleby / 汉弗瑞

Humphrey Appleby is the permanent secretary in [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]] and a central figure in [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]]. The episode reads him as the refined voice of the permanent civil service: outwardly helpful, rhetorically elegant, and structurally committed to preserving departmental control.

Humphrey's comedy comes from competence rather than simple obstruction. He can praise reform while reshaping it, bury action in procedure, exhaust [[JamesHacker|Hacker]] with paperwork, turn open government into managed secrecy, and use long, ambiguous sentences as a political instrument.

The episode also ties Humphrey to [[BaillieCollege|Baillie College]], his Oxford college, where foreign-student fees, budget pressure, honorary degrees, and honours recommendations reveal how elite education and administrative prestige reinforce one another.
