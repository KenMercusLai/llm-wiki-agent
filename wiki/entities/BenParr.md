# Ben Parr

Ben Parr is named in [[tech-20260313-0313-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260313-0313-mp-tech-pod-128]] as one of the [[MoteBook]] creators reportedly being brought into [[Meta]] after Meta acquired the AI-only social network. The episode does not profile him separately, but treats his movement as part of the broader [[AITalentCompetition]] around AI product builders.

His significance in this source is tied to execution more than biography. MoteBook is described as entirely [[VibeCoding|vibe coded]], but [[JewelBurkeSolomon]] cautions that its acquisition still depended on the creators' background and domain knowledge, not only on access to AI coding tools.
