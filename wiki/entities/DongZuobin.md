# 董作斌

董作斌 is named in [[57-shuojie-hanzi-shiniaopi-weisha-shi-shi-zitou-642970319]] through [[JiaguwenDeGushi|《甲骨文的故事》]], one of the episode's recommended references. The source uses the book to foreground oracle-bone evidence as the starting point for many Chinese-character explanations.

In the episode's argument, Dong's relevance is less biographical than evidentiary. Early script material helps correct attractive but unsupported modern explanations and helps explain why [[ShuowenJiezi|《说文解字》]] remains important but not unchallengeable.
