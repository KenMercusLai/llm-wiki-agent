# 鸭哥

鸭哥 is a guest in [[20-ge-wenti-gao-dong-openclaw-baohong-jizhi-benzhi-bianhua-chuangye-jihui-lk6bzkdxti47vehjvs9sgxotrvto]], discussing [[OpenClaw]] from an agent-builder and product-design perspective. He frames chat AI as a "consultant" and OpenClaw as an "intern" because OpenClaw can execute, inspect results, and iterate through [[AgenticWorkflow]] rather than only answer questions.

## Source Position
- He emphasizes feedback loops, [[LocalAgentExecution]], context, memory, and instruction following over treating OpenClaw as a model-only breakthrough.
- He built related personal-agent experiments, including a heartbeat mechanism and smart-home control, shifting the developer target from human-facing front ends toward AI-readable OpenAPI descriptions.
- He is skeptical of excessive anthropomorphism for engineer-facing tools, preferring agents that talk less and complete more work.
- He treats [[AgentHarness]], memory, context compaction, orchestration, and [[AISkills]] as the infrastructure that makes agent products useful.
- He highlights the safety tradeoff: local execution is valuable because it has context and tools, but too much permission creates [[AgentPermissionBoundaries]] risk.
