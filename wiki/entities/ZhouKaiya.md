# 周开亚 / Zhou Kaiya

周开亚 / Zhou Kaiya appears in [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]] as the Nanjing University professor contacted by [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] during their search for the [[Baiji|baiji]]. The episode places him inside the China segment of [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》]], where foreign visitors, scientific work, local receptions, and symbolic baiji publicity collide.

The page keeps the claim source-scoped: in this wiki, Zhou's role is the scientist-link in the episode's baiji story rather than a complete biography. His presence connects the [[YangtzeRiver|Yangtze River]] case to field science and [[ConservationIntervention]] even though the expedition does not find a living baiji.
