# Lei Lutai / 雷履泰

Lei Lutai is the piaohao manager associated with [[Rishengchang]] in [[no-209-jinshang-wangshi-zou-xikou-dao-qiaojia-dayuan-ranhou-mei-le-999367322]]. The episode credits him with seeing that a merchant shop's internal remittance mechanism could become an external business, helping turn the former Xiyucheng pigment shop into the Rishengchang piaohao.

The source uses Lei as the personification of [[ShanxiPiaohao]] innovation, but not as a lone-founder myth. [[MaoHonghui]] is presented as another important early participant, and the later Lei-Mao conflict is used to explain how rivalry helped spread the piaohao model across competing firms.

## Key Claims
- Lei's importance lies in converting an internal settlement convenience into a scalable public remittance service.
- The source connects Lei to operational trust: drafts, branch letters, codebooks, handwriting, seals, and silver-quality conversion all had to work together.
- His conflict with Mao Honghui is framed as both organizational friction and industry diffusion.
