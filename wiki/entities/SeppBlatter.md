# Sepp Blatter

Sepp Blatter appears in [[shangye-xiaoyang-44-shijiebei-kuojun-yu-fifa-de-quanli-douzheng-993354969]] as both a predecessor in the [[FIFA]] expansion lineage and a figure tied to the corruption crisis that opened the path for [[GianniInfantino]]. The episode links his era to the move from a 24-team to a 32-team [[FIFAWorldCup]].

The source uses Blatter to show that [[WorldCupExpansion]] did not begin with Infantino. Earlier FIFA leaders also treated more participating countries as a way to globalize football, increase tournament value, and build political support across member associations.
