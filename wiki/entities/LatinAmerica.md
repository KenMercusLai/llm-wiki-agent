# Latin America

Latin America is a recurring regional frame in recent [[TheIntelligence]] sources. [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]] treated the region through [[LatinAmericaRightwardShift]] and [[SecurityBacklashPolitics]], with [[Colombia]] as the election case.

[[latin-lessons-the-donroe-doctrine-boost-6a4233e26b6690f46aae4e3d]] adds an economic and strategic-investment frame. The episode says the region attracted $205bn in foreign direct investment in 2025 despite broader developing-world weakness, because improved macroeconomic stability, [[UnitedStates]] geopolitical attention, [[China]] competition, and [[CriticalMineralsGeopolitics]] made the region unusually attractive.
