# John Terry

John Terry appears in [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]] as the [[ChelseaFC]] player whose slip and missed penalty extended the 2008 Champions League final shootout. The episode uses that moment as the hinge that allowed the later [[EdwinVanDerSar]] and [[NicolasAnelka]] exchange to happen.

The source does not analyze Terry's miss as a data problem. Its role is situational: a shootout can turn on physical execution and randomness as well as [[GameTheory]], [[DataDrivenPenaltyPreparation]], and [[PenaltyShootoutPressure]].
