# Yihao

Yihao is presented in [[openclaw-zhihou-shui-jiang-dingyi-zhudongshi-ai-de-xin-zhanchang-duitan-airjelly-huang-bote-lplswo8r829akxwgyurfkojelku6]] as an angel investor in [[AirJelly]]. He says he first noticed [[Mycontext]] around September 2025, met [[HuangBote]], and was attracted by the founder's long-running focus on context, proactive AI, independent judgment, and hunger for talent.

## Position In The Episode
- Describes AirJelly's "magic moment" as feeling as if someone is watching the user's work across IM, Facebook, and other tools and can connect the thread.
- Frames proactive AI around continuous learning and right timing: the product should deliver the relevant result when the user can use it.
- Says [[QuickStone]] had already invested in two proactive-direction teams in 2025, including AirJelly.
- Highlights 2026 AI investment directions: vertical but aggressive agent applications, agent infrastructure, and hardware that gives agents more context.
- Describes AI-native fund work where [[ClaudeCode]] and similar tools help scan GitHub projects, small companies, and fragmented tools, then structure projects, people, and relationships.
- Positions VC value as dynamic learning with founders, market-level pattern recognition, talent help, and business-development support.
