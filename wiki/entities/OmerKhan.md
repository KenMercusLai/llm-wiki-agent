# Omer Khan

Omer Khan is the host of [[TheSaaSPodcast]] and the interviewer in [[he-demoted-his-saas-to-sell-a-service-and-4xd-revenue-in-12-months]]. In this episode, he frames [[FarzadRashidi]]'s story as a SaaS founder demoting the software product on the homepage and leading with a done-for-you service.

Omer's role in this source is to connect the Responna story to the wider market language of AI-native services and "service as software." His questions move the conversation from the customer-pricing anecdote into fulfillment, automation, productized operations, and AI visibility tactics.
