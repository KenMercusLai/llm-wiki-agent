# Kino Loy

Kino Loy is the [[NarkinaFive]] work-group leader discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode reads him as one of [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]'s most moving ordinary figures: he begins by believing discipline and sentence completion will lead to release, then helps lead the revolt once that promise is exposed as false.

His final inability to swim matters in the source because it separates leadership from heroic invulnerability. Kino helps others escape even though he cannot fully save himself, making him a key example of [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]] and the self-risk side of [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
