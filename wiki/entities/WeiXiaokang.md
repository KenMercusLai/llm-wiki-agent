# 魏小康 / Wei Xiaokang

Wei Xiaokang is the guest in [[shaoyou-de-shendu-canyu-guo-zijie-meituan-zuzhi-jianshe-de-ren-duitan-ai-chuangyezhe-wei-xiaokang-lof0mb7u5buzec1qtxqrt9kraiud]], presented as someone who participated deeply in [[ByteDance]] and [[Meituan]] recruiting and organization building before moving into AI product and AI recruiting entrepreneurship.

His main wiki role is to connect founder organization design to recruiting mechanics. He argues that organization form should follow [[BusinessModelOrganizationFit]], that early startups should avoid premature large-company performance and level systems, and that founders should spend more time on [[RecruitingSupplyStrategy]], motivation matching, and [[ReferenceCheckHiring]] than on interview-heavy reactive hiring.

The episode also makes Wei part of the wiki's [[AIOrganizationDesign]] branch. He expects AI to change sourcing, role boundaries, and AI coding adoption, but rejects the idea that [[OnePersonCompany]] becomes the default optimum: the more realistic form is a small team of strong people, each using AI to carry broader responsibilities.
