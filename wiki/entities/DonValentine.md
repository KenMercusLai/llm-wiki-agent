# Don Valentine

Don Valentine appears in [[tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final-tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final]] as the [[SequoiaCapital]] investor connected to [[AltosComputer]] and later to [[RonConway]]'s move toward investing. Conway says Valentine joined the Altos board, went on the IPO roadshow, and later asked Conway what he wanted to do next after operating companies.

Valentine's role in the source is a practical [[FounderInvestorLearning]] bridge. He invited Conway to observe boards and watch how founders received advice, helping Conway see that investing could let him advise founders, advocate for them, and help with customers without running the company day to day.

[[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] deepens that mentorship branch. Conway says Valentine invited him to board meetings, modeled founder mentorship, and remained a close friend through the end of Valentine's life. The episode uses Conway's final visit with Valentine to reinforce the broader theme that durable relationships, not only formal board roles, became part of Conway's investing style.
