# 立信会计

立信会计 is the accounting-firm and education context in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]]. The episode describes [[GuZhun]] entering [[PanXulun]]'s accounting office at a low level, learning by observation and self-study, then producing government and bank accounting work at a very young age.

The source uses 立信 to show that accounting firms in Republican-era Shanghai served governments, private businesses, state-owned enterprises, foreign firms, trading houses, and financial institutions. This makes the firm part of [[AccountingInfrastructure]] rather than a narrow professional-services footnote.
