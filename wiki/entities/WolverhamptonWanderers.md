# Wolverhampton Wanderers / 狼队

Wolverhampton Wanderers appears in [[vol-262-qu-xibanya-mai-zuqiu-julebu-yichang-huangdan-de-shangye-maoxian-992614191]] through a third-season cooperation with [[JumillaCF]]. The source says Wolves, under [[Fosun]], loaned 11 players to Jumilla, while Jumilla provided playing time and a lower-tier development environment.

## Key Claims
- The cooperation looked like a satellite-team exchange, but the source says it was not a real exit mechanism for Jumilla's Chinese investor group.
- Wolves' role shows that a lower-tier club can be useful as player-development infrastructure for a larger club while still being financially fragile on its own.

## Connections
- [[Fosun]] - owner context named by the source.
- [[JumillaCF]] - partner club in the source.
- [[FootballClubAsCommunityAsset]] and [[FootballClubControlRisk]] - concepts that explain why the exchange did not solve the underlying asset problem.
