# Komodo Island / 科莫多岛

Komodo Island is the setting for the [[KomodoDragon|Komodo dragon]] section of [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. The episode's travel comedy builds through airport ticket failure, a half-broken small plane, and preparations to attract the animals, but the arrival scene is not untouched wilderness.

The island becomes a [[WildlifeTourismSpectacle]] case because [[DouglasAdams]] finds tourist infrastructure, welcome signs, crowds, and feeding performance. The source uses that surprise to ask how protection, access, fear, and entertainment can become tangled around rare animals.
