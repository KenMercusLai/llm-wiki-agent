# Olin Foundation

Olin Foundation appears in [[e233-guigu-youyi-de-quanli-wang-ruhe-xingcheng-liaoliao-bide-tier-de-sixiang-qimeng-pintu-2a529abf-3c14-4ae7-a0a5-fe1340a66c7d]] as a conservative donor institution supporting right-leaning campus media. The episode describes it as one of the main funding sources behind [[StanfordReview]] and a wider [[CampusConservativeMediaNetwork]].

The source frames the foundation as infrastructure for conservative counter-mobilization inside universities. Outside funding let publications challenge campus administrations, progressive student politics, and university media without relying only on school-controlled resources.

## Source Position
- The episode says the foundation's founder, John Olin, came from a defense-industry background and wanted to counter left influence in universities.
- It states that the foundation funded many right-wing student publications and closed in 2005 after spending down its funds.
- The page preserves this source-scoped account and does not attempt a full philanthropy history.
