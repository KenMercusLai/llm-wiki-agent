# 小赵（《离婚》） / Xiao Zhao

小赵 is the most openly malicious office figure in [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]] as read by [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]]. The episode presents him as someone who enjoys humiliation, trades in access to power, handles sexual exploitation around officials, and turns other people's fear into leverage.

His role clarifies the darker edge of [[BeipingOfficeWorld]]. The office is already absurd through favors, superstition, promotion, and rank, but Xiao Zhao shows how such a world lets petty cruelty become useful. His attempt to extract [[ZhangDageLihun|张大哥]]'s house and daughter makes [[DingErLihun|丁二]]'s later action feel both terrible and narratively decisive.
