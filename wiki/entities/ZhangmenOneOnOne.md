# Zhangmen One-on-One

Zhangmen One-on-One was [[ZhangYi]]'s previous online education company before he founded [[WeilaiBuyuan]]. In [[jushen-zhineng-de-taotian-da-paomo-zhong-ta-yijing-ba-jiqiren-songjin-300-ge-jiating-duihua-zhang-yi-weilaibuyuan-chuangshiren-ceo-lic8b7dkxts3qjrs6af1rgbf4xrf]], Zhang recalls that the company once reached a reported 3 billion USD valuation and held a large share of online one-on-one tutoring, but was hit hard by China's Double Reduction policy.

The page matters to the wiki mainly as founder-context for [[ZhangYi]]. Zhang uses the experience to explain why his [[WeilaiBuyuan]] approach emphasizes longer-term direction, fewer anxious strategy pivots, sustainable business models, and the ability to survive financing cycles.
