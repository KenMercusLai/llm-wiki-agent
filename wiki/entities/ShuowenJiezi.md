# 《说文解字》

《说文解字》 is the classical Chinese-character dictionary discussed in [[57-shuojie-hanzi-shiniaopi-weisha-shi-shi-zitou-642970319]]. The episode treats it as foundational for character study while emphasizing that it should not be turned into an untouchable final authority.

The source's main qualification is evidentiary. [[XuShen|许慎]] did not have access to the later body of excavated oracle-bone, bronze-inscription, and other early materials available to modern scholars, so some explanations can be revised through [[CharacterFormEvolution]] and [[ChineseCharacterEvidenceDiscipline]]. The episode's discussion of 尿 and other characters uses this distinction to show how later shapes and textual authority can preserve both insight and error.
