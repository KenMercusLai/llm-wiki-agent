# Nicholas Miller

Nicholas Miller is the [[NationalConferenceOfStateLegislatures]] expert in [[tech-20251216-1216-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251216-1216-mp-tech-pod-128]], where he explains how states structure [[DataCenterTaxIncentives]]. His role is to translate data-center competition into state policy mechanics: sales and use tax exemptions, electricity exemptions, property-tax abatements, job requirements, capital-investment thresholds, and energy-related reassessment.

The episode uses Miller to connect AI infrastructure to public finance. Data centers may bring construction spending, large capital investment, property taxes, and some jobs, but he emphasizes that states still have to evaluate the revenue they give up against the benefits they receive.
