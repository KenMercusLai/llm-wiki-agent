# Jessica Alter

Jessica Alter appears in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]] as the main person [[DavidRusenko]] works with at [[LeapForward]]. Rusenko describes her as a venture partner, a two-time entrepreneur, and a former senior business-development leader at Figma.

The source frames Alter as complementary to Rusenko. She brings go-to-market and business-development judgment, while Rusenko brings engineering, product, and founder experience from [[Weebly]]. Together they represent [[FounderFriendlyInvestorSupport]] as an operating resource for climate founders rather than only a source of capital.
