# Toyota / 丰田

Toyota appears in [[vol-108-riben-wuda-zonghe-shangshe-zhongfan-wutai-zhongyang-lhamp3hn14iihcicpa8po5dvh6gq]] as a vertical [[KeiretsuBusinessGroups|keiretsu]] example. The episode contrasts horizontal keiretsu organized around banks and trading companies with vertical keiretsu organized around manufacturers, suppliers, dealers, and downstream demand.

The source says Toyota is sometimes treated as close to a newer zaibatsu-like group because of its industrial reach, but it still differs from older Mitsui, Mitsubishi, and Sumitomo lineages because it lacks the same historical big-bank core.
