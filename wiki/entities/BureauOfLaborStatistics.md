# Bureau of Labor Statistics

The Bureau of Labor Statistics is the U.S. statistical agency at the center of [[would-you-trust-an-economist-with-your-economy]]'s official-data trust case. The source says President [[DonaldTrump]] accused jobs numbers of being fake or rigged, criticized a large revision, and fired the economist in charge of publishing the jobs report.

The episode presents BLS credibility as an institutional asset created by technical procedure, statistical revisions, and politically neutral staff. [[DianeKPMGChiefEconomist]] and [[AaronSojourner]] both reject the idea that BLS staff quietly fabricate numbers for partisan ends, while Sojourner warns that attacks and firings can still weaken public confidence in data people use for real decisions.
