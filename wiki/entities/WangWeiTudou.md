# 王微 / Wang Wei (Tudou)

Wang Wei is the founder of [[Tudou]] in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode uses him to represent a creator-first version of Chinese online video: Tudou began closer to podcasting and user uploads, then became a platform where early web creators could find audience.

After Tudou's delayed IPO and merger with [[Youku]], Wang Wei left with a "江湖再见" message and founded [[ZhuiguangAnimation]]. His arc connects early UGC ideals to later professional animation and film production.
