# Ube.co

Ube.co is the organic purple sweet potato pet treat company discussed in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. The source describes the product as aimed at pets with sensitive stomachs and skin, with sales mainly direct-to-consumer plus a small number of local stores around Los Angeles and Hawaii.

The Advice Line question is how to find reputable PR support and expand reach. [[JeniBritton]] and [[GuyRaz]] first push for clearer messaging: the founder's science background, the health benefits of ube, the original dog need, and the playful purple product identity should be easier to understand before the company spends heavily on PR. The episode reports more than $50,000 but less than $100,000 in sales and about a 30% returning-customer rate online.
