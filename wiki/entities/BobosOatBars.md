# Bobo's Oat Bars

Bobo's Oat Bars is the food company that shared a Boulder kitchen and employees with [[JustinsNutButter]] in [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]]. The episode uses the shared production arrangement to show how early CPG companies can stretch scarce manufacturing capacity before they have dedicated facilities.
