# Division by Zero

[[DivisionByZero|《除以零》 / Division by Zero]] is one of the [[TedChiang|Ted Chiang / 江峰南]] stories discussed after [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]] in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode summarizes it through mathematician Laura, who encounters a coherent formal route to a contradiction such as 1 equaling 2.

The source reads the story as a mathematical version of [[RationalWorldviewCollapse]]. Laura's crisis is not just professional failure; mathematics had been the world where truth felt most secure. Her husband Carl's inability to share the same collapse makes the story also about love and communication under incompatible worldviews.
