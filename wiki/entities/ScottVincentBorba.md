# Scott Vincent Borba

Scott Vincent Borba is the beauty-industry co-founder of [[ELFCosmetics]] discussed in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. The episode credits him with the value cosmetics concept and with beauty-market experience from companies such as Hard Candy and Neutrogena.

Scott later left e.l.f. after he and [[JoeyShamah]]'s family also worked on the Borba nutraceutical beverage business. The episode describes the split as amicable and notes that Scott was later ordained as a Roman Catholic priest in Fresno, California.
