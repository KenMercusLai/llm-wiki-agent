# 顾颉刚

顾颉刚 appears in [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] as the main scholarly background for [[LiShui|《理水》]]. The episode presents him as the representative figure of [[GushiBian|古史辨]], a movement that questioned inherited accounts of ancient rulers such as Yao, Shun, and [[YuTheGreat|大禹]].

The source-scoped allusion is the claim that "禹" might be an insect rather than a historical person. The episode treats Lu Xun's satire as more than personal grievance: [[LuXun|鲁迅]] is shown arguing with a scholarly posture that can become detached from living people, even when historical doubt itself remains intellectually important.
