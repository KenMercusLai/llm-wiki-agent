# Thiel Fellowship

Thiel Fellowship appears in [[e233-guigu-youyi-de-quanli-wang-ruhe-xingcheng-liaoliao-bide-tier-de-sixiang-qimeng-pintu-2a529abf-3c14-4ae7-a0a5-fe1340a66c7d]] as one of [[PeterThiel]]'s selection mechanisms. The episode places it beside [[StanfordReview]] and venture investing as another way to identify contrarian young people before they become institutionally obvious.

The source highlights the fellowship's famous contrarian question: what important thing do most people agree with but you disagree with. In the episode's reading, the question works as a worldview filter as much as an entrepreneurial prompt.

## Source Position
- The fellowship is treated as [[PoliticalTalentPipeline]] infrastructure, not only a startup grant.
- It helps connect anti-institutional education critique to Thiel's broader approach to talent and power.
