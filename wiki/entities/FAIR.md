# FAIR

FAIR is the research-lab context in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] where [[XieSaining]] worked with researchers including [[KaimingHe]]. The source presents FAIR as attractive because it concentrated top computer-vision researchers at a time when Xie chose it over [[OpenAI]].

## Culture Shift
The interview contrasts FAIR's bottom-up research culture with a later period after [[ChatGPT]] when large AI labs increasingly held alignment meetings to decide one- or two-year research direction. Xie treats that shift as partly contrary to exploratory [[ResearchTaste]], where a project often pivots through failure before its final story becomes clear.
