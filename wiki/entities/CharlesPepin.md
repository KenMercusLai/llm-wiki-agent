# 夏尔·佩潘 / Charles Pepin

夏尔·佩潘 / Charles Pepin is the French philosophy author credited in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as a co-creator of [[PlatoGoesToWork|《柏拉图上班记：哲学家的职场百态》]]. The episode treats his essays as the layer that explains why the office jokes are philosophically grounded rather than random name play.

His contribution matters most in the episode's discussion of [[LaborMoralAmbivalence]]: the source uses the book's prose to connect ancient disdain for labor, Christian punishment, Protestant work ethic, and modern corporate morality.
