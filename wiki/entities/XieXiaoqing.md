# Xie Xiaoqing / 谢晓清

Xie Xiaoqing is the [[RongzhongGroup]] figure in the financing and Dongsheng Real Estate dispute covered by [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]]. The episode presents Xie as the counterparty to [[LanShili]] in a contested 2008 equity-transfer arrangement involving a 315 million yuan agreement and partial actual payment.

Lan framed the deal as a loan-security or takeover problem; Xie's side framed it as a legal equity transfer tied to a project already carrying substantial debt. The source does not treat either public story as sufficient on its own, and instead places Xie inside the larger [[FounderNarrativeReliability]] and distressed-financing problem around [[EastStarGroup]].
