# Ram Shriram

Ram Shriram appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as one of [[Google]]'s early angel investors and as a key ally to [[RonConway]] in closing the institutional round. Conway lists Shriram alongside [[DavidCheriton]], [[JeffBezos]], and [[AndyBechtolsheim]] among early backers who helped move Google from Stanford project toward company.

Shriram's most important role in the episode is [[VentureSyndicateOrchestration]]. Conway says he and Shriram worked to get [[KleinerPerkins]] and [[SequoiaCapital]] to invest together despite the firms' initial resistance.
