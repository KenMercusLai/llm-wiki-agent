# JD.com / 京东

[[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]] uses JD.com as one of the surviving "winner" platforms against which failed or acquired ecommerce companies are compared. The episode says [[Dangdang]]'s book-retail advantage was weakened once JD expanded categories, while later businesses such as [[Yihaodian]], [[Suning]], and community-group-buying entrants had to compete against JD's logistics, category breadth, and platform credibility.
