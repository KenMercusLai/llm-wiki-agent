# Hormel

Hormel is the food company that acquired [[JustinsNutButter]] in 2016 for $280 million, according to [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]]. [[JustinGold]] stayed with the company for about five years after the sale, later saying the acquisition did not turn the brand into Skippy but also did not give it enough focused growth attention.

## Key Points
- The sale was financially life-changing for the team, and the source says every person in the business had an equity stake.
- Justin felt conflicted because selling a local, values-oriented brand to a large corporation felt like selling out.
- Hormel later retained a significant stake after [[ForwardConsumerPartners]] acquired a 51% controlling interest in the brand.
