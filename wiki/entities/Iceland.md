# Iceland

Iceland appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the location of the beluga sanctuary for [[LittleWhiteAndLittleGrey]]. The episode focuses on a bay off the south coast where the [[SeaLifeTrust]] is trying to create a more natural but still cared-for setting for long-captive whales.

The same bay is linked to [[Keiko]], whose attempted post-captivity release became a cautionary reference for the sanctuary model. In this source, Iceland is less a general country case than the environmental and logistical setting for [[CaptiveWhaleSanctuary]].

## Connections
- [[SeaLifeTrust]] - organization operating the sanctuary.
- [[LittleWhiteAndLittleGrey]] - belugas moved there.
- [[Keiko]] - earlier whale-rehabilitation precedent connected to the bay.
- [[CaptiveWhaleSanctuary]] - welfare model being tested in the source.
