# Aaron Swartz

Aaron Swartz appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] through the merger of [[Infogami]] and [[Reddit]]. [[SteveHuffman]] says the merged vision was broader than Reddit alone, combining lists and webs of structured things, but Reddit had users and therefore pulled the team's energy toward the live product.

The source's main contribution is relational. Huffman says he and Swartz were close for a period, then their relationship silently broke down after the acquisition. Huffman frames the unresolved break as a lasting regret because they never reconciled before Swartz died.
