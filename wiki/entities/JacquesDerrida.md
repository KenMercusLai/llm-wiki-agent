# Jacques Derrida / 德里达

Jacques Derrida / 德里达 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the figure trying to repair the copier after it has been taken apart. The source notes the structuralist/deconstructive joke around a machine that keeps being dismantled and reassembled.

His scene is paired with [[WalterBenjamin|本雅明]] because the copier first appears as a mechanical-reproduction gag, then becomes a deconstruction gag once repair begins.
