# Adam Williams Gallery

Adam Williams Gallery appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] through [[PatrickWilliams]], who says he has seen new and younger Old Masters buyers only in the last two years. The gallery is therefore a dealer-side observation point for [[OldMastersMarketRevival]].

The source uses the gallery to show that market revival is not only an auction-house statistic. Dealers can reframe older works for buyers who value scarcity, stories, figurative imagery, and authenticity in an AI age.
