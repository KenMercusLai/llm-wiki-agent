# Sam Uys

Sam Uys appears in [[planet-money-vs-the-nbas-tanking-problem]] as a women's soccer commentator interviewed about the [[NationalWomensSoccerLeague|NWSL]] after it abolished its draft. The source identifies her as a World Cup champion and uses her comments to explain the player side of draft reform.

Uys's contribution is the [[SportsPlayerAutonomy]] frame. She argues that draft systems should be evaluated not only from the league, team, or fan perspective, but also from the viewpoint of young players who may be required to move across the country into very different personal and political environments.
