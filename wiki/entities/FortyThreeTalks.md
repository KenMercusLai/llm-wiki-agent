# 43talks

43talks is the guest's offline, invite-only discussion format in [[dushu-jiushi-zai-du-yige-ren-de-f-li4qt9zs2bss4tklnj3yg9y-quo1]]. The source describes it as a recurring way to answer "my relationship with the world" through small closed-door gatherings around "AI + X" themes, several guests, and roughly fifty participants.

In the episode, [[FortyThreeTalks]] matters less as an event brand than as a practice of frame collision. It sits beside reading, meeting friends, and using AI: each is a way to encounter another person's [[XFFXFramework]], test one's own frame, and stay connected to the world rather than only collecting finished answers.
