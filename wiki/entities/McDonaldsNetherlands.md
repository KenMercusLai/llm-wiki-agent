# McDonald's Netherlands

McDonald's Netherlands is the brand unit discussed in [[tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128]] after it released and then pulled a 45-second AI-generated Christmas ad. The episode says the ad was disclosed as AI-made, but still drew online backlash.

In the wiki, the case makes [[AIGeneratedAdvertising]] concrete. The issue is not only whether synthetic media looks convincing; it is whether audiences trust a large company that uses AI for public-facing creative work when it could have hired artists, writers, filmmakers, or production crews.
