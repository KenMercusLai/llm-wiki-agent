# Creole Petroleum

Creole Petroleum appears in [[chevron-venezuela-and-the-paradox-of-plenty]] as the oil company that built the Caripito camp where [[MiguelTinkerSalas]] was born. The source uses the camp to show how foreign oil companies shaped daily life inside [[Venezuela]]'s oil economy, not only drilling and exports.

The company's role is mainly social infrastructure. The camp had U.S.-style amenities and segregated spaces for senior staff, junior staff, and workers, making Creole Petroleum a concrete example of [[OilCompanyEnclave]] organization inside a wider [[Petrostate]].
