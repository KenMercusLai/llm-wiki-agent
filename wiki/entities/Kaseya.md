# Kaseya

Kaseya is referenced in [[how-danny-jenkins-bootstrapped-threatlocker-from-150k-debt-to-200m]] through a July 2021 incident that affected MSP-managed environments and accelerated demand for [[ThreatLocker]]. In the episode, [[DannyJenkins]] says a Kaseya vulnerability pushed ransomware to about 40,000 businesses worldwide and that ThreatLocker was able to block it for protected customers.

## Connections
- [[ThreatLocker]] - company whose demand reportedly surged after the incident.
- [[DannyJenkins]] - guest describing the event's effect on customer adoption.
- [[MSPChannelDistribution]] - channel context because Kaseya served MSP-linked environments.
- [[ZeroTrustSecurity]] and [[DefaultDenySecurity]] - security controls the episode presents as relevant to ransomware prevention.
