# 曹雪芹 / Cao Xueqin

In [[183-li-shangyin-he-hongloumeng-pianpi-xing-guaizhang-na-guan-shiren-feibang-981580260]], 曹雪芹 appears as the authorial center behind [[HongLouMeng|《红楼梦》]] and its relation to [[LiShangyin|李商隐]]. The episode notes the suggestive but uncertain echo between Cao Xueqin's 芹溪 and Li Shangyin's 玉溪, then treats the stronger case as textual: quotations, images, reading scenes, and character affinities matter more than name echo alone.

The source frames Cao Xueqin as an author writing under pressure, with indirectness, layered allusion, and emotional ambiguity compared to Li Shangyin's late-Tang obliqueness. This makes him part of [[ClassicalPoeticIntertextuality]] and [[YuxiIntoHonglou]], but also requires [[InterpretationAndOverinterpretation]] as a guardrail because the episode's argument mixes textual evidence with psychological and historical analogy.
