# 万达集团 / Wanda Group

Wanda Group appears in [[vol-264-ba-shijiebei-zuowei-fangfa-999416048]] as part of the Chinese sponsor history around the [[FIFAWorldCup]]. The episode says Wanda signed a 15-year agreement with [[FIFA]] in 2016 and entered the highest sponsorship tier, marking a later stage after [[YingliGreenEnergy]] became the first Chinese World Cup sponsor in 2010.

For the 2026 tournament, the episode says the highest-tier Chinese position previously associated with Wanda is now occupied by [[Lenovo]]. That transition helps the source show how Chinese World Cup sponsorship moved from symbolic first entries and conglomerate visibility toward technology and overseas-business alignment.
