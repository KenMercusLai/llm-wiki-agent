# Shell

Shell appears in [[roaring-trades-oil-majors-secret-success-story-6a4636f160cad2674e6d9674]] as a European oil major whose trading operation is presented as a major but often underappreciated contributor to profits. The episode groups Shell with [[BP]] and [[TotalEnergies]], arguing that their trading desks profit from global price spreads, physical logistics, and volatile energy markets.

The source makes Shell part of a broader [[EnergyTradingScaleAdvantage]] pattern: a company with field, refinery, terminal, storage, customer, and tanker information can sometimes earn more from moving and arbitraging energy than outsiders expect from a producer label.
