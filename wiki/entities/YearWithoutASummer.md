# Year Without a Summer / 无夏之夏

The Year Without a Summer appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the 1816 climate anomaly following the Tambora eruption. The episode uses cold, bad weather, crop failure, hunger, and disease as the background that kept the Geneva circle indoors near [[VillaDiodati|Villa Diodati / 迪奥达蒂别墅]].

Its role in the source is not only meteorological. The strange weather helps turn social idleness, ghost-story reading, and scientific debate into a constrained creative setting where [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]] begins [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] and [[JohnPolidori|John Polidori / 约翰·波利多里]] produces a vampire-literature afterlife.
