# Story of Your Life

[[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》 / Story of Your Life]] is the [[TedChiang|Ted Chiang / 江峰南]] story discussed in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode reads it as a story about language, time, causality, and the emotional cost of knowing a loved one's whole life before it unfolds.

The story alternates between [[LouiseBanks|Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯]]'s alien-language work and her second-person address to her daughter. That formal split is central: what first looks like memory gradually becomes part of [[NonlinearTimePerception]], where beginning, middle, and end are no longer ordered the way human narration expects.
