# Sea Otter / 海獭

Sea otter appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] first as a survival and gambling object on [[BeringIsland]], then as a commercial resource that helped draw Russian merchants into the North Pacific. The episode notes that skins became valuable enough that they were saved on the return voyage while many of [[GeorgWilhelmSteller]]'s scientific specimens were left behind.

The source uses sea otters to show how quickly [[ExplorationToExtractionPipeline]] can form. A route and a new animal-resource signal become commercial opportunity, company conflict, ecological depletion, and part of the later [[RussianAmericanCompany]] and [[Alaska]] story.
