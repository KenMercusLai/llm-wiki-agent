# Lei Jun

Lei Jun is the founder figure through whom [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]] explains [[Xiaomi]]'s route into smartphones. The episode traces him from Wuhan University and Kingsoft through investment work, then into Xiaomi's founding team and the decision to build user community and software traction before launching hardware.

Lei's source role is strategic: he connects software experience, internet distribution, user participation, supply-chain negotiation, low-margin pricing, and later offline/overseas repair into one founder-led execution system. The same source notes the tradeoff that Xiaomi's strong execution has long been closely tied to Lei himself.
