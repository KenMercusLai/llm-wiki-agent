# Wes Streeting

Wes Streeting is one of the possible [[LabourPartyUK]] successors discussed in [[a-keir-death-experience-britains-pm-clings-on-698b0dc3337b2a3b2ef0aa07]] and [[coming-in-andy-britains-prime-minister-in-waiting-6a3512974a2a3be0f419384b]]. The earlier source presents him as damaged by association with [[PeterMandelson]] and by low party popularity, which weakens his route as a replacement for [[KeirStarmer]].

The Burnham source gives Streeting a more active role in the succession field. It says he resigns from the cabinet and is positioned as a possible challenger, though [[AndyBurnham]] remains the heavy favourite if the contest reaches Labour members.
