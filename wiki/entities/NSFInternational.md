# NSF International

NSF International appears in [[why-is-there-a-supplement-craze-if-they-dont-even-work]] through [[MarionNestle]]'s advice about supplement testing marks. The episode says an NSF mark can indicate third-party testing for listed ingredients, amounts, and non-harmful levels.

In this source, NSF does not solve the whole supplement problem. It belongs to [[ThirdPartySupplementTesting]], which may improve confidence about contents and contaminants while leaving [[SupplementStructureFunctionClaims]] and effectiveness questions separate.
