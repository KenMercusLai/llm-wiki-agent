# Shengpai Notice

Shengpai Notice is the host-built podcast workflow system discussed in [[ai-hui-xie-daima-le-weishenme-ni-haishi-zuo-bu-chu-chanpin-1]]. The system uploads or imports audio, detects recording scenarios, applies templates to generate summaries, and supports sharing plus collaborative annotation so guests can mark changes.

## Key Role
- It is the episode's positive [[VibeCoding]] case: AI helped build an internal tool that matched an already understood workflow.
- The hosts contrast its success with a larger failed attempt where AI implemented a broad product document without enough human decomposition and correction.
- Its quality is attributed to [[AIEngineeringThinking]] practices such as product docs, test-driven development, end-to-end checks, screenshots, documentation, and review.
