# Sea Life Trust

Sea Life Trust appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the organization behind the [[Iceland]] sanctuary for [[LittleWhiteAndLittleGrey]]. The episode says the trust moved the belugas from a Shanghai theme park to a bay associated with [[Keiko]]'s earlier rehabilitation.

The source frames the trust's work as difficult and unfinished. Its sanctuary model aims for more space, sea water, and round-the-clock care without pretending that long-captive whales can automatically return to open-ocean life.
