# vivo X Fold6

vivo X Fold6 is the foldable-phone product case in [[268-ai-shidai-geren-gongzuotai-hui-zhongxin-huidao-shouji-ma-lgprs5juhhrjykbzasaqvdlzx8fs]]. The episode uses it less as a spec review than as an example of how [[Vivo]] wants foldables to become AI-era work surfaces.

In the source, X Fold6's larger screen is discussed through a main-window-plus-side-app mode, four-window parallel use, app groups, one-sentence workbench launching, AI file management, and meeting-assistant behavior. The product therefore extends [[FoldablePhoneProductivity]] from "bigger screen" toward [[MobileAIWorkstation]], where the phone helps organize tasks, files, conversations, and AI assistants in light-office moments.
