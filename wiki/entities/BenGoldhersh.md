# Ben Goldhersh

Ben Goldhersh appears in [[tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a heavy user of screen-limiting tools and co-founder of [[MatterNeuroscience]]. He says he has tried lockbox phone cases, Brick, Freedom software, and other tools, but still finds the phone difficult to avoid because it is both useful and magnetically engaging.

Goldhersh's "Staff of Destiny" makes [[ScreenTimeFriction]] literal: a phone case screwed into a large walking stick makes casual scrolling inconvenient. [[MatterNeuroscience]]'s proposed six-pound phone case turns that same idea into a commercial product.
