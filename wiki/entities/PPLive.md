# PPLive

PPLive is the P2P streaming platform founded by [[YaoXin]] and discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode uses its live-streaming performance during large events to show why [[P2PStreaming]] was important before mature CDN-heavy video infrastructure.

PPLive also illustrates the fate of many early streaming companies: technical edge and user traffic were not enough once content rights, financing, and platform backing became decisive.
