# McNally Jackson

McNally Jackson appears in [[bookstore-economics]] as the independent bookstore near the South Street Seaport where [[AlexiHorowitzGhazi]] and [[AlexGoldmark]] check the [[PlanetMoneyBook]] on launch day. The source uses the store as a contrast with [[CarmichaelsBookstore|Carmichael's Bookstore]]: the book appears in a front-window display but is shelved inside according to its business/economics category.

The episode's McNally Jackson scene reinforces [[RetailShelfPlacement]] by showing that the same book can receive different visibility depending on how each store interprets publisher category metadata and local merchandising needs.
