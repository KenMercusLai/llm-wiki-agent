# Bambu Lab / 拓竹科技

Bambu Lab appears in [[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]] as one of the companies associated with [[DJI]] alumni. The episode uses it as evidence that DJI is not only a product company, but also a talent-training environment whose methods and people can seed new hardware companies.

In the source, Bambu Lab matters less as a 3D-printing case than as part of [[HardwareTalentSpillover]]. Investors' interest in "DJI-system" teams suggests that DJI's engineering, product, and supply-chain culture can travel, but it also means DJI faces stronger retention pressure if employees see better upside outside the unlisted parent company.
