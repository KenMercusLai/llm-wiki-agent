# FamilyMart / 全家便利店

FamilyMart appears in [[vol-108-riben-wuda-zonghe-shangshe-zhongfan-wutai-zhongyang-lhamp3hn14iihcicpa8po5dvh6gq]] as the downstream retail case for [[Itochu|Itochu's]] vertical integration. The episode says many Itochu-related companies were already FamilyMart suppliers, so the acquisition is framed as a way to coordinate an existing supply chain rather than as a detached financial bet.

In the source, FamilyMart helps make the [[JapaneseSogoShosha|sogo shosha]] model visible in daily life. A trading company can sit behind convenience-store products, logistics, suppliers, financing, and retail demand, even when consumers experience only the storefront.
