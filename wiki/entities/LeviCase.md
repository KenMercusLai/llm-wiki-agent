# Levi Case

Levi Case is the [[SwingSculpt]] founder who calls into [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. The source says he launched the business after leaving a finance job, had about $25,000 in sales, was having his best month so far, and relied mainly on organic social media, Instagram, TikTok, and Meta ads.

His question is how to reduce friction for a product that customers have not seen before and must help create by submitting swing material. [[KennethCole]] asks whether golfers want to memorialize swings they may not like, leading Levi to clarify that the business is more about memories from golf trips, family, friends, and time on the course than swing mechanics alone.
