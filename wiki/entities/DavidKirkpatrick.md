# David Kirkpatrick

David Kirkpatrick appears in [[how-much-money-president-trump-and-his-family-have-made]] as the reporter whose estimate anchors the [[PlanetMoney]] episode. The source says he counts only money or benefits that seem unlikely to have materialized without [[DonaldTrump]] being president, making his work a concrete example of [[OfficeLinkedProfitAccounting]].

## Source Position
- Kirkpatrick presents the nearly $4 billion figure as a conservative estimate rather than a ceiling.
- His method excludes ordinary preexisting business income and includes family gains when the episode argues the presidency created or materially improved the opportunity.
- The episode uses his categories to move from anecdotal ethics concerns into a structured accounting of merchandise, legal fees, media settlements, hospitality, family finance, and crypto.
