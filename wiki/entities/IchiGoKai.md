# 一五会

一五会 appears in [[137-sangeng-banye-juran-yao-chi-xiangjiao-shide-zailai-yigen-835431951]] as a Japanese disability group connected to [[ShikanoYasuaki|鹿野靖明]]'s understanding of independent living. The episode presents it as part of the move from facilities designed around administrators, parents, and professionals toward living arrangements defined by disabled people themselves.

In the source, 一五会 matters because it gives [[DisabilityIndependentLiving]] a collective vocabulary. The demand is not only for basic survival support, but for independent rooms, equal relations, home-like settings, and the right of disabled people to decide how support should be organized.

## Key Claims
- The group helps shift care from institutional management to disabled people's own life decisions.
- Its ideas connect 鹿野's personal insistence on living at home to [[JapaneseDisabilityRightsMovement]].
- The episode uses 一五会 to show that accessibility and community living are political achievements, not natural background conditions.
