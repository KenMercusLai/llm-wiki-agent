# Discourse on Method / 《谈谈方法》

[[DiscourseOnMethod|《谈谈方法》]] is the [[ReneDescartes|Descartes]] text that anchors [[174-du-dikaer-shi-jian-dashi-966856702]]. The episode presents it as his first published work and as a comparatively readable entry point because it is written in French for a wider audience rather than only in Latin for scholars.

The source's reading centers on the book's method rather than on a catchphrase. [[MethodicDoubt]] clears away inherited belief and uncertain sensation, [[CogitoErgoSum|我思故我在]] gives the first indubitable point, and [[RationalistMethod]] then tries to build knowledge through clear intuition and logical deduction.

## Source Position
- The episode repeatedly recommends this text before harder works such as [[MeditationsOnFirstPhilosophy]] and [[PrinciplesOfPhilosophy]].
- Its publication is placed after [[GalileoGalilei|Galileo]]'s trial, when Descartes had become more cautious about public conflict around science and theology.
- The text is treated as a practical training in doubt, clarity, and self-examination, not merely a historical artifact.
