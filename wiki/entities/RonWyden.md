# Ron Wyden

Ron Wyden appears in [[tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the senator previously associated with the federal [[FourthAmendmentIsNotForSaleAct|Fourth Amendment is Not For Sale Act]]. [[JeremyScott]] says [[Montana]]'s law closing the [[DataBrokerLoophole]] was largely modeled on that federal proposal.

In this source, Wyden matters as a legislative signal: the privacy problem has moved from abstract concern over data brokers to proposed warrant requirements for government purchases of information that would otherwise require judicial approval.
