# Mike Isaac

Mike Isaac is the [[NewYorkTimes|New York Times]] reporter interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] in [[tech-20260219-0219-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260219-0219-mp-tech-pod-128]] about [[Meta]]'s AI spending, advertising payoff, [[MetaAI|Meta AI]], [[RayBanSmartGlasses|Ray-Ban smart glasses]], and the company's shift away from virtual-reality urgency.

Isaac's contribution to the wiki is the business-strategy framing: Meta's AI spend is not only a frontier-model race, because better targeting can strengthen the existing ad business now. At the same time, he treats [[PersonalSuperintelligence]] as a still-unproven consumer-product direction, with [[ChatGPT]] holding stronger public mindshare and [[RealityLabs]] showing how quickly Meta's priorities can move when a major bet underperforms.
