# Chobani

Chobani appears in [[the-sneaky-way-companies-get-new-chemicals-into-our-food]] because the episode says [[DailyHarvest]] was later purchased by Chobani after the [[TaraFlour]] crisis. The source does not present Chobani as responsible for the original ingredient decision.

Its wiki role is to close the company-consequence part of the case. The episode uses the later purchase, the continued existence of [[Smirks]], and the absence of a public FDA fine in [[BillMarler]]'s account to support its broader [[PostHarmFoodRegulation]] critique.
