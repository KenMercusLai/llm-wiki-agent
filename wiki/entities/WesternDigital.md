# Western Digital

Western Digital is the storage company cited in [[tech-20260303-0303-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260303-0303-mp-tech-pod-128]] as evidence of hard-drive scarcity. The episode says the company had said on its most recent earnings call that it was largely sold out of hard drives for the year.

Within the wiki, Western Digital becomes a hard-drive indicator for [[MemoryChipShortage]] and [[AIHardwareSupplyChainPressure]]. The episode uses its supply constraint to show that AI data-center demand can affect professional [[DigitalPreservation]], consumer backup choices, and the practical availability of storage hardware.
