# 《哈佛经典谈判术》

《哈佛经典谈判术》 is the book recommended by [[QinZong|秦总]] in [[106-bie-zai-haipa-chongtu-kuai-lai-xuexi-tanpan-shu-742539645]]. The episode presents it as a practical negotiation book associated with [[DeepakMalhotra|Deepak Malhotra / 马尔霍特拉]] and [[MaxBazerman|Max Bazerman / 马克斯·巴兹曼]], and emphasizes that good negotiation is learned through preparation, feedback, and method rather than born from charm.

The book cluster anchors the episode's progression from [[BATNA]], [[ReservationValue]], and [[ZoneOfPossibleAgreement]] into [[AnchoringEffect]], [[ConcessionSignaling]], [[MultiIssueNegotiation]], [[ContingentAgreement]], and bias management. In the episode's reading, its central value is not a bag of speaking tricks but a disciplined way to make conflict calculable, information-rich, and relationship-aware.
