# 王兴 / Wang Xing

Wang Xing appears in [[shaoyou-de-shendu-canyu-guo-zijie-meituan-zuzhi-jianshe-de-ren-duitan-ai-chuangyezhe-wei-xiaokang-lof0mb7u5buzec1qtxqrt9kraiud]] as the founder reference behind [[Meituan]]'s organization and strategic self-understanding. [[WeiXiaokang]] cites Wang's internal framing that company success should be understood partly as being a splash in a larger historical wave rather than as pure founder genius.

For the wiki, Wang matters here less as biography than as a founder-judgment anchor. The episode uses his view to connect humility, timing, business model, and organizational fit: a founder still needs persistence and strong execution, but also has to see which wave the company is riding and which organization form the business actually requires.
