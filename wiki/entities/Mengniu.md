# 蒙牛 / Mengniu

Mengniu appears in [[vol-264-ba-shijiebei-zuowei-fangfa-999416048]] as one of the Chinese brands in the World Cup sponsorship system. The episode says Mengniu is a second-tier sponsor for the 2026 [[FIFAWorldCup]] and has appeared across three consecutive World Cups alongside [[Hisense]].

The source does not analyze Mengniu's tactics as deeply as Hisense or [[Lenovo]], but it uses the company to show that Chinese consumer brands now treat World Cup sponsorship as a long-running global marketing surface rather than a one-off domestic advertising event.
