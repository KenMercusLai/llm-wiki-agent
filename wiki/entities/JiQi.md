# Ji Qi

Ji Qi is one of the [[Ctrip]] founders in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode frames him as the early execution and entrepreneurial operator in the founding group, then follows his move from online booking into hotel operations.

The source connects Ji to [[HomeInns]], [[HantingHotel]], and [[HuazhuGroup]]. That path matters for the wiki because it links [[OnlineTravelAgency]] demand aggregation with the supply-side logic of standardized hotel chains and later hotel-group attempts to reduce Ctrip dependence through direct booking.
