# Eddie Kim / Edward Kim

Eddie Kim, also named Edward Kim in the episode title, is a [[Gusto]] co-founder in [[tsr-s4-gusto-v3-tsr-s4-gusto-v3]]. Before Gusto, he entered [[YCombinator]]'s Summer 2008 batch with [[Pickwing]], a photo-printing and mailing startup that struggled as physical photo printing declined.

Eddie's family background grounds the episode's [[OperationalPainFounderInsight]] claim. His father's medical practice depended on back-office work by his mother, including payroll, insurance billing, bookkeeping, tax calculations, manual checks, and IRS mailing. That memory made small-business payroll pain concrete before [[Gusto]] turned it into software.

The source also uses Eddie to explain why the three-founder relationship worked. He says ego is a common reason co-founders break up, and describes an early explicit role conversation that put [[JoshReeves]] in the CEO role, Eddie in the CTO role, and [[TomerLondon]] in the chief product officer role.
