# St. George's School / 圣乔治学校

St. George's School / 圣乔治学校 is the English-tradition boarding school in [[Argentina]] where [[TomMichell|Tom Michell / 汤姆·米切尔]] works in the episode discussed by [[138-qie-ke-wo-zai-haitan-shang-jiandao-yiwei-q-laoshi-842331726]]. It becomes the main setting for [[JuanSalvadorPenguin|Juan Salvador / 胡安·萨尔瓦多]] after Michell brings the rescued penguin back from [[Uruguay]].

The school's wiki role is not institutional history but community setting. The episode uses the school to show how an animal can cross ordinary boundaries: students feed and clean up after the penguin, workers explain their problems to it, [[MariaStGeorges|玛利亚]] becomes its first ally, and a timid student gains confidence after swimming with it. This makes the school a concrete case for [[CompanionAnimalHealth]] and informal, nonjudgmental care.
