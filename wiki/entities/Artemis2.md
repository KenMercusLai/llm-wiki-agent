# Artemis 2

Artemis 2 appears in [[tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260211-0211-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[NASA]] mission that could send humans around the [[Moon]] for the first time in more than five decades. The episode treats the mission as a near-term milestone inside a longer effort to build sustained lunar presence and prepare for deeper human spaceflight.

The source's main contribution is not mission hardware detail, but the policy frame around the mission. [[Artemis2]] becomes a trigger for questions about [[LunarResourceGovernance]], [[SpaceResourceExtraction]], [[ArtemisAccords]], safety zones, [[China]]'s lunar ambitions, and the energy systems required for long-term activity.
