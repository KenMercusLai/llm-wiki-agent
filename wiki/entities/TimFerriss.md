# Tim Ferriss

Tim Ferriss is the [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. The episode presents him as a founder-advice voice focused on experiments outside one's main lane, identity diversification, offline connection, and avoiding the assumption that every business must chase the same growth endpoint.

He uses [[CoyoteGame]] as his own example of an off-menu project: an analog tabletop card game developed over roughly two years to create new skills, new relationships, and less screen-centered social time. In the caller segments, he advises [[Gob]], [[EBCo]], and [[KBecker]] to stage risky decisions through focused tests rather than permanent strategic swings.
