# Kate Crawford

Kate Crawford is the [[LongNow]] speaker in [[kate-crawford-mapping-empires]]. The source presents her as an AI scholar and researcher using history, visual culture, infrastructure, political economy, and field observation to analyze artificial intelligence as a material system.

Her role in the wiki is to connect the AI branch to extraction and representation. Crawford argues that AI should be mapped through [[AIMetabolicInfrastructure]]: scraped data, commercial datasets, data centers, minerals, water, energy, labor, carbon, and e-waste. She also treats generative media as a political and cultural force through [[AISlop]], slopaganda, and [[ModelCollapse]].
