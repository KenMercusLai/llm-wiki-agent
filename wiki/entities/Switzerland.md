# Switzerland

Switzerland appears in [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]] through [[Lugano]], the Swiss city positioning itself as a European [[Bitcoin]] hub. The episode keeps the national frame narrow: it contrasts Swiss francs and Bitcoin as payment options and has [[MicheleFolletti]] argue that Switzerland's money-laundering risk is more tied to fiat and cash than to Bitcoin.

The source makes Switzerland a setting for [[MunicipalBitcoinAdoption]] and [[VirtualAssetAMLRisk]], not a full national crypto-policy survey. [[LuganoPlanB|Plan B]] is therefore tracked as a city initiative inside Switzerland rather than as evidence that the whole country has become Bitcoin-based.
