# 宋子文

宋子文 is discussed in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]] as the likely historical reference behind [[WarOfFaith]]'s Shen Tunan role. The episode connects him to [[CentralBankOfChina]], Nationalist government finance, and the way financial institutions can become tools for fiscal extraction and political control.

The source's treatment is functional: Song is less a biography than a node for understanding how central-bank authority, government funding pressure, elite networks, and market signaling could shape Republican-era finance.

[[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] adds Song as a disaster-relief organizer. The episode says he helped build the 1931 flood relief mechanism, worked with technical and international experts, and used international finance to secure a U.S. wheat loan. That extends his page from state finance into [[DisasterResponseStateCapacity]], while also showing how relief finance could become [[DisasterReliefMismatch]] when debt, food type, and survivor needs did not align.
