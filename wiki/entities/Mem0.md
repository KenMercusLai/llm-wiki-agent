# Mem0

Mem0 is the memory-layer company mentioned in [[weishenme-guigu-kaishi-zhongxin-dingyi-ai-jiyi-s10e20-a70c41aa-41ae-488d-a6e2-63c3de5b9ec3]]. The source says Mem0 is a startup in the YC orbit that had recently raised funding and provides memory capabilities for companies building on large-model services.

[[KangHongwen]] treats Mem0 as market validation for the memory-layer category, but distinguishes it from [[CliptoAI]]. In his framing, Mem0 is more cloud-oriented and company-facing, while Clipto is trying to build a local-first, multimodal personal memory stack that must solve device heterogeneity and [[OnDeviceMemoryScheduling]].
