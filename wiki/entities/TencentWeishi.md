# Tencent Weishi

Tencent Weishi appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as [[Tencent]]'s restarted short-video product after [[Douyin]]'s rapid growth. The hosts describe Weishi's restart as relying on traffic buying, MCN content import, and Tencent traffic resources, but still falling behind Douyin in scale and retention.

For the wiki, Weishi is a case where distribution resources did not automatically create a self-sustaining UGC ecosystem. The source argues that short video depends on creator supply, content ecology, interaction habits, and network effects, not only on traffic pushes or copied features.
