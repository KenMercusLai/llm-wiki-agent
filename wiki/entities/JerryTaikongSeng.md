# Jerry / 太空僧

Jerry, also identified in the episode as Zhihu aerospace answerer "太空僧," is the aerospace practitioner interviewed in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]]. He explains [[LongMarch10B]] recovery as a full engineering chain rather than a single spectacular catch, moving from vehicle configuration and return ignition to [[SeaNetRocketRecovery]], [[MarineRecoveryPlatformControl]], and reuse inspection.

The source uses Jerry's explanations to translate aerospace details for non-specialists. His recurring emphasis is that successful recovery depends on coordinated systems: engines, propellant management, grid fins, servo actuation, net damping, ship positioning, sea-state tolerance, and post-flight data review all matter before recovery can become operational reuse.
