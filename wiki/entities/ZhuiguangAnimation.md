# 追光动画 / Zhuiguang Animation

Zhuiguang Animation is the animation company founded by [[WangWeiTudou]] after his exit from [[Tudou]], discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode uses it to show that Tudou's founder moved from platform competition back toward content creation.

The company is relevant to [[UGCToProfessionalCreatorPipeline]] because it connects early online-video ideals with later professional Chinese animation films such as "White Snake" and "Chang An San Wan Li."
