# Outrank

Outrank is an SEO product discussed by [[ThibautLouisLucas]] in [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]]. Lucas presents it as useful for ramping SEO quickly and for increasing the chance that products are mentioned by AI systems that depend on web presence, search results, blog posts, and mentions.

## Key Claims
- Outrank reflects [[TeaMaker]]'s emphasis on high-intent acquisition and reusable distribution infrastructure.
- Lucas treats SEO as strategically important because it captures users who are already searching for a problem or category.
- The product connects SEO to [[AIDiscoverySEO]], where model-mediated discovery still depends partly on public web signals.
