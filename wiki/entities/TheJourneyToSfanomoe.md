# 《前往斯法诺莫埃的旅途》 / The Journey to Sfanomoë

《前往斯法诺莫埃的旅途》 / The Journey to Sfanomoë is a Poseidonis story by [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]] mentioned near the end of [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]]. The episode describes two wise men who cannot prevent Poseidonis from sinking, build a spherical craft, and travel to the alien world Sfanomoë.

The source uses the story to show that Smith's work can shift from horror into cosmic metamorphosis. The travelers' bodies grow plantlike on the alien world, and they accept fusion with the planet rather than treating transformation only as punishment.

## Key Claims
- The story extends [[SmithStoryWorlds]] from lost-world fantasy into planetary journey.
- It turns apocalyptic failure into alien adaptation rather than heroic rescue.
- Its acceptance of transformation complicates the episode's darker [[CosmicHorror|cosmic horror]] examples.
- The story connects Smith's antiquity and mythic decline to speculative space travel.
