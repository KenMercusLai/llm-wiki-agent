# Westside Federation For Senior And Supportive Housing

The Westside Federation for Senior and Supportive Housing appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] as the organization running [[EuclidHall]]. The episode says it operates 22 properties across New York and provides social support, making Vera Hill's room part of a managed supportive-housing context.

Its role in the wiki is to distinguish [[SupportiveHousingManagement]] from merely legalizing cheap shared rooms. The same physical format can be unsafe, neglected, temporary, or stabilizing depending on operator capacity, services, resident mix, and public support.
