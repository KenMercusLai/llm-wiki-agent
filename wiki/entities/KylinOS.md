# Kylin OS

Kylin OS is the domestic operating-system competitor mentioned in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says Tongxin ranks behind Kylin in the Chinese desktop operating-system field and attributes Kylin's stronger position partly to state-backed context.

## Source Position

- Its role is market-structure context rather than a detailed technical case.
