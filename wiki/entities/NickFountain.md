# Nick Fountain

Nick Fountain appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] participant giving a Valentine to [[404Media|404 Media]]'s reporting on [[USImmigrationAndCustomsEnforcement|ICE]] and technology.

His segment highlights reporting method as much as reporting output. Fountain praises 404 Media for being open about how it searches federal procurement records, turning contracts, vendors, and government purchases into a practical [[ProcurementRecordsJournalism]] path for accountability.
