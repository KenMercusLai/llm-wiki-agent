# 北宋 / Northern Song

The Northern Song is the historical setting for [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode places [[SuShi|苏轼]] inside a world where literary talent, examination success, memorial-writing, local office, censorial accusation, and court faction all belonged to the same scholar-official career.

The source's Northern Song is neither a quiet literary golden age nor a simple villainous bureaucracy. Fiscal strain and security concerns give the [[WangAnshiReforms|王安石变法]] a serious motive; [[SongFactionalPolitics|宋代党争]] gives Su Shi's criticism real consequences; and offices in [[Hangzhou|杭州]], [[Xuzhou|徐州]], [[Huangzhou|黄州]], [[Huizhou|惠州]], and [[Danzhou|儋州]] show how central politics and local governance constantly intersect.
