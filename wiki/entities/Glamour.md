# Glamour

Glamour appears in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] as the magazine whose interest pushed [[ELFCosmetics]] into e-commerce. The magazine wanted to feature the products but needed readers to be able to buy them, so [[JoeyShamah]] paid for an e-commerce build before the company had planned a direct-to-consumer operation.

The episode treats Glamour as a media-driven [[CustomerPull]] and [[FastProductValidation]] catalyst: a product originally intended for dollar-store shelves found early demand through editorial attention and online orders.
