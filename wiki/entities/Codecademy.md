# Codecademy

Codecademy appears in [[tsr-s3-replit-v2-tsr-s3-replit-v2]] as the education company that first pulled [[AmjadMasad]]'s open-source [[Replit]] work into a startup path. The episode says Codecademy's founders were using Amjad's project during [[YCombinator]], and that Zach Sims flew to Jordan to persuade Amjad and [[HayaOdeh]] to move to New York.

The source uses Codecademy as a bridge between Replit's early educational use and its later broader platform ambition. Amjad's time there exposed him to startup intensity, but also made him cautious about founding a company too quickly. The experience therefore connects [[LearningExperienceDesign]], [[AccessibleProgrammingPlatform]], and [[FounderProductFit]] while showing why Replit's founders took years before committing.
