# David Cameron

David Cameron appears in [[keep-qualms-and-carry-on-a-decade-after-brexit-6a3a53028a3c8822ab345d4b]] as the prime minister whose January 2013 promise of an in-or-out [[EuropeanUnion]] referendum set [[Brexit]] in motion. The episode presents the pledge as a political gamble made under Conservative Party pressure and UKIP's rise.

The source treats Cameron's referendum promise as an attempt to settle an internal party problem that instead became a national constitutional and strategic rupture. In that framing, Cameron is less the executor of Brexit than the politician who converted party-management pressure into a public vote with irreversible consequences for the [[UnitedKingdom]].
