# Sphere Abacus

Sphere Abacus appears in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] as the Bell Media-linked distribution participant that supplied an advance for [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]]. The episode places that advance alongside [[Crave]]'s license fee, tax credits, subsidies, and reinvested producer fees inside [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]].

Its role shows why distribution is not only what happens after a show is finished. In this source, anticipated international sales help fund production upfront, connecting [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] to [[ProducerOwnedIPUpside]] and the risk producers accept when they retain rights.
