# Kimberly Becker

Kimberly Becker is the founder of [[KBecker]] and a caller in [[advice-line-with-tim-ferriss-august-2025-84f10963-70ff-444d-87df-cfedab442943]]. The episode presents her as a textile designer who worked in American cotton mills before many closed, then built an independent clothing brand using higher-quality fabrics and sustainable production logic.

Her question is how to reduce inventory risk without asking customers to wait so long that they abandon the purchase. The advice she receives is to test limited made-to-order drops, keep some bestsellers in stock during transition, and use sketches, production glimpses, and lightweight community tools to make the waiting period feel like part of the product relationship.
