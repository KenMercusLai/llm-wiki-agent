# Siemens EDA / 西门子EDA

Siemens EDA appears in [[ep270-yi-mei-xinpian-de-manchang-zhengtu-women-li-suanli-ziyou-haiyou-duoyuan-lm7lxlmcnjwnawtq-9typc-fnrci]] as one of the major overseas [[ElectronicDesignAutomation|EDA]] tool vendors named alongside [[Synopsys]] and [[CadenceDesignSystems|Cadence]]. The source uses the trio to explain why chip design depends on deep industrial software infrastructure.

In this episode, Siemens EDA is not analyzed as a standalone company. Its role is to make the EDA oligopoly visible and to show why domestic semiconductor independence has to include software tools, verification flows, and process compatibility, not only factories or chips.
