# Major League Baseball Players Association

The Major League Baseball Players Association appears in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as the player-side organization behind [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]]'s challenge and the later bargaining path toward [[BaseballFreeAgency|baseball free agency]]. The source describes the union warning Flood that similar cases had failed, finding [[ArthurGoldberg]] to represent him, and later negotiating from a stronger position after arbitration weakened the [[BaseballReserveClause|reserve clause]].

The page's wiki role is sports labor infrastructure. It connects the episode's baseball history to [[SportsCollectiveBargaining]], [[SportsLaborRevenueSharing]], [[SportsPlayerAutonomy]], and the broader question of how athletes convert public attention and legal pressure into bargaining power.
