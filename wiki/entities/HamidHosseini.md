# Hamid Hosseini

Hamid Hosseini is the [[OPEX]] spokesperson interviewed in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]]. He describes, based on an Indian contact's account, an alleged permission process for vessels seeking to pass through the [[StraitOfHormuz]] during the U.S.-[[Iran]] conflict.

The source uses Hosseini as a high-value but limited-information witness. He says the ship owner sent vessel, flag, destination, cargo, and crew information to the [[SepahNavy]], then paid a per-barrel toll in crypto before the ship continued toward [[India]]. Because he would not connect the host with the underlying contacts, the wiki treats the toll account as source-attributed rather than verified policy.
