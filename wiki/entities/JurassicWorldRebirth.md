# 《侏罗纪世界：重生》 / Jurassic World: Rebirth

《侏罗纪世界：重生》 / Jurassic World: Rebirth appears in [[146-bu-ai-zhuluoji-shijie-kuailai-konglong-bihusuo-dang-siyangyuan-886472126]] as the foil that sends the hosts toward [[DinoSanctuary|《恐龙庇护所》 / Dino Sanctuary]]. The source is not a full film review; it records the hosts' dissatisfaction with a dinosaur blockbuster that, for them, leans too far toward generic thriller-monster spectacle.

The episode still grants the film a few possible strengths. It notes some franchise callbacks, a tyrannosaur rising motion that seems comparatively plausible, and a swimming scene that can be treated as speculation rather than certainty because large theropod trace evidence leaves room for discussion. The sharper criticism is that the film does not foreground [[DinosaurAsLivingAnimal]] enough to satisfy viewers who came for dinosaur wonder rather than only danger.

## Key Claims
- The episode's critique is about expectation fit: a Jurassic film can be a popcorn movie, but the hosts wanted more of the animals' specific appeal.
- The source separates weak story logic from science choices; some speculative details can be acceptable if they are framed as plausible imagination rather than settled fact.
- The film becomes useful inside the wiki mainly because it clarifies the alternative value of [[DinoSanctuary|《恐龙庇护所》]]: animal individuality, evidence-bounded reconstruction, and care labor.
