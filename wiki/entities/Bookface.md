# Bookface

Bookface is the internal [[YCombinator]] community software [[GarryTan]] says he designed in [[socialradarspod-garrytan-final]]. The need appeared as YC batches grew from roughly two dozen companies toward larger groups where founders no longer knew everyone directly and outsiders could pretend to be YC companies.

The source makes Bookface a concrete [[StartupCommunityInfrastructure]] case. It solved a trust problem, a discovery problem, and a community-memory problem at the same time: founders could identify one another, ask questions, and carry institutional knowledge across batches as YC scaled.

[[tsr-s5-jared-v3final-tsr-s5-jared-v3final]] adds Bookface to [[JaredFriedman]]'s broader [[YCInternalSoftware]] lineage. Jared treats Bookface, [[HackerNews]], application tools, legal-document automation, prompts, and agents as evidence that [[YCombinator]] operates partly through software rather than only partner memory and meetings.
