# Bitcoin

Bitcoin appears in [[ep88-chuanyue-lianghua-zhifu-ximengsi-ai-hui-rang-putongren-geng-rongyi-zhuanqian-haishi-geng-nan-lhvigzza2ugmayezkbrxufkmp4l1]] as a trading asset rather than a cash-flowing investment in the episode's [[JimSimons]]-style framework. The episode argues that crypto markets may contain many trading opportunities because of [[CryptocurrencyMarketStructure]], but it treats Bitcoin's long-term value as dependent on future buyers rather than on business fundamentals or cash flows. [[ep44-momo-koudai-limian-de-qian-juran-shi-zang-de-li-4f9d60jmelqituybbioyyflxr]] adds that Bitcoin's volatility can make it less attractive for some laundering chains than dollar-like instruments such as [[Stablecoins]], even though crypto rails still create [[VirtualAssetAMLRisk]].
