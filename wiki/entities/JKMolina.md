# JK Molina

JK Molina is the influencer and distribution partner who helped scale [[TweetHunter]]. In [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], [[ThibautLouisLucas]] says Molina's launch helped Tweet Hunter grow from roughly $3,000 to $20,000 in monthly revenue in two or three weeks.

## Key Claims
- Molina's audience and method matched the product's promise, making him more than a simple affiliate.
- The deal gave Molina up to 25% of profits and exit proceeds, which Lucas frames as a co-founder-level commitment to distribution.
- Molina continued working on distribution until the [[Lempire]] acquisition.
