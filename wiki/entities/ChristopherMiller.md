# Christopher Miller

Christopher Miller is the author of [[ChipWar]] and the guest in [[tech-20260210-0210-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260210-0210-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, he explains the distinction between [[GPU|GPUs]], [[TPU|TPUs]], and other specialized AI chips by tying architecture choices to speed, power use, workload predictability, R&D budgets, and software ecosystems.

His contribution to the wiki is an accessible bridge between semiconductor strategy and AI infrastructure economics. Miller argues that [[Google]] could justify custom chips because its large services create repeated computation patterns, while [[Nvidia]] remains hard to displace because general-purpose flexibility and software support matter across the broader AI ecosystem.
