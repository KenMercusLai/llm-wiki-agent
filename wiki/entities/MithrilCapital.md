# Mithril Capital

Mithril Capital appears in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]] as the lead investor for [[Helion]]'s first post-[[YCombinator]] Demo Day round. [[DavidKirtley]] says Helion spent months doing mutual diligence with investors instead of immediately closing a round after high interest.

In the source, Mithril Capital helps illustrate [[HardTechFundraising]]. Helion wanted a smaller group of larger investors willing to understand the physics, engineering, timeline, capital requirements, and risk profile, rather than investors treating fusion like ordinary software traction.
