# Lawrence J. Peter

Lawrence J. Peter appears in [[the-laws-of-the-office-revisited]] as the author associated with the [[PeterPrinciple]]. The episode traces the principle to his bestselling satirical book about hierarchies and promotion.

His role in the source is to name a recurring management failure: good performance in one job is often used as evidence for promotion into a different job, even when the new role requires different skills. [[StephanieByrne]]'s self-demotion gives the source's concrete case for escaping that mismatch.
