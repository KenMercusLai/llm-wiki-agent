# Hightouch

Hightouch is the enterprise data and AI platform for marketers discussed in [[tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly-tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly]]. [[KashishGupta]] says the company began in [[YCombinator]] Summer 2019 as a natural-language travel booking startup, then moved into helping companies activate customer data for sales and marketing after COVID made travel unattractive.

The episode frames Hightouch as an [[EnterpriseDataActivation]] case. Enterprises already had large customer-data stores in systems such as [[Snowflake]] and [[Databricks]], but still struggled to get that data into advertising, lifecycle marketing, and sales tools under volume and governance constraints. Hightouch's architectural answer was to avoid storing customer data and instead make SaaS tools reflect the customer's own database.

Gupta says Hightouch has repeatedly redefined itself: first as a data connection product, then as a marketer-friendly interface, and then as an AI decisioning and agent platform for marketing teams. That makes it a case for [[EnterpriseFirstProductFit]], [[CustomerEvidenceStrategy]], and [[AIMarketingDecisioning]] rather than only a data-pipeline company.
