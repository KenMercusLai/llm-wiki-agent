# Insta360 / 影石

Insta360, also discussed as 影石, appears in [[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]] as [[DJI]]'s strongest camera-category competitor outside drones and gimbal cameras. The episode frames Insta360 as the leading 360-degree camera company and treats its category as a possible challenge to DJI's Pocket-style gimbal cameras.

The source's key product contrast is workflow. Gimbal cameras help creators frame stable Vlog and daily-life shots at capture time, while 360-degree cameras reduce capture-time framing pressure but push more work into editing. That makes Insta360 central to [[PortableCreatorCameras]] because the category competition is about creator workflow, not only optical quality.
