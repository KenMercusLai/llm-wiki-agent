# Kevin Kelly

Kevin Kelly appears in [[kate-crawford-mapping-empires]] as the Q&A interlocutor pressing [[KateCrawford]] on whether public, sovereign, or publicly accountable AI could exist; whether AI critique is partly capitalism critique; and what would need to change for AI to benefit more people over decades.

His role in this source is to turn Crawford's historical-material critique into governance questions. The exchange helps define [[PublicInterestAI]] around public accountability, sustainability, consent-based data, and careful domain selection rather than simply building national copies of the largest private AI stacks.
