# Redpoint

Redpoint appears in [[tsr-s5-jared-v3final-tsr-s5-jared-v3final]] as the venture firm [[Scribd]] chose for its Series A after the company launched, drew heavy startup-media attention, and received term sheets from Redpoint, [[CRV]], and [[SequoiaCapital|Sequoia]]. [[JaredFriedman]] says the round was about $4 million at a $21 million post-money valuation.

The source uses Redpoint mainly as financing context. The firm marks Scribd's shift from nearly broke post-launch startup into a venture-backed company that still had to survive traffic decline, [[UserGeneratedContentCopyrightRisk]], and later the 2008 financial crisis.
