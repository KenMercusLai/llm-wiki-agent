# Luther, Oklahoma

Luther, Oklahoma appears in [[peace-fire-further-us-iran-strikes-6a4f7acffe878dc8e27e6fbf]] as the [[Route66]] town where the [[ThreateFillingStation]] stands. The episode describes it as around 80 miles from Tulsa and 30 miles from Oklahoma City, and as sitting between two Black towns.

The place matters because distance and safety shaped Black road travel. In the source, Luther is not only a nostalgic highway stop; it is part of the geography that made [[BlackTravelInfrastructure]] necessary.
