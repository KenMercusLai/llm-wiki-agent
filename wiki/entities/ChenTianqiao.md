# Chen Tianqiao

Chen Tianqiao appears in [[e242-zuikuai-bannian-ai-paotong-zi-jinhua-yu-chen-tianqiao-shouxi-kexuejia-liaoliao-guigu-moxing-bi-zheng-zhi-di]] as the strategic figure behind [[Apodex]]. The source says he funded, founded, and actively directs the company, with a strong preference for Heavy Duty Solver work over consumer chat, image generation, or video generation.

The episode presents Chen's influence as both organizational and model-level. [[LiBeibin]] says Chen frequently discusses strategy with the team and corrects research drift when academic instincts diverge from the company's broader direction. He also says Apodex's model constitution is largely shaped by Chen, including an emphasis on truthfulness and on decomposing hard problems before rushing into search.
