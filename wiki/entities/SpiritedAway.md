# 《千与千寻》 / Spirited Away

[[SpiritedAway|《千与千寻》 / Spirited Away]] is named in [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]] as a modern otherworld story that can be compared with [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》]]. The episode uses it to show how a child entering a strange rule-world remains a durable narrative pattern.

For the wiki, the film extends [[OtherworldPortalNarrative]] and [[FemaleSelfPossession]]: the emphasis is not merely on crossing into fantasy, but on a young protagonist learning how to act when familiar names, labor, bodies, and social rules change. That is why the source links it to Alice's active self-command.

## Key Claims
- The film belongs to the same broad otherworld-entry pattern as Alice.
- Its relevance lies in child agency under strange rules, not only in visual fantasy.
- The comparison helps the episode connect Victorian children's literature to modern animation.
