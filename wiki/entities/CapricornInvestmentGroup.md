# Capricorn Investment Group

Capricorn Investment Group appears in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]] as the lead investor for [[Helion]]'s Series B. [[DavidKirtley]] uses the investor story to explain why long-term investor fit matters when a company is building [[CommercialFusionPower]] instead of chasing faster side revenue.

The source links Capricorn to [[DeepTechProductFocus]] because Kirtley says supportive long-term investors give him room to reject tempting markets such as helium-3 sales when those markets do not solve Helion's core electricity mission.
