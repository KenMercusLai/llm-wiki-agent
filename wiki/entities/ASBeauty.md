# AS Beauty

AS Beauty is the beauty company [[JoeyShamah]] discusses after his [[ELFCosmetics]] exit in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. The episode says AS Beauty acquired brands including Laura Geller Beauty and Julep after a bankruptcy process.

The company shows Joey applying a later-career playbook based less on starting a brand from scratch and more on acquiring, operating, licensing, and reviving consumer brands.
