# Marcus Licinius Crassus / 克拉苏

Marcus Licinius Crassus / 克拉苏 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as the third figure in the First Triumvirate with [[JuliusCaesar]] and [[Pompey]]. The episode uses his presence to show how late-republic politics depended on personal alliances among military reputation, wealth, offices, and family strategy rather than only formal republican procedure.

His death removes one of the balancing forces between Caesar and Pompey. Together with the death of Caesar's daughter Julia, this loss makes the alliance harder to hold and helps expose the underlying [[LateRomanRepublicCrisis]].
