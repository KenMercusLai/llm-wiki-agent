# 《有声电影》

《有声电影》 is a [[LaoShe|老舍]] story discussed in [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]]. The episode retells it as a family outing where logistics, seating, snacks, coughing, gossip, and distraction overwhelm the actual film until the characters have barely seen or heard it.

The hosts especially note the opening situation: someone forms a theory about sound film before actually experiencing one. The source therefore uses the story as a comic social analogue to [[ObservationBeforeInference]], with the failed moviegoing also adjacent to [[CivicAudienceEtiquette]].
