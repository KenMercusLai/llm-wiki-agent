# 张志浩

张志浩 is the [[LishiBoke|《历史播客》]] guest in [[69-xianliao-tuili-wenxue-lishixuezhe-ke-bu-jiu-shi-zhentan-ma-666244465]]. The episode presents him as someone with cultural-relics or archaeology training who also talks about literature and other historical topics.

His main contribution to the wiki is [[HistoricalDetectiveReasoning]]. He frames an archaeological site as a very old crime scene: the researcher has to compare fragments, traces, later stories, and missing evidence rather than simply receive the past as a finished narrative.
