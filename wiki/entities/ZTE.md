# ZTE

ZTE is one of the "中华酷联" companies in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode emphasizes that ZTE's telecom-equipment background and operator relationships made it well suited to the 3G contract-phone era, including rapid production of many customized models for different standards and carrier demands.

The source treats ZTE's handset story as a channel-transition problem. Carrier customization produced volume, but it did not create enough independent consumer-brand strength once subsidies receded and the market shifted toward product, software, retail, and brand identity.
