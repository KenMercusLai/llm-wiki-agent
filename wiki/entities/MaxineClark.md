# Maxine Clark

Maxine Clark is the [[BuildABear]] founder interviewed by [[GuyRaz]] in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]]. The episode presents her as a retail operator who moved from Hecht's, [[MayDepartmentStores]], Venture Stores, and [[PaylessShoeSource]] into founding a child-centered [[ExperientialRetail]] concept at age 48.

Her role in the wiki is to connect customer empathy with execution detail. The Build-A-Bear idea came from listening to children, but Maxine's prior work gave her the operational tools to write a ten-year plan, negotiate mall leases, handle vendor relationships, protect trademarks, attract [[BarneyEbsworth]], and eventually hand the company to [[SharonPriceJohn]].
