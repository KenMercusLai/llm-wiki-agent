# 老李（《离婚》） / Lao Li

老李 is the central figure in [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]] as read by [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]]. The episode presents him as a half-awake intellectual who wants romance, ideals, and a real life, yet cannot easily turn disgust into protection, confrontation, or departure.

His weakness remains morally complicated. He fails [[LiTaitaiLihun|李太太]] during the dinner humiliation and projects poetry onto [[MaShaonainaiLihun|马少奶奶]], but he also helps [[ZhangDageLihun|张大哥]] when others avoid him and finally leaves Beiping with wife, children, and [[DingErLihun|丁二]]. The source therefore treats him as a case for [[OrdinaryEscapeCourage]]: not a clean hero, but an ordinary person who manages one costly move.
