# 芦苇 / Lu Wei

芦苇 is the screenwriter figure linking [[CreationOfTheGodsI|《封神》]] and [[FarewellMyConcubine|《霸王别姬》]] in [[64-bawangbieji-fengmo-yu-chenghuo-656094350]]. The episode begins from the host noticing his involvement in 《封神》, then uses that connection to revisit why 《霸王别姬》 remains a central film for the host.

The source presents Lu Wei as a writer whose work is sensitive to tyranny, public confession, and the gap between official story and lived coercion. It also recounts the host's memory that Lu Wei had been imprisoned after being charged over dancing and had written from prison experience; the wiki keeps this as a source-local account rather than independently verified biography.
