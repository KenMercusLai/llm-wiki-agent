# Eibon / 伊波恩

Eibon / 伊波恩 is a sorcerer figure discussed in [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]] through [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]]'s more comic and adventurous mythos stories. The episode describes him as a great wizard connected with the Book of Eibon and with [[Tsathoggua|萨托古雅]], whose help allows him to flee religious persecution through a gate to Saturn.

The episode's Eibon material matters because it complicates a simple horror-only image of the [[CthulhuMythos|Cthulhu Mythos / 克苏鲁神话]]. His escape, forced coexistence with pursuer Morghi, encounter with Saturnian residents, and mistaken prophecy all belong to [[HorrorHumorWeirdFiction]] as much as to cosmic terror.

## Key Claims
- Eibon anchors the episode's relatively "optimistic" Smith story thread.
- His friendship with [[Tsathoggua]] shows Smith's mythos figures as sources of strange aid as well as threat.
- His Saturn exile turns doctrinal persecution into absurd survival comedy.
- The episode uses him to show that [[SmithStoryWorlds]] can move from ancient fantasy into planetary displacement.
