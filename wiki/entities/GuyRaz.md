# Guy Raz

Guy Raz is the [[HowIBuiltThis]] host who interviews [[JustinGold]] in [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]] and hosts the Advice Line episode [[advice-line-with-jeffrey-hollender-of-seventh-generation-f8ce2410-1176-47d9-be44-03f9fa945714]] with [[JeffreyHollender]]. The Justin's episode uses his interview format to draw out the founder story behind [[JustinsNutButter]], while the Advice Line episode shows him diagnosing consumer-product positioning, sampling, social content, and growth questions with active founders.
