# Harvey

Harvey is the legal-AI company used in [[ai-jibao-26q2-cong-coding-dao-rsi-qiangzhe-yu-qiang-de-weilai-1-171-1]] as the concrete case for [[EnterpriseOwnedModels]]. The source says Harvey worked with [[AppliedCompute]] to post-train a legal-domain model based on [[GLM52]] or a related GLM line, and that the resulting model performed strongly on Harvey's legal-agent benchmark.

The episode's point is strategic rather than purely legal: companies with high-value proprietary data, clear evaluation systems, and frequent expert workflows may prefer owning or post-training a domain model instead of relying only on [[OpenAI]] or [[Anthropic]] frontier services.
