# 《历史播客》

In this wiki, the show matters because it supplies the guest perspective that turns detective fiction into a method question. The episode uses [[LishiBoke|《历史播客》]]'s historical orientation to connect mystery reading with archaeology, archive comparison, public memory, and [[PersonalArchiveAsHistory|ordinary preservation]].
