# Su Yu / 苏煜

Su Yu is presented in [[139-agent-de-zongshu-he-su-yu-liao-agent-jishushi-openclaw-moment-bianjie-de-xiaomi-he-shehui-de-fushe-luffrgudeiighqxam49tfqci63no]] as an Ohio State University computer-science professor and founder of [[NeoCognition]], working on [[LanguageAgent]], [[ComputerUseAgent]], MMMU, Mind2Web, CACT, and related agent evaluation and interaction problems.

His main contribution in the source is a historical synthesis. He uses [[MemoryAutonomyFramework]] to connect early logical agents, expert systems, neural RL agents, [[SemanticParsing]], and modern LLM-based agents into one continuity: agents are systems that remember enough about a world and act autonomously enough to achieve goals inside it.

Su's product and research thesis is that [[OpenClawMoment]] made agent capability legible through permission, always-on, local-context, and personal-agent interaction patterns, while the next technical bottleneck is [[ContinualLearning]] for domain-specific [[WorldModels]] and [[SpecializedIntelligence]].
