# Maria Corina Machado

Maria Corina Machado is the Venezuelan opposition leader discussed in [[fault-lines-venezuelas-paltry-earthquake-response-6a44e38375e7a3e96111931b]]. The episode says she is trying to return to [[Venezuela]], but that American officials appear reluctant to support the move during the earthquake-response crisis.

In the source's political reading, Machado's return could complicate Washington's backing of the [[DelcyRodriguez]]-led arrangement and sharpen tensions around the promised [[DemocraticTransitionElection]]. She functions as the opposition pressure point against a disaster response that the public already views as weak.
