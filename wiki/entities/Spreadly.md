# Spreadly

Spreadly is the second weak startup idea [[DanSiroker]] and [[PeteKoomen]] tried before [[Optimizely]], discussed in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]]. Unlike [[CarrotSticks]], it came from their own distribution problem, but Dan says the incentive model did not work.

The source uses Spreadly as an intermediate founder-fit lesson. Building from one's own pain helped more than guessing a school product from the outside, but the product still needed behavior that users actually wanted to repeat. [[YCombinator]] then helped the founders focus on the clearer [[ABTestingForMarketers]] opportunity.
