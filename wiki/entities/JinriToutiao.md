# Jinri Toutiao

Jinri Toutiao appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as [[ByteDance]]'s information-feed product and the benchmark [[Tencent]] tried to answer with [[TiantianKuaibao]]. The episode's information-flow section says Tencent News, Jinri Toutiao, and Tiantian Kuaibao were closer competitors before short video became the defining battlefield.

The source uses Jinri Toutiao to illustrate [[RecommendationDistributionAdvantage]]: ByteDance's strength was not only a content app, but an operating method around recommendation, growth, data, and ad conversion that later transferred into [[Douyin]] and [[OceanEngine]].

[[quanmian-yazhi-buliu-kongdang-zijie-tiaodong-ruhe-zuo-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-7-ji-lqszvmur6jv8b9xt8rjgmwfdqtec]] adds Jinri Toutiao as the original growth-method source. [[XuHongliang]] says Toutiao-style push, sharing回流, red dots, feed monetization, and LTV accounting were later reused in [[Douyin]], [[TikTok]], and ByteDance's broader growth platform.

[[faquan-liebian-jisuban-ruhe-yong-hongbao-sheji-zengzhang-zijie-tiaodong-di-8-ji-lijpz1zgy9h2k4qd0c45ixwenu-0]] adds [[ToutiaoLite]] as Jinri Toutiao's defensive and adaptive Lite-app layer after [[QuToutiao]] proved the net-earning content pattern. The source says the Lite version could learn the reward mechanics while leaning on ByteDance's stronger content, recommendation, advertising, and LTV system.
