# 三美电机

三美电机 appears in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the corporate backer around [[AnneSanitaryNapkin|安妮卫生巾]]. The episode uses it mainly through [[MoriBuichi|森布一]]'s support for [[ItaiTaeko|板井太子]] and through the later corporate changes that contributed to Anne losing its leading position.

The page is source-scoped. Its value here is not a full electronics-company history, but the way a corporate parent and its capital allocation affected whether a stigmatized [[MenstrualProductSocialHistory|menstrual product]] could be launched at mass scale.
