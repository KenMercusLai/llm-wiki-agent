# Boris Johnson

Boris Johnson appears in [[keep-qualms-and-carry-on-a-decade-after-brexit-6a3a53028a3c8822ab345d4b]] as a pivotal [[Brexit]] figure. The episode says his decision to back Leave mattered in a narrow referendum, and that he later led the [[UnitedKingdom]] into a harder form of Brexit that formally took effect in 2020.

The source also links Johnson to [[PostBrexitImmigrationPolitics]]. Despite sovereignty and migration-control rhetoric during the Brexit campaign, the episode says net migration later surged under Johnson's post-Brexit system, producing disillusionment among voters who expected migration to fall.
