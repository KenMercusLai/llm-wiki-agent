# Kindaichi Kyosuke / 金田一京助

Kindaichi Kyosuke / 金田一京助 appears in [[shaqingqing-x-hanzhao-shichuan-zhuomu-wo-baochi-de-sixiang-doushi-yinwei-queqian-894931314]] as one of [[IshikawaTakuboku|石川啄木]]'s most important friends and supporters. The episode emphasizes that Kindaichi helped Takuboku emotionally and financially, even though Takuboku later experienced the friendship as constraint and the relationship broke down for a time.

For the wiki, Kindaichi matters because he shows that Takuboku's surviving work was not produced by solitary genius alone. Friends, editors, bosses, and later literary allies helped carry a poet whose talent was real but whose life was materially unstable, making the source a case for [[LiteraryPublishingAsMaterialSupport]].

## Key Claims
- Kindaichi is presented as a friend whose support made Takuboku's life and work more possible.
- His later respect for Takuboku's talent complicates a purely moralizing view of Takuboku's dependence.
- The friendship also shows Takuboku's self-destructive tendency to damage the relationships he relied on.
