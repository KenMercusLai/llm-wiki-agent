# Gino Wickman

Gino Wickman is the founder of [[EOSWorldwide]] and author of *Traction*, as discussed in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. [[MarkAbbott]] says he met Wickman around 2012 and told him he wanted to build software around EOS-like ideas.

## Position In The Episode
- Represents the methodology and implementer ecosystem that shaped [[Ninety]]'s early product and distribution.
- Told Abbott that EOS had tried software but that software was not in the organization's DNA.
- His response left space for Abbott to pursue EOS-compatible software while still needing to earn trust in the community.

## Connections
- [[EOSWorldwide]] — organization he founded.
- [[Ninety]] — software company built around the ecosystem.
- [[FrameworkLedSaaS]] — pattern where software depends on credibility with a preexisting framework community.
