# Hanting Hotel

Hanting Hotel appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as [[JiQi]]'s post-[[HomeInns]] hotel-chain venture. The source says it began as "Hanting Inn" and later became Hanting Hotel, with cleanliness as an early positioning point.

In the source, Hanting is a bridge from [[Ctrip]]'s online booking world to [[HuazhuGroup]]'s hotel-chain standardization world. It helps explain why large hotel groups can later bargain differently with an OTA than individual hotels or homestays can.

## Connections
- [[JiQi]] — founder/operator connected to Hanting in the episode.
- [[HuazhuGroup]] — later group brand architecture.
- [[Ctrip]], [[OnlineTravelAgency]], and [[HomestayDifferentiation]] — online platform and lodging-market context.
