# Kyle Vogt

Kyle Vogt appears in [[socialradarspod-emmettshear-v2]] as the technical cofounder who helped [[JustinTV|Justin.tv]] build its own live-video infrastructure. [[EmmettShear]] says the team found Kyle by emailing an MIT hacker list while looking for hardware help, then came to see him as a true cofounder because his contribution was central.

The source's strongest Kyle Vogt point is infrastructure learning. Justin.tv initially used a CDN, but cost and feature limits pushed the team to build more of the video system internally. Kyle's early system included a proxy that saved a copy of the stream as it passed through, making him part of the technical path from a lifecasting stunt toward [[Twitch]].
