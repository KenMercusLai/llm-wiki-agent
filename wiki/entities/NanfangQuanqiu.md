# 南方全球

南方全球 is the QDII product context for [[e158-zichan-peizhi-yu-youxiao-qianyan-qu-zhao-genghaode-geng-buyiyangde-geng-tiejin-shidaide-luzri-gwmqhb02k9xmf6mcdsaqkc]]. [[YunLei]] describes it as an older product with useful overseas-investment qualifications and a broad toolbox: stock ETFs, overseas active funds, bond ETFs, gold ETFs, oil ETFs, and REIT-related products.

The episode's claim is not that the product qualification alone creates return. Its value is that a QDII vehicle can implement [[AssetAllocation]] and [[FOFProductDesign]] choices when the manager uses a clear strategic base, limited tactical allocation, [[TreasuryDurationRisk]] awareness, and client-result discipline.
