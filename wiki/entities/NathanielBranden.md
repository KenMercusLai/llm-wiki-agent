# Nathaniel Branden / 纳撒尼尔·布兰登

Nathaniel Branden / 纳撒尼尔·布兰登 appears in [[135-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-san-dan-823746670]] as [[AynRand|Ayn Rand / 安·兰德]]'s disciple, organizer, and lover in the episode's [[Objectivism|Objectivism / 客观主义]] branch. The source says Branden helped Rand build a movement-like intellectual circle and promote rational self-interest through public talks, debate, and discipleship.

The episode's main use of Branden is the break. It says Rand, who had defended romantic freedom, reacted with fury when Branden later formed a relationship with a younger woman, then publicly severed ties and tried to destroy his standing in the movement. Branden therefore becomes the concrete case for [[FreeLovePracticeGap]] inside the Rand section.
