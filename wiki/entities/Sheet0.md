# Sheet0

Sheet0 is the startup founded by [[WangWenfeng]] and discussed in [[openclaw-zhihou-wo-zhi-xiang-weilai-3-6-ge-yue-de-shiqing-duitan-sheet0-chuangshiren-wang-wenfeng-lu-d4y7qifag6-rc79tp-roxjp4z]]. In this source, the company is moving from a longer-term reliability and explainability thesis toward productizing its own internal [[AIManagingAI]] workflow: AI reads product or engineering tasks, uses coding agents to implement and test them, submits [[GitHub]] PRs, and leaves engineers as final reviewers.

The episode frames Sheet0 as a small, high-leverage AI-native team. Wang says the company had seven people instead of the larger headcount it might previously have hired, spent about $20,000 on AI coding in the prior month, and treats unbounded token access as part of training the organization to become a much higher-output team.

## Source Position
- Sheet0's new product direction is aimed first at engineers, developers, founders, super product managers, and builders who already have strong ownership and many ideas.
- The company wants to raise ordinary AI usage from a low baseline to a competent operating level, not merely sell another model wrapper.
- The pivot reflects Wang's revised startup method: follow visible bottlenecks over the next three to six months instead of over-optimizing for distant terminal states.
