# Peter Navarro

Peter Navarro appears in [[vol-113-cong-jiqian-ye-zhiku-wenjian-zhong-goule-telangpu-2-0-zhizheng-jihua-beihou-de-ren-jigou-sixiang-he-mailuo-lihnknc-3om5evexocb6a6zdwueu]] as the more tariff-friendly authorial voice in [[Project2025]]'s trade chapter. The episode says the chapter contains both tariff-skeptical and tariff-supportive views, with Navarro representing the position that trade deficits harm manufacturing, blue-collar wages, supply chains, and the defense-industrial base.

The source's Navarro section is important because it translates trade conflict into national-security and labor-market language. Once trade deficits are framed through [[SupplyChainSovereignty]] and industrial capacity, the episode argues that countries such as [[China]] cannot respond only with ordinary commercial logic.
