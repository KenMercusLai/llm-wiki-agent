# Voltaire / 伏尔泰

Voltaire / 伏尔泰 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as a salesperson fighting [[JeanJacquesRousseau|卢梭]]. The episode places their conflict inside company sales competition, making philosophical disagreement look like a fight over orders, credit, and status.

The source also uses the scene to let [[FriedrichNietzsche|尼采]] criticize Enlightenment rationality as another possible object of worship. Voltaire therefore links the sales fight to [[PureRationalityTrap]] and the episode's suspicion of corporate reason.
