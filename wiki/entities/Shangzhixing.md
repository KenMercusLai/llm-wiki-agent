# Shangzhixing

Shangzhixing is the early hotel-booking and membership-card business associated with [[WuHai]] in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode treats it as one of the operating roots that helped [[Ctrip]] move from a small booking operation to a scalable [[OnlineTravelAgency]].

Its importance is not only the brand itself but the method: airport card distribution, phone-based conversion, standardized hotel prices, and user identity through membership cards before mobile apps made account-based booking normal.

## Connections
- [[WuHai]] — operator connected to Shangzhixing.
- [[Ctrip]] — later platform that absorbed these operating methods.
- [[OnlineTravelAgency]], [[TravelSuperAppConvenience]], and [[HotelPlatformPricingPower]] — concepts tied to user acquisition and supplier pricing.
