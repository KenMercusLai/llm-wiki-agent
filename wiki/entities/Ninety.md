# Ninety

Ninety is the SaaS company discussed in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. [[MarkAbbott]] describes it as a platform for helping leadership teams align around vision, planning, performance, meetings, rocks, scorecards, issues, and execution rhythms.

## Key Points
- Grew to nearly $44 million in ARR and more than 18,000 customer companies by the time of the episode.
- Began from Abbott's long-running belief that company-building needed better tools, disciplines, and operating-system support.
- Built around the [[EOSWorldwide]] ecosystem, first as "EOS compatible" and later as a licensed product.
- Used [[CommunityLedSaaSGrowth]] through coaches, implementers, peer groups, and self-implementing companies before scaling paid marketing.
- Raised $55 million across rounds involving Insight Partners, Blue Cloud Ventures, and Catalyst Ventures.
- Is embedding AI in the platform and considering a longer-term move toward AI-native software through products such as [[Mas]].
