# LOVOT

LOVOT is a companion robot referenced by [[Shibo]] in [[wo-yudao-le-di-yige-zhenzheng-xiang-mai-de-peiban-jiqiren-duihua-shibo-yueban-dongli-chuangshiren-gonglu-boke-lrydelizm0-hbk68u5cqe3ti-epb]]. The source says Shibo bought one before founding [[YuebanDongli]] and still keeps it powered on, largely because its simple interaction pattern feels emotionally appropriate.

## Source Position
- Used as a reference for restrained interaction: it can approach a person, signal desire to be held, and create affection without adding many functions.
- Helps explain why [[Xiaoban]] does not simply pursue human speech or a larger tool set.
- Shows that [[CompanionRobots]] may generate value through timing, posture, and social rituals rather than task completion.

## Connections
- [[Shibo]] — owner and observer of the product.
- [[Xiaoban]] — product shaped partly by lessons from LOVOT.
- [[RobotLiveliness]] — design goal that can come from simple embodied behavior.
