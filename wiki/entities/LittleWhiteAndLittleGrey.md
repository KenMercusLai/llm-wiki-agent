# Little White and Little Grey

Little White and Little Grey are the two belugas at the center of the whale-sanctuary segment in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]]. The episode says they were caught in [[Russia]] when small, sent to a Shanghai theme park in [[China]], and later moved by the [[SeaLifeTrust]] to [[Iceland]].

The source treats their case as a practical test of [[CaptiveWhaleSanctuary]]. They cannot simply be released because long captivity weakened survival skills, but the attempt to move them from indoor concrete pools into a netted natural bay has been slowed by stress, health problems, weather, the pandemic, and an oil spill.
