# Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji / 富岳三十六景

Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji / 富岳三十六景 is the [[KatsushikaHokusai|Katsushika Hokusai]] print series used as the formal reference for [[RogerZelazny|Zelazny]]'s [[TwentyFourViewsOfMountFujiByHokusai|《富岳二十四景》]] in [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]]. The episode emphasizes the idea of seeing Mount Fuji from different positions and turns that visual sequence into a way of understanding the story's travel, confrontation, and final title-only blankness.
