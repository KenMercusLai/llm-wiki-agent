# Yinglingdian AI / Þï▒þüÁµ«┐

Yinglingdian AI, or Þï▒þüÁµ«┐, is the [[AIForScience]] company founded by [[HaotianOdin]] and discussed in [[ai4s-xuyao-kuangren-yu-yexinjia-duihua-yinglingdian-odin-ruguo-shen-cunzai-wo-zenneng-rongren-ziji-bushi-shen-gonglu-boke-lhceyip6dqomrwk38uvqjwoomxyz]]. The source describes the company as a roughly 30-person startup with tens of millions of dollars in financing, aiming first to become a strong [[AIDrugDiscoveryPlatform]] and eventually to move toward general scientific AI.

The company's technical identity is the [[AllModalMolecularWorldModel]] bet. Instead of treating small molecules, proteins, RNA, and DNA as separate modeling domains, the company wants to identify shared structure across molecular modalities and use AI to model, simulate, and design interactions among them. [[ODesign|O-Design / Odyssey]] is the early exploration, while [[LDesign|L-Design]] is presented as a near milestone closer to a molecular world model.

The company's current business stance is shaped by [[PlatformPipelineBiotechStrategy]]. The source says Yinglingdian does not plan to build its own drug pipelines in the short term because pipelines could distract the team from AI and make large pharmaceutical customers see the company as a competitor. That stance complements but differs from [[Kaiwuji]]'s [[MaterialsPipelineCompany]] route: both are AI-for-science companies, but one source emphasizes using the model internally to own material IP, while this source emphasizes keeping the platform neutral for drug-discovery customers.
