# e.l.f. Cosmetics

e.l.f. Cosmetics is the value beauty company discussed in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. [[JoeyShamah]] and [[ScottVincentBorba]] built it around the idea that eyes, lips, and face products could sell for one dollar without feeling generic or low quality.

The episode presents e.l.f. as a CPG scaling case where [[LowPriceBrandPerception]], [[RetailIncrementality]], [[DirectToConsumerCashFlow]], [[AccidentalVirality]], and [[SalesVelocity]] matter as much as the product formulas themselves. Its early path moved from rejected dollar-store pitches to magazine PR, e-commerce, [[HEB]], [[Target]], and later [[TSGConsumerPartners]] and [[TPG]] capital.
