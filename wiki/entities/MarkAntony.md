# Mark Antony / 马克·安东尼

Mark Antony / 马克·安东尼 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]]'s trusted general, political ally, and crucial aftermath figure. The episode highlights the Lupercalia scene where Antony offered Caesar a crown, one of the incidents that convinced republican opponents that Caesar was testing the boundary of kingship.

After the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination]], Antony matters because [[MarcusBrutus]] opposed killing Caesar's allies. That choice let Antony help control the public funeral and later join [[OctavianAugustus|Octavian]] and Lepidus in renewed civil conflict against the republican side.

The source later follows Antony into the final conflict with Octavian and [[CleopatraVII]], making him one of the bridges from Caesar's personal rule to the [[RomanEmpire|imperial settlement]] that followed.
