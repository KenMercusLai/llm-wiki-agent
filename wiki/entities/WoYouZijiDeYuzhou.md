# 《我有自己的宇宙》

《我有自己的宇宙》 is [[QianJing|钱静]]'s book discussed in [[sp-02-wo-you-ziji-de-yuzhou-ruhe-zai-hundun-zhong-zuo-ge-qingxing-de-xingdongpai-748593778]]. The episode presents it as a practical psychology book about becoming clearer and more self-directed inside disorder rather than as a promise of effortless self-realization.

The book's episode-facing idea is that a person should not confuse "having myself" with small private comforts, social-media performance, or reflexive rebellion. The durable self has to be tested in labor, contact with others, value creation, family separation, ambition, and repeated decisions under constraint.
