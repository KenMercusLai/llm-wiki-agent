# Erica Barris

Erica Barris appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] participant giving a Valentine to the U.S. penny. Her segment calls the penny "the one that got away" and treats it as an object of memory and language even as the source says new penny minting is ending.

The episode uses Barris to separate [[CoinRetirementEconomics]] from cultural attachment. The penny may be costly to produce and less needed in a digital-payments economy, but it still survives in sayings, tactile memory, and everyday idioms.
