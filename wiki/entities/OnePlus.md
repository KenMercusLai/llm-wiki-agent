# OnePlus

OnePlus appears in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]] as a focused smartphone brand founded by Liu Zuohu, who came from [[OPPO]]'s Blu-ray business. The episode positions OnePlus as different from many short-lived domestic phone attempts because it leaned on OPPO supply-chain support while building an enthusiast and overseas presence, especially in markets such as India.

The source also notes that OnePlus later merged teams more fully with OPPO, making it a case of brand focus within a larger hardware system rather than a fully independent durable phone giant.
