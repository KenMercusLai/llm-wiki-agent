# ArtX

ArtX is the art-gallery software idea [[PaulGraham]] discusses in [[socialradarsseason1-pg-v4-updated-finallll-socialradarsseason1-pg-v4-updated-finallll]] as the failed predecessor to [[Viaweb]]. Graham says galleries did not buy or sell art this way, and the team could barely persuade galleries to accept free websites.

The important wiki point is that ArtX failed as a market idea but left behind useful technical work. Its website-generator base could be redirected toward online stores, turning a weak gallery market into the [[WebBasedSoftware]] and ecommerce path that became Viaweb.
