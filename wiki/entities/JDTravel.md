# JD Travel

JD Travel appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as a possible later online-travel competitor to [[Ctrip]]. The source uses it as one example among adjacent Chinese internet platforms that may enter hotel and travel booking.

The entity's relevance is competitive rather than historical in this episode: if ecommerce platforms can add hotel, ticket, and itinerary services, they can pressure part of [[TravelSuperAppConvenience]] even without Ctrip's full OTA history.
