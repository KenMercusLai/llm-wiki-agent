# 《越女剑》

《越女剑》 appears in [[112-xianliao-jinyong-diyidan-mingjiao-jingran-jintian-hai-cunzai-754593095]] as [[JinYong|金庸]]'s most ancient and most romantic Jianghu point. The episode emphasizes that the novella is not pure invention: it develops material from the Yue maiden and "Yuan Gong" sword-testing tradition associated with older Chinese records.

The source uses the novella to connect [[WuxiaIntertextualCraft]] with [[MythicSourceLayering]]. The white ape, ancient swordswoman, and near-mythic martial power show how Jin Yong can inherit earlier legendary motifs while making them feel like the origin horizon of later wuxia.

In the hosts' larger chronology, 《越女剑》 also marks what later Jin Yong worlds lose. The freedom and overwhelming martial force of this early setting contrast with Song institutional complexity, Ming social control, and the eventual decline of Jianghu ethics under court power, conspiracy, and firearms.
