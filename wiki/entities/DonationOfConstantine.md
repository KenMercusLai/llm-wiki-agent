# Donation of Constantine / 君士坦丁献土

The Donation of Constantine enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as the episode's historical forgery comparison for [[Majestic12Documents]]. QinZong and Beimin use it to show how language, material, and historical analysis can expose a document that authority once wanted to preserve.

Its role is to clarify [[ScientificSelfCorrection]] by contrast. A religious or political institution may resist correction because authority is at stake; scientific method is supposed to make correction part of legitimacy.
