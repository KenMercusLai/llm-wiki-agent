# Great Treason Incident / 大逆事件

The Great Treason Incident / 大逆事件 enters the wiki through [[shaqingqing-x-hanzhao-shichuan-zhuomu-wo-baochi-de-sixiang-doushi-yinwei-queqian-894931314]] as the late-Meiji crackdown on anarchists and socialists that deeply concerned [[IshikawaTakuboku|石川啄木]] near the end of his life. The episode says the event pushed Takuboku toward clearer socialist reading and politically charged writing.

Within this source, the event is not only political background. It gives shape to [[PoliticalClosureAsLiteraryPressure]]: the feeling that an era has become blocked, surveilled, and morally airless can move poetry from private complaint toward public indictment.

## Key Claims
- The incident links Takuboku's private distress to state repression and ideological closure.
- It helps explain why late Takuboku includes socialist reading, political essays, and works connected to the case.
- The source treats the incident as part of [[ModernJapaneseInteriorDiscovery]] because the inner life being discovered is also historically pressured.
