# Home Inns

Home Inns appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] through [[JiQi]]'s shift from [[Ctrip]] into hotel operations. The source says Ctrip and a hotel partner worked on Home Inns, with Ji involved in preparation and operation before later leaving.

The page matters because Home Inns connects online booking demand aggregation to standardized hotel supply. That supply-side learning later feeds the episode's discussion of [[HantingHotel]], [[HuazhuGroup]], and hotel groups trying to build direct-booking channels.
