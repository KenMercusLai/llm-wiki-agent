# Alex (GMI Cloud)

Alex appears in [[e230-1-wan-yi-shouru-yuqi-beihou-yingweida-de-dianfeng-yu-ruanlei-d97446f1-d6e3-4894-89d1-dca0a362b10b]] as the GPU-cloud and data-center operations guest associated in the episode with [[GMICloud]]. His segment shifts the discussion from chip roadmaps to deployment reality: land, power, modular construction, GPU procurement, firmware, hardware faults, scheduling, and SLA.

He distinguishes [[NeoCloud]] providers from hyperscalers by emphasizing bare-metal efficiency, k8s cluster management, earlier access to new [[Nvidia]] GPUs, and productized model/kernel optimization. His point is that GPU cloud becomes valuable only after the provider can run clusters reliably enough for [[MaaSInfrastructure]] customers.
