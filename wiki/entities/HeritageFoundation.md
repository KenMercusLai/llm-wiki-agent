# Heritage Foundation

The Heritage Foundation appears in [[vol-113-cong-jiqian-ye-zhiku-wenjian-zhong-goule-telangpu-2-0-zhizheng-jihua-beihou-de-ren-jigou-sixiang-he-mailuo-lihnknc-3om5evexocb6a6zdwueu]] as the conservative think tank behind [[Project2025]] and [[MandateForLeadership2025]]. The episode stresses that Heritage's Mandate tradition predates Trump and was built as a governing handoff tool for Republican administrations.

In this source, Heritage matters because it supplies continuity. [[DonaldTrump]]'s personal style is volatile, but Heritage's policy manuals, authors, historical claims about Reagan and Trump's first term, and transition infrastructure make conservative personnel and policy preparation more durable.
