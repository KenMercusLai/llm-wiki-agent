# 《续玄怪录》

《续玄怪录》 is the companion strange-tale collection folded into [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]] beside [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]]. The episode does not keep the two collections as a strict bibliographic lecture; it uses both to map the larger [[TangChuanqi|唐传奇]] world of riddles, gods, dragons, repayment, and immortality tests.

Its source role is especially clear in the 妙寂, dragon-family, and [[DuZiChun|《杜子春》]] discussions. These stories let the episode move from word puzzles and revenge into [[TangStrangeTaleRepaymentEthics]] and [[TranscendenceAgainstHumanFeeling]].
