# European Commission

The European Commission appears in [[tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the regulator investigating [[Google]] over possible competition-rule violations tied to use of digital publishers' content in AI search. The episode uses the investigation as one reason [[GoogleAIOverviews|Google AI Overviews]] may be changing how source links appear.

This page extends the wiki's [[EuropeanUnion]] and [[PlatformAntitrust]] branch from privacy and platform regulation into AI search attribution. The Commission matters here because source visibility, publisher compensation, and search market power become regulatory questions once AI-generated answers depend on publisher material while reducing click-through traffic.
