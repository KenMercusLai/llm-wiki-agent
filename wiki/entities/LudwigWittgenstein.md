# Ludwig Wittgenstein / 维特根斯坦

Ludwig Wittgenstein appears in [[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]] through the episode's claim that the Munchausen self-rescue image can describe the difficulty of using language to explain the world from within the world.

The source uses him to extend [[MunchausenSelfBootstrapping]] beyond general rationality into language. A children's-story impossibility becomes a way to name the limits of explanation itself.
