# Abelardo de la Espriella

Abelardo de la Espriella is the right-wing Colombian presidential winner discussed in [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]]. The source says he wins a narrow runoff in [[Colombia]], benefits from high turnout, and receives endorsement from [[DonaldTrump]].

The episode treats de la Espriella as a [[SecurityBacklashPolitics]] figure rather than only as a partisan winner. His promise is tougher action against gangs and armed groups after frustration with [[GustavoPetro]]'s "Total Peace" strategy, but the source also stresses constraints: legally protected spending, weak congressional support, and the danger that aggressive policing or mass imprisonment can slide into abuses without legal restraint.
