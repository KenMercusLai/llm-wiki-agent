# 微医 / 挂号网

WeDoctor began as Guahao.com, founded by [[LiaoJieyuan]] in 2010, in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode describes it starting from online appointment registration at Huashan Hospital and later expanding to many hospitals and provinces before rebranding as WeDoctor.

The source treats WeDoctor as the appointment-booking and internet-hospital route through [[InternetHealthcare]]. Its Wuzhen Internet Hospital and later [[DigitalHealthCommunity]] work show a shift from moving hospital appointments online toward city-level triage, chronic-disease management, and resource allocation.
