# 牟一凌 / Mou Yiling

牟一凌 is introduced in [[vol-112-yici-feigongshi-de-2024-fansi-he-2025-zhanwang-duihua-lanxiaokang-x-mouyiling-lkasjtodqgmnbpyambqoxl-fcsyv]] as the [[MinshengSecurities|民生证券]] strategy chief. In the episode she provides a sell-side strategy frame for 2024's inconclusive style swings, China's production-price divergence, consumer policy, U.S. dollar contradictions, AI valuation patience, and possible 2025 upside through [[BeltAndRoadExternalDemand]].

Her core contribution is to separate macro strength from listed-company profit. She argues that Chinese national competitiveness, high production volume, or stronger industrial capacity can coexist with lower prices and weaker earnings if [[ChinaSupplySideClearing]] has not occurred.

## Key Claims
- 2024 looked like a year of many near-reversals, but most did not fully validate, so style narratives stayed unstable.
- Production-driven growth can raise output and electricity use while worsening PPI and profit pressure.
- Consumer subsidies that lower effective prices may release quantity and welfare without becoming classic inflationary demand stimulus.
- The most interesting 2025 possibility is an offensive [[BeltAndRoadExternalDemand|Belt and Road demand]] breakthrough rather than only defensive overseas expansion.
