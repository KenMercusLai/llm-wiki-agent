# Crete

Crete is the Mediterranean island at the center of [[59-kelitedao-yangguang-haigui-shenhua-he-erzhan-zhanchang-646904469]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] presents it as more than a holiday setting. The episode uses the island's position between the Greek mainland, Anatolia, Egypt, and the wider Mediterranean to connect [[GreekMythology]], [[MinoanCivilization]], later imperial rule, the [[BattleOfCrete]], sea-turtle observation, and prehistoric extinction.

The source's Crete is a layered place. Myth supplies Zeus, Europa, Minos, the Minotaur, Theseus, Ariadne, Daedalus, and Icarus; archaeology supplies [[KnossosPalace]], the Heraklion museum, bull imagery, marine motifs, palace water systems, and Linear A; World War II supplies airborne invasion, occupation, local resistance, tunnels, Allied retreat, and memorial sites; ecology supplies sea turtles, clear harbor water, squirting cucumber, dwarf mammoths, flightless owls, and Cretan deer.
