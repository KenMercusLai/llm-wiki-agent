# Henry Maine

Henry Maine appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] as the episode's shorthand for a 19th-century patriarchal-eternity view. Under Roman law and Biblical influence, that view treated father-rule and patriarchal family order as humanity's original social form.

The episode uses Maine as the foil that makes [[JohannJakobBachofen]] important. Bachofen's [[MotherRight|《母权论》 / Mother Right]] may be weak as direct evidence, but it breaks the certainty that patriarchal order was never historical, never contingent, and never contested.
