# OpenAI Frontier

OpenAI Frontier appears in [[tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128]] as [[OpenAI]]'s platform for businesses to build and manage AI coworkers. The episode frames the launch less as a simple software product than as an adoption problem: companies need workflow redesign, governance structures, rules, policies, liability decisions, compliance boundaries, risk controls, and employee buy-in.

The source says OpenAI is working with four major consulting firms to help companies adopt these AI coworkers. That makes OpenAI Frontier a concrete example of [[BusinessLedAITransformation]], where model capability must be translated into operational roles, governed access, and measurable work.
