# Joey Shamah

Joey Shamah is the central guest in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] and the co-founder associated with scaling [[ELFCosmetics]]. The episode connects his Brooklyn Syrian Jewish community and family apparel-business background to e.l.f.'s attention to purchase orders, inventory, markups, overseas sourcing, and practical selling.

Joey's role shifts across the episode from first-time founder and hands-on operator to crisis problem solver during the Bloomingdale's rumor, then to seller and post-exit operator after [[TSGConsumerPartners]], [[TPG]], and [[TarangAmin]] entered the company. After leaving e.l.f., he built [[FitForLife]] and [[ASBeauty]].
