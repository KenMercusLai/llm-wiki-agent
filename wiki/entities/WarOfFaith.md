# 追风者

追风者 is the television drama used by [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]] as a gateway into Republican-era Shanghai finance. The episode treats the show as a narrative device for discussing [[GuZhun]], [[SongZiwen]], [[CentralBankOfChina]], bank trainee systems, silver dollars, treasury bonds, Soviet-area finance, and currency warfare.

The source is not a pure drama review. It repeatedly distinguishes the show's compressed or melodramatic scenes from the slower institutional logic of [[RepublicanChinaBankingSystem]], [[AccountingInfrastructure]], and [[TreasuryBondSpeculation]].

## Connections
- [[GuZhun]] — historical figure used as the main real-life comparison for Wei Ruolai.
- [[SongZiwen]] and [[CentralBankOfChina]] — historical-power and central-bank context behind the Shen Tunan discussion.
- [[BankTraineeSystem]] — institutional pattern behind the drama's bank practice-student plot.
