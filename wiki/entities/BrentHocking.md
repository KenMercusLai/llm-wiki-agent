# Brent Hocking

Brent Hocking is [[TomRinks]]' childhood friend and the founder figure behind [[DeLeonTequila]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. He wanted to enter the spirits business with a high-end tequila brand, giving [[RinksCanetti]] a luxury consumer-brand project before [[SunBum]].

The episode uses Brent's tequila project to show Tom's broader thesis that people buy brands partly because of how those brands let them feel about themselves.
