# Dong Zhongshu / 董仲舒

Dong Zhongshu / 董仲舒 is named in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] as part of the intellectual background that linked Confucian political order with Heaven, omens, and imperial sacredness. The episode places him near the development of heavenly-human resonance and related explanations for dynastic legitimacy.

His wiki role is source-scoped. The episode uses him to show that [[WangMang|王莽]]'s omen belief was not merely private absurdity; it drew on a broader [[WesternHanDynasty|Western Han]] political culture where [[MandateOfHeavenLegitimacy]], [[ChenweiPolitics]], and [[AuspiciousOmenPolitics]] could enter statecraft.

## Key Claims
- Dong Zhongshu anchors the source's account of Confucianism and Heaven-linked politics.
- The episode treats omen-centered reasoning as a wider political language, not a lone ruler's eccentricity.
- His intellectual afterlife helps explain why Wang Mang's claims could sound plausible to officials and scholars.
