# Linda McMahon

Linda McMahon appears in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] as [[DonaldTrump]]'s education secretary and the public face of the [[DepartmentOfEducation]]'s student-loan cap policy. The source says she argues that reducing how much the government lends can help reduce college costs.

In the wiki, her role is tied to [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]] rather than a broader profile. The episode treats her claim as the policy thesis to be tested against the [[BennettHypothesis]], [[GraduateSchoolDebt]], and borrower-access risks.

- [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]] and [[BennettHypothesis]] - policy logic associated with the cap.
