# Leng Ganghua

Leng Ganghua is the programmer credited in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]] with creating [[HiweedLinux]], the early community Linux distribution that later became part of the [[Deepin]] lineage.

## Source Position
- The episode says Leng built Hiweed in spare time while working as a network engineer.
- His work is associated with Debian-based packaging and Chinese localization.
- The source notes that he does not appear in the later [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] equity structure, while treating the reasons as uncertain.
- An unconfirmed GTK+ versus QT route disagreement is mentioned as one possible explanation for his divergence from the later company path.

- [[HiweedLinux]] — project he is credited with creating.
