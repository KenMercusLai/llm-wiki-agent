# Ibrahim Almohanna

Ibrahim Almohanna appears in [[the-secret-meeting-that-launched-opec]] as a former Saudi oil adviser explaining how [[OPEC]] describes its own purpose. The source uses his comments to complicate the simple claim that OPEC only wants high prices.

In the episode, Almohanna's role is to present OPEC as a supply manager rather than a pure price setter. That matters because very high oil prices can encourage efficiency, electric vehicles, and substitution, while very low prices can damage producer revenue. His framing connects OPEC to [[OilProducerSupplyCoordination]], [[ProductionQuotaDiscipline]], and [[SwingProducerRole]] rather than to a one-dimensional high-price goal.
