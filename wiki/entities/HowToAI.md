# How to AI

How to AI is [[ChristopherMims]]' practical AI book discussed in [[tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260202-0202-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode describes it as a guide to cutting through hype, mastering AI basics, and using AI to transform work without treating the tool as a replacement for human expertise.

The book's first law, as presented in the episode, is that AI is an assistant, not a replacement. That makes the book a bridge between [[AIAssistantAugmentation]], [[ExpertiseAmplifiedAIUse]], [[MundaneAIUseCases]], and [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]].
