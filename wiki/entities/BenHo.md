# Ben Ho

Ben Ho appears in [[would-you-trust-an-economist-with-your-economy]] as an economics professor and author of a book on trust. The source says he studies tools from behavioral economics and [[GameTheory]] for effective apology after mistakes.

Ho gives the episode its explicit repair model. He argues that economists should first acknowledge lost trust instead of becoming defensive, then explain past mistakes, make present methods more transparent, and offer future commitments backed by costly signals that are hard to fake.
