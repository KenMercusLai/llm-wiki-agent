# Anker 2023 Lab

Anker 2023 Lab is the internal deep-technology initiative named in [[144-dui-yang-meng-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-xiaofei-dianzi-si-yu-sheng-di-san-lei-gongsi-duan-ce-moxing-chanpin-fangfa-youxi-moshi-lnjleqjgjo1txupouvygmdv7oo8b]]. [[YangMeng]] uses it as evidence that [[AnkerInnovations]] is trying to move from a channel-and-product-strong consumer-electronics company toward deeper core technology.

The lab's most concrete example in the episode is [[InMemoryComputingForEdgeAI]] for headphone voice isolation. The project starts from a small user problem, noisy calls, but Yang treats it as an entry point into edge-side neural-network inference under strict power constraints.

## Source Position
- The lab was created after Anker's 2022 crisis and is part of the company's effort to move from five-series reliability toward seven-series innovation.
- Its role is not described as generic research theater; it is tied to productized technology such as chips and model-powered voice enhancement.
- Yang presents the lab alongside [[CreatorCulture]], implying that deep technology requires both technical investment and a different talent density.
