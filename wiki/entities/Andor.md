# 《安多》 / Andor

[[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] is the [[StarWars|Star Wars]] television series discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode frames it as a prequel to [[RogueOne|《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One]] and a growth story for [[CassianAndor]], but its larger value is political: it shows how [[GalacticEmpire]] works through policing, bureaucracy, cultural replacement, prison labor, and ordinary fear.

The source divides the show into arcs around the pursuit of Cassian, the [[Aldhani]] payroll raid, the [[NarkinaFive]] prison, and the [[Ferrix]] uprising. These arcs let the episode connect entertainment narrative to [[FalseChoiceGovernance]], [[InstitutionalOvercompliance]], [[CarceralLaborGovernance]], [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]], and [[SacrificingOthersEthics]].
