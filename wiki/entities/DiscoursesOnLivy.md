# 《论李维》 / Discourses on Livy

《论李维》 / Discourses on Livy appears in [[72-junzhulun-du-ta-shi-yichang-weixian-de-maoxian-673440837]] as the main evidence that [[NiccoloMachiavelli|Machiavelli]] cannot be reduced to a cynical adviser of tyrants. The episode says the work praises republican institutions, creating a productive tension with [[ThePrince|《君主论》 / The Prince]].

For the source, that tension is not a contradiction to erase. It shows that Machiavelli can value republican liberty while also analyzing what must happen inside a princely regime once such a regime exists.
