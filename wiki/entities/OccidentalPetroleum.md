# Occidental Petroleum

Occidental Petroleum appears in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]] as the fossil-fuel company backing a [[Texas]] [[DirectAirCapture]] hub. The episode contrasts that project with [[HeirloomCarbonTechnologies|Heirloom Carbon Technologies]], which says it will not take fossil-fuel investment.

Occidental's role in the source is mainly legitimacy pressure. Oil-company involvement can strengthen industrial and political support for [[CarbonRemovalPoliticalDurability]], but it also raises [[CarbonRemovalMoralHazard]] concerns that carbon removal could justify continued drilling or delay direct emissions cuts.
