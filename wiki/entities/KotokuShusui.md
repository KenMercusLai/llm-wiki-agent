# Kotoku Shusui / 幸德秋水

Kotoku Shusui / 幸德秋水 appears in [[shaqingqing-x-hanzhao-shichuan-zhuomu-wo-baochi-de-sixiang-doushi-yinwei-queqian-894931314]] through the [[GreatTreasonIncident|大逆事件]] and [[IshikawaTakuboku|石川啄木]]'s late political attention. The episode says Takuboku followed the case, accessed related materials through personal networks, and became more clearly drawn toward socialist thinking near the end of his life.

Kotoku's role is not a full political biography here. He matters because the state's repression of socialists and anarchists helps turn Takuboku's personal misery into [[PoliticalClosureAsLiteraryPressure|political closure as literary pressure]].

## Key Claims
- Kotoku is the political figure whose case materials helped sharpen Takuboku's late socialist interest.
- The source frames the Great Treason repression as a major background to Takuboku's final works and essays.
- Kotoku connects Takuboku's private-poetry branch to [[IntellectualResponsibilityUnderAuthoritarianism]] and [[ArtDignityUnderPoliticalPressure]].
