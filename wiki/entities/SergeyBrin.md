# Sergey Brin

Sergey Brin appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as [[LarryPage]]'s [[Google]] co-founder. The episode describes their Stanford work on Backrub and [[PageRankSearchRelevance]], then follows how they negotiated investor access around [[KleinerPerkins]], [[SequoiaCapital]], [[AOL]], and [[Yahoo]].

Brin is also part of the episode's political-translation story. During the [[Gmail]] privacy controversy in Google's IPO quiet period, [[RonConway]] recalls redirecting a meeting after Brin started explaining a Mars-related whiteboard to Jackie Speier, because the political audience needed practical reassurance rather than visionary abstraction.
