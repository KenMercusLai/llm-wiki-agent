# Lugano

Lugano is the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] lakeside city discussed in [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a test case for [[MunicipalBitcoinAdoption]]. The episode says the city launched [[LuganoPlanB|Plan B]] in 2022 to attract crypto companies and make [[Bitcoin]] usable in everyday commerce as well as city services.

The source makes Lugano useful to the wiki because the city is neither a pure investment story nor a pure ideology story. Hundreds of shops and restaurants reportedly accept Bitcoin, and municipal services such as taxes and parking fines can be paid with it, but [[CryptoPaymentPracticalityGap]] remains visible in public transportation, fuel, bills, dentists, and some medical services. Lugano also shows [[CryptoPublicLegitimacy]] as a civic problem: local skepticism and the damaged [[SatoshiNakamoto]] statue sit beside the mayor's defense of the initiative.
