# Spotify Wrapped

Spotify Wrapped is the [[Spotify]] feature discussed in [[tech-20260213-tech-pod-128-tech-20260213-tech-pod-128]] as a driver of engagement and sharing. The episode says Wrapped gives listeners a year-end review of their listening habits and helped support Spotify's fourth-quarter user growth.

The feature matters to the wiki as a clean case of [[PersonalizationAsSocialIdentity]]. Wrapped converts behavioral data into an object users can share because it signals taste, mood, personality, and group belonging. The episode's AI angle is that Spotify can extend the same curation logic into [[AIPromptedPlaylistCuration]], where users ask for music through ordinary language rather than manually searching or organizing.
