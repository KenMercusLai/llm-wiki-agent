# Tomer London

Tomer London is a [[Gusto]] co-founder in [[tsr-s4-gusto-v3-tsr-s4-gusto-v3]]. The episode says he built a customer-support startup in Israel before coming to [[StanfordUniversity]] for a PhD in electrical engineering, then met [[JoshReeves]] through a Stanford alumni mentorship program.

In the source, Tomer is tied to product, engineering, design, and operational reliability. He describes Gusto's product-market fit as clearer in retrospect than it felt while the team was still impatient for growth, and he explains that the company uses multiple payment processors so payroll is not dependent on a single provider.

Tomer also supplies the clearest version of [[CoFounderRelationshipMaintenance]]. He says the founders' one-on-one walks included saying thank you, saying sorry, and giving feedback on what the other person could do better.
