# Petr Cech

Petr Cech appears in [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]] as the [[ChelseaFC]] goalkeeper who used [[IgnacioPalacioHuerta]]'s report during the 2008 Champions League final shootout against [[ManchesterUnited]]. The report advised that when [[CristianoRonaldo]] paused in his run-up, the goalkeeper should not move early because Ronaldo usually shot right afterward.

The source presents Cech's save as the clear success case for [[DataDrivenPenaltyPreparation]]. It shows how a behavioral tendency can matter in a high-speed contest where the goalkeeper must often commit before the ball is struck.
