# 海外中国研究丛书

海外中国研究丛书 is the book series named in [[kaitianchuang-zhi-lu-jian-tuijian-ji-ben-shu-726703080]]. The host connects [[ShaoQianBook|《烧钱》]] and an unnamed book about immortality practice to the series, emphasizing that these are professional sinological works even when the host is only browsing them casually.

## Key Claims
- In this source, the series functions as a route into academic China studies for a general reading-list episode.
- The host's stance is light but respectful: the books are described as serious scholarship, while the personal reading mode is closer to browsing than specialized study.
- The series links Chinese folk religion, ritual economy, immortality practice, and material life to the show's broader reading map.
