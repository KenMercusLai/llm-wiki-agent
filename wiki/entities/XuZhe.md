# Xu Zhe / 许哲

Xu Zhe is the main technical guest in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]]. The episode presents him as moving from technical indicators and market prediction toward arbitrage, options, [[ConvexityExposure]], and a deeper suspicion of simple induction.

His investing account centers on [[BlackSwan]], [[FatTailRisk]], [[Antifragility]], [[BarbellStrategy]], and [[TailRiskHedging]]. He argues that the problem is not predicting the market, but designing a portfolio whose structure can benefit from volatility and extreme states while controlling ordinary loss.

Xu Zhe also extends the investing frame into [[ImpermanenceAndNoSelf]]. He connects market uncontrollability with impermanence and no-self, then treats practice as learning to see and release the desire for a controlling observer or a guaranteed better outcome.
