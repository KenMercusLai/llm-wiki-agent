# Light Cone

Light Cone is the [[YCombinator]]-partner podcast mentioned near the end of [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]]. [[HarjTaggar]] says it reflects the way YC partners naturally talk about startups and related ideas.

In the wiki, Light Cone extends the [[StartupCommunityInfrastructure]] and [[BuilderCenteredInstitutions]] branch from internal YC advice into public media. The episode does not analyze Light Cone in depth, but places it beside [[TheSocialRadars]] as another form of startup-memory and founder-advice distribution around YC.
