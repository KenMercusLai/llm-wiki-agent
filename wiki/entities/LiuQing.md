# 刘擎 / Liu Qing

刘擎 / Liu Qing appears in [[sp-04-liuqing-linyao-qiangsha-yibao-jutou-an-beihou-de-sikao-shang-guanyu-mincui-sixing-he-sixing-790230209]] as a [[DongqiangXidiao|东腔西调]] regular guest and East China Normal University professor. In the source, he helps turn the [[LuigiCaseSuspect|Luigi]] case from a news reaction into a political-theory discussion about health-care anger, terrorism, populism, violence, and the death penalty.

His role is less to supply case facts than to keep moral categories in tension. He asks whether assassination, sentencing, and social-justice pursuit can be separated, notes that revolution and terrorism can lie on a historical continuum, and frames the case as an occasion to rethink [[CapitalPunishmentRetributionProblem|capital punishment]] rather than assume that a heinous offender automatically loses human status.
