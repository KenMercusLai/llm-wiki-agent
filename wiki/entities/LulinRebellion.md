# Lulin / 绿林

Lulin / 绿林 is the uprising force in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] that eventually enters Chang'an during the fall of [[WangMang|王莽]]. The episode says Wang Mang hid on the Jian Terrace before being recognized and killed, after which his body and head became objects of revenge and symbolic humiliation.

The source uses Lulin as part of the endgame of [[XinDynasty|Xin dynasty]] failure. Alongside [[ChimeiRebellion|赤眉]] and the [[KunyangBattle|Battle of Kunyang]], it makes clear that [[MandateOfHeavenLegitimacy|sacred legitimacy]] could not protect a regime whose reforms, borders, prices, and coercion had produced deep social breakdown.

## Key Claims
- Lulin is central to the episode's narration of Wang Mang's death.
- The violence against Wang Mang's body shows political hatred exceeding ordinary regime change.
- The uprising belongs to the wider failure of Wang Mang's governance rather than a stand-alone military anecdote.
