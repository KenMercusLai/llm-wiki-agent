# Neo Cognition

Neo Cognition is described in [[139-agent-de-zongshu-he-su-yu-liao-agent-jishushi-openclaw-moment-bianjie-de-xiaomi-he-shehui-de-fushe-luffrgudeiighqxam49tfqci63no]] as [[SuYu]]'s agent research lab. Its short- and medium-term focus is [[SpecializedIntelligence]]: methods that let agents quickly become expert workers inside a particular domain, profession, organization, or software environment.

The lab's thesis is that once general model intelligence becomes cheaper and more available, differentiation moves to fast learning inside "small worlds." That turns [[ContinualLearning]], [[WorldModels]], [[PersistentAgentMemory]], and real production deployment into core research infrastructure rather than afterthoughts.

- [[SuYu]] — founder and source of the lab's thesis in the episode.

- [[ContinualLearning]] and [[WorldModels]] — learning bottlenecks the lab is organized around.
