# Mike Homer

Mike Homer appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] as [[RonConway]]'s path into [[Netscape]]. Conway says Homer was consulting for Netscape, later became VP of marketing, and helped the company survive and thrive during the browser war with [[Microsoft]].

In Conway's account, Homer is a culture and ecosystem figure. He helped make Netscape's intense "work hard, play hard" environment legible to Conway, and Conway later remembered him at a Netscape anniversary event after Homer developed CJD and died.
