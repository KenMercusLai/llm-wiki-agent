# 《富岳二十四景》 / 24 Views of Mt. Fuji, by Hokusai

《富岳二十四景》 / 24 Views of Mt. Fuji, by Hokusai is the [[RogerZelazny|Zelazny]] story discussed in [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]] as an art-structured revenge story about a woman confronting an ex-husband who has chosen digital existence. The story's chapter structure is built around views of Mount Fuji and explicitly echoes [[KatsushikaHokusai|Katsushika Hokusai]]'s [[ThirtySixViewsOfMountFuji|Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji]].

The episode reads the story as resistance to coercive digital transcendence. The ex-husband's uploaded state does not remove possessiveness, loneliness, or the wish to control his former wife. The protagonist's walk through landscapes, images, and titled scenes makes natural presence and embodied courage part of the answer to disembodied surveillance.
