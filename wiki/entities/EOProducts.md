# EO Products

EO Products is the natural personal-care company founded by [[SusanGriffinBlack]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. The episode references the company's earlier origin story from a 10-gallon pot in a garage and then focuses on the post-2019 period, when COVID created a huge hand sanitizer surge followed by a sharp demand decline.

EO's crisis path adds a resilience case to the wiki's CPG branch. Demand growth created inventory and vendor exposure, then falling demand forced layoffs and negotiation. Susan's story highlights how [[RelationshipLedGrowth]] can matter inside operations, not only in sales: trust with a packaging supplier helped EO turn a large obligation into a workable repayment plan.
