# Kulvir Taggar

Kulvir Taggar is [[HarjTaggar]]'s cousin and co-founder in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]]. He co-applied to [[YCombinator]] in Winter 2007 with [[BoSo]], then worked with Harj as the idea shifted into [[AutomaticAuctions]].

The source gives Kulvir an important family-context role. Harj says Kulvir's mother had entrepreneurial experience and encouraged the families to let the founders try, helping counter the skepticism Harj faced because his parents had experienced a failed baby-clothes store and preferred a stable career after Oxford.
