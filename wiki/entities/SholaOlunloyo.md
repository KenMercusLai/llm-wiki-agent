# Shola Olunloyo

Shola Olunloyo appears in [[chef-vs-robot]] as the chef and food-industry consultant who judges the human-versus-robot taste test. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode uses him to connect culinary quality to [[RestaurantOperationalFragility]] and thin restaurant margins.

Olunloyo correctly identifies the human-made and robot-made dishes in all three rounds. He awards the human chef the beef chow fun and wok stir-fried beef, while giving [[RobbyWokbot]] the fried rice because its seasoning and flavor impact work better in that dish.
