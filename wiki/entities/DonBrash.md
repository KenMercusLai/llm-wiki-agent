# Don Brash

Don Brash appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the central banker chosen to force New Zealand inflation down. The episode says he restricted the money available to regular banks and asked workers and businesses to behave as though inflation were already near the new target.

Brash's role is the painful implementation side of [[InflationTargeting]]. New Zealand reached the target earlier than planned, in 1991, but the episode stresses that unemployment rose above 11% and stayed high for years.
