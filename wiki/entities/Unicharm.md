# 尤妮佳 / Unicharm

尤妮佳 / Unicharm is discussed in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the Japanese sanitary-products company behind Sofy and as a later competitor that overtook [[AnneSanitaryNapkin|安妮卫生巾]]. The source presents founder 高原庆一郎 as noticing Anne's launch claim, studying the product, and building a more aggressive paper-absorbency and distribution business.

The company's importance is the mature-market stage of [[MenstrualProductSocialHistory]]. After a pioneering brand makes the category speakable, later competitors can win through production reliability, channel trust, absorbent-paper technology, and adjacent product transfer such as disposable diapers.
