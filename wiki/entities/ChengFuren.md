# 程夫人 / Madam Cheng

Cheng Furen is [[SuShi|苏轼]] and [[SuZhe|苏辙]]'s mother in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode presents her as a formative educator rather than a background family figure: she teaches the brothers, manages a household that is not always financially easy, and models moral restraint.

The source uses three stories to ground Su Shi's later public conscience. Cheng Furen has a discovered jar reburied rather than treated as easy wealth, forbids harming birds and living things, and answers Su Shi's question about the Fan Pang story by saying that if he can be Fan Pang, she can be Fan Pang's mother. The episode turns those anecdotes into a lineage for [[ConscienceOverFaction]] and [[ScholarOfficialPublicDuty]].
