# Gilgamesh / 吉尔伽美什

Gilgamesh is the king and epic hero of [[TheEpicOfGilgamesh|《吉尔伽美什史诗》]] as read in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The episode presents him as superhuman in strength and status but immature in self-knowledge: he begins as an overwhelming ruler and becomes vulnerable through friendship, loss, and fear of death.

The source's important claim is that Gilgamesh is not only an arrogant hero image. [[Enkidu|恩启都]]'s death, repeated mourning, the failed sleep test, and the lost plant make him a case for [[EpicHeroHumanLimits]]: a hero's greatness can consist in finally accepting the boundary between human life and divine permanence.
