# SenseTime

SenseTime is the AI company discussed in [[132-dui-xinghaitu-chuangshiren-gaojiyang-de-3-xiaoshi-fangtan]] as [[GaoJiyang]]'s early deep-learning exposure. The source says Gao first systematically trained neural networks there and became impressed by data-driven models that learned patterns instead of relying on hand-written if-else rules.

[[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] adds [[CaoXudong]]'s SenseTime period. Cao says he joined in 2015 and worked on product-related R&D lines, which helped him move from research taste into AI commercialization and delivery. He also uses SenseTime-era AI as a contrast: the previous wave of AI often involved many special-purpose small models, while the newer wave uses larger general models and pretraining.
