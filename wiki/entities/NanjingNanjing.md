# 《南京！南京！》 / Nanjing! Nanjing!

《南京！南京！》 / Nanjing! Nanjing! appears in [[e234-weilai-shipai-dianying-hai-cunzai-ma-yu-daoyan-luchuan-liaoliao-ai-gei-yingshiren-de-kongju-yu-ziyou-b2be7093-3366-4ee2-8a7a-625f06206ae5]] as [[LuChuan]]'s concrete example of why [[LiveActionFilmUnderAI]] still matters. He recalls struggling with an execution-scene line for a character played by Fan Wei, then finding it on the shooting day through director-actor interaction.

The source uses the example to define what AI should not flatten: the emotional discovery that happens when people, bodies, timing, and production pressure meet in one unrepeated moment. It does not argue that every scene deserves live-action cost; instead, it says AI makes the justification sharper.
