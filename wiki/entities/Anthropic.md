# Anthropic

[[tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds Anthropic as a defense procurement and use-policy case. The episode says [[Claude]] is already used by the [[USDepartmentOfDefense]], including for classified purposes, while [[PeteHegseth]] reportedly pushed [[DarioAmodei]] for broader access. Anthropic's stated red lines around mass surveillance and autonomous weapons make the dispute a [[FrontierModelUsePolicyConflict]], and the reported threat of a supply-chain-risk designation turns it into [[DefenseAIProcurement]] rather than only a $200 million contract question.

[[google-de-ai-celve-bu-du-moxing-du-shenme-google-cloud-next-xianchang-s10e09-073d7ee7-7bac-4958-b45a-083cc2f866e6]] adds Anthropic as a competitor-customer case inside [[Google]]'s AI strategy. The hosts observe Anthropic's presence at Google Cloud Next and describe it as both a frontier-model rival to [[Gemini]] and a [[GoogleCloud]]/[[TPU]] customer or partner surface, illustrating how a [[FullStackAIPlatform]] can profit from rival model usage even when model companies compete at the top layer.

[[140-dui-yao-shunyu-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-qing-yunxu-wo-xiao-feng-yixia-zai-anthropic-he-gemini-xun-moxing-jishu-yuce-yingxiongzhuyi-yi-guoqu-ll7qiciwwgfssorhr4yy-uuqae8h]] adds [[YaoShunyu]]'s inside account of Anthropic's coding push and organization.
