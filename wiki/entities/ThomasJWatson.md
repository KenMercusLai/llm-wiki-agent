# Thomas J. Watson / 托马斯·沃森

Thomas J. Watson / 托马斯·沃森 is the central business figure in [[133-ibm-yu-nacui-weishenme-putongren-bu-ying-zanmei-jizeizhuyi-815684110]]. The episode presents him as the executive who turned CTR into [[IBM]], built a disciplined corporate culture, expanded international tabulation-machine business, and kept close control over [[Dehomag|德霍梅格]] even while preserving its German-facing identity.

The source's judgment is severe: Watson is framed as a sophisticated risk manager who understood political danger, war risk, and public opinion while still protecting the German business. His acceptance and later return of a Nazi decoration, his "trade brings peace" rhetoric, and his postwar asset recovery all become evidence for [[CorporateComplicityWithAuthoritarianism|corporate complicity with authoritarianism]] rather than simple commercial naivete.
