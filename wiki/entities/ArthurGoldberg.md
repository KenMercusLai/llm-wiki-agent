# Arthur Goldberg

Arthur Goldberg appears in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as the former [[SupremeCourt|Supreme Court]] justice who represented [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]] in his challenge to [[MajorLeagueBaseball|Major League Baseball]]. The source says Goldberg took the case if the [[MajorLeagueBaseballPlayersAssociation|players union]] covered his expenses.

Goldberg's role is to connect Flood's labor grievance to [[BaseballAntitrustExemption|baseball antitrust]] and [[PublicOpinionLitigationStrategy|public-opinion strategy]]. The episode presents the case as a court fight and a legitimacy fight, beginning with Flood's letter to [[BowieKuhn]].
