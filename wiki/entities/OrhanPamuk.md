# 奥尔罕·帕慕克 / Orhan Pamuk

奥尔罕·帕慕克 is the Turkish novelist whose [[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]] anchors [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]], a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode on murder, Ottoman miniature painting, and cultural identity. The source presents him less through biography than through method: he turns a detective story into a structure for asking how people see, remember, desire, narrate, and inherit art traditions.

In the episode's reading, Pamuk's achievement is to make the novel's form carry its argument. The shifting first-person chapters, talking images, murderer confession, color symbolism, archive scenes, and Shekure's final warning all make [[MultiPerspectiveMurderNarration]] part of the art question rather than a decorative device.
