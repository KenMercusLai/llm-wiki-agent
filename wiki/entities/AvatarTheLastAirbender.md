# Avatar: The Last Airbender

Avatar: The Last Airbender is named in [[tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128]] as one of the [[Netflix]] titles in the second-season retention discussion. The episode says [[Bloomberg]]-reported internal data showed a follow-up season losing 50% or more of the original viewers.

In this wiki branch, the title functions as a high-recognition case for [[StreamingAudienceRetention]]. Familiar intellectual property may help a first season attract attention, but the source still treats return viewing as dependent on loyalty, timing, release cadence, and competition inside a crowded catalog.
