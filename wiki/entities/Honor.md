# Honor

Honor appears in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] through its robot division's performance in a Beijing humanoid-robot marathon. The episode says Honor robots won in remote-control and autonomous-navigation categories, and that custom high-torque motors plus liquid cooling helped sustain long high-speed running.

In this source, Honor is a case for large-company entry into [[HumanoidRobotCommercialization]]: consumer-electronics manufacturing, capital, talent density, motors, cooling, and organization capacity can quickly matter in a field often framed around robotics startups.

[[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]] adds Honor's smartphone origin as [[Huawei]]'s online-oriented sub-brand. The episode says Honor learned from [[Xiaomi]]'s [[InternetPhoneModel]], used price-performance, community, and online marketing, and by 2017 became a leading internet-phone brand before later market restructuring.
