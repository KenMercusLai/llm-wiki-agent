# Deckers

Deckers is the footwear company that acquired [[UGG]] from [[BrianSmith]] in 1995 and later scaled it into a much larger global brand. In [[ugg-brian-smith-how-an-epiphany-surfers-and-500-launched-an-iconic-sheepskin-footwear-company-56230048-59d6-43c2-b386-7d06ea89f939]], Deckers appears as the better-capitalized acquirer whose summer sandal business complemented UGG's winter demand.

## Key Points
- Deckers had cash and operating momentum after its success with Teva.
- The acquisition fit seasonally because Deckers' sandal business was strongest in summer while UGG was strongest in winter.
- Smith chose a sale partly because UGG's growing preseason demand required more production finance than he believed he could secure.
- Deckers later amplified UGG through fashion positioning, retail expansion, celebrity visibility, and broader brand management.
