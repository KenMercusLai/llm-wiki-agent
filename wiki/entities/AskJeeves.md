# Ask Jeeves

Ask Jeeves appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as one of the search companies competing before [[Google]] became dominant. [[RonConway]] had prior context with Ask Jeeves and says the broader search market was crowded, strategically important, and still weak on relevance.

The source uses Ask Jeeves as part of the contrast that made [[PageRankSearchRelevance]] legible. Existing players had traffic, brands, and public-market attention, but Conway believed Google had a better answer to the ranking problem.
