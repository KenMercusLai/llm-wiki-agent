# Ferrix

Ferrix is the strong-tie home community discussed in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] through [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]. The episode describes it as a place where [[MaarvaAndor]]'s standing, funeral, and recorded message can produce collective grief and anger rather than a private family scene.

The source's key point is that [[CassianAndor]] does not return as a savior who leads the town. The uprising comes from local experience under [[GalacticEmpire]], the torture and death around the community, and a young person's improvised bomb, making Ferrix the episode's clearest setting for [[OrdinaryPeopleResistance]].
