# NATO

NATO appears in [[continental-rift-natos-tense-summit-6a4cc6b0c4772b27e88e898e]] through an annual summit designed to look short, disciplined, and incident-free. [[AntonLaGuardia]] says the formal agenda is to show European progress on defence spending, strengthen the defence industrial base, and recommit support for [[Ukraine]].

The episode uses that controlled summit to examine [[NATOAllianceCredibility]]. The risk is not only whether members hit spending targets, but whether [[DonaldTrump]], American force posture, [[Russia]]'s grey-zone pressure, and doubts about [[EuropeanDefenseAutonomy]] make deterrence look less certain. In the source, NATO's credibility rests on trust that members will respond to an ambiguous test before hesitation becomes alliance fracture.
