# 吴承恩

吴承恩 is the conventional author attribution for [[JourneyToTheWest|《西游记》]] in [[43-xiyouji-duo-ni-shi-shenme-yaojing-618154182]]. The episode notes that this attribution is the answer many people learn in school, and connects its authority to later local-record and modern-scholarship acceptance rather than treating it as a simple fact that ends the question.

In the source, 吴承恩 matters less as a biography than as one pole in an authorship problem. [[QiuChuji|丘处机]] and [[YangShen|杨慎]] show how other attribution traditions can redirect the novel toward Daoist history or Ming political allegory. That makes 吴承恩 part of [[AccretiveTextFormation]] and [[ClassicReadingComplexity]]: a classic may be canonical while still carrying uncertain authorship, earlier source layers, and later scholarly stabilization.
