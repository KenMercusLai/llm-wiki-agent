# Reform UK

Reform UK appears in [[coming-in-andy-britains-prime-minister-in-waiting-6a3512974a2a3be0f419384b]] as the right-wing party defeated by [[AndyBurnham]] in the Makerfield by-election. The source says Burnham wins with 55% of the vote while Reform takes 35%, making the result both a route into Parliament for Burnham and a warning sign for Reform.

The episode's broader claim is that Reform's dominance of British politics may be weakening. In this account, [[LabourPartyUK]] can still win a contested by-election when it has a strong candidate, the [[ConservativePartyUK]] can pressure Reform from the traditional right after winning Aberdeen South, and [[RestoreBritain]] can pressure it from a harder-right position.

[[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] qualifies that weakness by showing Reform's favorable terrain. The source says [[Clacton]] may be the most pro-Reform part of the [[UnitedKingdom]] and that [[NigelFarage]] remains the strong favorite there, even as a finance investigation and [[CountBinface]]'s satirical challenge make the by-election a case of [[PoliticalFarceAccountability]].
