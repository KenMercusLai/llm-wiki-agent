# 林玉萍 / Lin Yuping

Lin Yuping appears in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]] as a contemporary comparison for [[FieldworkSexualViolence]]. The episode refers to her account of being sexually assaulted by a research subject during 2014 anthropology fieldwork in Zhengzhou, followed by monitoring and pressure from the alleged perpetrator's colleagues.

The source uses Lin's case to prevent a false geographical or cultural simplification of [[HenriettaSchmerler]]'s murder. Sexual violence in fieldwork is not presented as something that belongs only to reservations, remote places, poverty, or Indigenous communities; it can emerge wherever gender, access, state or professional power, and institutional self-protection intersect.
