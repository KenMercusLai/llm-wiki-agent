# Justin Kan

Justin Kan appears in [[socialradarspod-emmettshear-v2]] as [[EmmettShear]]'s cofounder through [[Kiko]], [[JustinTV|Justin.tv]], and the path that later produced [[Twitch]]. In the Kiko period, he and Emmett applied to [[YCombinator]] as Yale seniors with a working browser calendar demo.

Justin's role is especially visible in the Justin.tv origin. The live-streaming idea centered on Justin wearing a camera and broadcasting his life continuously, partly because he was more willing than Emmett to be famous on camera. That lifecasting product was often boring, but audience conversation and gaming moments created the usage signals that later pointed toward Twitch.
