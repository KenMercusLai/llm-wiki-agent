# Sprinkle Bites

Sprinkle Bites is the protein sprinkles startup discussed in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. The product comes in single-serve packets, contains five grams of protein per packet, and was created by working mothers who wanted to make eating easier and more fun for children.

The company has surpassed $100,000 in sales, sells through Shopify and [[ThriveMarket]], and has early reorder proof from Thrive. Its Advice Line question is whether private labeling could help a larger retailer educate the functional-sprinkles category before Sprinkle Bites enters as the premium brand. [[ShaziVisram]] and [[GuyRaz]] warn that private label could give away margin and category ownership before the brand has fully established itself.
