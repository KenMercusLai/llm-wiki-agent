# Central Bank of China

Central Bank of China is the Nationalist-era central-bank institution in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]]. The episode places it inside [[RepublicanChinaBankingSystem]] as both a monetary institution and a state-finance tool, especially in the drama's recruitment and construction-bond storylines.

The source contrasts the dramatic image of a young bank genius entering the center of finance with a more institutional view: central-bank authority depends on recruitment, accounting, market signaling, fiscal needs, and political sponsorship.
