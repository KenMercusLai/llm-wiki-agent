# Liang Jianzhang

Liang Jianzhang is one of the [[Ctrip]] founders in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The source presents him as the technical and analytic member of the founding mix, with a background spanning the Fudan juvenile class, Georgia Tech, Oracle, and later economic and demographic research.

The episode uses Liang's return to the CEO role as a turning point after [[Qunar]] and [[Elong]] became more threatening. His push into mobile apps, direct supplier links, e-booking, and stronger hotel agreements is framed as an operational recovery that also strengthened the platform levers later criticized under [[HotelPlatformPricingPower]].
