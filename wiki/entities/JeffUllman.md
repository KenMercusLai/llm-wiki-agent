# Jeff Ullman

Jeff Ullman appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as a [[StanfordUniversity]] professor named by [[RonConway]] in the early [[Google]] research context. Conway says [[LarryPage]], [[SergeyBrin]], [[RajivMotwani]], and Ullman co-authored a paper titled "What Can You Do With a Web in Your Pocket?"

The episode uses Ullman less as an independent biography than as evidence that Google emerged from a dense Stanford research environment. His mention supports the broader [[StanfordStartupSourcing]] and [[PageRankSearchRelevance]] branch.
