# Roman Empire / 罗马帝国

The Roman Empire / 罗马帝国 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as the institutional outcome that the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination of Caesar]] failed to prevent. The episode's sequence runs from Caesar's death through popular backlash, the rise of [[OctavianAugustus|Octavian / Augustus]], conflict with [[MarkAntony]] and [[CleopatraVII]], and the eventual emergence of imperial rule.

The source emphasizes the irony: the conspirators killed the figure they saw as a would-be king, yet the result was not restoration of the [[RomanRepublic|Roman Republic]]. Power concentrated instead in a colder, more patient successor who could package monarchy-like authority inside forms that looked less openly royal.
