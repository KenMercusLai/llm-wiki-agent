# Villa Diodati / 迪奥达蒂别墅

Villa Diodati appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the Geneva-area setting of the 1816 ghost-story challenge involving [[LordByron|Lord Byron / 拜伦]], [[PercyShelley|Percy Shelley / 珀西·雪莱]], [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]], [[ClaireClairmont|Claire Clairmont / 克莱尔·克莱尔蒙特]], and [[JohnPolidori|John Polidori / 约翰·波利多里]].

The episode treats the villa as a literary event site created by weather, social entanglement, reading, boredom, and scientific conversation. In the wiki it anchors the immediate origin scene for [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]] while also linking to [[YearWithoutASummer|Year Without a Summer / 无夏之夏]] and vampire-fiction afterlife.
