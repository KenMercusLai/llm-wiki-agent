# Adam Bain

Adam Bain appears in [[tsr-s3-eddylu-v2-tsr-s3-eddylu-v2]] as an early high-value [[GOAT]] customer who later became the company's first independent board member. [[EddyLu]] says GOAT initially suspected fraud when expensive sneaker purchases came from Twitter executives, then emailed Bain directly.

The source uses Bain's story as a concrete [[RelentlesslyResourcefulFounder]] case. When Bain asked for help finding a specific sneaker, GOAT sourced it elsewhere, listed it on the platform under a random seller, and the founders hand-delivered it in San Francisco. Years later, Lu disclosed the full story during an investor-conference fireside chat.

## Key Claims
- Bain replied to Lu's early check-in email and asked for help finding a specific sneaker.
- GOAT converted the request into a high-touch customer-service moment before the company had a polished operating system.
- Bain later became a friend of the company and GOAT's first independent director.
