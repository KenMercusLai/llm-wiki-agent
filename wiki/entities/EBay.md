# eBay

eBay appears in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]] as the company where [[TonyXu]] worked before Stanford. The episode names [[BobSwan]] as the CFO Xu worked for and [[JohnDonahoe]] as the CEO who encouraged Xu to apply to business school.

The source does not analyze eBay's marketplace model. Its role is mainly biographical: eBay gave Xu exposure to senior operators before he moved into [[StanfordUniversity]], [[StanfordStartupGarage]], [[PaloAltoDelivery]], and eventually [[DoorDash]].

- [[DoorDash]] and [[PaloAltoDelivery]] - later marketplace company and prototype.
