# Yunmengze / 云梦泽

Yunmengze is the ancient wetland invoked in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] to explain how [[Hubei]] and the middle [[YangtzeRiver|Yangtze]] region once absorbed and lived with water. The episode describes it as a huge temperate wetland with rich species and flood-buffering capacity.

The source uses Yunmengze to contrast [[WetlandAdaptation]] with later fixed settlement, rice agriculture, dikes, reclamation, and urban infrastructure. As wetland capacity shrank, floods became less a periodic condition to work around and more a threat to built systems that could not easily move.
