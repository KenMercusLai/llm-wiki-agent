# 李天天

Li Tiantian is the founder of [[DXY]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode says he came from a medical family, studied at Harbin Medical University, discovered [[PubMed]] while doing academic work, and built an early medical literature search site with self-taught web tools.

The source uses Li as a founder example where the product came from a narrow professional pain point rather than from a top-down internet platform thesis. That makes his path useful for the wiki's broader distinction between authentic domain demand and generic platform transplantation.
