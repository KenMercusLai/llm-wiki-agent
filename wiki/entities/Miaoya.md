# 妙鸭

妙鸭 is the AI portrait product discussed in [[130-zhang-yueguang-chuangye-liangnian-shouci-fangtan-miaoya-bushi-ai-native-chanpin-liucheng-dao-shangxiawen-sheji-one-way-door-he-yinv-youxi]]. [[ZhangYueguang]] describes it as a strong internet product that used AI well, but not as a full [[AINativeProductDesign]] example, because its user flow and output remained relatively bounded.

The product selected portrait photography because image generation was mature enough, consumer willingness to pay was direct, and the use case could be narrowed around "true, alike, beautiful." The source says [[Miaoya]] chained models to handle realism, identity preservation, and aesthetic templates, reached rapid revenue growth, and then consumed the team in compute, bugs, payment, and public-relations issues.

## Product Position
- Treated as a portrait and new-consumer business rather than only an image tool.
- Considered for higher-priced custom service and offline pop-up expansion, potentially with human-in-the-loop refinement.
- Used as a counterexample showing that AI capability can power a strong product without making the design fully AI-native.
