# 有光

有光 is the publishing brand represented by editor-in-chief [[AnQi|安琪]] in [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]]. In the episode, it is the brand that brought [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]]'s [[YiGeAfuhanNvrenDeLaixin|《一个阿富汗女人的来信》]] to Chinese readers.

The source presents 有光 less as an abstract imprint than as a working publishing system. Its team handled author contact, advance royalty support, finance coordination, Persian-to-Chinese translation, review, cover redesign, production, marketing, and reader feedback.

## Key Claims
- 有光's role shows how a publisher can become a bridge between a precarious author and a distant readership.
- The episode treats publishing as a chain of material decisions, not only a cultural endorsement.
- Team labor matters: finance, printing, text editing, marketing, and editorial judgment all shape whether the book reaches readers.
