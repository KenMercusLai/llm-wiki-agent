# Greg McAdoo

Greg McAdoo appears in [[socialradarspod-brianchesky2-final]] as the [[SequoiaCapital]] investor who helped [[BrianChesky]] and [[Airbnb]] see a larger market inside an idea that had sounded narrow to earlier investors. Chesky says McAdoo identified vacation rentals as a large existing industry, which reframed Airbnb from an "air beds" concept into a broader lodging and marketplace opportunity.
