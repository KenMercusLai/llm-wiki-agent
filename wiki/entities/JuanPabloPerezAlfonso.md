# Juan Pablo Perez Alfonso

Juan Pablo Perez Alfonso appears in [[chevron-venezuela-and-the-paradox-of-plenty]] as the Venezuelan oil minister who helped turn oil wealth into a state-bargaining problem. The source says he helped negotiate the 50-50 agreement that required foreign oil companies to share half of what they took, making him central to [[OilConcessionBargaining]] before full national control.

The episode also places him in the origin of [[OPEC]]. By encouraging oil-producing countries to coordinate rather than compete, Perez Alfonso helped extend Venezuela's domestic oil nationalism into international [[OilProducerSupplyCoordination]] with Iran, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait.

His later warning gives the episode its darker institutional frame. Perez Alfonso told [[TerryKarl]] to study what oil was doing to [[Venezuela]], calling oil "the devil's excrement"; the source uses that warning to connect [[Petrostate]] wealth, [[OilNationalization]], and [[PoliticalResourceCurse]].
