# Michel Foucault / 福柯

Michel Foucault / 福柯 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the electronic monitoring officer at [[ThinkReasonLtd|思考理性有限公司]]. His office is filled with screens showing employees' desks and even restrooms.

The source uses this role to make [[OfficeSurveillancePrivacy]] concrete. Instead of explaining surveillance abstractly, it puts Foucault in charge of visible, searchable employee monitoring, then lets [[Plato|柏拉图]] question whether such observation is morally acceptable.
