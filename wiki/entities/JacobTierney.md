# Jacob Tierney

Jacob Tierney is one of the creators interviewed in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] about [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]]. The episode presents him as central to the show's production discipline: he directed all six episodes, supported block shooting, and argued that excessive takes can indicate a scene or writing problem rather than actor failure.

Tierney's importance in the wiki is his link between creative ethics and economic constraint. His production philosophy supports [[ConstraintDrivenMediaProduction]] because fewer unnecessary takes, finished scripts, and collaborative problem solving can protect both the crew and the budget. His skepticism toward AI-written work also extends [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]] and [[CreativeLaborAIBacklash]].
