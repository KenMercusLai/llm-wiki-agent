# Dave Reibstein

Dave Reibstein is the [[WhartonSchool]] marketing professor interviewed in [[tech-20260316-0316-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260316-0316-mp-tech-pod-128]] about the [[CryptoConsumerConfidence|Consumer Cryptocurrency Confidence Index]]. In the episode, he frames crypto confidence as a possible market signal that may interact with [[Bitcoin]] prices, while cautioning that many consumers use crypto more like a risky investment than a currency.

His wiki role is to connect consumer research with [[CryptocurrencyMarketStructure]]. Reibstein's comments make crypto demand partly a behavioral and demographic question: age, available capital, regional distrust of centralized institutions, payment use, and perceived gambling risk all shape how consumers approach crypto exposure.
