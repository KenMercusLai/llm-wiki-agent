# 角田光代 / Mitsuyo Kakuta

角田光代 / Mitsuyo Kakuta is the Japanese author whose novel [[ThirtySeven|《37》]] anchors the second half of [[63-wode-30-xia-bancheng-daan-ruoyinruoxian-654281972]]. The episode does not use her primarily as a biographical subject; it uses the novel as a way to think through a 37-year-old woman's marriage refusal, career divergence, loneliness, and uncertainty.

In the source, Kakuta's value is that she does not make adult choice feel clean. 小华 can refuse a proposal and still feel unsettled when her former boyfriend quickly marries someone younger; she can run a satisfying store and still be disturbed when her partner wants a more ambitious commercial path.
