# Michael LaSara

Michael LaSara is the independent sales rep whom [[TomRinks]] credits as crucial to [[SunBum]]'s early survival in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source says LaSara tested the sunscreen in the water, promoted it aggressively, and helped make the brand visible in surf and retail contexts.

His role matters because Sun Bum's early brand did not spread only through design. It needed field sales, stickers, full displays, and store-level persuasion to turn recognition into orders.
