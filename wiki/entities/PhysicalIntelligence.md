# Physical Intelligence

Physical Intelligence appears in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] through Pi 0.7, which [[ChenZhePeter]] describes as a VLA route augmented by a lightweight world model. The episode frames the company's iteration from Pi 0.5 to 0.7 as evidence that robot policies can incorporate future-state image prediction without abandoning [[VisionLanguageActionModels]].

[[e244-duan-dao-duan-vs-shangxia-fenceng-jiqiren-lujing-zhizheng-zhengzai-zhuanxiang-fc9a3737-81a9-49cf-a7d6-530c77df836e]] adds Physical Intelligence as a comparison point for [[SuduTechnology]]. [[HanZheng]] frames PI-style robot-brain work as stronger in high-level reasoning and VLA-like policy learning, while arguing that [[OpenWorldRobotManipulation]] still needs bottom-level manipulation skills, [[Structured3DRobotData]], and [[Sim2Real]].
