# White Mountain Apache / 怀特山区阿帕齐

White Mountain Apache is the reservation and community setting in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]]. The episode introduces it through a history of conflict with white colonizers, massacre, surrender, reservation control, and administrative pressure before following [[HenriettaSchmerler|Henrietta Schmerler]]'s 1931 fieldwork there.

The page should stay source-scoped. The episode explicitly resists treating violence by [[GurneySeymour]] as a statement about Apache people or an inherent "culture." Its sharper point is institutional: reservation officials, anthropologists, press narratives, and local witnesses all operated under political pressure, fear, distrust, or self-protection, which complicated truth-finding after the crime.
