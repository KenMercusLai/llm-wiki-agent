# Sergio Martínez-Beltrán

Sergio Martínez-Beltrán appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the NPR immigration reporter explaining the administration's detention-center expansion. The source uses his reporting to move from the workforce-training question into [[ImmigrationDetentionExpansion]] and the geography of new detention capacity.

In the episode, Martínez-Beltrán says detention has reached record levels and that many large facilities are being built or expanded in small, economically depressed towns. That makes his segment the bridge into [[DetentionAsLocalEconomicDevelopment]].
