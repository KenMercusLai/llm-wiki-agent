# GECAS

GECAS, General Electric Capital Aviation Services, appears in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] as the aircraft-leasing and financing actor that helped [[EastStarAirlines]] move from a license and route ambition toward actual aircraft access. The episode says [[LanShili]] initially received little attention from aircraft manufacturers until GECAS became involved.

In the source, GECAS is not just a vendor. It represents the [[AviationFinanceLeasing]] layer that allowed a new private airline to operate aircraft it could not simply buy outright. That made scale possible, but it also linked the airline to hard repayment and lease obligations when the 2008 downturn exposed [[LeveragedAviationExpansion]].
