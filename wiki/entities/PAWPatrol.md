# PAW Patrol

PAW Patrol is the children's franchise associated with [[SpinMaster]] in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The episode uses it as the main example of a long-running consumer IP system that has to keep renewing attention through new seasons, specials, characters, toys, and movies.

For the wiki, PAW Patrol is a children's entertainment version of the broader [[ConsumerBrandMoat]] problem. A franchise can become a durable business only if new product forms and story updates continue to give children, parents, and retailers reasons to care after the initial launch.
