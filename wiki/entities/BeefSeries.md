# Beef

Beef is a [[Netflix]] series cited in [[tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128]] as another example in the reported second-season audience drop-off. The episode says [[Bloomberg]]-reported internal data placed it among shows whose follow-up seasons lost at least half of the original audience.

The source uses Beef to separate launch attention from returning loyalty. In the wiki, it connects Netflix's content strategy to [[StreamingAudienceRetention]], where the question is not only whether viewers start a show but whether they finish, remember, and come back after a gap.
