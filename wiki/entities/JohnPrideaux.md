# John Prideaux

John Prideaux appears in [[trailer-tocqueville-road-trip-6a31522c9b6fd8dcaba7b494]] and [[trailer-tocqueville-road-trip-6a315642780d0f7579af216c]] as the host of [[TocquevilleRoadTrip]]. He frames [[AlexisDeTocqueville]]'s 1831 journey through the [[UnitedStates]] as a route for asking whether Tocqueville's democratic analysis still fits the country.

In the trailer, Prideaux's reporting plan is social cross-section rather than abstract commentary. He says the series will speak with people from high society, prisons, presidential-supporter circles, and communities hurt by government power, making [[AmericanDemocraticResilience]] a lived question.
