# 孙若曦 / Sun Ruoxi

Sun Ruoxi is a [[TalkSanlian|Talk三联]] speaker in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]], where she reads [[TheEpicOfGilgamesh|《吉尔伽美什史诗》]] through clay tablets, textual fragmentation, repeated grief, friendship, and mortality.

Her account emphasizes [[Gilgamesh|吉尔伽美什]] as a powerful but emotionally unfinished king whose encounter with [[Enkidu|恩启都]] turns strength into vulnerability. She uses the failed immortality quest, the sleep test, and the stolen rejuvenation plant to build the episode's [[EpicHeroHumanLimits]] branch.
