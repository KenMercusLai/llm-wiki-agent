# 《暗眼小僧》 / Angan Kozo

《暗眼小僧》 is the first story in [[KonjakuZokuHyakkiKumo|《今昔续百鬼·云》]] as summarized in [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]]. It follows [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]] and [[NumagamiRenji|沼上蓮次]] during a stormy fieldwork trip that seems to involve a river yokai but eventually turns on a dog named 河童, stolen documents, and a family property dispute.

The plot begins with a misheard cry near a river, a local house full of dogs with yokai names, and a village divided over land sales and inheritance. The apparent kappa mystery becomes a human tragedy after 金坂平四郎 dies while carrying stolen documents and the old guard dog 河童 also dies after pursuing him.

## Key Claims
- The story's mystery depends on a language trap: "河童" can be heard as a yokai, but in the local household it is also a dog's name.
- The village conflict connects private inheritance, filial resentment, business development, and community pressure rather than a purely supernatural threat.
- [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良]]'s wrong deductions still matter because they open the [[ToriyamaSekien|鸟山石燕]] image to [[YokaiImageExegesis|妖怪图像阐释]].
- 富美's final reading turns the image and case toward ethics: both sides lose something, and the point after the death is reconciliation and responsibility.
