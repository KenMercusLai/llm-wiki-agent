# 林优里 / Hayashi Yuri

林优里 / Hayashi Yuri is the Kyoto ALS patient discussed in [[119-anlesi-xianchang-zhengyin-shengming-ruci-zhengui-770771087]]. The episode says she was diagnosed in 2011, repeatedly expressed a wish for euthanasia, and in 2018 connected through Twitter with a doctor who said he would help if prosecution could be avoided.

Her 2019 death becomes the episode's sharpest Japanese legal-warning case. The court, as summarized by the source, viewed the doctors as non-primary, non-specialist physicians who lacked sufficient knowledge of her symptoms, did not involve her main doctor or family, and received money, making it difficult to treat the act as purely patient-centered mercy.
