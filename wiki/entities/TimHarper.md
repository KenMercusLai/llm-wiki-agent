# Tim Harper

Tim Harper is the [[CenterForDemocracyAndTechnology|Center for Democracy and Technology]] guest in [[tech-20260105-0105-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260105-0105-mp-tech-pod-128]], where he explains a report on generative AI in the 2024 U.S. presidential election. He argues that the most visible feared harms did not dominate the cycle, but that campaigns did use AI to work faster and at larger scale.

Harper's useful distinction is between spectacular synthetic-media deception and operational campaign acceleration. He treats 2024 restraint as fragile, because voluntary norms and voter-backlash fears may erode as campaigns grow more comfortable with AI and as future cycles raise pressure to match opponents.
