# Camp Social

Camp Social appears in [[strait-and-narrowing-the-iran-deal-crumbles-6a57608bf821a8210984481f]] as an adult sleepaway camp in Pennsylvania's Pocono Mountains. The segment describes adults making friendship bracelets, tie-dyeing shirts, hiking, kayaking, putting phones away, and using camp structure to create quick offline relationships.

The source uses Camp Social as the concrete case for [[AdultSummerCamps]]. [[LivSchreiber]] frames the product as relief from constant adult decision-making, while the episode connects the camp's childhood imagery to [[KidAlting]] and its social promise to the [[FriendshipRecession]].
