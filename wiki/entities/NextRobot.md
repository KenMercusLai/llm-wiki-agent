# Next Robot

Next Robot is the manufacturer named in [[chef-vs-robot]] for [[RobbyWokbot]]. The episode says Robby costs about $36,000 and that many kitchens rent wokbots for about $5 an hour, making the company part of the source's [[RobotChefCostQualityTradeoff]] rather than only a technology supplier.

The source does not make a broad company-strategy claim about Next Robot. It uses the manufacturer to anchor the cost side of [[RestaurantAutomation]]: robots become attractive when purchase or rental economics beat skilled-labor scarcity, training time, and throughput limits.
