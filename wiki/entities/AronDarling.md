# Aron Darling

Aron Darling is the passenger used at the opening of [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] to make [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]]' financial distress concrete. He had booked Spirit travel for a work event and repeatedly checked the flight status because he worried the airline's bankruptcy and possible liquidation might affect the trip.

Darling's role is anecdotal but useful. He represents the customer who may choose a cheap fare while still worrying that the carrier's business model has become unstable, linking household travel decisions to [[BudgetAirlineCostSqueeze]] and [[AirlineMarketPriceDiscipline]].
