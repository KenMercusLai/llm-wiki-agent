# Drive to Survive

Drive to Survive is the Netflix series that [[formula-1-1]] treats as a major modern growth lever for [[FormulaOne]]. The episode says [[LibertyMedia]] and [[Netflix]] used the show to reveal human drama, team politics, and behind-the-scenes stakes that casual viewers could understand before they understood race strategy or technical rules.

The series is important to the wiki because it turns sports-media distribution into product education. By making the people and conflicts legible, it strengthens the [[SportsEntertainmentFlywheel]] that later supports race attendance, media rights, sponsorship, and team values.

## Source Position
- Mercedes and Ferrari initially did not participate, which gave other teams narrative oxygen.
- Pandemic timing helped the first two seasons find a large streaming audience.
- The show expanded F1's reach among newer fans, including younger, female, and U.S. audiences.
