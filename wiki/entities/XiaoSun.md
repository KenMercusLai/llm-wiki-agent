# 小孙

小孙 is the post-2000 developer and guest in [[ep119-duihua-xiao-sun-qixing-800-gongli-ba-ziji-jiuchu-shenyuan-ningyuan-meitian-gongzuo-22-xiaoshi-wo-ye-buxiang-zai-shangban-le-lmj5rmebef8y8p0ayfptqoqnhiu2]]. He has worked as a developer, joined early startup projects, helped build [[CreateWise]], won AI programming competitions, returned to employment briefly, then left again after an 800-kilometer ride and a renewed decision to build his own work.

## Key Points

- XiaoSun's core distinction is between assigned work and recognized work: he says he can work extremely long hours for something he treats as his own, but loses execution when he does not believe in the task.
- His [[CreateWise]] experience gave him early founder validation through competition wins, demo shipping, users, and Product Hunt attention, but also exposed gaps in decision-making, communication, collaboration, business judgment, and personal runway.
- The 800-kilometer ride from Shanghai to Qingdao becomes the episode's physical symbol of [[CareerSelfRescue]], giving him a way to leave a low-confidence state and act again.
- His current direction links Dali, Chiang Mai, live streams, recurring gatherings, and a digital-nomad platform/community into [[DigitalNomadCommunityBuilding]].
