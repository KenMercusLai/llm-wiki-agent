# Chen Jiageng / 陈嘉庚

Chen Jiageng is the Minnan overseas Chinese entrepreneur and philanthropist used in [[no-207-minnan-wangshi-zhongshen-renjian-banshichu-zhanghai-shengzhong-wanguo-shang-992579918]] to make the diaspora-return branch concrete. The episode follows him from Jimei and Singapore through pineapple, rubber plantations, rubber products, education investment, Jimei schools, Xiamen University, and wartime overseas Chinese mobilization.

The source positions Chen as a representative of a pattern rather than only as an individual success story: money earned in Southeast Asia returns to the hometown as schools, public goods, and political support. This connects him to [[OverseasChineseMutualAidNetworks]], [[QiaopiRemittanceNetworks]], and [[DiasporaCapitalManufacturingClusters]], where overseas earnings, trust, obligation, and local development become one historical system.

## Source Position
- Chen Jiageng shows the education version of overseas Chinese return investment.
- The episode emphasizes that he protected school funding even when the rubber business suffered.
- His wartime organizing extends hometown obligation into broader national mobilization.
