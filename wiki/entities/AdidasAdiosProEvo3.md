# Adidas Adios Pro Evo 3

Adidas Adios Pro Evo 3 is the elite racing shoe discussed in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as the source's concrete product behind the reported 2026 London Marathon sub-2 result. The episode says the shoe combines sub-100-gram weight, TPEE-like foam, carbon-ring structure, and an extremely light upper.

## Key Claims
- The shoe's importance is framed less as a wholly new material breakthrough and more as a production-ready combination of low weight, softness, and enough race durability.
- The source says earlier Chinese concept shoes also reached sub-100-gram ideas, but Adidas paired the concept with elite-race proof and eventual sales.
- Its limited supply and high price may reflect yield and cost constraints while also creating halo demand for more accessible models.
