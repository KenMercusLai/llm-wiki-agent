# 张大哥（《离婚》） / Zhang Dage

张大哥 is the universal "big brother" figure in [[LihunNovel|《离婚》]] as summarized by [[02-lihun-guodegang-dou-buru-laoshe-youyisi-525913046]]. He is sociable, competent, decent, and good at arranging other people's lives through favors, matchmaking, meals, mediation, and relationships.

The episode treats him as a central example of [[DecentLifeAsControl]]. His harm does not come from clear malice; it comes from making the accepted version of a proper life feel inescapable. When his son is arrested and [[XiaoZhaoLihun|小赵]] demands his house and daughter, the system he normally manages turns against him.
