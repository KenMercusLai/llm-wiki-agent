# Kashish Gupta

Kashish Gupta is presented in [[tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly-tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly]] as founder and co-CEO of [[Hightouch]]. He describes the company as a YC Summer 2019 startup that began with travel booking, pivoted after COVID, and became an enterprise data and AI platform for marketers.

In the episode, Gupta's operating doctrine is customer evidence plus founder risk. He says Hightouch overweighted customer input, chose enterprise customers despite outside advice to start small, and later doubled sales headcount before the historical data fully proved the plan. His version of [[FounderMode]] emphasizes broader founder context, written disclosure to executives, and the idea that the founder can take risks employees may rationally avoid.
