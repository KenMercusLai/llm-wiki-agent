# 杜少卿

杜少卿 is the figure in [[RulinWaishi|《儒林外史》]] whom [[58-rulin-waishi-jiashe-kaogong-chengle-weiyi-chulu-645207859]] reads as closest to [[WuJingzi|吴敬梓]]'s ideal scholar. The episode presents him as generous, resistant to official flattery, and willing to help people whom fashionable local society has abandoned.

The source especially stresses his emotional sincerity. Du Shaoqing treats an old steward with kinship-like care, refuses concubinage, loves his wife openly, and acts from feeling rather than status calculus. In the episode's interpretation, he is not simply "modern" or "advanced"; he is a counterexample to [[ImperialExaminationAsOnlyExit]], showing a person whose worth is not measured by examination success, official proximity, or ritual performance.
