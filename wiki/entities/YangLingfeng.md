# Yang Lingfeng / 杨凌峰

Yang Lingfeng is the co-founder and CEO of [[YangcongXueyuan]], interviewed in [[167-yangcong-xueyuan-yang-lingfeng-yong-ai-zhizao-jiejing-shi-zai-shasi-zhenxuexi-1-167-1]] about AI-era education and self-directed learning. The episode's metadata spells the name as 杨临风, but the transcript body consistently uses 杨凌峰.

Yang's core argument is that education technology should lower the threshold for real thinking rather than accelerate answer acquisition. He defines [[SelfDirectedLearning]] through willingness, ability, tools, and belief, and argues that ordinary students often want to learn but have been trained by repeated negative feedback to expect failure.

His AI position is pragmatic rather than model-centric. AI can support planning, contextual explanation, strategy distribution, benchmarked model choice, and classroom question handling, but [[AIShortcutRisk]] grows when AI follows students' desire for less effort instead of helping them build understanding, confidence, and agency.
