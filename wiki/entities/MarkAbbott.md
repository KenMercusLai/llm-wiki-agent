# Mark Abbott

Mark Abbott is the founder of [[Ninety]] and the guest in [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]]. In the episode, he explains how his private-equity and board experience led him to build software for company operating rhythms, first through the [[EOSWorldwide]] community and later as a broader SaaS platform.

## Position In The Episode
- Saw the need for a company operating system as early as 2005 after investing in more than 100 companies and serving on many boards.
- Joined the EOS implementer community before building the product, treating trust and ecosystem learning as part of company formation.
- Chose to bootstrap through early customer traction, then raise capital only after the company reached greater scale.
- Describes post-funding hiring mistakes as a lesson in [[StageAppropriateHiring]].
- Frames AI as both an accelerant and a threat: software is easier to build, but company trust, distribution, security, compliance, and execution remain difficult.
