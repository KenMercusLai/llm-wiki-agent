# Reserve Bank of New Zealand

The Reserve Bank of New Zealand appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the institution where [[ArthurGrimes]] helped design modern [[InflationTargeting]] and [[DonBrash]] implemented the hard disinflation. The episode treats it as the central-bank laboratory that influenced later inflation targets elsewhere.

The institution matters because the source links central-bank credibility to public expectations. A target could work only if workers, businesses, and markets believed the bank would act consistently enough to make low inflation a plausible equilibrium.
