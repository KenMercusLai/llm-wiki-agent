# 《大厂小民》

《大厂小民》 is [[XiaoMan]]'s nonfiction book discussed in [[da-chang-xiao-min-women-bixu-kezhi-dui-xitong-yu-shangan-de-qidai-lps-6oqp9ljyfw6wpfkutcks6dgf]]. The episode presents it as an immediate, interview-supported account of ordinary people inside large internet companies, especially workers who are close enough to the system to be shaped by it but not powerful enough to command it.

The book's value in the wiki is its ambivalence. It records layoffs, middle-platform danger, outsourcing inequality, tool rationality, and the collapse of the "big company equals landing ashore" story, while still acknowledging that large-company rules, welfare, compensation, and individual managers can protect people better than many smaller or less formal workplaces.
