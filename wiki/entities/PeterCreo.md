# Peter (Creo)

Peter is presented in [[e238-liaoliao-harness-shidai-ai-first-de-zuzhi-jiagou-cong-xinren-ren-dao-xinren-ai-51260de8-60ef-4b76-b3e5-2e559c4a0923]] as [[Creo]]'s CTO, with prior work on [[Meta]]'s Llama base-model team and [[Apple]] multimodal/model work. The episode uses his engineering account to define [[HarnessEngineering]] as the system layer around agents rather than only prompt or context optimization.

Peter argues that AI can now handle more planning and coding, but architecture remains a human bottleneck because security, sandbox-host interaction, latency, and system tradeoffs require experienced judgment. He also describes Creo's agent-driven CICD, bug triage, and low-risk autofixing workflows as examples of [[AICodingVerification]] becoming part of the harness.
