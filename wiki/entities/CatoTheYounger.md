# Cato the Younger / 小加图

Cato the Younger / 小加图 appears in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] as a severe republican opponent of [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]]. The episode presents him as clean, Stoic, contemptuous of crowd politics, and committed to speech and republican norms.

In the source, Cato matters less as a full biography than as the moral pole of anti-Caesar politics. He embodies the elite republican fear that Caesar's charm, debt, army, and popular support would eventually overturn the [[RomanRepublic|Republic]]. His daughter Porcia's marriage to [[MarcusBrutus]] also connects Cato's moral reputation to the assassination circle.
