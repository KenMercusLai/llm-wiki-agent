# 多田克己 / Tada Katsumi

多田克己 is the real yokai researcher whom [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]] identifies as the model for [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]]. The episode presents him as a friend of [[KyogokuNatsuhiko|京极夏彦]], a participant in world-yokai organizations and meetings, and a prolific editor or author of yokai reference works.

The page is source-scoped: its role in the wiki is not a full biography, but a bridge between the fictional Tatara and real [[EvidenceBoundFolkloreInquiry]] around yokai.

## Key Claims
- Tada's presence helps the episode show that Kyogoku's yokai material is not ornamental; it is tied to a real research culture around images, names, places, and local stories.
- The fictionalization of Tada into [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]] turns scholarly obsession into a comic but serious narrative engine.
