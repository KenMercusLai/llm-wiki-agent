# Qianli Technology

Qianli Technology is the AI-and-car terminal company discussed in [[131-yin-qi-churen-jieyue-xingchen-dongshizhang-de-fangtan]]. [[YinQi]] presents Qianli as one of his two core chair roles, alongside [[StepFun]], and as the first terminal path for applying foundation-model capability in the physical world.

## Source Position
- Qianli is framed as an [[AIPlusTerminals]] company where vehicles provide a concrete carrier for model capability.
- The first scenarios named are intelligent driving, cockpit assistants, and Robotaxi.
- Yin treats the car cabin as a strong human-machine interaction space because voice and multimodal interaction may work more naturally there than on a phone screen.
- Qianli is also part of Yin's [[Megvii]] retrospective: he says that if he could rerun the prior decade, the car market might have been the better strategic battle.
- The source claims intelligent driving still has roughly a three-year war ahead, but that route and business-model uncertainty have become clearer than in earlier AI 1.0 markets.
