# Yunnan Homestay Association

Yunnan Homestay Association appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as a complainant-side organization in the episode's discussion of [[Ctrip]] antitrust pressure. The source says homestay operators complained about forced promotions, price controls, automatic price changes, differentiated display, and loss of pricing autonomy.

The page matters because the source treats homestays as different from standardized chain hotels. Homestays sell style, location, host experience, and local atmosphere, so platform comparison can weaken [[HomestayDifferentiation]] by reducing varied products to a comparable bed price.
