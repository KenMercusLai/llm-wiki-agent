# Yangtze River / 长江

The Yangtze River is the main river system in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]]. The episode follows the 1931 flood through winter snow, spring rain, summer downpours, upstream flood peaks, the Three Gorges route, the two-lake plain, and the [[Wuhan]]/[[Hankou]] urban disaster.

The source treats the Yangtze not as a passive backdrop but as part of a long human-water system. [[WetlandAdaptation]], rice agriculture, levees, urban expansion, reclaimed land, and transport infrastructure all changed how floodwater became social damage.

[[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]] adds the river as the habitat and loss context for the [[Baiji|baiji]]. In the episode's reading of [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》]], [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] fail to find the dolphin while encountering meetings and baiji-branded publicity, making the river a case for [[ConservationPublicityGap]] and late [[FunctionalExtinction]] concern.
