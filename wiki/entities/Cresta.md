# Cresta

Cresta is the enterprise AI company in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]] where [[Jove]] leads a near-hundred-person [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] team focused on agent deployment. The episode presents Cresta as a [[ContactCenterAI]] case: it uses years of customer-experience text and voice data to identify high-volume, SOP-heavy workflows, build AI agents, test them through simulation and customer data, and monitor production metrics such as satisfaction, call duration, and case or email resolution.
