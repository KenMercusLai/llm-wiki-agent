# 《笑傲江湖》

《笑傲江湖》 is one of the central [[JinYong|金庸]] works in [[112-xianliao-jinyong-diyidan-mingjiao-jingran-jintian-hai-cunzai-754593095]]. The episode reads it as a political novel about the instability of "正" and "邪", faction labeling, ideological violence, and the way power damages everyone who reaches for it.

The source ties the novel's force to [[HongKong|Hong Kong]] in the late 1960s. It presents the 1967 unrest, left-right struggle, denunciation language, bombs, and threats against Jin Yong as part of the pressure behind [[JinYongPoliticalReading]]. This does not make the novel a one-to-one allegory, but it explains why slogans, sects, purity claims, and leader worship matter so much.

日月神教 is treated as a transformed fictional descendant of [[ManichaeismInChina|明教]]. The episode links that choice to possible Fujian and [[Quanzhou|泉州]]-region religious survivals, making the novel's sect history part of [[WuxiaIntertextualCraft]] rather than an arbitrary villain label.
