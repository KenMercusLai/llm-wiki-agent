# 顾准

顾准 is presented in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]] as the historical accounting prodigy behind part of [[WarOfFaith]]'s Wei Ruolai reference frame. The episode contrasts the drama's poor Jiangxi-origin Central Bank trainee with Gu's Shanghai background, early vocational education, work at [[LixinAccounting]], and unusually young publication record in government and bank accounting.

The source emphasizes Gu's ability more than a simple inspirational arc. He is described as a teenager who learned by listening, self-study, and solving accounting disputes, then later contributed to Shanghai's post-1949 tax and economic-order reconstruction.

## Source Position
- Gu's path shows how technical accounting skill could become political and institutional capacity.
- His relationship with [[PanXulun]] is used to show that strong professional mentorship can coexist with argument, irregular temperament, and institutional trust.
- His later role in taxation and production-supply-sales rebuilding connects [[AccountingInfrastructure]] to state capacity rather than only private business service.
