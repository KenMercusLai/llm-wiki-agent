# 阿里（《放学后的小巷》）

阿里 is the student protagonist discussed from the first [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] story in [[168-fangxuehou-de-xiaoxiang-zhi-qingchun-zhi-nanian-de-youyi-richang-tuili-qiangshi-huigui-953669535]]. He enters the episode as an unimpressive new student seated near [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]], then becomes the observer who notices the story's small anomalies.

阿里's deductions turn the story into [[DignityProtectingDeduction|体面保护式推理]]. He notices the mismatch between [[MengTongxueFangxuehou|孟同学]]'s seven-speed shifter and six-speed wheel set, connects it to 严良's unseen prize bike, tests the tofu-pudding cilantro explanation, and infers that 严良 may be hiding family financial pressure. His response is not accusation but a practical gesture: protecting the explanation in front of others and bringing a hot bowl of tofu pudding.

## Key Claims
- 阿里 models daily-life detection through attention to small, ordinary details.
- His reasoning is emotionally disciplined because he avoids turning discovery into exposure.
- His friendship is shown through awkward action rather than adult-style advice or charity.
