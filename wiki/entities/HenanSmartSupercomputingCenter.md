# 河南智能超算中心 / Henan Smart Supercomputing Center

Henan Smart Supercomputing Center appears in [[shangye-xiaoyang-43-ai-shidai-shui-zai-gei-fuwuqi-jiangwen-992085076]] as the concrete project case for prefabricated AI data-center cooling. The episode says [[Grundfos]] used a container-style integrated cooling station for the center, with pumps and intelligent variable-frequency control installed and tested before arriving on site.

The center matters in the wiki less as a standalone institution than as an example of how [[DataCenterThermalManagement]] becomes an engineering-delivery problem. Fast AI infrastructure rollout depends not only on chips and buildings, but also on whether cooling modules can be customized, tested, delivered, connected, and maintained quickly enough for dense compute.

## Key Claims
- The episode says the prefabricated cooling-station approach helped keep the project cycle to roughly 40 days.
- Preinstallation and pretesting are presented as ways to reduce site complexity and commissioning risk.
- The case links water quality, scaling, contamination control, and equipment lifespan to long-term data-center reliability.
- The example extends [[DataCenterPhysicalResilience]] from attack or power disruption into everyday thermal operations.
