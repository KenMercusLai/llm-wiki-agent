# Sharon Price John

Sharon Price John appears in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]] as the former Hasbro and Stride Rite executive hired to lead [[BuildABear]] after [[MaxineClark]] stepped down as CEO in June 2013. The episode says Maxine gradually learned to trust Sharon, avoid constant interference, and accept that different decisions could still work.

Her role in the wiki is the operating side of [[FounderSuccession]]. The company survived beyond the founder's daily control because the successor had relevant consumer, retail, and brand experience, making the case adjacent to [[FounderRoleTransition]] and [[StageAppropriateHiring]].
