# Richard Branson

Richard Branson appears in [[tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio-tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio]] as the [[VirginGroup|Virgin]] figure [[BlakeScholl]] pursued for an early [[BoomSupersonic]] customer-intent signal. The episode says Branson had previously tried to buy Concordes and cared publicly about high-speed passenger travel, making him a credible target for Boom's first public airline-support story.

Scholl's ask to Branson was not framed as an investment. He showed a wooden aircraft model painted in Virgin colors and asked for a statement that Virgin wanted the first aircraft when they flew. The source treats that support as [[HardTechCustomerIntentProof]]: a signal that helped Boom look less like a render-driven aerospace fantasy before [[YCombinator]] Demo Day.
