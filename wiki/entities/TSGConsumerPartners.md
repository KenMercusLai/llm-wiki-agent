# TSG Consumer Partners

TSG Consumer Partners is the private equity firm that bought a 49% minority stake in [[ELFCosmetics]] in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. The episode places the deal at roughly a $70 million valuation, while [[JoeyShamah]] and his family kept majority control.

The TSG deal matters as a staged [[StartupGovernance]] and [[FounderRoleTransition]] moment: Joey gained liquidity and guidance without immediately giving up control of the company.
