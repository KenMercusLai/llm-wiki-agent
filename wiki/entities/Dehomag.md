# Dehomag / 德霍梅格

Dehomag / 德霍梅格 is the German Hollerith-machine company at the center of [[133-ibm-yu-nacui-weishenme-putongren-bu-ying-zanmei-jizeizhuyi-815684110]]. The episode describes it as the German-facing subsidiary through which [[IBM]] and [[ThomasJWatson|Thomas J. Watson]] supplied, customized, and maintained punched-card systems for [[NaziGermany|Nazi Germany]].

The source treats Dehomag as more than a foreign affiliate. Its local identity helped IBM preserve access under Nazi rule, while IBM's control helped keep the German operation inside a transnational business system. That structure makes Dehomag the episode's clearest institutional case for [[CorporateComplicityWithAuthoritarianism|corporate complicity with authoritarianism]] and [[DataEnabledPersecution|data-enabled persecution]].
