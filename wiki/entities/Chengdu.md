# Chengdu / 成都

Chengdu / 成都 is the city setting in [[66-yuchi-yu-huajiao-liao-chi-de-wo-ke-jiu-bu-kun-le-659957536]], where [[FuchsiaDunlop|胡霞 / Fuchsia Dunlop]] studies in the 1990s and becomes absorbed by [[SichuanCuisine|川菜]]. The episode evokes an older urban street life of mobile vendors, dan dan noodles, bean curd, alcohol sellers, haircut stalls, markets, and cooks before later redevelopment changes the city's texture.

In the source, Chengdu matters because it gives Dunlop repeated contact with ordinary food rather than only restaurant display. The small noodle shop, huajiao initiation, kitchen observation, and [[MarketFreshnessCulture|market freshness]] encounters make the city a practical classroom for [[CulinaryGrammar]].

## Key Claims
- Chengdu appears as a sensory and social environment, not only a geographic label.
- The episode's Chengdu is tied to small vendors, market routines, and everyday food accessibility.
- Dunlop's attachment to the city helps explain why her Chinese food writing is framed as [[CulinaryIdentityTransformation]] rather than tourism.
