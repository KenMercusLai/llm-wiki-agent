# Tim Fontaine

Tim Fontaine is the Indigenous Canadian writer named in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]] as the creator of [[TheKingIsDead|The King Is Dead]], a project in development with [[Crave]]. [[BrendanBrady]] describes the project as an action-adventure comedy set in the 1700s about Indigenous people traveling to England to try to kill King George III.

Fontaine matters in the wiki because the episode treats [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]]'s success as leverage for more distinctive Canadian projects, not only as a one-off romance hit. That links him to [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]], [[UnderservedRomanceAudience]], and the question of which stories can move through constrained production systems.
