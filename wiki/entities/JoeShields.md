# Joe Shields

Joe Shields is the cartoonist who worked with [[TomRinks]] on [[PsychoChihuahua]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. Tom met him through Perron Screen Printing and admired his character work before they created the Chihuahua line.

The episode places Joe inside the [[TacoBell]] dispute: he and Tom say they developed campaign materials with Taco Bell, sued after the later Chihuahua ads appeared, and eventually won at trial before settlement.
