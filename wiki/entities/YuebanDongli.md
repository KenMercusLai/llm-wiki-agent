# Yueban Dongli

Yueban Dongli is the company founded by [[Shibo]] to build [[Xiaoban]], a consumer bipedal [[CompanionRobots]] product. In [[wo-yudao-le-di-yige-zhenzheng-xiang-mai-de-peiban-jiqiren-duihua-shibo-yueban-dongli-chuangshiren-gonglu-boke-lrydelizm0-hbk68u5cqe3ti-epb]], the company is described as a roughly 25-person team, including about 15 full-time staff and 10 interns, moving quickly on a hardware, AI, and emotional interaction stack.

## Product And Technical Position
- Builds around household companionship rather than manipulation, chores, or generic assistant functions.
- Uses [[Qwen]] and a small-parameter [[OnDeviceFastSlowBrain]] architecture to keep emotional responses and movements low-latency.
- Trains and tests interaction behavior with a [[FamilyWorldSimulator]] because real household companion-robot data is scarce before delivery.
- Treats soft materials, bipedal motion, non-human language, expressive eyes, and charging behavior as product decisions that support [[RobotLiveliness]].
