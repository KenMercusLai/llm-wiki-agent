# Percy Shelley / 珀西·雪莱

Percy Shelley appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as a Romantic poet, [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]]'s partner, and a key carrier of [[RomanticScienceCulture]]. The episode emphasizes his fascination with electricity, chemistry, experiment, and anti-authoritarian education, including school-era and household electrical demonstrations.

The source also keeps the scandal and harm visible: Percy had already eloped with Harriet Westbrook before his relationship and flight with Mary, and [[ClaireClairmont|Claire Clairmont]] joined the journey. His role therefore belongs both to the Romantic-circle story around [[VillaDiodati|Villa Diodati / 迪奥达蒂别墅]] and to the episode's larger question of how ideas, desire, experiment, and responsibility collide.
