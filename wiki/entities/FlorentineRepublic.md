# Florentine Republic / 佛罗伦萨共和国

The Florentine Republic / 佛罗伦萨共和国 is the political setting that gives [[72-junzhulun-du-ta-shi-yichang-weixian-de-maoxian-673440837]] its historical force. [[NiccoloMachiavelli|Machiavelli]] served the restored republic after [[GirolamoSavonarola|Savonarola]] fell, working in government, organizing military affairs, opposing dependence on mercenaries, and participating in the campaign against Pisa.

The episode uses Florence to explain why [[ThePrince|《君主论》]] is not abstract villainy. Machiavelli wrote after seeing republican fragility, elite return, foreign invasion, church politics, and the collapse of institutions he had served.
