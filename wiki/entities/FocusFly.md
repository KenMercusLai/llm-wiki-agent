# FocusFly / 专注飞机

FocusFly, also called 专注飞机, is [[Una]]'s independent focus-timer app discussed in [[ep102-duihua-una-quanqiu-toubu-siwei-daotu-app-store-yunying-fuzeren-qinshou-aso-shizhan-jingyan-lscdlnogyhiohre091lwi-ayshug]]. The product turns focus sessions into a flight journey where users set departure and destination points, making concentration feel more immersive for ADHD, procrastination, study, and deep-work contexts.

In the episode, FocusFly functions less as a full product-history case and more as a grounded example for [[AppStoreOptimization]]. Una uses the product to explain how a new app can secure brand-word indexing, choose long-tail category terms, shape screenshots around target scenes, and collect ratings without waiting for rare late-stage milestones.
