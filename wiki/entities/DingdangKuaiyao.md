# 叮当快药

Dingdang Kuaiyao is the drug-delivery O2O company in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode contrasts it with many collapsed O2O projects because it survived by emphasizing 7-by-24-hour service, short delivery promises, self-built front warehouses, pharmacies, and delivery teams.

In the source, Dingdang Kuaiyao shows the narrow but real durability of pharmacy logistics inside [[HealthcareO2O]]. Drug delivery is still constrained by frequency, cost, regulatory limits, and customer acquisition, but it is more operationally standardizable than home nursing, doctor visits, or broad online diagnosis.
