# David Card

David Card appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through research cited with [[JesseRothstein]] and [[MoisesYee]] on wage premiums across industries. The episode uses that work to make [[ManufacturingPayPremium]] concrete: a worker moving from restaurants into manufacturing is cited as receiving an average 35% pay bump.

The source does not make Card the main interview subject. His role is evidentiary, grounding a claim that manufacturing can pay better than many service jobs while still not being the highest-paying or universally good option.
