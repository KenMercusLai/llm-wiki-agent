# Keiko

Keiko appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the orca from Free Willy whose rehabilitation and release shaped later thinking about whale sanctuaries. The episode says he was rehabilitated in the same Icelandic bay later used by the [[SeaLifeTrust]] and died the following year of pneumonia in a Norwegian fjord.

The source uses Keiko as a cautionary precedent: full release can satisfy a moral desire for freedom while still failing the animal if it has lost the capacity to survive. That history helps explain why [[CaptiveWhaleSanctuary]] emphasizes care, space, and gradual adaptation for [[LittleWhiteAndLittleGrey]] rather than immediate open-ocean release.
