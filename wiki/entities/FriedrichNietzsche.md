# Friedrich Nietzsche / 尼采

Friedrich Nietzsche appears in [[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]] as one of the thinkers the episode links to the [[MunchausenBaron|敏锡豪森男爵]] self-rescue motif. The source says Nietzsche uses the image to criticize attempts to make freedom or metaphysical grounding prove itself from nowhere.

In the wiki, this places Nietzsche inside the episode's broader [[MunchausenSelfBootstrapping]] cluster: comic impossibility becomes a compact way to talk about philosophical self-foundation.
