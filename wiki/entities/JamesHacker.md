# James Hacker / Jim Hacker / 詹姆斯·哈克

James Hacker, rendered in the episode as 詹姆斯·乔治·哈克, is the elected minister at the center of [[YesMinister|《是，大臣》 / Yes Minister]] and later [[YesPrimeMinister|《是，首相》 / Yes, Prime Minister]]. In [[118-shi-dachen-xinla-fengci-yongbuguoshi-768665425]], he enters office wanting reform, open government, reduced bureaucracy, and political credit.

The source reads Hacker as comic because his ideals are real but unstable under status anxiety, media fear, prime-ministerial pressure, and personal vanity. He wants to control the department, but [[HumphreyAppleby|Humphrey Appleby]] and the civil-service system control information, drafts, timing, red boxes, and acceptable options.

The [[BaillieCollege|Baillie College]] story sharpens this reading. Hacker can use budget cuts and honours lists to pressure officials, but his own desire for an honorary doctorate lets the same patronage system capture him.
