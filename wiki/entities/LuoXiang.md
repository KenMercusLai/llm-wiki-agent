# 罗翔 / Luo Xiang

Luo Xiang / 罗翔 is the legal scholar and public intellectual whose [[TheParadoxOfLaw|《法律的悖论》]] is discussed in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]]. The episode presents him less as a dispenser of fixed legal answers than as a guide to legal judgment under conflicting values, limited reason, and public emotion.

In the source, Luo's recurring function is to make criminal law morally and institutionally harder. He is associated with [[LegalParadoxThinking]], [[LawMoralityBoundary]], [[CriminalLawAsPowerLimitation]], [[PunishmentTheoryTradeoff]], [[VeilOfIgnorance]], and [[LiberalismPaternalismBoundary]], all of which resist treating punishment as simple revenge or law as mere command.
