# Tom Rinks

Tom Rinks is the brand builder interviewed by [[GuyRaz]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The episode traces his path from art student and commission furniture salesman to Surf Michigan shirts, University of Michigan basketball apparel, [[PsychoChihuahua]], the [[TacoBell]] lawsuit, a Christian video nonprofit, [[RinksCanetti]], [[DeLeonTequila]], and finally [[SunBum]].

The source frames Tom's strength as cultural observation plus sales: he repeatedly notices an emotional or visual gap, turns it into a brand surface, and then uses retail persuasion to test whether customers and buyers feel it. It also keeps the operating boundary visible: Tom says [[AdamFrancis]] was needed at Sun Bum because margins, production, scalability, and financial management were not his strongest skills.
