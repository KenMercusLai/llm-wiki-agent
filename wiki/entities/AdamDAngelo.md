# Adam D'Angelo

Adam D'Angelo appears in [[tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3-tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3]] as a [[Quora]] executive who advised [[AlexandrWang]] to attend at least some college after Wang's gap-year work at Quora. The episode uses the advice to explain why Wang went to MIT before starting [[ScaleAI]] through [[YCombinator]].

[[tsr-s4-samaltman-v4-tsr-s4-samaltman-v4]] adds D'Angelo as the board member [[SamAltman]] says he mostly spoke with during the November 2023 [[OpenAIBoardCrisis]]. Altman describes him as acting in good faith while also noting that D'Angelo was only one board vote.
