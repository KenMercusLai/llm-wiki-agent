The source treats WhatsApp as part of Meta's distribution advantage in social AI content: a generator inside existing communication apps does not need to build a standalone social network from scratch.

Its relevance in this ingest is tied to [[AIPublicLikenessGeneration]], because image generation based on public usernames and likenesses can move through familiar social and messaging contexts rather than remaining in a separate creative tool.
