# Elan Lee

Elan Lee appears in [[board-games-3-whats-in-a-name]] as a co-founder of [[ExplodingKittens]] and one of the people guiding [[PlanetMoney]] from a playable prototype toward a retail game. His central role in the source is to state the hard product lesson behind [[GameMechanicToRetailProduct]]: a game can work mechanically and still fail if no one picks up the box.

Lee pushes the discussion away from the hosts' literal used-car instinct and toward what the product communicates at retail. In the source, that makes him the bridge between rules, shopper attention, and the commercial decision to choose [[SellMeASasquatch]].
