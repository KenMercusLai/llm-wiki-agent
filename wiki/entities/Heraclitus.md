# Heraclitus / 赫拉克利特

Heraclitus / 赫拉克利特 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] at his own farewell party. The episode says he has repeatedly resigned and been rehired, turning departure into a company routine.

The joke combines Heraclitus' river-change motif with [[FriedrichNietzsche|尼采]]'s eternal-return frame as office procedure: a farewell party, gifts, resignation, and rehiring can become an exploitative loop rather than genuine change.
