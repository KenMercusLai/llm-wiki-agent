# Rebecca Lendl

Rebecca Lendl is a [[LongNow]] host introducing [[KateCrawford]] in [[kate-crawford-mapping-empires]] and [[StefanSagmeister]] in [[stefan-sagmeister-finally-something-good-yqqxftsvema]]. The Crawford source uses her introduction to frame the talk around technology, power, planetary resources, and the shift from human-centered to machine-centered representation.

In the Sagmeister podcast version, her introduction frames [[InformedOptimism]] as a long-term correction to attention systems that reward speed, catastrophe, and outrage while missing slow improvements.
