# Constellation Energy

Constellation Energy appears in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]] as part of the utility and power-delivery structure for [[HelionOrion]], the [[Helion]] project associated with [[Microsoft]]'s planned 50 MW electricity purchase.

In this source, Constellation's role is a reminder that [[CommercialFusionPower]] has to pass through conventional power-market, utility, and customer-delivery arrangements even if the generation technology is novel.
