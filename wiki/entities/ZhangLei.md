# Zhang Lei

Zhang Lei is named in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]] as one of the [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] co-founders alongside [[LiuWenhuan]] and [[WangYong]]. The episode later mentions him in the [[TongxinSoftware]] equity context.

## Source Position
- Zhang's role is less developed than Liu Wenhuan's or Wang Yong's, but he is part of the formal company stage of [[Deepin]].
- The source uses his presence to show that the project moved from an individual/community distribution into a multi-founder corporate structure.
- He remains a supporting figure in the later Tongxin shareholder map.
