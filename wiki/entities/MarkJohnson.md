# Mark Johnson

Mark Johnson is the [[TheIntelligence]] correspondent explaining the [[Bangladesh]] election segment in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]]. He frames the vote as the first competitive election since 2008 and says perhaps 40% of voters have never had the chance to cast a real ballot.

His reporting connects street-level election excitement to deeper transition risks. He travels with [[TariqRahman]], contrasts the [[BangladeshNationalistParty]] with [[JamaatEIslamiBangladesh]], and argues that the next government will probably have to find a route for rehabilitating the [[AwamiLeague]] if [[Bangladesh]] is to avoid excluding a large body of voters.
