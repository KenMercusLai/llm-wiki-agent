# Ilya Sutskever

Ilya Sutskever appears in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] through two calls with [[XieSaining]]. The first followed Xie's 2018 decision not to join [[OpenAI]]; the second came in 2024 after SSI was newly formed and Ilya invited Xie to work together.

[[hanyang-wakeng-meijie-jiasuo-hao-neirong-duitan-weishenme-zuo-fuyou-tiandi-59b766f5-ee1d-4040-a650-a96b7b4b70d5]] adds Ilya as a [[LongFormConversation]] example through [[LexFridman]]. [[HanYang]] uses the interview to show why technically valuable intuition can appear as metaphor, feeling, and reading history rather than as a clean AI-industry answer.

[[tsr-s4-samaltman-v4-tsr-s4-samaltman-v4]] adds Ilya as a central actor in [[SamAltman]]'s account of the November 2023 [[OpenAIBoardCrisis]]. Altman says Ilya texted him the night before, sent the Google Meet link shortly before the board meeting, and later expressed regret over his role while employees rallied for Altman's return.

## Route Contrast
The source uses Ilya as the strongest contrast case for Xie's route choice. Xie respected the invitation but declined again because his own work at [[NYU]] and later [[AMILabs]] was oriented toward [[WorldModels]], [[RepresentationLearning]], and [[JointEmbeddingPredictiveArchitecture]] rather than the same route Ilya was pursuing.
