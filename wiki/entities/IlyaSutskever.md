# Ilya Sutskever

Ilya Sutskever appears in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] through two calls with [[XieSaining]]. The first followed Xie's 2018 decision not to join [[OpenAI]]; the second came in 2024 after SSI was newly formed and Ilya invited Xie to work together.

## Route Contrast
The source uses Ilya as the strongest contrast case for Xie's route choice. Xie respected the invitation but declined again because his own work at [[NYU]] and later [[AMILabs]] was oriented toward [[WorldModels]], [[RepresentationLearning]], and [[JointEmbeddingPredictiveArchitecture]] rather than the same route Ilya was pursuing.
