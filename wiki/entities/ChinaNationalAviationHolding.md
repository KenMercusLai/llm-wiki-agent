# China National Aviation Holding / 中航集团

China National Aviation Holding appears in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] as the group-level state-owned aviation counterparty behind the attempted restructuring of [[EastStarAirlines]]. The episode links it with [[AirChina]] in the early-2009 negotiation over East Star's debts, equity, and continuation.

The source uses China National Aviation Holding to show how the private-airline crisis moved from company finance into state-sector consolidation and local-government coordination. [[LanShili]]'s rejection of the plan and later attempt to bring Zhengzhou into the conversation made the deal a control and regional-asset conflict rather than a narrow valuation negotiation.
