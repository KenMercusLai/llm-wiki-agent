# Georges Danton / 丹东

Georges Danton / 丹东 appears in [[125-mala-zhisi-beibi-de-ansha-huo-zhengyi-de-chujue-783972516]] as one of the revolutionary figures grouped with [[JeanPaulMarat|Marat]] and [[MaximilienRobespierre|Robespierre]] in the episode's simplified [[JacobinClub|Jacobin]] leadership cluster.

The source does not give Danton a long independent biography. His function is connective: he helps mark the moment when the [[FrenchRevolution|French Revolution]] moves from constitutional reform and factional debate toward a politics of emergency, popular pressure, denunciation, and terror.

The page therefore acts as a supporting node for [[RevolutionaryTerrorPolitics]] rather than a full Danton synthesis.
