# Zhuque-3 / 朱雀三号

Zhuque-3 is the [[LandSpace]] reusable-rocket project used as a comparison case in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]]. The episode says an earlier Zhuque-3 attempt performed well through much of flight and guidance, but final landing ignition became unstable and recovery failed.

The source frames Zhuque-3 as part of China's commercial reusable-rocket track. [[JerryTaikongSeng]] argues that failures can still produce valuable real-flight data, and the episode treats a future Zhuque-3 success as a possible confidence signal for commercial space if it combines orbital delivery with successful recovery.
