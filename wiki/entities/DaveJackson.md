# Dave Jackson

Dave Jackson appears in [[tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final-tsr-s3-ronconwayep1-v4final]] as the founder of [[AltosComputer]]. Former [[NationalSemiconductor]] colleagues recommended [[RonConway]] to Jackson as a strong sales and marketing hire, which brought Conway into Altos near the company's beginning.

The episode mostly uses Jackson as part of the Altos operating story. Conway says Jackson focused heavily on Europe while Conway focused on U.S. sales and marketing, letting Altos build a broad distribution network during the microcomputer timing window. Jackson's large post-IPO paper wealth also helps ground the source's [[EquityCompensationUpside]] theme.
