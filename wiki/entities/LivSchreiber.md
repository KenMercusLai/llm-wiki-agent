# Liv Schreiber

Liv Schreiber appears in [[strait-and-narrowing-the-iran-deal-crumbles-6a57608bf821a8210984481f]] as the founder of [[CampSocial]]. The episode quotes her explanation that a pre-planned camp schedule can relieve adults from constant decision-making and let them simply participate.

In the wiki, Schreiber belongs to the [[AdultSummerCamps]] branch rather than a broad founder profile. Her relevance is the product insight that adults may pay for structured offline social time because friendship, nostalgia, and decision relief are hard to arrange in ordinary adult life.
