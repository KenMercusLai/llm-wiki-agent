# World Values Survey

The World Values Survey appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as the cross-national survey instrument used to compare frontier AI model answers with human cultural patterns. The episode says the survey has tracked beliefs and moods since 1981 across more than 100 countries.

In the source, the survey's value is comparative. It maps societies from traditional to secular and from survival-oriented to self-expression-oriented, giving [[AIModelValueSurveying]] a way to say that many AI models resemble rich-country, secular, self-expression-oriented societies rather than being neutral or placeless.
