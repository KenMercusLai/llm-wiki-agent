# Steller's Sea Cow / 斯特勒海牛

Steller's sea cow appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] through [[GeorgWilhelmSteller]]'s observations during the [[BeringIsland]] survival winter. The episode says the animal helped feed the stranded crew, while Steller's specimens and descriptions were constrained by the practical need to return alive.

In this source, Steller's sea cow functions as a sharp island-ecology signal. It links scientific discovery, emergency subsistence, later marine-animal exploitation, and the broader pattern captured by [[IslandExtinctionAfterHumanArrival]] and [[ExplorationToExtractionPipeline]].
