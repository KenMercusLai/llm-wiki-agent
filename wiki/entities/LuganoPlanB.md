# Lugano Plan B

Lugano Plan B is the city-backed [[Bitcoin]] initiative discussed in [[tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260114-0114-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says [[Lugano]] launched it in 2022 to become a European Bitcoin hub, attract crypto companies, and make Bitcoin usable in shops, restaurants, and municipal services.

The initiative matters as [[MunicipalBitcoinAdoption]] rather than only branding. The source describes free crypto payment terminals for businesses, Bitcoin acceptance by hundreds of merchants, and city-service payments such as taxes and parking fines. Its limits matter just as much: [[MiaLiponi]] says short-term Bitcoin-only living is possible, but [[CryptoPaymentPracticalityGap]] remains in transport, fuel, bills, dentistry, and some medical services.

Plan B also carries a [[CryptoPublicLegitimacy]] problem. The source pairs official enthusiasm and almost 110 crypto-sector companies with resident skepticism, crime associations, and the vandalized [[SatoshiNakamoto]] statue. [[MicheleFolletti]]'s defense frames the project as less anonymous than cash, but that defense remains source-scoped to the mayor's argument.
