# Universa Investments

Universa Investments appears in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]] as one of the tail-risk strategy references that initially attracts [[ZhangXiaoyu]]. The episode uses Universa alongside [[NassimTaleb]] and [[MarkSpitznagel]] to motivate the question of whether a portfolio can be built to preserve or gain value when extreme market moves arrive.

The source's treatment is cautious: Universa is not presented as an easy template for ordinary investors, because [[TailRiskHedging]] depends on option pricing, execution, investor patience, and the ability to carry quiet-period costs.

## Connections
- [[NassimTaleb]] and [[MarkSpitznagel]] — reference figures connected to the Universa thread in the episode.
- [[TailRiskHedging]], [[ConvexityExposure]], and [[Antifragility]] — strategy ideas for which Universa serves as a reference point.
