# 《为什么伟大不能被计划》 / Why Greatness Cannot Be Planned

《为什么伟大不能被计划》 / Why Greatness Cannot Be Planned is the book discussed in [[139-weishenme-weida-buneng-bei-jihua-youzhizhe-shijingsheng-ma-849965735]]. The episode presents [[KennethStanley]] and [[JoelLehman]] as AI researchers whose work on evolutionary computation, image breeding, and maze-search experiments supports a broader critique of goal-first thinking.

The book's wiki role is methodological. In the source, it gives names and examples to the claim that important discoveries often arrive through [[NoveltySearch]] and [[SteppingStoneExploration]] rather than through a linear plan toward a predefined endpoint. The episode applies that claim beyond algorithms, using it to reinterpret reading, career choice, research funding, public metrics, and social policy.
