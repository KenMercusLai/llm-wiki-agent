# Ida Lupino / 艾达·卢皮诺

Ida Lupino is introduced in [[107-xianliao-weida-daoyanmen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-744150818]] as an actor who became a director and later worked extensively in television after film-industry exclusion. The episode specifically mentions her connection to noir filmmaking through The Hitch-Hiker and to later television-directing work.

In the wiki, Lupino extends [[EarlyWomenFilmPioneers]] into the mid-century industry boundary between film and television. Her career makes visible how women creators could keep directing by moving across formats even when film institutions narrowed their room to work.
