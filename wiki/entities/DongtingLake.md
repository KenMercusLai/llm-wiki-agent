# Dongting Lake / 洞庭湖

Dongting Lake appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as part of the flood-control conflict around the middle [[YangtzeRiver|Yangtze]] region. The episode says that keeping natural openings could relieve pressure on major dikes, but could also send floodwater toward lake communities.

In the source, Dongting Lake is therefore less a scenic place than a risk-distribution node. It shows why dikes, breaches, diversions, and "protection" can become inter-village, inter-provincial, and political conflicts.
