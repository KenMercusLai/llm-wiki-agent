# Zona Franca de Manaus

Zona Franca de Manaus is the special economic zone Brazil created in 1967 to pull factories and jobs into [[Manaus]]. In [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]], the zone offers large tax breaks to companies and becomes the visible success story behind Manaus's growth from a fading rubber town into an industrial city.

The same policy also defines the source's warning. [[BoscoSaraiva]] says the industrial hub would become economically unviable without tax incentives, so the free zone becomes a case of [[IndustrialSubsidyDependence]]. The episode does not claim the policy failed completely; it says job creation and urban growth did not automatically become self-sustaining technological upgrading.
