# LinkedIn

LinkedIn appears in [[tech-20260121-0121-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260121-0121-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a platform example in [[AvivOvadia]]'s discussion of [[ContentCredentials]] and media provenance. The episode says only a few platforms have implemented the relevant provenance standard, naming LinkedIn as one example.

The source does not analyze LinkedIn's broader business or moderation history. Its role here is to show that [[AIContentProvenance]] depends on platform adoption: standards can exist, and some cameras or phones can create credentials, but users benefit only when major distribution surfaces preserve, read, or display those signals.
