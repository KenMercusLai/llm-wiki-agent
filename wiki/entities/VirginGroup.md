# Virgin Group

Virgin appears in [[tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio-tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio]] as the early public customer-support signal for [[BoomSupersonic]]. The source centers on [[RichardBranson]] rather than a detailed Virgin corporate analysis: Boom sought a statement that Virgin wanted the first aircraft once they flew, and that statement arrived in time for [[YCombinator]] Demo Day.

The Virgin signal matters because Boom still lacked normal customer proof. A weak LOI from a startup airline was not enough, and the episode says [[MichaelSeibel]] pressed [[BlakeScholl]] on whether he had anything real. Virgin's support helped convert Boom from a mocked "internet founder airplane" story into a hard-tech startup with a recognizable aviation-market signal.
