# G. W. F. Hegel / 黑格尔

G. W. F. Hegel / 黑格尔 appears in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]] alongside [[ImmanuelKant|Immanuel Kant / 康德]] in the episode's treatment of retributive punishment. The source uses him as part of the idea that punishment can recognize an offender as a responsible subject rather than only as a dangerous object to manage.

The episode does not turn this into support for severe punishment. Hegel's role stays within [[PunishmentTheoryTradeoff]], where responsibility, dignity, deterrence, proportionality, and state-power limits all have to constrain each other.
