# Liu Wenhuan

Liu Wenhuan is one of the key people in the Deepin-to-Tongxin history presented in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode describes him as a Wuhan Linux community organizer, security-industry founder, investor, and later important backer of [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] and [[TongxinSoftware]].

## Source Position
- Liu is connected to WHLUG, early Chinese Linux community activity, and the security circle.
- The episode says he was one of the co-founders of [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] alongside [[ZhangLei]] and [[WangYong]].
- He is portrayed as someone who kept investing through Deepin's difficult early years.
- The source later describes him as still holding important voting power in [[TongxinSoftware]].

## Connections
- [[Deepin]] and [[HiweedLinux]] — community and technical lineage he helped commercialize.
- [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]] — company-stage entity.
- [[OpenSourceCommunityCommercialization]] — pattern illustrated by his role.
- [[TongxinSoftware]] — later company where the source says he remains influential.
