# Barbara Dingus

Barbara Dingus is the [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] flight attendant quoted in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]]. Her observation is that some passengers said they would never fly Spirit again but later came back, making the airline's business model legible through actual repeat behavior.

Her role connects cabin service to economics. Dingus supplies a worker-side view of the [[StatedRevealedPreferenceGap]]: customers could dislike the experience and still reveal that low fares mattered more at purchase time.
