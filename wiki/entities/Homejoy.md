# Homejoy

Homejoy was the home-services marketplace built by [[AdoraCheung]] and [[AaronCheung]] after multiple [[PathJoy]] pivots, as discussed in [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]]. The company is best known in the source as a cleaning marketplace where early validation came from local outreach, a simple website, phone calls, Craigslist supply recruiting, and Adora doing cleaning work herself.

The episode treats Homejoy primarily as a cautionary scaling case. Cheung says the company grew to nearly 300 employees, expanded across many cities, and entered a $19-cleaning price war before retention and operating quality were strong enough. That makes Homejoy a source case for [[ScalingBrokenProduct]], [[PriceWarGrowth]], [[ServiceMarketplaceQualityControl]], [[CustomerDiscoveryByDoingWork]], and the boundary between [[UnscalableFounderWork]] and [[ManualOperationsDebt]].
