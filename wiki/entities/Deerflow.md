# Deerflow

Deerflow is an open-source project discussed by [[HeTao]] in [[duihua-minimax-yan-junjie-m3-10x-jihua-10t-moxing-he-zhineng-de-zhongju-lqtilt8flvmv99v0gshhyfyraibe]]. It began as an effort to make expensive Deep Research-like capability more accessible with Chinese models, then expanded toward multimodal desktop-worker tasks such as reports, charts, podcasts, animation, and music.

## Source Position
- Deerflow is presented as both a product direction and an open-source community.
- The project has over 1K contributors, which creates breadth but also governance and codebase-quality pressure.
- Its ambition is broader than research answers: it aims at desktop knowledge work and multimodal output.
- The project is linked to the MiniMax token plan as an example of how model access can shape builder activity.
- Its codebase challenges illustrate [[AICodingVerification]] and long-term engineering responsibility.
