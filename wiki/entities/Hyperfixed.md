# Hyperfixed

Hyperfixed is the show context that routes [[JedKronfeld]]'s listener question into [[theres-no-business-like-dough-business]]. The episode identifies [[AlexGoldman]] as its host and says [[PlanetMoney]] receives the Wetzel's Pretzels question through Hyperfixed.

Its role in the wiki is as a question-discovery source rather than the main economics authority. The ordinary observation - three Wetzel's Pretzels locations in one station complex - becomes a reported economics case once Planet Money traces it through [[WetzelsPretzels]], [[JohnFisher]], and [[RickyAlam]].
