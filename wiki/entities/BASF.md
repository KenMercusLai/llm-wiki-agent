# BASF

BASF is a chemical and materials company mentioned in [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]] as part of the upstream material-supplier layer behind modern running-shoe midsoles. The source uses BASF to show that [[RunningShoeTechnology]] depends on chemical companies as well as consumer-facing brands.

## Key Claims
- Midsole competition is partly a materials-supply-chain competition, not only an internal brand-design contest.
- Suppliers such as BASF shape which foams and performance properties shoe brands can commercialize.
- The source contrasts European materials leaders with Chinese suppliers trying to close the gap.
