# Delta Air Lines

Delta Air Lines appears in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] as one of the large legacy carriers that challenged [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] after Spirit proved that many travelers would trade comfort for cheaper fares. The episode groups Delta with [[AmericanAirlines|American Airlines]] and [[UnitedAirlines|United Airlines]] in the shift toward lower upfront fares through basic economy.

Delta's role is source-scoped: the page does not assess the airline's broader strategy. In this episode, Delta represents the incumbent capacity to copy the attractive part of an entrant's offer while retaining a larger route network, loyalty program, and corporate customer base.
