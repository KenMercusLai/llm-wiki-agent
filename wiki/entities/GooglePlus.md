# Google Plus

Google Plus appears in [[socialradarspod-davidlieb-final]] as the internal Google product context that complicated [[GooglePhotos]]. [[DavidLieb]] says photo work was moved from Android into the Google Plus organization between Google's acquisition signing and the Bump team's arrival, leaving him expected to help increase photo posting to Google Plus rather than build the standalone photos product he believed was needed.

The page's role is not to summarize all of Google Plus history. In this wiki, it currently functions as an organizational example: a strategic acquisition can be redirected by an existing product mandate, and the acquired founder may have to fight the host company's internal incentives before the product's own logic can surface.
