# SynthID

SynthID appears in [[tech-20260122-0122-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260122-0122-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the watermarking signal that helped [[CaseyNewton]] identify a suspicious employee badge as AI-generated. In the source, [[Gemini]] reports that the badge image was generated in whole or in part with Gemini, giving Newton evidence that the source was trying to deceive him.

The page's wiki role is concrete provenance infrastructure. Unlike a broad standard such as [[ContentCredentials]], SynthID is presented here as a model-provider watermark/detection path that can reduce part of the [[AIRealityVerificationTax]] when journalists or platforms can query it and interpret the result.
