# Tang Haocheng

Tang Haocheng is the [[YiLaoYongYi]] guest in [[ep69-ai-shidai-lailin-touzi-buzai-shi-danji-moshi-lppxmdl3wlrrtswxwpzekz-qzygn]]. The episode presents him as an MIT financial-engineering graduate and AI entrepreneur working on an investment-assistance product for ordinary investors.

His investing story begins with using [[Netflix]] as a consumer and shareholder, then learning that strong growth can still disappoint a market that expected even more. That experience anchors the episode's move from company familiarity toward [[EarningsExpectationGap]], information systems, and investor process.

## Source Position
- Tang argues that the ordinary-professional investor gap is not simply intelligence, but information access, workflow, research samples, and review discipline.
- His earlier [[Magnify]] work used natural-language technology to help users search ETF and mutual-fund products.
- His current product thesis is that [[FinancialAIAgents]] can collect information, extract signals, compare arguments, and track user-selected assets without replacing final human judgment.
