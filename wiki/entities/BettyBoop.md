# Betty Boop

Betty Boop appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the public-domain character used for [[PlanetMoney]]'s official economics Valentine. [[JenniferJenkins]] says the 1930 version entered the public domain on January 1, 2026, allowing the team to use that image in a new card.

The source gives Betty Boop two roles. Legally, she demonstrates [[PublicDomainReuse]] after copyright expiration. Culturally, Jenkins frames her as an early comic character not defined by a relationship to a male character, which makes the reuse feel less like generic old IP and more like the return of a distinctive character to shared creative use.
