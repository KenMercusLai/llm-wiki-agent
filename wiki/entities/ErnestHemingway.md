# Ernest Hemingway / 海明威

Ernest Hemingway / 海明威 appears in [[01-maitian-shouwangzhe-20shiji-de-meiguo-jiabaoyu-523554422]] as part of the [[JDSalinger|J. D. Salinger / 塞林格]] wartime biography branch. The episode says Salinger met Hemingway in Paris during World War II, placing the younger writer's continued writing practice inside a larger modern American literary and wartime context.

The source uses Hemingway mainly to strengthen the claim that Salinger's later writing and withdrawal cannot be dismissed as adolescent posturing. Salinger had seen war and literary adulthood before [[TheCatcherInTheRye|《麦田里的守望者》]] became a youth-culture symbol.

[[135-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-san-dan-823746670]] adds a small comparative relationship mention. The episode names [[MarthaGellhorn|Martha Gellhorn / 玛莎·盖尔霍恩]] in the [[HGWells|H. G. Wells / H. G. 威尔斯]] branch and uses her later connection to Hemingway while discussing male literary charisma and respect for women.
