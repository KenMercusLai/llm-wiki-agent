# Iran

Iran appears in [[the-mourning-show-the-politics-of-khameneis-funeral-6a4b75084955fc4dc12ffcc7]] through [[AliKhamenei]]'s funeral, the Islamic Republic's succession problem, the recent war with America and Israel as described by the episode, and unresolved diplomacy around sanctions, the nuclear file, the [[StraitOfHormuz]], and regional influence.

[[missing-peace-will-israel-imperil-iran-deal-6a3bae9fe1238e54b0e886c3]] adds a prior diplomacy-focused Iran case. The episode says America and Iran signed a memorandum of understanding around the [[StraitOfHormuz]], frozen assets, and uranium-stockpile talks, but frames the package as vulnerable to [[Israel]]-[[Hezbollah]] fighting in [[Lebanon]].

[[coming-in-andy-britains-prime-minister-in-waiting-6a3512974a2a3be0f419384b]] adds the economic interpretation of the same memorandum. The episode frames [[IranPostwarEconomicRelief]] as a possible coup for Iran: blockade relief, sanctions relief, asset unfreezing, and a proposed $300bn reconstruction fund could make Iran look strengthened despite war damage, reduced oil exports, and extreme inflation.

The source frames Iran's state capacity as contested. Funeral logistics, slogans, official attendance, and regional procession geography are presented as evidence of organized regime control, while [[MujtabaKhamenei]]'s disappearance from public view keeps the question of actual command unresolved. An earlier [[KejiLuandun]] source uses an Iran-related conflict to discuss [[DigitalInfrastructureWarRisk]], making Iran one of the wiki's recurring cases for geopolitics crossing into infrastructure risk.

[[gulf-co-operation-counsel-what-next-for-the-region-6a4387d73fa89e3338f83da6]] adds the Gulf-facing consequence of the same war context. The episode treats uncertainty after the Iran war as a direct [[GulfStabilityRisk]] problem for the [[GulfCooperationCouncil]], especially if America and Iran cannot reach a durable settlement.
