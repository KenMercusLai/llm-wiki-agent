# Smirks

Smirks appears in [[the-sneaky-way-companies-get-new-chemicals-into-our-food]] as the U.S. importer that pitched [[TaraFlour]] to [[DailyHarvest]]. The episode says Daily Harvest was looking for a protein additive, while tara flour was marketed as a natural high-protein ingredient.

The company matters because importers can sit between foreign producers and consumer brands in [[FoodAdditiveRegulation]]. In the source's account, [[BillMarler]] used [[FoodSafetyLitigationDiscovery]] to trace the ingredient from [[MolinosPeru|Molinos]] through Smirks into the Daily Harvest product.

## Connections
- [[TaraFlour]] - ingredient Smirks imported and pitched.
- [[DailyHarvest]] - customer in the source's supply-chain story.
- [[MolinosPeru|Molinos]] - producer linked by the episode.
- [[SecretGRAS]] and [[GRASSelfCertification]] - regulatory route that made the ingredient's status hard for outsiders to see.
