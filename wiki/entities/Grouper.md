# Grouper

Grouper appears in [[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] as the group-dating startup [[TomBlomfield]] joined after leaving [[GoCardless]]. The product matched three people with three others for a low-pressure social date, grew quickly, and generated cash.

The source uses Grouper as a product-fit caution. Tom says many users tried it once and did not return, and the summary suggests that the lack of user agency in matching may have weakened repeat usage. In Tom's career arc, Grouper sits between payments infrastructure and digital banking: it showed fast consumer growth, but it did not become the durable company or domain he stayed with.
