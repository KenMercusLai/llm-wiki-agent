# 《索拉里斯星》 / Solaris

《索拉里斯星》 / Solaris is named in [[45-jiqirendashi-duo-xiwang-laimu-neng-pingjia-yixia-chatgpt-a-621855112]] as the [[StanislawLem|斯坦尼斯拉夫·莱姆]] work many Chinese readers may encounter first. The hosts argue that it can give a misleadingly solemn impression of Lem if it becomes the only reference point.

In the episode's contrast, [[TheCyberiad|《机器人大师》 / The Cyberiad]] is closer to the Lem they want to foreground: funny, satirical, linguistic, cybernetic, and aggressively inventive. The comparison therefore makes Solaris a gateway and foil rather than the main object of analysis.
