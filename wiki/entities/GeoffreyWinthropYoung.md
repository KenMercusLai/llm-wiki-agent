# Geoffrey Winthrop Young

Geoffrey Winthrop Young appears in [[89-jianqiao-yepanzhe-rang-women-zhuangyan-de-shangfang-jiewa-710382629]] as the author associated with [[TrinityCollegeClimbingGuide|《三一学院攀爬指南》]], an earlier Cambridge climbing guide that precedes [[TheNightClimbersOfCambridge|《剑桥夜攀者》]]. The episode uses him to show that [[CampusNightClimbing]] had a remembered guide tradition before [[NoelHSymington]]'s Whipplesnaith book.

His role in this source is mainly genealogical. He anchors the idea that illicit or semi-illicit campus climbing could be documented, stylized, and passed down as underground knowledge while still being socially distinct from formal mountaineering.

## Connections
- [[TrinityCollegeClimbingGuide]] - earlier guide attributed to Young in the episode.
- [[TheNightClimbersOfCambridge]] and [[NoelHSymington]] - later book and author in the same lineage.
- [[CampusNightClimbing]] - subculture the source places him within.
- [[UniversityOfCambridge]] - setting for the guide tradition.
