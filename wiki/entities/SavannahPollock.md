# Savannah Pollock

Savannah Pollock appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as a [[FolkstonGeorgia|Folkston]] native who opposes the local ICE detention center. The source says she recognizes why many residents see the facility as a reliable job source, especially when benefits such as insurance matter as much as wages.

Pollock's argument is that the community should not tie itself to a system that harms Black and brown bodies or casts detained people as criminals. She also warns that building a town's future around immigration policy is dangerous because presidential priorities can change every four years.
