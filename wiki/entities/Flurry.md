# Flurry

Flurry appears in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]] as an existing mobile analytics product [[SpenserSkates]] considered while trying to understand [[Sonalight]] retention. The source says available tools, including Flurry, did not answer the depth of behavioral product questions the founders wanted.

The episode also says some competitors shifted business models; Flurry is used as part of that market gap rather than as a standalone company history. Its wiki role is to clarify why [[Amplitude]] focused on product analytics rather than general traffic or ad-network analytics.
