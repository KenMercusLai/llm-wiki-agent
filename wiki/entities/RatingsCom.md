# Ratings.com

Ratings.com is the display-review context named in [[the-latest-tv-innovations-have-their-critics]]. The episode introduces [[SamuelBretton]] from Ratings.com to explain why TV quality should be understood as a set of tradeoffs rather than a single linear upgrade path.

In the source, Ratings.com matters because it supplies a product-testing perspective on [[TVMotionStutter]] and modern TV strengths. The episode uses Bretton's comments to contrast sports viewing, where clarity and brightness can help, with movies, where low-frame-rate pans can reveal stutter.
