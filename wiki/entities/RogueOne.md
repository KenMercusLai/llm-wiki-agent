# 《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One

[[RogueOne|《侠盗一号》 / Rogue One]] is the film context for [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode describes it as the [[StarWars|Star Wars]] story that explains how the [[RebelAlliance]] obtained the Death Star plans, with [[CassianAndor]] as one of the key participants.

Because viewers already know Cassian's later sacrifice in the film, [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]] has to make its prequel tension come from political formation and moral change rather than survival suspense. The episode uses that difficulty to foreground [[AntiHeroicResistanceNarrative]].
