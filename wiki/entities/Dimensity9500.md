# Dimensity 9500

Dimensity 9500 is the MediaTek smartphone-chip platform discussed in [[ai-shidai-de-chaoji-rukou-haishi-shouji-ma-s10e17-523a0d42-4c16-4dd6-a2ab-9277fec1a731]]. The episode uses it as the concrete case for [[HandsetChipCoDesign]] between [[Vivo]] and [[MediaTek]], saying that planning began roughly two to three years ahead and involved earlier scenario definition than old late-stage tuning relationships.

In the source, Dimensity 9500 matters less as a benchmark number than as an example of AI-era chip planning. The platform is tied to sufficient AI compute, NPU architecture, software tooling for model execution, and system-level scheduling across CPU, GPU, NPU, display, media, and foreground app workloads.
