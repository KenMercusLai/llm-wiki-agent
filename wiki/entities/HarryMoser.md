# Harry Moser

Harry Moser appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] as a representative of the [[ReshoringInitiative]] and the domestic-production case for manufacturing. The episode uses his argument to show why some supporters value factories for strategic goods, emergency resilience, and national-security capacity rather than only for cheap consumer prices.

His role in the wiki is source-scoped. He anchors the pro-reshoring side of the episode, while the same source then tests that argument against [[ManufacturingPayPremium]], [[ManufacturingJobQuality]], and [[GoodJobsForNonCollegeWorkers]].
