# Yearly Co.

Yearly Co. is the fine jewelry brand founded by [[AnneWilliams]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The brand's signature product is a solid 14-karat gold bangle rooted in Anne's family tradition of marking each year of marriage with a gold bracelet.

The episode presents Yearly Co. as a [[CommodityPriceExposure]] case. Gold-price increases over the prior 12 to 18 months lifted average order value but reduced order volume, forcing repeated price increases and pushing the original bangle toward a much higher price point. Guy and [[RonnenHarary]] advise Anne to preserve the milestone story and custom sizing promise while testing other materials or adjacent keepsake categories that do not make the brand dependent on gold alone.
