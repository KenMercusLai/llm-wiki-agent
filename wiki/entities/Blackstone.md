# Blackstone

Blackstone appears in [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]] as one of the financial institutions referenced in [[Anthropic]]'s enterprise AI partnership context. The episode uses firms like Blackstone to explain why private equity can be a powerful deployment channel: PE firms have portfolio companies, CEO-level influence, investment-operation workflows, and pressure to show LPs that they are acting on AI.

[[vanguard-1]] adds Blackstone through [[Vanguard]]'s move toward private assets under [[SalimRamji]]. In that source, the alliance is a strategic test: Vanguard wants broader access and retirement/advisory relevance, but private-market products can carry higher fees and more complexity than the low-cost index model associated with [[JohnBogle]].
