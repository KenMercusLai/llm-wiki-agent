# Charles Fort / 查尔斯·霍伊·福特

Charles Fort appears in [[95-dushi-chuanshuo-lieqi-gushi-he-women-neixin-shenchu-de-jiaolv-723831611]] as the collector of strange reports and "excluded" materials that conventional science often set aside. The episode uses Fort and later Forteans to show an adjacent field to [[UrbanLegend|urban legend]] research: anomalous stories can be archived and compared even when they should not be accepted uncritically.

The source quotes the Fortean posture as a useful tension: do not simply believe, but do not close inquiry before looking. In the wiki, Fort therefore belongs near both folklore and [[ScientificSkepticism]] rather than inside either pure credulity or pure debunking.
