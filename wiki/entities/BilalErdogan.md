# Bilal Erdogan

Bilal Erdogan is [[RecepTayyipErdogan]]'s youngest son and one of the possible successor figures in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]]. The source says he does not hold public office but sits on boards of pro-government foundations and has become more visible in rallies, media, and appearances with his father.

The episode frames Bilal as politically significant but vulnerable to the perception that government is becoming a family business. His possible promotion to lead the [[JusticeAndDevelopmentParty]] is presented as the clearest signal that he is being groomed.
