# 《抱孙》

《抱孙》 is a [[LaoShe|老舍]] story discussed in [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]]. The episode presents it as a harsh satire of grandson obsession, confinement ritual, family hierarchy, superstition, and fear of surgical medicine.

The story becomes the source's main case for [[BirthRitualMedicalConflict]]. The hosts connect its tragedy to modern issues around postpartum custom, family consent, cesarean-section fear, hospital mistrust, medical conflict, and the danger of letting face, ritual, and family authority outrank the patient.
