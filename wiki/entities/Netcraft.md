# Netcraft

Netcraft is the cybersecurity company cited in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]] for tracking AI-generated brand impersonation websites. [[GinnySpicer]] says the sites can be convincing on the front end, while repeated code patterns and unusual code artifacts may reveal AI-generated production.

The company enters the wiki as an evidence source for [[AIAssistedWebsiteScams]]. Its reported finding of 100,000 AI-generated websites impersonating nearly 200 brands makes the episode's claim operational rather than speculative: AI-assisted fraud can be detected as a pattern across many brand targets.
