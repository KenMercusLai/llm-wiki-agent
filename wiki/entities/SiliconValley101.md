# 硅谷101

硅谷101 is the podcast/show context for [[e243-te-lang-pu-huanxing-hongpai-zhiwai-meiguo-ziben-ruhe-yingkong-quanqiu-zutan]], [[e241-paoxie-jishu-diedaishi-malasong-paojin-2-xiaoshi-kao-ren-haishi-kao-xie]], [[e242-zuikuai-bannian-ai-paotong-zi-jinhua-yu-chen-tianqiao-shouxi-kexuejia-liaoliao-guigu-moxing-bi-zheng-zhi-di]], [[e240-openai-lianshou-pe-zaxia-40-yi-meiyuan-liaoliao-guigu-zuihuo-xin-zhiwei-fde]], and [[e225-saasye-shuqianyi-shizhi-zhengfa-ai-ruhe-biange-zuzhi-jiagou]]. In E243, the show extends its sports-business branch from running shoes into football finance by using [[PremierLeague]], [[ManchesterUnited]], [[ArsenalFC]], [[LiverpoolFC]], [[ChelseaFC]], and [[UEFA]] to examine [[AmericanSportsCapitalInEuropeanFootball]], [[FootballClubFinancialEngineering]], and [[FootballCommercializationFanConflict]]. In E241, the show moves into sports and consumer products by using [[Gaide]], [[Nike]], [[Adidas]], [[NikeVaporfly]], and [[AdidasAdiosProEvo3]] to examine [[RunningShoeTechnology]], [[MarathonPerformanceEcosystem]], and [[PerformanceFootwearMarket]]. In E242, the show frames [[RecursiveSelfImprovement]] as a current Silicon Valley model-company battleground and uses [[Apodex]] to examine how [[DeepResearch]], coding, [[AIVerification]], and [[ResearchTaste]] might become a self-improving AI system. In E240, it uses [[Cresta]], [[InvisibleTechnologies]], [[Jove]], and [[Oliver]] to examine why [[ForwardDeployedEngineer]] work, [[ForwardDeployedProductManager]] specialization, [[AIWorkflowTriage]], and [[PrivateEquityAITransformation]] are becoming central to enterprise AI adoption. In E225, it frames enterprise agents as a challenge to SaaS seats and organization design through [[BairongIntelligence]], [[DigitalEmployees]], and [[ResultAsAService]].
