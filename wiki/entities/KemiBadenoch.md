# Kemi Badenoch

Kemi Badenoch appears in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] as the [[ConservativePartyUK]] figure under whom the Conservatives are described as undergoing a modest revival. The source uses that revival as part of the wider [[UnitedKingdom]] party map around [[NigelFarage]], [[ReformUK]], [[LabourPartyUK]], and [[AndyBurnham]].

The source does not center Badenoch personally. Her role is to show that Reform's national strength is not uncontested: even while [[Clacton]] is favorable to Farage, the traditional right may still pressure Reform through Conservative recovery.
