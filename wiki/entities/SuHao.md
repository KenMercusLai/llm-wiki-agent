# Su Hao / 苏浩

Su Hao is introduced in [[e244-duan-dao-duan-vs-shangxia-fenceng-jiqiren-lujing-zhizheng-zhengzai-zhuanxiang-fc9a3737-81a9-49cf-a7d6-530c77df836e]] as a researcher who helped connect the [[ImageNet]] data-set mindset to 3D object understanding. The episode uses his trajectory to explain why robotics needs more than internet-scale 2D image and video data.

In the source's account, [[ShapeNet]], [[PartNet]], and PartNet Mobility extend object data from 2D recognition toward 3D shape, object parts, and mobility constraints, but still fall short of the full geometry, material, friction, elasticity, and dynamics needed for [[OpenWorldRobotManipulation]].
