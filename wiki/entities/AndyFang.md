# Andy Fang

Andy Fang appears in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]] as one of the [[DoorDash]] co-founders. [[TonyXu]] describes Fang and [[StanleyTang]] as the undergraduate computer-science side of the founding group that joined Xu and [[EvanMoore]] around small-business problems at Stanford.

The episode uses Fang mainly as part of the founding-team context behind [[PaloAltoDelivery]] and DoorDash's early [[JankyMVP]]. The broader lesson is that the team validated delivery through customer and merchant behavior before the product had polished software or a conventional marketplace interface.
