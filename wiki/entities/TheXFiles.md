# The X-Files / X档案

The X-Files enters the wiki through [[185-mogui-chumo-de-shijie-guanyu-yinmoulun-ufo-waixingren-bangjia-an-maitianquan-x-dangan-jiemi-ri-996181368]] as the popular-culture expression of the UFO slogan "I want to believe." The episode uses the slogan to name a real emotional posture inside [[UFOConspiracyCulture]]: mystery can be more attractive than verification.

The hosts counter that posture with "I want to know." In the source, this is not anti-story puritanism; it is [[ScientificSkepticism]] asking that belief remain answerable to evidence, alternatives, and correction.

[[95-dushi-chuanshuo-lieqi-gushi-he-women-neixin-shenchu-de-jiaolv-723831611]] adds the show as both a receiver and producer of [[UrbanLegend|urban legends]]. In that episode, The X-Files is part of the media system that can preserve older rumors, intensify UFO childhood fear, and give conspiracy material a more attractive narrative form.
