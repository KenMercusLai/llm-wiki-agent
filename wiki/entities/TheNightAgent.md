# The Night Agent

The Night Agent is one of the [[Netflix]] series cited in [[tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128]] as part of the platform's second-season viewer drop-off. The episode says [[Bloomberg]]-reported internal data showed its follow-up season losing 50% or more of its original viewers.

Its wiki role is evidentiary rather than biographical: the show helps illustrate how a breakout first season can produce broad sampling without guaranteeing durable fandom. That makes it a useful case for [[StreamingAudienceRetention]] and the limits of the [[BingeReleaseModel]] when production gaps and catalog competition intervene.
