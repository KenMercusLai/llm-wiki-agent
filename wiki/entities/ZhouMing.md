# 周明

周明 is introduced in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]] as an editor and guest in a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] discussion of [[Andor|《安多》 / Andor]]. The source presents him as strongly positive on the series, especially because it makes [[StarWars|Star Wars]] politically and institutionally interesting again rather than relying only on spectacle or franchise familiarity.

His comments emphasize how [[GalacticEmpire]] changes local life in small, administrative ways. The [[Aldhani]] discussion, where pilgrimage is displaced by imperial spectacle and work, anchors the episode's [[FalseChoiceGovernance]] concept.
