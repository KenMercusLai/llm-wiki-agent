# ABC

ABC is the television network that financed and distributed Disney programming in [[the-walt-disney-company-1]]. The episode presents the ABC deal as mutually useful: [[WaltDisney]] needed financing and public reach for [[Disneyland]], while ABC needed breakout content to compete in television.

The Disneyland television show promoted the coming park and Disney's film catalog, while the Davy Crockett craze showed that television could create new IP demand quickly. In the wiki, ABC is a key node in [[VerticalMediaDistribution]] because it connected financing, audience formation, programming, merchandise, and theme-park promotion.
