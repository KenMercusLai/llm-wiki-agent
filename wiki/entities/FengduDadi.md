# 丰都大帝

丰都大帝 is one of the underworld rulers mapped in [[136-chunri-mingmei-liaoliao-guishen-827911486]]. The episode traces a shifting line from older northern ghost-country and mountain-death ideas through Daoist registers and later Song-era associations with Fengdu county in Chongqing. Its point is not that one version is correct, but that the figure shows [[ChineseFolkReligionLayering]] in action.

In the source, Fengdu Dadi matters because his domain is imagined administratively. The dead may need a road pass, seals, permissions, and correct procedure before moving through the underworld. This makes him a key anchor for [[UnderworldBureaucracy]], where the other world borrows the paperwork, offices, and gatekeeping familiar from this world.
