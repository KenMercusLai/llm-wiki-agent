# Citgo

Citgo appears in [[venezuelas-recent-economic-history-update]] as the Venezuelan state-owned oil company used by [[HugoChavez]] for a symbolic U.S. outreach program. The source says Chavez offered discounted heating oil to poor people in the [[UnitedStates]] through Citgo.

In the wiki, Citgo makes [[OilRevenueDependence]] visible as political power. Oil revenue did not only fund imports and domestic programs inside [[Venezuela]]; it also gave Chavez a way to pursue influence abroad while the country remained exposed to a later oil-price collapse.
