# Rod Eggert

Rod Eggert is the professor cited in [[battlefield-rare-earths-how-the-us-lost-to-china]] for research on [[China]]'s rare-earth policies from 1975 to 2018. The episode uses his work to argue that China's dominance came from long industrial development rather than only from mineral deposits.

Eggert's role in the source is explanatory. He frames rare earths as inputs into engineered high-tech products and says they fit China's broader strategy to become a global manufacturing hub. The episode then connects that strategy to low-cost finance, trained scientists and engineers, domestic competition among rare-earth firms, and policies that pushed processing into China.
