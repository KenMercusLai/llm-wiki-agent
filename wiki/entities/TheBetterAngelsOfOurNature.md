# 《人性中的善良天使》 / The Better Angels of Our Nature

《人性中的善良天使》 / The Better Angels of Our Nature is the [[StevenPinker]] book invoked in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]]. The episode uses it to contrast modern claims about violence decline and feminization with [[JohannJakobBachofen]]'s mythic and spiritual-evolutionary account of mother-right.

The book's role in the source is limited but important: it helps the hosts distinguish "women's voice may reduce violence" from "ancient matriarchy is historically proven." This contrast becomes part of [[FemaleCivilizingPower]].
