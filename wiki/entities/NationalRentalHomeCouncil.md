# National Rental Home Council

The National Rental Home Council appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] through [[AdrienneToddman]]. In the episode, it represents the industry-side argument that laws restricting corporate ownership of homes could discourage rental-community construction.

The council matters to the wiki as an advocacy context rather than a neutral research source. Its position helps define the policy-risk side of [[CorporateLandlordTradeoffs]]: a crackdown aimed at existing landlords could also reduce [[BuildToRentHousing]] if written too broadly.
