# 蜜獾吃书

蜜獾吃书 is the podcast/show context for [[ritan-chuantai-jiemei-liaotianhui-chongqi-rensheng-yeshi-wo-zui-yaojiao-616116425]], [[44-yunqi-de-youer-dubo-chengyin-yinwei-women-haipa-ziyou-619995308]], [[46-zhe-shijie-weiyi-de-ni-qing-xiangxin-na-duo-manman-zhanfang-de-hua-623717720]], [[47-niao-youshenme-haokande-yuanlai-dandinghe-shi-tude-625613924]], [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]], [[49-liwuya-lai-le-liaoliao-women-weisha-ai-yuedu-629280098]], [[50-kaitangshou-jieke-jiean-baogao-nvfayi-de-zhengyi-zhizuo-630666604]], [[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]], [[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]], [[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]], [[158-quanyuan-mingzhentan-tuili-shupian-lai-yibao-929377122]], and [[tuokouxiu-de-xiaojia-fuyou-zhishang-keneng-hui-fei-yingzhe-tianguang-927160655]]. In the restart-life source, [[QinZong|秦总]] introduces herself as a host of the show and joins [[Hookie]] and [[YinJie|殷姐]] for a [[Ritan|日谈]] crossover Women's Day conversation.

The episode moves the show into the wiki's life-design branch. Rather than reviewing a book directly, it uses [[BrushUpLife|《重启人生》]] as a shared cultural frame for talking about life restarts, professional choice, pet loss, mother-daughter grief, and [[FemaleSelfPossession]].

[[44-yunqi-de-youer-dubo-chengyin-yinwei-women-haipa-ziyou-619995308]] adds the show's direct book-discussion mode through [[TheLureOfLuck|《运气的诱饵》]]. It connects gambling anthropology to [[MachineGamblingAddiction]], [[MachineZone]], [[PrivateActuarialism]], and broader [[AddictiveInteractionDesign]] in digital products.

[[46-zhe-shijie-weiyi-de-ni-qing-xiangxin-na-duo-manman-zhanfang-de-hua-623717720]] adds the show's autism and caregiving branch through [[UniquelyHuman|《这世界唯一的你》 / Uniquely Human]]. It connects [[QinZong|秦总]]'s tutoring memory to [[AutismAsHumanDifference]], [[BehaviorAsCommunication]], [[AutismSafeEnvironmentDesign]], [[AutismFamilySupport]], and [[AutismCureScamRisk]].

[[47-niao-youshenme-haokande-yuanlai-dandinghe-shi-tude-625613924]] adds the show's natural-science reading branch through [[BirdsAreInteresting|《鸟有什么好看的》]]. It connects humorous book discussion to [[KawakamiKazuto|川上和人]], [[BirdwatchingAsAttention]], [[OrnithologicalFieldwork]], [[ScientificSamplingDiscipline]], [[IslandEcologicalSuccession]], [[InvasiveSpeciesManagement]], and [[ObservationBeforeInference]].

[[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]] adds the show's modern Chinese literature and social-satire branch through [[LaoShe|老舍]] and [[KaiShiDaJi|《开市大吉》]]. It connects humorous reading to [[LaoSheSatiricalHumanism]], [[CivicAudienceEtiquette]], [[MedicalScamPackaging]], [[BirthRitualMedicalConflict]], and [[RenqingOrder]].

[[49-liwuya-lai-le-liaoliao-women-weisha-ai-yuedu-629280098]] adds the show's meta-reading branch with [[LiWuya|李乌鸦]]. Instead of discussing one book, it connects reading shame, forgetting, original-text texture, childhood access, classics, and fiction's experiential value to [[ReadingAsLifeExperience]], [[ClassicReadingComplexity]], [[FamilyReadingEcology]], and [[NonInstrumentalLiteraryReading]].

[[50-kaitangshou-jieke-jiean-baogao-nvfayi-de-zhengyi-zhizuo-630666604]] adds the show's true-crime and social-history branch through [[JackTheRipperCaseClosed|《开膛手杰克结案报告》]]. It connects [[PatriciaCornwell]]'s disputed accusation against [[WalterSickert]] to [[ContestedForensicAttribution]], then shifts from suspect-hunting toward [[VictimStigmaInTrueCrime]], [[VictorianWomenPrecarity]], and [[ModernCriminalInvestigationFormation]].

[[154-wode-mingzi-jiao-hong-yong-yiqian-lingyi-ye-de-xintiao-wancheng-zhechang-mousha-xia-919262400]] adds the show's Turkish-literature and art-history branch through [[OrhanPamuk|奥尔罕·帕慕克]] and [[MyNameIsRed|《我的名字叫红》]]. It connects murder mystery to [[OttomanMiniaturePainting]], [[FrankishPortraiturePressure]], [[MiniatureStyleSignatureTension]], [[BlindnessMemoryArtIdeal]], [[MultiPerspectiveMurderNarration]], and [[SecularHappinessAgainstIdeology]].

[[156-yige-afuhan-nvren-de-laixin-zhishang-de-guang-he-chuban-beihou-de-gushi-922362198]] adds the show's Afghanistan, women's-writing, and publishing-process branch through [[HadiyaHaidari|哈迪亚·海达里]]'s [[YiGeAfuhanNvrenDeLaixin|《一个阿富汗女人的来信》]] and [[AnQi|安琪]] of [[YouguangPublishing|有光]]. It connects literary testimony to [[AfghanWomenFirstPersonWriting]], [[ProtectionAsControl]], [[WarGenderedCivilianHarm]], [[LiteraryPublishingAsMaterialSupport]], [[TranslationPublishingWorkflow]], and [[EditorAsCulturalProjectManager]].

[[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]] adds the show's tall-tale, fantasy, and philosophy branch through [[AdventuresOfBaronMunchausen|《吹牛大王历险记》]]. It connects [[MunchausenBaron|敏锡豪森男爵]], [[RudolfErichRaspe]], [[BaMaGua|《扒马褂》]], [[TallTaleTradition]], [[AbsurdRationality]], [[MunchausenSelfBootstrapping]], [[AdultSatiricalChildrensClassics]], and [[SuperheroTallTaleContinuity]].

[[158-quanyuan-mingzhentan-tuili-shupian-lai-yibao-929377122]] adds the show's puzzle-mystery branch through [[QuanYuanXianYiRen|《全员嫌疑人》]] and [[QuanYuanMingZhenTan|《全员名侦探》]]. It connects light detective fiction to [[PuzzleSnackMystery|推理薯片]], [[WatsonPower|华生力]], [[ClosedCircleMystery|暴风雪山庄]], and the idea that puzzle construction can be a real literary pleasure without needing to become serious realism.

[[tuokouxiu-de-xiaojia-fuyou-zhishang-keneng-hui-fei-yingzhe-tianguang-927160655]] adds the show's contemporary nonfiction and creator-psychology branch through [[Xiaojia|小佳]] and [[FuyouZhishang|《蜉蝣直上》]]. It connects stand-up, prose authorship, Minnan family memory, funeral custom, complex women, book sales, public reviews, and stepping back from televised competition to [[StandUpAsSelfNarration]], [[MinnanFamilyWriting]], and [[CreatorEvaluationPressure]].
