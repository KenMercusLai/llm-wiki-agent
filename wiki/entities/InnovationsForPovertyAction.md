# Innovations for Poverty Action

Innovations for Poverty Action appears in [[the-laws-of-the-office-revisited]] through the Uganda example in [[AliceEvans]]'s social-change segment. The episode says the organization ran a video campaign showing people reporting gender-based violence and being supported by their community.

The source uses the campaign as a [[TruthfulSocialProof]] case: visible examples of real support can change what people believe others will tolerate or back, helping behavior shift without pretending a norm already exists when it does not.
