# Christy Clement

Christy Clement is the founder caller behind [[VashonIslandCoffeeDust]] in [[advice-line-with-christina-tosi-of-milk-bar-fd7f2406-fda8-4c1d-936c-d20210215f96]]. She started the business after spice blends helped her move away from cream and sugar in coffee, then grew it through direct, marketplace, wholesale, local shop, coffee shop, and Amazon channels.

Her question in the episode is how to design the business so people who receive Coffee Dust as a gift become loyal buyers. The advice she receives centers on packaging, bundling, visible daily ritual, customer-generated use cases, and lower-friction usage.
