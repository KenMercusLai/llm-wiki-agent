# Momo

Momo is the default personal AI assistant inside [[Moxt]], described in [[agi-lai-le-wo-yong-le-yizhou-toupi-fama-duitan-zhang-haoran-moxt-lianhe-chuangshiren-lkiysdddezlyzh8rt2grbbm4r-gq]]. [[ZhangHaoran]] presents Momo as the first [[AICoworkers]] experience in a workspace where agents can know the user's preferences and the surrounding [[OrganizationalContext]].

The episode uses Momo to show how Moxt differs from a standalone chatbot. Momo can be configured to the user's preferences, can participate in workspace comments, can help draft and revise documents after conversations, and can sit alongside other role-specific AI coworkers created by a team.

Momo also exposes the interface problem Moxt is trying to solve. Zhang Haoran says current IM tools such as [[Slack]] make it awkward to bring a personal assistant directly into private conversations, forcing users to copy and forward context. Moxt's workspace model tries to remove that friction by giving the assistant a native place to live.
