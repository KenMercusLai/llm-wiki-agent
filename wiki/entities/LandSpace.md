# LandSpace / 蓝箭航天

LandSpace, or 蓝箭航天, appears in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]] as one of the Chinese private space companies working toward reusable rockets. The episode discusses its [[Zhuque3]] program as a commercial-space comparison to the state-led [[LongMarch10B]] sea-net recovery.

The source does not treat LandSpace as following the same route as Long March 10B. It uses the company to distinguish private rocket economics and risk tolerance from resource-concentrated, multi-institute recovery systems such as [[SeaNetRocketRecovery]].
