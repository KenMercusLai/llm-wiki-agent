# John Hunter / 约翰·亨特

John Hunter appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as an anatomist and collector whose work helps explain the bodily imagination behind [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]]. The episode links Hunter to anatomical collections, resurrection-style experiments, artificial respiration, electrical stimulation, and the display of unusual bodies.

Hunter is also tied to [[CharlesByrne|Charles Byrne]], whose remains become a case of collection, consent, and scientific ambition. That places Hunter at the boundary between [[CadaverTradeAnatomyFear]], [[EarlyTransplantationImaginary]], and [[ExperimentalScienceEthics]].
