# 阿布谨也 / Abe Kinya

阿布谨也 is the Japanese historian discussed in [[111-huayi-modishou-kuailai-he-lishixuejia-yiqi-tuili-tonghua-zhenxiang-751190766]] as the author of [[PiedPiperMedievalEurope|《花衣魔笛手：传说背后的欧洲中世纪》]]. The episode presents him as a social historian whose attraction is not that he solves [[PiedPiperOfHamelin|花衣魔笛手]] with one hidden key, but that he reconstructs the medieval world around the legend.

His method connects archival clues, local geography, [[Hamelin|哈默尔恩]] city history, migration, war, class conflict, famine, plague, and despised occupations into [[EvidenceBoundFolkloreInquiry]]. The episode uses him to model [[LegendAsSocialHistory]]: old stories may preserve historical pressure even when their literal plot has been reshaped by later religious, civic, and fairy-tale needs.
