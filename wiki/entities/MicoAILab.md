# Mico AI Lab

Mico AI Lab is the AI application team inside [[MicoWorld]] discussed by [[Simon]] in [[ep101-duihua-simon-ai-chuangyezhe-de-diyi-xiang-jibengong-shi-ba-zhang-suan-mingbai-lhrrhfslnd1z9cuu2vkuxbb5pvjx]]. The source says the lab was formed around spring 2023 after the ChatGPT/AIGC wave made the company decide it needed to explore how generative AI could support social and pan-entertainment products.

The lab is positioned as application-oriented rather than a frontier-model research group. Simon says the harder question is not whether an AIGC capability can be reproduced from papers, engineering, and compute, but whether it lands in a product scene where costs, user willingness to pay, content quality, and market size make sense.

## Key Points
- The lab evaluated [[CharacterAI]]-style chat companionship but found the memory and prompt-cost dynamics unattractive at the time.
- It chose AI game/social directions because games have clearer user segments, mature payment behavior, and better-known paid feature surfaces.
- Hiring criteria differ by role: ordinary game engineers need interest and integration ability, while AI specialists need strong content and business understanding.
- The team benefits from being an internal venture: it has resources and cash-flow backing from [[MicoWorld]] but can still run relatively independent projects.
