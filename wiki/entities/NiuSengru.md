# 牛僧儒

牛僧儒 is the late-Tang chancellor and writer at the center of [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]]. The episode presents him through two linked frames: a [[NiuLiPartyStruggle|牛李党争]] political actor associated with [[LiZongmin|李宗闵]] against [[LiDeyu|李德裕]], and the authorial figure behind [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]].

The source's useful move is to keep both sides visible. 牛僧儒 is not reduced to a faction label, because the stories reveal a writer interested in utopias, riddles, foxes, dragons, object spirits, Daoist ritual, and the hard edge between human feeling and transcendence. But the literary image is also not detached from politics, because [[ZhouQinXingJi|《周秦行纪》]] and the party-struggle discussion show how authorship and reputation could become weapons.
