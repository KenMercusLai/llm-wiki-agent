# 孟婆

孟婆 is the underworld figure associated with the soup of forgetting in [[136-chunri-mingmei-liaoliao-guishen-827911486]]. The episode treats her as a solution to a narrative problem in rebirth stories: if souls reincarnate, why do people not remember previous lives?

The source gives multiple versions rather than one standard biography. In one, Meng Po is a Western Han woman who studies texts and Buddhist scripture, urges people to avoid killing, and is later assigned to the underworld. In another, souls enter Meng Po's lodge, receive soup from beautiful women, and only afterward discover that splendor has become wasteland and beauty has become bones. The forced-drinking variant keeps forgetting inside [[UnderworldBureaucracy]]: even memory loss has a procedure and an enforcement mechanism.
