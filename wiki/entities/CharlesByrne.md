# Charles Byrne / 查尔斯·伯恩

Charles Byrne appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as the "Irish giant" whose body and afterlife help explain the episode's reading of the creature in [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]]. The source connects the creature's size, strength, intelligence, and endurance to anatomical collections and public fascination with unusual bodies.

Byrne's case is also ethically loaded because [[JohnHunter|John Hunter]] is associated with acquiring and displaying his remains. The wiki therefore connects Byrne to [[CadaverTradeAnatomyFear]] and [[ExperimentalScienceEthics]], where knowledge-seeking and bodily dignity collide.
