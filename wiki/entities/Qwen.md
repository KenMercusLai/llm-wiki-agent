# Qwen

Qwen is Alibaba's model family and the technical focus of [[ali-qianwen-lizhi-yuzhen-zai-jiwanren-de-tieqiu-li-ruhe-timian-shengcun-keji-luandun]]. The episode describes Qwen as one of China's strongest [[OpenSourceAIModels]], alongside [[DeepSeek]], and emphasizes its value for developers who need models of different sizes, deployment constraints, and fine-tuning use cases.

## Source Position
- Considered important for China's AI competitiveness and overseas developer adoption.
- Valued partly because open source makes the model usable by smaller teams and international startups.
- Expected by the hosts to continue open-source activity, though release timing, model scope, and commercial boundaries may change.
