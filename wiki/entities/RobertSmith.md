# Robert Smith

Robert Smith appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the [[PlanetMoney]] host opening the Summer School World Tour. His role is to frame the episode's movement between [[Australia]]'s water market and [[NewZealand]]'s inflation-targeting experiment as a comparative economics lesson.

The episode pairs Smith's hosting with [[JustinWolfers]]' explanatory role: the conversation keeps the water and inflation stories tied to the broader question of how rules make markets, prices, and expectations work.

[[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] adds Smith as a [[BusinessHistoryPodcast|Business History]] host whose [[CurtFlood]] episode is carried by [[PlanetMoney]]. The source uses his and [[JacobGoldstein]]'s business-history framing to connect [[MajorLeagueBaseball|MLB]] free agency to [[LaborMonopsony]], antitrust, public opinion, and [[SportsCollectiveBargaining]].
