# National Football League

The National Football League appears in [[how-we-got-free-agents-in-baseball]] as a comparison case for the spread and limits of professional-sports free agency. The source says similar challenges were happening in football after [[CurtFlood|Curt Flood]]'s baseball case, but that NFL free agency remained more limited until the 1990s.

The NFL also supplies the episode's salary-cap comparison: unlike [[MajorLeagueBaseball|Major League Baseball]], the NFL uses a salary cap while still allocating around half of revenue to player salaries, according to the source.
