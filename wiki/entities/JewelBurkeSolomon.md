# Jewel Burke Solomon

[[tech-20260313-0313-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260313-0313-mp-tech-pod-128]] has Solomon return to [[MarketplaceTech]] for a Bytes episode on [[Amazon]] AI guardrails, [[YouTube]]'s media dominance, and [[Meta]]'s acquisition of [[MoteBook]]. Her main contribution is to connect fast AI adoption with review and governance: AI coding tools should be checked before deployment, YouTube can use AI and [[Alphabet]] data to improve creator and targeting tools, and MoteBook's outcome should be read through AI talent competition and founder expertise rather than as a generic vibe-coding shortcut.

Jewel Burke Solomon is the [[CollabCapital]] guest interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] in [[tech-20260213-tech-pod-128-tech-20260213-tech-pod-128]]. In the episode, she interprets [[Alphabet]]'s long-term borrowing for AI projects, the early security and social questions around [[MoteBook]], and [[Spotify]]'s growth around [[SpotifyWrapped|Spotify Wrapped]].

Her contribution to the wiki is a weekly-news synthesis rather than a single technical thesis. She connects [[AIInfrastructureDebtFinancing]] to data-center investment and startup liquidity, treats agent-only social spaces as both fascinating and unsafe, and reads Spotify's consumer growth through [[PersonalizationAsSocialIdentity]] and curation.
