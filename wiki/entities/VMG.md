# VMG

VMG is the San Francisco investment group discussed in [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]] as the institutional investor that backed [[JustinsNutButter]] around 2012. The source says VMG invested about $47 million when the company was doing about $20 million in annual sales.

## Key Points
- [[JustinGold]] chose VMG because he viewed it as the best scaling partner rather than simply the highest offer.
- VMG's investment helped the company hire experienced food operators, develop products, expand stores, and grow beyond grocery.
- The episode links the investment to [[FounderRoleTransition]] because Justin had to share more operating control as the business scaled.
