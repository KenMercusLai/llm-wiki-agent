# 龄官 / Lingguan

[[167-ming-ruo-zhao-shuang-weishenme-hongloumeng-bu-xuyao-daoming-949917324]] uses 龄官 as the clearest personal case for [[LowStatusRegistry|贱籍身份]] and [[HouseholdTroupeStatusBoundary]] inside [[HongLouMeng|《红楼梦》]]. She resembles [[LinDaiyu|林黛玉]] in temperament and romantic intensity, but her performer status means that the comparison itself can be insulting in the novel's social world.

The episode stresses that Lingguan does not fully own even her name. Her relationship with Jia Qiang mirrors Bao-Dai feeling in miniature, but law and status make the outcome far harsher: an official family descendant cannot ordinarily marry a low-status performer, so her difficult behavior is read as knowledge of blocked fate rather than simple romantic irrationality.
