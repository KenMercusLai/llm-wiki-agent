# Understanding America Study

Understanding America Study appears in [[tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the long-running panel run by the [[UniversityOfSouthernCalifornia]] that [[CarlyUrban]]'s team used for a randomized device experiment. Some respondents were required to use smartphones, while others were required to use a desktop, laptop, or tablet.

The panel is important here because it lets the source move beyond correlation. The episode does not only compare lower scores among people who happened to use phones; it describes an assignment design that tests whether the device itself can create a [[SmartphoneSurveyPenalty]].
