# Snowflake

Snowflake appears in [[tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly-tsr-ycoffsite-kasishgupta-v1-audioonly]] as one of the enterprise data-warehouse systems that [[Hightouch]] customers already used as their customer-data foundation. [[KashishGupta]] uses Snowflake to explain why Hightouch's market was not simply another customer-data store: enterprises wanted to activate data from their own database rather than copy it into each downstream marketing platform.

In the source, Snowflake is part of the infrastructure backdrop for [[EnterpriseDataActivation]]. The value shifts from collecting the data to safely connecting governed warehouse data into advertising, lifecycle marketing, and sales workflows.
