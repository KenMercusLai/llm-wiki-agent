# 多多良胜五郎 / Tatara Katsugoro

多多良胜五郎 is the yokai researcher who leads [[KonjakuZokuHyakkiKumo|《今昔续百鬼·云》]] as presented in [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]]. The episode describes him as brilliant, obsessive, directionally strange, socially frustrating, and comic: he can talk endlessly about yokai and fieldwork, but ordinary practical judgment often collapses around him.

In [[AnganKozo|《暗眼小僧》]], Tatara drags [[NumagamiRenji|沼上蓮次]] through storm, hunger, and dangerous mountain travel because local yokai traces might disappear. He is repeatedly wrong as a detective, yet his wrong theories still bring folklore evidence, image details, homophones, and local memories into view.

## Key Claims
- Tatara embodies [[EvidenceBoundFolkloreInquiry]] in an eccentric fieldwork form: he wants firsthand local material before war, development, and memory loss erase it.
- His method is not reliable police deduction, but it is useful for [[YokaiMystery|妖怪推理]] because it asks why a scene becomes intelligible as yokai.
- The source says he is modeled on [[TadaKatsumi|多田克己]], a real yokai researcher and Kyogoku friend.
