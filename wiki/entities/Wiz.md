# Wiz

Wiz is the cloud security platform named in [[tech-20260213-tech-pod-128-tech-20260213-tech-pod-128]] for its review of [[MoteBook]]. The episode says Wiz reported access to sensitive information, including email addresses, making MoteBook a security caution rather than only a novelty agent-social experiment.

In the wiki, Wiz's relevance is narrow but useful: it turns [[AISocialNetworks]] and [[AgenticEconomy]] from product speculation into an operational security question. If agents are invited into third-party spaces, [[AgentPermissionBoundaries]], account separation, data exposure, and platform hardening become first-order requirements.
