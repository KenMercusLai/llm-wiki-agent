# On Disobedience / 论不服从

On Disobedience / 《论不服从》 is the [[ErichFromm|Erich Fromm]] work used as a comparison point in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]]. The episode presents it as more theoretical and philosophical than [[TheArtOfInsubordination|《不服从的艺术》]], which the hosts read for practical dissent tactics.

The page's role in the wiki is comparative rather than a full independent reading of Fromm. In this source, it helps separate a moral critique of obedience from the episode's operational question: how to speak, persuade, organize, endure, and keep judgment after deciding not to obey.
