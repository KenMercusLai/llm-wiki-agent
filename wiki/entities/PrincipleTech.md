# 原则科技

原则科技 is the early startup context where [[XiaoSun]] worked before [[CreateWise]] in [[ep119-duihua-xiao-sun-qixing-800-gongli-ba-ziji-jiuchu-shenyuan-ningyuan-meitian-gongzuo-22-xiaoshi-wo-ye-buxiang-zai-shangban-le-lmj5rmebef8y8p0ayfptqoqnhiu2]]. XiaoSun describes its mission as making software for independent developers and freelancers so they could make money more efficiently.

## Key Points

- The company explored creator-tool directions including screen recording, [[LusunTeleprompter]], and a live-stream assistant.
- XiaoSun says the team cared about Baidu traffic, SEO, paid acquisition ROI, after-sales groups, and direct service to users.
- The live-stream assistant reached dozens of users but struggled with customer connection, technical difficulty, and server cost.
- The episode uses PrincipleTech to show how [[SelfDirectedWork]] can start with excitement and then weaken when feedback, growth, or execution clarity fade.
