# Aldhani

Aldhani is the setting of the payroll-raid and local-culture discussion in [[42-anduo-fengqi-yu-qingping-zhi-mo-616391638]]. The episode says the raid itself is less complex than the surrounding picture of how [[GalacticEmpire]] changes a people: pilgrimage, sacred viewing sites, local ritual, state spectacle, work, and "help" are all rearranged until the older gathering nearly disappears.

In the wiki, Aldhani anchors [[FalseChoiceGovernance]]. The point is not only that force exists, but that a regime can make its preferred life path feel like the practical option while the older collective practice loses people, time, and legitimacy.
