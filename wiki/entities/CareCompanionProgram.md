# Care Companion Program

The Care Companion Program is the in-country name [[EdithElliott]] gives in [[socialradars-edithelliot-v3]] for [[NooraHealth]]'s family caregiver training work. The program trains relatives and other caregivers around maternal and newborn care, post-surgical recovery, cardiac surgery, general surgery, and related health needs.

Its importance in the wiki is that it makes [[FamilyCaregiverTraining]] concrete. The program does not treat family presence as background emotion; it turns that presence into structured readiness through contextualized images, practical instruction, timing, language, and confidence-building inside hospital and household workflows.
