# Zhao Dongping / 赵东平

Zhao Dongping is presented in [[144-dui-yang-meng-de-4-xiaoshi-fangtan-xiaofei-dianzi-si-yu-sheng-di-san-lei-gongsi-duan-ce-moxing-chanpin-fangfa-youxi-moshi-lnjleqjgjo1txupouvygmdv7oo8b]] as [[AnkerInnovations]]' cofounder and the global-sales complement to [[YangMeng]]'s product and abstraction strengths. In Yang's account, Zhao handled global sales while Yang focused on product definition, creating an early split between overseas channel execution and product creation.

## Source Position
- Zhao is described as more sales- and execution-oriented, complementing Yang's more abstract, logical, product-focused style.
- The early Anker pairing combined overseas marketplace understanding, global sales, product definition, and Shenzhen supply-chain proximity.
- The source uses Zhao less as an independent biography than as part of Anker's founder-configuration explanation.
