# Share AI

Share AI is [[LaiXinlu]]'s agent-infrastructure company discussed in [[tan-mi-claude-code-gao-dong-agent-harness-dui-tan-lai-xin-lu-lkluk3i7c4gzw4jvxee7odsfgis3]]. The company built [[LearnClaudeCode]] as a way to study [[ClaudeCode]] and explain practical [[AgentHarness]] design.

The episode presents Share AI's K-series toolchain as an attempt to provide agent-native infrastructure across cloud, browser, mini-program, and other JavaScript-capable environments. Its direction is Unix-like but lightweight: give agents files, commands, runtime state, observation, and coordination without assuming a heavy traditional sandbox everywhere.
