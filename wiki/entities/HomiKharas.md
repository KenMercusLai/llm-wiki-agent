# Homi Kharas

Homi Kharas is the economist in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] who helped coin the term [[MiddleIncomeTrap]] with another [[WorldBank]] economist. The episode uses his explanation to move from the specific [[Manaus]] case to the wider problem of countries that industrialize enough to become middle income but then stop converging with rich countries.

Kharas describes the old development path as infrastructure, schools, roads, simple manufacturing, rising skills, higher wages, and then more advanced production and services. His argument in the source is that this path produced some exceptional successes, such as [[SouthKorea]] and [[Singapore]], but it does not give today's middle-income countries a single reliable recipe.
