# Jeni Britton

Jeni Britton is the founder of [[JenisSplendidIceCreams]] and the [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. The episode presents her as a food founder who left art in 1996, built from Columbus, and later uses that experience to advise early CPG founders on taste, retail buyers, sampling, capital, and communication.

Britton also discusses [[Flora]], a fiber company built around produce trimmings and microbiome health. She connects Flora and ice cream through a broader idea that food companies can make people feel better while still needing real product appeal and operating discipline.
