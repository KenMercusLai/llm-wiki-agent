# Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] through [[RonConway]]'s account of a Google round that never happened. Conway says Sandberg emailed him in November 2001 about preparing another funding round, then later said [[Google]] likely would not need it as [[AdWords]] revenue began working.

The episode uses Sandberg as a signal that Google's monetization crossed from venture dependence into self-sustaining growth. Her mention connects [[DistributionBeforeMonetization]] to the moment when distribution and search usage became financeable through advertising revenue.
