# Muse Image

Muse Image is [[Meta]]'s AI image generator discussed in [[tech-20260710-tech-pod-128-tech-20260710-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says the product can create photorealistic images on [[Instagram]] and [[WhatsApp]], with Muse Video expected later.

The source's privacy concern is [[AIPublicLikenessGeneration]]. [[MariaCurie|Maria Curi]] says a user can prompt the feature with a public username and generate images in that person's likeness if the profile is public, unless the person has opted out; she also says Meta would point to an automatic under-18 off setting.
