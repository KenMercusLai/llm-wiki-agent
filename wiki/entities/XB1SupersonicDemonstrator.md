# XB-1 Supersonic Demonstrator

XB-1 is the [[BoomSupersonic]] prototype aircraft described in [[tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio-tsr-s5-blakescholl-v3-finalaudio]]. The source says Boom built XB-1 after [[YCombinator]] to prove it could build a supersonic jet and to train an organization for the later [[OvertureSupersonicAirliner|Overture]] airliner.

XB-1 is a concrete hard-tech MVP case. It did not itself solve the full commercial-airliner business, certification, or manufacturing problem, but it made technical execution visible, generated flight-test learning, and gave the team a way to build capability that could not simply be hired as a complete off-the-shelf organization.

The episode's emotional and operating peak is XB-1 breaking the sound barrier after earlier subsonic flights. Scholl says the flight-test team made go/no-go decisions away from senior leadership, while the livestream used a chase-plane setup and treated the milestone as both technical proof and a cultural moment for the company.
