# Co-Counsel

Co-Counsel is the legal AI product [[Casetext]] built after [[JakeHeller]] and a co-founder tested an unreleased [[GPT4|GPT-4]] model from [[OpenAI]] in [[tsr-ycoffsite-jakeheller-audioonly-v1final-tsr-ycoffsite-jakeheller-audioonly-v1final]]. The source presents the product as the result of broad workflow testing: legal research, document review, contract drafting, and other customer-requested tasks that earlier GPT-3-era systems could not handle reliably enough.

The product matters because it turns model capability into [[VerticalWorkflowAI]]. Heller's account is not just that a stronger model existed, but that a domain company could translate it into legal work, sales urgency, and a strategic acquisition. After [[ThomsonReuters|Thomson Reuters]] acquired Casetext, Heller says Co-Counsel became prominent enough inside the acquirer that he saw it closely associated with the broader company.
