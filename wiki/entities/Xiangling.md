# 香菱 / Xiangling

[[183-li-shangyin-he-hongloumeng-pianpi-xing-guaizhang-na-guan-shiren-feibang-981580260]] uses 香菱 as an important counterexample to the idea that [[LinDaiyu|林黛玉]]'s stated dislike means [[HongLouMeng|《红楼梦》]] rejects [[LiShangyin|李商隐]]. The episode notes that Xiangling's poetry learning and final moon poem carry Li-Shangyin-like coldness, longing, moon imagery, and frustrated reunion.

Xiangling also supplies direct textual evidence: in the shooting-cover scene, she identifies the Li Shangyin source for "宝钗无日不生尘". For the episode, this shows that Li Shangyin is present in the novel's learned play, not only in modern readers' later associations.
