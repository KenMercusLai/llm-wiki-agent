# 《齐物论》

《齐物论》 is the [[Zhuangzi|庄子]] text invoked in [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] through [[QiSi|《起死》]]. The episode treats its language of equalizing distinctions as powerful in philosophical context but fragile when used to pacify a resurrected poor man who has lost his possessions.

The story also shows the text becoming cultural capital. The police and the "隐士局长" respond differently once they know Zhuangzi is connected to officials and that the director appreciates [[QiWuLun|《齐物论》]] as an upper-class text. The source therefore makes the text part of [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]], not because philosophy is false, but because social users can turn it into status and convenience.
