# The Dagda / 达格达

The Dagda / 达格达 appears in [[142-kaierte-shenhua-gushi-diyidan-deluyi-hui-mengjian-bianxing-yang-ma-865479403]] as a major divine figure in the episode's [[CelticMythology]] creation and genealogy sequence. The host introduces him as a "good god" figure associated with the descendants of [[Danu|达努]] and the mythic line that eventually reaches Ireland.

The page is source-scoped. The episode does not build a full Dagda biography; it uses him to show how Celtic myth begins with generative water, sacred trees, divine descendants, and later settlement history rather than with one standardized pantheon list.
