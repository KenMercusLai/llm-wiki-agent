# Gary Donnelly

Gary Donnelly / 盖雷·唐纳利 is the physicist paired with [[LouiseBanks|Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯]] in [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]]. In [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]], his physics experiments become the route from alien contact into [[FermatsPrincipleOfLeastTime]].

The episode uses Gary's failed and successful communication attempts to show that what humans consider simple is not necessarily universal. Newtonian sequences are awkward for [[Heptapods|heptapods / 七肢桶]], while the light-refraction problem maps better onto their whole-path perception.
