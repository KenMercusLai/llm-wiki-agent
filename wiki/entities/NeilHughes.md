# Neil Hughes

Neil Hughes appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the analyst perspective on Australia's water markets. The source says his team estimated that water trading in the southern basin produced benefits worth about 12% of water rights, or roughly $117 million per year.

Hughes is also used to explain why outside investors can sometimes help [[WaterRightsTrading]] by increasing the chance that buyers and sellers find each other. The episode notes that this view is contested, which keeps the page tied to [[MarketSpeculation]] as a design problem rather than a simple endorsement.
