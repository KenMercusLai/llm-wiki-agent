# Spenser Skates

Spenser Skates is the founder and CEO of [[Amplitude]] in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]]. The episode traces him from Cambridge and [[MIT]] through startup discovery via [[Battlecode]], [[DrewHouston]], [[PaulGraham]] essays, and [[HackerNews]], then into [[Sonalight]] and the pivot that became Amplitude.

His source role is a technical-founder learning arc. He begins as an engineer who wants to build a startup before he has a durable idea, learns from Sonalight that a compelling demo can still fail retention, then turns internal retention analysis into [[ProductAnalytics|product analytics]] after customer conversations show the problem is shared. The later Amplitude story makes him a case in [[SalesAsLearnableSkill]], [[FounderLedSales]], [[DirectListingDiscipline]], [[PublicMarketCommunication]], and [[FounderLifeArchitecture]].
