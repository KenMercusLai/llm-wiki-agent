# 疯投圈

疯投圈 is the podcast/show context for [[132-xuegao-jianghu-fengtouquan-1-132-1]], an episode analyzing China's ice-cream market through brand positioning, channel economics, cold-chain logistics, store-made retail, trial conversion, and franchise expansion. The episode uses an "industry war" framing similar to coffee, tea drinks, and hot pot, but treats ice cream as a less mature and less frequently consumed category in China.

[[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]] adds a hardware-company case through [[DJI]]. The episode extends the show's category-war lens from food retail into drones, portable creator cameras, Chinese hardware globalization, low-altitude regulation, and alumni-led startup spillover.

[[141-kafei-zhanzheng-2026-jigouhua-yu-bentuhua-feng-tou-quan-1-141-1]] returns to consumer chains through China's coffee market. It argues that the coffee war has moved from startup expansion toward [[CoffeeChainInstitutionalization]], [[CoffeeChainLocalization]], [[PremiumEverydayBrandTension]], and [[BeverageCategoryConvergence]] as [[Starbucks]], [[LuckinCoffee]], [[BlueBottleCoffee]], [[MixueBingcheng]], and [[Guming]] face institution-led ownership and category overlap.

[[139-paopao-mate-he-pinduoduo-zhide-touzi-me-feng-tou-quan-1-139-1]] adds a public-market investing-method episode through [[PopMart]] and [[Pinduoduo]]. Rather than recommending stocks, the episode uses [[GoodCompanyGoodStock]], [[EarningsGrowthAcceleration]], [[InvestmentCatalyst]], and [[AICompressedInvestmentResearchAdvantage]] to show how business analysis, valuation, time horizon, catalysts, AI research, and investor temperament interact.
