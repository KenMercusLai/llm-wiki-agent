# 易果生鲜 / Yiguo

Yiguo is the early fresh-grocery ecommerce company in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode uses it to mark the beginning of a long fresh-food platform wave before later front-warehouse, store-warehouse, and community-group-buying models tried to solve the same cold-chain and density problems.
