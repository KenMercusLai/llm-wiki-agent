# Alerta

Alerta is the early company name behind [[EricMigicovsky]]'s pre-[[Pebble]] smartwatch effort in [[tsr-s4-ericm-v2-tsr-s4-ericm-v2]]. The source says the first watch product was called [[ImpulseWatch|Impulse]], worked mainly with [[BlackBerry]], and attracted viral attention as the "BlackBerry watch" before the market shifted toward iPhone.

Alerta matters because it shows Pebble was not an instant Kickstarter idea. Eric had already spent years prototyping, trying to recruit engineers, surviving through pitch competitions, using a Waterloo co-op employee, entering [[YCombinator]], ordering inventory, and learning the gap between press attention and durable [[CustomerPull]].
