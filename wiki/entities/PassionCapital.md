# Passion Capital

Passion Capital appears in [[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] as one of the investors in [[GoCardless]] after its [[YCombinator]] Demo Day. [[TomBlomfield]] says GoCardless raised about $1.5 million from [[Accel]] and Passion Capital before returning to London.

In this source, Passion Capital is mainly a London fintech-funding node rather than a fully developed firm profile. Its role connects GoCardless to the [[FintechRegulatoryWindow]] in the United Kingdom, where payment rails, regulatory openness, and startup capital made fintech company-building more plausible in the 2010s.
