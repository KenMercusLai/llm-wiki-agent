# 《刽子手返乡》 / Home Is the Hangman

《刽子手返乡》 / Home Is the Hangman is the [[RogerZelazny|Zelazny]] story discussed in [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]] as a robot trauma and personhood case. The source describes a detective moving between virtual and real worlds while investigating deaths connected to a former space-exploration robot called the Hangman.

The episode reads the Hangman less as a simple machine threat than as a being shaped by human operators, sensory/emotional connection, a violent formative event, and the burden of returning to Earth after being shut down. Its goal is presented as not merely revenge, but heading toward the stars, which makes the story central to [[RobotHumanBoundary]].
