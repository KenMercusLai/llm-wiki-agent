# CBH Homes

CBH Homes is the Boise, Idaho homebuilder discussed in [[tech-20260101-0101-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260101-0101-mp-tech-pod-128]] through vice president [[RhondaConger]]. Conger says the company thinks of itself as a technology-minded homebuilder and uses AI as part of a broader speed and operations culture.

In the source, CBH Homes uses AI for [[HomebuildingAIOperations]] across sales follow-up, long-term customer communication, data analysis, and warranty support. The strongest operational example is the warranty department, where an AI agent answers routine questions first and gives customers a 24/7 path for urgent after-hours issues.

The source presents CBH's AI posture as growth-oriented rather than replacement-oriented, but that is kept source-scoped. The episode does not independently measure customer satisfaction, escalation quality, cost savings, employee redeployment, or error rates.
