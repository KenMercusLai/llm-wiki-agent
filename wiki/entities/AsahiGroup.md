# Asahi Group / 朝日集团

Asahi Group is the central company case in [[dang-heike-gongpo-le-riben-de-guomin-pijiu-chule-jugong-daoqian-tamen-hai-neng-zuo-shenme-feat-top-of-japan-keji-luandun]]. The episode describes a ransomware incident that disrupted Japanese domestic order handling, shipping, customer service, and factory operations, making the absence of [[SuperDry]] on supermarket shelves a visible sign of backend business-system failure.

The source treats Asahi less as an isolated victim than as a business-continuity case. Once order, inventory, logistics, and ERP systems are unavailable, production capacity alone cannot keep supply moving. The hosts infer that Asahi's refusal to pay ransom likely depended on some recoverable backup position, even if the recovery was slow or operationally painful.
