# Didi

Didi appears in [[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] as one of the Chinese mobility ecosystem partners [[CaoXudong]] names for [[Momenta]]'s Robotaxi route. Cao says Momenta does not plan to run a Robotaxi platform or self-operated fleet in China; it would rather act as an ASG supplier with partners such as Didi, [[AutoNavi]], and [[T3Chuxing]].

In this source, Didi's importance is not as a full company case, but as an example of why [[RobotaxiEconomics]] depends on distribution, operations, and platform access in addition to the autonomous-driving stack.
