# Creo

Creo is the AI-agent company discussed in [[e238-liaoliao-harness-shidai-ai-first-de-zuzhi-jiagou-cong-xinren-ren-dao-xinren-ai-51260de8-60ef-4b76-b3e5-2e559c4a0923]]. The episode presents [[PeterCreo|Peter]] as CTO, [[ChenKaiCreo|陈凯]] as CEO, and [[ClarkCreo|Clark]] as go-to-market co-founder, and uses the company as a case study for [[HarnessEngineering]] and [[AIFirstOrganization|AI-first organization design]].

Creo's product direction, as described in the source, is to let users build their own agents rather than rely on one general agent. Those agents should understand user workflows, improve from repeated use, reduce cost over time, and support self-healing. The target users are mainly SMBs and small teams, which the guests say can adopt AI-first workflows more easily than large legacy organizations.

The episode also uses Creo's internal workflow as evidence for its thesis: the team says most code is AI-written, feature cycles are much shorter than traditional SaaS development, and agents help with testing, bug triage, market research, data queries, and go-to-market output.
