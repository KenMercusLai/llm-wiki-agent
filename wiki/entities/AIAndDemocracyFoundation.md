# AI and Democracy Foundation

The AI and Democracy Foundation appears in [[tech-20260121-0121-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260121-0121-mp-tech-pod-128]] through its CEO and founder [[AvivOvadia]]. In the episode, the foundation's role is to study how society can govern effectively and whether democratic institutions can be made robust against AI-driven information pressure.

The page's wiki role is institutional rather than encyclopedic. The source uses the foundation to connect [[AIInformationPollution]], [[InformationApocalypse]], and [[RealityApathy]] to a response agenda: provenance standards, verification tools, and democratic designs that do not leave every user or journalist to absorb the full [[AIRealityVerificationTax]] alone.
