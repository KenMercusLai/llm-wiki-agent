# Kenneth Stanley

Kenneth Stanley is one of the authors of [[WhyGreatnessCannotBePlanned|《为什么伟大不能被计划》 / Why Greatness Cannot Be Planned]] as discussed in [[139-weishenme-weida-buneng-bei-jihua-youzhizhe-shijingsheng-ma-849965735]]. The episode presents him as an AI scientist and uses that authority to connect evolutionary computation and algorithmic search to a critique of life planning and institutional metrics.

The source keeps Stanley's relevance at the level of method rather than biography. His role in the wiki is to anchor [[ObjectiveDeception]], [[NoveltySearch]], and [[SteppingStoneExploration]] as ideas coming from computational and evolutionary-search examples, not only from motivational advice.
