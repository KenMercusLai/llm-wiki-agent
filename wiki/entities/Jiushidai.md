# 旧世代

旧世代 is the podcast/show context for [[jiu-shidai-diantai-28-shiti-youxi-de-shidai-zhongjie-zhiji-buru-chongxin-dingyi-yongyou-998430652]], an episode about the source-reported end of new [[PlayStation]] physical discs and what that means for player ownership.

In this source, the show combines industry economics with player culture. It treats physical games as a disappearing distribution system, but also as a container for collection, memory, resale, and confidence that digital licensing does not fully replace.
