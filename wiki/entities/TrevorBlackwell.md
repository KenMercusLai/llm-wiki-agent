# Trevor Blackwell

Trevor Blackwell is presented in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]] as a practical programmer, [[Viaweb]] engineer, [[YCombinator]] co-founder, and founder of [[Anybots]]. The episode traces his path from Saskatoon to Harvard, where [[RobertMorris]] introduced him to [[PaulGraham]] as an unusually strong programmer, and then through the early web-commerce, Yahoo, robotics, and YC eras.

Blackwell's role in the source is less public storyteller than behind-the-scenes builder. He built the Viaweb demo in Smalltalk to test browser-based server updates, maintained the hot server room, helped create the bogometer, reformatted early YC applications, wired parts of the first Mountain View office, and kept pursuing human-sized walking robots because the future he had expected from science fiction still had not arrived.
