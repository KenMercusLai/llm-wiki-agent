# 《亨丽埃塔与那场将人类学送上审判席的谋杀案》

《亨丽埃塔与那场将人类学送上审判席的谋杀案》 is the book discussed in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]]. The episode says the authors are [[HenriettaSchmerler|Henrietta Schmerler]]'s nephew and niece, who began investigating the case 58 years after her death and spent about 30 years reconstructing it from family materials, letters, clippings, and evidence.

The book's function in the episode is restorative and institutional. It restores Schmerler as a young scholar and family member, then asks how media, court strategy, reservation politics, and anthropology itself transformed her into a cautionary story. That makes it a source for [[VictimStigmaInTrueCrime]], [[AnthropologyFieldworkSafety]], and [[DisciplinarySelfProtectionAfterHarm]].
