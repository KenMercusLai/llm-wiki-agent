# James Cameron

James Cameron appears in [[129-ruhe-chengwei-zhenzheng-de-buwanmei-zhuyizhe-hunshen-shi-jiner-799220151]] as a source-scoped example for separating excellence from [[PerfectionismAsAvoidance]]. The episode cites him as someone others call a perfectionist, while his own framing is closer to doing what is correct, finishing the work, and moving on.

The page should not generalize from this one mention into a full account of Cameron's filmmaking method. In this wiki branch, his role is narrower: he helps the episode argue that high standards can coexist with [[ImperfectActionPractice]] when they do not prevent completion.
