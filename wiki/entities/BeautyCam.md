# BeautyCam / 美颜相机

BeautyCam is discussed in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as one of [[Meitu]]'s original consumer products. The episode says [[Kaipai]] emerged from a teleprompter-like function inside BeautyCam after product data showed unusual growth.

The source uses BeautyCam as evidence for Meitu's bottom-up product discovery: new AI or productivity tools can emerge from existing user behavior rather than from abstract platform plans. That makes BeautyCam part of the path from consumer utility toward [[VerticalWorkflowAI]].
