# 豪大

豪大 is a guest in [[20-ge-wenti-gao-dong-openclaw-baohong-jizhi-benzhi-bianhua-chuangye-jihui-lk6bzkdxti47vehjvs9sgxotrvto]], discussing [[OpenClaw]] through hands-on experiments in AI products, agent practice, and community bots. His main contribution is explaining why the product can feel like a growing assistant rather than a static tool: users experience memory, personality, IM latency, and autonomous tool use as a relationship they can train.

## Source Position
- His AHA moment came when OpenClaw generated a narrated horror-story audio by calling a TTS tool on its own, which he describes as a feeling of "raising" the agent.
- He emphasizes [[IMAgentInterfaces]] as a psychological design change: waiting for a reply in chat can feel different from waiting for a stuck webpage task.
- He experimented with a community bot that starts from account registration and gradually becomes a recognizable participant, connecting OpenClaw to agent social spaces.
- He argues that [[ClaudeCode]], [[Codex]], and similar agents already had strong capability, while OpenClaw made that capability more accessible and emotionally legible.
- He treats open-source contribution and skill sharing as part of why OpenClaw spread.
