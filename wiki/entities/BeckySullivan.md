# Becky Sullivan

Becky Sullivan appears in [[can-world-cup-mania-grow-mls-in-the-us]] as the [[NPR]] sports correspondent whose World Cup reporting gives the [[PlanetMoney]] episode its club-level scenes. She reports from the [[SeattleSounders]] barge watch party and says she noticed [[MajorLeagueSoccer]] teams scrambling to take advantage of the tournament.

Her role is to make the economic question observable in local settings. Instead of treating the 2026 [[FIFAWorldCup]] only as a governing-body or media-rights event, her reporting follows how MLS clubs try to convert crowds, facilities, and tournament emotion into local league demand.
