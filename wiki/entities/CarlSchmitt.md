# Carl Schmitt

Carl Schmitt appears in [[e233-guigu-youyi-de-quanli-wang-ruhe-xingcheng-liaoliao-bide-tier-de-sixiang-qimeng-pintu-2a529abf-3c14-4ae7-a0a5-fe1340a66c7d]] as a second philosophical influence used to interpret [[PeterThiel]]. The episode summarizes Schmitt through the friend-enemy distinction, anti-liberal political realism, and the theory of exception.

The source connects Schmitt to [[ExceptionStateSurveillanceTechnology]]. In this interpretation, Thiel's response to 9/11 and support for [[Palantir]] are tied to the belief that liberal institutions can fail when they refuse to name enemies or act decisively under crisis.

## Source Position
- The episode explicitly notes Schmitt's Nazi affiliation and treats his ideas as politically dangerous as well as influential.
- It uses Schmitt as an explanatory source for Thiel's post-9/11 security politics, not as a complete account of Palantir or American counterterrorism.
