# University of Cambridge

The University of Cambridge appears in [[tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260318-0318-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the affiliation for [[TomasHoloneck]], the researcher interviewed about [[AIGriefBots]] and post-mortem avatar ethics.

In this source, Cambridge functions as the academic context for turning digital resurrection from a consumer-product story into a research and governance problem involving consent, privacy, dignity, psychological effects, and access to [[DigitalMemorialization]].

[[89-jianqiao-yepanzhe-rang-women-zhuangyan-de-shangfang-jiewa-710382629]] adds a very different Cambridge branch through [[TheNightClimbersOfCambridge|《剑桥夜攀者》]]. There, the university is the architectural and institutional field for [[CampusNightClimbing]], [[CampusPrankEthics]], and [[RoofscapeAttention]]: curfews, locked gates, college towers, roof routes, and the symbolic target of [[KingsCollegeChapel]] make campus order visible by showing how students tested it from above.
