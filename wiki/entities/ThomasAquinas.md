# Thomas Aquinas / 托马斯·阿奎那

Thomas Aquinas / 托马斯·阿奎那 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as a Catholic workers' union envoy who defends God's place against [[BaruchSpinoza|斯宾诺莎]]'s claim that the company is really moved by "NATURE."

The source later uses him during the [[Voltaire|伏尔泰]] and [[JeanJacquesRousseau|卢梭]] sales fight, where he criticizes commercial competition for making people more brutal. Aquinas therefore links the episode's theology jokes to its criticism of workplace competition.
