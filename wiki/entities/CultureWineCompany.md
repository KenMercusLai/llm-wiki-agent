# Culture Wine Company

Culture Wine Company is the importer, distributor, and DTC seller of new-wave South African wines founded by [[PeterAndrews]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. The company works with small family-owned producers using organic farming and minimal-intervention winemaking.

The episode frames Culture Wine as both a company-building and [[CategoryCreation]] case. Peter is not only selling bottles; he is trying to convince U.S. buyers and consumers that South African wine belongs in a premium context. The advice is to focus on a few strategic food-and-wine markets, train distributors and sommeliers deeply, and use restaurant reorder behavior as [[CustomerPull]] and [[LocalMarketProof]].
