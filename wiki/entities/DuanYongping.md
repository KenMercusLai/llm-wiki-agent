# Duan Yongping

Duan Yongping appears in [[ep90-cong-meijiamo-shijiebei-kan-dong-qiquan-huaerjie-de-zhongji-wuqi-lmb62l64uojzsq1uvrr0tj81tg1p]] as a modern investor example for [[OptionSellingDiscipline]]. The episode says he used put selling to enter Pop Mart at a lower effective cost and call selling on [[Nvidia]] to collect premium while accepting sale above a chosen price.

The source uses Duan to connect options back to value-investing intent: the strategy is only coherent when the investor knows the asset, has the balance-sheet capacity to accept assignment, and is genuinely willing to buy or sell at the defined price.
