# Duan Yongping

Duan Yongping appears in [[ep90-cong-meijiamo-shijiebei-kan-dong-qiquan-huaerjie-de-zhongji-wuqi-lmb62l64uojzsq1uvrr0tj81tg1p]] as a modern investor example for [[OptionSellingDiscipline]]. The episode says he used put selling to enter Pop Mart at a lower effective cost and call selling on [[Nvidia]] to collect premium while accepting sale above a chosen price.

The source uses Duan to connect options back to value-investing intent: the strategy is only coherent when the investor knows the asset, has the balance-sheet capacity to accept assignment, and is genuinely willing to buy or sell at the defined price.

[[e44-li-xiaobo-duihua-mengyan-zheci-jiu-zheyang-ba-lpcrvfgnseaed-eambk9ofnvuq2]] adds Duan as the reference point for [[Benfen]], plainness, and doing the right thing without over-performing for others. [[LiXiaobo]] and [[MengYan]] use him to discuss whether founder culture can transmit method-level principles such as ordinary mind, stopping when wrong, and knowing what should be done.

[[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]] adds Duan's consumer-electronics organization branch. The episode connects him from Xiaobawang into [[BBK]], then explains how BBK's split businesses became the roots of [[OPPO]] and [[Vivo]] and their [[OfflineHandsetChannelSystem]].
