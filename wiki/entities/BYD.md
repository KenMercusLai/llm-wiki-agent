# BYD

BYD appears in [[latin-lessons-the-donroe-doctrine-boost-6a4233e26b6690f46aae4e3d]] as the Chinese company behind a Brazil factory cited in the episode's [[LatinAmericaInvestmentBoom]] segment. The source treats the project as part of [[China]]'s response to renewed [[UnitedStates]] activity in the region.

In this source, BYD is not analyzed as an automaker on its own. It is evidence that industrial location, manufacturing capacity, and regional market access can become tools of geopolitical influence in [[LatinAmerica]].
