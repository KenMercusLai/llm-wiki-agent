# PPG

PPG is the men's-shirt vertical ecommerce case in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode describes an asset-light, advertising-driven model that grew quickly but was undermined by supply-chain control, low repeat purchase, ad dependence, and allegations around founder behavior.
