# Adam Rosendorff

[[AdamRosendorff|Adam Rosendorff]] appears in [[tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3-tsr-s4-tylershultzpart2-v3]] as a former [[Theranos]] lab director and one of the higher-level sources [[JohnCarreyrou|John Carreyrou]] had while reporting on the company. [[TylerShultz|Tyler Shultz]] says he could tell from Carreyrou's questions that the reporter knew things Tyler himself did not know, including information about Ian Gibbons's death.

Rosendorff's role matters because the episode frames the Wall Street Journal reporting as corroborated across levels of the company. Tyler could provide data, emails, and first-person lab experience, but Carreyrou's higher-level sources helped show that the problems were not only a young employee's interpretation. In wiki terms, Rosendorff strengthens the link between [[WhistleblowerEvidenceDiscipline]] and [[MedicalTestingValidationIntegrity]].
