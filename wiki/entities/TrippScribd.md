# Tripp (Scribd Co-Founder)

Tripp is the co-founder named in [[tsr-s5-jared-v3final-tsr-s5-jared-v3final]] as [[JaredFriedman]]'s partner through the early pre-smartphone Uber-like idea, [[Wholist]], and then [[Scribd]]. The source says he first contacted Harvard's CS undergraduate list looking for a technical co-founder, which is how he met Jared.

In the episode, Tripp's main role is co-founder persistence. After [[PaulGraham]] rejected the initial idea, Jared and Tripp argued by email and were accepted into [[YCombinator]] once they agreed to change ideas. Later, Tripp convinced Jared to move to San Francisco after multiple failed idea sprints, setting up the brainstorming cycle that produced Scribd.
