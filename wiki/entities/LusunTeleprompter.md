# 芦笋提词器

芦笋提词器 is the creator-tool product branch discussed through [[PrincipleTech]] in [[ep119-duihua-xiao-sun-qixing-800-gongli-ba-ziji-jiuchu-shenyuan-ningyuan-meitian-gongzuo-22-xiaoshi-wo-ye-buxiang-zai-shangban-le-lmj5rmebef8y8p0ayfptqoqnhiu2]]. It sits between screen-recording tools and a later live-stream-assistant idea for teachers and self-media creators.

## Key Points

- XiaoSun presents the product as part of a broader attempt to help independent creators and freelancers work or earn more efficiently.
- Growth constraints included SEO traffic limits, competition, desktop-user ceilings, user-group saturation, and technical iteration cost in an older project.
- The related live-stream assistant created contact with dozens of users but ran into user-connection, technical, and server-cost problems.
