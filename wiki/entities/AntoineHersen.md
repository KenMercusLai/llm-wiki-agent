# Antoine Hersen

Antoine Hersen is a [[PlanGrid]] co-founder discussed in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. [[TracyYoung]] says Antoine was diagnosed with a rare cancer at 27 and kept programming until his last days while the team lived and worked together in a Sunnyvale hacker house.

His role in the wiki is part of PlanGrid's emotional foundation. The source does not treat grief as separate from startup operation: the team kept doing demos, fixing bugs, and fundraising while mourning, and Young says continuing for Antoine motivated the earliest phase of [[PlanGrid]].
