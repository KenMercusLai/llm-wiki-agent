# Youju

Youju is the podcast/project represented by [[Xiaoning]] in [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]]. The episode says Youju first focused on "AI games" and then broadened toward [[AIInteractiveEntertainment]] to better include tools, creator platforms, AI NPCs, companion-like products, social experiences, and interactive video.

## Key Points
- Youju treats "interactive" and "play" as the core boundaries for the field.
- It tracks both production-side AI and product-form changes in entertainment.
- Its framing connects game-industry production constraints with AI-native creator and consumer behavior.
