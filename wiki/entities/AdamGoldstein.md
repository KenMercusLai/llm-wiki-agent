# Adam Goldstein

Adam Goldstein appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as [[SteveHuffman]]'s [[Hipmunk]] co-founder. The episode does not profile Goldstein in depth, but it makes him central to the return-to-Reddit decision: Huffman told him [[Reddit]] wanted him back, and Goldstein asked Huffman not to go until Hipmunk's M&A process was done.

In the wiki, Goldstein's role is mainly to make [[FounderReturnCrisis]] concrete. Huffman's 2015 return was not only a career move; it also disrupted an active second company and a co-founder promise.
