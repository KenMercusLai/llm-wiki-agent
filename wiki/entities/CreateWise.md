# CreateWise

CreateWise is the AI creation startup discussed in [[ep119-duihua-xiao-sun-qixing-800-gongli-ba-ziji-jiuchu-shenyuan-ningyuan-meitian-gongzuo-22-xiaoshi-wo-ye-buxiang-zai-shangban-le-lmj5rmebef8y8p0ayfptqoqnhiu2]]. [[Minghao]]'s podcast-production experience gave the team an initial creator pain point, while [[XiaoSun]] describes the project as aiming beyond podcasts toward a broader AI creation product.

## Key Points

- The team built a hard-coded demo for an Amazon-related competition after only about a week of work and received an award.
- In a later ByteDance competition, XiaoSun spent nights building functions and the team won the day's championship and prize money.
- The project eventually had a user group of more than one hundred people and had implemented login, registration, Stripe payment, and other product loops.
- Product Hunt attention, including a number-one ranking, did not remove the team's commercial pressure because the product still lacked enough revenue for XiaoSun's personal runway.
- The case shows that [[VibeCoding]], [[AIHackathons]], and public launch momentum can create speed and morale before durable [[CustomerPull]] and [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]] are proven.
