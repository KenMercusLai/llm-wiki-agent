# 《哈尔罗杰历险记》

《哈尔罗杰历险记》 is the science-adventure series [[Beimin|北明]] recalls in [[177-xiaoshihou-de-shu-zenme-jiu-name-haokan-971996036]]. The episode presents it as a childhood reading case where adventure, natural history, travel, and explanation work together: two brothers move through oceans, mountains, deserts, rainforests, and animal worlds while the story keeps asking how unfamiliar phenomena actually work.

The source links the series to Willard Price's travel, National Geographic-style field knowledge, and the readerly pleasure of seeing mystery become investigation. Its wiki role is therefore not a full bibliography of the series, but a representative node for [[ChildhoodScienceReading]].

## Key Claims
- The series made science attractive by attaching explanation to danger, geography, animals, and sibling adventure.
- The episode contrasts evidence-based explanations, such as animal behavior and "wild man" footprints, with mere mystery.
- Its importance is partly atmospheric: it gave a child the feeling that the world was wide, knowable, and worth exploring.
