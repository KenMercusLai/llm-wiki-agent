# 钱苍水

钱苍水 is the author of [[ZhonghuaXieshi|《中华蟹史》]] as introduced in [[zhongqiu-kuaile-669967741]]. The source identifies him as from Taicang, Jiangsu, and treats him source-locally as a crab-history compiler whose work gathers food practice, literary references, folk material, place-name clues, and old records of fear or unfamiliarity around crabs.

The page should be read as source-scoped rather than as a complete biography. His role in the wiki is to anchor the episode's [[FestivalFoodMaterialHistory]] branch: a local and presumably crab-loving author makes an everyday seasonal food available as historical evidence.

## Key Claims
- The episode values Qian less as a biographical subject than as the person who collected a broad crab archive.
- His Taicang/Jiangsu association matters because the source's crab discussion depends on watery local ecologies, regional eating habits, and place-name memory.
- The book's range lets crab culture appear through eating, literature, folklore, and material history at once.
