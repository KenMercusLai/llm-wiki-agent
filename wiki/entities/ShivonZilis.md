# Shivon Zilis

Shivon Zilis appears in [[tsr-s4-samaltman-v4-tsr-s4-samaltman-v4]] as one of the former [[OpenAI]] board members [[SamAltman]] says might have prevented the November 2023 [[OpenAIBoardCrisis]] if she had remained on the board.

The episode does not describe her board service in depth. It uses her departure, together with [[ReidHoffman]] and [[WillHurd]], to make a governance point about board continuity and composition in mission-sensitive AI companies.
