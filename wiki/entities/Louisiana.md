# Louisiana

Louisiana appears in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]] as the planned location for a larger [[HeirloomCarbonTechnologies|Heirloom Carbon Technologies]] [[DirectAirCapture]] project. The episode says the project could initially remove up to 200,000 tons of CO2 per year, though the timeline was uncertain when [[AmyScott]] visited Heirloom.

The state also matters politically. [[AaronBurns]] of [[Carbon180]] says [[UnitedStatesCongress|Congress]] protected the planned Louisiana hub even as the Trump administration rescinded funding for other Biden-era carbon-removal projects, making Louisiana a case in [[CarbonRemovalPoliticalDurability]].
