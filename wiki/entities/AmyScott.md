# Amy Scott

Amy Scott is the Marketplace reporter in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]]. Her visit to [[HeirloomCarbonTechnologies|Heirloom Carbon Technologies]] gives the episode its concrete [[DirectAirCapture]] explainer: the listener sees limestone, kiln heating, CO2 separation, and repeated atmospheric absorption rather than only hearing a climate-policy abstraction.

Scott's reporting also links the technical demonstration to public funding and legitimacy questions. The segment follows from Heirloom's [[California]] facility to its planned [[Louisiana]] project, then to [[Carbon180]]'s explanation of why some carbon-removal hubs survived federal funding cuts.
