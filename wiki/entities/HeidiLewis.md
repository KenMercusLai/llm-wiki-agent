# Heidi Lewis

Heidi Lewis appears in [[tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260112-0112-mp-tech-pod-128]] as an Altadena neighbor rebuilding with [[ConcreteFilledWallSystems]]. The episode describes her exterior-wall approach as lightweight blocks or panels snapped into position before concrete is poured between them, making the house effectively concrete-centered for fire resistance.

Her role is to give the source a contrasting path beside [[DavidBrancaccio]]'s [[CrossLaminatedTimber]] approach. Lewis's house is framed as highly fire resistant but materially and visually different from the older storybook cottages that shaped the neighborhood memory, which connects her case to [[FireResilientConstruction]], [[SustainableConstructionCarbonTradeoff]], and [[BiophilicDesign]].
