# Chengmai Technology

Chengmai Technology is the Nanjing software and adaptation company discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode links it to [[WangJiping]] and says its Wuhan assets were combined with [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]]-related assets to form [[TongxinSoftware]].

## Source Position
- Chengmai is presented as a mobile software, hardware adaptation, and technical-services company before the Tongxin combination.
- Its role in the episode is to connect Deepin's community/project lineage with a listed-company and enterprise-delivery context.
- The source treats the combination as a key step toward [[TongxinUOS]] and [[XinchuangOperatingSystems]].
