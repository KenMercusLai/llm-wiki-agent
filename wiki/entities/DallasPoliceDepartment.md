# Dallas Police Department

Dallas Police Department appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the data setting for [[MatthewRoss]]'s research on field training. The source says Ross used Dallas Police Department data to study how aggressive field training officers affected later use-of-force patterns among recruits.

The department is not discussed as a standalone institution. Its significance is evidentiary: it provides the source's empirical bridge from local policing research to questions about [[USImmigrationAndCustomsEnforcement|ICE]] training, supervision, and field culture.
