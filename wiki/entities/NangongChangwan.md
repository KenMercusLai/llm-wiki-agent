# 南宫长万

南宫长万 is the Song-state strongman whom [[109-xianliao-zuozhuan-zhi-chunqiu-yundonghui-747508773]] nominates for long-distance running and weighted cross-country in its [[SportsAsHistoricalReadingFrame|Spring-Autumn sports-meet]] frame. The episode recounts his capture by Lu, his return to Song, and the humiliation of being called a "Lu prisoner," which becomes the emotional prelude to his killing of Song Mingong.

His athletic role comes from the flight story: after the regicide and failed internal power move, he flees from Song to Chen with his mother, a distance the episode presents as roughly 260 li in a day. The story then turns grotesque and legendary: Chen accepts Song bribes, drugs him, binds him in rhinoceros hide, and the episode notes the tradition that he wakes and bursts the binding before being killed.
