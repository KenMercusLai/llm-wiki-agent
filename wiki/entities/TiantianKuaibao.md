# Tiantian Kuaibao

Tiantian Kuaibao appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as the [[Tencent]] information-feed product the hosts describe as once being closest to [[JinriToutiao]]. The episode cites its high daily active user peak and a January 2017 comparison where Tencent News, Jinri Toutiao, and Tiantian Kuaibao all had meaningful scale.

The source's strategic claim is that Tiantian Kuaibao missed the recommendation window because of organization adjustment, algorithm-team changes, and debates over content tone. It becomes the counterfactual case for how Tencent might have remained stronger in information feeds if it had sustained recommendation investment.
