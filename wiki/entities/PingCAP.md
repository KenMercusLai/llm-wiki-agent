# PingCAP

PingCAP is the open-source database company discussed in [[guanyu-ai-kaiyuan-shangyehua-yu-quanqiuhua-de-jingyan-jiaoxun-he-fangfalun-duitan-pingcap-cto-dongxu-ljw8va0evobhz4ojzrulqzjvxw5]]. [[Dongxu]] describes the company as founded in 2015 around [[TiDB]], a distributed relational database, with early choices around open source, transaction-database ambition, globalization, and later cloud service.

In the source, PingCAP's company-building lesson is that infrastructure software can create value before revenue when users adopt it deeply, contribute engineering capacity, and trust the project's process. Its later shift toward cloud service is framed as a way to commercialize without undermining open-source trust; Dongxu says cloud now contributes more than 70% of ARR in the episode's account.
