# 胡戈 / Hu Ge

Hu Ge appears in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]] through the viral spoof-video case "A Murder Caused by a Bun." The episode uses him as one of the clearest examples of early Chinese online-video humor and remix culture.

For the wiki, Hu Ge is a marker of [[UGCToProfessionalCreatorPipeline]] and early [[ContentEcosystemGovernance]] questions: platforms distributed user creativity, but copyright, parody, and professional media boundaries were still unsettled.
