# Jesse Rothstein

Jesse Rothstein appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through research cited with [[DavidCard]] and [[MoisesYee]] on industry wage premiums. The episode uses the finding to compare manufacturing with restaurants, retail, finance and insurance, utilities, and mining/oil/gas.

His role is to support the episode's more precise wage claim. Manufacturing is shown as meaningfully better paid than some sectors, but the cited comparison also keeps it from becoming a mythic best-job category.
