# Bill Bishop

Bill Bishop appears in [[tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov-tsr-ycoffsite-chrisbest-audioonly-v1final-mov]] as [[Substack]]'s first real user. He wrote [[Sinocism]], a China-focused newsletter that already had an audience and demand before Substack supplied the connected publishing, email, and payment infrastructure.

In [[ChrisBest]]'s account, Bishop's launch produced about $100,000 on the first day and changed the [[YCombinator]] interview from a zero-revenue application into a live demand signal. Best also says Bishop remained Substack's biggest customer for years, which made the case powerful but not automatically repeatable.
