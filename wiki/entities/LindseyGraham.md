# Lindsey Graham

Lindsey Graham is the U.S. senator from South Carolina profiled in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]]. The episode uses his sudden death at 71 to read a longer institutional story: Graham moved from the older bipartisan, hawkish Republican establishment into a [[DonaldTrump]]-aligned party while trying to preserve influence over foreign policy.

The source describes Graham as a military man and lawyer who served four House terms before entering the Senate in 2003. His short service stints in Afghanistan and Iraq are used to explain his belief that America should intervene early abroad rather than let enemies become stronger.

The episode's central political contrast is Graham's move from Trump critic to Trump ally. He initially warned that Trump was bad for the [[RepublicanParty]] and America, then became one of Trump's strongest supporters after Trump proved electorally dominant. That makes Graham a concrete case of [[RepublicanForeignPolicyAdaptation]]: access to power reshaped public posture even while his hawkish instincts remained.
