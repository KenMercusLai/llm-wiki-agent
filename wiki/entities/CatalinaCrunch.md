# Catalina Crunch

Catalina Crunch is the low-sugar, high-protein cereal and snack company founded by [[KrishnaKaliannan]] and discussed in [[catalina-crunch-krishna-kaliannan-from-homemade-keto-cocoa-puffs-to-breakfast-aisle-breakthrough-5aa86015-e7c2-448e-a84e-d1b34d2fcac2]]. The episode presents the company as a personal-health-driven CPG case: Krishna's type 1 diabetes, epilepsy, and ketogenic diet created the product insight, while scale required [[CPGManufacturingScaleUp]], [[PackagingAsProductExperience]], [[CPGDistribution]], [[DirectToConsumerCashFlow]], and retail repositioning around keto.

## Key Points
- The first product was a low-sugar cereal designed to deliver childhood cereal taste and crunch without the carbohydrate and blood-sugar profile of mainstream cereal.
- Early demand came from direct online sales, word of mouth, social media, and a type 1 diabetes Facebook group.
- Scaling moved from apartment production to a commercial kitchen, then co-manufacturing, then a self-run Indiana coating and packaging operation.
- The [[WholeFoodsMarket]] launch around January 2020 became a major retail breakout after the product claim shifted from low sugar to keto-friendly.
- The company later expanded into snack mixes, cookie bars, and cookies after customers treated the cereal as a snack.
- [[DougBehrens]] became CEO in 2024, allowing Krishna to focus more on nutrition strategy.
