# Patrick Burns

Patrick Burns appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] as the U.S. Navy figure who disclosed an internal investigation connected to [[KaraHultgreen|Kara Hultgreen]]'s fatal F-14 accident. The episode describes him as supportive of gender integration and equality while still willing to risk his military career to surface a more complicated account of the accident.

The source uses Burns as a [[PoliticalTruthTelling|political truth-telling]] case where facts are uncomfortable partly because they are easy for hostile audiences to weaponize. His example extends the wiki's whistleblower branch beyond corporate fraud into identity-protective avoidance and the cost of preserving a difficult public record.
