# Princess Aiko

Princess Aiko appears in [[missing-peace-will-israel-imperil-iran-deal-6a3bae9fe1238e54b0e886c3]] as [[EmperorNaruhito]]'s only child and the public face of [[JapaneseImperialSuccession]] pressure. The episode says she is highly popular but cannot inherit because succession is limited to men in the male line.

The source treats Aiko's popularity as a legitimacy problem for the existing rule. When public support for a female emperor is reported at around 90%, barring Aiko makes the [[JapaneseImperialFamily]] look increasingly misaligned with social opinion.
