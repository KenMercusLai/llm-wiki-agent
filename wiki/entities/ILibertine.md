# I, Libertine

I, Libertine appears in [[how-to-make-a-book-into-a-bestseller]] as the fake book that [[GeneShepard]] asked radio listeners to request from bookstores. The source says the prank became large enough that a publisher eventually helped turn the invented title into a real published book.

The episode uses I, Libertine as an early case of [[BestsellerStatusFeedbackLoop]]. The lesson is not that sales were honestly measured, but that visible or rumored demand can recruit stores, readers, publishers, and media into creating the product they thought already existed.
