# 郑游

郑游 is a figure from [[DaChangXiaomin]] discussed by [[XiaoMan]] in [[da-chang-xiao-min-women-bixu-kezhi-dui-xitong-yu-shangan-de-qidai-lps-6oqp9ljyfw6wpfkutcks6dgf]]. He starts as an outsourced worker, actively learns how to manage supervisor expectations, asks how to get good performance, arrives early, takes on tasks from full-time employees, and eventually passes the process to become a formal employee.

His story is not treated as simple meritocracy. The episode uses his relief after gaining full internal access to show how workplace inequality can be built into cards, apps, internal networks, facilities, information, and status. ZhengYou therefore extends [[WorkplaceHiddenRules]], [[BigCompanyHalo]], and [[SystemHumanity]] by showing how an ordinary worker can both suffer from and skillfully navigate institutional gates.
