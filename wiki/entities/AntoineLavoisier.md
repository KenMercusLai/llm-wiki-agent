# Antoine Lavoisier / 拉瓦锡

Antoine Lavoisier / 拉瓦锡 appears in [[125-mala-zhisi-beibi-de-ansha-huo-zhengyi-de-chujue-783972516]] as the scientific figure whose conflict with [[JeanPaulMarat|Marat]] exposes the episode's anti-expert and anti-institution thread. The source stresses that the common claim "Marat killed Lavoisier" is not literally accurate because Marat died first, but it still assigns Marat indirect responsibility for years of attacks.

The episode presents Lavoisier as a public scientist, official, and reformer whose roles became dangerous labels during the [[FrenchRevolution|French Revolution]]. His association with the Academy, taxation, gunpowder administration, and elite science made him vulnerable when revolutionary suspicion turned against learned institutions.

The page anchors [[ScienceUnderRevolutionarySuspicion]]: revolutionary equality and anti-privilege language can expose real abuses, but it can also turn scientific expertise into evidence of elite conspiracy.
