# Franz Boas / 博尔斯

Franz Boas appears in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]] as the senior [[ColumbiaUniversity]] anthropology figure who supported [[HenriettaSchmerler]]'s independent fieldwork. The episode says he provided 500 dollars and recommended the [[WhiteMountainApache|White Mountain Apache]] field site.

The source later places Boas inside the case's moral and disciplinary ambiguity. It says Boas and [[RuthBenedict]] resisted a simple Christian-civilizational punishment frame for [[GurneySeymour]], while the episode asks when cultural respect becomes a way to soften accountability for sexual violence. His page therefore connects to [[CulturalRelativismVictimProtectionBoundary]] as much as to fieldwork training.
