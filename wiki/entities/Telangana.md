# Telangana

Telangana appears in [[peace-fire-further-us-iran-strikes-6a4f7acffe878dc8e27e6fbf]] as the Indian state whose law allows up to 15% of an adult child's salary to be transferred to neglected parents. The episode uses it as the opening case for Asian [[FilialPietyLaws]].

The Telangana case is important because it makes family duty directly financial. In the source's frame, this is one response to [[ElderCareStateCapacity]] gaps, but it also raises the question of whether wage garnishment solves care needs or merely shifts state responsibility onto younger adults.
