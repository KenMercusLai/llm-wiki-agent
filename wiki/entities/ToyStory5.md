# Toy Story 5

*Toy Story 5* appears in [[starmergeddon-british-pm-resigns-6a390b674a8189f2c3b4e6f1]] through a cultural review. The episode presents the film as a timely sequel in which Bonnie's tablet displaces imaginative play and deepens loneliness.

The source's interpretation makes the film a [[ScreenTimeParenting]] case. Its sharpest critique is aimed less at the child or the device than at distracted parents who set rules, fail to enforce them, and miss signs that their child is unhappy.
