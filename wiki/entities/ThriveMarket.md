# Thrive Market

Thrive Market is the marketplace channel mentioned in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]] for [[SprinkleBites]]. Rachel says the company launched on Thrive in December, received a reorder within 30 days, and moved 88-pack boxes weekly for 13 straight weeks.

In the episode, Thrive functions as an early proof point rather than just a sales channel: its reorder and weekly movement give Sprinkle Bites evidence for [[CustomerPull]], [[SalesVelocity]], and category interest before the company decides whether private label or partnerships make sense.

## Connections
- [[SprinkleBites]] - brand sold through the channel.
- [[CustomerPull]], [[SalesVelocity]], [[CPGDistribution]], and [[ProofPointReuse]] - concepts illustrated by the reorder signal.
