# Tapio

Tapio was a LinkedIn-focused SaaS product created by [[ThibautLouisLucas]] and his team when [[TweetHunter]] was already around $100,000 MRR. The product extended the social-growth playbook to LinkedIn while remaining separate from the Twitter product.

## Key Claims
- Tapio was kept separate from [[TweetHunter]] so each product could focus deeply on one social network and because [[JKMolina]]'s Tweet Hunter deal did not apply to LinkedIn revenue.
- Lucas looked for a LinkedIn influencer partner and found [[AlexBerman]] to replicate the distribution strategy used with Tweet Hunter.
- Tapio was acquired with Tweet Hunter by [[Lempire]], in a deal that ultimately paid about $8 million after earnout milestones.
