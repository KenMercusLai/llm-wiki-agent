# Carbon180

Carbon180 is the carbon-removal policy organization represented by [[AaronBurns]] in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]]. The episode uses Burns to explain why federal support for [[DirectAirCapture]] did not simply disappear under the Trump administration: some projects lost Biden-era funding, while [[UnitedStatesCongress|Congress]] protected hubs in [[Louisiana]] and [[Texas]].

Carbon180's wiki role is the policy and coalition layer around [[CarbonRemoval]]. Burns argues that support remains bipartisan in some host states, which makes the organization useful for connecting direct-air-capture technology to [[CarbonRemovalPoliticalDurability]] rather than only to chemistry or startup economics.
