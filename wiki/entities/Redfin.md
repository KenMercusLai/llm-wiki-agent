# Redfin

Redfin appears in [[live-anthropic-co-founder-on-ai-and-jobs]] as the company context for [[DarylFairweather]], who is introduced as its chief economist. The source does not analyze Redfin's business model directly; it uses Fairweather's role to show how company-employed economists can translate housing data, behavioral incentives, and public-policy debates for broad audiences.

In the episode, Redfin's relevance is mainly the housing-market lens. Fairweather uses the interview to argue that affordability depends on adding supply, eliminating single-family-zoning constraints, and thinking beyond current homeowners toward future residents.
