# Electric Air

Electric Air appears in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]] as a [[LeapForward]] portfolio company formerly described as Helios in the episode. [[DavidRusenko]] says the company aims to build a national-scale residential HVAC contracting company and install heat pumps for about 50 percent less than other options.

The source uses Electric Air to show [[EconomicClimateTechAdoption]] in a household market. Heat pumps reduce emissions only if customers can buy, install, and maintain them at acceptable cost and quality, so the startup problem includes service operations, labor, installation workflow, and local trust rather than only hardware.
