# 领航者号 Recovery Ship

领航者号 is the sea recovery platform discussed in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]]. The episode describes it as a large recovery ship carrying a roughly 54 m by 54 m tension-net frame for catching the returning [[LongMarch10B]] first stage.

The ship matters because [[SeaNetRocketRecovery]] makes the recovery platform part of the rocket system. The source says the ship must handle sea-state motion, maintain position with a DP2 dynamic positioning system, communicate with the rocket at millisecond scale, and let the flexible net absorb the vehicle's residual energy without imposing a destructive rigid shock.

- [[LongMarch10B]] — vehicle caught by the ship's net in the episode.
