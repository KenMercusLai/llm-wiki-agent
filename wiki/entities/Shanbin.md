# Shanbin

Shanbin is Xiaohongshu's community technology lead and the event lead interviewed in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. He explains the [[XiaohongshuHackathonPeakCompetition]] as an attempt to find people who are truly creating under the AI wave, not simply teams reacting to a trend.

His most reusable idea is "era age": in the AI period, youth is partly about how early someone enters the new tool wave, how sensitive they are to it, and whether they act. This shifts the event from a conventional competition into a search for emerging builder identity.
