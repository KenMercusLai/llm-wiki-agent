# FTX

FTX appears in [[socialradarspod-brianarmstrong-final]] as the collapsed crypto exchange whose failure harmed the broader industry's reputation. [[BrianArmstrong]] says FTX gave crypto a "black eye" and intensified skepticism that already existed around scams, hype cycles, and speculative run-ups.

The source uses FTX as a trust contrast with [[Coinbase]]. Armstrong says that if an exchange holds customer funds one-to-one, it should not face a traditional bank-run dynamic. The FTX collapse therefore becomes a case where custody, reserves, governance, spending, and credibility signals matter inside [[CryptocurrencyMarketStructure]].
