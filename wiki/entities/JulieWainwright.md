# Julie Wainwright

Julie Wainwright is presented in [[seven-allegedly-fake-chanel-bags-vs-the-realreal]] as the founder who launched [[TheRealReal]] in 2011. The source says the idea came from seeing unused luxury goods sitting in closets and storage while buyers remained wary of fakes on looser marketplaces.

Her role in the wiki is to connect founder opportunity recognition to [[AsymmetricInformation]]. The supply existed, but a durable marketplace needed authentication, operations, and customer confidence rather than only listings.

## Key Claims
- Wainwright's founding insight was that idle luxury inventory could become marketplace supply if buyer trust was solved.
- The RealReal's early differentiation came from making authentication part of the business model.
- Her case extends [[MarketplaceFrictionReduction]] because the platform reduced the effort and risk buyers faced when evaluating used luxury goods.
