# The Indicator

The Indicator appears in [[indicators-of-2025-and-what-to-watch-in-2026]] as the short-form economics show paired with [[PlanetMoney]] for a year-end crossover. The source uses The Indicator's format for fast economic storytelling: each participant selects a statistic that can stand for a larger economic pattern.

In this episode, the show context matters because the indicators are not treated as full forecasts. [[ConsumerSentimentIndicator]], [[EffectiveTariffRateShock]], [[CAPERatioValuationSignal]], [[FederalFundsRateAsPolicySignal]], [[ElectricityAffordabilityIndicator]], and [[KShapedConsumerSpending]] are all used as compact entry points into broader debates about mood, trade policy, valuation, Fed independence, affordability, and inequality.
