# Seattle Sounders

The Seattle Sounders appear in [[can-world-cup-mania-grow-mls-in-the-us]] through [[BeckySullivan]]'s reporting from an official World Cup watch party on a commercial shipping-container barge. The barge had been turned into a beer-garden-style soccer space with turf, families, a huge screen, and loud audio.

For the source, the barge matters less as a venue novelty than as a concrete example of [[SportsFandomNetworkEffects]]. When Cape Verde scores its first World Cup goal against Uruguay, the crowd scene shows the kind of shared emotion [[MajorLeagueSoccer]] clubs hope can attach World Cup energy to local soccer communities.
