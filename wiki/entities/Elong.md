# Elong

Elong is one of the online-travel competitors discussed in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The episode says it was founded by Tang Yue and at one point received investment from [[ExpediaGroup]].

The source uses Elong alongside [[Qunar]] and [[TongchengTravel]] to explain how different online-travel paths eventually entered a capital-consolidation environment around [[Ctrip]].
