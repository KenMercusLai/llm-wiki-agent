# invoko.ai / Invoqo

invoko.ai, also referred to as Invoqo in the source, is [[Mengqi]]'s AI software startup in [[yige-ai-chuangshiren-de-xurongxin-zhuang-he-yumei-zhidian-duitan-invoko-ai-chuangshiren-mengqi-lsi79o-z19zplvmqdbpzzneogpk3f]]. The company first explored vertical Agent directions such as Sourcing Agent and growth Agent, then reduced emphasis on 2B work and shifted toward consumer productivity products including [[Clico]].

In the wiki, the company is a case for the gap between AI startup narrative and operating reality. Its path connects [[FounderEgo]], [[VerticalAgentSaaSification]], [[AICommercializationPressure]], [[AIStartupUnitEconomics]], and [[AIApplicationLayerMoat]]: strong model progress and hot concepts can help fundraising, but product value still depends on user workflow, stability, distribution, payment, and maintenance.
