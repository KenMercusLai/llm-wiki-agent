# Vladimir Putin

Vladimir Putin appears in [[far-crimea-war-comes-to-russias-door-6a3e560c26d5a6687a90c658]] as the Russian leader whose war narrative is tied to [[Crimea]]. The episode treats Crimea as militarily useful but also symbolically central to Putin's account of Russian history and identity.

The source's political claim is that Putin may face less a capacity problem than an exit problem. Ukrainian strikes can hurt infrastructure, fuel, logistics, and public confidence, but the episode says Russia can still finance the war; the harder question is how Putin could end it without making the result look like defeat.
