# 板井太子

板井太子 is presented in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the young woman founder behind [[AnneSanitaryNapkin|安妮卫生巾]]. The episode says she moved from a household invention service toward sanitary pads after recognizing that the real problem was not a toilet accessory but the continuing use of absorbent cotton by millions of menstruating women.

Her role gives the source its clearest [[FemaleSelfPossession]] and product-design case. Because she had used [[Kotex]] and understood Japanese women's fit, comfort, smell, disposal, body-line, and embarrassment constraints, the company built trial feedback, multiple sizes, quiet packaging, portable wrappers, and bright brand language into the product.
