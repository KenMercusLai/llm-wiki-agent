# Instafoods

Instafoods is the Philadelphia Chinatown restaurant in [[chef-vs-robot]] where [[KennyPoon]] operates [[RobbyWokbot]]. The episode uses the restaurant as the source's concrete test site for [[RestaurantAutomation]] rather than treating robot chefs as an abstract labor-market forecast.

Its role in the wiki is to make automation operationally specific. The source shows the machine giving ingredient prompts, cooking with electricity, handling stirring and washing, and changing hiring requirements, while still depending on a human operator to load ingredients.
