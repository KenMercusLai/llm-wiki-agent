# Meipai / 美拍

Meipai is referenced in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as an earlier [[Meitu]] attempt to move from tool utility into a video community or platform. The episode uses it as an analogy for [[Sora]]'s platform ambition: a tool can try to become a social surface, but timing, content ecology, product maturity, and competitive context all matter.

In the source, Meipai helps explain why Meitu later became more comfortable with tool confidence. Rather than competing with stronger platform companies on recommendation and social operations, [[Meitu]] is portrayed as returning to workflow, visual detail, and [[AIApplicationLayerMoat]].
