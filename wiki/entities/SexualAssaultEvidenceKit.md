# Sexual Assault Evidence Kit

The sexual assault evidence kit appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] through [[MarthaGoddard|Martha Goddard]]'s 1970s Chicago work. The source describes it as a tool designed to make sexual-assault evidence collection more reliable and usable across medical, police, and legal settings.

In the episode, the kit matters because it turns [[PrincipledDisobedience|principled disobedience]] into infrastructure. Goddard's challenge was not only to say institutions were failing survivors, but to create a repeatable object and adoption path that could change how those institutions handled evidence.
