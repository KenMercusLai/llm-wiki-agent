# 刘诚

刘诚 is the [[EurekaTherapeutics]] founder and CEO interviewed in [[e235-20-nian-nei-car-t-zhiyu-aizheng-yu-liucheng-boshi-liaoliao-aizheng-zhiliao-de-diceng-zhexue-90f96f60-25be-45ac-b832-56776a23d534]]. The source presents him as a long-time [[CARTCellTherapy|CAR-T]] operator whose company began working on CAR-T in 2014 and focuses on late-stage solid tumors.

His main contribution to the wiki is a treatment-philosophy frame: cancer is difficult because it emerges from a body's own cells, so therapies must solve the [[CancerImmuneRecognitionProblem]] without simply damaging normal tissue. Liu treats CAR-T as a powerful route because it modifies immune cells to recognize and attack cancer, while still acknowledging cost, safety, [[SolidTumorCARTConstraints|solid-tumor barriers]], and regulatory risk.

## Key Claims
- Liu distinguishes [[CARTCellTherapy|CAR-T]] from ordinary drugs by emphasizing that it is a living cell therapy using engineered immune cells.
- He argues that [[InVivoCART]] could solve much of the [[ExVivoCARTManufacturing]] cost and time burden, but only if specificity and dose control become clinically reliable.
- He is skeptical that [[AllogeneicCART]] can match autologous CAR-T because donor cells may be rejected and may not persist long enough.
- His 20-year cancer-cure forecast is defined as most people no longer dying from cancer or being unable to live normally with it, not as immediate universal eradication.
