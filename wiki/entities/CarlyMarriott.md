# Carly Marriott

Carly Marriott appears in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]] as the farmer perspective on Australia's water market. Her family farms sheep and crops in [[NewSouthWales]], using irrigation connected to the [[MurrayRiver]].

The episode uses Marriott's story to show the distributional pain behind [[WaterMarketDesign]]. She says water prices rose sharply in 2019, and her family helped organize a convoy to Canberra to protest the market's effects on farmers.
