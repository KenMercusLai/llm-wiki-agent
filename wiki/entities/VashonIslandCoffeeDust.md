# Vashon Island Coffee Dust

Vashon Island Coffee Dust is the spice-blend CPG company founded by [[ChristyClement]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-christina-tosi-of-milk-bar-fd7f2406-fda8-4c1d-936c-d20210215f96]]. The blends are used to flavor coffee without sweeteners, with ingredients such as cinnamon, cardamom, cayenne, cocoa, and sea salt. The company sells through its website, in-person markets, Etsy, wholesale, independent shops, coffee shops, and Amazon.

The episode reports that the business had about $277,000 in revenue, had been profitable from day one, and had been doubling revenue. Its strategic question is how to turn gift discovery into loyal repeat buying. [[ChristinaTosi]] advises improving packaging, bundling with a frother, making the product visible on the coffee counter, and expanding use cases into tea, whipped cream, fruit, and savory rubs; [[GuyRaz]] adds that daily-use friction matters and suggests thinking toward a K-cup-like convenience path.
