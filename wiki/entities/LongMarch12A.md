# Long March 12A / 长征十二号甲

Long March 12A is cited in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]] as another Chinese reusable-rocket comparison case. The episode says its first flight reached orbit successfully, but the first-stage terminal landing segment had an anomaly and recovery failed.

The page's relevance is comparative: [[JerryTaikongSeng]] uses Long March 12A together with [[Zhuque3]] to show that reusable-rocket progress often fails in the final ignition, guidance, or touchdown phase even after earlier ascent and return milestones look strong.
