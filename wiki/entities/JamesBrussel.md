# James Brussel

James Brussel is the psychiatrist discussed in [[121-jijian-fayi-jianshi-shouce-yiqie-fanzui-jieyou-henji-775064343]] through the "Mad Bomber" case. The episode presents his profile as strikingly accurate on the suspect's age, ethnicity, living situation, work habits, and clothing, while still placing profiling inside a broader evidence system.

In the wiki, Brussel grounds [[CriminalProfilingAsProbabilisticInference]]. Profiling can help investigators form hypotheses, but the source's larger forensic frame means it should point search and questioning rather than replace physical evidence, corroboration, and procedure.
