# Jordan Bardella

Jordan Bardella appears in [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]] as the [[NationalRally]] figure who had prepared to run for president if [[MarineLePen]] remained barred from office. The episode says Le Pen's return puts him back into second place.

Bardella's role matters because his positioning is not identical to Le Pen's. The source says he had tried to broaden National Rally's appeal toward more middle-class voters with a business-friendly and fiscally responsible programme, while Le Pen retains a stronger working-class and social-protection pitch.
