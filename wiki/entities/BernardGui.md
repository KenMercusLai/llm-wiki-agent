# 贝尔纳 / Bernard Gui

贝尔纳 / Bernard Gui is the inquisitor figure discussed in [[54-meigui-de-mingzi-xia-zhen-yu-jia-zheng-yu-xie-quanshi-yu-guodu-quanshi-638214699]] as part of the [[AvignonPapacy|阿维尼翁教廷 / Avignon Papacy]] delegation in [[TheNameOfTheRose|《玫瑰的名字》 / The Name of the Rose]]. The episode contrasts him with [[WilliamOfBaskerville|巴斯克维尔的威廉 / William of Baskerville]]: William tries to find out what happened, while Bernard uses accusation and confession to produce a politically useful result.

Bernard's arrest of Salvatore and the unnamed village girl, then the pressured confession of Remigio, turns investigation into [[PoliticalShowTrial]]. The episode stresses that he is not primarily solving the murders; he is creating evidence that can be used in the next day's religious and political negotiations.
