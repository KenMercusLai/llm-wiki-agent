# Edwin van der Sar

Edwin van der Sar appears in [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]] as the [[ManchesterUnited]] goalkeeper whose penalty tendencies were analyzed by [[IgnacioPalacioHuerta]] before the 2008 Champions League final. The report said van der Sar dived too often to his right, so [[ChelseaFC]] players repeatedly shot to his left.

The episode uses van der Sar's later adjustment as the case's main [[GameTheory]] turn. Once he recognized Chelsea's pattern, he signaled before [[NicolasAnelka]]'s kick that he knew what Chelsea had been doing; Anelka changed course, shot into a dangerous zone, and van der Sar saved it.
