# Peter the Great / 彼得大帝

Peter the Great appears in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]] as the political origin of the Bering story. The episode connects his European shipbuilding study, beard-cutting symbolism, Baltic war, Saint Petersburg construction, and search for sea routes to the [[RussianEmpire|Russian Empire]]'s attempt to stop being only a land power.

His deathbed instruction to explore northern and eastern routes becomes the trigger for the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]]. In the episode's reading, [[VitusBering]]'s later work carries Peter's strategic imagination into [[Kamchatka]], [[Okhotsk]], [[BeringStrait]], and the wider [[RussianNorthPacificExpansion]] branch.

## Connections
- [[RussianEmpire]] - state project Peter is shown modernizing and maritime-orienting.
- [[VitusBering]] - officer chosen to execute the eastern exploration program.
