# Olivia Joan Golly

Olivia Joan Golly is the TikTok creator and granddaughter of [[JoanJohnson]] whose family archive opens [[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]]. The episode describes her trying on couture and vintage clothing that belonged to Joan, including designer pieces that made the Johnson family's wealth and status visible to a contemporary audience.

Her role in the source is archival rather than operational. She gives listeners a personal doorway into [[JohnsonProducts]], showing why the business history matters beyond product names: it made Black entrepreneurial success tangible in clothes, memory, and consumer products that descendants still use.
