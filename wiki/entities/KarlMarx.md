# Karl Marx / 马克思

Karl Marx / 马克思 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] not as the union leader, but as the union's graphic designer. The episode emphasizes this as a displacement joke: labor politics is still present, but Marx is trapped in the practical office work of typography, layout, and file editing.

The intern accidentally undoing Marx's design work turns class struggle into software-keyboard rage. The scene keeps [[LaborMoralAmbivalence]] material: labor ideals still depend on documents, tools, formats, and fragile workflows.
