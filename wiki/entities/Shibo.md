# Shibo

Shibo is the founder of [[YuebanDongli]] and guest in [[wo-yudao-le-di-yige-zhenzheng-xiang-mai-de-peiban-jiqiren-duihua-shibo-yueban-dongli-chuangshiren-gonglu-boke-lrydelizm0-hbk68u5cqe3ti-epb]]. The episode describes him as a 26-year-old robotics founder who suspended a Beijing Institute of Technology PhD, has built nearly 30 original robots, and previously participated in a robotics company as a product lead.

## Position In The Episode
- Argues that [[CompanionRobots]] should create emotional coexistence rather than serve as command-driven tools.
- Presents [[Xiaoban]] as a bipedal, soft-bodied, non-human family companion whose value comes from [[RobotLiveliness]].
- Explains [[YuebanDongli]]'s technical stack through [[Qwen]], [[OnDeviceFastSlowBrain]], [[EmotionalInteractionModels]], and the [[FamilyWorldSimulator]].
- Emphasizes engineering discipline through "No excuse for engineer" while describing the team culture as practical work in service of a romantic goal.

- [[YuebanDongli]] — company he founded.

- [[Koji]] and [[ShizilukouCrossing]] — host and podcast context.
