# A7A5

A7A5 is the Russia-related stablecoin named in [[tech-20260212-0212-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260212-0212-mp-tech-pod-128]]. [[AriRedbord]] identifies it as the largest driver of sanctions-related crypto activity and one of the largest drivers of overall illicit crypto activity in 2025.

In the wiki, A7A5 is a concrete case inside [[StablecoinSanctionsEvasion]]. It shows why stablecoins are not only payment or Treasury-demand infrastructure: when value can move quickly outside traditional banking rails, sanctions enforcement must track token issuers, exchanges, facilitators, and cross-border counterparties.
