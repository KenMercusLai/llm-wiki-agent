# 李煦 / Li Xu

李煦 / Li Xu appears in [[101-caoyin-yu-kangxi-hongloumeng-xing-zhong-you-shi-735044876]] as [[CaoYin|曹寅]]'s counterpart in the Jiangnan trusted-servant network around [[KangxiEmperor|康熙]]. The episode says Cao Yin and Li Xu rotated through weaving and salt-inspection functions and were both relied on by the emperor for fiscal and practical work in the region.

Li Xu matters most in the source's account of [[ImperialFiscalElasticity]]. Salt revenue and official positions could be used to subsidize [[JiangningWeavingBureau|江宁织造]] and cover deficits, but that flexibility created exposure. After Cao Yin's death, Li Xu's requests to help manage or cover the Cao family's obligations show both loyalty and the danger of a system where household trust, public revenue, and private accounts are entangled.
