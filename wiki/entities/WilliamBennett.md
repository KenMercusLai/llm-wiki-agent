# William Bennett

William Bennett appears in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] as the former education secretary whose 1987 [[NewYorkTimes|New York Times]] opinion piece gave the [[BennettHypothesis]] its name. The episode treats Bennett's argument as the historical origin of the claim that federal student aid can let colleges raise prices.

The page matters because the Trump administration's [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]] rely on a modern version of that idea. Bennett is therefore a policy-origin figure rather than one of the episode's empirical researchers.
