# Qilin Ransomware Group

Qilin Ransomware Group is the attacker group named in [[dang-heike-gongpo-le-riben-de-guomin-pijiu-chule-jugong-daoqian-tamen-hai-neng-zuo-shenme-feat-top-of-japan-keji-luandun]]. The episode says Qilin claimed the [[AsahiGroup]] attack and posted screenshots of stolen information after Asahi stated that it had not contacted, negotiated with, or paid the attackers.

The source uses Qilin to explain the double-extortion pattern: attackers may both encrypt operating systems and copy data, then publish samples when payment fails. The hosts also discuss the strange market logic of ransomware "reputation": groups that want future victims to pay may claim they will honor decryption or deletion promises, even though the victim still faces legal, operational, and reputational risk.
