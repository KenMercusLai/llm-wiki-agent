# 无人知晓

无人知晓 is the podcast/show context for [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]]. In this source, the show hosts a three-way conversation among [[ZhangXiaoyu]], [[MengYan]], and [[XuZhe]] that moves from personal friendship into [[BlackSwan]], [[Antifragility]], options, life uncertainty, and [[NoBetterLife]].

The episode is treated in the wiki less as a narrow finance discussion and more as a bridge between investing philosophy, podcast friendship, and practice under uncertainty.
