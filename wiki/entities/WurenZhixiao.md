# 无人知晓

无人知晓 is the podcast/show context for [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]] and [[e42-mengyan-duihua-weiqing-chenmode-zhujue-lip25o2kub9dknpjhfgc4lz-76w0]]. In E43, the show hosts a three-way conversation among [[ZhangXiaoyu]], [[MengYan]], and [[XuZhe]] that moves from personal friendship into [[BlackSwan]], [[Antifragility]], options, life uncertainty, and [[NoBetterLife]].

E42 adds [[WeiQing]] and shifts the same reflective style from investing uncertainty toward engineering identity, [[Microsoft]] transformation, AI, language, attention, and the risk of humans becoming machine-like. The show is therefore treated in the wiki less as a narrow finance discussion and more as a bridge between technology, investing philosophy, podcast friendship, and practice under uncertainty.

E44 adds [[LiXiaobo]] and turns the reflective style back toward [[MengYan]] himself, using [[YouzhiYouxing]] to connect financial-product incentives, founder governance, [[InvestmentForBetterLife]], [[Benfen]], [[AsItIsPractice]], and [[RuminationVsReflection]]. This strengthens the show's role as a bridge between investing, company design, and ordinary-life practice.
