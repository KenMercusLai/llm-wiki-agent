# 东旭 / Dongxu

Dongxu is the [[PingCAP]] co-founder and CTO interviewed in [[guanyu-ai-kaiyuan-shangyehua-yu-quanqiuhua-de-jingyan-jiaoxun-he-fangfalun-duitan-pingcap-cto-dongxu-ljw8va0evobhz4ojzrulqzjvxw5]]. The source presents him as a programmer-founder who remains close to code, open-source culture, music, and art while reflecting on ten years of infrastructure entrepreneurship.

His main contribution to the wiki is a practical method for open-source infrastructure company-building: do hard foundational software, let trust accumulate through transparent process and adoption, avoid damaging the community with premature monetization, then find a compatible cloud-service business model. He also gives a founder-led view of globalization: founders must put body, language, organization, and sales into the target market rather than treating overseas business as remote expansion.
