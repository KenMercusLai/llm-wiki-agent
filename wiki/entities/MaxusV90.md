# Maxus V90

Maxus V90 is the van platform used as [[Guigui]]'s example of a [[BTypeRVMotorhome]] in [[ep122-yongyou-yiliang-fangche-shi-zhong-shenme-yang-de-tiyan-lhposkcf-otqhppghn1yjvpjgsrd]]. The episode describes the long-wheelbase, super-high-roof version as an original van body that can be fitted out internally rather than replaced by a separate box cabin.

The vehicle matters in the source because it makes the B-type tradeoff concrete: better driving feel, original-body safety, and easier city use than larger RV formats, but less living space and weaker winter insulation from the metal shell.
