# Daniel Franklin

Daniel Franklin is the [[TheIntelligence]] participant in [[keep-qualms-and-carry-on-a-decade-after-brexit-6a3a53028a3c8822ab345d4b]] who frames [[Brexit]] as a continuing source of British political and strategic strain. He links the [[UnitedKingdom]]'s repeated changes of prime minister partly to Brexit's legacy and argues that governance became harder after the referendum.

Franklin's closing frame is [[PostBrexitStrategicIdentity]]. He argues that Britain still has opportunities, especially in defence, but needs an honest assessment of its limits because changing leaders or rejoining the [[EuropeanUnion]] would not automatically solve the deeper national-role problem.
