# 毛冬 / Mao Dong

Mao Dong appears in [[ep268-mao-dong-x-tang-tang-da-huang-ni-daodi-za-le-shangcuan-xiatiao-de-huangjin-he-naxie-juebu-xiache-de-ren-lgujs-q24fzqcuutibty8k0bowbv]] as a podcast host and stand-up comedian who studied finance-related subjects and previously worked as a finance journalist. His role is to make the gold discussion concrete through the experience of buying a gold-linked product near a high point, then holding through a visible pullback.

The source uses Mao Dong to show the behavioral side of [[GoldMonetaryAnchor|gold]]: understanding the long-term hedge story does not remove the discomfort of seeing prices fall. His decision not to check the account constantly connects gold investing to [[DrawdownPsychology]], while his comments on his wife's small gold beans connect investment talk to [[HouseholdGoldSavings]].
