# XPeng Iron

XPeng Iron is the humanoid robot discussed by [[HeXiaopeng]] in [[143-dui-he-xiaopeng-de-di-er-ci-fangtan-gengda-duzhu-renxing-jiqiren-iron-dansheng-nachang-yiwai-jishu-jubian-xia-ceo-gx-he-fengheguai-ljekstsafrj-ovtm2bpl92s4nwoc]]. The source presents Iron as a test of [[XPeng]]'s [[PhysicalAI]] route: a general humanoid robot meant to enter life and work scenes rather than only perform an industrial or entertainment demo.

The interview says XPeng chose a human-like form because general robots may eventually interact across many human domains, including aging and household dependence. At the same time, the design tries to avoid becoming too human: the robot can wear clothes and have hair, but the source says it should not have its own face. The launch controversy over whether a person was inside led XPeng to cut open a leg in a follow-up proof video, turning a public doubt into a signal about user expectations for household and elder-care robots.
