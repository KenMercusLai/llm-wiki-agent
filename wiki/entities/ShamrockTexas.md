# Shamrock, Texas

Shamrock, Texas appears in [[putins-options-an-oligarch-speaks-out-6a50c5ebbafe2fa6a7f38210]] as [[JohnFasman]]'s final Route 66 travelogue stop. The episode uses the town to make [[InterstateBypassEconomics]] concrete: when interstate highways made [[Route66]] slower and less useful, Shamrock lost through traffic, businesses, and population.

The source also shows the partial reversal created by [[Route66NostalgiaTourism]]. The restored [[UDropInn]], its visual link to the film *Cars*, and the wider appeal of Route 66 bring visitors from the United States and abroad, turning bypassed infrastructure into heritage travel.
