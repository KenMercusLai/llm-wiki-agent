# 《脸上的门，口中的灯》 / The Doors of His Face, The Lamps of His Mouth

《脸上的门，口中的灯》 / The Doors of His Face, The Lamps of His Mouth is the [[RogerZelazny|Roger Zelazny / 罗杰·泽拉兹尼]] collection paired with [[ForABreathITarry|《趁生命气息逗留》]] in the publication context of [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]]. [[Doudou|逗逗]] says the two books were acquired together, while [[Beimin|北明]] contrasts their career position: this collection is presented as younger and hotter in tone than the more career-spanning 《趁生命气息逗留》.

The episode recommends it as a further route into Zelazny, especially for readers interested in creation, god-making, god-killing, and Promethean energy. It therefore sits beside [[LordOfLight|《光明王》]] as a gateway to [[MythicScienceFictionStyle]].
