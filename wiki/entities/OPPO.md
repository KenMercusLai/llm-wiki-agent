# OPPO

OPPO is one of the mature Chinese smartphone leaders in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode roots OPPO in [[BBK]]'s audiovisual business under Chen Mingyong, then presents it with [[Vivo]] as the "OV" line that won through dense offline retail, regional agent incentives, sales staff, advertising, after-sales service, and stable organization.

The source argues that OPPO's success should not be reduced to celebrity ads. Its channel design and retail execution made it difficult for online-first competitors to copy, especially in lower-tier cities and township markets.
