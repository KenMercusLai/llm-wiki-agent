# Jianying / 剪映

In the source, Jianying functions as the strong horizontal competitor. Its presence makes [[AIApplicationLayerMoat]] harder for Meitu, because the defensive answer must come from narrower taste, workflow, beauty-detail, ecommerce, and ROI contexts rather than from generic editing alone.
