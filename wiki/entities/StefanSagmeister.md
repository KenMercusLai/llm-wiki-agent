# Stefan Sagmeister

Stefan Sagmeister is the designer and speaker in [[stefan-sagmeister-finally-something-good-yqqxftsvema]]. The talk presents him as using old paintings, clothing, labels, public installations, exhibitions, books, hospital corridors, and participatory objects to communicate [[InformedOptimism]] through [[FinallySomethingGood]].

His role in the wiki is to connect long-term thinking to design craft. Instead of treating human-progress data as a spreadsheet or policy argument, he asks how [[BeautyInCommunication]], [[ProgressDataVisualization]], and [[ParticipatoryExhibitions]] can make long horizons emotionally available.
