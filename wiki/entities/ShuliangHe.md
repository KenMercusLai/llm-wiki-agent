# 叔梁纥

叔梁纥 is the Lu strongman and father of [[Confucius|孔子]] whom [[109-xianliao-zuozhuan-zhi-chunqiu-yundonghui-747508773]] nominates for weightlifting. The episode recounts the story that he held up a city gate with both hands so soldiers who had entered could withdraw safely.

His role in the wiki is source-scoped: the episode uses him to make the familiar Confucius family line suddenly physical. The connection matters because the source then jokes that Confucius himself may have inherited or embodied unusual strength, even if he did not want to be remembered primarily for it.
