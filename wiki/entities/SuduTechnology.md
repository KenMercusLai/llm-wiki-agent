# 速度科技 / Sudu Technology

速度科技 is the robotics company at the center of [[e244-duan-dao-duan-vs-shangxia-fenceng-jiqiren-lujing-zhizheng-zhengzai-zhuanxiang-fc9a3737-81a9-49cf-a7d6-530c77df836e]]. [[HanZheng]] describes it as a full-stack company that builds both robot brain and robot body, because manipulation capability, hardware configuration, simulator assumptions, and real-world transfer have to be designed together.

The source positions Sudu against a narrow end-to-end imitation route. Its preferred path emphasizes [[Structured3DRobotData]], [[Sim2Real]], hardware-specific noise modeling, and stable short manipulation skills before assembling long tasks through [[LayeredRobotArchitecture]].
