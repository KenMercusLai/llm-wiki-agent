# Mitch Morando

Mitch Morando appears in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]] as the sales coach [[SpenserSkates]] hired while learning to sell [[Amplitude]]. The episode says Mitch pushed Spenser away from simply demoing product features and toward understanding the customer's business pain, stakeholders, consequences, and story.

The source uses Mitch as evidence that sales can be coached as a craft. His role supports [[SalesAsLearnableSkill]] and [[FounderLedSales]]: a technical founder can improve faster through direct repetition and expert feedback than by reading frameworks alone.
