# 呆萝卜 / Da Luobo

Da Luobo is the Hefei community fresh-grocery case in [[no-200-dianshang-sanguo-zhi-qunxiong-zhulu-yaogua-gongzhang-chijian-juqian-yiji-108-zhong-sifa-974601500]]. The episode says it combined online ordering with offline neighborhood pickup, expanded to more than 1,000 stores, then collapsed under cash burn, arrears, and underestimated fresh-food operating cost.
