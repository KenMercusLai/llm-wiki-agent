# 唐纳 / Donner

Donner is the reservation official named in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]] as the local authority managing the [[WhiteMountainApache|White Mountain Apache]] reservation when [[HenriettaSchmerler]] arrived for fieldwork. The episode presents him as strict, irritable, and invested in maintaining reservation order.

His role is important because he controlled access and later participated in the post-crime interpretation of Schmerler's choices. The source says he allowed her to stay despite her weak introduction paperwork, assigned her a poor and isolated cabin, and later treated her insistence on living near research subjects as evidence of bad judgment. That makes him part of [[AnthropologyFieldworkSafety]] and [[DisciplinarySelfProtectionAfterHarm]] rather than only a local administrator.
