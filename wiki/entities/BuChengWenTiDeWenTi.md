# 《不成问题的问题》

《不成问题的问题》 is a [[LaoShe|老舍]] story discussed in [[48-kaishi-daji-diyiliu-de-youmo-fengci-627375908]]. The episode frames Ding Woyuan as a manager who keeps a loss-making farm socially comfortable: shareholders receive favors, their families are served, workers get slack, and everyone has enough small benefit to avoid demanding real accountability.

The story grounds the source's [[RenqingOrder]] concept. You Daxing's professional reform can improve the farm, but it collides with an environment where relationship maintenance, small advantages, and blame management are more robust than formal performance.
