# Charles Henderson

Charles Henderson is the [[Coalfire]] cybersecurity expert quoted in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. He argues that AI does not create a new kind of scam so much as make existing web impersonation cheaper, faster, and easier to scale.

His contribution is the episode's clearest [[AIEnabledScamIndustrialization]] frame. Before AI, convincing fake retail sites required more specialized skill or hired help; with AI coding agents, scammers can generate many official-looking websites with fewer prompts and less labor.
