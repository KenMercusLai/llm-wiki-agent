# Kenneth Cole

Kenneth Cole is the fashion founder and [[HowIBuiltThis]] Advice Line guest in [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. The episode presents him as a brand builder who links commerce, social purpose, and customer experience: a durable brand needs a distinct voice, a repeatable story, and a reason customers want to come back rather than only a way to sell once.

Cole applies that perspective to [[PedestrianProject]], [[IsraelAcabla]], and [[SwingSculpt]]. He pushes [[MattJacobs]] to make foot care emotionally positive, [[EmmaFiquade]] to clarify what her fashion label stands for and protect customer connection beyond wholesale, and [[LeviCase]] to frame a golf-swing sculpture as a memory object rather than only a technical swing artifact.
