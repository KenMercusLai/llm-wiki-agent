# Chip War

Chip War is the book by [[ChristopherMiller]] cited in [[tech-20260210-0210-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260210-0210-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In this wiki, the book functions as source context for Miller's semiconductor explanation rather than as a separately ingested text.

The episode uses Miller's chip-industry perspective to connect [[GPU|GPUs]], [[TPU|TPUs]], [[AIChipSpecialization]], software ecosystems, and R&D scale. Its practical contribution is to show why AI chips are not interchangeable commodities: they encode different assumptions about workload, energy, flexibility, and platform control.
