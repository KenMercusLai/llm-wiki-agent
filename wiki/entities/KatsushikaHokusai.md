# Katsushika Hokusai / 葛饰北斋

Katsushika Hokusai / 葛饰北斋 enters the wiki through [[sp-03-chuantai-chen-shengming-qixi-douliu-zela-zini-yu-tade-mengjing-757948106]], where [[RogerZelazny|Zelazny]]'s [[TwentyFourViewsOfMountFujiByHokusai|《富岳二十四景》]] is discussed as a story structured around Hokusai's [[ThirtySixViewsOfMountFuji|Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji]]. The episode uses Hokusai not as art-history background alone, but as part of the story's form: each view of Fuji helps organize movement, perception, natural presence, and the protagonist's resistance to coercive digital transformation.
