# 《虚境奇谭》

《虚境奇谭》 is the [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]] book discussed in [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]]. The [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode recommends it as a direct reading experience because Smith's effect depends on dense wording, poetic image accumulation, strange proper names, and a literary texture that summary cannot reproduce.

The episode uses the book to widen the wiki's speculative-literature branch from familiar science fiction and satire into [[WeirdFiction|weird fiction]], [[CthulhuMythos|Cthulhu Mythos / 克苏鲁神话]], and [[CosmicHorror|cosmic horror]]. Its examples include explorers consumed by underground Martian powers in [[TheVaultsOfYohVombis|《深谷住民》]], a doomed official passed from one supernatural receiver to another in [[SevenGeases|《七咒赋》]], and exiles who survive on Saturn in [[TheDoorToSaturn|《通往土星之门》]].

## Key Claims
- The book is presented as a better entry into Smith's style than any plot-only retelling.
- Its central appeal is not only horror but the combination of horror, beauty, antiquity, parody, and cosmic distance.
- The episode praises the Chinese translation and book design as part of the reading atmosphere.
- Its stories connect to [[SmithStoryWorlds]], including Hyperborea, Averoigne, Zothique, and Poseidonis.
- The book gives the podcast a concrete branch for [[HorrorHumorWeirdFiction]] and [[AntiAnthropocentricSatire]].
