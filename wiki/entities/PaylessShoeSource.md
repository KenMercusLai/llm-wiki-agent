# Payless ShoeSource

Payless ShoeSource appears in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]] as the large footwear chain where [[MaxineClark]] became president before leaving corporate retail to found [[BuildABear]]. The episode presents the role as prestigious but increasingly distant from the customer contact Maxine valued.

Payless matters to the wiki because it explains why Build-A-Bear was strange to outsiders but not operationally naive. Maxine's Payless background gave her credibility with suppliers, landlords, agencies, and bankers, while her dissatisfaction with distance from customers pushed her toward [[ExperientialRetail]] and [[CustomerCoCreation]].
