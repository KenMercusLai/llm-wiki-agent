# 丹顶鹤 / Red-crowned Crane

The red-crowned crane is the title species in [[47-niao-youshenme-haokande-yuanlai-dandinghe-shi-tude-625613924]]. The episode's closing fact is that the crane's red crown is bare skin rather than red feathers, overturning the host's childhood image of "鹤顶红" as a feathered red patch.

In the wiki, the crane functions less as a conservation case than as a reminder inside [[BirdwatchingAsAttention]] and [[ObservationBeforeInference]]: culturally familiar animals can still be poorly observed at the level of basic anatomy.
