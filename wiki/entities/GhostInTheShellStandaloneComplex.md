# 《攻壳机动队 STAND ALONE COMPLEX》 / Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex

《攻壳机动队 STAND ALONE COMPLEX》 / Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex appears in [[01-maitian-shouwangzhe-20shiji-de-meiguo-jiabaoyu-523554422]] as a pop-cultural afterlife of [[TheCatcherInTheRye|《麦田里的守望者》]]. The hosts focus on the [[LaughingMan|笑脸男]] story as a brighter reorganization of Holden-like idealism in a cyberpunk setting.

The source says the anime explicitly connects to the famous teacher line from Salinger's novel and turns the pattern into a story about a brilliant, wounded young person trying to protect a world already captured by adult hypocrisy and institutional rot. In the wiki, this makes the series part of [[LiterarySubcultureSymbolization]] rather than only an anime reference.
