# Simon Kuper

Simon Kuper appears in [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]] as the football writer explaining how little systematic penalty data many teams had before the modern analytics era. The episode uses his comments to show why [[IgnacioPalacioHuerta]]'s database could matter: an outsider with disciplined records could sometimes know more about penalty habits than clubs themselves.

Kuper's role connects the source to football economics and analytics rather than only to match drama. The episode points listeners to [[Soccernomics]], linking his broader work with [[StefanSzymanski]] to the same effort to explain football behavior through evidence, incentives, and strategy.
