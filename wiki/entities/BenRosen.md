# Ben Rosen

Ben Rosen appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-v4-tsr-s4-ronconway-v4]] as [[RonConway]]'s early investing partner when Conway was moving from operator and informal angel investor into a more focused internet-era investing practice. Conway describes Rosen as a former technology analyst at Morgan Stanley and a venture capitalist at [[SevenRosenVenturePartners]].

The partnership divided deal flow by geography: Rosen curated East Coast opportunities while Conway worked the West Coast. More importantly, Rosen gave Conway a technology-savvy partner for the [[InternetSoftwareThesis]], helping narrow attention before the internet category was obviously large.

Rosen also found [[AskJeeves]], which Conway presents as a major early win. In the source, Rosen's role is not only capital partnership; he is part of the [[StartupInformationLayer]] that connected analysts, founders, venture firms, and internet software companies before modern seed infrastructure existed.
