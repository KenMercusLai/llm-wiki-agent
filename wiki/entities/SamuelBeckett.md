# Samuel Beckett

Samuel Beckett is one of the writers used in [[stock-options-how-to-hedge-an-ai-bubble-698efe7e7301331f1f674bd5]] to illustrate [[GeorgesBeauchard]]'s literary judgment. The source says Beauchard recognized the importance of an Irish writer producing novels and plays in French, even while editors dismissed the work as unreadable or derivative.

The episode says Beauchard eventually sold *Waiting for Godot*, *Molloy*, and *Malone Dies* together, years before Beckett won the Nobel Prize for Literature.
