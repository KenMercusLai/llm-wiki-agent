# Dave Baldwin

Dave Baldwin appears in [[can-world-cup-mania-grow-mls-in-the-us]] as president of the [[ChicagoFire]]. Because Chicago is not a 2026 [[FIFAWorldCup]] host city, Baldwin frames the club's choice as whether to ignore the tournament or invest behind it anyway.

The episode uses Baldwin's Costco-sampling analogy to explain the Fire's marketing logic. The club spends more than $2 million on a large watch-party experience so non-soccer fans can try the atmosphere at low cost before the team attempts to convert them into ticket buyers, merchandise customers, or longer-term fans.
