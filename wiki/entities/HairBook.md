# 《头发》 / Hair

《头发》 / *Hair* is the book by [[KurtStenn|Kurt Stenn / 库尔特·斯坦]] discussed in [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]]. The episode presents it as a wide-ranging account of hair that spans biology, evolution, medicine, culture, politics, religion, grooming, and forensic evidence.

Within the episode, the book's value is that it makes an ordinary body material analytically dense. Hair is not only decoration or a private worry about balding; it is an evolved skin structure, sensory interface, heat-management clue, reproductive and health signal, identity marker, ritual object, and evidence trace. That makes the book a source case for [[HairAsBiosocialSignal]] and an extension of [[MaterialHistoryNarrative]] into bodily material culture.
