# Johnson & Johnson / 强生

Johnson & Johnson appears in [[e235-20-nian-nei-car-t-zhiyu-aizheng-yu-liucheng-boshi-liaoliao-aizheng-zhiliao-de-diceng-zhexue-90f96f60-25be-45ac-b832-56776a23d534]] through its collaboration with [[LegendBiotech]] on a multiple-myeloma [[CARTCellTherapy|CAR-T]] product. The source uses that partnership as a commercialization example rather than as a broad company profile.

In the episode, the Legend-Johnson & Johnson case supports the claim that ex vivo CAR-T can become a major drug business in blood cancers even while manufacturing remains expensive. It also shows why big pharma interest in [[InVivoCART]] is commercially legible: if CAR-T efficacy is strong but [[ExVivoCARTManufacturing]] is the bottleneck, moving manufacturing into the patient could expand access and margins.
