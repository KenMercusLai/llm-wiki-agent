# Lois Weber / 洛伊斯·韦伯

Lois Weber is one of the early women directors introduced in [[107-xianliao-weida-daoyanmen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-744150818]]. The episode presents her as a major silent-era filmmaker, noting her association with long-form narrative film and her attention to social issues such as abortion, capital punishment, corruption, race, and religion.

Weber extends [[EarlyWomenFilmPioneers]] by showing that women directors were not only present but also used cinema for ambitious social and moral subjects. In the source's structure, she helps turn the episode from famous male-director scandal toward a recovery of overlooked women creators.
