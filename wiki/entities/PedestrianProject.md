# Pedestrian Project

Pedestrian Project is a modern foot-wellness brand co-founded by [[MattJacobs]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. The source says the Astoria, New York company sells moisturizers, creams, lotions, pain-relief products, salt soaks, a foot file, reflexology balls, and a ball-of-foot insert.

The Advice Line question is whether Pedestrian Project should take share from the existing foot-care category or spend to educate consumers into a more proactive routine. [[KennethCole]] advises the company to make the part of the body "below the knees" emotionally visible and positive, while [[GuyRaz]] advises prioritizing conversion through Amazon searches, retail sell-through, and specific communities such as runners, hikers, teachers, and nurses before funding broader category education.
