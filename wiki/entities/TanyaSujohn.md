# Tanya Sujohn

Tanya Sujohn is the [[LondonCollegeOfCommunication]] academic cited in [[tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260102-0102-mp-tech-pod-128]]. She explains that digital well-being and detox products often use familiar engagement features such as dashboards, tracking, streaks, and friend layers to give users a sense of control over phone use.

Her main contribution is the source's paradox: products that promise less phone use may borrow from the same interface language that keeps people using phones. She also shifts responsibility away from pure individual blame by arguing that people use these technologies as they were designed to be used.
