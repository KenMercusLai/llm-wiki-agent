# 《女性卫生用品的社会史》

《女性卫生用品的社会史》 is the [[TanakaHikari|田中光]] book discussed in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]]. The episode presents it as a Japan-centered account of sanitary pads, tampons, menstrual cups, menstrual belts, absorbent cotton, wartime scarcity, and commercial education around menstruation.

The book matters in the wiki because it makes [[MenstrualProductSocialHistory]] legible as [[MaterialHistoryNarrative]]. Ordinary products become evidence for religion, folk taboo, state health policy, war resource allocation, gendered medical advice, school education, packaging, advertising, and consumer-brand competition.
