# Long March 10A / 长征十号甲

Long March 10A is the crewed Long March 10 variant referenced in [[ruhe-douzhu-yike-huojian-s10e21-b66fdf0f-d428-4f0c-8412-b0c7581132d0]]. The episode says [[LongMarch10B]] could move quickly because its first stage directly adopted or reused technology from the Long March 10A crewed version.

The source uses Long March 10A less as a standalone topic than as development context: reusable launch work can move faster when new test vehicles inherit mature engines, tankage, control, and structural systems from a broader rocket family.
