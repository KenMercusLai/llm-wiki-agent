# Visme

Visme is the company context where [[FarzadRashidi]] says [[Responna]] began as an internal marketing tool. In [[he-demoted-his-saas-to-sell-a-service-and-4xd-revenue-in-12-months]], Farzad describes joining Visme as its first marketing hire and relying heavily on SEO because the company was bootstrapped.

The internal Responna tool helped Visme find relevant websites for backlinks, collaborations, and brand mentions. That origin makes Visme part of the wiki's [[InternalToolProductization]] and [[AIDiscoverySEO]] branch: a tool built to solve one company's growth problem later became a separate product and then a productized service.
