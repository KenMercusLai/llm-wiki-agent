# Anula Jayasuria

Anula Jayasuria appears in [[tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3-tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3]] as an investor who helped [[SurbhiSarna]] find possible early backers for [[NVisionMedical|nVision Medical]]. The episode places her in the difficult first-financing chapter, after Sarna had spent roughly a year and a half trying to raise the initial $250,000.

The source does not make Jayasuria the whole financing story. Her introduction helped open a path that still required a matching commitment, Sarna's willingness to take no salary and move home, another investor match, and later [[TimDraper]] matching the $250,000 to give the company a $500,000 prototype budget.
