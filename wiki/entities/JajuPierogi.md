# Jaju Pierogi

Jaju Pierogi is the Polish dumpling company discussed by [[CaseyWhite]] in [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. The source says the brand uses Casey's grandfather's recipes and sells through grocery stores, direct-to-consumer online, and events, with about 2,700 stores and $2.5 million in sales the prior year.

The Advice Line question is whether a growing CPG company can keep scaling without raising a lot of outside equity. [[JeniBritton]] and [[GuyRaz]] advise Casey to consider SBA loans, bank loans, friends-and-family capital, grants, creative financing, and advisor or board support before giving up control. The caller update says SBA loans and grants helped Jaju launch in Giant and Target stores in the Midwest while food service grew by 40%.
