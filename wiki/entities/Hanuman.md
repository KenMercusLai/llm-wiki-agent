# 哈努曼 / Hanuman

哈努曼 / Hanuman appears in [[43-xiyouji-duo-ni-shi-shenme-yaojing-618154182]] as one possible source layer for [[SunWukong|孙悟空]]. The episode notes similarities such as protective circles, long-distance magical travel, and havoc in an enemy realm, while also stressing that Hanuman cannot be the sole explanation for Sun Wukong.

In the wiki, Hanuman therefore belongs to [[MythicSourceLayering]] rather than to a simple one-to-one influence claim. The episode's value is comparative: it shows how [[JourneyToTheWest|《西游记》]] can absorb Indian epic motifs while also drawing from local Chinese water-monster lore, monkey-demon stories, Daoist terminology, and pilgrimage history.
