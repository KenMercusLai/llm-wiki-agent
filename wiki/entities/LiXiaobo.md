# Li Xiaobo / 李晓波

Li Xiaobo is the conversation partner in [[e44-li-xiaobo-duihua-mengyan-zheci-jiu-zheyang-ba-lpcrvfgnseaed-eambk9ofnvuq2]]. He opens the episode by saying he deliberately avoided heavy preparation because he had long observed [[MengYan]]'s writing, product touchpoints, and values, and wanted the conversation to unfold from trust rather than a fixed outline.

His role is to keep pulling the discussion from financial product design back to life practice. He challenges [[YouzhiYouxing]]'s pre-purchase quiz design, asks why a company cannot simply pursue a healthy long-term thing, and repeatedly connects [[Benfen]], [[InvestmentForBetterLife]], [[AsItIsPractice]], and [[RuminationVsReflection]] to ordinary choices about attention, family, consumption, and calm.
