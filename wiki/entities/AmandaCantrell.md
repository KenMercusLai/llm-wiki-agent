# Amanda Cantrell

Amanda Cantrell appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] as the renter whose search for a house in Murfreesboro, Tennessee opens the episode's [[InstitutionalSingleFamilyRental]] question. She noticed many available homes seemed to be owned or managed by large companies and worried that corporate investors were taking homes away from would-be buyers.

Her case matters because it gives the policy debate an ordinary household entry point. The episode does not dismiss her concern; it uses her experience to test whether visible corporate ownership is actually the main cause of high housing costs, then compares that feeling against [[HousingAffordabilitySupplyMechanics]] and [[CorporateLandlordTradeoffs]].
