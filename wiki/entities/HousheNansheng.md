# 后舍男生 / Houshe Nansheng

Houshe Nansheng appears in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]] as a representative early Chinese internet-video creator case. The episode uses them to mark the pre-professional UGC moment when campus lip-sync and homemade videos could become nationally recognizable.

Their wiki role is less about a company and more about audience formation: early video platforms such as [[Tudou]] helped create shared internet culture before long-video competition became dominated by copyright and membership economics.
