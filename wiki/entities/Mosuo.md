# Mosuo

Mosuo society appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] as the episode's clearest warning against careless matriarchy labeling. The source says Mosuo patterns can be matrilineal or mother-centered in important ways, but they should not be simplified into proof of female political rule because male uncle authority can still matter for external or family decisions.

The Mosuo example anchors [[KinshipPowerDistinction]]: descent through women, residence around a mother's household, and actual control of political or economic resources are analytically different. The episode uses that distinction to resist romanticizing mother-centered societies.
