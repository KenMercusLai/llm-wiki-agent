# International Hotel

The International Hotel appears in [[two-indicators-for-lowering-the-rent]] as a San Francisco SRO whose 1977 eviction became a landmark displacement case. The episode says it housed mostly elderly Filipino and Chinese people and that more than 2,000 protesters tried to stop the evictions.

The source uses the International Hotel to make [[SROLossAndHomelessness]] concrete. Its eviction sits inside the broader 1970s disappearance or conversion of around a million SRO rooms, showing how [[SRORegulatoryErasure]] and redevelopment pressure could remove a cheap housing rung for older, poor, immigrant, and single residents.
