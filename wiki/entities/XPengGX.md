# XPeng GX

XPeng GX is the full-size six-seat flagship SUV discussed in [[143-dui-he-xiaopeng-de-di-er-ci-fangtan-gengda-duzhu-renxing-jiqiren-iron-dansheng-nachang-yiwai-jishu-jubian-xia-ceo-gx-he-fengheguai-ljekstsafrj-ovtm2bpl92s4nwoc]]. [[HeXiaopeng]] presents it as [[XPeng]]'s renewed high-end vehicle effort and as a carrier for the company's [[PhysicalAI]] strategy.

The source says GX combines learning from cars, flying-vehicle redundancy, robotics task understanding, steer-by-wire chassis, new electronic architecture, and intelligent-driving control. Its target user is described as a family user over 30 who wants a large vehicle but is still willing to explore new technology, making GX a bridge between high-end family SUV expectations and [[AIPlusTerminals]].
