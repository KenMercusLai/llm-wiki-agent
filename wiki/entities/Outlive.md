# 超越百岁 / Outlive

《超越百岁》 is the longevity book cited in [[zhongnian-san-zhanghu-xianjinliu-jirou-shuimian-lnyomru5v2yzo1-otuyw2mdj-vae]] for the claim that muscle functions like a longevity organ and that strength training resembles retirement saving. The episode uses the book less as a book-report object than as a source of metaphors for [[MuscleAsLongevityInfrastructure]].

Within this wiki, the book sits between [[EmbodiedAging]] and preventive-health practice. It helps the episode translate aging from a vague future fear into reserve capacity: skeletal muscle, grip, posture, fall recovery, training adaptation, protein intake, and sleep-supported repair.
