# 超哥

超哥 is a host voice in [[xianliao-xiang-song-zhao-lusi-yi-ben-duan-ge-ji-bianji-tuishu-wu-suo-bu-yong-qi-ji-905512770]], appearing in the [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] and [[YouWenTiYa|有问题呀]] crossover with [[QinZong|秦总]] and [[LiWuya|李乌鸦]]. The source presents him as someone less immersed in young-actor fandom, which lets him reconstruct the [[ZhaoLusi|赵露思]] discourse from search results and video/comment exposure.

His most important contribution is reader response. He is especially struck by an [[IshikawaTakuboku|石川啄木]] short song about arriving at today like a stone rolling downhill, giving the episode one of its clearest bridges between poetry and ordinary exhaustion.
