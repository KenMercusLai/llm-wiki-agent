# Carrie George

Carrie George is the [[ComputingResearchAssociation]] expert interviewed by [[StephanieHughes]] in [[tech-20260217-0217-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260217-0217-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, she explains why U.S. computing enrollment has fallen unevenly across subfields.

George's main contribution to the wiki is the distinction between traditional computing enrollment and specialized computing demand. She says computer science, software engineering, and information systems are down, while computer engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and AI-related programs are stable or growing. She also connects enrollment shifts to student awareness of weak job outcomes, AI anxiety, international-student declines, and long-term research capacity.
