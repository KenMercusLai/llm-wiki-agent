# Himiko

Himiko appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] through [[Beimin|北明]]'s discussion of early Japanese examples. The episode presents her as a possible case of a woman holding military, political, and diplomatic authority, making her closer to a real female-power example than purely symbolic goddess material.

The source still uses Himiko cautiously. A powerful queen does not prove a universal matriarchal stage, but she helps the episode keep the [[MatriarchyQuestion]] open without collapsing every female figure into either myth or exception.
