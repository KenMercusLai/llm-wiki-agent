# Justin Wolfers

Justin Wolfers is the economist guiding [[PlanetMoney]]'s Summer School World Tour in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]]. He frames countries as laboratories where economic principles can be tested under different institutions, using [[Australia]] and [[NewZealand]] as the first two cases.

In the water segment, Wolfers explains [[WaterMarketDesign]], [[WaterRightsTrading]], [[TragedyOfTheCommons]], and the ambiguous role of [[MarketSpeculation]]. In the inflation segment, he connects [[InflationTargeting]] to credibility, transparency, and [[MultipleEquilibria]].
