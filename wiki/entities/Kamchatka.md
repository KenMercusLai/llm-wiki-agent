# Kamchatka / 勘察加

Kamchatka is the far-eastern destination and operating base in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. The episode treats it as both a geographic uncertainty and a logistics endpoint: the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]] reached it only after a cross-Siberian transport ordeal, and the [[GreatNorthernExpedition]] later used its coast and harbor system for voyages toward [[Alaska]].

The source also makes Kamchatka a colonial-burden site. Transporting supplies to the Pacific side, requisitioning labor, and responding to local resistance expose the violent underside of [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]] and the [[RussianEmpire|Russian Empire]]'s North Pacific program.
