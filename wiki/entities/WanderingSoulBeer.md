# Wandering Soul Beer

Wandering Soul Beer is the craft beer brand owned by [[MattSmith]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. The brand began after Matt and his family experienced the stillbirth of his daughter Melody, and the first beer, Melody Maker, sold out enough to turn the project into a business.

The episode presents Wandering Soul Beer as a [[StoryLedConsumerBranding]] and [[FounderWorkBoundaries]] case. The personal origin gives the brand meaning and differentiation, but Matt also handles sales, labels, recipes, marketing, and social media himself from home. [[RonnenHarary]] and [[GuyRaz]] advise him to create physical workspace separation and non-negotiable time away from the business so the story does not swallow the founder.
