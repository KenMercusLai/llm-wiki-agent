# Dolby

Dolby appears in [[the-latest-tv-innovations-have-their-critics]] through [[MaheshBalakrishnan]], its VP of Consumer Technology. The episode presents Dolby as developing a more selective approach to motion processing, where smoothing can be guided by creator metadata and applied only where needed.

The source links Dolby to [[SelectiveMotionSmoothing]] rather than to generic TV enhancement. Its relevance is the attempt to reduce [[TVMotionStutter]] without globally triggering the [[SoapOperaEffect]] that makes film content look unlike film.
