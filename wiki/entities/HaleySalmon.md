# Haley Salmon

Haley Salmon is the [[TheIntelligence]] correspondent quoted in [[fault-lines-venezuelas-paltry-earthquake-response-6a44e38375e7a3e96111931b]] on [[Venezuela]]'s earthquake response. Her segment links public anger to the first 48 hours after the disaster, when many Venezuelans felt abandoned by the state.

Her reporting makes the episode's disaster politics concrete: shortages of machinery, fuel, medical capacity, and organized help are not treated as isolated logistics problems, but as evidence of deeper [[DisasterResponseStateCapacity]] decay.
