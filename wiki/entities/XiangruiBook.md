# 《祥瑞》

《祥瑞》 is the [[ZhangXiangrong|张向荣]] book used by [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] to reread [[WangMang|王莽]] and the end of the [[WesternHanDynasty|Western Han dynasty]]. The episode presents the book as an attempt to place Wang Mang inside sacred imperial legitimacy, Confucian political ideals, consort-kin power, and the omen environment that made usurpation thinkable.

The book's wiki role is to connect individual biography with political theology. Through the episode, it frames Wang Mang as an outcome of [[MandateOfHeavenLegitimacy]], [[DynasticLegitimacyBackfire]], [[ChenweiPolitics]], and [[AuspiciousOmenPolitics]], rather than as an isolated abnormal ruler.

## Key Claims
- The source uses 《祥瑞》 to avoid both simple condemnation and simple rehabilitation of Wang Mang.
- Its interpretive center is how sacred signs, moral reputation, and Confucian ideals moved inside state power.
- The episode summarizes the book's endpoint as a failed attempt by Confucianism to tame the monarch.
