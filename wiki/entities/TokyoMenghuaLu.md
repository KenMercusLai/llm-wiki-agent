# 《东京梦华录》

《东京梦华录》 is the Northern Song urban-custom text used in [[152-yese-rushui-hua-qixi-niulang-yuanben-shi-baoan-minsuxue-xilie-tiankeng-la-902820062]] to show how [[QixiFestival|七夕 / Qixi]] became a city market and festival-object system. The episode cites its descriptions of pre-Qixi selling and making of fruit figures, double lotus, water toys, seedling boards, sprouting displays, and 摩侯罗 dolls.

For this wiki, the text matters because it turns Qixi from only a celestial love story into [[MaterialHistoryNarrative]]. Festival objects, toy markets, craft miniatures, offerings, and urban preparation show how belief and entertainment become concrete goods, seasonal labor, and public display.

## Source Position
- The text grounds the episode's Song urban Qixi layer.
- It supplies evidence for how festival practice moved through markets, objects, and domestic display.
- It provides the base comparison for later Guangdong "拜七娘" material in the source.
