# Tricia McLaughlin

Tricia McLaughlin appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the [[USDepartmentOfHomelandSecurity|DHS]] spokesperson who told NPR that officers were receiving the same number of training hours as before. Her role in the wiki is attached to the episode's disputed [[FederalLawEnforcementTraining]] account.

The source contrasts McLaughlin's statement with NPR's description of DHS-provided numbers indicating 14 weeks of training and with documents released by [[RichardBlumenthal]]'s office that appeared to show fewer hours for new recruits. The wiki should keep those claims source-scoped and unresolved.
