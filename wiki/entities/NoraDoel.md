# Nora Doel / 诺拉·多尔

Nora Doel / 诺拉·多尔 is [[FrankDoel|Frank Doel]]'s wife in the correspondence around [[EightyFourCharingCrossRoad|《查令十字街84号》 / 84 Charing Cross Road]]. In [[134-chaling-shizi-jie-84-hao-zaici-yuanliang-shijie-de-liyou-821075226]], she enters the exchange as the relationship widens beyond buyer and bookseller into family photographs, daily-life detail, and shared regret.

Nora matters because she makes the correspondence communal. [[HeleneHanff|Helene]] is not only writing to one man across the ocean; her gifts and jokes come to include a household and a bookshop circle. After Frank's death, Nora's response to Helene's condolence preserves both gratitude and the painful fact that Helene never met him in person.
