# U.S. Customs and Border Protection

U.S. Customs and Border Protection appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as a [[USDepartmentOfHomelandSecurity|DHS]]-linked enforcement agency whose recruits are trained through [[FederalLawEnforcementTrainingCenters]]. The source also uses CBP with [[USImmigrationAndCustomsEnforcement|ICE]] when [[SethStoughton]] discusses questionable conduct by federal immigration agents.

The episode's main point is not a standalone CBP profile, but the way rapid enforcement scale raises shared questions about [[FederalLawEnforcementTraining]], supervision, field culture, and accountability across immigration-facing federal agencies.
