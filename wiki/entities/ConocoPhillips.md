# ConocoPhillips

ConocoPhillips appears in [[chevron-venezuela-and-the-paradox-of-plenty]] as one of the American oil majors that left [[Venezuela]] after [[HugoChavez]] tightened control over the oil industry. The source contrasts ConocoPhillips and [[ExxonMobil]] with [[Chevron]], which negotiated with Chavez and stayed.

The company matters as an exit case. The episode says ConocoPhillips and ExxonMobil argue that Venezuela still owes them billions from assets taken about two decades earlier, tying the company to the legal and capital-risk side of [[OilNationalization]] and [[PDVSA]] control.
