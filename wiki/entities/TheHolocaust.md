# The Holocaust / 犹太人大屠杀

The Holocaust / 犹太人大屠杀 is the historical catastrophe behind the second half of [[103-ta-shi-cichuan-yewu-de-guang-ta-shi-hanna-alunte-737939789]]. The episode recounts the "final solution," the Wannsee Conference, forced labor, gas chambers, and the murder of six million Jews as the background to [[HannahArendt|Hannah Arendt]] and [[HeinrichBlucher|Heinrich Blücher]] realizing the scale of Nazi extermination.

The source uses the Holocaust to challenge comforting explanations of evil. It says postwar attempts to find a special "Nazi personality" can make perpetrators look like a separate species, while Arendt's [[EichmannInJerusalem|《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》]] forces attention back to administration, ordinary language, role performance, and failed judgment through [[BanalityOfEvil]].
