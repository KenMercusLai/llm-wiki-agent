# Johnny Fidelis Santos

Johnny Fidelis Santos appears in [[the-giant-factory-town-that-might-be-a-giant-mistake]] as the [[PortChibatao]] source who explains the flow of cargo into [[Manaus]]. He says roughly 70 percent of the cargo arriving there comes from Asia.

His role in the episode is to make the factory model visible. The containers entering Manaus carry many of the high-tech components that local factories assemble into finished products, supporting the source's argument that the free-zone economy remains closer to [[SubsidizedAssemblyIndustrialization]] than full local technological upgrading.
