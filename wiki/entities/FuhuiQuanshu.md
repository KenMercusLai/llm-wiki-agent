# 《福惠全书》

《福惠全书》 is [[HuangLiuhong|黄六鸿]]'s administrative work and one of the three source pillars in [[JonathanSpence|史景迁]]'s [[TheDeathOfWomanWang|《王氏之死》]]. In [[148-wangshi-zhisi-duanjing-tuiyuan-shang-de-lishi-zhuangjing-892588934]], it supplies county-level governing texture that [[TanchengCountyGazetteer|《郯城县志》]] and [[LiaozhaiZhiyi|《聊斋志异》]] cannot supply by themselves.

For the episode, the book is valuable because it captures practical administration: tax difficulty, suicide admonition, local disorder, case evidence, and the use of popular belief in investigation. It anchors [[MicrohistoryFromHostileArchives]] by showing how an official text can preserve traces of people who did not control the record, including [[WangShiTancheng|王氏]].
