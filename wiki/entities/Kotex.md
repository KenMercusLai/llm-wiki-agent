# Kotex / 高洁丝

Kotex / 高洁丝 is the disposable sanitary-pad brand discussed in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the early U.S. reference point for modern pads. The source says it helped define the product format before postwar Japanese consumers could easily buy, name, or discuss sanitary napkins.

The episode also uses Kotex to show how [[MenstrualStigma]] affects distribution. Early advertising and self-service purchasing reduced the need to ask a clerk aloud for a stigmatized product, making the buying interface part of [[CommercialMenstrualEducation]].
