# Calculating Empires

Calculating Empires is the large timeline and critical cartography project [[KateCrawford]] discusses in [[kate-crawford-mapping-empires]]. In the source, Crawford describes it as a 24-meter-long map of communication, computation, classification, and control from around 1500 onward.

The project matters because it turns AI from a near-term product story into a long historical pattern. It places AI beside printing, perspective, colonization, capitalism, logistics, measurement, classification, automation, militarization, and enclosure, making [[AIMetabolicInfrastructure]] and [[PublicInterestAI]] easier to see as historical and political questions.
