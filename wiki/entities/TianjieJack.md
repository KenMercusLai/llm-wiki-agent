# Tianjie Jack

Tianjie Jack is a ZhenFund investor and guest in [[agent-yuannian-di-500-tian-shenme-zai-xiaoshi-shenme-zai-dansheng-weishenme-women-bugai-zai-touzi-gui-siwei-de-ruanjian-lhwdxfpke3bmamjk4e6knk-5sn-b]]. He explains the thesis that investors should avoid "GUI thinking" software, meaning products whose core imagination remains bounded by screens and manual human operation.

## Position In The Episode
- Argues that model capability improved faster than expected, while agent productization remained weaker than expected.
- Clarifies that the [[HeadlessSoftware]] thesis is not anti-GUI; GUI remains important for trust, review, and human communication.
- Uses a Gmail and [[Codex]] workflow to show how an agent can access a product's underlying data while bypassing much of the traditional GUI.
- Frames [[AgenticEconomy]] as infrastructure for future agent-to-agent or agent-to-service value exchange.
- Introduces [[TokenGrant]] and [[YouyouAgent]] as early experiments around agent-native creation.
