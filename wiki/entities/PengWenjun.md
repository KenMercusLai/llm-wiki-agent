# 彭文君

彭文君 is cited in [[jiufan-zhongting-mifeng-jingjixue-956460448]] as director of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences bee research institute. The episode uses his 2022 interview claim to frame the economic upside of China's pollination services: honey output is valuable, but the potential crop-yield value of well-developed pollination could be much larger.

Within the wiki, Peng matters because he bridges the source's China section from [[HoneyQualityStandards]] into [[PollinationServiceMarket]]. His cited argument implies that the Chinese beekeeping economy may need to reduce dependence on low-priced honey and build the contracting, grower education, and subsidy mechanisms that make pollination a paid agricultural service.
