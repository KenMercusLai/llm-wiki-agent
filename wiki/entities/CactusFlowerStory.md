# 《仙人掌之花》

《仙人掌之花》 is the first story discussed in [[131-richang-zhimi-tuili-shi-wo-wei-neng-shuo-chukou-de-zhenxin-805617229]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] uses it as the warm example of [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]]. The episode describes a sixth-grade class whose "cactus superpower" graduation research annoys school adults but wins protection from a soon-retiring teacher.

The mystery turns on misdirection. The children appear to be staging a plant telepathy performance around an agave-like plant and paper questions, while their real project is making a bottle of liquor and a flowering plant gift for the teacher. Its solution exemplifies [[UnspokenFeelingAsMystery|说不出口的心事]] because the hidden truth is not crime but gratitude the children cannot state plainly.

## Key Claims
- The story shows daily-life mystery at its gentlest: clues, performance, and trickery are used to hide affection.
- The children's identification with cactus-like plants makes their misbehavior legible as a claim to unruly selfhood.
- The teacher's last "battle" is not against a criminal but against adults who would flatten the children's strange research.
- The reveal turns an impossible "superpower" into a human-scale gift.
