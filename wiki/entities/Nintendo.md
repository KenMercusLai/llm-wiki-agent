# Nintendo

Nintendo appears in [[jiu-shidai-diantai-28-shiti-youxi-de-shidai-zhongjie-zhiji-buru-chongxin-dingyi-yongyou-998430652]] as a comparison point for whether console companies will keep supporting physical game ecosystems after [[Sony]]'s source-reported retreat from new physical discs.

The source treats Nintendo as part of the broader console-platform question rather than as the central case. Its role is to show that [[PhysicalGameEraDecline]] may spread through industry precedent even where players still associate a platform with cartridges, collection, and offline ownership.
