# Air Purifier / 空气净化器

Air purifier appears in [[97-huafen-zheng-yu-renlei-cong-niandeteren-aqiu-dao-kongqi-jinghuaqi-728113059]] as a household technology linked to hay-fever avoidance. The episode says early attempts to make indoor air tolerable for pollen-allergy sufferers helped connect air purification, air conditioning, and the modern private home.

The source's point is not that every cooling or filtering technology has a single origin. It uses the air purifier to show how [[HayFeverEnvironmentalHistory]] creates markets and infrastructure: when [[AllergySourceControl|source control]] is hard and outdoor pollen cannot simply be eliminated, people build filtered indoor space as a health adaptation.
