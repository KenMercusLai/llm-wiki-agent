# DeLeon Tequila

DeLeon Tequila is the high-end tequila brand built with [[BrentHocking]], [[TomRinks]], and [[RinksCanetti]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The episode uses DeLeon as Tom's clearest pre-[[SunBum]] example of emotional consumer branding.

The source says the brand used a luxury bottle, a sterling silver cork, skull-and-snake imagery, and prices from $125 to $850 to turn ordering straight tequila into a self-image product rather than only an alcohol purchase.
