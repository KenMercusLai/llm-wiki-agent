# Margaret Mead / 米德

Margaret Mead appears in [[113-henglieta-yu-nachang-jiang-renleixue-songshang-shenpanxi-de-moushaan-756562827]] through the [[ColumbiaUniversity]] anthropology response to [[HenriettaSchmerler]]'s murder. The source says Mead argued that women could be killed even in so-called civilized communities, while also criticizing Schmerler for not securing enough female protection.

The episode uses Mead to show how a discipline can recognize women's danger and still slide into [[VictimBlamingAsInstitutionalDefense]]. Her response is presented as part of a broader institutional field where scholars worried about reputation, field access, and rules for women researchers after the case.
