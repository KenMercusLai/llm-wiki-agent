# Perplexity

Perplexity appears in [[ai-startup-hits-8-6m-arr-with-v0-mvp-and-eur85-pricing]] as one of the AI search tools that [[PeakAI]] monitors for brand visibility. The episode groups Perplexity with [[ChatGPT]], [[Gemini]], and similar systems as buyer-research surfaces that can influence product discovery.

[[tech-20251218-1218-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251218-1218-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds Perplexity as the cautionary example for [[AISearchAdvertising]]. The episode says Perplexity reportedly pulled back from plans to integrate ads after limited early ad revenue, using that case to show that AI-search monetization requires more than inserting sponsored content into answers.
