# Meng Yan / 孟岩

Meng Yan is one of the speakers in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]]. In the episode, he often asks the ordinary-investor version of [[XuZhe]]'s more technical claims: if [[NassimTaleb]] and professional tail-risk funds have already made these ideas public, why are they not easier to copy, and what should non-specialists do instead?

His main contribution is to connect [[FatTailRisk]] to long-term index investing, cash reserves, and insurance. That keeps the episode from implying that every listener should trade options; instead, [[PassiveInvesting]], [[InsuranceRiskTransfer]], and [[InvestmentRiskManagement]] become ordinary-person ways to survive a fat-tail world.
