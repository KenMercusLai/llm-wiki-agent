# Jie Dechen

Jie Dechen (杰德辰, also referred to as Jett in the transcript) is a founding engineer at [[Paperboy]] and one of the guests in [[renlei-he-ai-agent-de-zuijia-peihe-fangshi-hai-mei-bei-faming-duitan-paperboy-ltgxurpseowqggfvgc32aurymt-o]]. Before Paperboy, he worked on open-source and CTF projects, including [[EarthKit]], a multimodal project that inferred where a photo was taken.

## Views In The Source
- He argues that ordinary computer-use data is dense: observing 60 minutes of OS activity may teach more about a user than observing 60 minutes of chat messages.
- He describes Paperboy's memory system as a continuously updated markdown-like representation of a user's profession, recent days, recent minutes, and current seconds.
- He identifies OS-wide autocomplete as an early product surface across WeChat, command lines, GitHub PRs, and other contexts.
- He prefers [[Codex]] for its open agent loop, desktop craft, computer use, browser use, and multi-agent interface direction, while seeing [[ClaudeCode]] as proof of Anthropic model quality.
- He names robotics and AI security as attractive opportunity areas, connecting agent capability to [[EmbodiedAI]] and [[AIGovernanceAndCompliance]].
