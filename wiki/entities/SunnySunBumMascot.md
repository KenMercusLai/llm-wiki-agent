# Sunny / Sun Bum Mascot

Sunny is the ape mascot for [[SunBum]], created during the design process led by [[TomRinks]] and [[ReneKennedy]] in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source says the final mark looked essentially like the logo still associated with the brand.

The mascot mattered because Sun Bum could put the ape on stickers, towels, balls, hats, ads, billboards, and retail displays without always needing the brand name. That made Sunny a concrete case of [[MascotLedBrandRecognition]] and [[VisualBrandSystem]] working together.
