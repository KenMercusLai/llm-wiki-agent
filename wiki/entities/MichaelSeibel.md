# Michael Seibel

Michael Seibel appears in [[socialradarspod-brianchesky-final]] as the [[JustinTV]] founder-network contact who helped move [[Airbnb]] closer to [[YCombinator]]. After [[BrianChesky]] was stranded in Austin without a place to stay, Seibel found him in a hotel lobby, invited him to stay with the Justin.tv group, heard the Airbnb pitch, and later introduced the founders to angel investors.

The source uses Seibel as an example of how failed field experiences can become useful founder-network events. The Austin trip did not validate the product at scale, but it exposed the payments problem and connected Chesky to a YC-adjacent founder who could make investor introductions.
