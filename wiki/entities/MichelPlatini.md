# Michel Platini

Michel Platini appears in [[shangye-xiaoyang-44-shijiebei-kuojun-yu-fifa-de-quanli-douzheng-993354969]] as the expected European succession figure whose path was damaged by the FIFA corruption crisis. The episode uses him mainly to explain why [[GianniInfantino]], initially a temporary [[UEFA]] candidate, had room to become a serious [[FIFA]] presidential contender.

Platini's role in this source is contextual rather than analytical: his fall helped shift the presidential race toward a candidate who made [[WorldCupExpansion]] the central electoral promise.
