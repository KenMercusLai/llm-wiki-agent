# LibLib

LibLib is the AI creator-tool and community company where [[Roi]] worked before founding [[Mujian]], as described in [[ta-xiang-zao-yi-ge-ai-shidai-de-chaoji-youlechang-duitan-roi-mujian-chuangshiren-ceo-ltfh-on-4qqz-ardqury4g0jejec]]. Roi says she participated in early creator-ecosystem cold start, helped with AI drawing activities that reached wider attention, and later worked on creator tools.

The source uses LibLib less as a full company case and more as Roi's transition point: she saw the energy of AI-native creators and tool building, but later decided that she wanted a long-term direction closer to AI plus entertainment and interaction, with herself as the first-seat operator.
