# American Airlines

American Airlines appears in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] as one of the legacy carriers that competed more directly with [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] after adopting lower upfront ticket options. The episode groups American with [[DeltaAirLines|Delta Air Lines]] and [[UnitedAirlines|United Airlines]] in the "revenge of the legacy carriers" story.

American's role in this source is to illustrate [[BasicEconomyCopycatStrategy]] and [[AirlineLoyaltyProgramMoat]]. A large network and loyalty ecosystem can make a cheap ticket from an incumbent more attractive than the same headline price from a smaller budget carrier.
