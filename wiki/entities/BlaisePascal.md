# Blaise Pascal / 帕斯卡

Blaise Pascal / 帕斯卡 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the employee caught gambling online during work by [[MichelFoucault|福柯]]'s monitoring system. The episode connects the joke to Pascal's ideas about distraction, boredom, death, and God.

The workplace translation is not just "slacking off." It links distraction to a deeper need to avoid unbearable thought, which lets the source connect Pascal to [[WorkplacePacing]] without treating every pause from work as simple laziness.
