# Una

Una is the [[YingdiHaike]] guest in [[ep102-duihua-una-quanqiu-toubu-siwei-daotu-app-store-yunying-fuzeren-qinshou-aso-shizhan-jingyan-lscdlnogyhiohre091lwi-ayshug]]. She is introduced as the founder of [[FocusFly]] and as the person responsible for [[AppStore]] operations for a globally leading mind-mapping product.

Her practical position is that [[AppStoreOptimization]] is a repeatable growth workflow, not a one-time keyword-fill task. She breaks ASO into [[AppStoreKeywordStrategy]], [[AppStoreProductPageConversion]], [[AppStoreRatingsAndReviews]], ranking monitoring, and [[AppleSearchAds]] validation, while warning independent developers away from fake downloads, fake reviews, and misleading hot-product imitation.
