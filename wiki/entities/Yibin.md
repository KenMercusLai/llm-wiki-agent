# Yibin / 宜宾

Yibin appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as a latecomer city trying to turn from liquor, tea, and state-owned-economy strength toward advanced manufacturing. The episode says Yibin used government coordination, a university-city strategy, research connections, green electricity, hydropower, lower power prices, and high-service local government work to attract battery production.

The decisive anchor in the source is [[CATL]]. Yibin's case shows that a city without deep original battery roots can still become relevant when it can provide power, land, coordination, talent pipelines, and a chain-leading customer or producer.
