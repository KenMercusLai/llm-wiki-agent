# John Donahoe

John Donahoe appears in [[socialradarspod-tonyxu-final]] as the [[EBay]] CEO who encouraged [[TonyXu]] to apply to business school, especially [[StanfordUniversity]]. Xu presents the advice as part of his shift from finance and strategy exposure toward developing the human side of business.

The source's main use of Donahoe is biographical context for Xu before [[DoorDash]]. It helps explain why Xu entered Stanford without a fixed startup plan but became open to the relationships and customer discovery that later produced [[StanfordStartupGarage]] and [[PaloAltoDelivery]].
