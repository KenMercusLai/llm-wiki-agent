# Sun Bum

Sun Bum is the sunscreen and beach-care brand built by [[TomRinks]] and discussed in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The episode presents the brand as a design-led CPG breakout: a woodgrain bottle, yellow-and-brown palette, clean type, surf/streetwear cues, and [[SunnySunBumMascot|Sunny]] gave an ordinary sunscreen category a stronger identity than legacy competitors.

Sun Bum's early growth came from surf shops, boutique accounts, high-end hotels, stickers, full retail displays, and a deliberately beach-coded [[CocoaBeach]] presence even while operations started in Grand Rapids. The source says sales rose from about $100,000 in year one to $1.1 million in year two, and that [[SCJohnson]] bought the company in 2019 for a reported $400 million.
