# Tea Maker

Tea Maker is [[ThibautLouisLucas]]'s bootstrapped SaaS holding company. In [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], Lucas says the company had recently crossed roughly $1 million per month in revenue across five live products with a team of about 10 people.

## Key Claims
- Tea Maker serves founders and small business owners, with products intended to help them grow and make money.
- The company is organized around a [[SaaSHoldingCompany]] model: multiple products, shared customers, similar acquisition channels, and reusable distribution systems.
- Lucas's role inside Tea Maker is increasingly focused on [[DistributionLedProductBuilding]], while co-makers build and operate individual products.
- The model depends on recurring use and [[CustomerPull]], not just launch attention or one-time demand.

- [[ThibautLouisLucas]] - founder.
