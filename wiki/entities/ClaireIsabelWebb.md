# Claire Isabel Webb

Claire Isabel Webb is the interviewer in [[claire-isabel-webb-nina-miolane-the-geometry-of-consciousness-iulbwkinatk]]. The source presents her as framing [[NinaMiolane]]'s research against public debates over whether AI is conscious, then pushing the conversation toward time, affect, regret-like replay, social complexity, and the limits of measurement.

## Role In The Source
Webb's questions make the talk less narrowly technical. She repeatedly connects [[NeuralGeometry]] to broader issues in [[ConsciousnessMeasurement]]: whether artificial systems might experience time differently, whether affect could be detected or generated, and how far spatial-navigation geometry can generalize into social worlds.
