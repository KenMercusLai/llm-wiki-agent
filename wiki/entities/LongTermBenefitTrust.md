# Long-Term Benefit Trust

The Long-Term Benefit Trust is the governance structure [[EricRies]] attributes to [[Anthropic]] in [[eric-ries-incorruptible-by-design-wrgromn5peq]]. Ries treats it as a modern benefit-trust example designed to help an AI company resist [[FinancialGravity]] while facing unusual capital needs and public-risk stakes.

## Key Claims
- The trust is presented as evidence that some frontier AI founders recognize both the promise and danger of their technology.
- Ries says Anthropic's structure has held up "so far," while acknowledging that AI capital demands create unprecedented pressure.
- The trust connects AI safety questions to [[AIAlignmentGovernance]] because model alignment depends partly on how the people and company are governed.
