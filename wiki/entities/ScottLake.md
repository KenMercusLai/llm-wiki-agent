# Scott Lake

Scott Lake is [[TobiasLutke]]'s co-founder in the [[Snowdevil]] and early [[Shopify]] story told in [[shopify-tobias-lutke-how-a-snowboarder-built-a-150-billion-business-2019-d0b07b6a-125e-4896-babd-678d19957306]]. He worked at the local company Toby nearly joined, then partnered with him after Toby learned that Canadian work-permit limits made ordinary employment difficult.

## Key Points
- Handled much of Snowdevil's vendor, inventory, and business setup while Toby focused on technology.
- Helped run Snowdevil as a real retail business rather than only a drop-shipping or software experiment.
- Came up with the name Shopify by combining "shop" and "simplify."
- Held the CEO role before leaving around 2008, after which Toby had to learn the CEO job himself.
