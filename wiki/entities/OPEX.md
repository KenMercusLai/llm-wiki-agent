# OPEX

OPEX is identified in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]] as Iran's oil, gas, and petroleum products exporters union. [[HamidHosseini]], described as an OPEX spokesperson, gives the episode its account of an alleged permission-and-toll process for ships passing through the [[StraitOfHormuz]].

The page is source-scoped because the episode does not build a broader institutional history of OPEX. Its wiki role is to connect Iranian energy-export knowledge to [[MaritimeChokepointTolling]], [[OilProducerSupplyCoordination]], and the shipping consequences of [[IranSanctions]].
