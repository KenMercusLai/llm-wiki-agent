# 内务府 / Imperial Household Department

内务府 / Imperial Household Department is the Qing imperial-household bureaucracy that frames [[ImperialBondservantGovernance]] in [[101-caoyin-yu-kangxi-hongloumeng-xing-zhong-you-shi-735044876]]. The episode explains that early Qing emperors were wary of eunuch power, so household bondservants from the upper three banners took over many palace supply, service, and special-mission functions.

In the source, the department matters because it blurs the modern boundary between public state administration and the emperor's private household. [[CaoYin|曹寅]] and related weaving officials could manage textiles, procurement, salt-linked finance, rare objects, religious errands, and information for [[KangxiEmperor|康熙]] precisely because they belonged to this trusted household system.
