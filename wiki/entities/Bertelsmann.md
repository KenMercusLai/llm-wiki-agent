# Bertelsmann

Bertelsmann appears in [[tsr-ronconway-part4-v2-tsr-ronconway-part4-v2]] as the media company that later invested roughly $100 million in [[Napster]]. [[RonConway]] says the investment saved Napster when there were no backup options, but much of the money went to legal fees rather than product recovery.

The funding day also produced one of the episode's sharpest founder-crisis moments. [[SeanFanning]] wanted to resign and start a licensing company, but Conway told him that the namesake founder leaving on funding day could jeopardize the deal; Conway promised that [[SVAngel]] would later back what became [[Snowcap]].
