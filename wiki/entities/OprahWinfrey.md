# Oprah Winfrey

Oprah Winfrey appears in [[129-ruhe-chengwei-zhenzheng-de-buwanmei-zhuyizhe-hunshen-shi-jiner-799220151]] as a source-scoped example for distinguishing excellence from [[PerfectionismAsAvoidance]]. The episode uses her statement that excellence and perfectionism differ to support its claim that a person can care about quality without making impossible standards a barrier to action.

Her role in this branch is illustrative rather than biographical. The source uses her beside [[JamesCameron]] to show why the target is not low standards, but standards that make completion, learning, and [[ImperfectActionPractice]] impossible.
