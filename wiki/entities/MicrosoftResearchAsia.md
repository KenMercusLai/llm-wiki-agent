# Microsoft Research Asia

Microsoft Research Asia appears in [[momenta-ipo-hou-zai-fang-cao-xu-dong-jiu-shi-xiang-zuo-mei-you-jin-tou-de-ai-1-172-1]] as a formative research environment for [[CaoXudong]]. Cao says he worked in [[SunJian]]'s group there and shifted from a more theoretical and logical style toward paying attention to experiments, phenomena, and results that did not match his expectations.

The source uses MSRA less as an institution history and more as part of Cao's training path into [[Momenta]]: statistical learning, computer vision, experiment discipline, product pressure at [[SenseTime]], and later autonomous-driving commercialization become linked stages rather than separate biographical facts.
