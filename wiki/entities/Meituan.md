# Meituan

Meituan appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as a possible later competitor to [[Ctrip]] in hotel and travel booking. The source places it in the same platform-governance conversation as earlier Chinese antitrust cases around large internet platforms.

The episode does not analyze Meituan's full business. Its role is to show that [[OnlineTravelAgency]] competition can come from adjacent local-services platforms with traffic, payment, merchant relationships, and consumer app frequency.

## Connections
- [[Ctrip]] — travel platform whose position Meituan may pressure.
- [[Fliggy]], [[Douyin]], and [[JDTravel]] — other later potential competitors named by the source.
- [[PlatformAntitrust]], [[TravelSuperAppConvenience]], and [[OTAPlatformConcentration]] — related concepts.
