# Richard Dawkins / 理查德·道金斯

Richard Dawkins appears in [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]] as the writer of the English reissue preface to [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》 / Last Chance to See]]. The episode uses his preface to underline [[DouglasAdams|Douglas Adams]]'s combination of scientific intelligence, imagination, and humor.

His role is mainly framing rather than argumentative. Dawkins's admiration helps the hosts set the book apart from ordinary conservation instruction: Adams's gift was making evolutionary and ecological strangeness funny without making it trivial.

[[95-dushi-chuanshuo-lieqi-gushi-he-women-neixin-shenchu-de-jiaolv-723831611]] adds Dawkins through the concept of the meme. The episode uses meme-style cultural replication to explain how [[UrbanLegend|urban legend]] motifs travel, mutate, and survive without a named author.
