# Kaipai / 开拍

Kaipai is discussed in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as a [[Meitu]] productivity/video app that began from a teleprompter-like function inside [[BeautyCam]]. The episode says the feature's usage data justified spinning it out as an independent app, and that its first-year official revenue was more than 10 million yuan.

For the wiki, Kaipai is a product-discovery case: [[Meitu]] did not only forecast a future AI workflow, it observed a concrete behavior inside an existing app and turned it into a new surface. That makes it part of [[AIApplicationLayerMoat]] and [[VerticalWorkflowAI]] when production work moves from scattered features into repeatable workflows.
