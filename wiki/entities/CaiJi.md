# 蔡姬

蔡姬 is the Cai noblewoman whom [[109-xianliao-zuozhuan-zhi-chunqiu-yundonghui-747508773]] turns into the episode's water-sport nominee. In the source's retelling, she rocks a boat while married to [[QiHuanGong|齐桓公]], frightening and angering him; he sends her back to Cai without fully ending the marriage, and Cai later remarries her.

The episode treats the resulting Qi attack on Cai as a case where domestic insult, marriage status, diplomatic pretext, and larger military strategy blur. Cai Ji is therefore not framed as a major political actor in her own right; she is a person whose body and marriage become usable inside interstate conflict, which connects the episode's humor to the harsher gendered reality of Spring-Autumn diplomacy.
