# MEDICUBE

MEDICUBE is a Korean beauty brand named in [[137-cong-shunde-zhuroupo-dao-hanguo-shengshuidong-naxie-ai-wufa-qudai-de-tiyan-xiaofei-feng-tou-quan-1-137-1]]. The episode uses it to show how Korean beauty can combine cosmetics, beauty devices, and Korean medical-aesthetic associations into [[KBeautyGlobalTrust]].

The wiki relevance is that MEDICUBE makes beauty trust partly technological and clinical. In the episode's framing, Korea's global credibility in beauty is not only celebrity taste; it also includes dermatology, procedures, devices, and a general consumer belief that Korean beauty know-how is advanced.

## Source Position
- MEDICUBE is used as a representative K-Beauty brand rather than a deeply analyzed company.
- The hosts connect it to the broader Korean medical-aesthetic mindshare.
- The brand supports the claim that Korea can export trust in beauty categories more naturally than many Chinese beauty brands can.
