# Stephanie Pesta

Stephanie Pesta appears in [[board-games-3-whats-in-a-name]] as a member of the [[ExplodingKittens]] art team presenting theme and naming options for the [[PlanetMoney]] game. Her pitch includes misfit mascots, [[SellMeASasquatch|Sell Me a Sasquatch]], and cats acting as salespeople, traders, and vendors.

Her role is useful because it shows how [[ProductNamingAsPositioning]] and [[VisualBrandSystem]] begin before final art exists. Each theme is a different way to teach the shopper what kind of game this is and how it should feel.
