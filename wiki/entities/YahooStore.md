# Yahoo Store

Yahoo Store is the post-acquisition ecommerce product context for [[Viaweb]] in [[tsr-s2-trevorblackwell-v2]]. After [[Yahoo]] acquired Viaweb, the source describes [[TrevorBlackwell]] building a prototype ad-buying product for small Yahoo Store merchants who might purchase online ads with credit cards in smaller increments.

The episode uses Yahoo Store to show both the promise and limits of early web commerce inside a large company. The merchant base made self-serve advertising plausible, but the surrounding sales organization and pricing assumptions were not aligned with the smaller, market-like ad model that later became important elsewhere.
