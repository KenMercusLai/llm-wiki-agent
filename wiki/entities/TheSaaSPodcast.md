# The SaaS Podcast

The SaaS Podcast is the show context for [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]], [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], [[eric-ries-on-how-founders-quietly-lose-their-company]], [[ai-startup-hits-8-6m-arr-with-v0-mvp-and-eur85-pricing]], [[finding-product-market-fit-after-3-years-of-failed-ideas]], [[how-danny-jenkins-bootstrapped-threatlocker-from-150k-debt-to-200m]], and [[he-demoted-his-saas-to-sell-a-service-and-4xd-revenue-in-12-months]]. In these episodes, [[OmerKhan]] interviews SaaS founders and startup thinkers about growth, product validation, distribution, funding choices, hiring lessons, AI-era strategy, founder governance, AI-search category formation, compliance automation, cybersecurity category creation, and SaaS-to-service pivots.
