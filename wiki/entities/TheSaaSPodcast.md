# The SaaS Podcast

The SaaS Podcast is the show context for [[community-led-saas-growth-how-ninety-hit-44m-arr]], [[bootstrapped-saas-12m-arr-across-5-products-with-a-team-of-10]], and [[eric-ries-on-how-founders-quietly-lose-their-company]]. In these episodes, Omer Khan interviews SaaS founders and startup thinkers about growth, product validation, distribution, funding choices, hiring lessons, AI-era strategy, and founder governance.
