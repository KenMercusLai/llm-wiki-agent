# Valley of the Dolls

Valley of the Dolls appears in [[how-to-make-a-book-into-a-bestseller]] as [[JacquelineSusann]]'s bestseller and as the episode's example of targeted book-sales strategy. The source says Susann used relationships with booksellers to route purchases through stores believed to matter for the [[NewYorkTimesBestsellerList|New York Times bestseller list]].

The book functions as a case where [[BestsellerListOpacity]] creates tactical behavior. If the list is weighted by a hidden sample of stores, then promotion can become a search for likely reporting channels rather than a simple effort to maximize all sales equally.
