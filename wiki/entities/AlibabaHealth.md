# 阿里健康

Alibaba Health is Alibaba's healthcare and pharmaceutical-commerce vehicle in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode connects it to Alibaba's 2014 acquisition of control over CITIC 21CN, the drug electronic supervision-code asset, and the later transfer of Tmall's online medicine business into Alibaba Health.

In the source, Alibaba Health represents the ecommerce-heavy route through [[InternetHealthcare]]. Its growth depends more on [[PharmaceuticalEcommerce]], online pharmacy policy, prescription-drug boundaries, and traffic integration than on replacing hospitals with pure online diagnosis.
