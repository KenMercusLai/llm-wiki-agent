# PathJoy

PathJoy was [[AdoraCheung]] and [[AaronCheung]]'s early services-marketplace company before it became [[Homejoy]]. In [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]], Cheung says the original idea focused on therapists and life coaches, attracted supply, but did not generate enough customer demand.

The PathJoy story matters because it shows a demand-side failure before Homejoy's supply-side and operations lessons. The team spent roughly two years and many pivots before the home-cleaning direction exposed a problem with stronger consumer demand.
