# TigerEye

TigerEye is the enterprise-software company [[TracyYoung]] and [[RalphGoody]] were building after [[PlanGrid]] in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. The source says the company was still in stealth at the time, so the episode is more useful for its operating philosophy than for product specifics.

TigerEye is presented as a second-time founder experiment in deliberate company design. Young says the company wrote explicit commitments, intends to hire and fire by values, is handpicking people for talent, hard work, and humility, is using a cross-platform codebase to avoid PlanGrid's platform-parity drag, and operates as a 100% remote team with more deliberate communication and in-person offsites.
