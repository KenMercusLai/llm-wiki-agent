# 《遗忘的脑力》

《遗忘的脑力》 is the book discussed in [[143-yiwang-de-naoli-jixing-buhao-ke-zha-zheng-a-869667634]]. The source presents it as a short popular-neuroscience work by an author with both brain-science and clinical background, but the episode summary does not name the author.

The book's wiki role is conceptual rather than bibliographic. In the episode, it supplies the frame for treating forgetting as necessary brain work: memory has to be selected, repeated, emotionally weighted, biologically maintained, and sometimes cleared so that learning, personality, and mental health remain workable.
