# Conservative Partnership Institute

The Conservative Partnership Institute, or CPI, appears in [[vol-113-cong-jiqian-ye-zhiku-wenjian-zhong-goule-telangpu-2-0-zhizheng-jihua-beihou-de-ren-jigou-sixiang-he-mailuo-lihnknc-3om5evexocb6a6zdwueu]] as part of the post-2020 right-wing organizational ecosystem around [[DonaldTrump]]. The episode says CPI gave former Trump officials and conservative staffers a base after the 2020 defeat and helped incubate or connect groups aimed at nominations, elections, state-level conservative factions, and staff training.

The source uses CPI to show that Trump 2.0 is not just a candidate returning to office. It is backed by [[NewRightPolicyNetwork]] capacity: offices, jobs, training, activists, and personnel pipelines that can survive between election cycles.
