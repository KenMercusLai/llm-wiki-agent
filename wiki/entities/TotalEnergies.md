# TotalEnergies

TotalEnergies appears in [[roaring-trades-oil-majors-secret-success-story-6a4636f160cad2674e6d9674]] as one of the European majors whose energy-trading arm is treated as a large, opaque profit source. The episode places it beside [[BP]] and [[Shell]] in a group estimated to trade tens of millions of barrels per day of oil and gas, far above their own production volumes.

For the wiki, TotalEnergies helps show that an oil major can be a market intermediary as well as a producer. Its role belongs to [[EnergyTradingScaleAdvantage]], where asset ownership, physical logistics, and global information can become trading edge.
