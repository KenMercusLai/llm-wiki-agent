# Medallion Fund

The Medallion Fund is the [[RenaissanceTechnologies]] fund used in [[ep88-chuanyue-lianghua-zhifu-ximengsi-ai-hui-rang-putongren-geng-rongyi-zhuanqian-haishi-geng-nan-lhvigzza2ugmayezkbrxufkmp4l1]] to illustrate the power and limits of [[QuantitativeInvesting]]. The episode presents its returns as the result of many weak, repeated, low-correlation signals rather than a few high-conviction company calls.

## Key Claims
- The fund's edge is compared to casino logic: small probabilistic advantages become powerful when repeated many times with disciplined sizing.
- Its example makes ordinary retail copying unrealistic because the fund depends on proprietary data, compute, talent, execution, and organizational design.
- The fund also illustrates why capacity matters: an edge can disappear if too much capital chases it or if the trader's own activity changes the market.
