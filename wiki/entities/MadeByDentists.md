# Made by Dentists

Made by Dentists is the oral-care brand Tom Rinks is working on at the end of [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source says the brand was founded by two dentist sisters from Ireland and that Tom redesigned it for the U.S. market.

The episode uses Made by Dentists as evidence that [[TomRinks]] still treats brands as creative projects after [[SunBum]]. The children's toothpaste flavor names, including Alien Blood and Eyeball Juice, fit his pattern of making everyday products emotionally specific and memorable.
