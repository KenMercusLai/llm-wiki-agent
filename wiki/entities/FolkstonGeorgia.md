# Folkston, Georgia

Folkston, Georgia is the local case in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]]. The source describes it as a rural community of nearly 5,000 people, mostly Black, with about one-third of residents living below the poverty line and one of the largest ICE detention facilities in the United States.

The episode uses Folkston to show how [[PrivateImmigrationDetention]] becomes a development strategy when a town needs jobs, hospital access, emergency medical care, and public revenue. The same reporting also surfaces moral tension when detainees can be heard describing poor treatment from behind barbed wire.
