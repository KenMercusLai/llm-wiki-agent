# Homer

Homer is the ancient epic poet invoked in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] as the source tradition behind [[TheOdyssey]]. The episode uses Homer less as a biographical subject than as a test of [[ClassicReadingComplexity]]: a famous text can remain alive precisely because later periods keep remaking it.

The source places [[ChristopherNolan]]'s version in a long chain of reinterpretations, from ancient tragedy to Enlightenment translation, modernist literature, and contemporary film debate. That makes Homer central to [[HomericAdaptationModernization]], where adaptation reveals the receiving culture as much as the ancient work.
