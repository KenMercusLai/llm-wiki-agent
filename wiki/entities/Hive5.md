# Hive 5

Hive 5 is the moisturizer product from [[IslandBeeCompany]] discussed in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. [[FelixCollin]] says it uses five hive ingredients: raw honey, beeswax, propolis, pollen, and royal jelly.

In the episode, Hive 5 matters because it gives Island Bee a product story that is less commodity-like than raw honey alone. That makes it a potential bridge from a place-based apiary into broader consumer-product distribution, but only if the brand can explain the product clearly and scale production without breaking the family business.
