# Chance Weldon

Chance Weldon appears in [[its-my-tree-why-cant-i-cut-it-down]] as litigation director at the [[TexasPublicPolicyFoundation]]. He became interested in the [[CantonMichigan]] tree case after local reporting on the township's large remediation demand.

Weldon's argument turns the episode from a local-tree story into a constitutional property-rights case. He first framed severe tree restrictions as close to forced occupation of private property, then advanced a narrower [[PermitProportionality]] argument that Canton's standardized fee system was not sufficiently tied to each tree's specific public value or harm.
