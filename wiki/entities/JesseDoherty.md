# Jesse Doherty

Jesse Doherty appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the [[WashingtonPost|Washington Post]] journalist whose article about the Microsoft Excel Collegiate Competition becomes [[JessJang]]'s Valentine.

The source values Doherty's article because it treats spreadsheet competitors with sports-journalism seriousness: semifinals, finals, screens projected for spectators, and spreadsheet puzzles become an event people can follow. In the wiki, Doherty's role is to make [[CompetitiveSpreadsheeting]] legible as performance rather than only technical office labor.
