# Signature Bank

Signature Bank appears in [[socialradarsseason2-dimitri-final]] during the March banking crisis discussion. After [[SiliconValleyBank]] was shut down, [[DimitriDadiomov]] says [[ModernTreasury]] also created status information for Signature Bank when it entered receivership that Sunday night.

The source uses Signature Bank as evidence that startup and fintech banking risk was not confined to a single institution. For Modern Treasury customers, the second receivership widened the need for [[FinancialOperationsResilience]], cross-bank visibility, and backup payment flows.
