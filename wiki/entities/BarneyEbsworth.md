# Barney Ebsworth

Barney Ebsworth appears in [[build-a-bear-maxine-clark-a-former-shoe-executive-launches-a-stuffed-animal-empire-8e5da7d7-4e2b-4978-b625-f72cb5d57d88]] as the local entrepreneur and investor who contacted [[MaxineClark]] after reading about [[BuildABear]]. The host says Barney invested several million dollars and held roughly a 20% stake.

His role in the wiki is an early-capital example where investor interest followed visible [[CustomerPull]] and a concrete customer experience rather than only a spreadsheet. The episode emphasizes that Barney understood service and customer experience, which made him a better fit for an [[ExperientialRetail]] company.
