# Komodo Dragon / 科莫多巨蜥

The Komodo dragon is the focal species in the Indonesia section of [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. The hosts retell the setup through rumors of "dragons", a poison expert's deadpan advice, broken travel arrangements, and the idea that a dead goat would attract the animals by smell.

The segment becomes more uncomfortable when [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] reach [[KomodoIsland]] and find tourist infrastructure, a welcome sign, and feeding spectacle rather than untouched wilderness. That makes the Komodo dragon a key case for [[WildlifeTourismSpectacle]]: rare animals can be protected, marketed, feared, and staged at the same time.
