# FINRA Foundation

FINRA Foundation appears in [[tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the organization behind the financial-knowledge survey trend that prompted the episode's methodological question. The episode says its surveys showed a 15% decline in U.S. personal-finance knowledge from 2009 to 2021.

The source does not treat the decline as automatically false. Instead, it uses [[NationalFinancialCapabilityStudy]] device data to show why [[FinancialLiteracyMeasurement]] needs to account for [[SurveyModeEffects]] before the trend becomes a policy conclusion.
