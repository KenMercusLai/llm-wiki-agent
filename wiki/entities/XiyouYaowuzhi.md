# 《西游妖物志》

《西游妖物志》 is recommended in [[43-xiyouji-duo-ni-shi-shenme-yaojing-618154182]] as a light but useful guide to the source histories of [[JourneyToTheWest|《西游记》]] characters and monsters. The episode uses it especially for [[ZhuBajie|猪八戒]], whose appearance in the取经 tradition is treated as later and more layered than popular memory suggests.

In the wiki, the book functions as a secondary-source signal for [[MythicSourceLayering]]. It helps the episode move from television familiarity toward older images, zaju, religious iconography, folk motifs, and monster genealogies without pretending that every connection is final proof.
