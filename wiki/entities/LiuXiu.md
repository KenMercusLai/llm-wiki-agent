# Liu Xiu / 刘秀

Liu Xiu / 刘秀 appears in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] as the Liu restoration figure whose victory at the [[KunyangBattle|Battle of Kunyang]] helped expose the [[XinDynasty|Xin dynasty]]'s collapse. The episode contrasts Wang Mang's huge and strange-looking forces with Liu Xiu's battlefield survival and the unraveling of Wang Mang's political reality.

The source also uses Liu Xiu to complete the omen-politics arc. It says Liu Xiu also believed in mandate and prophecy, but later imperial power learned to control [[ChenweiPolitics|谶纬]] interpretation more tightly, preventing the free-for-all omen production that had aided [[WangMang|王莽]].

## Key Claims
- Liu Xiu functions as Wang Mang's successful Liu-house counterpoint.
- Kunyang shows the gap between ritualized state grandeur and political-military effectiveness.
- The later handling of prophecy shows that Wang Mang's tools were not abandoned so much as recentralized.
