# 大老师 / Dalaoshi

大老师 is the [[QingdaoKuaima|轻刀快马]]-side speaker in [[vol-102-aoguo-jiuye-bingheqi-de-riben-nianqingren-qu-nali-xunzhao-xingfugan-chuantai-qingdao-kuaima-lmpluij7rcfq49cxcyb65x3f7zfn]]. The episode uses his Japan-based observations to discuss inflation, [[NISA]], ordinary attitudes toward investing, shame culture, company belonging, concerts, hidden youth, and everyday practices of calm.

His role in the source is comparative rather than predictive. He repeatedly treats [[Japan]] as culturally and historically different from [[China]], while still using Japanese examples to ask how people live after confidence and expansion narratives weaken.
