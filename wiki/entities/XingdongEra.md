# Xingdong Era

Xingdong Era, or 星动纪元, is discussed in [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] as a Chinese humanoid-robot company testing logistics sorting with China Post and SF Express-like scenarios. The source says Xingdong Era has trained and tested full-autonomous sorting, flipping, scanning, and package handling workflows, making it a China-side counterpart to [[FigureAI]]'s logistics demo.
