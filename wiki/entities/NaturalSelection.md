# Natural Selection / 自然选择

Natural Selection is the AI product company founded by [[Tristan]] and discussed in [[135-he-ziran-xuanze-chuangshiren-tristan-liao-elys-saibo-fenshen-linghun-context-de-huoqu-yu-liudong-he-ai-shejiao-wangluo-ltwegwvo7grn-v-rft0txlmqmcty]]. The source says the company began around early 2024, first exploring a Project Her-style companion agent and later building both [[EVE]] and [[Elys]].

The company is framed less as a foundation-model lab than as an [[AINativeProductDesign]] organization. [[Tristan]] says Natural Selection will not pretrain a base model, but will use product design, post-tuning, smaller model adjustments, emotional reasoning patterns, memory systems, and context flow to build AI companion and social experiences.

## Key Points
- Its stated emotional center is human loneliness and the desire for better connection, even when the product language talks about silicon life and AI coexistence.
- [[EVE]] is the companion/game side, where AI personas, memory, and interaction create virtual emotional value.
- [[Elys]] is the social-network side, where [[CyberAvatars]] and [[ContextFlywheel]] try to turn user context into real-world connection.
- The company culture is described as first-principles-oriented, ambitious, and "gentle" in its concern for human emotional value.
