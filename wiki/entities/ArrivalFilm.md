# Arrival

[[ArrivalFilm|《降临》 / Arrival]] is the film adaptation used in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]] as a route back to [[TedChiang|Ted Chiang / 江峰南]]'s [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]]. The episode credits the film with making the story familiar to many Chinese readers while emphasizing that the film adds crisis, geopolitics, and commercial narrative pressure.

The contrast matters because the original story's center is quieter: [[LouiseBanks|Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯]]'s encounter with [[Heptapods|heptapods / 七肢桶]] reorganizes language, time, and love rather than turning alien contact primarily into a conflict plot.
