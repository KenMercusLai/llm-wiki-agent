# Pony Ma

Pony Ma appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as [[Tencent]]'s founder and the direct counterpart to [[ZhangYiming]] in the 2018 WeChat Moments exchange that helped ignite the [[ToutengWar]]. The episode treats his "defamation" reply as a public spark that escalated a conflict already shaped by short-video competition, youth-content safety, and WeChat blocking disputes.

The source also uses Pony Ma to represent Tencent's institutional self-image: Tencent saw itself as the operator of [[WeChat]], [[QQ]], games, IP, and long-lived user relationships, not only as a traffic distributor. That helps explain why ByteDance's fast-moving recommendation and growth systems felt like more than a single-product threat.
