# Vikrant Lal

Vikrant Lal appears in [[the-latest-tv-innovations-have-their-critics]] as another viewer who prefers older plasma display behavior to newer TV motion presentation. The source says he argues that his 15-year-old plasma TV looks and sounds better to him than current models.

His contribution is to show that [[PlasmaTVPreference]] is not only nostalgia or price sensitivity. In the episode, it is tied to the way older displays handle film motion and to the viewer's perception that modern sharpness can make cinematic movement feel unnatural.
