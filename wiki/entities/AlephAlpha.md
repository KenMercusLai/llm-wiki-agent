# Aleph Alpha

Aleph Alpha is the German AI company mentioned in [[biancheng-de-neiranji-shidai-neihe-konghuang-71-1-71-1]]. The hosts bring it up while asking why Europe appears to have fewer high-profile AI companies than the U.S. or China.

## Source Position
- Aleph Alpha is used as a visible European AI example, not as a deep company case study.
- The episode mentions its reaction to [[DeepSeek]] and frames the moment as evidence that DeepSeek's impact mattered even if some observers did not view it as a purely groundbreaking technical breakthrough.
- The company supports the source's [[EuropeanAIIndustrialConstraints]] concept: frontier AI competition requires capital, compute, product markets, talent, and willingness to spend heavily.
