# Midjourney

Midjourney is the AI image-generation tool named in [[e234-weilai-shipai-dianying-hai-cunzai-ma-yu-daoyan-luchuan-liaoliao-ai-gei-yingshiren-de-kongju-yu-ziyou-b2be7093-3366-4ee2-8a7a-625f06206ae5]]. [[KESA]] used it to make images while iterating the AI short 《加班夜》, and [[LuChuan]] discusses Midjourney-like workflows as a way to create large numbers of character, scene, and action keyframes quickly.

In this source, Midjourney matters as a previsualization and ideation tool more than as a final-film system. It helps creators see and revise ideas cheaply, but it also exposes the limits that lead Lu Chuan toward [[IndustrialGradeFilmModels]]: stable style, professional delivery, and film-level aesthetic control require more than a large pile of attractive images.
