# 重启人生 / Brush Up Life

重启人生, referenced through the Japanese drama premise in [[ritan-chuantai-jiemei-liaotianhui-chongqi-rensheng-yeshi-wo-zui-yaojiao-616116425]], provides the episode's organizing thought experiment. [[Hookie]] introduces the setup: a woman dies at 33 and is offered a choice between another life path and reliving the current one with memory.

The source does not mainly analyze the drama as television. It uses the premise to ask what memories, mistakes, losses, and choices the hosts would revisit. That makes the drama a prompt for [[LifeRestartThoughtExperiment]], [[RegretIntegration]], and [[FemaleSelfPossession]].
