# eBird

eBird is the bird-observation platform referenced in [[ganguan-fangda-shijie-he-renning-liao-guanniao-ziran-yu-ziyou-e583dac2-bad8-4208-8d35-0c3de8594779]] as an example of how birdwatchers can turn personal lists into [[CitizenScience]] data. The episode describes bird lists as records of place, date, time, species, and quantity, sometimes extended with behavior, habitat, and writerly detail.

In the source, eBird also represents the game-like side of observation platforms: rankings, badges, and reviewer checks can motivate participation while requiring evidence for unusual records. That makes it a bridge between [[BirdwatchingAsAttention]] and formal research infrastructure.

- [[CitizenScience]] - core data-contribution pattern.
