# Blue Dot

Blue Dot appears in [[tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1-tsr-s3-davidrusenko-v1]] as a [[LeapForward]] portfolio company. [[DavidRusenko]] describes it as offering a rewards card for EV charging and helping small fleets manage charging reimbursement through car APIs.

The company is used as a concrete example of [[EconomicClimateTechAdoption]]. Its climate relevance comes through electric-vehicle charging, but the customer value is practical finance and workflow: rewards, reimbursement, and fleet expense handling.
