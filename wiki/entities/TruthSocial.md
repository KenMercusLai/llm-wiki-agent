# Truth Social

Truth Social is the social platform discussed in [[ep77-si-shi-wan-nian-xin-fu-ye-zhuan-le-san-shi-si-yi-te-lang-pu-jiao-ni-ruhe-gao-qian-lnrx8vkdjedxbmmcwxlnwow-pcab]] as the operating asset behind [[TrumpMediaAndTechnologyGroup]]. The episode says [[DonaldTrump]] launched it after being removed from major social platforms, then used a SPAC route to take the business public under DJT.

## Source Position
- The source frames Truth Social's market value as coming primarily from Donald Trump's political identity and audience rather than ordinary social-media operating strength.
- The hosts cite low daily activity, small revenue, large losses, and weak profit visibility to argue that its quoted market value should be treated through [[PoliticalMemeStock]] and [[PaperWealthVsCashValue]].
- Trump's own posts on the platform are treated as market-moving events, connecting Truth Social to [[PolicyAnnouncementTradingRisk]].
