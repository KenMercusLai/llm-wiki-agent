# MIUI

MIUI is [[Xiaomi]]'s early Android-based software system in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode treats MIUI as the proof of Xiaomi's "users before phones" path: localization, interface polish, weekly updates, and enthusiast feedback gave Xiaomi a community and demand signal before it had its own handset.

In the source, MIUI makes [[InternetPhoneModel]] more than online sales. It turned software iteration and community identity into leverage when Xiaomi later negotiated hardware supply and launched the Xiaomi 1.
