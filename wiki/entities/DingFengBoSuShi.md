# 《定风波》 / Ding Feng Bo

《定风波》 appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as the key text for interpreting [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s resilience. The episode quotes and discusses "莫听穿林打叶声，何妨吟啸且徐行", but rejects the idea that the line means ignoring the world.

The source reads the poem through [[EarnedOptimism]]. "莫听" means the wind and rain have already been heard, but are not allowed to occupy the whole heart. That makes the poem a concentrated version of the episode's larger claim about exile, fear, grief, and continued action.
