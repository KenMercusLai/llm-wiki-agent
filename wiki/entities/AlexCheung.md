# Alex Cheung

Alex Cheung is [[AdoraCheung]]'s brother and current co-founder at [[Instalab]], as mentioned in [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]]. The episode does not give a detailed biography, but it places him in the same family-founder pattern as [[AaronCheung]] and the earlier [[Homejoy]] story.

His wiki relevance is mainly relational: Instalab is the company where Adora applies lessons from Homejoy to a higher-trust healthcare service, including full-time employment for phlebotomists and a more controlled patient experience.
