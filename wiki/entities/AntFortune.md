# 蚂蚁财富 / Ant Fortune

Ant Fortune / 蚂蚁财富 is the wealth-management platform context in [[ep268-mao-dong-x-tang-tang-da-huang-ni-daodi-za-le-shangcuan-xiatiao-de-huangjin-he-naxie-juebu-xiache-de-ren-lgujs-q24fzqcuutibty8k0bowbv]], which is presented as a [[TalkSanlian|Talk三联]] episode jointly launched with the platform's "合盘托出" project. The source uses it to explain how online platforms changed gold buying from a high-friction physical purchase into small, recurring, account-based accumulation.

The episode's Ant Fortune branch centers on [[OnlineGoldAccumulation]]: one-yuan starts, automatic recurring purchases, virtual gram balances, community posting, and eventual redemption into bars or jewelry. This makes the platform relevant to [[FinancialPlatformIncentives]] and [[InvestmentRiskManagement]], because a friendlier user experience can support discipline but can also make buying feel game-like.
