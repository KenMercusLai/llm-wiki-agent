# Kristin Schwab

Kristin Schwab reports the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode [[tech-20260120-0120-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260120-0120-mp-tech-pod-128]] on [[SurveillancePricing]]. Her reporting turns an abstract pricing and privacy issue into a concrete consumer test by comparing a signed-in and anonymous Walmart.com shopping session for the same toothpaste and shipping address.

The source uses Schwab's Walmart example carefully. The price difference makes retail pricing opacity visible, but Schwab does not claim that the test proves personalized pricing; [[Walmart]] offers real-time price changes and market variation as alternative explanations.
