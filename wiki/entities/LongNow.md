# Long Now

Long Now is the host context for [[indy-johar-civilizational-optioneering-tyeyt7r-zfu]], where [[IndyJohar]] presents a talk on civilizational optioneering. The source frames Long Now thinking as needing revision under conditions of climate breakdown, ecological collapse, geopolitical fragmentation, general-purpose technology races, and weakening social contracts.

The talk challenges a preservation-only version of long-termism. Instead, it asks what should be maintained so that a self-aware planet retains many possible futures. That makes [[PlanetarySelfAwareness]] a bridge between the wiki's space, AI, governance, and ecology themes rather than only a time-horizon institution.
