# Malta

Malta appears in [[the-leaked-tapes-that-show-how-the-rich-avoid-taxes]] as the treaty counterparty and retirement-account jurisdiction behind the [[MaltaTaxLoophole]]. The episode emphasizes the mismatch between U.S. Roth IRA restrictions and Maltese retirement structures that, in the source's account, allowed much broader contributions and asset types.

The source does not present Malta as a general biography of the country. It uses Malta as an example of how a small jurisdiction's retirement-account rules can become globally consequential when paired with the [[UnitedStates]] tax system and a bilateral treaty.

## Source Position
- Malta's retirement-account rules are central to the source's explanation of the loophole.
- Late-2021 clarification by U.S. and Maltese officials is presented as narrowing the treaty interpretation.
- The episode frames Malta as a jurisdictional node in [[TaxTreatyArbitrage]], not only as a place.
