# Chen Congzhou / 陈从周

Chen Congzhou is the architectural historian and conservation figure cited in [[no-209-jinshang-wangshi-zou-xikou-dao-qiaojia-dayuan-ranhou-mei-le-999367322]]. The episode uses his phrase "老城老到底，新城新到家" as the planning principle behind [[PingyaoAncientCity]] preservation: keep the old city genuinely old while allowing new development to happen elsewhere.

The source uses Chen's principle to distinguish preservation from decorative nostalgia. A heritage city cannot survive if old buildings are selectively replaced while tourism branding pretends continuity; it needs a spatial plan that protects the old fabric and moves incompatible modern functions outside it.

## Key Claims
- Chen's phrase gives the episode a compact rule for preserving old urban fabric without freezing all development.
- The principle supports [[RuanYisan]]'s planning intervention around Pingyao.
- It helps connect architectural preservation to later [[HeritageTourismCommercialization]] without treating tourism as the original reason to preserve.
