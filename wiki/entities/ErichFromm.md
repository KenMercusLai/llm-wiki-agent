# Erich Fromm

Erich Fromm appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] through [[OnDisobedience|《论不服从》 / On Disobedience]]. The hosts use him as the theoretical foil for [[TheArtOfInsubordination|《不服从的艺术》]]: Fromm's work is framed as a more philosophical and moral account of obedience, while the episode's main book is valued for practical methods.

The source does not develop Fromm's broader intellectual biography. Its wiki role is to anchor the episode's distinction between understanding obedience as a human and social problem, and learning how to dissent with strategy, evidence, and courage.
