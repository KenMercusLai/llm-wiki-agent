# Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams

Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams is the ice cream company founded by [[JeniBritton]] and used as the operating backdrop for [[advice-line-with-jeni-britton-of-jenis-splendid-ice-creams-2025-6dadc048-8cec-44ec-a3cf-ec314a00e41e]]. The source describes the company as having at least 80 scoop shops and about 12,000 retail locations.

The episode uses Jeni's less as a founder-origin story than as practical evidence for CPG advice: small-city experimentation, product quality, tasting, retail buyer logic, founder control, and customer language all show up in Britton's answers to caller problems.
