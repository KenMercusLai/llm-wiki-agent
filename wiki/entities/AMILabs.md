# AMI Labs

AMI Labs is the company presented in [[133-dui-xie-saining-de-7-xiaoshi-ma-la-song-fangtan-shijie-moxing-taochu-guigu-ami-labs-liangci-jujue-ilya-yang-likun-li-feifei-he-42]] as the world-model startup being built by [[XieSaining]] and [[YannLeCun]]. The source also refers to Neolab/AMI and says the initial organization had about twenty-five people, with Paris as headquarters and offices in New York, Montreal, and Singapore.

## Strategy
The source describes AMI as pursuing [[WorldModels]] outside the dominant LLM-centered Silicon Valley story. Xie frames the company as a "reverse [[OpenAI]]": rather than only training on downloaded internet data and then pushing a model outward, AMI wants to work with real-world partners that already have physical problems, data, and deployment contexts.

## Organization
AMI is not presented as a non-profit or pure research lab. The source says it needs a business model, but also wants to preserve research culture, give young researchers visibility, and avoid making them interchangeable parts inside a huge closed machine. This makes the company a case for [[AIOrganizationDesign]] and [[DecentralizedWorldModelStrategy]].
