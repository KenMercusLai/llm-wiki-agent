# Ken Chenault

Ken Chenault appears in [[socialradarspod-brianchesky2-final]] as an [[Airbnb]] board member during the COVID crisis. [[BrianChesky]] says Chenault warned that the travel shock could be much worse than 9/11 or 2008 for the industry, helping frame the moment as an existential crisis rather than a temporary slowdown.

In the source, Chenault's role is not detailed operational execution. His importance is crisis framing: he helped push Chesky toward urgent decisions about cash, communication, stakeholder tradeoffs, financing, and company focus.
