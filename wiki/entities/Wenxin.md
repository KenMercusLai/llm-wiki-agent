# Wenxin

Wenxin is [[Baidu]]'s AI model and product family discussed in [[dang-yinchaoji-baidu-kaishi-shixue-shi-tianzai-haishi-renhuo-keji-luandun]]. The hosts use it to illustrate Baidu's "early to AI, late to product mindshare" problem: Baidu had long talked about all-in AI, but the hosts say their own daily AI usage goes to products such as [[Doubao]] and [[Yuanbao]] rather than Wenxin.

The episode does not argue that a closed-source or paid route is automatically wrong. Its narrower claim is that the route only works if the product and model become strong enough to attract users, talent, capital confidence, and revenue, making Wenxin part of the source's [[AICommercializationPressure]] story.
