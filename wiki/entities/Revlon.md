# Revlon

Revlon appears in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]] in two roles. [[JoeyShamah]] names it among the mass cosmetics competitors that controlled shelf space, and later says Revlon reached out before the strategic-buyer process that included [[LOreal]].

The episode uses Revlon as part of the incumbent beauty context that [[ELFCosmetics]] had to bypass through low price, PR, e-commerce, and retailer proof.

[[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]] adds an earlier incumbent-entry case. In that episode, [[GeorgeEJohnson]] says [[JohnsonProducts]]' public reports helped larger white-owned companies understand the profitability of Black hair care, and Revlon later created its own straightener. The source uses Revlon to show how a market proven by a community-embedded founder company can become legible to larger competitors.
