# Josie Dallap

Josie Dallap is the [[TheIntelligence]] contributor whose analysis anchors the Israel segment of [[youve-come-a-long-way-bibi-israels-crucial-election-6a59f9ae461a6a41901ae410]]. She frames [[IsraeliElection2026]] as a post-October-7 judgment on [[BenjaminNetanyahu]], wartime outcomes, coalition politics, and unresolved national questions.

Dallap's main source contribution is prioritization. She argues that [[HarediConscription]], education, the economy, and coalition arithmetic may dominate the campaign even though the [[PalestinianQuestionInIsraeliPolitics]] remains the deeper strategic conflict.
