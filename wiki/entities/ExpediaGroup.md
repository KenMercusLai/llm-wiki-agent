# Expedia Group

Expedia Group appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] in two roles: as an investor context for [[Elong]] and as one of the global online-travel giants compared with [[Ctrip]] and [[BookingHoldings]].

The source does not make Expedia a detailed case. It uses the company to place China's OTA history inside a broader global platform category where hotel supply, travel search, booking flow, and app habits can concentrate power.
