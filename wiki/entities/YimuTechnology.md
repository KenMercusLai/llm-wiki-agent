# Yimu Technology / 一目科技

Yimu Technology is the tactile-sensing company discussed in [[cong-hui-tiaowu-dao-you-ganzhi-chujue-shi-jiqiren-tongwang-zhineng-de-menpiao-ma-s10e19-f448a656-3004-430b-a853-79d1e77dcb53]]. Founder and CEO [[EricLiZhiqiang]] presents the company as a physical access-layer infrastructure provider for [[EmbodiedAI]], focused on [[TactileSensing]] rather than building a full robot brain or full robot body.

The episode says Yimu's differentiation comes from combining chips, optical design, materials, simulation, algorithms, and data. Its [[OpticalTactileSensing]] route uses a thin photonic sensing stack and soft durable materials to turn skin deformation into force, texture, friction, and slip signals. The company also discusses opening simulation tools, contributing tactile data, and building [[TouchNet]] as an [[ImageNet]]-like dataset for touch.
