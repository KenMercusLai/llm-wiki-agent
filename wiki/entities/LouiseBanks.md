# Louise Banks

Louise Banks / 路易斯·班克斯 is the linguist protagonist of [[StoryOfYourLife|《你一生的故事》]] in [[104-ni-yisheng-de-gushi-zhongdian-yijing-zhuding-haiyao-chufa-ma-739384680]]. The episode follows her from ordinary language analysis into a transformed cognition shaped by [[HeptapodB]].

Louise's importance is both intellectual and emotional. She works with [[GaryDonnelly|Gary Donnelly / 盖雷·唐纳利]] to understand [[Heptapods|heptapods / 七肢桶]], but the result is not simply translation. Learning the written system gives her access to [[NonlinearTimePerception]], including foreknowledge of her future marriage, divorce, and daughter's death.
