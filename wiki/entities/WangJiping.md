# Wang Jiping

Wang Jiping is the [[ChengmaiTechnology]] founder and early [[TongxinSoftware]] chair discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode describes him as a software engineer who returned from the United States and built Chengmai around mobile software, hardware adaptation, and technical services.

## Source Position
- Wang is presented as the entrepreneur who helped combine Chengmai-related assets with the Deepin side to form Tongxin.
- He is described as Tongxin chair from its formation until around 2023.
- The source contrasts his founder-operator style with [[LinWei]]'s later investor or professional-manager style.
