# Dali Container Ship

The Dali container ship enters the wiki through [[tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128]], where the episode says it struck Baltimore's [[FrancisScottKeyBridge]] and caused the bridge to collapse.

In this source, Dali is not analyzed as a shipping company or vessel-history case. Its relevance is as the triggering event for a public-infrastructure rebuild that now emphasizes [[StructuralHealthMonitoring]], [[SensorDrivenInfrastructureMaintenance]], and [[BridgeLoadCapacity]] around the [[PortOfBaltimore]].
