# Tim Draper

Tim Draper appears in [[tsr-s4-ericm-v2-tsr-s4-ericm-v2]] as one of the investors [[EricMigicovsky]] raised from after [[YCombinator]] Demo Day. In the episode, this funding belongs to the early [[Alerta]] and [[ImpulseWatch|Impulse]] period before [[Pebble]]'s [[Kickstarter]] breakout.

The source uses the financing as a caution rather than a victory lap. Eric says the money contributed to bad decisions, including ordering one to two thousand early watches from a contract manufacturer before demand and platform durability were proven.
