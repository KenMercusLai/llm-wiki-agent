# Felix Collin

Felix Collin is the [[IslandBeeCompany]] operator and caller in [[advice-line-with-ronnen-harary-of-spin-master-paw-patrol-3894a69a-a4de-4bd8-b5f5-d3bc195994d5]]. He is helping steward a three-generation apiary on Martha's Vineyard while deciding how much growth the family business should pursue.

Felix's question is whether to focus on relationship-driven B2B opportunities or direct-to-consumer channels such as social commerce. His answer that he wants sustainable growth and does not want to sell makes the case less about channel tactics alone and more about [[FamilyBusinessScaling]]: growth has to fit family capacity, production reality, and the desired future identity of the company.
