# 杜继彦

杜继彦 is the public-relations lead discussed in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] for [[AnneSanitaryNapkin|安妮卫生巾]]. The episode highlights his unusual field research: wearing menstrual-support garments to experience discomfort, investigating used absorbent cotton, and trying to understand the disposal and embarrassment problems surrounding menstruation.

The source uses him as a useful but limited male-empathy case. He recognized real pain and shame, yet his fixation on making used products disappear into the sewer could miss what women testers prioritized: comfort, lightness, safety, fit, portability, and discretion. That makes his story a caution inside [[CommercialMenstrualEducation]] and [[PackagingAsProductExperience]].
