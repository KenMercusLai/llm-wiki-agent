# Henry Harteveldt

Henry Harteveldt is the airline industry analyst cited in [[spirit-airlines-and-the-future-of-cheap-flights]] on the post-pandemic pressure facing [[SpiritAirlines|Spirit Airlines]] and other budget carriers. The source uses him to explain why higher fuel, materials, labor, and pilot costs are especially hard for airlines whose core offer is cheap fares.

Harteveldt's segment adds the demand side of [[BudgetAirlineCostSqueeze]]. He says even some relatively high-earning travelers reduced leisure flying or skipped air travel, weakening the price-sensitive customer base that budget airlines depend on.
