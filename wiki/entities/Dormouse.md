# Dormouse / 睡鼠

The Dormouse is the sleepy third figure at the mad tea party in [[180-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-xia-975539611]]. The episode notes both the comic treatment of the character, including being pushed into the teapot, and the period detail that children could keep dormice as pets in teapots.

The Dormouse's "M" story becomes another example of [[WonderlandLanguageGames]]: the point is not ordinary plot completion but the way sound, letter category, interruption, and dream association keep the scene moving. The character therefore helps make the tea party a small [[TimeStasisSystem]] rather than a normal social gathering.

## Key Claims
- The Dormouse joins creature comedy to Victorian domestic detail.
- His story shows how alphabetic constraint can replace ordinary plot causality.
- The character helps the tea-party scene feel circular, drowsy, and unrecoverable.
