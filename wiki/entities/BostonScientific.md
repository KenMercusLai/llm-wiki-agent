# Boston Scientific

Boston Scientific appears in [[tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3-tsr-s4-surbhisarna-v3]] as the acquirer of [[NVisionMedical|nVision Medical]]. In [[SurbhiSarna]]'s account, the company became the trusted strategic buyer after nVision had FDA clearances, clinical data, and growing interest from multiple larger medical-device companies.

The source treats the acquisition as a [[MissionDrivenAcquisitionDecision]] rather than only a liquidity event. Sarna says she had declined earlier offers because she wanted patient cancer data, then later weighed whether Boston Scientific's team, culture, and commercialization capacity could carry the product further than another difficult independent financing round.

The post-acquisition section connects Boston Scientific to [[PostAcquisitionFounderIdentity]]. Sarna stayed on and helped with hospital preselling and commercialization work, but COVID disrupted the planned April 2020 launch after preparations in Boston earlier that year.
