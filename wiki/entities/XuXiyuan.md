# 徐熙元

徐熙元 appears in [[130-tuokaerqiuke-guaidan-gushiji-xunzhao-he-gaobie-803543569]] as the person to whom the host dedicates the episode's final reading of 〈变形中心〉 from [[BizarreStories|《怪诞故事集》]]. The source does not build a biography; it uses her as the emotional endpoint of a literary act of searching and farewell.

The host says she wants to dedicate the story to 徐熙元 because she sees her as someone who, from a young age, chose to become a wolf and truly became a gentle, beautiful "mother wolf." In the wiki, that makes 徐熙元 part of the [[TransformationAsExit]] branch: the point is not factual zoology, but a metaphor for agency, tenderness, and a form of departure others can witness but not control.

## Key Claims
- 徐熙元 functions as the episode's dedicatee and emotional addressee.
- The "mother wolf" image links personal mourning to the story's broader questions about chosen form, family witnessing, and acceptance.
- The source's title pairing of "寻找" and "告别" becomes concrete through this dedication.
