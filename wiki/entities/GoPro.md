# GoPro

GoPro appears in [[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]] as the action-camera pioneer that [[DJI]] overtook. The episode uses GoPro as a hardware-cycle warning: a company can create or dominate a category, but once product iteration, supply chain, software, and ecosystem momentum fall behind, market-share loss can become nonlinear.

In the source's account, GoPro still matters because it defined the action-camera use case around extreme sports and first-person recording. DJI's challenge was not to invent action cameras from nothing, but to use stronger hardware execution and brand momentum to take share inside [[PortableCreatorCameras]] after the category had become mature enough for mainstream creators.
