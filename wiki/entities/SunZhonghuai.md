# 孙忠怀 / Sun Zhonghuai

Sun Zhonghuai is the [[TencentVideo]] executive discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode credits his period with Tencent Video's turn toward large-scale rights acquisition, self-produced drama, self-produced variety, and a push to become a top-two long-video platform.

For the wiki, he anchors the organization-and-funding route of Chinese long video: [[Tencent]] could spend into copyright and production, but still needed executive commitment and content organization to catch up.
