# 王弗 / Wang Fu

Wang Fu appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s first wife. The episode presents her as perceptive and practically intelligent, especially in her ability to read people and warn Su Shi when his openness or trust might expose him.

Her role helps the source humanize Su Shi's public image. The later cultural icon who can seem effortlessly broad-minded also depended on domestic counsel, social perception, and people close to him who saw risks he did not see. In that sense Wang Fu supports both [[AuthorMythDeflation]] and [[ConscienceOverFaction]].
