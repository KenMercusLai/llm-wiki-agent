# Friedrich Engels

Friedrich Engels appears in [[178-muquanlun-weida-de-cuowu-or-shenke-de-dongjian-muquan-shehui-cunzai-guo-ma-974657368]] as one of the later thinkers influenced by [[JohannJakobBachofen]] and [[LewisHenryMorgan]]. The episode does not make Engels the main subject; it uses him to show how [[MotherRight|《母权论》 / Mother Right]] traveled into wider debates about family, property, kinship, and women's status.

Within this source, Engels matters because Bachofen's contested mother-right frame helped open a non-eternal view of patriarchy. Even if the ancient matriarchy thesis is not proven, the possibility that family forms changed historically is central to the episode's [[MatriarchyQuestion]].
