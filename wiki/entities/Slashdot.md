# Slashdot

Slashdot appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as one of [[SteveHuffman]]'s main product influences for [[Reddit]]. Huffman was a heavy Slashdot user and admired its comment community, but the site still relied on editors as gatekeepers.

Slashdot also appears in [[socialradarspod-paulb-final]] as the place where [[PaulBuchheit]] first noticed [[YCombinator]]. Together, the sources make Slashdot a useful marker of early technical internet culture: it exposed builders to programming ideas, startup news, and community behavior before later social platforms matured.
