# Aleta Stone

Aleta Stone appears in [[putins-options-an-oligarch-speaks-out-6a50c5ebbafe2fa6a7f38210]] as the volunteer guide at the [[UDropInn]] in [[ShamrockTexas]]. Her account gives [[JohnFasman]]'s final Route 66 segment its local memory of decline after the interstate arrived and partial revival through nostalgia tourism.

Stone says Shamrock declined after the interstate bypassed Route 66 traffic, with businesses closing and residents leaving to make a living. She also describes visitors returning because of Route 66, the U Drop Inn, period clothing, music, and the film *Cars*. In the wiki, she anchors the local witness side of [[InterstateBypassEconomics]] and [[Route66NostalgiaTourism]].
