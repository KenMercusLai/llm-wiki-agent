# IDC

[[tech-20260113-0113-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260113-0113-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds IDC through [[TomMinelli]], who explains the [[MemoryChipShortage]] as a familiar memory boom-bust cycle intensified by AI data-center demand. The episode says IDC expects the shortage to remain a problem through 2026 and potentially into 2027 if AI infrastructure spending continues.

IDC is the market-intelligence firm cited in [[tech-20260303-0303-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260303-0303-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The [[MarketplaceTech]] episode says IDC attributes higher prices and an unprecedented [[MemoryChipShortage]] partly to data-center demand.

The source uses IDC as the market-data anchor for [[AIHardwareSupplyChainPressure]]. Its role is not to explain preservation practice directly, but to connect AI data-center expansion to component pricing, hardware scarcity, and downstream pressure on consumer and archival storage.
