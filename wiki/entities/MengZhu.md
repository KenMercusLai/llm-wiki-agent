# Meng Zhu

Meng Zhu appears in [[the-laws-of-the-office-revisited]] as the researcher explaining later evidence around [[ParkinsonsLaw]]. The episode says lab and field research found that longer deadlines can lead people to expand the work.

Her practical contribution is the remedy side of the segment. Shorter deadlines and rewards for fast completion can reduce unnecessary expansion when a task can be done well under tighter constraint, making deadline design part of [[WorkplaceIncentiveDesign]].
