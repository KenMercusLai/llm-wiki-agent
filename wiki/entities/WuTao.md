# 吴涛

吴涛 is one of the [[NeiheKonghuang]] hosts in [[biancheng-de-neiranji-shidai-neihe-konghuang-71-1-71-1]]. He brings the episode's Japanese-learning, cloud-consulting, AI-coding, and low-level-programming examples.

## Source Position
- His Japanese-learning story grounds the episode's claim that [[AITranslation]] lowers reading and travel friction without fully replacing language learning as a way to acquire another mental model.
- His cloud consulting examples frame AI as a productivity externality: he describes becoming the human translator, verifier, and corrector around AI-generated work.
- His "programming internal-combustion era" metaphor anchors [[AIProgrammingEngineShift]].
- His interest in ARM assembly and Brainfuck supports the episode's broader [[SecondRenaissance]] angle: some technical practice remains valuable as play, craft, and self-cultivation even after automation.
