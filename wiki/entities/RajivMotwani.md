# Rajiv Motwani

Rajiv Motwani appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as a [[StanfordUniversity]] professor close to [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]] during the early [[Google]] search work. [[RonConway]] says Motwani's expertise aligned closely with the search problem, making him part of the informal research network around [[PageRankSearchRelevance]].

Motwani also appears as one of the Stanford professors who invested in [[SVAngel]] and could introduce interesting PhD students. The source therefore places him at the intersection of technical advising, early company formation, and [[StanfordStartupSourcing]].
