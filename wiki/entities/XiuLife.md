# Xiu Life / 秀生活

Xiu Life is the unmanned convenience-store project discussed in [[no-203-businiao-lanshili-979295393]] as part of [[LanShili]]'s post-acquittal return. The episode says the project was placed into a Hong Kong-listed company structure through a 51% equity transaction, with performance commitments that later failed badly.

The source treats Xiu Life as evidence that Lan's third-stage entrepreneurship had shifted from heavy aviation assets toward "light asset plus capital operation" stories. Its subsequent loss, near-zero valuation treatment, and resale for a small price make it a concrete [[CapitalMarketShellStory]] example.
