# Ginny Spicer

Ginny Spicer is the [[Netcraft]] speaker quoted in [[tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260223-0223-mp-tech-pod-128]]. She explains that AI-generated impersonation sites can be convincing to ordinary consumers, even when backend code patterns or artifacts help security teams identify them.

Her role in the wiki is to connect consumer-facing [[FakeRetailWebsiteImpersonation]] to security detection work. The episode uses her comments to show that the problem is not only poor user judgment; polished fake sites can reasonably pass as legitimate on a mobile screen.
