# Frank Cantone

Frank Cantone appears in [[why-is-there-a-supplement-craze-if-they-dont-even-work]] as the supplement manufacturing CEO who walks [[SarahGonzalez]] and [[JaneBlack]] through making a hypothetical [[PlanetMoney]] supplement. He describes product forms such as capsules, tablets, powders, soft gels, and gummies, along with custom runs, stock formulas, flavor choices, and label-claim boundaries.

The episode uses Cantone as an inside-the-industry voice rather than a critic. He believes in supplements and says he rejects impossible or unsafe requests, but his manufacturing explanation still shows why [[RecurringSupplementCommerce]] and [[SupplementStructureFunctionClaims]] can make the category easy to enter and hard for consumers to interpret.
