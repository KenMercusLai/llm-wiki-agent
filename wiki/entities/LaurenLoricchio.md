# Lauren Loricchio

Lauren Loricchio is the [[TaxNotes]] reporter in [[the-leaked-tapes-that-show-how-the-rich-avoid-taxes]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode relies on her reporting, pitch materials, source network, and leaked recordings to turn the [[MaltaTaxLoophole]] from a technical treaty story into an account of how high-end tax shelters are marketed and defended.

Her contribution is access. The source uses her pitch-deck evidence from [[DominionFiduciaryServices]], estimates about hundreds of users and billions of dollars in accounts, and recordings involving [[KennethKeyes]] to show [[TaxAvoidanceEvasionBoundary|tax legality]] being fought over by professionals.
