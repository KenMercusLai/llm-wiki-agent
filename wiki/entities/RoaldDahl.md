Roald Dahl / 罗尔德·达尔 enters the wiki through [[171-xianliao-shier-shengxiao-zhi-ma-guanyin-dashi-de-xingqu-aihao-ji-laotouhuan-juese-de-linggan-959614962]] as the author of [[Matilda|《马蒂尔达》]]. The episode uses the stage musical adaptation as a horse-year sponsor frame, connecting Matilda's courage, reading, and resistance to the symbolic force of [[Horse|马]].
