# Marubeni / 丸红

Marubeni appears in [[vol-108-riben-wuda-zonghe-shangshe-zhongfan-wutai-zhongyang-lhamp3hn14iihcicpa8po5dvh6gq]] as one of the five major [[JapaneseSogoShosha|Japanese sogo shosha]]. Like [[Itochu]], it is described as having stronger textile, retail, and merchant roots than the older zaibatsu-centered companies.

The episode uses Marubeni as a diversification case. It mentions copper resources, coffee-bean imports, U.S. agricultural retail, forestry, and an automobile supply-chain example that spans upstream iron ore and coking coal, shipping, steel, parts, dealership channels, and financing.

Marubeni's role in the episode is to make the "trading company" label feel too narrow: the company is shown as a coordinator of resources, logistics, production, sales, and capital rather than a broker standing between one buyer and one seller.
