# 王朝云 / Wang Chaoyun

Wang Chaoyun appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as the woman whose phrase "一肚皮不合时宜" becomes a compact description of [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s political temperament. The episode uses the phrase affectionately but seriously: Su Shi's mismatch with the times is not just awkwardness, but a refusal to stop saying what he sees.

The source also places Wang Chaoyun in the [[Huizhou|惠州]] exile stage, where she dies during hardship and illness. Her presence keeps Su Shi's exile story from becoming only literary triumph; [[EarnedOptimism]] has grief and dependency inside it.

## Connections
- [[SuShi|苏轼 / 苏东坡]] - intimate companion and interpreter of his temperament.
- [[Huizhou|惠州]] - exile place where the source says she died.
- [[ConscienceOverFaction]] - concept captured by "不合时宜".
- [[EarnedOptimism]] - resilience frame that includes grief rather than erasing it.
