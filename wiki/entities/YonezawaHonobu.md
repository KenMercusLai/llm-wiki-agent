# 米泽穗信 / Yonezawa Honobu

米泽穗信 is introduced in [[131-richang-zhimi-tuili-shi-wo-wei-neng-shuo-chukou-de-zhenxin-805617229]] as a representative writer for school-centered [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]]. The episode first names 《冰果》 / 古典部 as a familiar example, then turns to [[ShoshiminSeries|《小市民》系列]] for a dessert-centered case.

In the source's frame, 米泽穗信 matters because his characters make reasoning itself socially dangerous. 小鸠 and 小佐内 both want to appear ordinary after earlier trouble caused by their sharp inference, yet their everyday interactions keep pulling them back into small puzzles.

## Key Claims
- The episode positions 米泽穗信 as close to the typical daily-life mystery mode.
- [[ShoshiminSeries|《小市民》系列]] makes intelligence and ordinariness a recurring tension rather than a simple superpower.
- The source's dessert case shows how tiny evidence-handling details can carry both puzzle pleasure and character rivalry.
