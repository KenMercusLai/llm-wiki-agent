# Wang Tao / 汪滔

Wang Tao is the [[DJI]] founder figure in [[140-da-jiang-hai-neng-di-kong-fei-duo-jiu-feng-tou-quan-1-140-1]]. The episode uses him less as a biography subject than as the control point for DJI's founder-led company model: an unlisted, highly profitable hardware company that has to decide how much liquidity, autonomy, and capital-market exposure it should allow as the business matures.

The source interprets Wang's recent public anxiety as connected to [[HardwareTalentSpillover]]. DJI's alumni network signals the strength of the company as a training ground, but it also raises the question of whether a founder-controlled and unlisted structure can keep enough top builders when outside investors are eager to fund "DJI-system" teams.
