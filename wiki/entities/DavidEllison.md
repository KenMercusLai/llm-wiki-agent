# David Ellison

David Ellison is named in [[tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the leader associated with [[Paramount]]'s hostile bid for [[WarnerBrosDiscovery]]. The episode uses the bid to discuss streaming market structure rather than Ellison's biography or deal mechanics.

His wiki role is therefore narrow: he is a media-executive node in the [[StreamingConsolidation]] branch, connecting Paramount's bid to [[Netflix]]'s competing agreement and consumer questions about search, pricing, and competition.

[[vol-265-kuayue-50-nian-de-meiguo-banben-zhizi-1001004591]] broadens that role into a family-capital and political-regulatory case. The [[ShangyeJiushiZheyang]] episode follows Ellison from an expensive acting and aviation-film false start into [[Skydance]], a [[Paramount]] co-financing relationship, [[TopGunMaverick]], and later Paramount / [[WarnerBrosDiscovery]] consolidation. It argues that his formal leadership still sits inside a structure shaped by [[LarryEllison]]'s capital, guarantees, and political access.
