# Han Zheng / 韩正

Han Zheng is the [[SuduTechnology]] guest in [[e244-duan-dao-duan-vs-shangxia-fenceng-jiqiren-lujing-zhizheng-zhengzai-zhuanxiang-fc9a3737-81a9-49cf-a7d6-530c77df836e]]. He argues that the hardest part of robotics is not making robots look human, but making them manipulate ordinary objects reliably in open environments.

His main thesis is that [[RealRobotDataStrategy]] alone cannot carry general manipulation because the field lacks a massive deployed fleet. He therefore emphasizes [[Structured3DRobotData]], [[Sim2Real]], hardware/software co-design, and [[LayeredRobotArchitecture]] where low-level manipulation skills can be composed into longer tasks.
