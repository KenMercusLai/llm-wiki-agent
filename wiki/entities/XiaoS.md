# 小S / Xiao S

小S appears in [[129-ruhe-chengwei-zhenzheng-de-buwanmei-zhuyizhe-hunshen-shi-jiner-799220151]] as an important example of visible imperfection and public self-acceptance. [[QinZong|秦总]] says her experience watching [[KangxiLaile|《康熙来了》]] helped her see that a woman could be funny, loose, not perfectly correct, not perfectly beautiful, and still occupy the stage with confidence.

The source uses 小S less as a celebrity biography than as a practical counter-image to gendered [[PerfectionismAsAvoidance]]. Her role connects public performance to [[FemaleSelfPossession]]: the point is not to become careless, but to loosen the demand that a woman must maintain a flawless image before being visible.
