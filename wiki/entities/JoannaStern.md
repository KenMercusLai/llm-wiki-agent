# Joanna Stern

Joanna Stern is the technology columnist interviewed in [[tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251212-1212-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] Bytes episode, she interprets [[Apple]]'s leadership departures, the unresolved search for a new AI device, the [[WarnerBrosDiscovery]] streaming bids, and the backlash to [[McDonaldsNetherlands]] using AI-generated advertising.

Her role in the wiki is a cross-domain commentator: she connects AI competition to leadership succession, hardware form factor, streaming consumer friction, and synthetic-media disclosure.
