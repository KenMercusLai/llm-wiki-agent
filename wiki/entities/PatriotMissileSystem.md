# Patriot Missile System

The Patriot missile system appears in [[putins-options-an-oligarch-speaks-out-6a50c5ebbafe2fa6a7f38210]] as the scarce anti-ballistic layer in [[Ukraine]]'s air-defense problem. [[OliverCarroll]] says Ukraine can intercept many incoming drones, but that the larger vulnerability is anti-ballistic interceptors, where global production is far below Ukraine's needs.

In the wiki, Patriot is used less as a technical product page than as a concrete case inside [[AirDefenseSaturation]] and [[DroneDefenseEconomics]]. The episode says Russian combined attacks can include drones, cruise missiles, ballistic missiles, and anti-ship missiles, so Ukraine's defensive problem is not solved by high drone interception rates if the most dangerous missiles require scarce interceptors.
