# Richard Dorson / 理查德·多尔森

Richard Dorson appears in [[95-dushi-chuanshuo-lieqi-gushi-he-women-neixin-shenchu-de-jiaolv-723831611]] as the folklorist who helped name contemporary anonymous life-related stories as "urban belief tales." The episode uses him to place [[UrbanLegend|都市传说]] inside folklore research rather than treating it as only entertainment or superstition.

His role in the source is methodological: once urban legends are treated as folklore, their social function, variants, transmission route, and anxiety content can be studied without first deciding that every story is literally true.
