# AdWords

AdWords appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as the [[Google]] advertising strategy [[RonConway]] credits to [[SalarKamangar]]. The episode emphasizes that Google did not have a clear revenue model at the time of the [[KleinerPerkins]] and [[SequoiaCapital]] round, even though Conway was convinced the product solved search relevance.

AdWords therefore becomes the monetization bridge in the source. [[PageRankSearchRelevance]] and distribution through [[AOL]] and [[Yahoo]] created usage; AdWords later turned that usage into a business.
