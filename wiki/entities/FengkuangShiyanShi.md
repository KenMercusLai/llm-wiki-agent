# 《疯狂实验史》

《疯狂实验史》 is the book/topic frame for [[68-fengkuang-shiyan-shi-ai-zhe-gaisi-de-qiuzhiyu-664384549]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] uses a series of strange experiments to discuss curiosity, evidence, bodily risk, animal use, child subjects, and research ethics.

The episode does not treat "madness" as simple heroism. It reads the cases through [[ExperimentalScienceEthics]]: a shocking experiment may advance knowledge, expose a false assumption, or inspire literature, but it still has to be judged by who bears the risk, whether the result is reproducible, and whether later interpretation remains honest.
