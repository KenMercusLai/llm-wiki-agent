# Disneyland

Disneyland is the Anaheim theme park built by Disney and opened on July 17, 1955, in [[the-walt-disney-company-1]]. The episode traces the idea from [[WaltDisney]]'s train and miniature interests through [[WEDEnterprises]], site selection by Stanford Research Institute, [[ABC]] financing, television promotion, and a difficult opening day that still reached a huge audience through live broadcast.

In the wiki, Disneyland extends [[ExperientialRetail]] into [[ThemeParkAsMediaPlatform]]. It was not just a place to sell tickets; it lengthened guest time, increased per-capita spending, promoted film IP, sold merchandise and food, and made Disney's stories inhabitable.
