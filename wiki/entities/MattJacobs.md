# Matt Jacobs

Matt Jacobs is the [[PedestrianProject]] co-founder who calls into [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. He describes the company as a bootstrapped modern foot-wellness brand that has been in market for a little over three years, sells through DTC and Amazon, and recently expanded into Sprouts nationwide after doing a little over $500,000 in sales the prior year.

His strategic question is whether to take share inside the existing U.S. foot-care category or invest in education that expands foot wellness as a proactive routine. [[KennethCole]] pushes him toward emotional reframing, while [[GuyRaz]] pushes him toward conversion-first channel learning before heavier education spend.
