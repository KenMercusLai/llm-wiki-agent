# Volodymyr Zelensky

Volodymyr Zelensky appears in [[far-crimea-war-comes-to-russias-door-6a3e560c26d5a6687a90c658]] through the episode's escalation frame for [[Ukraine]]'s attacks on [[Crimea]], Moscow, and Russian energy infrastructure. The source says he warned that Moscow would burn if Ukraine burned, presenting the strikes as both retaliation and deterrent messaging.

In the wiki, Zelensky's main contribution from this source is to make [[WarVisibilityStrategy]] explicit: Ukrainian action is described not only as battlefield attrition, but as a way to force [[Russia]]'s public and elite audiences to notice the war's cost.
