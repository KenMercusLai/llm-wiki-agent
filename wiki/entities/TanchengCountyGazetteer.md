# 《郯城县志》 / Tancheng County Gazetteer

《郯城县志》 / Tancheng County Gazetteer is one of [[JonathanSpence|史景迁]]'s three main source bases in [[TheDeathOfWomanWang|《王氏之死》]]. [[148-wangshi-zhisi-duanjing-tuiyuan-shang-de-lishi-zhuangjing-892588934]] presents it as the record that makes [[Tancheng|郯城]] visible through disasters, taxes, lawsuits, official events, and local elites, while also showing the limits of such documents for ordinary women.

The gazetteer supports the episode's [[MicrohistoryFromHostileArchives]] frame. It can describe the 1668 earthquake, famine, social disorder, and tax burdens, but it does not preserve [[WangShiTancheng|王氏]]'s voice. The source therefore has to place the gazetteer beside [[HuangLiuhong|黄六鸿]]'s [[FuhuiQuanshu|《福惠全书》]] and [[PuSongling|蒲松龄]]'s [[LiaozhaiZhiyi|《聊斋志异》]].
