# 原因

原因 was [[ZhangYueguang]]'s earlier Live2D product aimed at female users, discussed in [[130-zhang-yueguang-chuangye-liangnian-shouci-fangtan-miaoya-bushi-ai-native-chanpin-liucheng-dao-shangxiawen-sheji-one-way-door-he-yinv-youxi]]. The product combined virtual character dressing, social/voice interaction, and paid skins or outfits, and reportedly reached tens of thousands of daily active users before shutting down.

The episode treats 原因 as an emotional product lesson. User mourning around shutdown made Zhang feel the presence of "living users," and the product became part of the lineage from Live2D companionship toward [[AIOtomeGames]] and [[AIFriendProducts]].

## Connections
- [[ZhangYueguang]] — founder/operator whose product taste was shaped by this experience.
