# Center for Democracy and Technology

The Center for Democracy and Technology appears in [[tech-20260105-0105-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260105-0105-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the organization behind a report on generative AI in the 2024 U.S. presidential election. [[TimHarper]] represents the report in the [[MarketplaceTech]] interview.

The report's role in the wiki is to move AI-election analysis away from only spectacular deepfake scenarios. Its findings connect campaign practice to [[AIPoliticalCampaignOperations]], state disclosure rules to [[AIPoliticalAdDisclosurePatchwork]], and future voting-information threats to [[AIElectionMisinformationRisk]].
