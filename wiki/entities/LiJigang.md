# Li Jigang / 李继刚

Li Jigang is the guest in [[e45-mengyan-duihua-lijigang-ren-heyi-zichu-lva2mfxese7v0sfv3mfpfhbdask]], where [[MengYan]] presents him as a reader, investor, and unusually strong reframer. Li describes himself as a reader who works across paper books, primary and secondary market investing, AI workflows, and personal note systems.

His distinctive vocabulary in the episode starts from "frames", "structure", and "order". He treats viewpoints as partial projections of a higher-dimensional reality, investing as a question of future relative wealth share, and company analysis as finding the constraints that define a business's possible space. That same habit carries into his AI thinking: [[AIAsTimeCompression]] changes the speed of knowledge work, while the human still needs [[WetStateHumanAgency]] to supply desire, will, taste, and connection.

Li's practical AI workflow centers on [[PromptAsIntentTransmission]], [[AMVPromptFramework]], [[PersistentAgentMemory]], and [[PersonalKnowledgeEcology]]. He uses AI to read papers, extract and extend book structures, automate repeated tasks, build small tools, and update a local system that tries to model his Memory and Soul. He also treats AI overuse as a body and pacing problem, not only a productivity gain.
