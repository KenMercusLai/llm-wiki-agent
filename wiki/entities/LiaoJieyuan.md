# 廖杰远

Liao Jieyuan is the founder of Guahao.com, later [[WeDoctor]], in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode presents his early breakthrough as persuading hospitals to release appointment supply online, first in limited proportions and later more fully.

The source uses Liao's company to show why [[OnlineAppointmentRegistration]] was a natural mobile-internet entry point for healthcare: it reduced visible queueing pain, but it still depended on hospital cooperation and institutional resource control.
