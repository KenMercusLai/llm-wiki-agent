# Swing Sculpt

Swing Sculpt is the custom sports-art business founded by [[LeviCase]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. The company turns a golfer's swing video into a custom metal sculpture by tracking the path of the club head and recreating that 3D path as abstract art.

The Advice Line question is how to reduce buyer friction for a novel product that requires customer education and customer input. [[KennethCole]] and [[GuyRaz]] push Swing Sculpt toward emotional keepsake positioning: Father's Day gifts, retirement gifts, golf trips, family memories, youth sports, and other moments may be more compelling than a golfer's technical pride in a perfect swing. The episode also points toward more process videos, testimonials, golf-pro or country-club partnerships, and possible future licensing of famous athletes' motions.
