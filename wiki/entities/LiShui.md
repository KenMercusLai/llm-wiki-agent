# 《理水》

《理水》 is the [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]] story that [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] uses to reread [[YuTheGreat|大禹]] as a worker-leader amid flood, scholarship, and bureaucracy. The episode contrasts flood victims tied to trees or eating leaves with culture-mountain scholars and officials who remain physically insulated from disaster.

The story's satire works through distance. Scholars debate whether Yu existed or whether his name means an insect, officials convert disaster food into tasteful objects, and only Yu arrives dirty, tired, and ready to ask people and inspect terrain. The episode links this to [[GuJiegang|顾颉刚]], [[GushiBian|古史辨]], [[SuoYinReading]], and [[DisasterAestheticization]].
