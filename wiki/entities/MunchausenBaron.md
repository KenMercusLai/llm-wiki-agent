# 敏锡豪森男爵 / Baron Munchausen

敏锡豪森男爵 is presented in [[157-chuiniu-dawang-lixianji-shubenhua-bamagua-he-chaoji-yingxiong-924722703]] as both a historical German nobleman with military experience and the fictional narrator of [[AdventuresOfBaronMunchausen|《吹牛大王历险记》]]. The episode stresses that the comic force comes from his cool, objective, modest-sounding narration of impossible events.

In the source, the Baron becomes a durable motif more than a normal character. Pulling himself out of a mire, riding cannonballs, catching and returning enemy shells, traveling to the moon, and surviving impossible hunting episodes all make him a carrier of [[AbsurdRationality]], [[MunchausenSelfBootstrapping]], and eventually [[SuperheroTallTaleContinuity]].
