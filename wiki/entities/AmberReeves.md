# Amber Reeves / 安伯·里夫斯

Amber Reeves / 安伯·里夫斯 appears in [[135-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-san-dan-823746670]] as the central relationship case in the [[HGWells|H. G. Wells / H. G. 威尔斯]] section. The source says Wells, while married, fell in love with Reeves, planned to elope, pulled back from the scandal after she became pregnant, and then encouraged her to marry someone else while continuing the affair.

The episode uses Reeves to make [[FreeLovePracticeGap]] concrete. A writer can praise sexual freedom or anti-bourgeois love while still managing reputation, demanding care, and leaving a younger woman with the practical costs of the relationship.
