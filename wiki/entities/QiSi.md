# 《起死》

《起死》 is the [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]] story that [[75-gushi-xinbian-kulou-bei-fuhuo-hou-hui-ganji-zhuangzi-ma-681010087]] reads as a reversal of the [[Zhuangzi|庄子]]-and-skull tradition. Instead of making resurrection prove a metaphysical lesson, the story lets [[YangDa|杨大]] demand his clothes, baggage, umbrella, and means of survival.

The source argues that the story tests [[QiWuLun|《齐物论》]] against concrete need. [[Zhuangzi|庄子]] can speak about life and death being equal, but once [[YangDa|杨大]] has no possessions and no livelihood, the philosophical language fails and the story shifts toward police, status, and bureaucratic protection. That failure becomes part of [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]]: doctrine becomes suspect when it helps the cultivated person escape responsibility while the poor person remains exposed.
