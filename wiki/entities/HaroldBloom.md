# Harold Bloom / 哈罗德·布鲁姆

Harold Bloom appears in [[51-yanshi-fanrenlei-tonghua-gushi-geliefu-youji-ke-shen-le-qu-le-632315320]] as a critic whose reading helps the host interpret the [[Houyhnhnms|慧骃]] section of [[GulliversTravels|《格列佛游记》]]. The episode uses Bloom to connect the Houyhnhnms with the third-voyage philosophers and scientists: both care for pure reason more than for ordinary human affairs.

In the wiki, Bloom's role is narrowly tied to [[PureRationalityTrap]]. He helps make clear why the apparently superior rational society can be another danger rather than Swift's final ideal.
