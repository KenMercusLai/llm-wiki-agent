# Sepah Navy

Sepah Navy appears in [[how-to-get-through-the-strait-of-hormuz]] as the naval authority that [[HamidHosseini]] says an Indian oil-ship owner contacted for permission to pass through the [[StraitOfHormuz]]. The source identifies it as part of the [[IslamicRevolutionaryGuardCorps]].

The episode does not independently verify the process, so the page keeps the claim tied to Hosseini's account. Its wiki role is to name the operational layer between [[Iran]]'s chokepoint leverage and a vessel-level permission request involving cargo, crew, flag, destination, and alleged crypto payment.
