# Alice Guy-Blache / 艾丽斯·盖-布兰奇

Alice Guy-Blache is introduced in [[107-xianliao-weida-daoyanmen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-744150818]] as one of the earliest women directors and a major figure in pre-Hollywood cinema. The episode says she completed films in the late nineteenth century, directed hundreds of works by the early twentieth century, co-founded the Solax company with her husband, and experimented early with music and basic effects.

In the wiki, Guy-Blache belongs to [[EarlyWomenFilmPioneers]]. Her role in the episode is less scandal-focused than corrective: the source uses her to show that women were present at the beginning of cinema, even though later film-memory often treated directing as a male lineage.
