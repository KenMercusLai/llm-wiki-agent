# Independence Bank

Independence Bank is the neighborhood bank that [[GeorgeEJohnson]] and other mostly Black businessmen took over in [[how-black-hair-care-grew-black-power]]. The source says the bank was meant to help Black entrepreneurs and families get loans when mainstream credit access was limited.

The bank matters because it makes [[JohnsonProducts]] part of [[CommunityEmbeddedBusiness]] rather than only a consumer-goods success story. The episode says Independence Bank loaned more than $100,000 to [[MartinLutherKingJr]]'s organization when payroll support was needed, linking Black business cash flow to civil-rights infrastructure.
