# Delete Request and Opt Out Platform

The Delete Request and Opt Out Platform, or DROP, appears in [[tech-20260309-0309-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260309-0309-mp-tech-pod-128]] as [[California]]'s state tool for residents to request deletion of personal information from registered data brokers. The episode says the [[CaliforniaDeleteAct]] mandated the platform and that brokers have until August 2026 to begin processing requests.

DROP is a concrete [[ConsumerDataDeletion]] mechanism rather than a complete privacy reset. It can help reduce data-broker-driven marketing, identity-theft exposure, and predatory targeting, but it cannot delete data from every online behavior trail, cookie, government system, recommendation loop, or unregistered data flow.
