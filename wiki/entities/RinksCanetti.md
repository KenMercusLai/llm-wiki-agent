# Rinks Canetti

Rinks Canetti was the branding agency formed by [[TomRinks]] and [[ReneKennedy]] in 2007, as discussed in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The agency's model combined branding fees with equity in companies it helped create or reshape.

The source presents the agency as the bridge between earlier one-off consumer properties and larger brand-building projects. [[DeLeonTequila]] showed the emotional-luxury version of the model, while [[SunBum]] became the breakout CPG case that eventually led Tom and Rene to close the agency and focus on the sunscreen brand.
