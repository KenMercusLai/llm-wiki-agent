# Wallace / 华莱士

Wallace is the Chinese value fast-food chain discussed near the end of [[e232-canyin-chuhai-you-xin-zhaor-ma-cong-dingtaifeng-yu-gongcha-naxia-meiguo-chudai-xiaoguan-liaoqi-3bb14dd4-0b13-4173-9d5b-0e63bf98ae1d]]. The episode treats its potential U.S. fried-chicken entry as high risk because the [[UnitedStates]] already has many low-price fast-food and fried-chicken options.

The source contrasts Wallace with [[Jollibee]]. Wallace may have price, but the episode argues that price alone is a weak memory point when local consumers already understand the category and have entrenched options. Without a diaspora base, flavor difference, or clear cultural scene, low-price positioning can become generic.
