# Dorothy Arzner / 多罗西·阿兹娜

Dorothy Arzner is one of the women directors highlighted in [[107-xianliao-weida-daoyanmen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-744150818]]. The episode identifies her as both a woman and a lesbian director, says she directed Paramount's first sound film, and places her within Hollywood's golden-age production system.

Arzner's significance in the wiki is part of [[EarlyWomenFilmPioneers]]: her career shows that women and queer filmmakers were inside mainstream film production earlier than simplified film-history memory often suggests.
