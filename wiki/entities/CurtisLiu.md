# Curtis Liu

Curtis Liu is [[SpenserSkates]]'s friend from [[MIT]] and co-founder in the [[Sonalight]] and [[Amplitude]] story told in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]]. The episode says the two met casually in a dorm lounge while playing video games before later trying multiple startup projects together.

Curtis initially went to [[Google]], then joined Spenser full-time. In the source, his role is mainly co-founder continuity: the team moved from early side projects into Sonalight, used Sonalight's retention problem to build analytics, and then committed to Amplitude after customer discovery showed broader demand.
