# Marius Miners

Marius Miners is the founder of [[PeakAI]] and the guest in [[ai-startup-hits-8-6m-arr-with-v0-mvp-and-eur85-pricing]]. In the episode, he describes moving from failed ideas through [[Antler]] into the [[AISearchAnalytics]] market, where the team validated demand before building production software.

## Key Claims
- Miners argues that B2B founders should listen for urgent customer problems before pitching their solution.
- He treats weak response signals as ambiguous until founders have tested messaging, positioning, target customer choice, and enough outreach volume.
- He presents LOIs as useful but weaker than actual payment, connecting [[PreProductSelling]] to [[ProductLedWillingnessToPay]].
- His Peak story emphasizes execution speed after category timing becomes visible.
