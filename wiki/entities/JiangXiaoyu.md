# 江小鱼

江小鱼 is a figure from [[DaChangXiaomin]] discussed by [[XiaoMan]] in [[da-chang-xiao-min-women-bixu-kezhi-dui-xitong-yu-shangan-de-qidai-lps-6oqp9ljyfw6wpfkutcks6dgf]]. She remains in a large company and is described as better at separating work from life, adjusting to organizational reality, and preserving a friendship with XiaoMan after direct work overlap ended.

Her role in the wiki is to keep the source from turning big-company survival into one moral answer. Where XiaoMan's path leads toward writing and exit, JiangXiaoyu represents a more adaptive, compartmentalized version of [[SystemHumanity]] and [[WorkplaceHiddenRules]]: one can live inside the system without making every office outcome the whole self.
