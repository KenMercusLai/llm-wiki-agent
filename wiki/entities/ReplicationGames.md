# Replication Games

Replication Games are the hackathon-style research-auditing events described in [[dont-hate-the-replicator-hate-the-game]]. [[AbelBrodeur]] first tested the format in Oslo in October 2022 after an open workshop invitation drew roughly 70 to 80 registrants.

The format is cooperative rather than prize-driven. Teams spend a day using [[ReplicationPackage|replication packages]] to reproduce published results, then run selected [[RobustnessChecks]] to see whether conclusions depend on fragile choices. The Montreal event in the episode had 71 replicators and showed why [[CrowdsourcedAcademicAuditing]] can change research norms even when most checked papers survive the day.
