In the wiki, the organization connects the [[Iran]] branch to [[StablecoinSanctionsEvasion]], [[VirtualAssetAMLRisk]], and [[AntiMoneyLaundering]]. The source uses the case to argue for targeted enforcement against illicit actors rather than suppressing lawful crypto use.
