# Nucor

Nucor appears in [[tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio-tsr-s5-davidkirtley-v2-audio]] as [[Helion]]'s second named customer after [[Microsoft]]. [[DavidKirtley]] says the Nucor plan is for a 500 MW behind-the-meter fusion generator at a steel manufacturing site.

The Nucor case matters because it moves [[CommercialFusionPower]] from grid-facing clean electricity into industrial-site power. In the source's framing, factories and manufacturing sites can become direct customers when they need large, tailored, reliable power rather than only residential utility service.
