# Nick Swisher

Nick Swisher appears in [[tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[IndianaWesleyanUniversity]] speaker describing AI-era higher-education marketing. The episode says Indiana Wesleyan spends about $500,000 annually, about 10% of its marketing budget, on efforts to influence how AI systems can discover and describe the school.

Swisher's comments make [[HigherEducationAIDiscoverability]] concrete. He says marketing increasingly means feeding signals into the AI ecosystem, and that college websites need to answer conversational questions about career prospects and degree outcomes rather than only optimize for short keyword searches.
