# Andrew Crosse / 安德鲁·克罗斯

Andrew Crosse appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as an electricity and mineral enthusiast who later claimed that tiny life forms appeared in an electrical experiment. The episode uses his "electric mite" story to illustrate the persistence of [[SpontaneousGenerationLifeOrigin|spontaneous generation]] and electricity-as-life speculation.

The source does not accept the claim as proof of life creation. It treats contamination as the more plausible explanation, making Crosse a useful case for [[ScientificSkepticism]] and [[ObservationBeforeInference]] inside the broader [[ScienceFictionContemporaryScienceIntegration]] branch.
