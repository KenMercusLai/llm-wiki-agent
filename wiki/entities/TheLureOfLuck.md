# 《运气的诱饵》

《运气的诱饵》 is the book discussed in [[44-yunqi-de-youer-dubo-chengyin-yinwei-women-haipa-ziyou-619995308]]. The episode treats it as an anthropological and philosophical account of machine gambling rather than a simple casino-industry expose.

For the wiki, the book is the source context for a cluster of concepts about [[MachineGamblingAddiction]], [[MachineZone]], [[PrivateActuarialism]], [[NearMissDesign]], and [[AddictiveInteractionDesign]]. Its value in the episode is that it shifts gambling addiction away from a narrow moral failure frame and toward an interaction among machine design, casino space, modern anxiety, and regulatory responsibility.
