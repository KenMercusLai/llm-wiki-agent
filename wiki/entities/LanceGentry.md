# Lance Gentry

Lance Gentry is described in [[justins-nut-butter-justin-gold-he-was-waiting-tables-then-he-reinvented-peanut-butter-b456c4b4-0243-4e46-9460-d0de7cf5d98b]] as a former Izze executive who became president and operating partner at [[JustinsNutButter]]. He helped [[JustinGold]] raise about $1 million, professionalize operations, sell into retailers, and manage the company during a key scaling period.

## Key Points
- Lance brought operating experience that complemented Justin's founder energy and product obsession.
- He helped across production, retail meetings, marketing, fundraising, and general management.
- The episode frames his illness and death from brain cancer as both a personal tragedy and a leadership inflection point for Justin.
