# Fidel Castro

Fidel Castro appears in [[dark-times-for-cubas-economic-experiment]] as the revolutionary leader whose 1959 victory moved [[Cuba]] into a socialist communist system. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode uses the Castro revolution as the starting point for Cuba's post-revolutionary economic experiment: state employment, assigned jobs, set wages, ration books, and dependence on the [[SovietUnion]] for favorable trade and cheap oil.

The source treats Castro's period less as biography than as the institutional origin of [[CubanDualEconomicStrategy]]. The U.S. embargo and Cold War alignment helped push Cuba toward [[ExternalPatronDependence]], while the state-led system created basic-service promises that later crises made harder to sustain.
