# China Farmers Bank

China Farmers Bank is named in [[ep23-minguo-jinrong-wangshi-zhuifengzhe-beihou-de-tiancai-shaonian-yu-yinxing-tixi-lu50lzwnfquabfare58gbhwr42py]] as one of the "four banks" in [[RepublicanChinaBankingSystem]]. The episode does not develop it in depth, but its inclusion shows that Nationalist-era finance was organized around differentiated institutional mandates rather than one undifferentiated bank system.

In the source, China Farmers Bank helps complete the map of central, international, transport, agricultural, trust, postal-savings, and cooperative finance institutions around state capacity.
