# Charlotte Corday / 夏洛特·科黛

Charlotte Corday / 夏洛特·科黛 is the woman who assassinated [[JeanPaulMarat|Jean-Paul Marat]] in [[125-mala-zhisi-beibi-de-ansha-huo-zhengyi-de-chujue-783972516]]. The episode presents her as a politically conscious moderate republican, aligned with the [[Girondins]] and opposed both to monarchy and to [[JacobinClub|Jacobin]] terror.

The source emphasizes Corday's agency. It rejects explanations that make her merely manipulated, lovesick, irrational, or secretly royalist. Her reading of Enlightenment writers, her admiration for classical republican examples, and her willingness to be executed place her near the wiki's [[TyrannicideRepublicanDilemma]] branch rather than a simple true-crime frame.

Corday's act did not stop [[MaximilienRobespierre|Robespierre]] or [[RevolutionaryTerrorPolitics|revolutionary terror]]. The episode's more limited claim is that she resembles [[MarcusBrutus|Brutus]] in preserving a political refusal even when the institutions she hoped to save could not be repaired by assassination.
