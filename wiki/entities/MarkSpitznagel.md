# Mark Spitznagel

Mark Spitznagel appears in [[e43-zhang-xiaoyu-mengyan-duihua-xuzhe-meiyou-genghaode-shenghuo-lrsfby01kuournly5mlkkzi-ayls]] as part of the [[NassimTaleb]] and [[UniversaInvestments]] tail-risk reference set. The episode uses him to frame the practical side of extreme-event investing: the attractive idea is to create upside in crises, but the implementation problem is difficult and expensive.

The source connects Spitznagel's role to [[TailRiskHedging]], [[ConvexityExposure]], and [[Antifragility]], while emphasizing that ordinary investors should not mistake a vivid strategy story for an executable personal plan.
