# The King Is Dead

The King Is Dead is the development project named near the end of [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]]. [[BrendanBrady]] says it comes from Indigenous Canadian writer [[TimFontaine]] and is in development with [[Crave]].

The project functions as the source's future-slate signal. After [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]] proves that a constrained Canadian show can travel internationally, The King Is Dead represents the creators' hope that [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]], retained rights, and buyer interest can support more distinctive work.
