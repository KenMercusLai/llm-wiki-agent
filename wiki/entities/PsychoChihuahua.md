# Psycho Chihuahua

Psycho Chihuahua was the apparel and character property created by [[TomRinks]] and cartoonist [[JoeShields]] before [[SunBum]], as discussed in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The line used a fierce small dog with rebellious copy, mixing cute scale with attitude.

The source makes Psycho Chihuahua important because it led to a licensing relationship and later lawsuit with [[TacoBell]]. It also foreshadows Tom's later belief that a durable mascot can make a consumer brand more memorable than founder visibility or celebrity endorsement.
