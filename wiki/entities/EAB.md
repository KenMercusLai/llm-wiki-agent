# EAB

EAB is the education company whose survey grounds [[tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260225-0225-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The [[MarketplaceTech]] episode says EAB found that about half of college-bound high school students are using AI tools during the college search, nearly double the prior spring's share.

In the wiki, EAB links [[AICollegeSearch]] to institutional response. The survey is used as evidence that AI-assisted college discovery has moved beyond isolated student experimentation and into a large enough behavior pattern for colleges to treat [[HigherEducationAIDiscoverability]] as a marketing and information-quality problem.
