# MiHoYo

MiHoYo appears in [[2026-ai-youxi-quanjing-saomiao-si-ceng-tujing-san-da-wuqu-yi-ge-gongshi-quekou-duitan-405-youju-xiaoning-lgk71gytqtsvkc-wipz0hkzkemne]] as a Chinese game company with a stronger technical belief and willingness to experiment with AI than many traditional game organizations. The episode's main MiHoYo example is [[Xingbugudi]], where an AI NPC is treated as both character IP and social infrastructure.

## Key Points
- [[Xiaoning]] describes MiHoYo as having a "technology saves the world" style technical faith.
- [[Xingbugudi]] is used as one of the more advanced examples of [[AINPCSocialInfrastructure]] in a multiplayer social context.
- MiHoYo's example contrasts with more cautious or differently paced AI exploration by [[Tencent]], [[ByteDance]], and [[NetEase]].
