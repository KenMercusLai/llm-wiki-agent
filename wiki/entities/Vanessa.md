# Vanessa

Vanessa is the guest in [[musical-ly-ruhe-chengwei-tiktok-pm-yanzhong-de-zijie-chanpin-wenhua-he-quanqiuhua-zhilu-zijie-tiaodong-di-5-ji-ludflvaw7lid-tci9gagbbxobvtz]]. She says she worked at [[ByteDance]] for about eight years, with more than seven years connected to [[MusicalLy]] and [[TikTok]], including the merger, globalization, safety-system integration, and later product iteration.

Her value to the wiki is the PM-level view of a large platform becoming global. She explains how [[TikTok]] moved from brand coexistence to a unified product, how safety review and recommendation infrastructure were connected, why music and audio reuse lowered creation friction, and how [[DataDrivenProductCulture]] shaped product decisions.
