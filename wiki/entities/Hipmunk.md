# Hipmunk

Hipmunk appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as the travel startup [[SteveHuffman]] founded with [[AdamGoldstein]] after leaving [[Reddit]]. Huffman says he wanted to run a business more directly than he had inside [[CondeNast|Conde Nast]], and Hipmunk's plane-ticket sales made the business model feel clearer because money changed hands.

The source also uses Hipmunk to show the cost of founder return. In 2015, Huffman was in the middle of a Hipmunk M&A process when [[Reddit]] asked him to return. Goldstein asked him not to leave until the process finished, but the Reddit crisis escalated and Huffman decided Reddit mattered enough to break that promise.
