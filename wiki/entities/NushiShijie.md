# 《女子世界》

《女子世界》 is the publication context that led [[LinYuqin|林玉沁]] to [[ChenDiexian|陈迭仙]] in [[71-meizhuang-diguo-hudiepai-yanqing-xiaoshuojia-de-guohuo-chuangye-wangshi-671521384]]. The episode focuses on its "化妆品制造库" column, where Chen taught readers formulas for products such as lip color, rouge, soap, hair oil, and face cream.

The magazine matters because it turns household reading into an industrial interface. A column addressed to women readers could circulate chemistry, create product desire, establish the writer's authority, and prepare a market for branded cosmetics.

## Key Claims
- The cosmetics column makes domestic space part of industrial history: formulas, measurements, ingredients, and household practice become a route into consumer production.
- The source does not assume all readers actually made complex products at home; the column also worked as trust building, fantasy, education, and [[StoryLedConsumerBranding]].
- Chen's use of pen names and romantic literary identity made science feel approachable and emotionally coded rather than only technical.
- The publication connects women's reading, beauty work, national-goods rhetoric, and product marketing in ways that later grand industrial narratives often miss.
