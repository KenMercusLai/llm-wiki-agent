# Daniel Newman

Daniel Newman is the guest in [[tech-20260218-0218-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260218-0218-mp-tech-pod-128]], where [[StephanieHughes]] interviews him for [[MarketplaceTech]] about whether AI can replace traditional SaaS and enterprise software.

Newman's core contribution to the wiki is the distinction between AI-generated software surfaces and enterprise-grade systems. He says [[VibeCoding]] can create a CRM-like prototype, but replacing business software requires databases, APIs, governance, compliance, security, and access to proprietary data. He also links agent-heavy workplaces to [[OutcomeBasedAIPricing]] by arguing that per-seat licensing weakens when companies operate many AI agents for each human employee.
