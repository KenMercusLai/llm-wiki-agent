# New Zealand / 新西兰

New Zealand is the setting for the [[Kakapo|kakapo]] section of [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. The episode presents it as an island ecology where flightlessness and a lack of mammalian predators once made sense, but where human arrival and introduced animals made those traits dangerous.

The source uses New Zealand to give [[IslandExtinctionAfterHumanArrival]] a hopeful counterpoint. Intensive protected-island management, tracking, breeding attention, and funding after public visibility helped the kakapo recover from a population in the dozens to more than 200.
