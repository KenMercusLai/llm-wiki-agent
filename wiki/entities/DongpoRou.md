# 东坡肉 / Dongpo Rou

Dongpo Rou appears in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]] as part of the popular cultural memory around [[SuShi|苏轼]]. The episode connects it especially with the [[Huangzhou|黄州]] stage, where cheap pork and limited means become material for appetite, humor, and survival.

The dish matters in the source because it is not treated as trivial color. It shows how Su Shi's cultural afterlife joins body, hardship, writing, and pleasure: the same exile who creates [[RedCliffFu|《赤壁赋》]] also insists on making ordinary eating bearable, which supports the episode's [[EarnedOptimism]] frame.
