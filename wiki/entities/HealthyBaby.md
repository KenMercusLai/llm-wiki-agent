# Healthy Baby

Healthy Baby is [[ShaziVisram]]'s later company discussed in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. Shazi describes it as making safe, high-performing products for new families, including diapers made without chemicals of harm, while pairing products with developmental content for parents.

The episode uses Healthy Baby to show a mature version of [[PurposeDrivenBusiness]]: the mission around safer baby environments, development, and parent calm has to be supported by product performance, evidence, and discoverable third-party validation.
