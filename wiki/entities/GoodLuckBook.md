# 《好运》

《好运》 is the book that inspires [[172-haoyun-shi-shenme-weisha-shuo-zong-bilei-hui-bai-haoyun-961254860]]. The episode does not present it as a rigorous academic source; QinZong explicitly says it can be criticized as shallow, mystical-seeming, hindsight-driven, or vulnerable to survivorship bias.

Its wiki role is therefore source-scoped. The book matters because the host treats it as an example of not over-filtering imperfect material: even a flawed book can provide a question, frame, or creative trigger if the reader keeps judgment active and extracts what is useful.
