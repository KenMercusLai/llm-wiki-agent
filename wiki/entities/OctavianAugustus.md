# Octavian / Augustus / 屋大维

Octavian / Augustus / 屋大维 is [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]]'s adopted heir and the figure who turns the failure of the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination]] into the beginning of imperial rule in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]]. The episode notes that Caesar's will named the young Octavian as heir, embarrassing the conspirators and giving Caesar's memory a new political vehicle.

The source contrasts Octavian with Caesar. Caesar appears charismatic, impulsive, and sometimes clement; Octavian appears colder, more patient, and closer to a successful [[MachiavellianRealism|Machiavellian]] operator. He defeats [[MarkAntony]] and [[CleopatraVII]], becomes Augustus, and helps found the [[RomanEmpire|Roman Empire]] while avoiding some of Caesar's more provocative gestures.
