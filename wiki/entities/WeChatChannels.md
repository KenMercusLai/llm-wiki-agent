# WeChat Channels

WeChat Channels appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as Tencent's later short-video answer inside [[WeChat]]. The episode frames it less as another standalone short-video app and more as an atomized content format embedded inside WeChat's social, identity, payment, and public-account infrastructure.

This makes WeChat Channels a different answer from [[TencentWeishi]]. The source suggests that Tencent's stronger route was not to clone [[Douyin]] as an app, but to let video become a native content component inside the platform where Tencent's [[SocialGraphMoat]] already mattered.
