# Jul / 朱勒尔

Jul / 朱勒尔 is the satirical cartoonist credited in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the visual co-creator of [[PlatoGoesToWork|《柏拉图上班记：哲学家的职场百态》]]. The episode emphasizes that many jokes depend on visual staging, facial design, office props, and philosopher caricature.

The source also notes his educational and cultural background near the end, using it to argue that the book's humor rests on scholarship and historical knowledge as well as comic timing.
