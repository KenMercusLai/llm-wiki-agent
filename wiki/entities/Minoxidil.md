# Minoxidil / 米诺地尔

Minoxidil / 米诺地尔 is the hair-growth ingredient discussed in the sponsor section of [[93-liaoliao-toufa-tu-le-jiu-hui-bian-qiang-ma-718794489]]. The episode describes it as an internationally recognized ingredient for treating hair loss and says it works by affecting peripheral blood vessels, cell activity, and the [[HairLossFollicleCycle|hair-follicle growth cycle]].

The source also gives usage cautions, including different concentration recommendations for men, women, and more severe hair-loss situations. Those claims are recorded as episode content, not individualized medical advice. In the wiki, minoxidil matters mainly because it turns hair loss from a vague anxiety into a follicle-cycle and diagnosis problem.
