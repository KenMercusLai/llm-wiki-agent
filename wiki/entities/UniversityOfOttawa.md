# University Of Ottawa

The University of Ottawa appears in [[dont-hate-the-replicator-hate-the-game]] as [[AbelBrodeur]]'s institutional affiliation. The episode uses Brodeur's position there to situate him as an economics professor trying to change research practice from inside social science.

In this source, the university is not the main story; it provides the academic home for Brodeur's work on [[ReplicationCrisis]], [[PHacking]], and [[ResearchIntegrityIncentives]].
