# Ben Jordan

Ben Jordan is the independent technologist interviewed in [[tech-20260108-0108-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260108-0108-mp-tech-pod-128]] about exposed [[FlockSafety]] camera feeds. In the episode, he says he used [[Shodan]] to find more than 60 exposed cameras and saw cases involving live feeds, archived footage, administrative interfaces, and possible footage-deletion controls.

His role in the wiki is to make the surveillance issue concrete rather than only policy-level. Jordan's examples connect [[SurveillanceCameraExposure]] to [[PublicSpaceRoutineTracking]], [[CrossDatasetPrivacyLinkage]], and [[CivilLibertiesSurveillanceRisk]]: a camera on a trail, a license plate, a zoomed-in phone, or a playground can become sensitive when the feed is public, archived, searchable, or linkable to other data.
