# Rene Kennedy

Rene Kennedy is the Copenhagen designer who partnered with [[TomRinks]] in [[RinksCanetti]] and helped create the final [[SunBum]] visual identity in [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source says his Danish design background shaped Sun Bum's clean typography, spacing, and layout.

Kennedy's role matters because the episode separates brand instinct from design execution: Tom supplied strategy, selling pressure, and emotional positioning, while Rene translated the research and inspiration boards into packaging, type, color, and the [[SunnySunBumMascot|Sunny]] ape mark.
