# Bahrain

Bahrain appears in [[gulf-co-operation-counsel-what-next-for-the-region-6a4387d73fa89e3338f83da6]] as the Gulf state the episode presents as more exposed after the Iran war. The source points to debt, limited reserves, and dependence on outside support as reasons Bahrain has less room to absorb prolonged uncertainty.

Its role in the wiki is to ground [[GulfStabilityRisk]] in uneven state capacity. The episode does not treat the Gulf as a single balance sheet: richer or better-buffered states can absorb confidence shocks differently from smaller or more indebted ones.
