# Smartisan

Smartisan is the smartphone company founded by [[LuoYonghao]] in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode presents it as a design- and expression-heavy route into China's phone war: Smartisan OS, the T1, the Nut line, and the TNT workstation all created attention around interaction taste, typography, industrial design, and launch-event performance.

The same source uses Smartisan as a hard execution lesson. Product ideals did not remove yield problems, supply-chain weakness, delayed delivery, quality issues, or the difficulty of turning a strong public persona into durable handset scale.
