# Triplebyte

Triplebyte is the engineering-hiring company [[HarjTaggar]] started with [[ArmandBenard]] and [[GuillaumeCabane]] in [[tsr-s4-harj-v3-tsr-s4-harj-v3]]. The company began from the belief that strong engineers could be identified through direct assessment rather than filtered mainly through elite credentials such as MIT resumes.

The source makes Triplebyte a central case for [[ObjectiveHiringAssessment]]. Its tests and interviews could help candidates without conventional status signals reach tech companies, including one example of a warehouse worker who moved into an engineering role after using the platform. But Harj says the company underestimated how much hiring depends on softer judgments, team fit, company identity, and inconsistent preferences across different interviewers.

The hardest section is the source's account of diversity hiring and [[JamesDamore]]. Harj says customers often wanted more diverse pipelines while also saying they wanted only top test scorers, which created ambiguous operating pressure. The episode records Harj's retrospective view of the internal Damore dispute, but it does not include full accounts from candidates, recruiters, or employees who disagreed with him.
