# 鹿野靖明

鹿野靖明 is the central figure in [[137-sangeng-banye-juran-yao-chi-xiangjiao-shide-zailai-yigen-835431951]], where [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] discusses [[SangengBanyeJuranYaoChiXiangjiao|《三更半夜居然要吃香蕉》]]. The source presents him as a severely disabled man with progressive muscle disease who builds a life outside institutional care through volunteers, friends, family, activism, and persistent demands.

The episode's key move is to keep 鹿野 morally complicated. He can be charming, angry, sexual, funny, manipulative, generous, exhausting, and tender. That complexity is the point: [[DisabilityIndependentLiving]] requires seeing the disabled person as a full subject, not as a symbol of noble suffering or passive need.

## Key Claims
- 鹿野's insistence on asking directly for help turns care into a person-to-person relationship rather than an abstract duty.
- His childhood and adolescence in [[YakumoHospital|八云医院]] help explain his later distrust of medical authority and his strong desire for home-based living.
- His involvement with [[IchiGoKai|一五会]] and disability-rights ideas gives his personal choices a social-movement frame.
- The episode treats his "difficulty" as ethically important because it pressures helpers to stop hiding behind pity, politeness, or savior identity.
- His volunteer network becomes a practical experiment in [[SubjectLedCare]] and [[VolunteerCareReciprocity]].
