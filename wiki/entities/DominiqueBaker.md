# Dominique Baker

Dominique Baker appears in [[can-the-trump-administration-make-college-cheaper]] as the researcher emphasizing access risk from student-aid cuts. The source says her work on financial-aid reductions suggests that students often stop enrolling when aid is capped without equivalent grants or scholarships.

Her contribution is the borrower-side warning in the [[FederalStudentLoanCaps]] debate. Even if caps eventually pressure some programs to lower tuition, the immediate mechanism may be that students with fewer resources delay, leave, or never start graduate school.
