# Enkidu / 恩启都

Enkidu is [[Gilgamesh|吉尔伽美什]]'s counterpart and friend in [[TheEpicOfGilgamesh|《吉尔伽美什史诗》]] as summarized by [[SunRuoxi|孙若曦]] in [[ep269-cong-manwei-yuzhou-dao-aodesai-weihe-zongyao-fanpai-yingxiong-shishi-ltklotheqpup6oamyflfzpxtipsw]]. The gods create him to check Gilgamesh's excessive force, and the two become companions after fighting.

In the source, Enkidu matters because his death converts heroic adventure into mortal knowledge. Gilgamesh's repeated descriptions of Enkidu's death become [[OralEpicRepetition|repeated grief speech]], turning friendship into the emotional mechanism through which the hero must face [[EpicHeroHumanLimits|human limits]].
