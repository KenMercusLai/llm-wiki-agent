# Greg Rosalsky

Greg Rosalsky is the [[PlanetMoney]] host and interviewer in [[why-economists-got-free-trade-with-china-so-wrong]]. The episode frames his conversation with [[DavidAutor]] as an expansion of a newsletter interview about why economists underestimated the cost of free trade with [[China]].

Rosalsky's role is to translate a technical labor-economics debate into a public-economics question: if free trade raised total output, why were some workers and towns left with durable damage? That makes the episode a bridge between [[EconomicWayOfThinking]], [[ChinaShock]], and current tariff politics.
