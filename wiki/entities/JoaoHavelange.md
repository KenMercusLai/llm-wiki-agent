# Joao Havelange

Joao Havelange appears in [[shangye-xiaoyang-44-shijiebei-kuojun-yu-fifa-de-quanli-douzheng-993354969]] as an earlier [[FIFA]] president associated with expanding the [[FIFAWorldCup]] to 24 teams in 1982. The source uses him to argue that tournament expansion is a long-running FIFA strategy rather than only [[GianniInfantino]]'s personal invention.

In the episode's history, Havelange represents the first major modern step in turning a smaller football tournament into a more global commercial and political asset.
