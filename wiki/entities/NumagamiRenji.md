# 沼上蓮次 / Numagami Renji

沼上蓮次 is the first-person narrator and practical counterweight to [[TataraKatsugoro|多多良胜五郎]] in [[KonjakuZokuHyakkiKumo|《今昔续百鬼·云》]], as introduced by [[115-jingji-xiayan-jinxi-xu-baigui-bu-yanjiu-yaoguai-zenme-haohao-zuoren-760601309]]. The episode describes him as a short-haired large man often mistaken for a monk, and as the person whose anger and commentary make Tatara's fieldwork mania readable.

The source gives Numagami a prewar and postwar arc. He once helped make the yokai fan publication 《迷家》, then lost home, work, and family stability during the war. His reunion with Tatara in the postwar black market leads to a small printing job and to his role as unpaid research assistant.

## Key Claims
- Numagami gives [[YokaiMystery|妖怪推理]] a grounded witness: he recognizes Tatara's scholarship while constantly noticing hunger, danger, money, embarrassment, and social consequences.
- His war-damaged backstory ties yokai fieldwork to [[FolkloreAsSocialMemory|民俗作为社会记忆]] rather than to simple hobbyism.
- The episode compares the pair to Don Quixote and Sancho, with Numagami as the practical companion repeatedly pulled into risk.
