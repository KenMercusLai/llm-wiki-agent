# 吴淑冰 / Wu Shubing

Wu Shubing is the [[TalkSanlian|Talk三联]] host in [[ep268-mao-dong-x-tang-tang-da-huang-ni-daodi-za-le-shangcuan-xiatiao-de-huangjin-he-naxie-juebu-xiache-de-ren-lgujs-q24fzqcuutibty8k0bowbv]]. She frames the episode around gold's price swings and keeps the discussion moving between investment logic, household use, and everyday buying behavior.

Her own example of putting a child's lucky money into gold makes [[HouseholdGoldSavings]] concrete: money that does not need to be spent for many years can be treated differently from short-term cash. The example also connects the source to [[PortfolioSuitability]] and [[InvestmentLiquidityTradeoff]], because the same asset can be reasonable or unreasonable depending on time horizon and liquidity need.
