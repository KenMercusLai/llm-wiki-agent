# Tencent Advertising

Tencent Advertising appears in [[touteng-dazhan-ba-nian-hou-zai-ba-zijie-he-tengxun-zai-gege-zhanchang-shang-de-jingzheng-zhuyi-chaikai-zijie-tiaodong-di-6-ji-lvglr-jws7o7utjauxnoqqasj91d]] as the counterpart to [[OceanEngine]] in the ByteDance/Tencent advertising battlefield. The episode describes Tencent's ad traffic as spread across products and teams, each with its own commercialization logic and product-level constraints.

The source does not say Tencent lacked valuable inventory. Its claim is that Tencent's social, game, media, and platform surfaces were harder to unify into a single performance-ad machine than ByteDance's recommendation-led traffic stack.
