# ManiSkill

ManiSkill appears in [[e244-duan-dao-duan-vs-shangxia-fenceng-jiqiren-lujing-zhizheng-zhengzai-zhuanxiang-fc9a3737-81a9-49cf-a7d6-530c77df836e]] as part of the research background behind [[SuduTechnology]]'s manipulation and [[Sim2Real]] route. The source uses it as an example of thinking in short manipulation skills that can be composed into longer tasks.

The episode links ManiSkill to the idea that stable grasping, placing, opening, inserting, screwing, and pouring can form a lower-level library beneath high-level planning. This makes ManiSkill a project-level anchor for [[LayeredRobotArchitecture]] and [[OpenWorldRobotManipulation]] rather than only a benchmark name.
