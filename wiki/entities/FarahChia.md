# Farah Chia

Farah Chia appears in [[peace-fire-further-us-iran-strikes-6a4f7acffe878dc8e27e6fbf]] as the [[TheIntelligence]] contributor explaining Asian [[FilialPietyLaws]] and the elder-care pressures behind them.

Her segment connects legal duty to demographic strain. She describes laws in [[Singapore]], [[China]], [[Telangana]], [[Malaysia]], and the [[Philippines]], while arguing that ageing, migration, rising family costs, and weak care infrastructure make punishment an incomplete substitute for [[ElderCareStateCapacity]].
