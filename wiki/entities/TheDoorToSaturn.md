# 《通往土星之门》 / The Door to Saturn

《通往土星之门》 / The Door to Saturn is the [[ClarkAshtonSmith|Clark Ashton Smith / C.A.史密斯]] story thread summarized in [[73-xujing-qitan-kongbu-youmo-zuihaode-kesulu-675865061]] around the wizard [[Eibon|伊波恩]], [[Tsathoggua|萨托古雅]], and flight to Saturn. The episode describes Eibon escaping a priestly pursuer through a gate, after which pursuer and pursued must negotiate survival in a foreign planetary world.

The story matters to the source because it is less bleak than [[TheVaultsOfYohVombis|《深谷住民》]] and less purely doom-driven than many [[CosmicHorror|cosmic-horror]] expectations. Its Saturnian episodes include alien social rules, escape from forced reproduction, a mistaken prophecy, and a practical settlement into exile.

## Key Claims
- The story shows Smith's ability to turn cosmic displacement into adventure and social comedy.
- Eibon and Morghi's forced coexistence makes religious certainty look fragile in an alien environment.
- The Saturnian residents' body norms and reproduction expectations extend [[AntiAnthropocentricSatire]] beyond fear into comedy.
- The mistaken prophecy episode belongs to [[AbsurdRationality]] because accidental meaning becomes socially functional.
