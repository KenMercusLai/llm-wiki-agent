# Christie's

Christie's appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as the London auction house where an unknown 17th-century painting of two yellow skulls sold for far above estimate. The episode uses that sale as the opening proof point for [[OldMastersMarketRevival]].

The source's use of Christie's is market-signal oriented. A single auction result does not prove a whole category revival, but it gives the segment a concrete price event that connects scarcity, attribution, visual appeal, and renewed buyer interest in older art.
