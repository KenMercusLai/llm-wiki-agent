# National Student Clearinghouse Research Center

The National Student Clearinghouse Research Center is the enrollment-data source cited in [[tech-20260217-0217-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260217-0217-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The [[MarketplaceTech]] episode says its data showed a fall in U.S. computer and information science enrollment, described as the first such drop since 2020.

The center's role in the wiki is measurement rather than interpretation. [[CarrieGeorge]] and the [[ComputingResearchAssociation]] provide the episode's explanation of why the decline is uneven across computing fields, while the Research Center supplies the headline enrollment signal behind [[ComputingEnrollmentDecline]].
