# Golden Fleece / 金羊毛

The Golden Fleece is the mythic object that drives [[Jason]]'s heroic quest in [[67-meidiya-gu-xila-qinxianglian-de-fuchou-ji-qi-xiandaixing-662400556]]. The episode summarizes its origin in an endangered royal child's escape, the sacrificial golden ram, and the fleece's later guarding in Colchis, then calls it a kind of narrative MacGuffin for the Argonautic adventure.

The fleece matters in the wiki less as an object than as a bridge from [[GreekMythology]] to [[MedeaPlay|《美狄亚》 / Medea]]. It explains why Jason reaches Medea's world, why [[MedeaCharacter|Medea / 美狄亚]] becomes his helper, and why the later domestic tragedy is haunted by an earlier heroic debt that Jason would prefer to forget.

## Key Claims
- The fleece organizes the quest structure that introduces Medea and Jason's bond.
- Medea's help in obtaining it makes the later abandonment more morally charged.
- The source uses the fleece to show how Greek myth works as a connected story system rather than as isolated tales.
