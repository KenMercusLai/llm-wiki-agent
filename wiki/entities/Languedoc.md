# Languedoc / 朗格多克

Languedoc / 朗格多克 is the southern French setting of the final section of [[39-zhexuejia-yu-lang-zai-langgeduoke-yongheng-de-xiatian-611233065]]. After the difficult London period, [[MarkRowlands|Mark Rowlands / 马克·罗兰兹]] moves there to write, and [[Brenin|布列宁]] spends his final year in a repeated routine of writing, walking, sea visits, bread buying, swimming, and evening rest.

In the episode, Languedoc is not only scenery. It is the place where the book's ideas about value, repetition, animal time, and death become concrete: a seemingly monotonous summer becomes the source's strongest image of [[PresentMomentAgainstDeath]].
