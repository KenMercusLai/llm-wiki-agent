# 小笠原群岛 / Ogasawara Islands

The Ogasawara Islands are the Tokyo-administered oceanic island setting in [[47-niao-youshenme-haokande-yuanlai-dandinghe-shi-tude-625613924]]. The episode describes them as remote from Honshu and, apart from bats, lacking naturally distributed mammals, which makes them important bird habitat and a useful setting for thinking about island evolution, endemic species, and ecological disturbance.

In the source, the island branch includes [[Nishinoshima|西之岛]], [[MinamiIoto|南硫磺岛]], the [[JapaneseBushWarbler|日本树莺]] subspecies case, and protection work around [[RedHeadedWoodPigeon|红头黑铃鸽]]. The islands connect local bird curiosity to [[IslandEcologicalSuccession]], [[InvasiveSpeciesManagement]], and [[ConservationIntervention]].
