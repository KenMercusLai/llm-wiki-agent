# Sheikh Hasina

Sheikh Hasina is the ousted Bangladeshi leader in [[dhaka-matters-an-election-for-bangladesh-698c5a3afeb59e13a3b8a94d]]. The episode says her [[AwamiLeague]] spent 15 years weakening institutions and depleting reserves before Gen Z-led protests escalated from campus rallies to mass demonstrations and her departure by helicopter.

Her absence still shapes the election. The source says relations with India are strained partly because India is sheltering her, while the temporary election ban on the [[AwamiLeague]] raises a longer-term legitimacy problem for [[Bangladesh]]'s [[DemocraticTransitionElection]].
