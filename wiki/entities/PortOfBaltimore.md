# Port of Baltimore

The Port of Baltimore is the freight context in [[tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260326-0326-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode says the old [[FrancisScottKeyBridge]] supported commuters and truckers moving goods around the port, and that larger and heavier loads near the port were limited by the former bridge's restrictions.

In the source, the port turns [[BridgeLoadCapacity]] from an abstract engineering specification into an economic and logistics requirement. [[JimHarkness]] explains that the new bridge's monitoring data can help engineers understand how the structure responds to increased loads that need to move to and from the port.
