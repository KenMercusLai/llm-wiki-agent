# Gaius Cassius Longinus / 卡西乌斯

Gaius Cassius Longinus / 卡西乌斯 is one of the main organizers of the [[CaesarAssassination|assassination conspiracy]] in [[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]]. The episode presents him as an early and active opponent of [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]]'s dictatorship and as someone who understood that the plot needed [[MarcusBrutus]]'s symbolic authority.

Cassius matters because he shows the conspiracy's practical side. The source's account does not make the assassination a spontaneous moral gesture; it required recruitment, timing, secrecy, and a plausible public meaning. Cassius's role sits between republican principle and elite political rivalry.
