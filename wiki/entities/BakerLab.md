# Baker Lab

Baker Lab appears in [[ai4s-xuyao-kuangren-yu-yexinjia-duihua-yinglingdian-odin-ruguo-shen-cunzai-wo-zenneng-rongren-ziji-bushi-shen-gonglu-boke-lhceyip6dqomrwk38uvqjwoomxyz]] as the research environment where [[HaotianOdin]] worked on [[AIProteinDesign]] before founding [[YinglingdianAI]]. The episode uses the lab as a source of technical formation, scientific prestige, and management inspiration.

The lab also functions as a boundary in the founder story. [[HaotianOdin]] says he judged early that [[DavidBaker]] had a chance to win a Nobel-level prize, but he also felt that remaining inside the lab would make him more likely to become an engineer within someone else's technical path. Leaving Baker Lab therefore becomes part of the source's [[FounderSignalDiscipline]] pattern: status and title can be useful, but they can also become a "jade lock" if they prevent a founder from pursuing the problem he thinks is primary.
