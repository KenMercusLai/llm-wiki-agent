# Juan Andres Guerrero Saade

Juan Andres Guerrero Saade, known as JAGS, appears in [[can-computer-hackers-get-inside-your-mind]] as a [[SentinelOne]] security researcher obsessed with the [[Fast16|Fast 16]] malware clue. The episode frames his work as malware archaeology: finding old fragments, reverse engineering them, and reconstructing what an operation may have been meant to do.

JAGS's key contribution is to keep the Fast 16 question open long enough for [[VitalyKamluk]] and [[AIAssistedMalwareReverseEngineering]] to reopen the case. He interprets the malware as a likely [[CyberSabotage]] weapon aimed at corrupting high-precision calculations, and he accepts [[EpistemologicalWarfare]] as a useful name for its deeper psychological effect.
