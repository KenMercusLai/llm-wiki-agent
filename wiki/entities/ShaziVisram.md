# Shazi Visram

Shazi Visram is the founder of [[HappyFamilyOrganics]] and later [[HealthyBaby]], and the guest advisor in [[advice-line-with-shazi-visram-of-happy-family-organics-c354a554-e764-44c6-89f9-e2192e3e5a86]]. In the episode, she advises founders from [[FreitBarefoot]], [[SprinkleBites]], and [[Plantamica]] on making unfamiliar consumer products easier to understand, prove, and sell.

Her advice emphasizes that mission and care do not substitute for product performance. She repeatedly pushes founders toward science, third-party validation, customer proof, channel-specific education, and small tests that show whether a product can create repeat behavior before the company scales or gives away strategic control.
