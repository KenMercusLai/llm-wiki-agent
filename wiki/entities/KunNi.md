# Kun Ni

Kun Ni is an early-stage investor based in Singapore and a contestant in [[ba-7-wei-heikesong-xuanshou-qing-jin-boke-guanjun-guai-cai-he-48-xiaoshi-bumian-de-yexinjia-lhozhsuqbw8csa5tj5tqc7saqrex]]. Her project [[VibeSong]] won the event's popularity award and tried to turn [[VibeCoding]] and hackathon participation into a lower-barrier online competition format.

She describes the live hackathon as surreal and relates strongly to the meme of making a big toy for the world in 48 hours. Her team planned to continue building the product and run webathon-style events in Singapore and Shanghai.
