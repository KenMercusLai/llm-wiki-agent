# GOG

GOG appears in [[jiu-shidai-diantai-28-shiti-youxi-de-shidai-zhongjie-zhiji-buru-chongxin-dingyi-yongyou-998430652]] as the episode's example of a more ownership-friendly digital-game model because DRM-free distribution lets players save and use game installers with less platform dependency.

The source treats GOG as an ideal that is attractive to players and preservation-minded users, while also doubting that this model will become the mainstream default for closed console platforms.
