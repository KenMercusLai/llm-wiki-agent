# Ryan Sutton-Gee

Ryan Sutton-Gee is one of the [[PlanGrid]] co-founders discussed in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. In the episode, [[TracyYoung]] says Ryan responded to the first iPad's construction potential by pushing the group to build the product themselves.

Ryan's role matters because PlanGrid needed both construction-domain knowledge and software-building capacity. The source later shows him focusing on fundraising after Demo Day while Young handled sales, separating early founder responsibilities inside a grieving but still operating team.
