# Chen Yiqiang

Chen Yiqiang is the MediaTek wireless-communications executive interviewed in [[ai-shidai-de-chaoji-rukou-haishi-shouji-ma-s10e17-523a0d42-4c16-4dd6-a2ab-9277fec1a731]]. He supplies the chip-company view on how AI changes terminal planning, NPU investment, software tooling, and system-level workload scheduling.

Chen's central point is that software and scenarios increasingly define hardware. Because advanced smartphone chips need two to three years of planning, [[MediaTek]] and handset companies such as [[Vivo]] must decide how much AI compute, NPU capability, memory bandwidth, and energy-management capacity to invest in before applications fully stabilize.
