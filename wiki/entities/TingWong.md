# Ting Wong

Ting Wong is the Philadelphia Chinatown Cantonese-style restaurant in [[chef-vs-robot]] where [[PhuongHoangChiang]] cooks the human side of the taste test. The episode uses the restaurant as the counterpoint to [[Instafoods]] and [[RobbyWokbot]].

Its wiki role is to preserve the human-skill benchmark in the automation story. Through [[JudyHuang]]'s explanation and Phuong's cooking, Ting Wong represents the argument that customers can detect [[WokHei]] and that skilled wok work carries cultural and culinary value beyond throughput.
