# State Administration for Market Regulation

The State Administration for Market Regulation appears in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]] as the reported regulator investigating [[Ctrip]] for antitrust issues. The source does not give a final legal conclusion; it uses the investigation as the entry point for platform history and governance.

In the episode, the regulator's future challenge is not framed as simply punishing large platforms. The hosts argue that agencies need data visibility over order flows, price changes, commissions, fulfillment, and platform rules to evaluate [[PlatformAntitrust]] and [[PlatformDataRegulation]] questions in practice.
