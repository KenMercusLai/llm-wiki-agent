# 盛世投资研习院

盛世投资研习院 appears in [[139-paopao-mate-he-pinduoduo-zhide-touzi-me-feng-tou-quan-1-139-1]] as the investment-education project founded by [[ICEInvestor]] in 2023. The episode describes it as an attempt to teach fundamental investing through both public-market experience and enterprise-operating experience.

Its relevance to the wiki is methodological. The episode uses the school context to frame investing as a learnable but demanding practice: investors need business analysis, valuation discipline, time-horizon awareness, behavioral control, and enough self-knowledge to avoid unsuitable games.
