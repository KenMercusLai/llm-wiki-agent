# Uruguay

Uruguay appears in [[138-qie-ke-wo-zai-haitan-shang-jiandao-yiwei-q-laoshi-842331726]] as the place where [[TomMichell|Tom Michell / 汤姆·米切尔]] finds an oil-covered [[MagellanicPenguin|Magellanic penguin / 麦哲伦企鹅]] on a beach near Punta del Este. The source uses the coastal setting to move from travel pleasure into [[OilPollutionWildlifeHarm]].

In this wiki branch, Uruguay functions as the rescue-side geography paired with [[Argentina]], where Michell later teaches and shelters [[JuanSalvadorPenguin|Juan Salvador / 胡安·萨尔瓦多]]. The border crossing also gives the episode its comic customs sequence and its source-scoped use of Argentine nationalism.
