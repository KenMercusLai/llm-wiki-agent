# Count Binface

Count Binface is the satirical candidate discussed in [[in-it-to-bin-it-nigel-farage-v-count-binface-6a58abcdc152a357db17c20e]] as [[NigelFarage]]'s comic challenger in the [[Clacton]] by-election. The source identifies the character as a dustbin-helmeted political persona performed by [[JohnHarvey]], with earlier runs against Theresa May, Boris Johnson, Rishi Sunak, Andy Burnham, and others.

The episode treats Count Binface as more than comic relief. He is unlikely to win, but his presence makes [[PoliticalFarceAccountability]] visible by turning an unusually artificial contest into public satire and protest against a powerful politician's self-created by-election.
