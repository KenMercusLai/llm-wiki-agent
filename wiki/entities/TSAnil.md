# TS Anil

TS Anil appears in [[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] as the executive who took over as [[Monzo]] CEO after [[TomBlomfield]] stepped away during the company's COVID-era funding and burnout crisis. The source says other executives continued the company alongside him.

The episode makes Anil part of a [[FounderSuccession]] case. Tom presents the transition as necessary after financing pressure, regulated-bank responsibility, insomnia, anxiety, depression, and decision impairment left him unable to keep making good calls. Under the later leadership team, Tom says Monzo grew from about $100 million in revenue and roughly $100 million in annual losses to just over $1 billion in revenue and about $100 million in profit.
