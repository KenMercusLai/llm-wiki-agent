# 惠州 / Huizhou

Huizhou is one of [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s southern exile locations in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode places it after renewed political attack and describes Su Shi moving farther from the center, but still responding to local suffering.

The source emphasizes two practical acts in Huizhou: Su Shi proposes burial for exposed remains and contributes to bridge repair, even selling his own belt to help fund the work. [[WangChaoyun|王朝云]]'s death there also marks the exile as grief-bearing rather than picturesque.
