# 《机器人大师》 / The Cyberiad

《机器人大师》 / The Cyberiad is the [[StanislawLem|斯坦尼斯拉夫·莱姆]] book discussed in [[45-jiqirendashi-duo-xiwang-laimu-neng-pingjia-yixia-chatgpt-a-621855112]]. The episode presents it as a cycle of stories about the machine constructors [[Trurl|特鲁勒]] and [[Klapaucius|克拉帕沃丘斯]], who solve problems by building machines and repeatedly discover that command, intelligence, and creation behave strangely once they are made literal.

The source highlights several stories: the machine that creates only things beginning with N, the machine that insists 2 + 2 = 7, the "giant law" that makes armies too intelligent to fight, the electronic poet, probability dragons, the PHD pirate's knowledge trap, and the water-crystal-princess story that makes humans look alien from a machine perspective.

The book is treated as [[CyberneticMachineSatire]] rather than only science-fiction spectacle. Machines in the episode turn language rules, mathematical error, war command, poetic production, probability, and information abundance into comedy, while still exposing real human structures.
