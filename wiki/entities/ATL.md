# ATL / Amperex Technology Limited

ATL appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as an early Chinese lithium-battery company whose process learning became part of the broader power-battery story. The episode says ATL licensed lithium-ion battery technology from Bell Labs, then had to solve production problems such as gas generation before the technology could become commercially reliable.

The source also links ATL to [[TDK]]'s precision coating background. That connection makes ATL a concrete case for [[BatteryManufacturingKnowHow]]: owning the recipe or equipment is insufficient unless the team can control coating, process stability, defects, and yield.
