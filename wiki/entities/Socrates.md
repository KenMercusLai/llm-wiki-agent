# Socrates / 苏格拉底

Socrates / 苏格拉底 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] as the intern mentor who receives [[Plato|柏拉图]] at [[ThinkReasonLtd|思考理性有限公司]]. The source makes him an office guide wearing Greek robes and a tie, turning philosophical transmission into onboarding.

His poison-hemlock death is repeated as a workplace accident when the intern confuses the coffee machine and hemlock-juice machine. The joke shows the episode's general method: familiar biographical motifs are not merely referenced, but rebuilt as office procedures and mishaps.

[[124-kaisa-zhisi-lishi-de-yunjiao-shifou-yi-shixiao-781894361]] mentions Socrates as another possible symbolic frame for [[JuliusCaesar|Caesar]]'s death. The source uses the comparison only as a memory problem: the same political death can be pulled toward martyrdom, tyranny, tragedy, or philosophical sacrifice depending on the viewer's frame.
