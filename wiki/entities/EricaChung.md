# Erica Chung

[[EricaChung|Erica Chung]] appears in [[tsr-s4-tylershultzpart1-final-tsr-s4-tylershultzpart1-final]] as a [[Theranos]] employee who shared [[TylerShultz|Tyler Shultz]]'s concerns about the company's testing practices. The source says her manager warned her that raising concerns would put her permanently on Theranos's radar, showing how [[OrganizationalSecrecyAsControl]] worked through fear before any public conflict.

Tyler met Erica after his resignation day and invited her to dinner with [[GeorgeShultz|George Shultz]] so George could hear from another employee. Tyler hoped independent corroboration would help, but George rejected the warning and repeated the claim that Theranos devices were being used in medevac helicopters in Afghanistan. Tyler says Erica quit the next day.
