# Baijian

Baijian is the professional-service platform described by [[ZhangShaofeng]] in [[weishenme-gongsi-yong-buhao-ai-cong-jiaolv-dao-xingdong-de-3-ge-guanjian-dongzuo-duitan-bairong-zhineng-zhang-shaofeng-lgarngnaqran2c9p4jssurvt6ces]]. The platform invites legal, business consulting, tax, and financial-planning professionals to work with [[BairongIntelligence]]'s digital employees and office systems.

[[e225-saasye-shuqianyi-shizhi-zhengfa-ai-ruhe-biange-zuzhi-jiagou]] adds Baijian's professional-service logic in more concrete terms: AI can produce and revise drafts, reports, slides, searches, and first-pass contract reviews, while qualified human professionals inspect, sign, and carry responsibility.

## Position In The Episode
- Zhang compares Baijian to a professional-service "Tmall": experts are separated from traditional firms and recombined on a platform with AI support.
- The platform is tied to Chinese companies' globalization needs, including language, tariff, exchange-rate, tax, employment, and location-decision complexity.
- It is not described as a classic acquisition roll up, but it shares the [[AIBPORollUp]] logic of restructuring expert services with agents and shared systems.
- The E225 source ties Baijian to [[ResultAsAService]] because professional-service buyers care about usable advice, accepted documents, and responsible sign-off rather than tool access.
