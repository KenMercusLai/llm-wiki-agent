# Kara Hultgreen

Kara Hultgreen appears in [[116-bufucong-de-yishu-fenxiang-yixie-shizhan-fangfa-haiyou-liliang-764416021]] as the U.S. Navy F-14 pilot whose fatal landing accident became an identity-charged truth case in the episode. The source says official explanation, later leaked internal investigation, training standards, equipment design, and debates about women in combat roles all became entangled.

The episode uses Hultgreen's case to show that public truth can be hard to face when it touches group identity. The point is not a simple claim about women, pilots, or blame; it is the difficulty of preserving evidence, complexity, and human respect when facts become ammunition for factions.
