# Union Square Ventures

Union Square Ventures is the venture-capital firm where [[ChristinaCacioppo]] worked before learning to code and founding [[Vanta]], discussed in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]]. Christina says she joined after responding to [[FredWilson]]'s blog post asking applicants to send links to their web presence.

In the source, USV matters less as a funding institution than as founder exposure. Christina attended early [[YCombinator]] Demo Days, evaluated whether companies fit USV's interest in large networks of engaged users, and learned that founders could come from many paths rather than one technical dropout stereotype.
