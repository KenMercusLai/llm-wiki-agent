# Eric Li Zhiqiang / 李志强

Eric Li Zhiqiang is the founder and CEO of [[YimuTechnology]] and the guest in [[cong-hui-tiaowu-dao-you-ganzhi-chujue-shi-jiqiren-tongwang-zhineng-de-menpiao-ma-s10e19-f448a656-3004-430b-a853-79d1e77dcb53]]. The source describes his background as spanning computer science, environmental engineering, AI applications, microfluidic sensing, and silicon-photonics sensing chips.

In the episode, Eric argues that touch is necessary for [[EmbodiedAI]] because vision cannot fully solve contact precision, occlusion, or force control. His central synthesis is that robot movement has advanced faster than dexterous hands and physical understanding, so [[TactileSensing]] should become part of the robot model stack through [[OpticalTactileSensing]], [[TouchNet]], and [[TactileTransformerEncoder]].
