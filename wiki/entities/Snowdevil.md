# Snowdevil

Snowdevil is the online snowboard store that became the origin point for [[Shopify]] in [[shopify-tobias-lutke-how-a-snowboarder-built-a-150-billion-business-2019-d0b07b6a-125e-4896-babd-678d19957306]]. [[TobiasLutke]] and [[ScottLake]] started it after Toby's work-permit situation made starting a business more practical than taking a local job.

## Key Points
- Began as a real snowboard retail business with inventory, vendor relationships, shipping, and customer orders.
- Forced Toby to confront the limitations of available e-commerce software for product storytelling, checkout, payments, search, and fulfillment.
- Launched after Toby built the store software himself in Ruby.
- Received an early first order from a customer in Pennsylvania, which became a lasting reference point for the emotional importance of a merchant's first sale.
- Generated outside requests from people who wanted to license the store software, pushing the founders toward Shopify.
