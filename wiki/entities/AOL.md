# AOL

AOL appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as one of the large pre-Google internet companies in the crowded search market. [[RonConway]] says [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]] wanted [[KleinerPerkins]] in the [[Google]] financing because the firm had an AOL connection that could help with an OEM or distribution deal.

In the episode, AOL is less a full company case than a distribution gate. Google could have strong [[PageRankSearchRelevance]], but the founders still wanted access to places where users already searched.
