# 猪哥 / 猪场严选

ZhuGe / 猪哥 is the guest in [[176-weishenme-yueshi-chirou-yueyao-guanzhu-dongwu-fuli-971057722]], where he presents himself as a "猪二代" from the pig-breeding industry and as the creator behind the [[Xiaohongshu]] account "猪场严选". The episode treats him as an industry insider who explains pork, breeding pigs, food safety, [[FoodAnimalWelfare]], and the working conditions of livestock employees from inside the production system.

His role in the wiki is not simply "animal advocate". He is a source-scoped bridge between meat production, consumer education, worker welfare, animal handling, slaughter methods, and practical industry constraints. That position lets the episode argue that animal welfare can matter to meat eaters without requiring immediate vegetarian purity or ignoring affordability.

## Key Claims
- ZhuGe uses public communication to explain pork quality, animal welfare, food safety, and livestock-industry work to ordinary consumers.
- His authority in this source comes from proximity to pig breeding and large pig companies, but the wiki should keep his industry claims source-scoped unless corroborated by later sources.
- His most important contribution is linking [[LivestockWorkerMoralInjury]] to animal welfare: people who raise, euthanize, transport, or slaughter animals can be harmed by the same low-welfare systems that harm animals.
- He treats welfare improvements as staged and practical: start with [[SlaughterWelfare]], reduce violent handling, improve feasible space and equipment, and make [[AnimalWelfareProductLabeling]] visible enough for consumers to reward.
