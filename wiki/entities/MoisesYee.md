# Moises Yee

Moises Yee appears in [[why-are-we-so-obsessed-with-manufacturing]] through research cited with [[DavidCard]] and [[JesseRothstein]] on cross-industry wage premiums. The episode uses the cited work to show that manufacturing's pay advantage is real but uneven.

The page is source-scoped: Yee is not developed as a standalone interview subject here. The research helps the wiki keep [[ManufacturingPayPremium]] evidence separate from claims about [[ManufacturingJobQuality]] or long-run industrial strategy.
