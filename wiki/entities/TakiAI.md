# Taki AI

Taki AI is the model described in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as having been trained only on text from before 1931. The episode uses it as a vivid case of training-data worldview: when asked about national pride or empire, it answers from a strongly British imperial perspective.

Taki matters less as a product page than as evidence for [[LanguageDependentAIBias]] and [[AIModelValueSurveying]]. If a model's text world is historically bounded, its answers can preserve values that later readers might treat as obsolete, harmful, or revealing of the corpus rather than of any deliberate policy choice.
