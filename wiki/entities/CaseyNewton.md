# Casey Newton

Casey Newton appears in [[tech-20260122-0122-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260122-0122-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[Platformer]] journalist who investigated a viral [[Reddit]] whistleblower post about a food-delivery app. His verification path moved from asking for identity proof to testing an official-looking badge and an 18-page document that seemed to corroborate the allegations too neatly.

Newton's case grounds [[AIGeneratedHoaxEvidence]] and [[AIJournalismTrust]]. The episode presents him as an experienced reporter whose normal source-checking instincts were strained by generative AI: a hoaxer could now produce plausible badges and documents quickly enough that authentication work became part of routine reporting.
