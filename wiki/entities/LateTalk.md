# LateTalk

LateTalk, or 晚点聊, is the podcast/show context for [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]] and [[ai-jibao-26q2-cong-coding-dao-rsi-qiangzhe-yu-qiang-de-weilai-1-171-1]]. The first source hosts a quarterly embodied-intelligence review with [[ChenZhePeter]], using industry events, robot demos, model releases, and commercialization signals to map the state of [[PhysicalAI]].

The Q2 2026 AI-quarter source adds [[HenryYin]] as a guest and shifts from embodied systems to frontier-model competition, [[Codex]] versus [[ClaudeCode]], [[AutoResearch]], [[RecursiveSelfImprovement]], enterprise-owned models, open-model substitution, computer use, and real-time voice.

[[166-xu-huazhe-zaici-jushen-chuangye-buxiang-cuoguo-zuida-de-xigua-1-166-1]] adds a founder-interview version of the same embodied-AI thread through [[XuHuazhe]] and [[PokeRobotics]]. The episode focuses less on quarterly industry mapping and more on why Xu believes [[PhysicalAGI]], household robots, [[AINativeRobotics]], and [[RobotActiveUseMetrics]] should define the next phase.

[[hanyang-wakeng-meijie-jiasuo-hao-neirong-duitan-weishenme-zuo-fuyou-tiandi-59b766f5-ee1d-4040-a650-a96b7b4b70d5]] mentions LateTalk as one of [[HanYang]]'s earlier content practices. In that source, the show functions less as a technical subject and more as part of the backstory for why [[FuyouTiandi]] tries to loosen [[MediaFormConstraint]] through [[LongFormConversation]].
