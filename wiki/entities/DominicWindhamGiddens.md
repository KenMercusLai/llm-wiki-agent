# Dominic Windham-Giddens

Dominic Windham-Giddens is the Barbados-based coffee entrepreneur and co-founder of [[CaneDogCoffee]] in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. He has been in the coffee business for 25 years and describes building the company with his wife, who is CEO.

His Advice Line question is how to scale internationally while remaining based in Barbados. The advice he receives centers on making the brand's place story more legible, using existing hotel relationships for introductions, and testing concentrated market entry before taking on broad international complexity.
