# Metamorphoses / 《变形记》

Metamorphoses / 《变形记》 is the [[Ovid|奥维德]] work invoked in [[130-tuokaerqiuke-guaidan-gushiji-xunzhao-he-gaobie-803543569]] during the episode's reading of 〈变形中心〉 from [[BizarreStories|《怪诞故事集》]]. 崔教授 uses the Ovidian frame to argue that transformation rests on similarity: the possibility of change depends on continuities between human beings, animals, plants, and the wider world.

In this wiki branch, the work functions as an intertext for [[TransformationAsExit]]. It lets the episode ask whether becoming something else is self-erasure, a form of death, a return to nature, or a more honest expression of one's will.

## Key Claims
- The episode uses 《变形记》 to make bodily transformation philosophically legible rather than merely bizarre.
- The source links metamorphosis to continuity across life forms, which complicates the sister's choice to become a wolf.
- The intertext connects modern speculative medicine to older mythic ways of thinking about body, identity, and form.
