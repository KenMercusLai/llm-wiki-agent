# National Rally

National Rally is the populist-right French party at the center of [[marine-warfare-le-pen-runs-for-president-6a4e1f2781fe1f6460b7117c]]. The episode presents it through [[MarineLePen]]'s presidential candidacy, [[JordanBardella]]'s temporary rise as substitute contender, and the long reputational inheritance from [[JeanMarieLePen]]'s National Front.

The party's strategic tension in the source is between electoral respectability and ideological continuity. Le Pen's support among working-class voters and her social-spending pitch can broaden the party differently from Bardella's more middle-class, business-friendly programme, making [[FarRightNormalization]] an internal as well as external project.
