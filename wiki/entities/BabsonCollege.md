# Babson College

Babson College is the institutional setting for [[tech-20260115-0115-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260115-0115-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the episode, [[ChristyGerdhary]] is introduced as an associate teaching professor of English and director of the Writing Center there, and [[TheGenerator]] is described as an interdisciplinary faculty group formed at Babson in fall 2023.

The source uses Babson as a case of educators building shared AI teaching norms rather than leaving each instructor to choose between bans, detectors, and unstructured tool use. Its wiki significance is therefore institutional: AI classroom policy becomes a faculty-development and learning-design problem, not only a student-discipline problem.

## Key Claims
- Babson is presented as a place where faculty began organized AI conversations soon after chatbots entered classrooms.
- The episode's Babson example emphasizes writing, values, care, and visible collaboration over simple AI prohibition.
- The group convened more than 30 Boston-area institutions, making the Babson case a local network rather than only one classroom practice.
