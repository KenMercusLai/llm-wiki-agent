# Peru

Peru enters the wiki through [[shangye-xiaoyang-45-eerninuo-yueda-yu-yuegui-1000164352]] as the central country in the [[FishmealSupplyChain]] built around [[PeruvianAnchoveta|anchoveta]]. The episode calls Peru the "Saudi Arabia" of fishmeal because Peruvian anchoveta catches and processing are said to produce roughly one-fifth of global fishmeal supply.

The source makes Peru a climate-and-resource-management case, not only a seafood exporter. When [[ElNino]] warms the eastern Pacific and reduces nutrient upwelling, anchoveta food sources shrink, catch quality changes, and the Peruvian government faces a choice between immediate fishmeal supply and long-term stock recovery through [[FisheriesResourceManagement]].
