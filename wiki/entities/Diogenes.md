# Diogenes / 第欧根尼

Diogenes / 第欧根尼 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] through the barrel and "get out of my sun" joke. The episode uses him to distinguish strong Cynic freedom from ordinary opportunistic cynicism.

He also refuses the open-office conversion, making him the source's clearest figure for bodily, spatial, and speech freedom inside [[ThinkReasonLtd|思考理性有限公司]]. That refusal links him to [[OfficeSurveillancePrivacy]] and [[InstitutionalizedSmallFreedom]].
