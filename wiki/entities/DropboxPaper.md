# Dropbox Paper

Dropbox Paper is the collaborative-document product that grew from [[Hackpad]] inside [[Dropbox]], discussed in [[tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio-tsr-s5-christinac-v2audio]]. [[ChristinaCacioppo]] describes the Paper team as building a new thing inside Dropbox and seeing heavy internal use before external launch.

The episode makes Paper important as a compliance bottleneck. When the team wanted to launch to Dropbox customers, legal review surfaced customer-contract security and compliance obligations. That experience helped Christina later identify [[SOC2Audit]] as a painful, repeatable startup problem rather than a one-off internal process.
