# Josh Schaeffer

Josh Schaeffer runs [[LunchMeetVHS]] in [[tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260226-0226-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The episode presents him as a collector-preservation operator who publishes a magazine about videotape culture, buys rights to videotape-only films, and produces VHS reprints.

Schaeffer's main contribution to the wiki is the social-history argument behind [[AnalogMediaPreservation]]. He treats VHS and [[VideoStoreCulture]] as evidence of what people made, watched, talked about, and encountered in physical stores before streaming made discovery more private and algorithmic.
