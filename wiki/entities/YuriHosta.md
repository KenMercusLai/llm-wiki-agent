# Yuri Hosta

Yuri Hosta appears in [[board-games-3-whats-in-a-name]] as the [[ExplodingKittens]] executive responsible for international business outside North America. The source uses him to explain why a strong U.S. name can become a [[CrossMarketNamingFriction]] problem.

Hosta says international business is a meaningful and growing share of Exploding Kittens sales, and he describes different country-level game preferences. France is framed as more receptive to layered card games, Spain and Italy to dynamic games, and Germany as a market where a therapy-themed game performed well enough to draw interest from the Freud Museum.
