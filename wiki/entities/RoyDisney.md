# Roy Disney

Roy Disney is Walt Disney's brother and the business/finance partner in [[the-walt-disney-company-1]]. The episode presents Roy as the counterpart who kept [[TheWaltDisneyCompany]] funded, negotiated through debt and outside capital, and pushed back on the financial risk behind Walt's biggest creative bets.

Roy's role becomes most visible after [[WaltDisney]]'s death. He renamed the Florida project [[WaltDisneyWorld]], postponed retirement, scaled back Walt's city vision, completed the Magic Kingdom and hotels without taking on debt, and died only months after the 1971 opening.
