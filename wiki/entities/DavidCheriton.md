# David Cheriton

David Cheriton appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as the [[StanfordUniversity]] professor and early [[Google]] investor who first told [[RonConway]] about Backrub. Conway says [[SVAngel]] had deliberately built relationships with Stanford engineering professors, and Cheriton's introduction became the key signal in that [[StanfordStartupSourcing]] strategy.

Cheriton also controlled timing. He told Conway about [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]] before the product was ready, then waited until the founders were ready to raise before arranging the Empire Tap Room meeting. In Conway's account, Cheriton made the technical opportunity legible by describing "PageRank and relevance."
