# Tarang Amin

Tarang Amin is the CEO brought into [[ELFCosmetics]] by [[TPG]] in [[e-l-f-cosmetics-joey-shamah-the-dollar-store-formula-that-built-a-cosmetics-giant-bbc71e65-9e47-41cb-8dd4-c61fcfc2572e]]. The episode says he later took e.l.f. public in 2016, after [[JoeyShamah]] and his family had rolled equity and stayed involved during the transition.

Tarang's role makes the episode a [[StageAppropriateHiring]] case: the company had moved from founder-led survival and retail scaling into a stage that needed larger consumer-company operating structure.
