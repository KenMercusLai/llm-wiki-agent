# 姚欣 / Yao Xin

Yao Xin is the founder associated with [[PPLive]] in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode uses him as the main person case for the P2P live-streaming branch of China's early online-video market.

His relevance is technical and historical: [[P2PStreaming]] solved real viewing bottlenecks before the industry became dominated by copyright budgets, long-video memberships, and large-platform backing.
