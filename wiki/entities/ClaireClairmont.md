# Claire Clairmont / 克莱尔·克莱尔蒙特

Claire Clairmont appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] as [[MaryShelley|Mary Shelley / 玛丽·雪莱]]'s stepsister and as a practical connector in the Romantic circle. The episode presents her as part of Mary and [[PercyShelley|Percy Shelley / 珀西·雪莱]]'s 1814 flight and as the figure whose relationship with [[LordByron|Lord Byron / 拜伦]] helped pull the group toward Geneva.

Her role is mainly connective but important: without Clairmont's Byron link, the [[VillaDiodati|Villa Diodati / 迪奥达蒂别墅]] gathering and its ghost-story challenge become harder to narrate. The source uses her to show how literary history can emerge from travel, desire, weather, and social arrangement.
