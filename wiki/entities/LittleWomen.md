# Little Women / 小妇人

*Little Women* enters the wiki as the famous [[LouisaMayAlcott|Louisa May Alcott / 奥尔科特]] work discussed in [[60-xianliao-weida-zuojiamen-de-bagua-di-yi-dan-648693204]]. The episode uses it less as a plot object than as an author-image problem: the book's wholesome, child-friendly reputation can obscure Alcott's reported frustration with writing for children and her stronger interest in Gothic or darker fiction.

The title had already been named in [[ClassicReadingComplexity]] through [[49-liwuya-lai-le-liaoliao-women-weisha-ai-yuedu-629280098]], where familiar classics are not treated as mandatory reverence or easy dismissal. Episode 60 adds a biographical layer: the work that defines an author for readers may not be the work that best reflects the author's own taste.
