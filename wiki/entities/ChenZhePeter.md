# Chen Zhe Peter

Chen Zhe Peter is the [[AlphaEast]] founding partner invited by [[LateTalk]] for the 2026 Q2 embodied-intelligence review. In [[jushen-jibao-26q2-shijie-moxing-dafeng-buting-he-buxiang-bei-tie-biaoqian-de-ren-1-170-1]], he interprets humanoid robot competitions, logistics demos, dexterous-hand releases, [[Cosmos3]], Physical Intelligence's Pi 0.7, and [[Generalist]] Gen 1 as signals about where robot bodies, data, hands, and model brains may settle in the [[EmbodiedAIValueChain]].

- He treats [[WorldModels]] and [[VisionLanguageActionModels]] as complementary rather than mutually exclusive, making [[WorldModelVLAFusion]] the episode's central model-route synthesis.
