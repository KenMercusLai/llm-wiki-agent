# Noel H. Symington / 诺埃尔·H.赛明顿

Noel H. Symington is identified in [[89-jianqiao-yepanzhe-rang-women-zhuangyan-de-shangfang-jiewa-710382629]] as the real person behind Whipplesnaith, the pseudonymous author of [[TheNightClimbersOfCambridge|《剑桥夜攀者》 / The Night Climbers of Cambridge]]. The episode says the book's photographs often preserve Symington's own climbing body, making him both narrator and participant in the [[CampusNightClimbing]] tradition.

The source also says Symington used the pen name "the butterfly collector", had a weak heart after rheumatic fever, still climbed Mount Kenya several times, and died in May 1970 at age 56. In the episode, those biographical details give the book a retrospective tone: the night-climbing legend becomes tied to a specific fragile, active, and self-mythologizing person rather than only to anonymous student bravado.
