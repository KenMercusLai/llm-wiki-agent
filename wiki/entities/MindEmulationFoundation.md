# Mind Emulation Foundation

Mind Emulation Foundation is the nonprofit [[DanSiroker]] started after [[Optimizely]], discussed in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]]. Dan describes it as an exploration of whether a human mind could eventually be emulated digitally.

The source's most important contribution is not the technical feasibility claim, but the verification problem. A Stanford neuroscientist's question about how one would know whether a mind emulation matched the person pushed Dan toward the more productizable idea of collecting what someone had seen, said, or heard. That line connects the foundation to [[RewindAI]], [[Limitless]], and [[PersonalAIMemory]].
