# Mac Mini

Mac Mini appears in [[tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260227-0227-mp-tech-pod-128]] as the [[Apple]] product selected for a new U.S. manufacturing push in Houston. The episode says this would be the first time the device is produced in the United States, while also noting that the Mac Mini is not one of Apple's most popular products and that most Apple manufacturing remains overseas.

The Mac Mini case makes [[TechManufacturingReshoring]] concrete but limited. It gives Apple a visible U.S. production story while leaving the larger supply-chain and workforce questions unresolved.
