# Crave

Crave is the Canadian streaming service that commissioned [[HeatedRivalry|Heated Rivalry]] in [[the-business-of-heated-rivalry]]. The episode says Crave's license fee covered only part of the budget, making it one component in a broader [[CanadianTelevisionFinancing]] stack rather than the sole funder.

Crave matters because the source treats it as creatively supportive but financially incomplete. The creators say Crave did not pressure them to optimize the show for distracted viewing, which lets the episode connect commissioning structure to [[AttentiveStreamingStorytelling]] and [[ConstraintDrivenMediaProduction]].
