# Mt. Gox

Mt. Gox appears in [[socialradarspod-brianarmstrong-final]] as the early exchange where [[BrianArmstrong]] bought [[Bitcoin]] at about $11 before the price fell to about $2. The episode uses it as part of Armstrong's early personal exposure to crypto before [[Coinbase]] existed.

In the wiki, Mt. Gox is a historical marker for early [[CryptocurrencyMarketStructure]] rather than a detailed exchange history. It helps explain why Coinbase's later [[RegulatedCryptoTrustStrategy]] mattered: early access to Bitcoin was technically and institutionally rough for normal users.
