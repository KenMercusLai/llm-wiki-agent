# vivo

vivo is the smartphone company represented by [[HanBoxiao]] in [[ai-shidai-de-chaoji-rukou-haishi-shouji-ma-s10e17-523a0d42-4c16-4dd6-a2ab-9277fec1a731]]. The episode presents vivo as a terminal-side AI operator that brings user scenarios, foldable-product requirements, system adaptation, application design, and product judgment into cooperation with [[MediaTek]].

In the source, vivo's method is to use scenes to pull technology. The company wants foldable phones to move beyond larger screens toward [[FoldablePhoneProductivity]], adapts smaller AI models for phone hardware, builds system versions for foldable AI workloads, and tests local applications such as meeting assistants, file assistants, and beauty-preference training.

[[268-ai-shidai-geren-gongzuotai-hui-zhongxin-huidao-shouji-ma-lgprs5juhhrjykbzasaqvdlzx8fs]] adds the [[Luanfanshu]] product-discussion version through [[XFold6]]. Vivo's foldable is used as a case for [[MobileAIWorkstation]]: larger-screen multitasking, app groups, AI file management, meeting assistants, and local context can make the phone a task workbench rather than only an AI entry point.

[[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]] adds vivo's historical route from [[BBK]]'s communications business into the "OV" offline-channel model. The episode pairs vivo with [[OPPO]] as a company whose lower-tier retail reach, agent incentives, sales staff, advertising, and after-sales service formed an [[OfflineHandsetChannelSystem]] that online-first rivals could not easily copy.
