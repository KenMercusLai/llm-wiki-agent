# Plato / 柏拉图

Plato / 柏拉图 appears in [[92-bailatu-shangbanji-yong-zhexue-gaoxiao-zhichang-716822350]] through the comic's intern figure, a distant relative of Plato who enters [[ThinkReasonLtd|思考理性有限公司]]. The source uses this apprentice role to let listeners tour the company and meet other philosophers one department at a time.

The episode's most direct Plato joke is the "cave" where luggage is stored, echoing the cave image while turning it into an office onboarding errand. Plato therefore functions less as a doctrinal topic than as the narrative viewpoint for [[PhilosophyWorkplaceSatire]].
