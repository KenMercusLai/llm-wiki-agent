# Qunar

Qunar is a major [[Ctrip]] competitor in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The source describes it as closer to a travel search and price-comparison engine, with business models around aggregation, advertising, and transaction commissions.

The episode uses Qunar to show that [[OnlineTravelAgency]] competition did not come from only one model. Search, comparison, vertical booking, mobile apps, supplier direct connection, and capital integration all pushed the market toward later [[OTAPlatformConcentration]].
