# 前途无量

前途无量 is the Chinese financial drama used in [[ep44-momo-koudai-limian-de-qian-juran-shi-zang-de-li-4f9d60jmelqituybbioyyflxr]] as a narrative hook for discussing bank fraud, fund flows, [[AntiMoneyLaundering]], and why illicit money needs to be disguised before it can be comfortably spent or reintegrated.

The episode does not treat the drama as evidence for a real case. It uses the fictional fraud-company setup to make abstract compliance mechanics more concrete before moving into ordinary account safety, bank monitoring, cross-border movement, and laundering channels.
