# Indeed

Indeed is the job-search and hiring platform whose posting data grounds [[tech-20251217-1217-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20251217-1217-mp-tech-pod-128]]. In the [[MarketplaceTech]] episode, [[CoreyStaley]] uses Indeed's February 2020 baseline to explain why tech job postings have stabilized but remain depressed.

The source makes Indeed useful to the wiki as a labor-demand signal. The reported [[TechJobPostingIndex]] of 67.2 suggests tech postings are about one-third below their pre-pandemic level, while health care postings are described as about 23% above baseline. The episode also uses Indeed postings to show [[AILaborMarketConcentration]]: AI-related skills are requested in only about 4% of postings, and AI language can mean technical AI work, recruiting automation, or sometimes buzzword signaling.
