# 格林兄弟 / Brothers Grimm

格林兄弟 appear in [[111-huayi-modishou-kuailai-he-lishixuejia-yiqi-tuili-tonghua-zhenxiang-751190766]] as a transmission point for [[PiedPiperOfHamelin|花衣魔笛手]]. The episode emphasizes that they placed the story in a German legend collection rather than a fairy-tale collection, which matters because the story claims a specific place, date, number of children, route, memorial markers, and source trail.

In the source, the Brothers Grimm are less an author page than a genre boundary. Their version helps the hosts move from [[AdultFairyTaleReading]] into [[LegendAsSocialHistory]]: a story can become fairy-tale-like in popular memory while still carrying a historical-evidence problem.
