# Tech for Campaigns

Tech for Campaigns appears in [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]] as the organization [[AdoraCheung]] worked with on a 2020 vote-by-mail project. The project focused on making vote-by-mail registration easier and measuring whether voters moved through the funnel to actual ballot return.

In the source, Tech for Campaigns matters less as an institutional history than as the operating vehicle for applying startup-style measurement to civic participation. Cheung and the team tested in primaries, focused on swing states, optimized ads, and used election-office data to track outcomes beyond click-through rates.
