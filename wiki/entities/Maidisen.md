# 麦迪森

麦迪森 is the [[YiLaoYongYi]] host and narrator in [[ep34-dang-gaoqingshang-he-fencungan-queshi-weiyou-gangtie-yizhi-chengchang-lsoyvsj7cppkk-efbxuemdldtrbu]]. In this solo episode, he does not claim to teach high emotional intelligence directly; he uses personal failures to show how [[SocialSignalInterpretation]], [[CommunicationBoundarySetting]], [[WorkplaceCommunicationRisk]], and [[LanguagePrecision]] can break down in ordinary scenes.

In [[ep26-xiang-zuo-ren-shang-zhi-ren-que-kun-zai-cheng-zhong-zhi-cheng-lgbvd08kgko5onekgvnu4aovfz6t]], 麦迪森 joins [[MagicJack]] and [[Xiaodai]] to discuss [[CityInTheCity]] as a bank-workplace case. His contribution connects the episode's colleague/friend boundary advice to the earlier communication theme: do not rush to label coworkers as good or bad, and do not let drama-style side-taking replace professional self-protection.

## Source Position
- He treats "分寸感" as a hard-to-formalize judgment that depends on context, audience, relationship, and timing.
- He uses his younger self as a negative example: too literal in romantic contexts, too slow to set workplace boundaries, and too loose with public phrasing.
- He frames better communication as a defensive habit: pause, draft internally, decide whether the sentence is needed, and avoid making ambiguity worse.
- In EP26, he carries that defensive communication posture into [[WorkplaceRelationshipBoundaries]] and the finance-workplace habit of delaying judgment until roles, incentives, and risks are clearer.
