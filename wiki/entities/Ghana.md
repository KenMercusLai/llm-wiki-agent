# Ghana

Ghana appears in [[latin-lessons-the-donroe-doctrine-boost-6a4233e26b6690f46aae4e3d]] as a contrast to [[Nigeria]] in the [[JollofIndex]] segment. The episode says Ghana's jollof costs have not risen as sharply because inflation returned to single digits after reaching 54% in December 2022 and a stronger currency cushioned import-dependent food baskets.

In the source, Ghana shows that global shocks do not translate mechanically into household food prices. Currency stability and inflation control can change how much diesel, imports, transport, and weather shocks become [[FoodInflation]] for ordinary families.
