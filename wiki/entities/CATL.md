# CATL / 宁德时代

CATL appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as one of the main Chinese power-battery leaders that benefited from the domestic market created by [[TenCitiesThousandVehicles]] and protected by the [[PowerBatteryWhitelist]]. The episode treats CATL as a long-term global leader rather than only a successful supplier to one automaker.

The source also uses CATL as a "chain-anchor" case for [[Yibin]]. Attracting CATL is presented as a decisive step because one large battery producer can pull materials, equipment, talent, and service suppliers into a local industrial system.
