# 魏熙

魏熙 appears in [[263-sora-si-le-adobe-die-le-meitu-he-qu-he-cong-lgjmyveooc8wpzr0yviggvzvdyfs]] as the guest who brings former advertising and [[Meitu]] product experience into the discussion of AI tools. The source says he previously worked on advertising products at Weibo and Meitu and now writes about technology.

His main role in the episode is to explain why [[Meitu]] can still have room if it uses product taste, user data, scenario knowledge, and [[ModelContainerStrategy]] rather than trying to compete head-on as a foundation-model company. He also distinguishes generic model output from [[VerticalWorkflowAI]] that handles quality, consistency, delivery, and business outcomes.
