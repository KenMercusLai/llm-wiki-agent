# Tracy Young

Tracy Young is the [[PlanGrid]] co-founder and [[TigerEye]] co-founder interviewed by [[JessicaLivingston]] and [[CarolynLevy]] in [[socialradarsseason1-tracyyoung-final-1]]. The episode traces her path from civil engineering and construction-site work into a software company built around [[ConstructionBlueprintVersionControl]].

Young's contribution to the wiki is the way she turns lived workflow pain into company design. At PlanGrid, she knew why paper drawings, job-site mobility, and version control mattered. At TigerEye, she applies [[SecondTimeFounderOperatingJudgment]] by being more explicit about values, hiring, architecture, remote work, spouse co-founding boundaries, and family support.
