# Israel Acabla

Israel Acabla is the women's clothing brand founded by [[EmmaFiquade]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-kenneth-cole-83717cb6-e000-4cde-898d-792249545f88]]. The brand makes dresses, separates, and outerwear in Toronto and is described by Emma as bringing more personality to a minimal aesthetic, with inspiration from her engineering background.

The source frames Israel Acabla as a wholesale-to-DTC relationship problem. Hudson's Bay and Nordstrom help distribution, and wholesale is about 85% of revenue, but that reach has not translated into direct customer growth. [[GuyRaz]] suggests cards, QR registration, repair or care programs, exclusive drops, and other mechanisms to invite wholesale shoppers into the brand, while [[KennethCole]] argues that a store can become a controlled brand environment when wholesale does not let the founder tell the full story.
