# Luigi Galvani / 路易吉·伽伐尼

Luigi Galvani appears in [[141-zhizao-fulankensitan-kehuan-zhimu-he-tade-shenqi-shidai-857923931]] through the frog-leg experiments that helped produce the [[AnimalElectricityDebate|animal electricity debate]]. The episode treats Galvani's work as one reason electricity could be imagined as connected to life itself, not only as a physical curiosity.

In the source, Galvani's argument is later challenged by [[AlessandroVolta|Alessandro Volta]], while his nephew [[GiovanniAldini|Giovanni Aldini]] carries galvanic demonstration toward the theatrical body-animation scenes that shaped [[GalvanicReanimationImaginary]] around [[Frankenstein|《弗兰肯斯坦》]].
