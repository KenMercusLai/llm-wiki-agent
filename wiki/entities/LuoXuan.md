# 罗玄 / Luo Xuan

罗玄 appears in [[268-ai-shidai-geren-gongzuotai-hui-zhongxin-huidao-shouji-ma-lgprs5juhhrjykbzasaqvdlzx8fs]] as a [[Luanfanshu]] guest discussing AI hardware, phones, agents, and foldable productivity. His main role is to test whether new AI devices create enough new value to pull users away from the phone.

He argues that AI glasses and similar devices often feel like digital products placed into a new shape rather than products designed from the original use case. In the same discussion, he treats context as a major bottleneck for personal agents: phones matter because they already contain photographs, habits, files, communication patterns, and other signals that can make [[SmartphoneAIHub]] and [[MobileAIWorkstation]] behavior more useful.
