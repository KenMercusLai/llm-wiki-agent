# Nicolas Anelka

Nicolas Anelka appears in [[how-to-win-a-penalty-shootout-with-game-theory]] as the [[ChelseaFC]] player whose saved penalty ended the 2008 Champions League final shootout. The episode says [[EdwinVanDerSar]] signaled before Anelka's shot that he had detected Chelsea's repeated direction choice.

Anelka's miss illustrates the psychological side of [[PenaltyShootoutStrategy]]. Once an opponent shows awareness of a data-driven pattern, the taker faces a new problem: follow the prepared plan and look predictable, or deviate under pressure and risk choosing a lower-quality option.
