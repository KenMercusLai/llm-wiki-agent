# John Doerr

John Doerr appears in [[tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2-tsr-s4-ronconway-part3-v2]] as the [[KleinerPerkins]] investor [[LarryPage]] and [[SergeyBrin]] wanted in [[Google]]'s 1999 financing. In [[RonConway]]'s account, the founders valued Kleiner partly because of its [[AOL]] relationship, not only because of capital.

The episode uses Doerr as part of [[VentureSyndicateOrchestration]]: Google wanted both Kleiner and [[SequoiaCapital]] in the same round even though the firms did not normally invest together at that time.
