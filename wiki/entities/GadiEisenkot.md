# Gadi Eisenkot

Gadi Eisenkot appears in [[youve-come-a-long-way-bibi-israels-crucial-election-6a59f9ae461a6a41901ae410]] as a former head of the IDF who entered [[BenjaminNetanyahu]]'s early war cabinet after October 7th, then resigned after criticizing the prime minister's strategy. The episode says he has since risen in the polls.

The source uses Eisenkot as a bridge between security credibility and opposition politics. His role suggests that [[IsraeliElection2026]] is not only about party identity but also about how voters judge wartime leadership and military strategy after October 7th.
