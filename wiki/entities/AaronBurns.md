# Aaron Burns

Aaron Burns appears in [[tech-20260713-tech-pod-128-tech-20260713-tech-pod-128]] as the [[Carbon180]] speaker explaining the federal funding status of [[DirectAirCapture]] projects. He says the Trump administration rescinded Biden-era funding for several projects, but that [[UnitedStatesCongress|Congress]] protected a planned [[Louisiana]] hub and a [[Texas]] hub backed by [[OccidentalPetroleum|Occidental Petroleum]].

Burns's role is to connect [[CarbonRemoval]] to [[CarbonRemovalPoliticalDurability]]. The episode uses his comments to show that direct air capture can retain support when host-state interests, industrial policy, and bipartisan climate-tech arguments overlap, even while other climate initiatives are cut.
