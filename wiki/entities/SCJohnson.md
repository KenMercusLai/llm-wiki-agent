# SC Johnson

SC Johnson is the consumer-products company that acquired [[SunBum]] in 2019, reportedly for $400 million, according to [[sun-bum-tom-rinks-the-secrets-of-a-master-brand-builder-2023-77ab41eb-99a8-4dd2-ae39-d066818afc21]]. The source frames the sale as a stage-fit decision rather than only a liquidity event.

Tom Rinks says international sunscreen regulation required manufacturing, offices, and regulatory capacity that a larger partner could supply better than the founding team. In that sense SC Johnson becomes the CPG acquirer that could carry Sun Bum beyond the founder-led and independent-operator stage.
