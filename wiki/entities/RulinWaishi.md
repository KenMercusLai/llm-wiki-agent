# 《儒林外史》

《儒林外史》 is the Qing-dynasty satirical novel at the center of [[58-rulin-waishi-jiashe-kaogong-chengle-weiyi-chulu-645207859]], a [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode that reads it beyond the school-text memory of [[FanJin|范进]] passing the exam. The episode presents the novel as a [[SatiricalGroupPortrait]] of scholars, gentry, families, officials, artists, and ordinary people living under a status order shaped by [[ImperialExaminationAsOnlyExit]].

The source emphasizes that the novel's force comes from indirectness. [[WuJingzi|吴敬梓]] lets stories about [[ZhouJin|周进]], Fan Jin, Yan Gongsheng, Yan Jiansheng, [[DuShaoqing|杜少卿]], [[WangMian|王冕]], Shen Qiongzhi, and the city eccentrics accumulate until the reader sees how credential hunger and official hierarchy deform ordinary relationships. That is why the episode links the book to [[WanErDuoFeng|婉而多讽]] rather than only to blunt anti-exam criticism.
