# He Xiaopeng / 何小鹏

He Xiaopeng is the [[XPeng]] founder, chair, and CEO interviewed in [[143-dui-he-xiaopeng-de-di-er-ci-fangtan-gengda-duzhu-renxing-jiqiren-iron-dansheng-nachang-yiwai-jishu-jubian-xia-ceo-gx-he-fengheguai-ljekstsafrj-ovtm2bpl92s4nwoc]]. The source frames him as a CEO trying to move XPeng from smart electric cars into [[PhysicalAI]], with [[XPengIron]], [[XPengGX]], autonomous driving, and globalization treated as connected strategic bets.

In the episode, He describes stopping a heavily funded older autonomous-driving system because he believed [[StitchedAIArchitecture]] could not reach true driverless capability or generalized robotics. His CEO role is presented as choosing when to make a high-variance technical bet, reorganizing around it, and accepting short-term instability when the old route has lower long-term ceiling.
