# 丁香医生

Dingxiang Doctor is the public-facing health-content and consumer-health branch of [[DXY]] in [[no-206-jiansuo-songyao-kanbing-hulianwang-yiliao-zhexie-nian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-22-991273500]]. The episode says it evolved from earlier medication-reference products such as "家庭用药" and became one of the visible professional voices in Chinese online medical popularization.

Its importance in the source is not only as an app brand. The 2018 article challenging Quanjian's claimed treatments is used to show how professional medical content can counter health misinformation, shape public scrutiny, and strengthen [[MedicalPlatformTrustCrisis]] as an industry boundary.
