# Vitus Bering / 维图斯·白令

Vitus Bering is the Danish-born sailor and Russian naval officer at the center of [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. The episode presents him as the commander of the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]] and later the expanded [[GreatNorthernExpedition]], linking his life to [[PeterTheGreat]]'s maritime ambitions and the [[RussianEmpire|Russian Empire]]'s eastward reach.

The source does not treat Bering as a simple heroic discoverer. His caution around the [[BeringStrait]], the divided authority of the second expedition, illness aboard the Saint Peter, and death on [[BeringIsland]] are all used to show how exploration depends on [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]], weather, bureaucracy, ships, food, and the survival of subordinates as much as personal courage.
