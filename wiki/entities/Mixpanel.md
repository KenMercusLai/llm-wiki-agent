# Mixpanel

Mixpanel is named in [[tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio-tsr-s5-spenserskates-v2audio]] as an existing analytics tool in the market when [[SpenserSkates]] and [[CurtisLiu]] were searching for better ways to understand [[Sonalight]] user retention. The source does not give a full Mixpanel history; it uses Mixpanel as one of the products that still did not solve the founders' specific behavioral questions.

The comparison helps define [[Amplitude]]'s wedge. Amplitude's source story is not "analytics did not exist"; it is that product teams needed more actionable retention and behavior analysis than the founders could get from the tools they tried.
