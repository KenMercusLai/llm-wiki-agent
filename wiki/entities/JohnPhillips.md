# John Phillips

John Phillips is the angel investor and mentor discussed in [[shopify-tobias-lutke-how-a-snowboarder-built-a-150-billion-business-2019-d0b07b6a-125e-4896-babd-678d19957306]]. The episode says he invested about $400,000 in [[Shopify]] around 2007, when the company had paying customers but was trending toward zero cash.

## Key Points
- Entered after [[TobiasLutke]] and [[ScottLake]] had already moved from [[Snowdevil]] toward Shopify.
- Provided capital during a period when Shopify's growth was real but not yet explosive enough to remove cash pressure.
- Became a mentor as Toby was still learning whether Shopify should be a lifestyle business or a venture-scale company.
