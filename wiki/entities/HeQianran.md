# 何倩然 / He Qianran

He Qianran appears in [[e229-cong-shougong-zuofang-dao-quanqiu-diyi-zhongguo-dongli-dianchi-nixi-shi]] as a guest with materials-science, battery R&D, and investment background. She frames global power-battery competition as a long marathon in which [[China]] has moved ahead through scale, supply-chain closure, and manufacturing process knowledge, while the [[UnitedStates]] remains stronger in some original technologies and startup research directions.

Her contribution is to separate invention from industrial victory. She argues that American labs produced or advanced important battery chemistries, but [[BatteryManufacturingKnowHow]], [[PowerBatteryIndustryChain]] density, and patient factory iteration decide whether those technologies become mass production.
