# Accel

Accel appears in [[tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3-tsr-s4-alexandrwang-v3]] through [[DanLevine]]'s pre-Demo-Day funding offer to [[ScaleAI]]. [[AlexandrWang]] says Scale's metrics were only "just okay," but [[YCombinator]] group partners advised the founders to accept the offer.

The source uses Accel as part of Scale's early financing path rather than as a broader firm profile. The investment context reinforces Scale as a [[StartupHighBetaBet]]: an unsexy data-labeling business whose upside depended on AI models becoming much more data-hungry.

[[tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio-tsr-s5-tomblomfield-v2-audio]] adds a fintech instance: [[TomBlomfield]] says [[GoCardless]] raised about $1.5 million from Accel and [[PassionCapital]] after [[YCombinator]] Demo Day before returning to London.
