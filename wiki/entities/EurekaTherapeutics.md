# Eureka Therapeutics

Eureka Therapeutics is the [[LiuCheng|刘诚]]-led biotech company discussed in [[e235-20-nian-nei-car-t-zhiyu-aizheng-yu-liucheng-boshi-liaoliao-aizheng-zhiliao-de-diceng-zhexue-90f96f60-25be-45ac-b832-56776a23d534]]. The source says the company began working on [[CARTCellTherapy|CAR-T]] in 2014 and focuses especially on using CAR-T for late-stage solid tumors.

The company's role in the source is not a full company history. It grounds Liu's claims about [[SolidTumorCARTConstraints]], including the difficulty of getting engineered immune cells into tumor tissue, keeping them active inside the [[TumorMicroenvironment]], and making the therapy safe enough to scale. The episode says Eureka's liver-cancer project is in U.S. phase II clinical development and is planned to move toward phase III.

## Key Claims
- Eureka is presented as a CAR-T company oriented toward solid-tumor use rather than only blood cancers.
- Its liver-cancer program is used as the concrete company context for Liu's optimism about CAR-T in difficult cancers.
- The source treats the company as an example of why the next CAR-T frontier is efficacy in solid tumors, not only cheaper manufacturing.
