# Yosano Akiko / 与谢野晶子

Yosano Akiko / 与谢野晶子 appears in [[shaqingqing-x-hanzhao-shichuan-zhuomu-wo-baochi-de-sixiang-doushi-yinwei-queqian-894931314]] through an anecdote about [[IshikawaTakuboku|石川啄木]] borrowing money from her by invoking his mother's illness and then being seen drinking with friends. The episode uses the story as an example of Takuboku's irresponsible behavior and persistent ability to attract support.

Yosano's role is brief but useful because it places Takuboku in a tanka and literary network wider than his immediate friends. It also keeps [[LiteraryGossipAsContext]] active: the anecdote illuminates support, charisma, and moral discomfort without becoming a complete explanation of either poet.

## Key Claims
- Yosano marks the wider literary milieu that could still respond to Takuboku's need or talent.
- The borrowing anecdote is stored as source-scoped biography, not as a full account of her work.
- The episode uses the story to keep Takuboku's charm and exploitation visible together.
