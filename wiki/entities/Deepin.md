# Deepin

Deepin is the Chinese Linux distribution lineage discussed in [[nage-bu-chuan-xizhuang-de-chengxuyuan-chechule-guochan-caozuoxitong-ershi-nian-mishi-keji-luandun]]. The episode traces it from [[HiweedLinux]] and Chinese localization work into [[WuhanDeepinTechnology]], then into the later split between a continuing community distribution and [[TongxinUOS]] as the commercial government-enterprise product.

## Source Position
- Deepin is framed as a community and technical project before it became part of a more commercial and institutional operating-system story.
- The episode says Deepin's DDE desktop matured after early financing and certification milestones.
- A possible GTK+ versus QT route disagreement is mentioned as an unconfirmed explanation for early personnel divergence.
- Deepin becomes the source's main example of [[OpenSourceCommunityCommercialization]].

## Connections
- [[HiweedLinux]] — predecessor distribution.

- [[LiuWenhuan]], [[WangYong]], and [[ZhangLei]] — company-stage figures in the source.
