# Federal Law Enforcement Training Centers

Federal Law Enforcement Training Centers appear in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] as the training infrastructure for recruits in agencies such as [[USImmigrationAndCustomsEnforcement|ICE]], [[USBorderPatrol]], and [[USCustomsAndBorderProtection|CBP]]. The episode says the Trump administration's tax and spending law allocated $750 million to the centers.

The source uses [[MarkBrown]]'s account of the main Georgia campus to show that [[FederalLawEnforcementTraining]] includes realistic scenarios, crowd-control instruction, firearms facilities, and a simulated city. The unresolved issue is whether rapid hiring changed training quality or quantity enough to matter once recruits enter field culture.
