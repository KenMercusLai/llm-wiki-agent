# U.S. Mint

The U.S. Mint appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] through the episode's discussion of ending new penny production. The source says the [[USTreasury|U.S. Treasury]] and Mint are going to stop minting new pennies, while existing pennies remain legal tender.

In the wiki, the Mint becomes the institutional node for [[CoinRetirementEconomics]]. The episode's stated reasons are that a penny costs more than one cent to make and that digital payment habits have reduced the coin's practical role.
