# RIAA

The RIAA appears in [[tsr-ronconway-part4-v2-tsr-ronconway-part4-v2]] as the music-industry trade association that sued [[Napster]] and helped turn the company from a breakthrough consumer product into a legal shutdown case. [[RonConway]] says the organization had major legal resources and pursued symbolic enforcement while Napster was becoming a mass cultural phenomenon.

The source also shows attempted repair. Conway says Napster brought monetization ideas to early Los Angeles meetings and that [[Snowcap]] later required renewed conversations with label leaders and RIAA-associated figures. The RIAA page therefore anchors both [[CopyrightPlatformConflict]] and [[DigitalMusicLicensing]] in this wiki.
