# Alexis Ohanian

Alexis Ohanian appears in [[socialradarspod-stevehuffman-final]] as [[SteveHuffman]]'s college friend and [[Reddit]] co-founder. He and Huffman traveled from UVA to Boston to meet [[PaulGraham]], applied to [[YCombinator]] with a mobile food-ordering idea, rejected a potential mismatched third founder, and turned around on the train after Graham invited them back for a different idea.

In the early Reddit story, Ohanian worked on naming, the domain, and the alien doodle before Huffman began writing code. The episode treats the Huffman-Ohanian pairing as an early example of founder compatibility mattering before the first product idea had been validated.
