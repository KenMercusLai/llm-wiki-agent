# 牛李党争 / Niu-Li Party Struggle

The 牛李党争 / Niu-Li Party Struggle is the late-Tang factional conflict used by [[76-xuanguailu-wantang-dangzheng-meidanwu-zaixiang-xie-damahou-de-gushi-683323220]] to place [[NiuSengru|牛僧儒]] before reading [[XuanGuaiLu|《玄怪录》]]. The episode names [[LiDeyu|李德裕]], [[LiZongmin|李宗闵]], and 牛僧儒 as central figures, and treats the conflict as more than personal animus.

The source frames the struggle as a mixture of faction, social background, policy disagreement, and reputation warfare. That makes it useful for [[LateTangPoliticalPoetics]]: literary authorship, suspected forgery, and moral accusation can become part of political conflict alongside court appointments and frontier policy.
