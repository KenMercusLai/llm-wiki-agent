# 芒果TV / Mango TV

Mango TV is the long-video platform tied to Hunan Broadcasting System in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode presents it as a distinctive case because it controlled valuable TV variety supply and chose to stop reselling key content to other platforms.

Mango TV's strategic asset was not only a streaming product, but content ownership and broadcast-production capability. Exclusive access to shows such as "Happy Camp," "Day Day Up," and later online variety formats helped it grow and become a rare profitable Chinese video platform.
