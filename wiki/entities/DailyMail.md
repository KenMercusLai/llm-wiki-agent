# Daily Mail

Daily Mail appears in [[tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260220-0220-mp-tech-pod-128]] as a publisher example in the debate over [[GoogleAIOverviews|Google AI Overviews]]. The episode says the Daily Mail's owner attributed a search click-through decline of as much as 89% partly to AI Overviews.

The wiki relevance is not a complete media-company profile. Daily Mail is a concrete case for [[OpenWebTrafficDecline]] and [[AIAnswerSourceAttribution]]: when AI search answers satisfy a user's query on the results page, publishers may still be cited but receive fewer visits, ad impressions, subscriptions, or direct reader relationships.
