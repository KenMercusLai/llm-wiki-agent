# Bloomberg

Bloomberg enters this wiki branch as the reported source of internal [[Netflix]] data cited in [[tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260715-0715-mp-tech-pod-128]]. The Marketplace Tech episode uses that reporting to ground the claim that returning seasons of shows such as [[TheNightAgent]], [[BeefSeries|Beef]], and [[AvatarTheLastAirbender|Avatar: The Last Airbender]] lost large shares of their original viewers.

The wiki treats the numbers as source-reported rather than independently verified. Bloomberg's role here is to make the [[StreamingAudienceRetention]] problem concrete enough for [[BrandonKatz]] to discuss Netflix's release model, production timing, catalog abundance, and cancellation economics.

[[tech-20260717-0717-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260717-0717-mp-tech-pod-128]] adds Bloomberg as the reported source for two technology-policy examples cited by [[MarketplaceTech]]: the expected shape of [[OpenAI]]'s [[JonyIve]]-linked device and a robotaxi operational edge case involving passengers asleep or unresponsive in [[Waymo]] vehicles. In this episode, Bloomberg reporting helps connect [[AIPlusTerminals]] and [[RobotaxiHybridDeployment]] to concrete product and city-operation details.
