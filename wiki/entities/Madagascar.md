# Madagascar / 马达加斯加

Madagascar is the first major travel setting in [[186-rang-ni-xiao-dao-tou-diao-reng-bu-wang-sikao-weida-de-xiaoshi-shijie-manyou-zhinan-998655210]]. In the episode's reading of [[LastChanceToSee|《消逝世界漫游指南》]], [[DouglasAdams]] and [[MarkCarwardine]] travel there to find the [[AyeAye|aye-aye]], encountering broken logistics and a deeper refuge paradox.

The island matters because the episode presents it as a former no-human refuge that later had to be subdivided into smaller human-made refuges. That makes Madagascar a setting for [[IslandExtinctionAfterHumanArrival]], [[ConservationIntervention]], and the source's recurring question of what humans should do after they have already changed the baseline.
