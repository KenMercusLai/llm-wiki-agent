# Jeremy Bentham / 边沁

Jeremy Bentham / 边沁 appears in [[100-fa-lv-de-bei-lun-xie-tian-xie-di-xing-hao-wo-men-hai-you-luo-xiang-733477302]] as the utilitarian reference for punishment theory. The episode uses Bentham's greatest-happiness frame to explain why punishment can be justified as a painful tool for reducing larger social harm.

The source also marks the danger in that logic. If punishment becomes only a calculation of deterrence, individuals can be used as examples and light offenses can be overpunished, which ties Bentham's role to [[PunishmentTheoryTradeoff]] and [[CriminalLawAsPowerLimitation]].
