# New South Wales

New South Wales is the Australian state setting for the water-market case in [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]]. The episode places [[CarlyMarriott]]'s sheep and crop farm in a drought-prone region connected to irrigation from the [[MurrayRiver]].

The state matters because the source is not describing water in the abstract. It uses a specific farming region to show how [[WaterRightsTrading]] can move scarce water toward higher-value uses while exposing growers to price spikes, information gaps, and political conflict.
