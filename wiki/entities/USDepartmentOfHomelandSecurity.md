# U.S. Department of Homeland Security

The U.S. Department of Homeland Security is the government agency at the center of [[tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260302-0302-mp-tech-pod-128]], which discusses lawsuits and concerns over how DHS collects or accesses private-sector data. [[JeremyScott]] says DHS can obtain data through [[AdministrativeSubpoenaDataAccess]], purchases from brokers, and [[SurveillanceAsAService]] tools.

The episode treats DHS as a case for the broader question of whether a government agency can use private companies as a data-acquisition layer. The concern is not only one agency's demand for information, but the policy gap created when privately collected data is searchable by law enforcement.
