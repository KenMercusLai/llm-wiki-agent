# Murray-Darling Basin

The Murray-Darling Basin is the broader southern-basin water-market context behind [[our-mission-find-the-worlds-best-economic-ideas-summer-school-world-tour]]. The episode says [[NeilHughes]]' team estimated that water trading in the southern basin produced benefits worth about 12% of water rights, or around $117 million per year.

The basin is the economic geography where [[WaterRightsTrading]] turns water into a tradable allocation problem. The source links that efficiency story to backlash from farmers such as [[CarlyMarriott]], making the basin a case where scarcity pricing, political legitimacy, and [[ClimateAdaptation]] meet.
