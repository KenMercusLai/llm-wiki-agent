# Peak AI

Peak AI is the [[AISearchAnalytics]] SaaS company discussed in [[ai-startup-hits-8-6m-arr-with-v0-mvp-and-eur85-pricing]]. According to [[MariusMiners]], it helps brands understand how they appear in AI answer and search tools such as [[ChatGPT]], [[Perplexity]], and [[Gemini]], including whether the brand is cited and how it is described.

## Key Claims
- The episode reports that Peak reached more than 2,000 customers, $8.6 million ARR, and 55 team members within 14 months.
- Peak began with a quickly built AI-assisted prototype, used that artifact for LOIs, and then rebuilt production software before launch.
- The product category had a clear enough roadmap that competitive advantage shifted toward execution speed, hiring, fundraising, product quality, and customer conversion.
- Peak positioned around mid-market and SMB accessibility rather than only enterprise pilots and high prices.
- Its own acquisition channels included social content, word of mouth, and [[GenerativeEngineOptimization]].
