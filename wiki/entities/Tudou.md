# 土豆网 / Tudou

Tudou is the early Chinese video platform founded by [[WangWeiTudou]] and highlighted in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]] as the more creator-community-oriented counterpart to [[Youku]]. The episode emphasizes its "everyone is a director" positioning, early UGC culture, and role in surfacing web-native creators.

Tudou's story also shows how capital timing and legal risk shaped platform fate. Wang Wei's divorce dispute slowed its IPO window, the company listed later than Youku, then merged with Youku in 2012 as copyright costs and content spending intensified.
