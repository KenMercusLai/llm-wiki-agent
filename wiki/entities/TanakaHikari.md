# 田中光

田中光 is the Japanese scholar named in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the author of [[JoseiEiseiYouhinNoShakaishi|《女性卫生用品的社会史》]]. The episode uses the book to introduce a research field around menstruation, sanitary products, public shame, and everyday material culture.

The wiki treats Tanaka here through the source's use of her work rather than as a complete biography. Her importance is methodological: she gives [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] a way to connect [[MenstrualStigma]], [[CommercialMenstrualEducation]], [[PackagingAsProductExperience]], and [[MenstrualPublicInfrastructure]] through a single product category.
