# 古永锵 / Gu Yongqiang

Gu Yongqiang is the [[Youku]] founder discussed in [[no-204-hulianwang-shipin-pingtai-hunzhan-cong-houshe-nansheng-dao-manchangde-jijie-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-21-980902194]]. The episode presents him as a Sohu-system executive who built Youku around speed, stability, and infrastructure at a time when online video still felt technically fragile.

His role in the wiki is to anchor the product-and-infrastructure route of early Chinese online video. After [[Youku]]'s public-market high point, he led the merged Youku-Tudou company and later entered [[Alibaba]]'s entertainment system after acquisition.
