# 克拉帕沃丘斯 / Klapaucius

克拉帕沃丘斯 / Klapaucius is one of the machine constructors in [[TheCyberiad|《机器人大师》 / The Cyberiad]], discussed in [[45-jiqirendashi-duo-xiwang-laimu-neng-pingjia-yixia-chatgpt-a-621855112]]. The episode presents him as [[Trurl|特鲁勒]]'s rival and counterpart, often testing Trurl's creations, sharing royal commissions, and turning technical brilliance into comic competition.

In the N-making-machine story, his challenge pushes the machine from harmless creation into the dangerous command to create nothingness. In other stories, he joins Trurl in inventing military-intelligence schemes, handling probability dragons, and escaping or profiting from absurd technical consequences.
