# Wang Shengli

Wang Shengli is the founder/operator associated with [[ModernExpressTravel]] in [[kunzai-xitong-li-de-jiudian-ni-buzhidao-de-xiecheng-longduan-lianchengshi-keji-luandun]]. The source says his company already had meaningful hotel-booking volume before [[Ctrip]] acquired it and brought Wang and the team into Ctrip's northern operation.

He matters in the source as an example of how early OTA advantage accumulated through people and process acquisition, not only software.
