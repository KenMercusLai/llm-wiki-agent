# L-Design

L-Design is the next model milestone [[HaotianOdin]] names for [[YinglingdianAI]] in [[ai4s-xuyao-kuangren-yu-yexinjia-duihua-yinglingdian-odin-ruguo-shen-cunzai-wo-zenneng-rongren-ziji-bushi-shen-gonglu-boke-lhceyip6dqomrwk38uvqjwoomxyz]]. The source presents it as closer to a [[AllModalMolecularWorldModel|molecular world model]] than the earlier [[ODesign|O-Design / Odyssey]] exploration.

The examples in the episode are cross-modal: designing proteins from small molecules, designing small molecules from small molecules, and designing DNA aptamers from proteins for detection. L-Design therefore matters as a model-name anchor for the company's broader claim that life-related molecular modalities should be modeled together rather than through isolated protein, small-molecule, RNA, or DNA systems.
