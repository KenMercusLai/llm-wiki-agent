# Mitsubishi Corporation / 三菱商事

Mitsubishi Corporation appears in [[vol-108-riben-wuda-zonghe-shangshe-zhongfan-wutai-zhongyang-lhamp3hn14iihcicpa8po5dvh6gq]] as one of the five major [[JapaneseSogoShosha|Japanese sogo shosha]]. The episode places it in the older zaibatsu lineage with [[MitsuiAndCo|Mitsui]] and [[SumitomoCorporation|Sumitomo]], emphasizing that the company is better read as trade, finance, information, and supply-chain infrastructure than as a narrow commodity trader.

The source uses Mitsubishi's food supply chain as a vertical-integration case: food ingredients, aquaculture and processing, meat products, suppliers, and retail links are treated as a coordinated network. The point is not that Mitsubishi must own every link outright, but that a sogo shosha can use stakes, commercial relationships, and logistics to make a chain more resilient.
