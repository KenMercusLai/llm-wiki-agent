# Battle of Kunyang / 昆阳之战

The Battle of Kunyang / 昆阳之战 is the military turning point used in [[126-wangmang-zhisi-shensheng-diguo-de-huixuanbiao-he-huixuanbiao-de-huixuanbiao-786255780]] to contrast [[WangMang|王莽]]'s symbolic regime with battlefield reality. The episode describes Wang Mang's forces as enormous and oddly composed in the sources, while [[LiuXiu|刘秀]] breaks out, seeks reinforcements, and wins a key victory.

The battle's wiki role is conceptual as much as military. It makes the gap visible between [[IdeologicalOvercontrol]], ritual grandeur, and actual political competence during the fall of the [[XinDynasty|Xin dynasty]].

## Key Claims
- Kunyang is presented as evidence that Wang Mang's state power looked larger than it functioned.
- Liu Xiu's success becomes part of the Liu restoration story.
- The battle sharpens the episode's contrast between signs of legitimacy and usable capacity.
