# Brett Baechek

Brett Baechek is named in [[tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3-tsr-s3-dansiroker-v3]] as one of [[Limitless]]'s two co-founders promoted from within by [[DanSiroker]]. The source says Brett was already doing co-founder-level work before receiving the formal title.

The episode uses Brett's promotion less as a biography than as a founder-structure lesson: Dan argues that promoting co-founders from within can be lower risk because values, work quality, and operating relationship are already visible.
