# Martin Spangberg / 马丁·斯潘贝格

Martin Spangberg is one of the Bering expedition officers in [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. In the [[FirstKamchatkaExpedition]], the source highlights his brutal transport ordeal through Siberian rivers, ice, snow, hunger, and equipment hauling as a concrete example of [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]].

In the [[GreatNorthernExpedition]], Spangberg is associated with the Japan and Kuril route thread. The episode presents him as discovering and mapping islands around northern [[Japan]] and the Kurils, while also clashing with [[VitusBering]] and [[AlexeiChirikov]] inside the expedition's unstable command field.
