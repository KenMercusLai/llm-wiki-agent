# Adora Cheung

Adora Cheung is the founder interviewed in [[tsr-s2-adoracheung-v5]]. The episode follows her from [[PathJoy]] and [[Homejoy]] through five years as a [[YCombinator]] partner, a 2020 vote-by-mail project with [[TechForCampaigns]], and her current company [[Instalab]].

Her core operating pattern is direct contact with the work. She cleaned homes herself to understand Homejoy supply, talked directly to Wisconsin voters before building a vote-by-mail funnel, and started Instalab from her own post-startup health problems. The episode turns her into a case for [[CustomerDiscoveryByDoingWork]], [[FounderProductFit]], [[FullFunnelCivicTechnology]], and [[FounderHealthDebt]].
