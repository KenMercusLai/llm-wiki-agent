# Pete Walker

Pete Walker appears in [[181-taohao-bingfei-nide-xingge-jianchi-zheme-jiu-xinku-le-979027261]] as the source the episode cites for the [[FourFTraumaResponse|4F trauma response]] vocabulary: fight, flight, freeze, and fawn. The episode uses this frame to argue that "讨好" is not a soft personality flaw but an automatic survival response under threat.

The key contribution in this source is conceptual. Walker's name lets the episode place fawning alongside other body-level threat responses instead of treating it as moral weakness, excessive kindness, or bad communication style.
