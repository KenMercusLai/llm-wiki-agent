# Russian Empire / 俄罗斯帝国

The Russian Empire is the historical state context for [[120-lanhudao-weida-shiye-zhong-de-yexin-yu-xianshi-772998218]]. The episode places [[VitusBering]] inside a larger imperial project shaped by [[PeterTheGreat]]'s westernization, sea-power ambition, Siberian administration, and desire to compete with other European colonial and trading powers.

The source's Russian Empire is not only a command center in Saint Petersburg. It is also a loose and coercive field of governors, forts, Cossacks, tax officials, Indigenous communities, forced requisitions, transport bottlenecks, and remote shipyards. That makes [[ImperialExplorationLogistics]] and [[RussianNorthPacificExpansion]] central to understanding the episode's version of exploration.
