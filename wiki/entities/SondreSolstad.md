# Sondre Solstad

Sondre Solstad appears in [[a-hawk-who-flew-on-political-winds-lindsey-graham-6a54b56575790d5f01515d55]] as the Economist participant explaining why the team tested frontier AI models with human value surveys. His role is to turn a vague concern about model bias into [[AIModelValueSurveying]]: asking models questions that can be compared with countries, voters, and political-cultural clusters.

Solstad's warning is practical rather than metaphysical. The source says AI bias may matter less for narrow errands or coding tasks, but becomes consequential when people ask models for advice, counsel, family decisions, or companionship. That makes his segment a bridge from survey measurement to [[AIAdviceMoralOutsourcing]] and [[HumanJudgmentUnderAI]].
