# Institute For Replication

The Institute for Replication is the organization [[AbelBrodeur]] created in 2022 in [[dont-hate-the-replicator-hate-the-game]]. The episode says Brodeur needed more than individual requests for data and code, so he built an institutional wrapper that made authors, journals, and participants take replication outreach more seriously.

Within the wiki, the institute anchors the governance side of [[ReplicationCrisis]] repair. It coordinates [[ReplicationGames]], sends neutral author-notification emails, gives authors a chance to respond, and helps junior researchers participate in [[CrowdsourcedAcademicAuditing]] without bearing all the interpersonal risk alone.
