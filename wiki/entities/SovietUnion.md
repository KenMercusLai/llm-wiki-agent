# Soviet Union

The Soviet Union appears in [[dark-times-for-cubas-economic-experiment]] as [[Cuba]]'s first major external patron after the 1959 revolution. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode says the Soviet Union bought Cuban goods above market value and sold Cuba oil below market value, letting Cuba's state-led system survive under the U.S. embargo despite poverty and economic inefficiency.

The Soviet collapse in 1991 becomes the first major test of [[ExternalPatronDependence]]. The source says Cuba's GDP fell by 35%, food became scarcer, and the Special Period began after cheap oil and favorable trade terms disappeared.
