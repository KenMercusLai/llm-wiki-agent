# Adam Smith / 亚当·斯密

Adam Smith / 亚当·斯密 appears in [[61-zicong-yongyou-jingjixue-de-siwei-fangshi-rensheng-dou-bian-jiandan-le-650559353]] through the episode's discussion of the invisible hand and the social order behind seemingly separate individual choices. The source uses morning traffic as an accessible route into Smith's question: how can a complex society coordinate many people who have different destinations, plans, and needs?

The episode also uses Smith to soften a caricature of economics as cold money-worship. The host notes that Smith was not only associated with market order but also with moral questions, helping the source frame economics as rational analysis with social concern rather than as a replacement for moral judgment.
