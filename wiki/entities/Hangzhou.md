# 杭州 / Hangzhou

Hangzhou is one of [[SuShi|苏轼]]'s major official and literary locations in [[105-su-dongpo-yiqiang-haomai-zhiqi-yi-dupi-buhe-shiyi-741072645]]. The episode first uses his Hangzhou通判 period to show that outside court service was not pure exile or leisure: Su Shi could write about bamboo shoots and West Lake while still remaining politically visible.

The later Hangzhou return grounds the source's [[LocalGovernanceAsCare]] claim. Su Shi dredges West Lake, uses silt to build the [[SuCauseway|苏堤]], stores grain for disaster relief, creates medical facilities, researches and distributes medicine, and organizes help for families tempted toward infant abandonment by poverty.
