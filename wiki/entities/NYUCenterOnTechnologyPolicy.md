# NYU Center On Technology Policy

NYU Center on Technology Policy is the institutional affiliation for [[ScottBrennan]] in [[the-little-known-regulatory-bodies-that-can-make-or-break-ai-data-centers]]. The [[MarketplaceTech]] episode uses Brennan's report to introduce [[PublicUtilityCommissions]] as a hidden but consequential layer of AI policy.
