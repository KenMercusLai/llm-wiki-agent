# Jennifer Jenkins

Jennifer Jenkins appears in [[betty-boop-excel-olympics-penny-isms-our-2026-valentines]] as the copyright and public-domain expert helping [[KennyMalone]] turn the newly public-domain 1930 [[BettyBoop]] into a [[PlanetMoney]] Valentine.

Her explanation gives the episode its copyright-history frame. Jenkins says 1930 works entered the public domain on January 1, 2026, after a long period when term extensions kept the public domain from receiving new U.S. works. The episode connects that freeze to lobbying associated with [[TheWaltDisneyCompany|Disney]], making her segment a bridge between [[IPOwnership]] and [[PublicDomainReuse]].
