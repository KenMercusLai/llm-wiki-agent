# Rhonda Conger

Rhonda Conger is the vice president of [[CBHHomes]] interviewed in [[tech-20260101-0101-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260101-0101-mp-tech-pod-128]]. She frames CBH as "a tech company that happens to build homes" and links that identity to the company's early AI adoption.

Conger's core contribution is an operator's account of [[HomebuildingAIOperations]]. She describes AI moving from basic writing tasks into sales follow-up, data analysis, and warranty support, with routine customer questions handled by an AI agent before employees spend time on more complex or ready-to-buy interactions.

Her labor claim is augmentation-focused: she says the goal is growth and employee capacity rather than job elimination. The source does not independently test that claim, so the wiki treats it as CBH's stated management posture rather than a proven labor outcome.
