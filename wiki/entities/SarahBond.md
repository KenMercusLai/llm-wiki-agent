# Sarah Bond

Sarah Bond is the [[PortlandOregon|Portland]] homeowner whose tree-removal dispute anchors [[its-my-tree-why-cant-i-cut-it-down]]. She and her husband Joel bought a house in southwest Portland in 2021, then became worried about a roughly 100-foot Douglas fir leaning toward the home.

The episode uses Bond's story to make [[TreeProtectionOrdinances]] concrete. Portland denied the family's removal permit after an inspector judged the tree healthy and important to neighborhood character, but the tree later fell onto the house during a storm while children were upstairs. Afterward, the city still required a retroactive tree permit or replacement compensation, prompting the family to sue.
