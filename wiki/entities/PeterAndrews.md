# Peter Andrews

Peter Andrews is the founder of [[CultureWineCompany]] in [[advice-line-with-susan-griffin-black-of-eo-products-58096820-613c-40ad-9743-f1d642447c91]]. He started the company after a 2023 trip to South Africa changed his view of the region's wine quality and potential.

His Advice Line question is how to divide attention among wholesale, DTC, and broader demand creation for South African wine. The episode treats his relationships in the wine industry, restaurant reorder rates, and small-market focus as the strongest route to scaling without spreading the company too thin.
