# Xiaoban

Xiaoban is [[YuebanDongli]]'s consumer bipedal companion robot, discussed by [[Shibo]] in [[wo-yudao-le-di-yige-zhenzheng-xiang-mai-de-peiban-jiqiren-duihua-shibo-yueban-dongli-chuangshiren-gonglu-boke-lrydelizm0-hbk68u5cqe3ti-epb]]. The product is positioned as an "alien cute creature" and family partner, not a pet copy, humanoid servant, AI speaker, or general-purpose household worker.

## Product Design
- Uses a 12-sound non-human language, expressive eyes, posture, belly gestures, and short arms to communicate intent without creating expectations of human conversation.
- Stands about 65 cm tall and weighs about 12 kg; the episode says it can run outdoors around 1.9 m/s and climb steps up to about 15 cm.
- Uses soft exterior materials, including high silicone-like coverage and belly softness, to make the robot safer and more huggable in a household setting.
- Has a sitting charging chair instead of a detachable battery, because the team believes removing a battery from the body would weaken the product's individual presence.
- Uses [[OnDeviceFastSlowBrain]], [[Qwen]], cloud long-term memory, and [[EmotionalInteractionModels]] to create low-latency responses and evolving personality tendencies.

## Market Position
As of the episode, Xiaoban's domestic price was 8499 RMB, with a preorder price of 6499 RMB and the fastest planned delivery around China's National Day holiday on October 1, 2026. The target users discussed include middle-aged women, families with children, and technology early adopters.
