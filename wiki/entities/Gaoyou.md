# Gaoyou / 高邮

Gaoyou appears in [[65-longwang-zhinu-1931-nian-de-changjiang-hongshui-658100922]] as one of the episode's sharpest local disaster scenes. The source says floodwater broke through at night and collapsed the city wall, with a conservative estimate of more than 2,000 people dying in their sleep.

The episode also uses Gaoyou in the religious-politics layer: the local City God temple had reportedly been removed before the flood, which made "Dragon King anger" or divine retribution explanations more plausible to survivors.
