# Pew Research Center

Pew Research Center appears in [[tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128-tech-20260209-0209-mp-tech-pod-128]] through smartphone-adoption estimates cited by [[CarlyUrban]]. The episode uses those estimates to show that smartphone ownership rose from about 35% in 2011 to about 85% in 2021.

The Pew context matters because the [[SmartphoneSurveyPenalty]] depends on a real shift in everyday device access. As smartphones became ordinary survey devices, [[SurveyModeEffects]] became more likely to affect long-running measures such as the [[NationalFinancialCapabilityStudy]].
