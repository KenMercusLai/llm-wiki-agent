# Storytime Toys

Storytime Toys is [[KaraDyer]]'s small toy business in [[the-supreme-court-struck-down-a-bunch-of-trumps-tariffs-now-what]]. The company matters in the wiki because it turns tariff law into an operating problem: product orders, supplier negotiations, testing cycles, and cash reserves all changed while tariff rates and refund rights remained uncertain.

The episode's Storytime Toys example shows how [[EffectiveTariffRateShock]] and [[IEPATariffAuthorityLimit]] reach far below large importers. Even after the Supreme Court ruling, the company's decisions were still exposed to [[Section122TariffAuthority]] and the practical difficulty of pursuing a refund.
