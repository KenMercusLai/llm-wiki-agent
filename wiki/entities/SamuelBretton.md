# Samuel Bretton

Samuel Bretton appears in [[the-latest-tv-innovations-have-their-critics]] as a [[RatingsCom]] representative explaining that television performance involves tradeoffs. In the episode, he says there is no perfect TV, and modern LED and OLED screens can be excellent for sports while still exposing motion problems in film-style content.

His role in the wiki is to ground the episode's consumer frustration in display evaluation rather than treating it as only personal taste. Bretton's comments connect [[TVMotionStutter]], [[MotionSmoothing]], and [[DisplayErgonomics]] to the practical fact that different content types reward different screen behavior.
