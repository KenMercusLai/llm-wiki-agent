# 森布一

森布一 appears in [[175-nvxing-weisheng-yongpin-de-shehuishi-bi-xiangxiang-zhong-geng-jingcai-969467044]] as the [[MitsumiElectric|三美电机]] executive who supported [[ItaiTaeko|板井太子]]'s plan for [[AnneSanitaryNapkin|安妮卫生巾]]. The episode says other executives dismissed sanitary products as an embarrassing women's-lower-body business, while Mori treated the plan as large enough to require serious scale.

His wiki significance is the financing and legitimacy side of [[CommercialMenstrualEducation]]. Anne's public, bright, large-scale launch required someone willing to treat menstruation products as socially useful mass goods rather than a shameful niche.
