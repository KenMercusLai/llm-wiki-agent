# Luxshare Precision

Luxshare Precision is the supply-chain company associated with [[WangLaichun]] in [[no-210-zhongguo-shouji-jianghu-xia-cong-meizu-xiaomi-chuizi-dao-ov-huawei-de-xin-shinian-zhongguo-hulianwang-gushi-24-1000932027]]. The episode says Luxshare began from connectors and cables, entered larger consumer-electronics supply chains through acquisition and process capability, and later became important in products such as AirPods.

The page's main wiki role is to keep the phone story from becoming only a brand story. Luxshare shows how repeated component execution, customer trust, and manufacturing learning inside [[ChinaHandsetSupplyChain]] can create large strategic suppliers behind the visible handset companies.
