# Institute for Science and International Security

The Institute for Science and International Security appears in [[can-computer-hackers-get-inside-your-mind]] through a report cited by the episode. The report is used as a clue linking [[LSDyna|LS-Dyna]] to Iranian scientists and to modeling explosive materials for nuclear payloads.

In the episode's argument, the institute's report helps narrow the interpretation of [[Fast16|Fast 16]]. It supports the inference that a malware rule engine affecting LS-Dyna calculations may have been aimed at [[NuclearWeaponsModeling]] rather than general engineering simulation.
