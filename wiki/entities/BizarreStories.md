# 《怪诞故事集》 / Bizarre Stories

《怪诞故事集》 / Bizarre Stories is the [[OlgaTokarczuk|托卡尔丘克]] short-story collection discussed in [[130-tuokaerqiuke-guaidan-gushiji-xunzhao-he-gaobie-803543569]]. The episode focuses on 〈旅客〉, 〈拜访〉, 〈接缝〉, 〈罐头〉, and 〈变形中心〉 rather than surveying the whole collection.

The collection becomes a source case for [[EverydayWeirdness]]: each story begins in recognizable circumstances and then alters the terms of recognition. A childhood fear becomes the old self, a household becomes a robot-care system, socks and stamps make reality unreliable, jars preserve a mother's hidden world, and a medical center makes a woman's chosen transformation into a family farewell.

## Key Claims
- The collection's "怪诞" is not only grotesque subject matter; it is a way of revealing ordinary fear and attachment from the side.
- 〈旅客〉 and 〈接缝〉 make internal states external through faces, reflections, seams, ink, and household objects.
- 〈拜访〉 and 〈变形中心〉 use speculative premises to make [[ScienceFictionRealism]] out of domestic labor, artificial intelligence, body design, autonomy, and kinship.
- 〈罐头〉 turns a comic grotesque premise into a sharp picture of dependency and delayed grief.
