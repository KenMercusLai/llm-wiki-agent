# Milk Bar

Milk Bar is the dessert and baked-goods brand founded by [[ChristinaTosi]] and discussed in [[advice-line-with-christina-tosi-of-milk-bar-fd7f2406-fda8-4c1d-936c-d20210215f96]]. In the episode, Milk Bar functions less as a full founder-history case and more as the operating background for Christina's advice: she has moved away from the CEO role toward creative and culinary work, while the brand continues to think about growth through reach, collaborations, direct-to-consumer gifting, and product experimentation.

The episode uses Milk Bar's shipping boxes and care-package behavior as an example of how packaging and gifting can become a growth surface. A product can arrive as a gift, make the giver look thoughtful, stand out visually at delivery, and then give the recipient a reason to become a repeat buyer.
