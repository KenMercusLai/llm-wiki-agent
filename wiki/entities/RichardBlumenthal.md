# Richard Blumenthal

Richard Blumenthal appears in [[the-ice-hiring-boom]] because documents released by his office appeared to show 250 fewer training hours for new ICE recruits. The source contrasts that document-based claim with [[USDepartmentOfHomelandSecurity|DHS]]'s repeated denial that training hours had been reduced.

His role in the wiki is oversight-specific. Blumenthal is part of the source's evidence dispute around [[FederalLawEnforcementTraining]], not a broader biography page.
