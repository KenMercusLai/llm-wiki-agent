# Kenny Poon

Kenny Poon appears in [[chef-vs-robot]] as the Philadelphia Chinatown restaurant operator using [[RobbyWokbot]] at [[Instafoods]]. The [[PlanetMoney]] episode says he has about 25 years in the restaurant business and presents him as the operator-side voice for [[RestaurantAutomation]].

Poon's case is practical rather than futuristic. He still adds ingredients by hand, but the robot handles timing, spinning, heating, saucing, and washing. He says the machine lets him hire for availability rather than wok skill, reducing training and staffing friction in a labor-intensive business.
