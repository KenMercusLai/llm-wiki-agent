# 180.爱丽丝梦游仙境：世界多荒诞，我也是自己的主宰（下）

## Summary
This [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode completes the reading begun in [[179-ailisi-mengyou-xianjing-shijie-duo-huangdan-wo-yeshi-ziji-de-zhuzai-shang-975483805]] by following [[AliceWonderlandCharacter|Alice / 爱丽丝]] from the [[CheshireCat|Cheshire Cat / 柴郡猫]] through the mad tea party, the [[QueenOfHearts|Queen of Hearts / 红心王后]], the croquet ground, the [[MockTurtle|Mock Turtle / 假海龟]], the trial, and the final waking scene. It argues that [[AliceInWonderland|《爱丽丝梦游仙境》]] is not random chaos: its strange rules make language, time, authority, evidence, and self-command visible. The close reading expands the wiki's Alice branch from bodily identity and puns into [[TimeStasisSystem]], [[ArbitraryAuthorityProcedure]], [[OtherworldPortalNarrative]], and Alice's active resistance to absurd power.

## Key Claims
- The [[CheshireCat|Cheshire Cat / 柴郡猫]] turns destination, sanity, and ontology into language problems: "where to go" depends on wanting somewhere, "madness" depends on the reference frame, and a grin without a visible body separates attribute from entity.
- The mad tea party is a [[TimeStasisSystem]]: because time has stopped at six o'clock, the characters cannot wash or reset the table and instead keep rotating through an accumulating mess.
- The raven-and-writing-desk riddle and the "M" stories are [[WonderlandLanguageGames]] whose function is not solution but dreamlike semantic motion, translation difficulty, and comic misfit.
- [[QueenOfHearts|Queen of Hearts / 红心王后]] episodes turn law and game procedure into [[ArbitraryAuthorityProcedure]]: croquet equipment moves, beheading commands proliferate, and the trial starts from judgment before evidence.
- Alice's final refusal is a stronger version of [[FemaleSelfPossession]] than the upper-half body-scale scenes: she keeps asking for rules until she can name the cards as cards and wake from the authority system.
- The episode keeps [[LewisCarroll|Lewis Carroll / 刘易斯·卡罗尔]]'s mathematics and logic visible through symbolic logic, topology-like transformation, the number 42, and later scientific metaphors such as the quantum Cheshire Cat and black-hole tea-party examples.
- The final comparisons to [[TheLittlePrince|《小王子》]], [[HayaoMiyazaki|宫崎骏]], [[SpiritedAway|《千与千寻》]], [[TheMatrix|《黑客帝国》]], [[Flatland|《平面国》]], and [[PeachBlossomSpring|《桃花源记》]] place Alice inside a wider [[OtherworldPortalNarrative]] tradition.

## Key Quotes
> "往哪走都无所谓" - the Cheshire Cat's answer when Alice does not know where she wants to go.

> "没有猫的笑脸" - the image that lets the episode discuss attributes detached from bodies.

> "你们不过是一地扑克牌" - Alice's final naming of the authority system as paper before she wakes.
