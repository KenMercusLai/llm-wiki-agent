# 75.故事新编：骷髅被复活后会感激庄子吗？

## Summary
This [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode rereads [[LuXun|鲁迅]] through [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]], especially [[LiShui|《理水》]] and [[QiSi|《起死》]], to move him away from both textbook monumentality and flattened "fighter" caricature. It treats the book's "oil-slick" myth rewriting as a method for mixing ancient stories with Republican-era scholars, bureaucrats, religion, science, and revolutionary language. Its main contribution is a source-scoped claim that Lu Xun's satire targets [[DisasterAestheticization]], detached evidential performance, and [[InstrumentalBeliefCritique]]: anything from Daoist doctrine to science or revolution becomes suspect when it is used as a tool for status, profit, or self-protection.

## Key Claims
- [[GushiXinbian|《故事新编》]] is presented as a late and internally varied Lu Xun project where ancient myth, modern figures, and game-like prose are deliberately mixed rather than kept in a clean historical past.
- [[LiShui|《理水》]] turns the flood-control myth of [[YuTheGreat|大禹]] into a satire of scholars, officials, and cultural prestige systems that can aestheticize disaster while remaining materially insulated from it.
- The episode reads the "禹是一条虫" argument as a pointed allusion to [[GuJiegang|顾颉刚]] and [[GushiBian|古史辨]], while still marking the method as interpretive and partly 索隐式.
- The source argues that Lu Xun is not simply anti-myth or anti-religion. He values myth as a root of religion, art, and literature while criticizing the way myth, evidence, and scholarship can become detached from suffering.
- [[LiShui|《理水》]] makes bodily harm and famine food into objects of evidence, display, and packaging, showing how power can turn suffering into research material or tasteful exhibit.
- [[QiSi|《起死》]] reverses the [[Zhuangzi|庄子]]-and-skull tradition by letting [[YangDa|杨大]] demand clothing, baggage, and practical survival rather than accepting metaphysical talk about life and death.
- The episode reads the failed use of [[QiWuLun|《齐物论》]] as a collision between abstract equalizing language and concrete loss: a resurrected poor man needs compensation, not ontology.
- The "隐士局长" episode shows how philosophy can become status capital. Officials admire [[QiWuLun|《齐物论》]] as an upper-class cultural marker while leaving [[YangDa|杨大]]'s survival problem unresolved.
- The source uses Lu Xun's "吃教" idea to frame a broader critique: religious vocabulary, science, revolution, loyalty, filiality, and celebrity worship all become corrupt when chosen only because they are useful at the moment.
- Lu Xun's scientific background matters because the episode's anti-tool critique is not anti-science. It is aimed at reducing science to "use" while leaving inherited values, status games, and opportunism untouched.
- [[GuoMoruo|郭沫若]] is used as a late source-scoped example of opportunistic reversal: the episode links his attacks on Lu Xun and later praise after Lu Xun's death to the same "whatever works" pattern.

## Key Quotes
> "从认真陷入油滑" - the episode's phrase for Lu Xun's own account of the book's mixed ancient-modern style.

> "禹是一条虫" - shorthand for the source's Gu Jiegang / ancient-history-doubt allusion in [[LiShui|《理水》]].

> "滑溜翡翠汤" - famine food repackaged as tasteful offering in the episode's reading of disaster performance.

> "彼亦一是非，此亦一是非" - [[Zhuangzi|庄子]]'s equalizing language failing before [[YangDa|杨大]]'s concrete loss.

> "吃教" - Lu Xun's term, as used by the episode, for instrumental religion.
