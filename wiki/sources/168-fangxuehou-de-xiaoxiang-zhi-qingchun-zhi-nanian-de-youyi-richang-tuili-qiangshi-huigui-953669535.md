# 168.放学后的小巷：致青春，致那年的友谊【日常推理强势回归！！】

## Summary
This [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode has [[QinZong|秦总]] and [[Beimin|北明]] return to mystery fiction through [[ZhongShengli|钟声离]]'s [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]]. The episode presents the book as [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]]: small anomalies around a bowl of tofu pudding, a borrowed bicycle, a mismatched gear shifter, and the absence of cilantro lead [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]] toward the hidden pressure on [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]]'s family. The larger synthesis is that mystery can be emotionally powerful when deduction becomes [[DignityProtectingDeduction|体面保护式推理]], and when a school-side alley becomes a dense [[SchoolAreaMemoryEcology|放学后记忆生态]] of food stalls, bookstores, arcades, embarrassment, freedom, and friendship.

## Key Claims
- The episode places Chinese mystery inside a long tradition that includes Republican-era detective fiction and older 公案 stories, while also noting the weaker modern support system named here as [[ChineseMysteryPublishingEcology|国产推理出版生态]].
- [[FangxuehouDeXiaoxiang|《放学后的小巷》]] is introduced as a recent Chinese [[DailyLifeMystery|daily-life mystery]] work whose short stories share a protagonist and school-area setting rather than a grand crime scene.
- The "堕落巷" setting turns snack stalls, egg pancakes, toy shops, card stalls, stationery shops, used bookstores, anime stores, and game arcades into a memory-rich investigative world.
- In the first story, "不加香菜", [[AliFangxuehou|阿里]], [[YanLiangFangxuehou|严良]], and [[MengTongxueFangxuehou|孟同学]] are connected through a gifted mountain bike, a borrowed bicycle, a seven-speed shifter, and three bowls of tofu pudding.
- 阿里 notices that the gear shifter on 孟同学's modified bike does not match the bike's six-speed wheel set, while 严良's prize mountain bike was seven-speed and has never been seen in use.
- 阿里 also notices that the tofu pudding lacks cilantro, even though 严良 normally likes cilantro and the stall still has cilantro available.
- The episode's central deduction is not simply that a bicycle was stolen. 阿里 infers that 严良 may have sold the prize bike because his family needed money, and that the no-cilantro tofu pudding is likely for a family member who dislikes cilantro.
- [[DignityProtectingDeduction|体面保护式推理]] is the source's emotional core: 阿里 guides 孟同学 toward a harmless explanation, avoids exposing 严良, borrows a bike, and responds with a bowl of hot tofu pudding instead of a public revelation.
- The episode argues that [[DailyLifeMystery|日常推理]] has a different strength from spectacular crime fiction: small, ordinary details can carry poverty, self-respect, responsibility, and friendship.
- QinZong's later school memories connect the novel to [[SchoolAreaMemoryEcology|放学后记忆生态]]: transfer grief, school friends, economic anxiety, used bookstores, comics, arcades, coupon mistakes, skipped classes, climbing walls, and collecting bottles become part of how the book's alley feels true.

## Key Quotes
