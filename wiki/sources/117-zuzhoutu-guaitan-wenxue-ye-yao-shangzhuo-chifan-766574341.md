# 117.诅咒兔：怪谈文学也要上桌吃饭

## Summary
This [[MihuanChishu|蜜獾吃书]] episode uses [[HanKang|韩江]]'s Nobel Prize context as a bridge into [[BoraChung|郑宝拉]] and [[CursedBunny|《诅咒兔》]]. The host argues that [[GuaitanLiterature|怪谈文学]] and other genre-coded forms can carry serious literary force without abandoning directness, bodily fear, revenge, folklore texture, or grotesque premises. The close readings of 《头》, 《诅咒兔》, 《伤疤》, and 《不受家》 center [[GenderedDomesticHorror]], capitalist violence, family exploitation, and [[PostRevengeEmptiness|复仇之后的虚无]] rather than simple horror entertainment or revenge satisfaction.

## Key Claims
- [[GenreLiteraryLegitimacy|类型文学正名]] is the episode's opening problem: the source asks why horror, guaitan, folk legend, and other genre-marked forms should be treated as less able to hold literary seriousness.
- [[HanKang|韩江]] functions as context rather than the main subject. Her magic-realist aura lets the host move toward [[BoraChung|郑宝拉]], whose stories are more openly怪谈 but still literary.
- [[BoraChung|郑宝拉]]'s stories are presented as direct rather than obscure: the episode values their blunt premises because they make pressure, disgust, loneliness, and harm visible before abstraction smooths them out.
- 《头》 turns a toilet-borne creature calling a woman "mother" into [[GenderedDomesticHorror]] about ignored female fear, bodily shame, reproduction, marriage, aging, and replacement.
- The title story 《诅咒兔》 begins as a family revenge object but refuses clean catharsis: the cursed lamp destroys the abusive company and family line, yet the aftermath remains lonely, spectral, and empty.
- 《伤疤》 is read as a revenge-and-justice story where destroying the monster also destroys the dependent village order, making fairness inseparable from cost.
- 《不受家》 uses a fox, gold, twins, blood, and inheritance violence to make exploitation painfully literal: wealth extracted from wounded bodies returns as family-internal repetition.
- The episode treats "capitalism and patriarchy" as useful but incomplete labels. The more durable thread is that revenge, exposure, or correction does not necessarily repair loss; people remain lonely after the monster is named or punished.

## Key Quotes
> "怪谈文学也要上桌吃饭" - the episode's title claim for genre literary legitimacy.

> "没什么大不了" - the family response that makes 《头》's private fear feel socially invisible.

> "请你放了我吧" - the repeated plea in the fox/gold story, linking captured animal, daughter, and final curse.
